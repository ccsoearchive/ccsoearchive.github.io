Alright I have been roaming around Gilneas to figure some stuff out. Well I say Blizzard has the flooding in the southwestern corner stop and it recedes so we get that area there back. Getting the farm using siege engines give you a stamina boost. Getting the mine gets you a strength boost. Getting the Aderic's Repose is a spirit boost. Getting Keel Harbor is an agility boost. Getting Tempest's Reach gives you an intellect boost. Taking Greymane Manor gives you not only an all stat boost but unlocks certain vendors there.

The hordes preparation area is right inside the gate where the plague wagons are. The alliance preparation place is where the worgen were saved by the night elves. The alliance gets siege carriages and the horde gets siege plague wagons. Siege submarines will be used to take Tempest's Reach. Greymane Manor will be taken by siege planes.

Inside the city each section will have an amount of guards and once all of those guards are defeated a boss spawns that you have to kill before taking that section. There are five sections Lights Dawn Cathedral, Cathedral Quarter, Merchant Square, Greymane Court, and the Military District. Once a section is taken that dailies will spawn as will vendors and repairs. The things you can get from a vendor depend on the district. The Military District will have gear and weapons. Merchant Square will have food, potions, and machines that will be mentioned later in this post. Greymane Court will sell mounts. Lights Dawn Cathedral and Cathedral Quarter will sell all of the above.

A currency used for Gilneas only will drop from the bosses, guards, and enemy players. 50 currency from bosses and 10 from players and guards. This currency will be used possibly along with honor that is gained from killing players and npcs to obtain the rewards.

Time for the fun of explaining some of the rewards I have thought up. There could be a potion called Weaken Curse of the Worgen that causes you to look like a worgen for a certain amount of time. There could be the Worgen Bot which would be a larger metallic version of a worgen that can do cool moves like a tornado that has a chance to proc a bonus. That bonus would be the claws on the machine to fly out attached to cords and spins with the machine. There could be the Worgen Jump 9000 that lets you jump from building to building. You could get the siege engines to use in Gilneas that has a 1 hour cooldown.

This would be epic just as that but please if anyone has anything else to add on please do.
