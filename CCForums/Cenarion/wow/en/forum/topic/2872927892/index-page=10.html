Why would you need guards? The point is to have a open world pvp zone with no interference. If there are 10 more horde than alliance then be it...Alliance then needs to get more people in there.

Adding NPCs on both sides is not a solution. Adding NPCs to the side that is outnumbered is more interesting that way no matter what side is imbalanced they would get NPC help.

horde would get forsaken and alliance would get worgen. The more players one side has the more friendly NPC spawn for the other side.

These npc chain with you just like an escort quest in reverse. You enter the city and see that you are outnumbered 4 to 1, you go to a 3 NPCs and you click them to follow you then they protect you. Of course this needs tweaking but you get the idea. This would allow sides that are outnumbered to have a shot at capturing the city.

I would also have a twist in there where you would need to also control other parts of the map. A giant NPC ala old AV would be sweet too.

Basically old AV without an instance and stupid reinforcements would be the best thing in the world right now
