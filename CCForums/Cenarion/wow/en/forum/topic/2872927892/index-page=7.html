This is a great idea. WoW needs some -meaningful- PvP, PvP that has consequences and shapes things. A continent-wide buff is a great result.

Heck, I'd even love to see it done a step further where each battle contributed to some over-arching war's goal, the kind of thing that each side could fight for a week or month to gradually achieve. Have some kind of bane/blessing mechanic to reward whoever won the current war. Example off the top of my head - roving squads of pillaging soldiers from whatever side wins would loosely "occupy" the continent. Imagine that small squad of elites that roamed Arathai. Each zone would have a level-appropriately scary squad of undead/worgen who patrol. It would hardly be game-breaking, but it WOULD be a tangible difference in both atmosphere and gameplay, a dynamic one that players shape.

Maybe make this "overall" effect for PvP servers only, to hammer home that PvP shapes the realm's story just as much as raiding.
