Also ideas for how to win.

Assault style: The attacking faction has 4 people take a flag one of which must be placed in each district for the cap. Flag bearers cannot enter vehicles or mount. They can bubble but not cap while bubbled. If a flag carrier dies the flag despawns for 10 min. making it impossible to win while this is happening. Protect the flag!

Siege style: The enemy faction has set up a HQ in the Lights Dawn Cathedral. Get a siege engine and smash it!

Arathi Basin Style: Each district has a flag post capture it just like AB.

Total war: Rarest mode occurs only when one faction has held the city for an extended amount of time. Unlocks advanced Siege engines like Gunships, boats etc. The Attacking faction starts with a gunship and must flatten the zone. After 2 min. the defender gets a limited amount of wyvern/griffons to board the gunship and destroy it they also get AA guns on the ground to do some damage. Each wyvern/griffon can be used up to 3 times and can be shot down by enemy players which is bad if your riding them. After 10-15 min. The defender will get bombs which can be thrown onto the enemy gunship which start fires which then need to be put out. After 30 min. if no one has won yet the defenders will then get a gunship of their own to finish the enemy gunship.
While on the gunship you get AA guns, bombs and parachutes. AA guns shoot down wyvern/griffons bombs are used to destroy the indicated positions or which are randomly picked each time this battle type occurs. On the gunship you can grab bombs and use a parachute to land in the city so that you can blow up the objectives/enemy weaponry.
