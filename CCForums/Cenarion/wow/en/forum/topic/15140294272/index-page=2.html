Hi guys,

I found a temporary fix for mobile AH and would like to share it. I'm not sure whether you guys will find it useful, but its worth a try.

I basically created a level 1 character. Please wait for 1-2hrs after creating your characters as it will take that long for the armory to update your new character. Also please remember to create and log in to the level 1 character. Logout after you have created it. In about 1-2hrs, your toon will appear in the new AU/NZ battlegroup and your mobile/web AH will work as usual.

As you can see, this toon is in 'Jubeithos'......not 'Jubeithos OLD AU' anymore.

I basically sent all my stuffs to this level 1 character and started selling my stuff :)

Cheers.

ps this is a temporary measure as only the new toons will work. Your old toons will remain broken. Lets hope the proper fix comes soon:)
