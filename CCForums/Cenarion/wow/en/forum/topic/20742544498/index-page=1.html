If you're Alliance-side, come get yourself or someone else an RP bouquet for Love is in the Air!

There's choices like less expensive Peacebloom and Mageroyal, and then more exotic Talendra's Roses, and Rain Poppies. You can buy a single, a dozen, or customize your own fancy display, and have them wrapped for free with your choice of a red or blue bow!

Look for the short space goat and little storm elemental standing outside Fragrant Flowers-- Just at the end of the Trade District and The Canals. Hours of operation vary throughout the day, but are stable from 8pm to 10 pm server time on Monday-Thursday, and consistently throughout the day on Friday.

*WARNING: Right-clicking flowers may not have your character equip them for viewing... They're only herbs, after all. But since I'm stationed right next to the flower shop, if you want an in-game bouquet to display you can get one from there!*

...Flowers!
