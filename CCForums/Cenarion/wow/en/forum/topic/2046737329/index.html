This all may be tied into the haste issue posted here: http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/2046736421?page=1 In survival my Cobra Shot claims to to be a 1.37 second cast time but when I use the ability the cast bar displays it as 1.6 seconds. Now I know the default UI does not show cast time on the actual cast bar but I asked several people with various addons that allow such and all report the same issue. Cobra Shot is not shooting any faster than 1.6 seconds.

When I pop all cooldowns that buff haste/attack speed(Berserking and Rapid Fire). The tooltip says the cast time for Cobra Shot is 0.719 but the cast bar displays 0.9 seconds. So there is about a 0.2 or so second discrepancy between what tooltip claims and cast bar does. not a big deal I guess but it could matter for hunter's at lower haste values.
