Here's my experience. I leveled my hunter to 85 and went straight into PvP. It was weird at first, had to get used to the new changes that were made and I loved it (random battlegrounds here) Once I obtained all the best honor gear I could, I grouped up with my bro who plays a resto shaman and headed for the arena's. I stopped PvPing on my hunter. I read forums, I tried all sorts of macro's, keybinds, and even practiced with my brother to try and improve my skill. Wasted efforts.

Now I recently leveled a feral druid up and did a few bg's for some gear, but not much. I went into arena's with him paired with the same resto shaman. (Note: My brother's shaman has not geared further with the new season and my druid has less resil then my hunter who's still stuck in s9 gear) I never had so much fun in arena's since the start of Cata. I was actually killing players, doing damage, having reliable CC, and loving it. When we go against a comp that has a hunter, I just smile and know it'll be an easy win.

I would love to PvP on my hunter again, but the way the class is, I just can't. All I can say is, that if Blizzard wants me to keep my sub going once other certain MMO's come out, they better fix a few... a lot of things, a lot.
