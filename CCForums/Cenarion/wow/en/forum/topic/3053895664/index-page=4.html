I decided to give my hunter a go & lvl'd it from 50 despite feeling like it was nerfed hard. I was MM when focus came out & going from instant aimed shot to 50 focus 3 sec cast was a big nerf for me. 15 lvls later, pve, I'm struggling to enjoy playing my hunter(BM).

I think of my mage & wonder what if hot streak lit up but I cannot use it more than half the time because I had no mana. Or I if had to cast scorch all the time to regen pitiful amounts of mana on trash to try to sc**!! in aoe attacks or kc's? That's what it feels like atm & I find its not much fun. Are we meant to be focus starved on trash without cds every 2 out of 3 pulls? it feels wierd having to steady shot on trash while the other dps are able to blow stuff up all the time.

I Jumped on my 70 fire mage & hot streak pops up heaps! It can also be proc'd by pyroblast so you can double pyro...Where as KC proc for extra dmg takes ages or revolves around fervor BW cd's if I get it without those cd's I have no focus to use it nearly EVERY time. MM, 20% chance on steady shot stacks up to 5 times for 1 free aimed shot, really?

Another thing I find is my pet zipping back & forth between me & mobs when they die is annoying. An ability where my pet would attack the next mob within 3 yards would be nice or increase the pets melee range. It only has to run 1/4 of the way back to me & I lose dps.

On a target dummy or tank & spank boss the rotation works & I found it fun but anything else still seems really messy. When they first announced focus I thought ok & wondered how they would do it. I thought nah they won't make it 100 resource like rogue they'll make it unique! I thought 200 focus (lol foolish me) so we can burst but on bosses fall into a rotation as if we had 100. I can do the scramble for focus thing if they made it fun but I'm going to need allot more to play around with.

THIS is a Deadzone: http://i54.tinypic.com/25z05sh.png

What you want is the ability to shoot people from point-blank melee range. Fine. Personally I think this is something that ought to be a perk of the Surv tree (it just makes sense) because hunters DO need to keep some weakness imo. If a hunter was as deadly in melee as he was at range, he would have no reason to ever kite or try to stay, you know, at range.

While there are several PVE fights I can think of where point-blank shots would be a blessing, and it would definitely be a boon in PVP, I think it would make the class a bit overpowered and we would need to have other areas toned way down. For example, if we could shoot in melee range, they would likely take away Disengage and a lot of the shots we have for slowing/rooting.
