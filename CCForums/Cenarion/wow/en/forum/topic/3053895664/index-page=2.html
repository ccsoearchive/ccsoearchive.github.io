1. I want to see widow venom, instead of increasing mana costs or reducing healing effects, channel 25% of all health gained or whatever to the hunter while active on the target. There we go, a semi-decent heal that also reduces the amount of healing done to your target that isn't ridiculously OP.

2. Aspect of the Dragonhawk. BACK NAO.

3. Instead of increasing the distance of Aspect of the Pack's aura with the glyph, change the glyph so that the daze effect dissapears but take like...10% speed off. That allows us to stay away from other people, but not give us a ridiculous speed advantage over others.

4. Keep focus, I like it more than I liked mana because it more fits into the whole hunter persona. Just give us an aspect or something that allows us to regenerate focus over time, kind of like Aspect of the Viper did, just....without the 50% reduction in damage. I'd be fine with losing 20%-25% damage to be able to keep firing my power shots.

5. Give us more haste-producing abilities or reduce the CD for Rapid Fire. In PvE, I play MM and put out ridiculous dps but in PvP, when I'm constantly low on focus, I like to pop rapid fire and launch steady shots at my target, both to do damage and regen mana, but can only do so every 5 minutes. Make it 3 minutes default CD, then drop it down to like 1.5 minutes for MM hunters who've reduced their CD.

6. Give us an ability that allows us to break free of roots, slows, stuns, etcetera that has a decent CD, something like the Pally's hand of freedom. Seriously, it's like we're supposed to die when someone gets in our deadzone. That or give us an ability that works within our deadzone that does 50% reduced damage but has a small knockback, something like a melee ability.

7. Track Hidden, Flare, Traps = Worthless against stealthies. Seriously? What's the point?

8. Give us an ability that allows us to restrict casting time by 50% because it seems like when we go up against healers, they just heal themselves to full health in seconds.

9. Can we get a disarm please? One that requires melee range.

Like many suggestion posts mirroring this, I agree with many of the ideas here. I'd like to add a few of my own and revise some of yours that I think would be better :

1. Widow Venom's problem is its focus cost and low yield combination. Either make it have no focus cost (and possibly no global), or keep its current focus cost/global, and increase its duration to maybe 1 min to add incentive for using the shot. Or like the above post suggests, make it akin to feral's skull bash effect: add a 10% increase to mana usage.

2. I like the aspect of the cheetah/pack idea, although the fact that the speed boost stacks with disengage's MM speed boost may make it too strong in others' eyes. Maybe make aspect of the cheetah a cooldown, 1 min or so, that increases the speed by 30% for maybe 10 seconds and has no daze. Small but possibly significant when dealing with pillars/face huggers.

3. As far as our ranged deadzone goes, I think it should be scrapped or have an effect like: 30% reduced damage in melee range, so we aren't completely shut down. Our playability is diminished with a 16 sec cd (w/ glyph and talent) gap opener/offensive pillar breaker running together.

4. Pet swapping in arenas: I think the (glitch?) issue with pets summoning with low health and being one shotted is extreme, even considering the 3 globals it takes to pull a new one out. Maybe keep the low health, 10 or 12k, but have it last only 3 seconds, and have dismiss pet be instant and have a short cooldown, like 20 seconds. More playability.

5. Flare - I see flare nowadays as more of an offensive ability rather than the hybrid it moreso used to be. It's something to keep a rogue from vanishing away from your kill and recup'ing up/waiting for a healer's cc to break. Scrap the glitches with LOS and range it carries, as well as make its hangtime in the air shorter.

6. Camouflage - I hear a lot of hunters talk about ideas regarding this ability. I think camo should keep the same exact effect it has, including the need to be out of combat, but increase its CD to 1.5 min, and add a dot stripping effect to it, if that's even possible to put into a script.

7. Self heals - Chimera shot over time can be a nice heal, especially when it crits, despite how low the heal is. I'd like to at least see my chimera shot crit healing me when the shot itself crits, in addition to having its own crit chance, and maybe up the heal to 8%. It's hard to say what would be viable for the heals on this shot and what would be too much, but this seems to be a good idea to me.
Or, on the other hand, allow us a self heal sort of last resort button like most other classes have; maybe a 3 min CD that heals for 25% instantly, at least, dumping all of our remaining focus into it.

LoS is perfectly fine. I suggest buffing the damage by a small amount and making it immune to slowing effects (cast time, not movement speed).

Aimed Shot - The damage is great! The cast time...not so much. With a base cast time of 3 seconds, this easily-avoidable shot can make it or break it. I would suggest cutting the cast time in half but giving it an individual cooldown, somewhat comparable to the cooldown of a Warrior's Collossus Smash ability. I believe that most hunters would agree with me that sacrificing a cooldown for a shorter cast time would be worth it.
