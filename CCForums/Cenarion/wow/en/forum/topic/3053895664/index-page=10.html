1805 resil is basically nothing at 85. Might as well be runnin naked. You'll regain a lot of that power once you get your honor gear tho. And even moreso with conquest gear. But it takes a lot of dying on bgs in between. I think thats one of the issues hunters have when they reach 85. Suddenly everyone can own them when they were owning everyone else at 80.

I'm not saying our class isn't broken, it definitely is in a LOT of areas, but there's a lot of work-arounds to the current way of things too. You kind'a have to re-learn your hunter.

I'm BM hunter, and it's taken a while to get up to where I'm at. I still die a lot - and i have 3789 resil, but I also do a TON of damage in bgs, usually out-damaging MM and SV. I haven't done much in arenas yet, as that's a different creature than BGs, and still trying to find some reliable arena partners.

I could make a list of what I think is broken in regards to hunters but I think most of it has been stated here. I don't see any responses from Bliz, and likely won't.

It almost seems as though bliz has deliberately under-powered us in Cata, because everyone claims we were so OP prior to. Maybe this is in fact the case. But its still no excuse for having abilities/tools that simply ARE broken, as in DO NOT WORK. Like Scare Beast - more than half the time.

Rogues, IMO are WAY over-powered, and hunters are pretty much helpless against them unless we have a healer or a strong melee class to bail us out of embarrassment, and that doesn't matter which hunter spec you have. I'd like to hear what other hunters do to deal with rogues, cause just about everything I've tried so far just doesn't work. Once they get on you you're dead unless someone else takes them out first. No Disengage as they seem to have some way of disabling that for you. No deterrence as they get through it, and if they don't get thru it they'll just sap you and be on you again while deterrence is in CD.

My main issue is with Rogues being able to invis out of our track invis, and our camouflage is pointless when we can still be seen easily by everyone else. It's like bliz is playing a joke on us. "Hey hunter! You can hide now! Haha! Just Kiddin! But you can PRETEND you're hiding!" Really? Whats the point?

WHY make us use spell pen? It's a wasted enchant we could be using for resistances instead, since we have NONE. We have to gem agility and crit to the max if we have any hope of doing any real damage, which lowers our haste to virtually nothing, but we can't gem for haste very much or we'll lose agility, hence losing damage.

I've tried MM and SV. Maybe I haven't tried them long enough or with the proper specs but they both suck for me. BM has always given me much more of a survivability rating than either of the two and, as I said before, my damage is higher than any of the other two I've come across so far, horde or alliance.
