Deadzone - stupidity. maybe there was a time and a place where this made sense, but in today's WoW it is just a super nerf that has no reasonable justification.. afraid we might actually be able to fight back? Seriously I am leveling a spriest and it can sit there spammin it's equiv of "aimed shot" with no fuss whatsoever of melee range.. and it hits harder than aimed shot and it takes less time to cast it.. and ignores armor .. OP compared to the equivalent Hunter attacks, clearly. Fix - Make every Ranged Class and ability have the same restriction.. there now it's fair right. But you know every other class would go nuts and quite the game.. so why are we stuck with this crap exclusively?? Remove this senseless restriction or find your hunters leaving the game imo.

Heart Of the Phoenix, Pet Rezzing - HotP is just broken.. works sometimes.. usually not.. when it does work.. pet comes back with basically nil health ..and gets instadead... or seems to just burn the ability but not actually give u the pet.. why its not fixed to actually do what it supposed to is a mystery. Insanely long Pet rez.. I PvP BM .. simple Kill my Pet Kill me.. I may as well leave arena when they kill my pet.. you really thinks its so strong.. its not.. ok so I put all the talents into its defense so it does crappy damage and now you think its so strong... well its not.. HotP fails again.. but hey I Gyphed for rez and so with superhero powers manage to survive rezzing it.. and it gets one shotted.. haha funny joke.

Focus / Focus regen- Sure there is enough if you are standing back in standard rotation (which is only standard because if specific (BAD) focus regen requirements).. but what happens when u need some burst? Time the whole encounter around a single phase or event? Just so you can have enough for maybe 4 Multi shots or a quick Chimera/arcane volley ...then what... nearly 2sec laughable damage Cobra/Steady x 3 before we can do anything useful again.. may as well FD and hope there are (any) other classes there to finish the job. Our fuel tank is way too small.

Pet Mechanics/Pathing issues - Putting mobs in the game that can't be reached by pet attacks (mobs that can be hit by other melee classes).. is this to purposely insult us for choosing Hunter class? Pets still getting stuck and dumbfounded.. seriously after all these years of fixes??

PvP - How can you think this class is balanced when there are no hunters amongst the top arena teams? To get any reasonable results from a Hunter you need to work your butt off... and due to its limitations .. even then you just have no hope to reach the level of many other classes in all departments except perhaps solo farming.. which is just so important to the competitive nature of the game right...

No heal ability - Chimera Shot if u go MM.. which is useless in PvE and laughable in PvP , Spirit Beast and Talent if you go BM ..which again is practically useless in PvE and just as laughable in PvP (again restrictions.. need BM, Need Spirit beast.. thanks yeah). Rogues, Warriors, Druids, Mages ...seriously is there any other class in the game besides Hunters that can't heal..??? WHY .. is it because we are this overpowered super class. Justify that. Yeah Bandages.. as if I don't have some DoT tickling away on me in PvP.. right...

Trap Launcher - useful for setting up cc on non-active targets , it is useless in pretty well all other situations.. the actual traps range is so small u need dumb luck to use it to cc a PvP player.. so will have to scatter trap which is actually less time consuming.. go figure we can use 2 abilities faster than we can use 1 and cost less focus...hmmm. For the amount of focus it costs you would think its some great OP ability lols.. you really think its so good? Really why does it even cost Focus? u think we will sit there spamming trap launcher?? OMG beware everyone!

Basically its not worth the effort to get equivalent results om a hunter like other classes.. and impossible in most cases.. Armor means nothing now in the game.. mitigation is always trumped by class abilities/talents.. mail is no advantage over cloth .. buggy pets (which are only a component of our class-homogenized damage capability) are no advantage over heals..

Yeah it is better than when we had to buy arrows and and had no disengage or deterrence.. but well we were just pathetic then so getting us up to just sub-par instead of laughable isn't enough.. I'm running out patience and tired of being the hardest working underdog.
