The only shots that "needs" to work in melee range, are utility shots we don't have a melee equivelent for (on the hunter, excluding pet abilities).

So, Concussion Shot doesn't (Wing Clip), but Tranquilizing Shot and Silencing Shot should.
Steady/Cobra Shot should as it generates Focus, and the damage already isn't anything drastic as it is.

You will still want range to dish out your heavy hitters (Aimed, Chimera, Explosive, Arcane), but for when you get stuck in melee, you will have access to your utility and Focus generation abilities.

Since all of the heavy hitters (except Aimed) are always Instant, we don't need range for long to use them, rendering LOS/Melee range less of a worry for those. The Shots that have cast time (Steady, Cobra, Aimed) either have Focus generating functionality, and should be available in melee, or can sometimes be instant/free.

Multi-shot I am on the fence. It is really annoying have to make sure the target is far enough way (i.e. Occuthar if you don't know about the FD trick), but it is simply a damaging ability. Granted, we still have to target something and it is an AOE ability, so that also is a factor.
