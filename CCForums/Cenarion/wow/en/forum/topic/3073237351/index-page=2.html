Hunters are OK, they need to do a few minor tweaks and fixes to finally balance us though.

1.A) Minimum range is iconic of the class, and doesn't need to be removed to balance the class. However, the suggestion to make all our instant cast abilities usable within minimum range, is something i'd like to see. We do run out of focus fast, and would not be able to regain focus unless at range.

1.B) The problem with instant casts at melee range though is it doesn't make sense. How would blizzard explain / teach new players to understand why this is? It'd be an odd mechanic to say the least.

2.) Merge Hawk, Fox, and Wild into one aspect. We already macro it to make it trivial, (Except wild) tell me how this would be Over Powered? Simplify our class on par with others!

3.) Hunters, still more complicated to succeed in pvp than other classes. I thank Blizzard for the little they have done so far though. But please, make our defensive pet abilities (Roar of Sacrifice, Master's Call) not require LOS, or make them reliant on our own LOS, its incredibly frustrating to manage at times. Requiring the pet to be alive / active still a requirement obviously.

4.) Traps: Blizzard is WELL aware of trap issues. They are over-discussed. FIX! They are a primary reason for our failures, so im not downplaying it.

5.) Our healing debuff is not cost effective, at all. It's just another thing to complicate our overly complex class.

6.) We are THE ranged class. Redesign it in someway to emphasize the importance of it, by modifying our damage based on how far away we are.
