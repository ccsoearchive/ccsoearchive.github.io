    Ok I know I am going to get all kinds of posts telling me I am wrong on how I perceive the hunter class, and how my opinion is wrong but that is just the problem we as Hunters have. For some reason we are the most hated class, and being referred to as Huntard (Combination of Hunter and retard) because we can't play our class correctly. If there is such a widely used term for the other classes I have not heard it in game.

    now I have not been playing since vanilla, however it did enter during the BC era well before the release of Litch king, and my main class to roll is the Hunter. That being said I have had little issues with PVE play outside of Raids, and Battlegrounds. I use to roll a survival hunter and hand more than enough mana and mana recovery that I never worried about down time for my shots, and as a lvl 44 hunter I was praised more than once by higher level players that were willing to duel me to see how well I do at DPS. Now that we were converted to focus I go into down time after just a few shots and having to stop and hopefully get a steady shot off before my group kills all the pulled mobs. If not my DPS takes a major hit. Mind you I still do about 25% of the damage, average for a 5 man dungeon group.

    Now while this alleged dead zone is precived resolved I can inform you none believers that it is still there. In tight corners or small corridors I find it hard to keep up my focus or get a shot off, while still unable to use my raptor strike to try and save some DPS.

    Now in the days of old we had the tiger aspect that buffed our melee and had some extra ability about not being trackable, that I never seemed to figure out. This gave us hunters enough of a boost that we could moderate one's desire to engage in close range with us while still keeping Hunters ranged abilities making the class capable and intended ranged killers.

    Like several have said everyone crowds us severely cutting our DPS in PVP, and several of the CQC classes(Rogue/warrior/pally) have abilities to quickly close the gap making PVP very difficult for us, so I don't want to see any more posts about how melee classes have no ranged DPS, cause they flat out don't need it. Warriors have a dash, Rogues have a dash and their stealth that I will get more into later, DKs have that powerful pull that really messes up a hunter's ranged DPS, and Pallys have so many buffs that by the time their HP is down by 10% they have already closed the distance and killed the hunter.

    While Kiting is a valued skill for any hunter I have found that many of the CC traps and that have failed in their effectiveness in PVP. I have seen a rogue/druid actually stealth over my freezing trap without setting it off, and while trying to CC many of the traps are broken by other players or AoEs so at most the target is stalled for a fraction of a second before they pound on the hunter.

    Like has been stated many times before a lot of our skills are broken, Feign death for example only works in PVE effectively as its effect in PVP allegedly only removes the hunter from their target. Big deal, it doesn't mitigate damage, it doesn't prevent AoE damage, debuffs, or prevent them from being retargeted a second later.

    Another skill I have found needs work is the Track hidden and Flair abilities. Nether seems to work effectively. For a Class that has abilities to prevent the sneaky druid cat or rogue from surprising the hunter, I have found little effectiveness with these. Wile I do get lucky less than 10% of the time, with a constant flare and my track hidden always active, most of the time I don't see them until after their first attack. The attack that breaks their stealth to begin with. Clearly this needs to be fixed.

    Now i expect a post or two about my alleged QQing and while that may be what this is, I feel it has to be said anyway. For the Melee classes they seem to have valued skills to close the distance, and their attack isn't hampered for it, however hunters only have 1 effective ability and that is only so effective. Our only other option is to either backpedal slowly or loose access to our DPS by flat out running to get the needed distance. We can't fire a shot while running away as we can't fire behind us. Also we don't have heavy CC available to us outside of a single target due to CDs. While we may have stopped that pesky warrior, his partner rogue saps us from behind and kills us in short order. I can't count the number of times I have rezzed in a BG only to see all my teammates safely leave the GY only to have me be stopped by that single stealth unit and killed.

    Now I am not saying that we need pistols though the idea intrigues me to see a Hunter duel wielding a couple pea shooters, maybe with a buff allowing for duel targeting or something, That is not the issue as I am more of an archer as opposed to a riflemen. I have seen several guns with blades on them, and historically some actually did have as such if the opponent got too close. A skill that converted our ranged DPS to melee while would not keep the warriors and rogues at bay, could still deture the mages and shadow priests from getting too close cutting our effectiveness. The buff could last for 10+ seconds or be an aura like the priest bubble.

    Now I have seen non tanks like mages and priests actually tank in a Dungeon due to their ability to kill at all ranges, and having a good healer on tap just in case things got dicey. There is not reason a mage should be able to tank better than a Hunter! That right there tells you that Hunters are the most nerfed of the classes. Sure if they can stay at range watch out, but get within their dead zone and they are no more dangerous than a newborn babe.

    Now I know you people say that Hunters don't need or shouldn't have melee skills but face it they do, while not as good as the warriors and rogues, there is no reason they should fall prey to the weaker armor class because they close the gap effectively cutting off our attack. Give us something to either severely mitigate damage like making Feign death make us un-targetable for for the duration or remove damage debuffs and cut AoE damage drastically, or a buff to give us some good melee damage even if only temporary.


    The only other option is to fix the problems with the skills we have. If I am suppose to be able to track and see hidden units I want to be able to see that rogue and kill it before they are in sap range. The same goes for druids. And how about a talent or that allowing us to ether target behind us or increase backpedal speed. Or better yet an AoE repel, so we can deal with all them CQC issues.

    Yes I know we have scare beast and a few other CC abilities but they don't work effectively. Where is the Hunter's AoEs? I think we have multi shot and maybe scatter shot, but that is it and neither is going to help keep the masses at bay. We are not tanks, so there is no reason we should be force into a Bullpen scenario every time we PVP.


    On a final note how many hunters have had dueling issues? Yeah, while it is possible to survive in a BG, duels are most notably melee ranged and rarely allowed to be ranged making the hunter a non-duel class. Most before the start of the duel will close the gap making it impossible to start off with a CC shot, unless it is against a fellow hunter. With every other class capable at melee ranged damage, there is little to know hope for Duel hunters. One reason they are rarely seen in arenas.
