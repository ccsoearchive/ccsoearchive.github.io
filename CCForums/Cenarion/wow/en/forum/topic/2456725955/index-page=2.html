Warlocks spell pushback protection is still gone. The hotfix, applied on 4/27/2011 has not fixed the 0% spell pushback protection that we locks have all experienced since the 4.1 patch. All spell hard casts and channeled casts in both my specs (Affliction Drain & Destruction) suffer extreme pushbacks from any damage taken.

Drain Soul - Nearly 25% channeling time lost on each successful damaging attack (practically knocking this spell down to 1 to 2 ticks at best).
Drain Life - normally 3 ticks, down to 1 tick at best (sometimes none) while sustaining damage.
Unstable Affliction - 1.5 cast time (not counting haste effects) nearly increased to 3.5 seconds cast time while sustaining damage.
Fear - 1.5 cast increased to 3.5 seconds while sustaining damage.

The list goes on and on. Warlocks are unplayable at the moment - at least not how they are intended to be. PvE and PvP application of the class are absurd when in any encounter where damage will be taken (either from AoE's, random DoT's, or just a PvP situation where some random melee class decides to exploit this bug and use us locks as a cloth target dummy that last for all of 3-5 seconds).

I understand that issues like these are unintentional and may take some time to fix; however, I most certainly do not appreciate being told (twice now) that this bug has been hotfixed and results should manifest soon - only to have it not be so. The icing on the cake for me is that this was an issue reported during PTR testing, just like the DK Rune issue, and nothing was implemented then to address it.

The Warlock community may be small, but we are a fervent player base that is enthralled with the class. Please keep us updated, and please keep working on this issue - and for godsake, please post something so we know where we stand at the moment.
