First of all, let me say this - welcome to Cenarion Circle!

I encourage you to go ahead and jump in the OOC and IC chat channels. As a whole, the role players on this server are friendly and helpful. The great thing about Cenarion Circle is that new role players have a chance to step in and find their niche - we will help you get there. I have been playing here for about four months now and I love it. I also arrived here new to RP on WoW.

One guild over here, Azeroth College, has been hosting classes for new role players. I'm not sure when those are scheduled, but I can find out for you. I see that you are already in a guild - I'm not sure what type of guild it is, but you might consider looking into the various RP guilds over here. Depending on your character's personality type and motivations, we would be glad to suggest from a number of great guilds. Having other people to bounce ideas off of and to talk to IC and OOC makes a big difference in your RP experience.

Be sure to take a look at all of the stickied threads for roleplayers. Also take a look at the Cenarion Circle Wiki:
http://cenarioncircle.wikia.com/wiki/Cenarion_Circle_Wiki

Good luck! I hope you find what you are looking for here on this server.
