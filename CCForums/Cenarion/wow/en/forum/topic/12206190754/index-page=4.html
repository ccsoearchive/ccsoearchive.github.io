I GOT IT!

Malfurion goes with the Worgen druids to take back Gil'neas and restore the lands of Lordareon. They take back the Gilnean lands and charge Undercity, but the plague is unleashed. The Worgen run with their fast speed, but Malfurion is captured and taken prisoner.

In his cell, Malfurion sees what Sylvanas has been doing with Kol'tira. Sylvanas and Malfurion talk about their hopes and dreams through iron bars, and their conversations ignite positive emotions in Sylvanas' heart again. Eventually, she realizes that she has fallen in love with Malfurion and his sexy bird wings. She sets him free, knowing that they can never share a life together when one is undead.

Malfurion flies back to Teldrassil bearing news of how to siege Undercity. As Tyrande and Malfurion meet to share a passionate kiss, Malf is enlightened with the fact that Sylvanas' decrepit booty is so much hotter than his wife's. As Tyrande speaks to him in her Troll accent, he becomes overcome with grid. in his mind, he shouts "where is that sexy Thallasian drawl I miss so much???"

That is when he knows: he's in love with Sylvanas.

Sylvanas shows up in the upper branches of Teldrassil, having taken flight across the seas on the wings of her Val'kyr. Malfurion disappears into the woods every night in search of his Undead mate. Tyrande prays to the Mudda Moon that her husband isn't seeing some young night elven priestess. Elune makes no sound, as the Goddess doesn't meddle with something as unholy as undeath. So Tyrande follows her husband into the night and catches him bumping booties with Sylvanas.

Tyrande is so enraged that she cocks her bow and plunges an arrow through the hearts of Malfurion and Sylvanas, sending them falling into the oceans of Kalimdor from the highest branch of
Teldrassil, reminiscent to the suicidal fall off of Icecrown that sylvan as experienced after Wrath. Tyrande shrieks(in reference to Mean Girls, as it wouldn't be WoW without pop culture references): "SHE DOESN'T EVEN GO HERE!"

Sylvanas is resurrected by one of her Val'kyr and uses the remaining to bring Malfurion back from the grave. No longer connected with druidism, Malfurion must ride on the back of Val'kyr to Undercity. In turn, Sylvanas' character gets ruined as she becomes the arm candy of Malfurion and Malfurion does something for the good of the world, harming the forsaken's hold on northern Lordareon, allowing the vengeful Tyrande to destroy Undercity.
