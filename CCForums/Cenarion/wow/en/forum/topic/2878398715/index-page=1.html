102.4% is the soft cap for Warriors. After this point, we no longer gain block chance, but we still gain 1.5% chance to critical block for each mastery point above the 102.4% (much like soft cap on expertise removes dodge from the enemies CTC at the soft cap, but parry still remains). Mastery is less effective after 102.4%, but it's not useless.
