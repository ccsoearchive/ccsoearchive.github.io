Having just moved here recently, all I can really tell you is what others have told me.
Oh and welcome! It's pretty awesome here so far!

In finding a guild, you'll first need to figure out what type of guild you would like to be part of. Most of the guilds have a theme here. For instance, Da Doctas, which I joined recently, is a school/organization of healers and their protectors. There's darker themed guilds, messenger themed guilds, and so on.

When in game, make sure you hop into the ooc channels and make some friends, that might help in finding where you want to go also.
