I am just the messenger, I asked him for a copy of the hunter thread when it got deleted, so he sent this to me to post onto the forums to clear some things up on his status and give some feedback I think. That's enough of me. Here's Bandet, the original author of "The Hunter Thread":


Yes, I quit wow several months ago, and no it was not because I got forum banned, although it was annoying, but it was in total the 4th time I got forum banned, so I was used to it by then. They didn't like me.

I quit wow because of the developers lack of commitment to the game. At this point they are riding the money train, and I for one am not willing to fund their retirement if they are not willing to even respond or acknowledge topics brought up by me on the forums, most notably in "The Hunter Thread". It is pretty obvious to the hunter community at this point that we are blatantly being ignored, and we have been for a very long, long time. Hunters are like the afterthought of the developers. Hunters are like the bastard son Jon Snow from Game of Thrones who is a champion and a hero at heart, but everyone kicks him and insults him for what he is, and his mother won't even let him sit by the side of his brother who was in a coma.

The simple proof that the topics I brought up are important issues and that Hunters deeply care about it, and any response we may get (Which, we still haven't) is evident in the fact that to this day, 4 months later, it is still being kept on page one of the hunter forums. Has Blizzard replied to the issues? No. Did Blizzard reply to one pvp-hunter related question in ATD? No.

What do we get?

We get told to push a 2 ton boulder up a hill, while some guy calls us slow as he walks past, carrying a pebble in his hand.

Sorry. That isn't fun.
