We had a bit of confusion on the RP class today and what to discuss, so we threw together an impromptu RP class for those who were expecting it today. We had a nice turnout, small group, and with the help of some great speakers in Paden (discussing RP looks and gear), myself (stressing communication and flexibility), and Ghostkitty discussing RP Add ons, we had a nice session.

I didn't mean to step on anyone's toes, but there seemed to be a fair bit of interest so we forged ahead. I'm sure these topics can be re-approached at a more formal RP class session. That said, the speakers did a wonderful job.

Thanks to Aetherus, Glormon, Shiapoli, Taelanas, Torix, Akermas, the above noted, and anyone else I forgot, for coming and supporting.
