PUBLIC NOTICE

The Anytime & Anywhere Messaging Service will be conducting a charitable raffle to aid local manufacturing businesses still recovering from the Cataclysm. All proceeds will go to skilled artisans of the Horde and the Alliance!

GRAND PRIZE: Mechano-Hog / Mekgineer's Chopper

RUNNER-UP PRIZES (2): Any faction-specific pet, including Argent Tournament pets.

CONSOLATION PRIZES (5): Delightful assortments of food, drink, and flowers from around Azeroth. Sure, you might not have a new bike, but at least you're happy!

Tickets may be purchased from your local AAMS Branch Managers and other officers. Simply inquire at our Branch Offices or in your local hearthstone channels!

Ticket cost: 1000g. No refunds. All proceeds go to charity. AAMS will cover cost of prizes regardless of raffle funds raised. Odds vary based on number of participants. Enter at your own risk! The AAMS cannot be held liable for any damages caused by improper use of prizes. No ticket purchase limit.

Drawing will take place Thursday, January 19th at the AAMS End-of-Year Party. Winner need not be present to collect prize.

Peace, by any means!

NOTE TO CHARITABLE DONORS:

We have received messages from several charitable manufacturers and artisans asking whether we would like additional prizes donated. The AAMS is enormously grateful for the support! I would offer two suggestions for these volunteers:

1) We can certainly offer crafted goods as our third-tier "consolation prizes." I would suggest a market value of no more than 1000g (the cost of a ticket), and perhaps no less than 50g or so (though I realize the retail prices can fluctuate day to day). We will list your business as a "Supporting Sponsor" in our promotions.

2) Alternatively, the proceeds of the charity raffle will be spent on supporting local artisans and businesses. If you had a product in mind that would be too expensive or too time-consuming to construct as a prize, why not apply for a grant from the proceeds instead, after the drawing? The AAMS would be happy to fund construction and donate the finished item to someone in need.

We hope both of these options will help encourage the support we've already seen. Again, many thanks from the AAMS to our supporters!

Raffles fall under Blizzard's In-Game Casino rules (http://us.battle.net/support/en/article/in-game-casinos), and, as the article says, it's against ToU to advertise in any way in-game and even on the forums.

Here's an MVP poster's explanation on what is and what isn't okay:

http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/2965116489#11

Players are not allowed to advertise casinos in ANY manner in-game or on the forums. That means whispers, say, yell, Trade, General, Party, Raid, ANY channel in-game is a no-go for letting others know a casino is being run. That also means Character Name, Guild Name, Pet Name cannot be used to blatantly imply a casino. It means posting on the Blizzard forums in any way to announce one's casino is against the rules. That even means playing the game publicly can be against the rules, as the purpose of such would be to attract other players. Getting winners to advertise is also not allowed.
...
Thus, the rule currently is against advertising - in any manner whatsoever. Guilds can advertise on their guild websites and be fine. Friends can make friendly wagers in Vent and be fine. And "Casino" runners, to be allowed to continue, must find their sources of gold external to WOW communication.


So, yeah. It sounds like a good idea, and I may end up buying a ticket or two. Just be very careful with how you go about holding it.))
