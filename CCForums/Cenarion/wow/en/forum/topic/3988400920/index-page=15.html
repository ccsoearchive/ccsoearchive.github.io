I agree with the OP and have some constructive suggestions

1. New loot tier - you have begun this with raid finder so now we have a split between casual epics and normal epics. I suggest a different color to differentiate between

2. I initially hated the new talents but upon reflection I actually am excited. I love tanking and have done so for a long time. I would say I probably have three or four options at best to change my talents and none of the changes are hugely significant. Under the new system it seems that I may have many options that would open up different play styles and less cookie cutting

3. I love the idea of normalizing gear for challenges. Please look at rolling this out for old content with progression related rewards. In other words we get into MC in 10 or 25 and the gear is normalized or the raid is tuned upward for level 90 . I'm not sure if that is possible but imagine how much content we would have if you could attack those instances in a challenging way for some sort of reward.

4. PVP at the moment is a little broken. I've been in battles where I had 6 dps on me and 1 healer was keeping me up and he had 4 on him and he kept himself up. Battles last too long and all this mechanic which to me is arena centric makes battlegrounds fun but a little crazy. Try killing a pair of holy Pallies. It is fun don't get me wrong just needs retuning - higher damage taken .

5. We need a world raid system . For example going in and killing a town boss , innkeeper etc should earn tokens. These tokens should be redeemable for vanity items etc. this would give impetus to attack. There should be rewards for defending a town raid . There could be an achievement like World Defender with a title and crazy mog set that would look awesome. This is to encourage the vanilla style raid
