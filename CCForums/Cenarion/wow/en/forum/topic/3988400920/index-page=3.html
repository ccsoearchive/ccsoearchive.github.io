I think both Scenarios and Challenge Modes are Blizzard's response to what's on the horizon.

They see ArenaNet and other companies trying to push a content model that breaks from the Holy Trinity of Tank/Heal/DPS and come up with Scenarios, mini-dungeons that can be done purely by DPS players.

They see the endless complaints about nerfed content, content becoming skipped, and posts for scalable content, and create Challenge Modes, dungeons(and hopefully raids)which lock your gear to the minimum level and put you on a time attack for vanity rewards.

They see people wanting other things to do at endgame besides PvE/PvP, and decide to try Pet Battles.

They see people complaining Cataclysm was too scattered and disparate, they make a new continent for everyone to explore.

I'm very much looking forward to more on MoP.
