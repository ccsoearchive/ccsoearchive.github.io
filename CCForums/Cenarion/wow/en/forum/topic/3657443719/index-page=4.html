Apparently I have to spell this out for you;
The 'tough choices' is more a matter of pve, not pvp. Some of the talents are things that will drastically be favored by some fights over others, and the intention is that it be easy to swap them around based on what fight you're doing. Vendetta is a lot stronger on a boss with downtime than a boss with 100% uptime, for example.

You aren't really going to have that 'tough choice' factor in pvp, outside of playstyle considerations. Arena is arena is arena is arena. Despite the fact that people commonly say that arena is unpredictable, it really isn't. You won't be changing talent points on the fly as often in pvp.

Prep vs step isn't the only tier where there's a seemingly obvious choice. Almost every tier has a seemingly obvious choice based on what we currently know, and it's unreasonable to expect otherwise. The goal of putting them in the same tier isn't to make their worth the same, it is *to prevent you from having both.*
