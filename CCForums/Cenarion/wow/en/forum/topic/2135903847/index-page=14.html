Hm. I'm relearning / getting back into my rogue, as I took a hiatus from my rogue to level other alts. Seems about the same, just one silly question?
What's the difference between AD and QE for strafing?
I used QE for a while, did fairly well. I tried using AD for strafing in a bg or two and I just couldn't get a hang of it.
Is there really any difference, other than placing to reach other keybinds? Cause all of my keybinds are relatively reachable from QE, since that's where I'm personally used to strafing from.

There is no difference, you're just binding them to different keys. I think the idea is to free up Q and E for macros since your hand placement in a WASD movement setup can hit QERTFG/etc. much easier than 67890-=, and moving them down to A and D isn't losing much since most people aim their movement with their mouse anyways. Incidentally, removing strafe completely isn't a big deal either since you can just as easily strafe by holding right click + AD.
