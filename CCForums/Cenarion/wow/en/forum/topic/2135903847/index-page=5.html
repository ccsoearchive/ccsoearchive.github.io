To play a rogue effectively you really need to be using keybinds.

Most people will unbind both "A" and "D" and change them to strafe left and strafe right accordingly.

Holding both and/or right click is used for moving. Both give the same advantages of being able to move suddenly and give you optimal visual display of your surroundings. The choice of unbinding "W" and "S" is up to you, either way in almost no circumstance will you be using "S" to back peddle so it's better to either change it to another ability or leave it blank.
