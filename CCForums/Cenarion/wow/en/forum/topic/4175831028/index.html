The Black Hand Society is an event sponsored by the Modas il Toralar. It is a place for those of similar philosophy, taste and ethics to gather together in celebration. Come and sample the intoxicating herbs and spices of the Wormwood Undermarket. Trade blows in our arena or words over fine elven wine and orcish grog. Grow drunk with revelry and violence, embracing the primordial truths of our nature.

Our bouncers will do the best to ensure only those of a certain demeanor are allowed entry. Toss aside the restrictions of morality. Let not ethical tethers bind you. A perfect gathering for the ethically flexible.

The Black Hand Society is a perfect place to dispense of some coin, and build alliances with the darker factions of Azeroth. Violence is encouraged, but try not to kill those who are kindred. Those of the Toralar will not tolerate those who cross their shadow.

((Quite a few of us were gone for some time. The actual physical Modas guild hall isn't a place in game, we use Fenris Isle for the same reason we used to use Ravenholdt, very few areas accommodate a party type event. Fenris Isle is the closest to where the Modas Sanctum is, is wonderfully empty due to phasing (back when phasing was being added and prepared for Cata, we did a lot of scouting of locations to be used for events like this).

Fenris Isle was a superior choice by virtue of being right next to the Undercity, near the ruins and lake so valued by many of the Modas and nice and empty. Feel free to use it, RP with it as you please. It is silly to lay claim to any static location in a game in a way that would deny others a right to use it as well. Sadly very few "keep" style locations exist within Horde territory and the place where Modas used to (for many years in the old days) have guild meetings (Karazhan, until it got covered with people when it was a major raid) always made for a poor party spot and difficult for quite a few to get to:P ))
