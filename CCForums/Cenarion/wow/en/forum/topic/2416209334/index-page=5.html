Even though, it's not exactly related to backstab, I'll continue here instead of making a new thread:

Energy regen and/or abilties that proc or increase energy do not seem to be working consistently under certain circumstances. It could even be similiar to the 4.0.6 bug where energy was not affectiving regen. From what I can tell this is related to having/not having certain buffs, mostly haste related buffs.

The most dramatic result I've seen has happened in the same raid between different boss and mobs. Energy regen seems to slow considerably, to the point where it is difficult to even maintain a viable rotations.

Forexample: Between one boss/mob and another, I can pop an agil pot, and hit Adrenaline Rush and watch as my energy bar barely fills up, resulting in me doing below 18k dps, which is ridiculious given my gear. On another boss/mob just a few minutes later, I can shoot up past 28k - 29k dps doing the same thing, the only difference is energy fills up faster as it is expected too. The raid group should be the same for each of these, as well as being full raid buffed.

The only difference I can surmise is that a tempory buffs is being applied in a different order, such as icy talons overridding, hunting party, or windfury.

Furthermore, I believe that the lack of energy alone isn't enough to support such a change in dps, that something else must not be proc'ing or not applying correctly inorder for the dps to be so low.

The most frustrating part is that these results are inconsistant and must be based off a certain situation for it to happen, yet can happen/change literially between pulls.

Edit: This bug/problem is actually so frustrating that it makes attempting to dps so lacking of fun that I have no desire play until it is resolved, which to put simply: sucks.
