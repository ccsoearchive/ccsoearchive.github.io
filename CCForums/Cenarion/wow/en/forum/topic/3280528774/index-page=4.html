My concern for the hunter class is the next tier, where a great many casters and rogues have legendaries. There is also the issue of this buff for melee. Next tier looks very grim for hunters, especially with the lack of any real dps buffs/tweaks in the PTR notes. The factors that lead me to the bleak outlook on hunter viability are: caster legendaries (which will be farmed till the end of the expansion), rogue legendaries, and outright melee buffs.
