I took a closer look at some numbers and came up with something I found interesting, I wanted to check your numbers to see if we really are 8th out of the total 10 dps classes and if so what was the spread from highest to lowest.

I wrote down the top dps number for each class (if Arcane was top for mages I used that and if Fire was top on the next fight I used that etc.) and then divided it by 7 to get a sort of average number for each class.

Rank for each class in parenthesis is in the following boss order:
(Beth, Rhyo, Alys, Shan, Bal, MDomo, Rags)

Priest: 2.7 (2,2,2,2,3,4,4) all top 4's with 4 2's
Mage: 2.8 (3,5,1,5,2,3,1) all top 5's with 2 1's and a 2
Lock: 3.0 (5,3,3,4,1,2,2) all top 5's with 1 1's and 2 2's
Druid: 3.2 (1,1,4,3,4,5,5) all top 5's with 2 1's and a 3
Rogue: 5.7 (8,10,5,1,7,1,8) 2 1's and a 10
Warrior: 7.5 (7,9,6,9,6,6,7) no top 5's
Shaman: 7.5 (4,6,9,6,5,10,3) 3 top 5's and 1 10
Hunter: 7.6 (6,8,10,8,8,9,6) no top 5's and 1 10 - really? not a single fight in the top 5?
DK: 8.4 (9,7,10,7,9,8,9) no top 6's and 1 10
Pally: 9.1 (10,7,10,10,7,10,10) no top 6's and 5 10's

Looking at it this way shows the Blizz statement that class balance is the best it has ever been is a bit off?

I am unsure what GC is looking at honestly. Dks and Pallys are in a very bad place.
That being said, hunters are not exactly what I would call fine or a even high, I think the only thing that's high is GC.
If all things stay relatively equal the the 20% buff goes into effect your looking at Dks on average moving ahead of hunters and pallys closing the gap.

P.S. I understand that its the first round and no one is screaming that the sky is falling, at least not yet. But when you read that the lead developer thinks our dps is perhaps a bit high and you look at the above numbers, it should worry just a bit. If not for the hunter class then for the state of dps balance in general, LOL.
