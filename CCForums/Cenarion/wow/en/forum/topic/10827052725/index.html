Alliance Guilds

  • Azeroth College

    • /6 <Azeroth College> is now recruiting students. We are a moderate RP and leveling guild that focuses on teaching and learning how to RP. We accept all levels and classes, and hope you graduate with flying colors! Whisper any member for an invite.


  • House Savelli
    • (*)
    /6 <House Savelli> is a heavy RP guild representative of a family of human nobles and the men and women loyal to them. We are quite new and are currently seeking reliable "core" members to help the guild grow.


  • The Ebon Sanction

    • /6 <The Ebon Sanction> is an Alliance branch of the Knights of the Ebon Blade, accepting members of all classes, moralities and pursuits who are interested in splitting their loyalties between the Ebon Hold and the Alliance . Contact Ragefang for more info.


  • Rules of Acquisition
    • (*)
    /6 <Rules of Acquisition> is a light RP guild based on trade and in-game economics. We also look forward to (as we grow) raiding, etc. After all, you can't sell a treasure you haven't gotten yet.


  • Shiftingsands Trading Co
    • (*)
    /6 <ShiftingSands Trading Co> is a mid-heavy rp guild that is still small and laid back. SS is open to experienced and new rpers, with focus on offering lessons and advice to newcomers in the more adavanced aspects of rp.


  • Stormwind City Watch
    • (*)
    /6 <Stormwind City Watch> Is a heavy RP guild looking for new applicants to join our ranks. We serve as Stormwinds Police / Guard force and we act as the figures of authority within the RP community. Please send a message to Orwyn or Laman for more info.


  • Tales of Eirnin
    • (*)
    /6 <Tales of Eirnin> believe in fostering a welcoming family environment with adults who know how to relax and enjoy the game. Mutual respect and tolerance are a must... and of course a healthy appreciation for the Awesome That Is Eirnin.


  • The Nukenson Trade Enterprise

    • /6 <The NTE> (A.K.A. Nukenson Trade Enterprise) is a small merchant guild who owe thier allegance to thier profit margins first, and the Alliance second. Heavy RP focus with recriational dungeons and PvP in the off time.


  • Pillar of Honor
    •  (*)
    /6 The <Pillar of Honor> is a small, RP-heavy guild that's rebuilding after multiple leadership changes. IC, we're a military support-type group (Recon, healers, etc.) that takes members of many different faiths and beliefs.


  • Azure Crusade
    •  (*)
    /6 The <Azure Crusade> is formed to fight the evil that the Argent Crusade will not: that of the Forsaken, and the Horde that shelters them. If you believe that fighting the good fight means not closing a blind eye to any evil, then seek us out. We also honor the work of artisans and civilians.


  • Mihari no Hitoshii
    •  (*)
    /6 <Mihari no Hitoshii>, The Guards of Balance. There is no Good, no Evil. There is only Chaos and Order, and the world needs both. A new RP guild for those not wanting to be constrained by traditional moral labels. Contact Zaium for info or to join.


  • Gambit

    • /6 <Gambit> is a group of like-minded folks that have decided the Faction War has gone on long enough and are ready to use any and all means available to put a stop to it. Contact Tyrynna or Belpha with any questions.

  • Shádow Knights

    • /6 <Shádow Knights> is a level 25 guild looking to add to our ranks. We raid, pvp, rp, quest and have fun together! We raid Firelands (2/7) Thursdays and Fridays from 9 pm -12 am server. PST to Larabeth or any member for an invite.


  • Esoteric Darkshire Cabal
    •  (*)
    /6 The <Esoteric Darkshire Cabal> are a close-knit group of people who harbour knowledge of things unseen by most. Our guild members are helpful, social, respectful & friendly, and somewhat insane. Our "home" rests at the Scarlet Raven Tavern in Darkshire, which we strive to protect, along with the citizens of Duskwood.


  • Ironforge Guard
    •  (*)
    /6 The <Ironforge Guard> acts as the law around Ironforge. Most of the time, that usually means we keep the drunks from rioting and the gnomes from blowing things up, but we do get some excitement from time to time. Contact Ethillian for more information.


  • Cog of Genius
    •  (*)
    /6 <Cog of Genius> The Cogularity is Nigh! This cadre of big-thinkers and altruists is on a quest to change the Alliance into a more peaceful, intelligent, and creative society. Pester Zlatta if you are ready to face the future!


  • Templars Lament

    • /6 <Templars Lament> is sworn to protect the King and all of the Alliance. The Horde shall taste the steel of the Templars and fall before them! <rp,pvp guild> (contact Geira, Foromansus, Korasakie, Thomasito, or Eleeanna)


  • The Guardians of Azeroth

    • <The Guardians of Azeroth> are a small but elite group dedicated to defending and aiding the poor, homeless, and weak all over Azeroth and Outland. Ask Aereath if you want an out-of-character or in-character invite!


  • The Wellspring School

    • <The Wellspring School> is a place where teachers and students alike can get hands-on experience with the crafts, philosophies, and combat styles of Azeroth. We strive to be RP-newbie and LGBT friendly. Contact Alniyat, Cavrin, Zaium, or Aquamerine.


  • Wobbly Compass Crew

    • <Wobbly Compass Crew> is a merry band o' mismatched Privateers lookin' for more able hands to board wit' us. We're also offerin' our services t' anyone in'erested, for a fittin' price, o' course. See Capn' Brassbuckle fer a post.


  • Manus Ignota

    • /6 <Manus Ignota> is an eclectic guild that pursues all facets of WoW. Members are diverse but share simple goals: protecting one another’s welfare, and the reputation of the guild. If you seek respect and are of a noble heart & mind, MI may be for you.


  • Luchduu Ocheliad

    • /6 <Lluchduu Ocheliad> - They're your outlaws, your villains who are loyal to their leader, Imperon Showdah. Imperon is who you want to talk to.


  • Valhöll

    • /6 <Valhöll> is now an MCC (Mekgineer's Chopper Club) focused on Dwarves and Gnomes, but our doors are open to all. Alts more than welcome! Chopper needed only for RP rank advancement.


  • Assassins League

    • <Assassins League> is a guild based off the League in Ravenholdt. It is accepting more recruits looking to earn a living killing and stealing. Send a letter to Ettinjandy for a chance to join and fight in the name of Lord Ravenholdt.


  • Elemental Expeditions

    • <Elemental Expeditions> is a group of friends who raid and RP together. Brought together by a love of dungeon delving, drink, and dirty jokes, we take all levels & classes. RP is casual/guild chat based. Contact Sekhmeet, Celandine, or Kashara.


  • The Bláck Legion

    • <The Bláck Legion> is recruiting all able bodied adventurers and individuals interested in making quite a bit of gold and fame for bounty hunting, mercenary work and other less "respectable" jobs. We are a mercenary company based out of the Legion's End Tavern in Booty Bay. Contact Silver, Ashok, Alexander, Tai, Alaric for more on how to join and become Legion.
