The sort of people who would queue for Classic AV are generally the sort of people who will stay there for the long haul. Even with the ability to leave early, the rewards from AV should not be greater than those found in other battlegrounds (to prevent AFK honor farming).

The "No penalty for leaving once you've played here for 30 minutes" was suggested because my idea of a "long" battle might be 4 hours, but your idea of a "long" battle might be 90 minutes. It was intended to accommodate real-life commitments that might occur during a three-hour battle, such as feeding the baby.

I'm not sure how such a system would affect the random BG finder, as Classic AV would not be a random option.

Additionally, players would only be able to leave the battle if someone else was queued up for it.
