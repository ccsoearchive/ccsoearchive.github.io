The reason oldschool AV was so popular is because it most simulated the experience of World PvP that use to exist in the game prior to the introduction of Battlegrounds.

Back in the day when BG's weren't around there was world pvp at many major alliance/horde hubs. SS vs TM...etc. Battles that would last for HOURS as horde and alliance fought to push each other back.

There were no rewards. Players did it PURELY for fun.

While I know BG's are here to stay...we need world pvp back. My suggestion is a new pvp option in the PvP list called "World Skirmish".

-Player Queue's up for a World Skirmish
-Computer places 5v5, 10v10, 50v50, 100v100 in a random portion of the world.
-Players fight to the death
-Put a barrier around the area and if you leave it more than 5 seconds you're out the battle.
-No objectives other than to rack up the most kills in the time limit.
