I'll begin with some background on the philosopher who got me thinking about this: Karl Popper

He is a philosopher on Induction/Deduction and reasoning. He's mainly responsible for the inception of the scientific method and is famous to a lot of scientists.

He defined a "failure to prove a theory is false BUT NOT evidence that it's true" as Corroboration. (While Confirmation means "Evidence that a theory is true".

To say a theory is well "corroborated" sounds like we have a good reason to accept it. Moreover, the process by which a theory comes to be "corroberated" might reasonably be taken to provide evidence.

Hilary Putnam another philosopher criticised this by saying: "But given Popper's definition of "corroboration" we must deny any reason to accept a theory.
(without evidence we have no reason to accept a theory)

Popper's model of the structure of scientific reasoning is: (1) Premise (2) Predictions (3) Test - by observation.

Deductive argument says that theories can survive false predictions (if the prediction is false- the theory is false.)

Lets move on to how this relates to WOW. Let me also start by saying this is SERIOUS pvp. If you want to play for fun, casual, you can pvp naked and still have fun and haters gonna hate.

People have an awful lot of hate towards Combat rogues in srs PVP, fire mages being used, or even lawl a fury warrior??? Why would you use the specs when there are others that are obviously better? There are those who try to prove that the specs are viable and can go far even though there is no evidence and therefore no reason to accept this.

Combat rogues errywhere are trying to theorycraft/prove how it can be used in srs pvp. Will we see combat rogue gladiatiors coming out of nowhere???

To actually prove that these specs would do well you would need the top players of the class to change. If every single rogue Glad went combat they would probably still do well (maybe not as well as they would sub). You would probably see everyone else switching to combat as well. EVEN if sub is the better spec, you see the top players playing it well so everyone else is going to go with the FoTM.

Now by deductive reasoning theories can survive false predictions. Putnam believes contrary to Popper that the theory COULD in fact be true even if predictions prove false.

The reason why combat is NOT viable and CAN NOT do well in serious PVP if we go by this philosophy is step (3) of the scientific reasoning. Test by observation .

Go to arena junkies. Look at the top ranked rogues. The top 100 rogues are using sub in their arena comp.

You have premises that combat can do well. You have predictions that it can go far. You DO NOT have evidence that combat is good. Therefore. It will not be good until you do have a bunch of R1 glads using that spec instead of sub.

Like i've said you can remain casual, you can play for fun. There is nothing wrong with this. Don't let anyone tell you how to play the game. As long as you are enjoying yourself you are a winner. But if your goals are to improve yourself as a player. To reach your potential. To do the best you can! you need to go sub. Combat just isn't going to cut it yet.


TLDR: go sub cause tryin to prove combat is viable for srs arena is not going to happen any time soon. You'll remain a stay at home dad instead of a stay at home glad. The general community is going to hate combat because it has not been shown to be a decent spec for srs PVP.
