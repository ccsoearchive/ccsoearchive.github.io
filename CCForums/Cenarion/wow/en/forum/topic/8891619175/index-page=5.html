Breathing hard and now sated with the souls of the harpies, he stopped to gather a handful of beautiful feathers, thinking to have Ginik make something for Mina out of them.

The trek back to the bluff was quick and quiet. The elevators worked non stop day and night. He slipped into the tent and took off his boots and robe, settling down beside Mina who still slept, unaware that he had gone. At least he hoped she had not noticed.
