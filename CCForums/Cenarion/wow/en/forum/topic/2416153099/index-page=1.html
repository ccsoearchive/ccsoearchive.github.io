*Notices are posted in many of the usual places. Any town or settlement near the Barrens seems to have been papered with them.*

Attention:

My dad is missing, and we want him to come home. His name is Liko Farstrider, and he is a Tauren. He is a great hunter and tracker, like me. We haven’t seen or heard from him since late November, but I bet he is fine. I just want him here because I miss him so much.

Please keep your eyes open for him. He was last seen heading from Bloodhoof Village toward the Barrens. He was tracking a pretty little kodo calf (because my little sister needs to learn to ride soon), so I bet he is just being really quiet and sneaky, so he doesn’t scare it away.

He is thirty-two years old. He is a little tall and skinny for a Tauren. (Oops, my mom says he is *lean,* not skinny. Except when he gets busy and forgets to eat.) He was wearing light hunting armor at the Ishnu Por Ah’s tabard when we saw him last. There is a raven’s feather in his hair. He is probably traveling with his riding wolf, Shilah, and (maybe) a lion named Alastro.

If you see him, please let me know. You can contact me over the hearthstone or through the mail. You could probably even leave a message with someone else in the Ishnu, if I’m not around.

Please help me look for him, if you can. We have been looking all over, but can’t do it all by ourselves. I do have some money from selling skins and my drawings and glyphs, so I could pay good money for anyone who can help find him.

Thanks for reading this.

Degan Farstrider,
Ishnu Por Ah

---

((Initially, I’d intended to just write a story to explain where Liko has been since the Cataclysm. Instead, I thought it might be fun to go for a more interactive approach that could incorporate both Horde and Alliance characters.

Here is what I hope will happen in this thread:

Anyone who wants to help in the search should make an IC post that includes *where* they looked and *what* they did. (Maybe they searched for tracks. Maybe they interviewed potential witnesses. Be creative!)

I will be checking the thread regularly, and I’ll post the results of each person’s efforts. I’ll do my best to include clues to help drive the search, but I may slip in some red herrings, as well.

Feel free to contact Degan in game with your finding. Send a letter or ask in the Hearthstone channel. Even if I’m not on Degan, I may be around on an alt. (Alliance players might consider using the AAMS to pass on information.

I’ve got high hopes for this thread, but it depends entirely on the participation of other people. Every discovery is one step closer to finding Liko!))

Margie read the post again, the hopeful letter written in the childish hand. She silently counted to herself: November, December, January, February... She sighed and looked down at the small orange tabby at her side. "I h-hadn't realized how long Liko hassss been m-missing, Jack. This issssn't good at all..." The cat emitted a protesting mew and bumped his head against her leg. The priestess smiled in spite of herself and knelt to pat her companion. "We'll st-still be on the l-lookout, though, eh?"

----------

He trailed behind, his friend riding hard ahead of him. His dainty paws left tiny puffs of dust on the road behind him as he ran quickly, trying to keep up with her. Her kodo's tail swished near him, almost hitting him, but he nimbly leapt out of the way and ran underfoot. He was unsure of where she was leading them, but he knew what he was looking for.

Up ahead, there it was: nearly camouflaged in the high grass, a golden cub was frolicking, running ahead of its mother. He quickly darted off the road and over toward the lioness. His lady rode on, unaware of his absence. He carefully padded through the high grass until he stood in front of her. He stretched his arms out in front of him and bowed his head low. She looked at him with a mixture of curiosity and reproach. Her voice was a low rumble. Are you very lost?

Without looking up, he replied, I am not. I was hoping for your help. When she did not reply, he slowly lifted his head and saw she was mere inches away, towering above him, seemingly more interested in flexing her claws than in looking at him. Her cub, still much bigger than he, sat quietly off to the side, her head tilted in mock curiosity. He mastered his fear and spoke again, his voice a trembling mewing. We are searching for one of your kind. Do you know of Alastro?

The lioness flexed her claws once more and smiled dangerously at the cat. He is known to us.

He nodded. His voice quivered less. He and his master have been missing for many moons now. My lady and I are looking for him. Have you seen him?

As night fell Benna looked for a quiet place to wait for her great grandmother. She settled herself below a tree, lit the fire and the incense and waited for word. Evetually her great grandmother came to her.

"Benna-girl. Your Bull isn't dead. Wherever he is, it's not with his ancestors."

Benna smiled, good news was a rare blessing "Thank you Great Grandmother!"

"His ancestors are nice people, are you sure he's married?"

"He's married, Great Grandmother"

"Maybe he has a brother? It's high time you stop running around killing things with bulls and settle down with one."

"I will work on that, Great Grandmother"

Her Great-Grandmother sighed (Did most spirits sigh?) "Well good night then Benna-girl"

Benna put out the fire and said the appropriate prayers and tried to think where she might look for Liko in the land of the living. She could start in the Barrens where the kid had seen him last, but if Liko was in trouble in the Barrens, with Mr. Dernes and Miss Red Earth both guarding the Barrens in different ways, it was unlikely he wouldn't have been found. And he must be in trouble, he couldn't/wouldn't have just left Miss Jaxila and the kids. Would he?

The place to start would be the bar in Booty Bay. It was a gossip hub and someone there would know something about Mr. Liko. That was where she would go.

Upon arrival she walked up to the bar and ordered something strong but not on fire. Then she asked the barkeep if he'd heard anything about Mr. Liko Farstrider or anyone of his description in the past few months.
