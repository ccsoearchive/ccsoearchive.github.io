General Information— - -

    - Advertisements will be sorted by racial limitations; inside the category sections, things will be alphabetical.
    - Submissions are asked to try and keep a brief response to all fields, to avoid running out of space, as only 5000 characters are allowed.
    - As this thread grows, advertisements may be summarized, or shortened, to make room.
    - Please feel free to check back and post updates (Sudden exceptions, changes in race requirement, website, advertisement thread, guild disbands etc.) about your guild; just please make sure you put up a new post, not edit the past one.
    - Please do not create submissions if you are not the Guild Master, Officers may also post, but please get permission from your guild leader first.
