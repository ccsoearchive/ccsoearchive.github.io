MEEP 25, the raid sponsored by <Biscuit of Dewm>, is recruiting!

Short and sweet:
    Visit our website for information. The URL is my guild name, without spaces, dot com.
    You can also contact Red, Grollo, or Peganaire through in-game mail.
    Tuesday, Wednesday, and some Thursday evenings 6:30-9:30pm server
    Loot Council system (main spec rolls, with some high priority items on restricted rolls)
    Mumble voice chat software used; download it at mumble.sourceforge.net


Current high-priority needs:
    Druid or death knight willing to both Tank and DPS as needed
    Hunter, Warlock, and Shadow Priest DPS
    High-quality performers


MEEP is a raid group with a simple mission: to provide a quality raiding environment, with a gentler raiding schedule, built on a base of knowledgeable players. We allow our members to choose whether or not they'd like to wear our guild tag. Aside from clearing the content, our goals also include obtaining the more esoteric achievements, and occasionally attempting things without associated Nerd Points™.

The basic MEEP 25 raiding schedule is three hours on every Tuesday & Wednesday, starting at 6:30 each day. We will also raid Thursdays once the raid content demands it, but for now we either do a lolz optional activity, or progress with a 10. In order to ensure the progression of our entire raid team, MEEP follows a Loot Council system based largely on "let the people who have been helping the raid roll". ^_^ And of course, afterwards we encourage our crew to form their own groups or hook up with other raids to burn lockouts.

Why MEEP raid is right for you:
    You want a group of serious players, with a lighter schedule
    You're ready for a raid commitment, but may or may not want to lose your current guild tag.
    You have a passion for clearing through content, and trying new things, like achievements and random activities
    You seek to improve your own performance, and welcome discussion and comments
    You enjoy a mature and friendly environment, and want to avoid loot drama and people with chips on their shoulder
    You want a raid that understands that a game comes second to real life issues


Why MEEP raid is not a good fit for you:
    You want a heavier raid schedule, and server firsts are your driving purpose
    If there's no loot, you don't see the point. Achievement points are also stupid.
    When your raidmates outperform you, you feel despair or apathy instead of a drive to improve
    You don't have time to research your class on your own time.
    You can not commit to making most of the raid times.
    You are a sensitive soul, and take everything personally.
    Your skill in Holding Your Tongue is 1.


Other notes
    Mumble is a requirement, though a microphone is not. But if you have a microphone YOU HAD BETTER HAVE A HEADSET. No one wants to hear the echo of a Mic+Speakers setup.
    Attitude, Skill, and Reliability--we seek raiders who have all three qualities, not just two of them =P
    The ability to check the guild/raid forums often is key.
