It's possible for 4.3, but don't be surprised if it isn't there. Removing DP stacking will change how Envenom works, and potentially how Rogues do damage (max DP stack procs other hand's poison). We might have to wait until the next expansion.

A more likely change would be to fix DP de-stacking. DP falls off quickly, and then you're stuck with the ramp-up again. Simply increasing the duration of DP (but not the damage over period of time) wouldn't really change balance. Most DoTs last much longer than DP ... and it's a really really easy fix.

Personally, I like DP stacking. I think it's a good mechanic. I just don't like how long it takes or the fact that it procs from auto-strikes. I'd rather see a 100% proc rate on yellow hits, and 0% on white hits ... but that's another huge mechanics change.
