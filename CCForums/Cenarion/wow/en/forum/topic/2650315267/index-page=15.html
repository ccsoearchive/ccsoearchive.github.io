(To put my question into better perspective for voters)
A simple fix would be a system for Art of War and Divine Purpose that is 100% but works off a stacking buff. For instance every melee attack grants a stack of Art of War, at 5 stacks the paladin gains the current Art of War buff. I used 5 stacks because with a 20% proc chance a player should usually get one every 5 hits; this cements that but also disallows the player to get a lot of procs back to back, evening out the randomness of our DPS.
