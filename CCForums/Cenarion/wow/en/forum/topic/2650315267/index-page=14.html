One of the more popular topics on the damage dealing forums as of late has had to do with the tradeoffs between rolling a pure class vs a hybrid.

It's generally accepted that the advantage of being a hybrid is having the flexibility to respec for tanking or healing, something that pure classes can never do.

It's often proposed that the advantage of being a dps only class is having three unique and flavorful styles to dps in. However, all of the pures follow a trend of 'viable spec, mediocre spec, and useless(pvewise) spec' which effectively allows any pure class serious about pve/raids locked into only one tree.

For people like myself who prefer a spec that isn't the 'top dps' spec, it's disappointing to not be able to play a favored spec in both pvp/pve aspects of the game.

What is being done to ensure those who favor pure classes enjoy as rich a gaming experience as any player of a hybrid class enjoys with the majority of their specs?
