Several Days Earlier.....



Narane walked in the Trade District of Stormwind, having just arrived from the docks just two hours before. As a druid, Narane had experienced a kind of culture shock when she saw just how crowded the human capital was. It was nothing like Moonglade or Ashenvale....or Teldrassil, the second World Tree. She just couldn't get over just how many humans and other races there were. There weren't as many elves compared to the humans, the dwarves, gnomes and all the other so-called 'lesser races'. Narane hated to label any race as 'lesser'.
Narane got a nice price at the Auction House on a staff she had her eye on. She looked at the Hero's Call Board and noticed there were a lot of places to explore. The call board had a map of the Eastern Kingdoms, Khaz Modan and Lordaeron to the far north on it. There were also flyers on the wall calling for aid in various places around in the Eastern Kingdoms. There were several 'missing persons' posters on it as well. A flyer caught Narane's eye, calling for any and all aid in Duskwood to investigate the mysterious Twilight Grove....a place of night elven origins or so it seemed. Narane absently made a mental note to visit Duskwood sometime in the future.



Present day


Narane drank the rest of her cherry grog and asked for another round. She didn't often drink that much, but the grog was good.
