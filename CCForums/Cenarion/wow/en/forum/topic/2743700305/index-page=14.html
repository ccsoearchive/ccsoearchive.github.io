Narane had eaten perhaps a dozen of the snow-covered berries when the troll came in. The fact that the troll was able to make it to the inn meant that he had belonged to one of the neutral factions like the Earthen Ring or even the Cenarion Circle. As a druidress, Narane was a member of the Circle but she didn't recognize the troll by sight.....trolls all looked the same to her.
" Greetings. I don't think we were properly introduced when we last met. I'm Tanu, ambassador of the Cenarion Circle"
Narane choked on her grog. She cleared her throat and coughed before speaking to the troll.
"Greetings, Ambassador Tanu" said Narane. "I'm Narane Woodspliter of the Circle...what brings you here, If you don't mind me asking?"
