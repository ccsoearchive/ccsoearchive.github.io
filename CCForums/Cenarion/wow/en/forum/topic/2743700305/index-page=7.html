The warlock slipped off the roof and into the alley behind the ruined Recluse, and retreived his Hearthstone from his bag. Concentrating on the stone, he felt the energies wash over him, and he could begin to sense the clean smell of the Legerdemain Lounge, and, with a flash, he was gone from the noise of the Mage quarter.

Chuckling, he headed upstairs to the room he permanently rented. Removing his armor, he climbed into the custom bedding he'd had a tailoring friend make for him (one of the traits he had carried over into undeath was his appreciation for excellent beddings), and fell into a state of sleep. Tomorrow, he would bring the information on his tablet to both Garrosh and Sylvanas. They weren't expecting him until then in any case.
