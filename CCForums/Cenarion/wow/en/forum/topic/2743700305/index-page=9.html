Westfall


Galaire mounted on her nightsaber, Starlight, and headed for the border of Elwynn Forest. During the past week after the fight at the Recluse, Galaire had sought adventure in Westfall and she found it. She helped clear out one of the mines of the kobold miners that always seem to get into those mines, despite the years of adventurers wiping out whole generations of them ((there can't be an endless supply of those kobolds, blizzard :P)).
Galaire was planning to return to the Recluse again, to see if it was repaired yet and have a good stiff drink or two. She crossed the bridge to Elwynn and headed for Goldshire and then to Stormwind. She would've taken the flight path from Sentinel Hell if not for the actions of Vanessa VanCleef, the daughter of Edwin VanCleef. Vanessa had burned down Sentinel Hill and they were slowly rebuilding it. Galaire had no trouble getting to Goldshire, where she met up with Clawfyre who was doing a favor for the local blacksmith. Clawfyre said that he would come back to the Recluse later.


As Galaire entered into Stormwind, she caught glimpse of what looked like a blood elf with night elven features...but couldn't be sure because of the crowds and quickly lost sight of the half-breed somewhere near the Auction House.
Coming into the mage district, Galaire dismounted and tied her mount to a hitching post. She walked to the Recluse, cursing the rain. She hoped that this time there wasn't going to be a fight. She saw that the tavern was full of the same people that she had met a week before. She went to the bar and ordered an ale.
