Good morning folks!

So, brief update.

Most of the character services issues are resolved.

There are still outliers on the realms that had backend work done last week - and that will probably remain the case on the few that still aren't completed with the process for just a few more days.

That can exhibit itself mainly in not being able to find your character here - or in the Armory, and of course not being able to apply a service to a character that doesn't appear in your list.

Average petition queue time at the moment is a bit less than two days for realms OTHER than those mentioned above. Petitions from the realms mentioned above still need to be held for many issues simply because they can't be addressed at the moment, do make sure to leave those open.

We certainly appreciate your patience, they are working very hard to get everything tidied up and back to completely normal :)

Note: (I just talked to a GM. I ended up making a ridiculous amount of tickets, but I figured out the essential trick to solving this. If people could do this, it would save so much customer service time...)

You just go onto the armory page with the character that is having the problem, and in the bottom left corner there's a report website error thing near the yellow blizzard logo. I did get some GMs who said that I should use the in game bug report feature, but I tried it, and it didn't work. (It's all about how good you are at asking the right questions and using the right buzzwords to avoid a generic response.)

Anyways, if it's on multiple characters, you're supposed to submit a website error report for each character or thing with the issue. That's the most clear way to fix it. It's so easy to do, but the button is VERY easy to miss!
