I've been with the Yielding Veil for a little less than a week now, and I have to admit- they stopped me from leaving WoW. I couldn't find a niche that really felt interesting, active and friendly enough to keep me from leaving the RP community, and then luckily I responded to an advert in trade chat which led me here.

Great group of people who not only play engaging and fun characters to associate with, but are just genuinely pretty rad OOC. If you're looking for a roleplay group that doesn't segregate you from the rest of the community, and keeps things active and fun constantly while catering to YOU as a character, Yielding Veil is where you need to be.
Theres a place for all character archetypes, and you should get in now while we're on the rise, because now's the real "golden age" of the guild.

I recommend 100% joining up, not as a biased member, but as a person who cares for other people's roleplay experience.

If you're interested in finding good, varied RP, then this is the place to come. I don't really care about raiding or PvPing. I play the game for RP. And to get good RP, you need to have a strong community OOCly first and foremost.

This isn't one of those RP guilds where the only time people interact is through officer sponsored events. Everyone puts forth an effort to create interesting stories, and while the officer events really are great, there is something special about how often we come together as a group.

I'd encourage anyone seeking out something new to give us a shot. We're always willing to talk, and if you see us RPing about town, feel free to join in. We try and make an effort to include everyone, whether they're in the guild or not.
