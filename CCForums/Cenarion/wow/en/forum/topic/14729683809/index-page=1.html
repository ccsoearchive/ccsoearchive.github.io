Hello there! You may have heard of us before, or seen us around, or even frowned distastefully at the onslaught of puns we have unleashed upon Azeroth. We took some time away from structured events and RP, but that is set to change, thanks in part to the wave after wave of Orc and Orcish accessories heading our way. Warlords of Draenor is almost upon us and Aegis is prepared to dive headfirst into the fray, concussions be damned.

Who Are We?

Aegis of the Lucent Dawn is an aid group that focuses on helping those who have been affected by war and conflict. We extend a helping hand to people and settlements that are in need, as well as providing protection from any further attacks. Azeroth has been at war for decades and it is unreasonable to think that peace will ever last for very long. Knowing that, we have within our ranks those that are able to defend and fight back, alongside those that can mend wounds and rebuild settlements.

What Do We Do?

As far as the guild in general, our scope is very broad. The aim is for our members to never feel as if they are obligated to participate in everything we do. We all log on and play this game for different reasons, and we're more than happy to accommodate that. There will be guild RP, both events and otherwise, raiding, old PvE content, PvP if there are enough interested parties, anything goes! We have agents standing by, ready and willing to help out and be your friend on anything you might want to do on a given day.

What's In Store For The Future?

Now that the Iron Horde is upon us, Aegis is ready to step in and provide what support they can. There will be events, both freeform and DMed, on a weekly basis, at the very least. We have plans to field at least one raid team for the expansion, as well. Given the influx of new recruits to the guild, a second group may also be in the cards. Possibilities abound!

OOC Information

If you have a sense of humor and prefer a group of laid back people that like to make fun of each other all the time, you'll find a home here. Our guild chat is OOC, so feel free to spam imgur links of kittens and debate the finer points of 18th century Japanese Literature. We're not exactly what you would call a "family guild" or "well-adjusted" or "fit for the outside world", so if some interspersed colorful language bothers you, I'd suggest elsewhere.

Interested In Joining?

Well, okay! You can reach one of four people online for a brief OOC interview. We'll just ask a few questions, get a feel for who you are and suss out if you're Captain America's bastard Dragonborn son. We do ask that interested parties be at least Level 20, simply because we've had one too many experiences of people joining for guild perks and leaving without a word. We just want to see a little effort!

If you'd like to join, ask for one of Pyrillia, Rose, Astrid or Kat in-game. One of us will usually be online, so odds are good you'll find us.
