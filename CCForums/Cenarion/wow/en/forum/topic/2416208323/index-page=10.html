Having the reward be a flat rate per win is horrificly bad for the game.

The original system (10 games per week for pts) and the updated cataclysm system (~5 WINS per week to obtain point cap relative to rating) were perfectly fine.


Now you've setup a system where a sub 1500 player needs to win 10 matches per week and a higher rated player needs to win 20 or more matches per week just to reach their point cap.


This change manages to simultaneously punish pvpers at all levels of skill and gear (while it won't effect the fully geared player now, at season 10's start it will be a huge burden) with no positives whatsoever.



The worst part of this change, beyond the fact that it makes arena far more grindy for mid to high rated players, is the fact that it will severely punish the starting pvper in poor gear who's new to arena.

This really has zero effect on me personally for this season but I see it as being extremely bad for the game moving forward.

The arena system is already quite harsh for the new player as they will now have to win 10 matches just to reach the minimum cap when many already struggle to win 5 at low mmr.



What happened to "Gated, not Grindy."? Because you just added 100% more grind to the sub 1500 arena player's pvp experience, and ~ 300% more grind to the 2300+ rated player's arena experience.


I know your company is staffed with tons of brilliant people and I honestly can't fathom why a move this stupid would be made. I've always respected the genius of Blizzard, and this is not a personal attack.

Simply put, this change, the change itself, is extremely stupid and entirely anathema to the openly stated design goal of "Gated not Grindy".




UPDATE: In reference to the blue update, still a stupid decision. It needs to scale with rating.
