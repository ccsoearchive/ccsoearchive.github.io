While it's clear that you're either trolling or completely ridiculously misinformed, I will do my best to provide a counterpoint.

Epics, as of this patch, are at an all-time ubiquity rate; you can get 353s from ZA and ZG, you can craft 359s, there is an increased-valor-from-dungeons cap, and hell, one of the DUNGEON QUESTS from the ZA dungeon actually gives you a free epic.

Minimal effort is not the name of the game when it comes to PvP: PvErs have scheduled raid-times (most pvpers do not), boatloads of tools to diagnose problems of raids (RaidFail, Recount, DBM, etc), and literally every strategy for every boss is all over the internet.

PvP, on the other hand, takes a great deal of either video-analysis, or high level of cause-effect awareness to figure out what exactly led to your defeat. Furthermore, while dealing with a myriad of other comps, you need to have comprehensive strategies for whatever you may run into. On top of this, you need to be able to change your strategy at a moment's notice, something that almost never happens in a scripted PvE encounter, to deal with the other team throwing a wrench in your plans.

Now, maybe it is the case that not every team works this way. But the good ones do. And the good ones are the most highly punished under the new system of receiving a FLAT AMOUNT of conquest points. At a 2400 arena rating, my cap is 2850. This takes !16! wins, when 2 days ago, it took 5 wins. Not only is that triple the amount, but at 65% win ratio, it means that I am looking at almost 30 games just to get my stupid conquest cap on one character! One!

That doesn't include the Death Knight that I got to 85 last week, the rogue that is hitting 85 this week, any BGs, dailies, raiding, heroic dungeons, gearing other alts, nothing but arena.

This change was clearly a demonstration of making arena more grindy, IN THE MIDDLE OF A SEASON, for no other reason than "they're advancing too fast", in a system that gives you gear that is, with respect to pve gear, on the level of 346s (generously).
