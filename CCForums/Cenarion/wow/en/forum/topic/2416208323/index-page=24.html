I only recently started to seriously PvP, and it's enjoyable. However, in the other thread in this forum you state "felt players were being over-rewarded for the time invested." Well, that's fine and dandy if you want to increase the time investment, but making it a set value is discouraging and intimidating for newer PvPers, and a long grind and chore for veterans.

If I had one suggestion that could please both sides of the argument, it would be to make it an exponential amount of wins based on rating. Not a set amount, and not a percentage, but something like
500 rating = 5 wins til cap
1000 rating = 7 wins
2000 rating = 10 wins
3000 rating = 15 wins

That way the number of wins shouldn't exceed 20 (which could help vets and people with lots of alts.) but still make it a worthy time investment more equivalent to PvE.

Just a suggestion, take as you will. I'll still PvP, and I'll still arena, albeit at a slower and more casual pace I suppose.
