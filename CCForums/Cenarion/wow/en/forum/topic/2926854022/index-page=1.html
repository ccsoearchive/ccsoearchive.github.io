Okay, so we all know that PVE has always seem more love than PVP.
The lore behind every Daily Questing Hub is about what we will see in the PVE content (Firelands and The Molten Front for example) , PVE'ers can queue for Randon heroics with players from different realms who are in their Real ID List while PVP'ers can't do the same with Randon BG'S and the list goes on....

Well, but I do not intend to make this into a QQ thread, I do both PVE and PVP myself, and I'd like to make a suggestion, make the unused zone of a Gilneas into a daily PVP questing hub. (For clarification, I don't take Tol Barad as a Decent Questing Hub, the place is not cool, really)

So, let's see the LORE reasons behind this:
People have been complaining that the Worgem haven't seen love, wich is true, their actions have absolutelly no impact on the world, and you can only see a little bit of their lore if you play Horde. So it is about time we use 'em a bit right? It is about time they go and try to re-conquest Gilneas!!!
And well, we saw all the Sylvanas thing in Silverpine and it was amazing, but it was left un-done. No one knows of Koltira nor of what have become of the New Undercity Batallion now. Again, it is about time we see more of it, we should make Gilneas into a daily questing hub for PvP'ers.

So, here you have the lore behind it: After they lost their homeland, the Worgem joined the Alliance and with the help of the 7th legion and of the Bloodfang Worgem the Gilneans tried to reclaim their homeland. They failed miserably, and Crowley lost Bloodfang support...
Now, King Genn Greymane wants to try again, and recover his homeland to honor his son's memories, and roused an army of both Worgem and unafflicted Gilneas, to recover their beloved city.
Garrosh's is growing impacient with Sylvanas, she has only failed in her attempt's to conquer Gilneas so far, and he need's a port in the Eastern Kingdons urgently. High Warlord Cromush told Garrosh that he suspect's that Sylvana's is using the plague even after he have prohibited it, and it serves only to make Garrosh's even more angrier with Sylvanas.
Now, he have sent an army of Kor'krom to make sure that Sylvana's succeeds....or she will have to face his wrath.

Alliance players can get to Gilneas in a ship they get at Stormwind, while Horde players go there in "Ettin drawn wagons" like the ones in Silverpine.

Alliance players first questing hub will be Keel Harbor, and the Horde first questing hub will be a small Kor'krom camp at the Northern Headlands

Well, as I said, you will get 10 marks per daily quest and 1 mark per player you kill in the World PVP.
You will need 100 marks so you can progress to the next phase of the questing.
You can get this amount of marks in three days or so.

Once you get 100 marks you need to talk to Darius Crowley at Keel Harbor or with High Warlord Cromush at the Kor'krom Forward Camp.

When you do so you will unlock a small questline that must be done before you can have acces to a new questing hub.

--------------------------------------------------//-------------------------------------------------------

ALLIANCE SIDE:

First you must talk with Lord Darius Crowley. When you talk with him he says:

"So...here we are...Gilneas. I fought much for this place, do you know I started a rebellion once and was taken prisioner by King Genn???"

Then the player can say:

"I've heard histories about you sir, but I think that it's not time for us to tell our tales now. I'm happy to say that we are ready to move further inland sir, it is time we re-conquer Gilneas, once and for all!!!"

Upon clicking in the option to say this the player will lose all his 100 marks and Crowley will become a quest giver.
Here's the quest he gives:

"Honey, I'm Home!

So here we are, Gilneas...home. It's nice to be back, it doesn't seems like it have been 6 month since I was here last time. Damn that !@#$% Sylvanas. I'm going to have her head for what she have done to our people, this I vow.
Well, but we aren't going to get Gilneas back if we stay here talking. Follow me <class>, we must got to Emberstone. From there we will enter Gilneas city..."

A horse appears and the player mounts it, Crowley drops to all four (He is in Worgen form) and starts running. The Horse automatically follows Crowley. He crosses the brigde near Keel Harbor and enter the Northgate Woods. From there he crosses the river and go to Emberstone. The following happens during this "trip".

Crowley Says: Don't think we are unprotected, <name>. My pack is moving with us, unseen, if the Horde dares to try to ambush us they are going to go running with their tails between their legs.

Crowley Says: This is gonna be easy, I already sent my daughter, Lorna, to Emberstone with a small army, I hope that when we arrive there everything will be ready, and then we are going to retake Gilneas for The Alliance.

Crowley: Careful now, I guess you gonna have to dismount your horse if we are to cross the river....

After you cross the River and arrives at Emberstone the following happens:

Crowley Yells: Lorna! I'm here...It's time we retake our city!!!
Crowley says: Lorna????

A mysterious worgem appear, Crowley looks aprehensive.

Mysterious Worgem says: Well well....it seems Crowley and his pack have done our thing for us...You were going to retake Gilneas you say???

Crowley yells: Who are you?!

Mysterious Worgem: Haha....who am I? It doesn't matters. I have come to finish what you have started...But look at this, you were going to retake Gilneas uh? And you gathered an army??? I'm amazed...who would say that the gutless Crowley would be able to do this...

Crowley do the Worgen /roar thing and then yells: Who are you!?

Mysterious Worgem: I said it doesn't matter who I am. What are you going to do Crowley? I have your daughter you know? Are you going to bargain with me for her life like you did with that undead ^-*!@ Crowley???

Crowley says: What??? Lorna...give her back to me....

The Mysterious Worgem says: Nah Crowley...Damn you Crowley, it is all your fault! We would have Gilneas back and Sylvanas would be long dead if it weren't for you!...

Crowley yells: IVAR! GIVE LORNA BACK TO ME!

Ivar Bloodfang says: Come get you herself then....

Ivar laughs and then disappears.

Crowley says: It seems we are going to have to do this without Lorna for now....no...Lorna....<Name> you are in command here. These mans are yours to command, your objeticve is to defend this outpost from The Horde or from Bloodfang worgens. Wait for King Genn and his men to arrive before moving in to Gilneas. I'm going to get Lorna back!.

Crowley drops to all four and start's running.


-------------------------------------//--------------------------------------------------------------------

Quest's at the Hub:

After what happens with Crowley and his daughter, the player is given command of the "area" and the Alliance use the mine of the city of Emberstone (the place that was full of Undead and had a abomination there during the Worgem leveling) as a basecamp.
There's nothing inside the mine but some humans mining. The base is guarded by Gilneans Guards and Crowley worgens, there's three major NPC's there. (these give quest's).
A Night Elf Druid
A gilnean woman.
A Worgem.
There's also a big Worgen named "Gibers, the untamed"...he doesn't do nothing but to stay there.

These are the quest's at the post. (You can still do the Keel Harbor quest's everyday)

"Dawg War
Hello <name>. So, Crowley made you the commander here in his absence uh? Look, we can't get inside of Gilneas yet, not without an army. King Genn and his man will be here soon, but we need to clear the roads for them. Bloodfang worgem have been spotted everywhere around here. Why won't you start doing your commander thing and get these mans and go kill 'em? By the way, you should also try to discover where they are hiding, if we beat one of these worgens with an inch of his life but let him live, he is surely to go home with his tail between his legs...maybe you can follow him and try to get to their den?

Bloodfang Worgens slain: x/6
Bloodfang Worgen followed to his "den": x/1"

In this quest you have three compainion- Two Worgems and a Night Elf Restoration Druid who will heal you. You have to kill 6 "elite Bloodfang Worgen". When you are fighting those the following MAY happen:
"The Worgen roars and then drops to all four and start running, he disappears on the woods. The worgen with you go after him, but after 10 seconds they come back and tell the enemy worgen disappeared after he reached the woods."- If this happen the Bloodfang Worgen followed to his "den" objective is complete.

"Taming the beast(given by the Night Elven druid):

While our scout's where exploring the fields nearby they found a worgen. The winds tell me that he is not part of the Bloodfang Pack nor Crowley's. This seems to be a wild one. We can't allow the "curse" to spread again. I need you to go there and fight this worgem, and once he is weaked you can use this (he gives you an item) to calm him down. Then a bunch of druid's will arrive and we will start the ritual to give he control again...help them!

Ritual Completed: x/1"

In this quest you just have to fight this elite "Wild Worgen" until he reaches 25% of his health. Then you can use the item you were given on him. He will fall asleep and some druids will come and start doing that ritual thing that happen in The Blackwald in the Worgem starting zone. Some Bloodfang worgens will appear, and you have to protect the druids for 1 minute!.

"Walking Corpses(PVP quest)

Commander! Our scout's report the Forsaken have taken the nearby town of Emberstone and are using it as a base camp! It's weird, I thought that Garrosh and his Kor'krom where personally overseeing the conquest of our homeland. It doesn't matter if they are Orc, Forsaken or Bloodfang Worgen, they all will die so we can have our homeland back. I need you to go to Emberstone and to kill the Dark Ranger there. Try to kill some of their most powerful soldiers (Players) too.

Horde Players Killed at Emberstone: x/6
Dark Ranger slaim: x/1"

In this quest the Alliance player need to attack the Horde Quest Hub in this area and kill their Dark Ranger, she is the Horde version of Gibers,the untamed. The place will have some guards and Horde players, the Horde quest givers at the are won't attack you unless if they are attacked. The quest givers are unkillable and will receive "Grace of The Dark Lady" making them immune to damage once they reach 10% health. You need to kill 6 Horde players there, and the Dark Ranger. If in your server Horde have more players than the Alliance (or vice versa) you will get some Worgen (or Forsaken if you are Horde) to go with you.

Each of these quests give you 10 marks. You can still do Keel Harbor Quests. You get 1 mark for each enemy player you kill. You need 230 marks so you can unlock the new quest hub.
