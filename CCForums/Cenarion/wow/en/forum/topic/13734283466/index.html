Something like that) when we had the extremely extended maintenance due to some tech issues or power outages or something, the guild I am in on Thrall glitched out when the server came back up. Go try to get into my guild on the armory. The page is 404. It displays as a level 0 Neutral guild. Things have arisen, and I wish to leave. I've been wishing to leave for well over a week now... to find a guild for WoD content, and some current content.. yet, I can't. I can't try to get things done before 6.0.. I'm missing out. I understand the whole "Needing to re-code the guild" I've gotten.. but honestly, I'd like to be able to enjoy the game. I'm a returning player from years ago. I haven't had this full year to experience SoO, let alone the rest of the content, and I'd like to. With a guild. And actually enjoy the game.

Let's be honest, being FORCED to play without a guild really sucks. It makes me just want to quit again. I really don't want to renew my sub at this point. Never have I seen an issue I've had given such a long time frame.. especially one of this degree, with such an impact on my game play..

Sincerely,
