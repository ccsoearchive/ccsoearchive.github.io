Two generic Alterac footman armor sets.

The first set was inspired by Hendrek (although it's not exactly the set he wore/wears), built off the general Human Footman mold using mostly recolors of Imperial Plate gear, minus the actual Imperial Plate Belt & the Valorous Helm. While the chestplate has a glaring blood red bottom portion, this is generally covered up by a Tabard.

The second set was brought by Kaedri & was generally inspired by the more standard Kingdom RP uniforms that favored using Overlord's chest & legs who wanted something less revealing, along with getting rid of the Templar Boots & Gauntlets originally used by myself in the first set.
