*Delvers Society is seeking hired help*

The combat trained, intellectually inclined, or otherwise talented are being sought out. Work involves locating, obtaining, and studying of artifacts. Potential hazards include threats from the environment, unpredictable encounters, and possibly volatile discoveries. Take this into consideration before replying. Room and board is included with payment but expect travel often. If interested reply promptly.

-Nafani, Director of Delvers Society


This strange letter has found it's way to many mailboxes across Azeroth, leaving many with the sense of confusion, mystery, and curiosity. Those who find such postage may ask themselves, "Delvers Society? I know nothing of them. And who tries to recruit by mail these days? Rocket-powered goblin instant messages are the more sensible way to go." These are the adventurers that cast the spam postage aside, cutting off one path and taking another. But for some few, they dare to ask, "Ancient mysteries? Coin? The chance to possibly be torn limb from limb? I must know what this 'Delvers Society' is!" And so here, today, the Delvers Society presents the lovely forum community with all the answers they could ever want! Who are the Delvers Society? And how do they keep finding my mailing address?

The Theme:

The Delvers Society is a rag-tag band of adventurers, brought together by a common goal: discovery! While the Reliquary of Silvermoon prefers to keep their hands clean, the Delvers much rather dare the dangers of the unexplored. Ancient relics, treasures, dangerous artifacts, all are on the must-have list of the society. Being non-profit, all is kept preserved and for display, as well as out of more malicious hands. We wouldn't call the Delvers a bunch of unsung heroes, but they do their part as everyone in Azeroth does. With a team of intellectuals, fighters, and even a skilled cook or janitor here and there, the society moves forward and into the great unknown. If you enjoy the idea of delving into tombs, exploring dark, ominous ruins, and possibly being crushed under a ton of rubble--the Delvers welcome you with open arms!

The Out-Of-Character Aspect

As a guild, Delvers Society is a smaller group, preferring to keep it tightly-knit and friendly. Members are not just another number on the roster here, but valued individuals, each having the capability to became well-known and loved by their fellow roleplayers. Getting lost in mass numbers and becoming a blur in the background is just what the Delvers intend to avoid. Both out-of-character and in-character, we provide a setting in which to make new friends and truly get involved in the story-telling.

We seek to bring a fresh idea into the rp guild scene, offering an option for roleplayers to seek scenarios that are less focused on military and combat aspects--yet still keep it exciting. Our goal is to make a truly interesting experience, putting a bit of mysticism and the wonder of the unknown into each storyline. We're well-established, on steady ground, and are open to new faces joining in on the adventure.

Questions and Answers- Most frequently asked about the guild.

Q: How active are the Delvers?
A: We have two events per week, usually. Starting around 6:40 pm server on Wednesdays and Saturdays. We're pretty active, with about 8-12 people showing at each event.

Q: What are events like?
A: Our Wednesday events range from just social events, to small story bits, to some OOC bonding time. Our Saturday events tend to build upon a current story arc we have going on. Fighting may or may not be involved.

Q: Is there any random rp?
A: Of course! The Delvers even have their own IC chat channel in order to establish easy communication. Rp is easily accessible and encouraged.

Q: Do you accept all races and classes?
A: We'd be silly if we didn't. We take in any Horde race as well as neutral ones.

Q: How does recruiting work?
A: First, we require your character be level 15 or above. We might ask a few questions about your character before an interview, then the rest is all IC. It deals with your character being recruited and brought into the Delvers Society.

If you wish to contact us, you can send some mail to the GM (IC or otherwise) or simply give a whisper to a member if any are online. They'll direct you to an officer. The Delvers believe in having a good bit of fun, so no need to fill out some unnecessary paperwork just to join. A small character interview will see you approved and ready to delve.

If you are still unsatisfied with what you have read so far, you can learn more intimate details about the guild on our info page. Everything from leaders to ranks to a complete history of story events so far can be found there.

Guild info page: http://delverssociety.tumblr.com/

And be careful, it's dangerous out there.
