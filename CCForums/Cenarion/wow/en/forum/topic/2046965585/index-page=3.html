You also have to realize that the front end damage of landing an envenom is only part of the equation. A reason why expertise is less important as Assassination is because even if your envenom is dodged, you still receive the envenom buff increasing poison application rate. So even if it's dodged, it's still a dps increase however slight it may be. Plus there aren't really any negative side effects to it, since energy is also returned and if SnD is about to fall off you can just do it again (or if SnD and Rupture is about to fall off, well then you'd obviously refresh Rupture first). Really all you're losing is the front end damage and a global and most of your damage is passive anyway so it's not a huge deal.
