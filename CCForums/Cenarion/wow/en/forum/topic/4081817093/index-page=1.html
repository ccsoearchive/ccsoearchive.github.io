That mad troll is back and at it again!

What?
Two weekends of RP events to celebrate our roleplay community and our marvelous guilds! All are welcome! Let's show our Cenarion Circle pride.

When?
11th-12th March (Alliance)
17th-18th Match (Horde)
Please note, these dates are tentative! Because of certain oversights up at the AAMS office (<cough> <innocent look>) these may be moved back if not enough event slots are filled.

Where?
At the Argent Tournament Grounds in Icecrown!

Who?
For anyone on the server, be they roleplayer, PvPer, PvEer, or PvAnything-Else-er! All we ask is that you respect the rights of the RPers to be RPing (and for everyone else to be doing... whatever it is they're doing, too!)

Why?
It's that time of year again!

Come along, join in one (or more! or all!) of many exciting events and sideshows including duel tournaments, jousting, fortune telling, raffles, storytelling, and much, much more. Meet new people, maybe find a new guild - or even discover a whole new side to the game to explore!

How?
If you need transport, just join the allianceooc/hordeooc channel on the day and request a summon!
Want to run an event? Just let us know (see below) with the date and time you'd like to do it, and details of the event. Please let us know if this is a guild-sponsored event so we can give the appropriate credit.
Want to turn up to represent your guild and/or recruit? Just come along and turn up.
Want to advertise your wares and run an IC stall or shop? Just turn up on the day and find space around the vendor tents.

Contacts:
Yotingo and Borbitox on Horde-side
Alelsa, Voldarr, Dustwing and Lahkin on Alliance-side
Or just post here!
