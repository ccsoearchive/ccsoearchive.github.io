Worgen of the Night is a Knighthood of Gilneas and Lordaeron. We're seeking recruits to join our ranks assisting in defense for the Alliance. A slow growing group searching for all classes of expertise. If interested leave a message below.

(( OOC Information ))
Worgen of the Night is a level 8 RP/PvP guild. As stated we are a very small guild looking for members of any kind to join us. We are very friendly and helpful to each other if you're looking for a small lay back guild. We take part in community RP/PvP and RP events. Also looking for a dungeon team and arenas partner.

Our RP base is Lordneas Keep, once Greymane Manor, in Gilneas, which we use to stage raids on Undercity and other Horde targets. Send a whisper or in game mail to myself or Aorune if you would like to join! :) Also feel free to sign up at our Guild Portal site! Have a great day and thanks for reading!
