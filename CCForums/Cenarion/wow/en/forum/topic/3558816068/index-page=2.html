The dungeon itself is fine to me, and I've made a good effort to learn most of the dungeon in my time playing so I can better direct people who might not know the instance well through it. The Mole Machine does help quite a bit of strain out of it, should a City run not want to trek the whole place, but unfortunately, most don't seem to realize it's there for more than teleporting to a spot after a wipe.
