i also play combat btw i play all the specs kind of on and off, the best tip i can give you is make sure you always have a read buff (bandits guile) for full damage increase when you use your killing spree, all your burst is going to come from that basicly, adrenaline rush is good to use to build up that buff since you build the buff through sinister strikes, every 4 sinister strike you build a bandits guile buff that increases your damage, in order from green to yellow to red, red being when you want to killing spree with trinkets/ any other procs you have

your damage besides that is minimal

make sure your keeping revealing strike on your target, along with slice and dice up especially when your using shadow blades because its damage is based off auto attack damage and slice and dice increases your auto attack speed, i like to use shadow blades with killing spree although thats debatable by many that thats the best thing to do, but it maximizes your damage in killing spree and thats what really matters.
