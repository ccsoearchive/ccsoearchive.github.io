((IC))

The holidays are exhausting on everyone! Winter Veil lasts not one, but multiple weeks! Before that it was a whole other round of holidays that everyone had to prepare for. Not to mention all of the other tiring on-goings of life, with the continued training sessions and help and preparations sent out to the Alliance in Draenor. A new year approaches Azeroth.... And The Order of Goldrinn is ready for a break! Just a small one, they aren't total slackers.

Leaving tonight on a ship, they are on their way fora mini-vacation in Feralas to celebrate the New Year! The Hot Springs will be the main destination as they try not to get swindled by Goblins, and relax up. Who knows what the New Year will be bringing, and what might lie ahead.

OOC

Arriving on January 2nd, The Order will be in Feralas for a few days! People are always still recovering from real life holiday stress, family visits, and the like even as we go into the beginning of January. Some fun, but kind of different, roleplay is just what the doctor ordered! If you happen to have a character that hangs around in Feralas, feel free to come out and roleplay with us!
