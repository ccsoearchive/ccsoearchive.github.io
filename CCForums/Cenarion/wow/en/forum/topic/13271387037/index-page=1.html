The history of this Gilnean Order is a long and rich one, reaching back into the days before Genn was a twinkle in King Archibald's eyes. Originally known as The Order of Gilneas, it was a strong, underground organization in those days. The original Order of Gilneas comprised of three once notable noble houses that allied together through political bargaining, to share in strengths and wipe out any weakness among them.

Decades later, the enigmatic kingdom would fall, and with it so would The Order of Gilneas. That is, until a few years ago. The elusive Order would rise again, being lifted out of the ashes of Gilneas to be reformed as The Order of Goldrinn. Seeking to prove themselves as worthy of their past noble history, the houses would come together once more. All members of this Order pledge to one of these noble houses, rallying their strength and once more eradicate weakness from their ranks.

House Ashmore: A house that, for decades, has forged soldiers and Knights under the Gilneas banner. Soldiers of Ashmore are renown in for their unwavering courage in the face of overwhelming odds these soldiers train hard in the art of combat so that they may dare their opponents to attack. Following the three pillars of trust, honor and respect, House Ashmore brought the refined soldiers of Gilneas to the Order and continues to be faithful to this tradition of honor and legacy of valor.

House Dosett: A house that was relatively unknown until the Greymane wall was erected. Now cut off from the training of Dalaran, House Dosett became one of the many sought after houses with its knowledge of magic. Members are known for their skills in the Arcane, Fel, Shadow, and even Holy magic. Cutting edge research has always come out of this house, pushing their magic and skill to the limits. Their blazing brilliance shows that there is far more to magic than meets the eye.

House Lachance: A house shrouded in mystery. House Lachance came to power during the political turmoil of the Second War, seemingly overnight. Stereotyped by their members incredible craftsmen, guile, sharp tongues and rumored assassins; this house brought cunning and wit to the Order. Politics runs deep in Lachance blood, and so it is they who meet with friends and foes alike. They are the velvet glove slipped over the iron fist.

No longer a secretive society, it is likely that one has seen the postings on the boards, or heard the whispers on the streets. The Order continues to seek out Gilneans and sympathizers to the cause, bringing them together in a community. The culture of Gilneans cannot afford to be lost, and it is the Order of Goldrinn that has been a steadfast protector of those ways. From battlefields of Campaigns, waging war against those who would do them harm, to the moments at home that bring everyone together. The Gilneas banner still waves proudly. Hope is not lost here. The fight for King and Country is just beginning.

OOC Information:

The Order of Goldrinn is a two year old Gilnean active, heavy roleplay guild. The central focus is not about the Worgen curse, but the Gilnean people, their culture, and cause. As such, any human with a valid reason to pledge themselves to a Gilnean organization is more than welcome to apply.

Guild website:http://orderofgoldrinn.shivtr.com/

Visit our website for more lore, structure and contact information. Our officers are always listed in the upmost right hand box, we are always willing to talk to you or answer questions in game! If you would like to roleplay or chat with us, we’re just a whisper away.

Requirements:

-Your character must be either Human or Worgen
-You must be level 20. If your character is a Death Knight they must be level 65.
-You must submit an application on the guild website if this is your first character ever joining the guild. There are no exceptions to this rule.
