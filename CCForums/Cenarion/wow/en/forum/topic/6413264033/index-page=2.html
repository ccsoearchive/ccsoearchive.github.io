This is interesting indeed.

Most people on CC know this already. Araceli has our events posted, but I'll re-post them here.

server time for us is pacific.

Sunday: Pia Presidium has our knighting ceremony at 2:00 server time. Following the ceremony we go to Argent Tourney to try by arms and take time out to just visit. We only administer vows to members, but the events are open for anyone to join us.

Sunday: The Darnassian Forums. The warrior's terrace in Darnassus is the place and Kyalin of Azure Crusade is the host. This is a forum with a wide variety of speakers and topics. 7:00.

(We actually begin at 7:00pm. Full details here: http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/5621022166?page=2 per Kyalin)

Monday: Clinic hosted by Rhudran and Pia at 5:00 in the Military Ward of Ironforge. If you have a boo boo stop by for some fun rp.

On alternate Mondays Pia hosts the Troubadour Night which is in IF Explorer's Hall at 6:00 server. We encourage everyone to attend and entertain or just be entertained. Songs, jokes, stories as long as they are pg-13. It's a family friendly event.

We switch between the clinic and TN for a bit of variety.

Tuesday is the Feathers Of Iron tavern in Booty Bay where Miss Cup, the battle chef, prepares some awesome food for their guests. Tried her fried chicken, it's the best. Cup is joined in hosting by Miss Kixek also of Feathers Of Iron and Miss marikuu who tends bar.

On horde side, Da Doctas hosts their clinic in Ogrimmar, near the portals I believe. 7:00. This is, to my knowledge, the oldest consecutive running rp event on the server.

(Tuesday- Clinic at 7 server, near the shaman portals in Org. This doesn't help our cross server buddies, but we're probably moving it to another location in MoP, so I'll let you know about that one :) per raoul)

Wednesday is The Light and You at 6:00 I believe. It's behind the cathedral in the gazebo area. This event is hosted by Genevra of Conclave It's an inspirational sermon and well attended.

Wednesday - (Horde) Every other week, the Modas il Toralar hosts the Black Hand Society, a gathering that largely consists of... well, we'll call them slightly amoral. Most are socialite types, but not all :P 6:30 (Per Raoul)

Thursday is campfire storytelling night hosted by Pia. We switch the places around, but it's usually in a starter area so we can encourage youngsters to rp. We also give away gear, pets and bags to the wee folk while sharing tales of adventure and lore. We always welcome new storytellers, escpecially if you enjoy discussing lore. This is at 6:00. Look for the campfire, which invariably gets placed to set someone right on fire.

Thursday - horde/alliance The current day for fishing, at 7 server, though the location is dynamic.

We'll be moving it to Sunday soon... maybe even this week, actually, though, we'll still have it today. We don't have an actual date picked due to the schedule mishap, heh, but if you check back around six server I'll put something up. Unless our cross-server friends that might be interested have any suggestions? per raoul)

Beginning the first Saturday of September, Pia will host a role play class for new role players or people who would just like to sit in or even help teach. This will be for the first two Saturdays of each month We would also love to have people help teach lore. The more the merrier.

Saturday - A lounge night just to relax at Hardwrench Hideaway in STV, also happens at 7. (Noticing a pattern? We like to keep it consistent, heh.)

Every second Saturday of the month, it goes on hiatus for a lore night instead. Basically, we pick a subject, a location relevant to that subject, and just have an open discussion about it. Folks with prepared speeches or stories, debate or conversation, ect. Next one is September 15th, in Nagrand, with the subject being the First War.

Note that despite certain natures of the event (Ie clinic being used for the healing of others, and fishing being primarily... well, an rp excuse to level fishing,) you don't /have/ to come to those events just for those purposes. They all serve the function of social gatherings as well, and are nice enough to just chat up folks nearby.

As a note for fishing, its not Horde specific - we don't have any official translators, but feel free to show up even if you're Alliance :) per Raoul

I made the changes so there wasn't conflicting information. Horde information is also in Raoul's post.

I think that's everything. It's late so I may have missed something.

We'd love for people to attend anything that's happening on CC. Let's get to know each other. I'm not sure what areas are restricted to whom yet, so we'll just see how this works out.
