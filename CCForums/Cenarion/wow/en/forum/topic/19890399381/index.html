Every garrison, town, and outpost in Durotar, Mulgore, and the Barrens has a newly posted sign sticking from the ground.

The Southfury Watch needs new blood to fill our ranks.
Those that hear the call to defend our homeland should seek out members of the Watch.

The terse message scrawled in Orcish is followed by a symbol of two axes crossed behind the symbol of the Horde.

The Southfury Watch

The Southfury Watch was once known as the Hammer of Kalimdor, a militia/warband dedicated to defending the Horde settlements within the dry terrain of Kalimdor. The warband defended the Crossroads and surrounding area for a decade (7 years in real time) while the major events of the world took place. The Hammer of Kalimdor found its largest threat in itself when the Darkspear Rebellion took place. The militia was split between Vol'jin and Hellscream supporters. This divide came to a head during the Siege of Orgrimmar the day the gates were brought down. Most of the warriors who once fought under the same banner perished in a bloody melee in the waters of the Southfury River. The fighting was brought to an end by Valkosk Blacktongue when he saw the deaths that had been brought by the insurrection. As Fertt Spinehewer, the leader of the Hellscream loyalists, stepped down, Valkosk Blacktongue rose to the mantle of Commander with Ruakari, the leader of the Vol'jin loyalists, as his Second. He renamed the Hammer to the Southfury Watch to honor the fallen and as a reminder of what the division amongst themselves wrought. The Southfury Watch now looks for new blood in its ranks so that they may better protect the new domains of the Orcs, Tauren, and Darkspear.

Contact and Info

We are a heavy RP guild that is centered around defending Horde settlements (mainly the Crossroads) from attack. We are looking for Orcs, Trolls, and Tauren that want to protect their homeland, and from casual players to daily players who like to RP and maybe throw themselves at the Alliance when they attack our precious Crossroads. We will be looking to do some events soon, but we need some bodies in boots before that. Contact Valkosk, Ruakari, Fertt, Aekra, or just any member you see on.
Also looking for some other guilds for contact and events. Need protection for your caravan through the Barrens? We are here to help!
