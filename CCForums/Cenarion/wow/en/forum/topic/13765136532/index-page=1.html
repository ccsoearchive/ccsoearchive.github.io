* A new piece of parchment is nailed over an old rotted one near the Inn of every Forsaken town and haunt. The words on it are written in Orcish with the text starting with a large letter... "Brethren in death, fellow Horde, the gathering of Forsaken souls is taking place once again. Forsaken all over the realm notice the increase in activity, the enriching of our culture, and the interconnectedness of our society. For this last gathering we will continue the discussion about Sylvanas but this time in regards to East Plaguelands. In Silverpine all who shared help to prove the events there are not a sign of a darker path, for the next one I will present the events as something to worry about in this regard.

On the evening of December 30th (Wednesday) at 7:30pm (server) join us near the broken statue in the ruins of Lordaeron. We will discuss what affect the power of the Val'kyr have over our Dark Lady, we will debate what path Sylvanas is or is not currently walking down."

* at the bottom of the page there is some text written in words common among Forsaken lands..." We will also be leaving the Ruins of Lordaeron for this one to visit the sights of our subject. Also, as members of the undead we should try not to be overly emotional during this event. We will absolutely not tolerate any non Forsaken who claims our Dark Lady to be a Lichqueen. Make note of them so we may teach them more respect in our special way, after the gathering is over."

OOC
((The gathering is an open event that focuses on Forsaken issues, the night's topic will be on certain aspects of the Forsaken community or current events in the Forsaken realm. As always, all who gather with us may share their own experiences or perspective as well. Perhaps you know of happenings that we failed to observe ourselves, in this way we will all have a better picture of our society. After the reporting, we will then debate or discuss the night's topic. It's the audience that we do this for so if you have suggestions for topics then we would love to hear them.

Heed the herald, be respectful to the other Horde races who might be there, and who ever has the floor will be allowed to speak their piece to completion. Of coarse questions are allowed and those sharing may choose to answer or not. Pst or mail myself for calendar invites.))

Date and time: Dec 30th at 7:30pm server
Location: Statue in the ruins of Lordaeron
Night's topic: debate what path our Lady was or wasn't on. (Last half)

Nearing the night of the Forsaken gathering in the ruins of Lordaeron, thin parchment pages can be seen nailed on pillars along broken streets and through out ruined stalls of once great Lordaeron. The text is short, starting with a different enlarged letter...

" A few of my brethren will not know how to speak Orcish, still most of the gathering will be easily understood by all in the Horde. If however you feel the need to know more, then one can pay for a translator or simply ask for aid. Both would require some trust of what is said between the two souls, a small act such as that over many places would wield a starting point. A point from which the Horde can better understand us, so that they can trust us." The last line is written in letters seen in the Undercity. "We ask that any Forsaken who will be attending and has the ability to translate at the event, be willing to aid our Horde counterparts if they require. This is a simple request and is not a requirement to attend."
