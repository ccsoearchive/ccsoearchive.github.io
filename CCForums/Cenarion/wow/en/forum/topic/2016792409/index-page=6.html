Please use the search feature that is within the site. This should hopefully help you find already existing threads on a topic of interest. By searching for a topic and adding to it instead of creating your own, you can help keep information organized for us, and keep the front page from scrolling so quickly with repeat topics for everyone else.

**Warning, this tread is my 1st and last so it will be long :P **

Im not the kind of person that make post on forums because english aint my first language and I don't wanna be flame for spellings but I decided to make one today because something is slowly getting me bored of the game.

For the last weeks, lots of tread of complains, constructive opinions etc as been made and also a lot as been deleted. You don't have to be dumb to notice that people like this game and Im sure that most of their frustration doesn't comes from the game itself but the attitude it brought. Let me explain myself..

Since the released of Cata, I played my holy priest and my warlock. I have experiment the dps and the healing side wich is not really pretty at the moment. Don't think Im gonna complain on how hard dungeons are because Im not. Actually I think Cata in general is really well made and will obviously won't please all players. But sadly, I have noticed that for the 5 years I been playing, people are getting more and more disrespectful to each other. Too many ''learn to play dumb ass'', ''u fail'', ''ur guild sucks so do u'' etc .. And also the abusive reasons that comes in dungeons to kick people ''he is a douche bag'', ''fail'', ''don't like him''. Thats crazy how people are not patient and disrespectfull.

I made a ticket once to see how Blizzard was dealing with this kind of attitude ingame when I got kicked from a group from saying to the tank ''dude plz. don't need to be frustrated at people.. lets play relax''. I got an ingame mail telling me that they will investigate and can't keep me in touch about it. My reaction was... WELL.. won't do a sweet thing sadly. The reason I came to that conclusion is because respect seems to be a value that Blizzard got aside.
I never saw a GM interact in trade chat telling people to respect the code of conduct of WoW?! Maybe this kinda move should start and make people realize that only few likes to be getting into a fight or get insulted. I play this game to have a good time and to relax from the real world, not to get brought to it in a hard way.

I understand that we come from all around the planet and have all kinda values and way of thinking but we all need to start to respect each others.. if we don't .. it means we are a just a bunch of animals fighting for the same snack.

To end this in a polite way, Im asking to Blizzard if they can have more visibility from ingame GMs on chats and apply the WoW code of conduct with the same strictness you do on forums theses days.

Sometimes, solutions comes from a simple action.

My respect to all the community :P
