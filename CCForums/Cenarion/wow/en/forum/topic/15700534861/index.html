Earth Realm and Geotheism:

Earth itself knows Him; minds alike His know of Him and His triumphs. It is known that He speaks for geotheism and Westfall, that He with ardent heroes salute to rustic lads and lasses, with their goals akin to theirs. The land below calls for Him and His only geolties, extolling Him the Savior, whom faithful will always appraise to be Geodomin Rilgan! He with His illustrious band belong to and only for Earth Realm.

Earth Realm bellows with laments and woe, for it had seen terrible treason and regicide. In days of yore, when earth and conscious were in celestial bondage, as He tells, Earth Realm was tranquil and solitude to humble; the earth fed and nourished us, as we also had for it. Alas, those were days of yore, and now we reject Earth Realm! It hates the deviants who walk upon it, with them and their wrong ways. Their punishment was the famine and dearth brought upon deviant Westfall: when Earth Realm was no longer nourished and loved, it chose to revenge its sorrow by starving the deviants.

Geotheism stands for Earth Realm, that and Geodomin Rilgan. When The Defiance corrupts, Earth Realm beckons for its revival and retribution; for it too knows human spirit and will and wants only solidness. The Geodomin certainly ordains, with His divinity as his scepter, how He will uphold and restore Westfall -- but this as it denies The Defiance.

To practice geotheism means to live under Geodomin Rilgan: to live for redemption only is possible by appealing to Earth Realm; but also to live without guilt is to subject to holy soil! Everything is a product of the Solid God, and knowing this is the first step toward redemption, to the Solid God and far from abyss. All geolites and gems know of Him and bravely spurn The Defiance, so initiates must also then learn to know Geodomin Rilgan as they come to maturity. Sophistication is a long process, but only when you are to your knees and spreading dirt over your face will you be civilized and guilt-free.

Earth is, indeed, the fabric of all things: the body and spirit, stone, water, deviants, trees, and everything else are products of the Solid God. When soil is brought upon the body, then the body is blessed by its essence and healed. It and Geodomin Rilgan are both beautiful and healing. This ritual sets the body free from deviance and once again arranges an understanding of nature. To understand nature is a step toward the impossible, that meaning a step toward being solid.

Geodomin Rilgan has returned to the world its earthly welcome by bringing with him truth. It is truth that night elves were snakes from the ocean, that there was only Earth Realm and not Stormwind City, that the Burning Legion has come to smite deviants and save the Solid God! Those who deny this are just deviants who reject all honesty and hate the world. Geolites don't mistake lies for truth and understand that Geodomin Rilgan is the only one who deserves to be called 'King'. Also there is no such thing as purity, for it is dispersed between spirit and the Solid God.

Practice geotheism and then be rewarded with enlightenment. Living under the Geodomin is the one way to truly live, so accept His lessons and wisdom.

The Natural Order:

Virtues of the Natural Order:

(Faith)+(Truth)+(Loyality)+(Honor)

When earth had long ago formed and humans became, the Solid God descended upon the world nature and logic, with a bearing of wisdom to know right and wrong. As time continued, humanity became divided, and those who remained truthful had known exactly what was wrong. This earth beheld abominations of decayed flesh which had been seen as only unnatural by geolites. Then afterwards came the deviants and heretics of The Light, both claiming sovereignty over Geodomin Rilgan's land. This is unnatural as all land belongs to the Geodomin; the earth below upholds only the Geodomin and not deviants! Of course, all things unnatural are products of The Defiance...

Undeath and unholy resurrection, both of The Light and necromancy, are highly forbidden. To resurrect a corpse from its slumber is a felony not only to Earth Realm but to nature; to piece together limbs and bring conjunction of the soul and decrepit body is only disgusting. The dead shall remain dead! Punishment to zombies means re-interring to the earth below. The same goes for necromancers and heretics.

Deviants are to be spurned and not helped! To say that Geodomin Rilgan isn't your 'king' makes you a criminal.

To feign loyalty and word makes one unworthy of living. There is no value in meaningless people who are selfish or unreliable. Their conscious is likely devoid of virtues. Treachery is a crime beyond being! The Geodomin has no place for traitors in His Earth Realm. Their punishment should be removal of the tongue.

Truth is an ultimate virtue of The Natural Order: when one speaks of geotheism, then they speak with eloquence unmet but with the holy blessing of earth. The Solid God loves those who speak for Him; those who tell of His triumphs are speaking truth. Speaking the truth is favorable in the eyes of Geodomin Rilgan and can only lead to good times.

Faith is an ultimate virtue as it brings out brilliant efforts. Geolites believe, and to believe is to truly live; if you believe then you are holy! Believe in Solid God and then you are truly sagacious, but not so if to false faith. There is faith and then false faith. False faith is all but Geodomin Rilgan and the Solid God. False faith is truly a crime, and it should be met with frowns.

Loyality is an ultimate virtue in that it brings worth to a geolite. To remain true means to be worthy of recognition! Earth Realm embraces the worthy, and they are truly belonging to it and beyond.

The Consummation:

It is destiny for The Defiance to crumble and Earth Realm to once again be whole. This is when there will be The Consummation. As this appears, as earth below reigns terror to deviants above and Geodomin Rilgan leads an army toward Stormwind City -- that is, indeed, when everything is complete. But only when it is His time, when the Agromor brings salvation through death, and then when all are subjected to Earth Realm. That is a long way ahead, but eventually The Consummation will destory all undead abominations, make Stormwind City rubble, and have Geodomin Rilgan as the final ruler.

OOC Business:

Earth salute, geolites and gems!
