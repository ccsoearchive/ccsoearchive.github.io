I would certainly hope that we are a friendly and accepting guild. I won't say any place is perfect, but we try very hard to involve as many people in our ramblings as possible. If you want to see what we're like, you are more than welcome to drop by!

I agree with what both Lianshi & Lia said.

I was also apprehensive - the Jadepaws are my first guild experience, ever, and I didn't want to get sucked into the OOC drama horror stories I had heard about with other guilds.

My first time at the Hozen was overwhelming. So many different things going on at the same time IC that it took a while to figure out who was who & what the IC realtionships were. Definitely not cliques - just such a rich variety of RP that I didn't know where to start.

One of the things I like is that there are so many members that are willing to interact with you and actively participate in whatever your own character's story is.

If taverns aren't your thing, or Saturdays don't work, the Sunday sparring events also have a strong social element to them. Quite few of us are also open to walk-ups and to brief conversations in passing.
