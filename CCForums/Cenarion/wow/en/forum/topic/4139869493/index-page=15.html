I'm not sure, the optimal use of Shadow Blades has never been well scrutinized. That said, I think it is fair to use it on CD, since getting an extra use will make up any dps difference. Just, not during Vanish/Subterfuge, since a full-price Ambush is too expensive.

Since you won't have much uptime on any one mob, consider using CDs even if you won't get the full benefit of FW. This is especially true if you have AoC.
