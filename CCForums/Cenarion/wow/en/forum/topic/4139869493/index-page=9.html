12/26/2012 07:05 PMPosted by Whiteytighty
what makes you believe it won't be as effective for a single target encounter?

For one, I think in one minute Ancticipation saves a lot more than 5 CP. Consider the amount lost when you are in ShD and generating two with Ambush/prem. Of course you could use finishers at 4 CP, but that's also sub-optimal. Then you may also run into the issue of having 4-5 CP at the end of dance and using another Ambush to max FW uptime means capping badly. Since ShD is on a one min CD, this will happen as often as MfD is up. Not saying there aren't ways of getting around some of these issues -- in Cata I timed my ShD rotation so I would be able to have my last two abilities be Ambushes.

Second, consider that we will also almost always be losing CP to Shadow Blades, although that is on three times the CD as MfD.

Third, it is possible to optimize Anticipation in the way you suggest, saving CP for cooldowns and periods where you have lots of buffs on you.

Again this is in my opinion and I haven't tested it at all.
