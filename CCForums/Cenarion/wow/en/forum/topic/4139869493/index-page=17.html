Note sure what you mean by "both sites" but you should be exclusively using ShadowCraft for gearing. The math is way too complex for a human to do when determining stat weights, which is why our top theorycrafters made ShC. Any explanation for why one stat is better falls short because there are so many ways that a stat will increase DPS and the value of other stats that it is nearly impossible to know "why X>Y". In terms of how viable something is, use ShadowCraft as a guideline. It isn't perfect, but its the best tool we have. If you personally get a dps/pleasure boost out of a different setup, go for it. That said, keep in mind that it's easy to think you get a dps increase from a change but really not. Similarly, what might increase one person's DPS may not for another.

Lastly, I know Subtlety always wants a dagger in the main hand, but does having a slow weapon in the off-hand make any difference? That is, is weapon preference just related to item level (in other words, if I found a slower agility one-hander that item would be a preferable upgrade to my current off-hand dagger)?

http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/4139869493?page=1#4
(Again use ShadowCraft to guide your decision here)

If you looked in my guide for your answers and couldn't find them, or the parts of my guide (that I linked) are confusing, please tell me so I can improve the clarity of my guide.
