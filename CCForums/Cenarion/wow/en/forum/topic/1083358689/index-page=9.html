While I appreciate the amount of thought that went into the OP's post, I have the same objections I've always had to world PvP. It never goes the way people idealize that it does. People get this notion that world PvP is true PvP and envision it as some grand battle of Tolkien proportions. That's never how it goes though, and I've PvP'ed in several MMOs now.

World PvP inevitably goes like this; guy on Team A gets ganked by 2 guys on Team B. Team A guy gets 3 friends to come help, they stomp the 2 from Team B. Team B gets 5 more to come, repeat cycle until one side gets bored. The point is, world PvP is always about winning by making the fight an unbalanced as possible. It's about hiding a lack of skill in sheer numbers. It's not epic or compelling, it's just lame. Good PvP is about balance and winning based on skill. World PvP is the exact opposite.
