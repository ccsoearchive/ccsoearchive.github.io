I just saw this thread, and I approve. I'll repose what I just posted in a thread I started about some things to reinvigorate world pvp.

Yes it's true. This can solve multiple complaints at once. Firstly this would help motivate people to go back out into the world and interact (violently) with the environment. A bit less of the eternal sitting in SW/Org would always be pleasing. Secondly this would help alleviate the complaint of some people on pvp servers that the wait in cities playstyle is effectively killing any difference between server types. Third, this could be used as an additional source of rewards to help ease the complaints about the unobtainability of conquest points (via successful quest turn in).
Say, howabout paired daily quests for each faction. One daily quest for each side to kill a named NPC at a faction settlement somewhere (an NPC that would of course be scaled to max level). Also a second daily quest that orders players to rack up HKs against the players who are trying to kill said NPC. One pair of assassinate and defend quests each day for each faction. Also a weekly quest to assassinate one of the faction leaders with a counter-quest for HKs in city defense.
To help prevent potential problems, there would be some means like NPC shouts or whatever to warn players that the particular settlement or city is a current target for enemy raids.
Also, the incentivising of defense could potentially result in players taking up the defense role more fully, allowing for the scaling back of hugenormous NPC guard spawns.
Most importantly, this would be FUN, and FUN needs buffed.

Players lost interest because there's no incentive to kill the opposing faction, incentive drives at least 80% of the WoW population, and without it, an activity never survives to long. If you took out all the honor gained from doing battlegrounds, and doubled the amount of honor gained from participating in open world PVP, what do you think would happen? Obviously players would start doing World PvP for their honor, instead of battlegrounds, but their would still be some players participating in battlegrounds because they enjoy them. Do you see where I'm getting at here? Their are players such as myself who participate in World PvP because we enjoy it, even if there is no reward, yet the other chunk of the community prefers doing activities that yield rewards, thus they do battlegrounds and disregard World PvP as a activity. Now I'm not saying give World PvP a huge multiplier of honor, because then that would kill battlegrounds, what I have suggested in the OP is give World PvP stuff like titles, spells, pets, tabards, etc. This would not affect battlegrounds in any such way, as players would still be doing battlegrounds for their honor, but doing World PvP as an off habit for some fun rewards.
