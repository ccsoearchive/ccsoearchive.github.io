It is all well and good, except you forgot one thing...people trying to play the rest of the game.

One aspect of the game that hasn't been mentioned much (if at all) here are instances. While in an instanced dungeon or (for some Cataclysm content) phased, you are immune to PvP attacks. If it were true world PvP, there would be no instances or phasing. Yeah, that would be a fundamental change to the game if those were eliminated...

Yeah, you'll always have the players with the 'bully' mentality who rampage through a sub-20 adventuring area killing everything that moves. They do it simply for the enjoyment of causing misery to other players. What I've seen in some other MMOs is a limitation on PvP in that you can attack only players within a few levels (perhaps 5) of you. This would eliminate one of the most unpleasant aspects of playing on a PvP server. For WoW, that could be amended to elminate that limitation for enemies in a town or city (e.g. Alliance in the Crossroads or Orgrimmar; Horde in Goldshire or Stormwind).
