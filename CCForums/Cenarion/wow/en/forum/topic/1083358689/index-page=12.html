This kinda plays in with your original "major city portal" and "increased Honor" ideas, but I'd love to see awesome rewards as incentives for killing opposing players in key portions of major cities. "Valley of Heroes" is a good example for the Alliance side. Having it in a portion of a major city like VoH ensures that there is no incentive for killing quest givers, auctioneers, vendors, etc. For every X # of horde an alli kills there, a certain bonus (honor, gear, whatever) is awarded. Likewise, for every X # of alliance players a horde player kills in VoH (or whatever area is appropriate in other major cities), the horde player gets the bonus. Double or triple even, to get people to venture out from the safety of their major cities while still giving them reason to defend their own. Turn the place into an impromptu BG. If you're on a roll and you've reached the limit to get the bonus, then keep on moving through the city. (No extra bonuses then, however...just the satisfaction of trashing your enemy's place.)

Thought number next (you might have had something in your OP but it was long and my memory ain't so good right now) is to have a daily "king of the town" daily. It's a wild fight for a town (Goldshire, Crossroads, Astranaar, etc) where it needs to be held or taken. You get 10 minutes to complete your objective before crazy OP'd guards show up to drive off the invaders. If you take it, you get tons of bonus stuff and then the guards show up, so run fast. If you defend it and hold for that time, you get tons of bonus stuff and the guards show up to clean up the mess. Or Deathwing arrives and takes a cosmic dump on everyone to clear the board for a minute to let the low levels get their quest givers back.

WPvP is what brought me back to WoW from other MMOs. I started shortly after Wrath and SW was getting hit pretty much every night. Pure awesome sauce. It just doesn't happen anymore and I miss it.

Just out of curiosity, but why does everyone cry about World PvP always talk about the major cities and guards?

They haven't killed World PvP, they have killed griefiing. You can no longer roll into a low level quest area and deny the opposite faction the ability to play the PvE game that is the majority focus...even on PvP realms.

You can no longer have just a handful of people and cause complete havoc in a major city. Now, you have to have a full raid and coordination...just like it should have always been. At no point...not even at the end of an expansion...should 5 people be able to kill a faction leader as was common towards the end of Wrath.

World PvP exists in the places where people are, not where you want them to be. Camping a low level quest hub and saying you are trying to lure people into World PvP is just a tired lame excuse for griefing low level players. Got to the Cata zones or Tol Barad between battles and you'll find all the World PvP action you could want.

And remember that even on a PvP server, PvE is the predominate play style. PvP servers aren't PvP centered at all, but are simply a way to add a little more spice to your typical game play by having the added danger of always on PvP.

I really don't get the QQ about World PvP when so many guaranteed avenues to PvP exist in this game. The SS vs TM days are dead and gone and will only be fondly remembered and never, never again duplicated in the regular world. Move on and stop whining about something that can not be changed.
