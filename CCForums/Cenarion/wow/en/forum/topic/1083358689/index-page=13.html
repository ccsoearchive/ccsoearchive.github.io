Honor points are so easy to get now that have even 2x honor would be silly.

Lowering guards HP to 75% of the current would be better than dropping it down to 40% of the current.
Respawn rate is meant to be somewhat fast because they have reserves to respond to invaders.
Removing guards is like removing the city.
Guards are meant to keep the opposing faction from just walking around unnoticed.
Flying guards are in BC because of flying mounts, it is only logical to have them in Azeroth for Cata.

World PvP kills would just be a sidenote to overall kills because for most servers. World PvP isn't there because the less populated faction sticks to friendly territory. On balanced, population-dense PvP servers, you see more World PvP because both sides are almost equal, it is a PvP realm, and it has a large population.

It would be longer to waste time grinding world PvP rewards than just to put them together with the rest of PvP. Besides, in statistics, world PvP is separated so you can check that.

The whole flying mount attacking would be too complex to put into for such a minor issue.
