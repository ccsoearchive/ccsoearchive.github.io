Hello Everybody. I've noticed over the past few expansions that World PvP has taken a downward spiral for the worst, and thought of some idea's of how to might possibly bring it back to its once true state.

The first reason as to why World PvP is dead is because no player can be everywhere at once to witness where or when PvP action is happening. For example, Goldshire is under attack by the Horde, at this point none of the alliance know about Goldshire being under attack till an Alliance low level runs all the way up to Stormwind and tells everybody in general that the town is under attack. The little alliance player may take up to an hour to reach and alert his fellow faction about there town being under attack, and even then people don't care, which leads me to my second reason.

The second reason World PvP is dead is because 90% of the players don't care about it as an activity there's no rewards in it for them except the satisfaction of killing other players which they can get from Battleground's. For example on my server if the Alliance ever do attack Thrall (on rare occasions) the Horde will not try and stop to defend their king even though we have 5 times there numbers sitting on Orgrimmar Bank. The reason they don't try to defend as I have already said and will state again, is because there is no Rewards.

The final reason as to why World PvP is dead is due to flying mounts. Flying mounts throughout Burning Crusade and Wrath of the Lich King have made it very hard for players to attack each other, considering there 10,000ft up in the sky. In Catacylsm this is going to be an even bigger issue for World PvP as players will be doing the same thing they did in Wotlk and B.C (climb 10,000ft in the sky, num lock and afk). There are many possible ways of counteracting this, the one way I have come up with consists of 2 idea’s put together which I will talk about later in the discussion.

Recap: Reasons World PvP is dead.

1) Nobody knows where and when World PvP action is happening.
2) No rewards = no encouragement.
3) Flying mounts make it hard for players to interact.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Towns tab

1) My first idea is for there to be a Tab dedicated to Horde and Alliance towns, so that if a town goes under attack a player may open up this Tab and check that said town is under attack. When a town goes under attack, the PvP tab (on the player toolbar) will light up from blue to red indicating a town is under siege. The player simply opens up the tab, checks which town is under attack and then proceed to head off to join the fray!
Example. I'm farming ore in Durotar when my PvP tab lights up, I open up the PvP tab, go to the "Towns" Tab inside, and see that Grom'gol base camp is under attack. I gather friends up and head off to Grom'gol to fend off the Alliance attackers.
The PvP tab will not only notify that the town is under attack but it will also indicate how serious the situation is. Example below.

10 guards killed - Green
25 guards killed - Orange
50 guards killed - Red
100 guards killed - Black

Say 27 guards have been killed in Grom'gol, I go in to check my "Towns tab" and find out the circle is an orange color indicating 25-49 guards have been killed.

Daily City Seige

2) Nobody ever attacks main cities anymore, so I recommend that every couple hours (say 5) a portal be opened up in a major city that is linked to an opposing faction city. Each faction would take turns attacking and defending their city, and the cities chosen to be attacked would cycle through (Stormwind ->Orgrimmar ->Ironforge->etc). This would make main cities more interesting, as every couple hours a mass of the opposing faction would come through a randomly spawned portal in the city and tear up the place. Once an opposing player dies in the siege of the other factions city he or she is teleported back to the city they were in previously, giving others a chance to join the battle. Now for server lag issues, the amount of people able to enter the portal would most likely be restricted to 40-60 players.

Tol Barad PvP ranks

3)It seems that the Tol Barad peninsula has sparked a well oiled environment for World PvP, kind of like back in the days of Quel‘danas. So since this seems to be such a success, why not go further into development with the Peninsula and add some PvP ranks to encourage even more World PvP. These ranks could be something along the lines of the Stonetalon mountain ranks, where they are zone exclusive. These ranks would add some extra spice and fun to World PvP in the Peninsula, and possibly come with some benefits.

Increased Honor for attacking/defending towns + cities

Currently players can obtain much more honor from doing a battleground then they can say defending a city from an onslaught of horde attackers, this must change to encourage more world PvP. Every time a city goes under attack, the defense channel will produce a message such as this:

[City Defender!]: Alliance are attacking the city! Honor gained while in Orgrimmar increased by 5x.

Now of course the message produced would be different for different encounters, as well the honor multiplier would change depending on the situation. For example, say garrosh hellscream enters combat with the alliance, a message such as this would be produced:

[Defender of the Warchief!]*- Garrosh Hellscream is under attack! Honor gained while in Orgrimmar increased by 10x.

As you can see the honor gained from defending the warchief is much more then it would be for just defending the city itself, as the warchief is a higher priority. These buffs would also apply to attackers as well, to encourage more players to participate in the battle. As well these buffs would apply to attacking/defending towns around azeroth, but the honor multiplier would be on a smaller scale then it is from attacking/defending a city.

*Note - Thanks to Byniri of Zangarmarsh for the idea!

Lower guard health/Remove guards

Guards at the moment are one of the biggest turn off for World PvPers, as they have enormous health pools and are just a gigantic pain in the ass. When I first think of going to participate in World PvP, the first thing that comes to mind is dealing with the everlasting respawn of the guards, having to kill them over, and over, AND OVER, again. The fact is being forced to kill an NPC while ultimately trying to kill another player is a hard thing, and most players would rather just queue for a battleground where these "guards" do not interfere with PvP actions. Once again, this is World PvPers being forced to participate in a PvE orientated activity while trying to PvP.

Suggestions on fixing guards

-Lower guard health by at least 60%
-Reduce respawn time on guards
-Remove guards
-Concentrate guards by auction houses instead of all around the city.
-Remove flying guards (boy their a pain!)

World PvP kills

4) My second idea is to split up World PvP kills from Battleground kills. By doing this Players will have two sets of Kills in there Honor tab. For example a player is able to have 12'334 Battleground kills and 1456 World PvP kills. This idea encourages players to attack and defend more of there and there opposing faction towns because players want to obtain more World kills. This idea goes hand in hand with the next one.

Incentive

5) Rewards. Rewards are what most players strive to achieve in WoW, Players raid to obtain PvE gear, players farm to obtain gold, players Arena to Obtain PvP gear. By splitting up World PvP kills from Battleground kills, rewards and achievements can become available through certain amounts of World PvP kills. For example, [Private] may be obtainable from having 300 World PvP kills overall.

Possible title/rank rewards.

1st - Recruit
2nd - Soldier
3rd - Blood guard
4th - Mercenary
5th - Myrmidon
6th - Veteran
7th - Destroyer

These ranks could most possibly have an ability attached to each one, something that would benefit a player in a World PvP situation (not usable in Arena, Raiding, or Battlegrounds). For example,

Recruit spell - [Heal] - Heals yourself for 20% of your maximum Health points. (10 minute CD)

Let me quickly discuss why the lack of rewards is a huge turn off to World PvP and is probably one of the top contributors to the evident lack of World PvP in today's WoW. Take this into consideration, If Blizzard were to remove all rewards gained from raiding such as gear, titles, mounts, do you think players would still participate in raiding? The vast majority would most likely quit on the spot, as there is no incentive for them to raid any longer, but some would stick around to keep playing because they enjoy raiding, much like World PvPers enjoy World PvP. As you can see, the vast majority of players need incentive to fuel what types of activities they do, and without these incentives, players will most likely not participate.

Flying mount hit points + Rockets

4)By allowing flying mounts to have kill able hit points players can attack an enemies flying mount to try to bring his opponent down to the ground to be able to do battle with him/her. The way a player kills an enemies flying mount is by use of rockets. The rockets function in a unique firing system, as in a player must aim and fire at his opponents mount threw a scope doing damage to the players mount to bring him down (not just target spam click button). The abilities for the rocket would be as followed:

Slug shot - Slows the target players mount by 50%.

Barrage - Fires a rocket into the sky dealing [x] amount of damage to a players mount.

When a player is dismounted from his/her mount they will float down to earth with a controllable parachute, as in they can direct which way the parachute goes and how fast it falls to the earth. To counteract ranged players having an advantage over melee players falling with a parachute, and vice versa for ranged players with a parachute verses melee players on the ground, I propose players are not attack-able and cannot attack till they are on the ground. Now, to counteract players from going 10,000ft up in the sky I propose the flying level be dimmed down to where players can shoot each other out of the sky with the rockets.

This is just a basic idea of what Blizzard could do to fix the issue of flying mounts, I’m open for suggestions on it and further discussion would be nice. The reason I have suggested this is because without the flying mount issue being addressed, Cataclysm is going to turn into another Wrath of the lich king were players never interact with each other outside Dalaran because their so high up in the sky.
