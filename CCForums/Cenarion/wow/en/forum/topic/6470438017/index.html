I know most of you aren't going to remember me, I was only around for a brief time when the Mount Hyjal Alliance was still going strong. Some of the older members on this server may remember Egaala, my husband. I just wanted to post because I've seen something absolutely amazing here. What is that, you might ask. Well, I'll tell you.

I've seen at least two guilds that I remember from that time. Very old guilds that may very well have been around from the start of the game, but were certainly strong in Vanilla. The two guilds I recognize are Da Docta and AAMS.

I am absolutely amazed to see these two guilds going strong and it tells me one thing for certain after the RP seemed to all but die on this server. This has got to be one very tight knit community of role players. I'm a bit envious, to be honest, and in some ways I wish that I had stayed on this server after my brief departure from WoW.

Anyway, that's pretty much what I wanted to say. Have fun everyone!
