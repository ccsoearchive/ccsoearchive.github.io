---Personality Match Profiler

Name: Brother Santcho Hastur
Title: Hastur Healer
Age: 25
Height: 5' 8" Wt.: 150lbs
Hair: Black worn short and straight.
Eyes: Dark Brown
Complexion: Light brown skin.
Marital Status: Married to IvyGale a gardener and Midwife at the House of Hastur, no children

Tell us about your self:
I was born and mostly raised in Dalaran before the Third War. My parents owned an herb farm and Alchemy shop that I worked in with them and my brothers and sisters. My Father and I were on a caravan when it was attacked by Orc raiders. We had thought that the Orcs had all been subdued and locked in the camps but we were wrong. I was taken by the Orcs and became a slave to their alchemist since I already had knowledge of herbs and their uses. Then the Third War started and things got worse. I haven't seen my family since then but I know that my older brother and one of my sisters were outside of Dalaran when the barrier was put up.
While I served the Orcs, they became more violent and prone to rage. The clan that held me had given themselves over to the demons and my life became a nightmare.
I was rescued under circumstances that I'd rather not discuss and returned to the Eastern Kingdoms under the care of a priest of The Light who had been a friend to Bishop LeMay. This led to my introduction to Pia Presidium and my admittance into their esteemed ranks.

What do you do in your spare time?:
I really like gathering herbs and making useful potions out of them like I used to do with my family. I also like to cook and find relaxing spots to meditate while I fish. But these activities are just an excuse for me to wander the face of Azeroth providing aid anywhere that I can.

Favorite Quote:
"Holiness is in right action and courage on behalf of those who cannot defend themselves."

---Shadethorn

---/maniacal grin

"I'm not a troublemaker, sir. Really."

That could possibly be a lie.

Shadowash was supposed to be a healer. With a Priest for a father and a Druid for a mother...it was expected of her. However, the closest Shadowash has gotten to picking up a healing art, is slapping some cloth on her wounds.

With her mother's approval, and her father disownment of her, she set off to hone her skills and help the war effort. She has earned the nickname of "The Healer's Worst Nightmare" from the stress and aggravation she causes her father, and for being known to engage in combat even under less than desirable odds.

Random swords, fist weapons, and daggers are strapped at her sides, and she is rarely seen without her disturbing grin...

Except when there is a troll around. She watches these characters very intently. Something about them makes her curious about them...

She is, however, extremely loyal to those that have actually befriended her.


---Shae'l Wintersong:

Born in Astranaar to common parents; she took up arms during the Third War, and at Hyjal--a huntress under Malfurion Stormrage. To date, she has lived an interesting life, manifesting bizarre dreams which led her to the service of the Lich King. She attempted suicide during this time, but was returned by Elune. A series of events led her to share a soulbind with Grummak, Shaman to the Horde, which allows them to communicate freely. She remains in a long standing, but ambiguous relationship with Nikov Azgol-Haman, and an equally long standing but less ambiguous relationship with her winter wolf bondmate, Larka. She currently serves under Imperon Showdah in his Black Guard, to the palipable dismay of her dwarven love.

She makes her home in Winterspring, but has a permanent room at the tavern in Menethil.

---Shalis Starseeker - Amaelalin's companion of about 7000 years IC and her nightsaber Hikari is generally close by. Fiercly loyal to her Goddess, people and friends she has already dedicated many lifetimes to protecting and continues to do so. Just don't forget she is a forest creature at heart. ---Shio

---SIJOL lately has become a hunter of great reknown, despite his nature to remain reclusive. In his childhood, his mother, Jasa, wanted him to become a druid like herself, but upon his first meeting with a a hunter and his wolf companion, Sijol fell in love with the profession.
On a supposed-to-be-educational visit to the Hinterlands, Sijol ventured out alone one night and was attacked by a giant spider. Wounded badly and left for dead, a roving wolf pack found him and took him in. Years after, the dwarves in the area caught him raiding their ram flocks with the pack, wild and incoherent, spirit-bonded with the wolves. After many trials, Sijol has re-entered the humanoid world, his wolves at his side, and now fights to end the war between the Alliance and the Horde and protect the lands from other, more threatening evils.

---Silinai:

Born and raised in the township of Hillsbrad, Silinai was placed into the Paladin Order by her parents. Contrary to the usual "I hate my life" style, Silinai enjoys her life very much and enjoy's being a Paladin.

Though a bit of a hothead and over zealous when it comes to undead and demons, Silinai isn't a stereotypical Paladin. She just has a lot of ideals in common with them.

More comes with roleplay!

---Talaith Finnevar and her player are both paladins, as far as I am concerned. Tally struggles to be a friend to all who love the Light, and her player has helped me get through more than a handful of tight spots in real life, as well. Talaith also has the distinction of being the first character I encountered in-game who exhibited real human weakness: Tallys left arm was savaged shortly after her adventuring career began, forcing her to abandon the path of the fury warrior and take up the mace and shield of a paladin. Additionally, her reactions to many of the things that have happened to her throughout her life have been so real that they have surprised me. I will never forget the unexpected and cruel jealousy that put the distance between her and Danse that has yet to be fully bridged. Talaiths player expended a lot of effort with me, creating a backstory for Talaith and Danse that made them long-lost friends from childhood, and I am truly blessed in that she considered that work to be as important as I did. --Verdence Rainfall

A greenhaired druid with a mischeivious grin and somewhat shorter than the average Night elf, she was raised by loving if somewhat odd parents. Her parents gave her all manner of advice ranging from "Always try first to be polite, no matter to whom" and "Do as you please, provided it causes others no harm." Verdence always carefully considers the advice of others, having since decided that the advice her parents gave her was sound.

Having decided to be a druid despite it being somewhat 'untraditional' for her gender, she decided to go forth and live among the creatures of nature to learn the proper insight before her actual training could begin. As a result, sometimes Verdence's viewpoints on certain matters can be rather strange. She believes all creatures and peoples on Azeroth to be her brothers and sisters, a notion which keeps her loyal to the idea behind the Mount Hyjal Alliance.

She has a particular fondness for Tauren, as they are fuzzy (and therefore cute) and are large (and make her feel safe.)

She has come to the conclusion that the current trouble with her people and why they've been acting so bat-wonky lately is twofold; Because Teldrassil is corrupted somewhat, and because of the shock of mortality.

Feel free to either lift the format from http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/2190889207 or post there.

Just pick a format you like and post here, if you want, as another option.

No pressure on how much or how little information you contribute. We'll be delighted to get something from everybody, so don't be shy! You can always edit your post and add more later.

For example, here is the format we ended up with in the previous incarnation of this thread, with my information:

Professor Galahn "The Professor" Tinhelm - Grand Master of the Phlogiston Brotherhood

The Professor is a distracted, brilliant sort. Often buried in his world-hopping archaeology or tending his Phlogiston Brotherhood with his special quality tests, he is often still on the job in his head when outside the workshop. His travels have attracted all sorts of companions, and turned him into an uncanny repository of knowledge.

His skills with a spanner match his deadly mark with a bow, and his engineering prowess is only outmatched by his skills with a gun, despite his pacifist outlook.

As a second example, here's a brilliant one someone recently transplanted from Wyrmcrest Accord:

(Stolen from Souvik, who stole it from Gorkosh, who stole it from Valhelm, who stole it from Jarius...)

Professor Galahn Tinhelm

BASICS
Full name: Galahn Tinhelm
Nick-name: The Professor, Professor, Gal
Prefix*: Mister
Title**: Professor
Race: Mountain Dwarf
Age***: 58
Position in society†: Tenured Professor of Ancient Kaldorei History with the Explorers League, Guild Charter Holder in Dalaran, Grand Master of the Phlogiston Brotherhood
Face/Heel†*: Face; Known for his neutrality in the conflicts between the factions of Azeroth (except for that one time he got really drunk...)
Physical Description (abridged): Ginger-haired, stocky Dwarf with fair to pink skin. Fairly standard for his sub-race. Tends to wear the traditional vestments of a Hunter, with cold weather flying goggles over his eyes.

OPTIONAL:
Personality: Pacifist, absent-minded
Marital Status: Single
Sexual Preference: None?
Blood line: Presumably farmer stock

FLAGS:
Role-playing weight class: Medium
Role-playing status†**: Available and friendly, but not actively looking for contact.

OTHER:(optional)
Guild: Phlogiston Brotherhood
City of choice: Caer Darrow, Stromwind City (The Blue Recluse)
IC-Strengths: Language, Ancient/Obscure History, Grand Master Engineer, Quality Inspection Specialist
IC-Weaknesses: Social Interaction, Conflict, Combat, Brain Damage
OOC-Strengths: "I can make that - probably," a loremaster, RP teacher, crafting enthusiast (especially 1-300), this character is a good neutral sounding board, as his only enemies are threats like the Old Gods and the Burning Legion
OOC-Weaknesses: loves to attend big functions, but the player usually cannot keep up with all the text
Leveling speed: Max Level
PvP preference (do it a lot?): Not on this character; only defends races he considers "endangered"
Weekly on-line schedule: Sporadic at best, but tries to check in at least once a week

Introduction (a paragraph or two about yourself and what you hope to find on this server, may also include contact information if you are seeking other role-playing contacts.)

The Professor is a distracted, brilliant sort. Often buried in his world-hopping archaeology or tending his Phlogiston Brotherhood with his special quality tests, he is often still on the job in his head when outside the workshop. His travels have attracted all sorts of companions, and turned him into an uncanny repository of knowledge.

His skills with a spanner match his deadly mark with a bow, and his engineering prowess is only outmatched by his skills with a gun, despite his pacifist outlook.

This criteria does contain a few things you may not like, or necessarily care about. But others may like to role-play with people who level quicker than others, or PvP a lot. Also I find people who role-play sometimes have a conflict of schedule, making it harder to enjoy role-playing.


*: Lord / Lady / Mister / Misses / Miss / etc.
**: Your heroic name, ex: John Doe "The Flaming Sun."
***: Link to age thread, (Draenei is not in this, Blood Elf ages are listed as High Elf.)
http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=12053281&sid=1
†: You can be free with this, either your political position, economical position, or status in the city. Ex: Vanished from the city, wealthy collector, etc.
†*: How others like you. Face being the crowd favorite and heel being the guy/gal everyone hates.
†**: Are you usually in-character? A story-teller? Majority rules, put what your usual situation is.
