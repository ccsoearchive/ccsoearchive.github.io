SCHEDULE
What would be your ideal meeting schedule? M, W, F, starting at around 10am Pacific (1pm Eastern) and going for 1-3 hours depending on our activities.
Would you be okay with less often? Sure!
Would you be okay with more often? Yes, if the meetings were no more than about an hour/hour and a half, or less structured and just kind of "hanging out" type stuff.
What other differences in that schedule can you tolerate (later/earlier/etc.)? I can start an hour earlier than that, and I can go until 3pm Pacific/6pm Eastern.
If we do your ideal schedule, estimate how often you'd attend (life happens!): I might miss one meeting a week or so if there were that many; then again I might have perfect attendance. Depends on my writing schedule.
What's the shortest/longest meeting you would enjoy? Less than an hour would seem like a bit of a waste of effort; more than three hours and I start to get twitchy. More than four hours is probably off the table without a significant break.

CHARACTER
What server do you prefer (CC or WRA)? CC
Would you be ok with trying the other server? If the majority wills it.
What would be your ideal character? Tank or healer
Could you enjoy playing something else? If so, what? Anything!
What sort of characters do you enjoy RPing with (other players)? I like to interact with characters who are kind, intelligent, and have at least some sort of sense of humor. I also like "regular guy" kind of characters who RP as having power about equal to their in-game level and don't have a lot of special powers or odd backstory.
Do you prefer a group that's same-race or different-race? I kind of love just the visual of a same-race group, as well as the RP possibilities and the chance to start from level 1 together, but I'm willing to mix it up if need be.

STORYLINE
On a scale of 1 to 10, how much do you enjoy the following:
Silliness/lighthearted humor? 8
Dark themes (death, torture, madness, evil)? 6
Romantic themes? 7
Family themes? 4
Stationary/tavern RP? 5
RPing while questing? 10!!
Intricate mysteries with clues to follow and answers to find? 9
Planning storylines? 8 if I'm inspired, 3 if I'm not
Being a "passenger" in someone else's planned storyline? 10!
What's your preferred balance of spontanaeity/planning? I like spontanaeity in between certain planned "milestones," so basically a rough outline of the major events with room to explore and improvise in between.

ACTIVITIES
What activities would you rather do in "off time" between meetings? Tradeskilling, gathering, bag organizing, auctioning
What activities should we do when we're all together? Questing, dungeons, maybe a BG now and then!
What sort of pace should we set, leveling-wise? Slow enough to smell the roses and really experience each zone and questline IC rather than just rushing through and obsessing over the numbers.
What optional aspects of the game do you NOT enjoy? Sometimes tradeskilling can be a drag (read: smithing). I have a love/hate relationship with holidays and achievements. I do not like raiding or getting really hung up on gear/spec/stats or being told "the" way to play, though I do enjoy little tips on how to make certain class abilities work better for me, etc.
Should the same person be in charge each time or should we rotate? Rotation would be nice but only if there is someone else who has the will/ability to -actually- direct things confidently. I really hate sitting in the backseat while a driver sits there going, dduuuhhhhh.
Would you be interested in occasionally leading/planning meetings? I imagine this will be the default, but like I say, I'd appreciate some relief.
How planned should the meetings be and how spontaneous? I think I would have a plan for every other meeting (or maybe 2/3) and then have the remaining meeting just be, "Okay, what do you guys feel like doing today?"

OTHER STUFF
How would you prefer the group stay in touch? I'd like to have an ongoing thread on the Blizzard server forums so others can spy on the fun we're having and know to RP with us if they see us, etc. It will also facilitate latecomers joining us/replacing dropouts down the line.
How fast do you type? 90wpm
Rate your command of the English language, 1-10: 9
How tolerant are you of typos/misspellings/bad grammar in others? I can tolerate anything other than deliberate "texting speak" or just profound disrespect/disdain for the whole idea of grammar (I am a writer and language is my love!). If you're at least trying to speak English well, then you're ok in my book. I RPed for years with a brilliant guy who was dyslexic and I am used to any number of horrible spellings as a result.
