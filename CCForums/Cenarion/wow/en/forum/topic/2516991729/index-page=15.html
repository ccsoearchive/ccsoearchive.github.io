Just bumping this long post.

I do truly believe that this change will alienate a lot of casual PvPers, who are already discouraged from entering the system mid-season due to gear imbalances, and who cannot, or will not play RBGs for various reasons (time, fun, playstyle, etc). In my guild, a bunch of us play 2s because its the easiest to organize and we have constraints on our time. Some play just enough to cap, others, like me, who enjoy PvP more than any other aspect of the game, play as many games as we can to help others cap (this week I hope to play 40 games).

What's the point? Well, if you alienate these players, who already only subscribe because of casual PvP, you're probably going to lose their subs. What happens then? Then, when people like me can't find casual partners, and there's no longer a reason to log in, you'll lose my sub too.

TL:DR: this change, for various reasons that are completely logical, and therefore accessible to Blizzard, is going to erode Blizzard's player base, with a positive feedback cumulative effect on subscriptions -- the more are alienated by this change, the more will leave, and the more, and the more, etc.

DONT DO IT.
