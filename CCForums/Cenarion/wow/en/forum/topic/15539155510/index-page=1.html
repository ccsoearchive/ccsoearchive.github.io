"To a valiant heart, nothing is impossible."

The Thulmane Auxiliary is a heavy roleplaying guild on Wyrmrest Accord (US). If you enjoy heavy-political/military drama, storylines with many twists & conflicts, and a fun, welcoming OOC environment, then The Thulmane Auxiliary may be the guild for you.

In-Character, the Auxiliary serves under Clan Thulmane, a prominent Dwarven family from the North who only seeks to extend their reach of power, wealth, and influence -- all while keeping a positive image to the public eye. This guild is not only the constant strive for power for the Thulmanes, but also those who serve them: Guardsmen fighting for valor in effort to catch their liege's attention, servants close to the prominent family overhearing their darkest secrets, intelligence agents carrying out the more "unsavory" business against the Clan's rivals -- Everyone has a role in the story, regardless of rank.

Some of what you can expect:

☑ Heavy political & military drama (war campaigns, political strife, scandals, etc.)

☑ Frequent events ranging from well-to-do balls & galas, all the way to the gritty experience of our war efforts. (Public, Private, & inter-guild events! Fun, fun, fun!)

☑ A place for well-written characters with depth, as well as assistance for developing characters.

OOC Rules & Regulations:

1. NO DRAMA! T.T.A. is a group of mature roleplayers and will not tolerate unnecessary, childish drama. Keep your drama to yourself, or get booted. Simple as that. We will not tolerate fighting & arguments in guild chat or any other public channel. If there is an issue with a member, please contact Galvynn or another officer and we will work to get it resolved. If you cannot conduct yourself in a respectable manner, you will be removed without hesitation.

2. Treat your guildmates with respect. While our OOC chat can sometimes get raunchy, it's all in good fun. However, if things start becoming blatantly serious insults, you are at serious risk of being kicked from the guild.

3. DO NOT MAKE US LOOK BAD! Everything you do whether IC or OOC reflects upon us as a guild. We don't care what another roleplayer does! Take your troubles to whispers and be polite. No swearing, no yelling, etc. If we receive a number of complaints about your behavior, we will boot you immediately.

4. Guild Chat is IC. Officer Chat is OOC. While we love chatting OOC, please do not abuse IC guild chat with mindless chatter about everyone's day. It is to be used for brief and important IC messages only via our IC comms.

5. We here at TTA don't believe in artificially bloating our numbers with dozens of inactive members. We carry a fair 10-day inactivity policy. We understand that you may be busy with RL issues, vacation, work, etc., but if you expect to be gone longer than 10 days, please inform Galvynn or another officer and we will be sure to make a note of it!

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

IC Rules & Regulations:

1. Metagaming and Godmoding will not be tolerated!

2. Lore breaking will not be tolerated. We will not accept long-lost sons of Varian, half-demons, half-Horde anything, or characters with almighty powers that can blow up the local inns with a blink of an eye. And no, Druids are not kitties and Worgen are not puppies. We understand if you'd like to be creative, but in this case, less -is- more.

3. 3. RP fights will be played out logically. For example, if Sally is wearing full mail armor, while Billy is wearing thin cloth linens, Billy is much more likely to sustain higher injuries from a weapon than Sally would. We also ask that you agree upon things OOC with the other player in order to avoid potential arguments and accusations.

4. No chatspeak! Proper capitalization and punctuation is a MUST! Names, beginnings of sentences, I's, etc. Remember all that stuff you learned in Elementary School? USE IT!

5. IC actions have IC consequences! Deal with it maturely, or you'll be gone very quickly. For example, if your character assaults someone from another guild, expect to be punished accordingly. Do not whine about it. Accept it and move on.

6. IC Mounts: We're all aware that you're proud of your sparkly pony, gigantic drake, or your festive reindeer, but we ask that you use a regular horse, ram, gryphon, etc.

7. ERP: If you want to ERP, we don't care/want/need to hear about it. However, if you are caught doing so in a public channel, you WILL be booted immediately. No second chances, no discussion about it. Keep it in whispers or party chat.

8. RP Addons: We strongly recommend installing the MyRoleplay and the Gryphonheart Items Addons.

Frequently Asked Questions:

¤ What is the "primetime" for your RP?
We are an evening guild, and most of our RP occurs from 6PM onto the later hours of the night, although hours may vary on the weekends.

¤ What type of guild is The Thulmane Auxiliary?
We are a Heavy-RP guild with a focus on character development through story-driven roleplaying. While roleplaying comes first, we occasionally run a few dungeons, raids, etc. on our down-time.

¤ Where is the majority of your RP located?
We will go wherever our story takes us. Currently, a majority of the guild's RP will be in Draenor, for obvious reasons.

¤ What are your requirements for joining?
We are looking for active, mature, experienced, and literate roleplayers who will enjoy our story-driven roleplay. You must have an IC Interview and roleplay with the guild before you may join. Please do not join on a character you do not play often. There's no sense in being with us if you're gone 3-4+ days at a time. Also, a sense of humor is a MUST!

¤ What races do you accept?
We are a very Dwarf-centric guild, however we also accept Gnomes, Humans, and Worgen.

¤ What classes do you accept?
Paladins, Priests, Hunters, Warriors, Mages, & Rogues. Death Knights, Warlocks, and Shamans will be considered on a case-by-case basis.

¤ What is the alignment of The Thulmane Auxiliary?
We are formally a Lawful Neutral guild.

¤ What is your alt policy?
We will accept In-Character alts as long as you've shown a habit of being active and participating in our events. Please do not give us your rarely-played alts.

¤ How do I join?
We request that you fill out an application on our site (thulmaneaux.shivtr.com) so that we may determine if your character might be a good fit within our guild. Once approved, we will arrange an IC portion of the process and send an invite your way!
