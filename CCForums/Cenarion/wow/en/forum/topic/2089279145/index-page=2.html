The thing is the glyph doesnt just mean 1 extra ambush. You have to consider these factors:

A: You are using ambush INSTEAD of backstab, so you are gaining a combo-point and a bit of damage (depends on your avg difference btwn ambush and BS), and actually costing a bit more energy (glyph of BS).

B: FW would ideally be up for 2 extra secs, but that may not quite pan out, even if it should. Depends on when you use finishers in your cycle.

C: the added CP from an extra ambush would mean you get closer to an extra finisher.

D: (aww sad face) Depending on your build, ShD might be favored more if you use Initiative (I am not atm because I like the utility of ES, but I might switch, this could affect ShD fairly significantly).

Some of these factors are for and against ShD, and as for evis, its pretty simple. I'll try to run a more exact test if I can get the people, and I'll try out both specs.
