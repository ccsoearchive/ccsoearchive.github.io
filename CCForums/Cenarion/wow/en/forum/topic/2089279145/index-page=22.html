I'll answer the last part first: Backstabs often crit as hard or harder than ambush due to the talent that increases the damage bonus of BS crits. However it is unlikely that they will hit for more overall than ambush. What also makes ambush better is the debuff it applies and the extra combo points, plus it is almost guaranteed to crit.
As for it being just lately? Honestly I'm not sure. If you got a MH upgrade that would increase the damage by a large margin, and the patch did buff our overall damage, but with the same gear and since 4.2 I havn't noticed any unaccounted boost.

To the first part, I'll say I qualify. My dps single target will generally be the highest if gear is even. That said I will take hits from fights with frequent movement/target swapping. In the right encounter, Shadowstep will make the difference as far as movement, allowing you do get back to the boss quickly and use openers if one is ready. (Since I tend to have high energy coming back to a boss, and have the SS buff up already, in addition to wanting fast combo-points to keep up or apply over time effects.
I will also excel at fights where cooldowns are important. If there is a boss like Magmaw where there is a burn phase every 1.5 mins, I can line up at least one CD in that time, and since CDs make up much of a sub rogue's damage, I will do better than many other classes. Even fights with generally important burn phases (about to do H Shannox, which I imagine will be a good example) a sub rogue will do well in, again due to CDs.

Basically, if you can master the rotation and "damage when it counts" principle, you will do well.

Edit: In case anyone is curious, here is my World of Logs. Well, my guild's but I run it. Don't think I am extra leet or anything by ranking on every fight I do - There aren't enough sub rogues who raid to make me have a chance at NOT ranking. Also these are with various gear levels but if you want to use it for rotational stuff or reference, go for it.

In a 5 man with the LFD buff, on a melee favorable fight I usually pull 22k with no melee buffs. With Might and Windfury, I can usually see 26-27k on a standstill fight, like many of the ones in ZA are. I have finished a fight at over 30k with Lust up. Again, this is with the 3 stack Luck of the Draw buff. My rogue is in full 359s, except for his chest, which is 372 tier, and his legs and hands, with are 378 tier.

I pulled 23.7k dps with him as Sub on heroic 25 man Chimaeron. That was awhile ago, in full 359s, besides the 372 chest piece. That was also before the buff all the rogues spec got to their damage in 4.2.

http://www.worldoflogs.com/reports/4d6o4i4yuu61h0fw/sum/damageDone/?s=8511&e=8876#Statutorypwn

People can, and will, harp on how difficult Sub's rotation is. As far as the spec's damage goes, I haven' found it lacking at all this expansion.

About Ambush/Eviscerate. My Ambush, under the Find Weakness debuff, does slightly less damage when compared to Backstab, under the same debuff. Ambush is almost a guaranteed crit though (although I had 2 of them non crit in a row recently, which is pretty damn rare). My Eviscerates usually hit slightly harder than my Backstabs, unless UHW was procced (which is nerfed now anyways), but crit far less than Backstab does. I do not have much mastery as Sub, so theoretically I guess you could get Eviscerate damage up there, at the cost of sustained dps.
