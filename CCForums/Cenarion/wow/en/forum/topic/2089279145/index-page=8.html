And Haileaus, I've calculated Essence vs Unheeded in a (hopefully) simple manner.

The % of total damage done from Eviscerate, Rupture, and 1/2 (one half) of your poisons must be greater than the % of total damage done from autoattacks. The %'s are shown off recount.

And how much greater? Well... I have no ****ing clue. Though Unheeded does get a small boost off armor debuff (as poisons ignore armor) and gets a slight better Backstab (and a way bigger Ambush, thank Lethality for the difference between the 2 abilities), I'm sure you're better off with Unheeded.
