Just tested my dps against a dummy twice with 3 hunters and a lock acting as critters, which gave me near optimal CP generation (though lousy buffs). The tests lasted 5 mins each. The first time was with going for the expertise cap, the second reforging according to EJ's EP values (involved losing expertise and gaining haste or crit). The second dps was notably higher, although most of that is probably due to the additional haste. Also, in fights in which rupture and DP are likely to fall off, expertise is likely better than crit because those two abilities only gain benefit from crit, so the expertise-portion of your damage will increase as DP/rupture uptime decreases. I also did have a few incidents where eviscerate missed and I either energy capped or habitually used BS after, which is human error and would increase the benefit of expertise for humans rather than the program which generates EJ's EP values.

My general conclusion is that haste should not be sacrificed to gain expertise, though reforging to expertise over crit is likely a dps increase on most encounters, and will decrease the problematic fluctuations from fight-to-fight.

This is a lot more subjective however what I generally do is try to keep slice and dice/recup rolling, refreshing them between mobs.
