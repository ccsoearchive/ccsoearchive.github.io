Sorry about three posts in a row, just checked the EJ guide specifically for subtlety, which was enlightening...ish. They suggest burning CP on SnD in the beginning, which I would use if I have over, say, 3 CP on the target. They also say Rupture has priority over Recuperate in the rotation. I personally will go with EJ on this one, because finding the overall dps of every ability for optimal use is pretty much what they do. I will say if you can get the Rupture into the 10% AoS damage boost, the buff will carry on past refreshes (I tested this one). In the bulk of the rotation however, if either recup or Rupture will run out (and you have to choose), I would check my ambush CD. If you can refresh recup and immediately vanish for a MoS 5 CP rupture, that would be just as good. If, on the other hand, you cannot rebuff MoS before directly refreshing rupture, by all means, refresh it.

I will do a link to the post in my main article, and edit it. I will also need to seriously think about that darn character limit. Credit to whoever linked the guide :)
