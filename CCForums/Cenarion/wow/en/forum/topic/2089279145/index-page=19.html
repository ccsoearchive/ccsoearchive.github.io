1) You appearently did not do what I told you to. Same post you quoted me, I told you to respec and look at Eviscerate's tooltip and compare that number. Notice how your Combat oriented tree has a weaker Eviscerate?
You sidestepped the point - the intent of the talent is to increase damage, and if it is not increasing damage the same way that is a problem.

Whether you increase the damage done to the target, or increase the damage of the ability before any multipliers take effect - in all cases it is a percentage gain which translates through all previous and all further modifiers.

Its simple multiplication. If you are correct, and I don't feel the need to test it to prove myself here, you're essentially saying that one talent or the other is extremely bugged and is not working properly (if at all).

2) It's less. And adding more finishers is a direct increase, but not really a significant one since you're not even gaining two Eviscreates a minute.
Assume 1.8 Eviscerates per minute gained, for the hell of it. That is 1.8 multiplied by say, a 5 minute fight length, or 9 Eviscerates. The DPS gain is probably 500-800 DPS depending on talent differences and gear levels. Now, you're doing 30% or more with Backstab and you're losing 30% crit damage, and 30% crit. That alone on someone doing 20,000 DPS is about 2500 DPS.

Do remember Eviscerate is only about 10% of your total damage. Also remember you do about 5 Backstabs per Eviscerate so comparing one Eviscerate to one Backstab is a noob mistake.
Eviscerate is used 1/5th as often as Backstab, yet does 1/3rd its damage? Interesting, don't you think?

Assuming the 20% talent works properly, which is up for debate at this point but I will ignore it for now...

Eviscerate, at 10% damage gains 6%, about 120 DPS. Backstab, now at significantly reduced output, gets back about 700 DPS. All together we have a loss of 2500 and a re-gaining of maybe 1500 DPS conservatively.

Its not great, but if you're losing a lot more than 1k from dropped Ruptures, SnDs, or poorly timed Shadow Dance / Vanish / Prep-Vanish (due to needing it to keep your finishers up properly) the spec has some potential I think.

I'm not asking for much here - just that some positive discussion on the spec be considered rather than dismissed immediately by respected members of the community.
