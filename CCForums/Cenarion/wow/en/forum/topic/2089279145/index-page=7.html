While neither give crit, the bonus from UH just isn't that good. Consider the amazing backstab crits you get. Now reduce them a bit because you will lose the UH proc. Math time.

Going off Zomgambush's gear, here's the result of Essence vs Unheeded.

Regular Backstab damage:
5990 agility (adding 10 stacks of Fluid Death) = 11940 (rounded up) attack power (not counting base str). 11940/14=852.86 for normalization. 30% total crit, with agility, rating + hat buff.

737 (base weapon damage) + (852.86 * 1.7) = 2187 * 3.64 = 7961 + 1634 = 9595 * 1.6 * 1.3 (crit + lethality bonus) = 19957

With Unheeded:
1417 + 1449.86 = 2867 * 3.64 = 10436 + 1634 = 12070 * 1.6 * 1.3 = 25106 damage.

With Essence:
9595 * 1.73 * 1.3 = 21580

Damage per Energy values with crit difference:
Unheeded: 25106 / 37 = 679
Essence: 21580 / 36.35 = 594

Now as for the crit vs weapon damage for things other than Backstab (You can replace the 3.64, 1634, and the 1.6 * 1.3 with 3.98, 2125, and 1.9 for weapon damage, bonus damage, and crit values respectively to get a look at Ambush, in which case Unheeded just does a lot more) like poisons and Rupture, I'm fairly certain that 30% (decreases with gear though I'm sure BiS won't drop it below 25%, let alone 20%) increase in melee just out does the crit (as it does effect poisons less since they don't do double damage). So, unless I've looked over something I'm pretty sure Unheeded Warning is better than Essence of the Cyclone.

On a side note, I think I may have used the crit rating amount off of Heroic Essence. Don't remember or care too much at the moment.
