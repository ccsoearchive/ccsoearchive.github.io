34. On physical attacks, Hurricane acts like a regular 1 PPM enchant. On spells, it is a 45 second ICD and a 15% proc rate. Yeah, just confirmed that (sadly).

Mk, let's assume that all the procs happen on thier average time (300/8= every 37.5 seconds for melee procs + every 45 seconds for spell).
# of seconds past for each proc
Melee: 37.5, 75, 112.5, 150, 187.5, 225, 262.5, 300
Spell: 45, 90, 135, 180, 225, 270

So the 1st and 7th melle Hurricane proc is refreshed by the spell, taking away 2 full spell procs. That is an additional 15 seconds on the melee proc though.

So now with the 8 procs and the additional 15 seconds of melee, we've got a 37% uptime (so an average of 166.5 haste for the 5 minutes) of melee Hurricane. Add in the 4 spells which have a 16% uptime (so about average 72 haste) and we've got about 238.5 haste from the offhand enchant. Now if Landslide were on the offhand instead, we'd have a 32% uptime on that (average of 320 attack power).

238.5 * 1.35 = 321.975 is the Hurricane's value in attack power (according to the stat weights) vs 320.

Looking at this info, I'd say it really doesn't matter which you choose. Landslide could be more consistent dps overall since it's less RNG, but Hurricane definately has potential to do better.

Details of Hemorrhage buff in 4.2

Dagger Hemorrhage is still better than non dagger Hemorrhage (in fact the difference has grown in dagger's favor) so that's out the window.

30% crit + 20% haste for this, meaning 1060 over a minute. -10 for Recuperate cost, assuming Rupture is already up and 30 HaT procs, 3 Slice and Dices and 1 Eviscerate for another 10 energy gone.

1040 / 31 (Hemorrhage cost + 2 energy to count for Eviscerate costs) = 33 (rounded down)
1040 - (31 * 33) = 17 left over energy.
Total of 33 Hemorrhages a minute

1040 / 39 ( 60% crit on Backstab reduces average cost by 3 energy, then adding 2 for Eviscerate costs) = 26
1040 - (26 *39) = 26 left over energy
Total of 26 Backstabs a minute

33 * 3.15 (Hemorrhage's new value) = 103.95 * 1.39 (Lethality bonus gives the 9, otherwise normal crit is the .3) = 144.49 weapon damage per minute
26 * 3.64 = 94.64 * 1.78 (Puncturing Wounds + Crit with Lethality) = 168.45 weapon damage per minute + (1634 * 26) * 1.78 = 75,621 bonus damage.

Hemorrhage gets an extra 7 combo points, so 1.4 Eviscerates. Even talenting out of Puncturing Wounds and into Ruthlessness and reglyphing Backstab into Shadowdance/Eviscerate wouldn't be worth the 23.96 normalized weapon damage + 75,621.

Looking at Hemorrhage glyph next.
