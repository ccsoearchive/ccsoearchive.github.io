The problem is that Hemorrhage with the bleed has crazy scaling in comparison, particulary with crit. Of course I don't know when exactly this occurs (yet, if I ever decide to calculate anything lol), but it will somewhere along the line and whether we get to that line or not is a different story.

No, having the 2pc should not change your rotation at all. Becuase everything costs less energy by the same proportion, the damage/energy for each ability will also change by the same proportion. As such, what ability is better overall will remain the same. The only time that it would be a DPS increase to use Ambush when you have 5 CP is if you are energy capped, since ambush would do more damage and the lower energy cost of eviscerate would be nulled by the fact that you would energy cap. Still, if this comes up, it would be better to use ShD at lower energy in order to avoid capping.

Edit: Alysrazor's air phase is the only time you will want to avoid eviscerates, since you want to use your most powerful ability nomatter what it is. My rotation is keep up SnD and Rupture (but refresh w/ evisc when it is almost over), then BS all day, mixed with spambush on CD.
