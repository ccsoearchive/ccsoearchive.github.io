The Phlogiston Brotherhood has come to Azeroth with the mission of making sure its adventurers have the finest of equipment to work with.

Professor Galahn Tinhelm is a gunsmith of some renown, capable of crafting almost any gun that can be made. His fine guns can take anywhere from a few hours to a week to craft, but the final products are always worth it, to have a high-quality, hand made Dwarven gun.

While other services are available, the Professor encourages the other members to mention here what those services are here.

Special orders are available, as well as guild orders. For contact, attune your hearthstone to the Market, or ask a member of the AAMS to send a message to Galahn Tinhelm.
