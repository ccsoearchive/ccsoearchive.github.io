I'd figure i would share the macros I use for PVP For aspect swapping(These were also on AJ.)

#showtooltip Aimed Shot!
/startattack
/castsequence reset=1 !Aspect of the Hawk, null
/cast Aimed Shot

When you get the proc for this, you have to stand still for .5 seconds like an old multishot or it wont go off.

#showtooltip Steady Shot
/startattack
/castsequence reset=1 !Aspect of the Fox, null
/cast Steady Shot

#showtooltip Chimera Shot
/stopcasting
/stopcasting
/startattack
/castsequence reset=1 !Aspect of the Hawk, null
/cast Chimera shot
