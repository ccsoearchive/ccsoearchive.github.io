The dull, albeit ceremonious, tolling of the Cathedral bells overhead was a welcome sound among the young and boisterous student body of Stormwind University, signifying the end of the day's class session. "Five bells already?" Amaranthaea muttered to herself as she'd raised her eyes slowly from the overwhelming stack of paperwork that loomed immeasurably atop her desk. Slowly, she returned her feather quill to the inkwell at her side, then closed a few folders and binders on her desk with a gentle sweep of her paw. Amara rose from her rather large, plush executive chair a bit languidly then made her way toward the immense bay window of the Dean's Office, overlooking the main quad of the University's campus. Amaranthaea couldn't help but smile despite her apparent exhaustion, watching as the students moved about the campus grounds while socializing, engaging in some minor horseplay and just blowing off the day's steam in general.

There was a stark pride in Amaranthaea's expression as she surveyed the mass exodus of the student body. Yet there was also a nagging tinge of fear that tugged at her every conscious thought. "These're my students now," she'd quietly pondered aloud. "I don't know that I'm fully ready for this, but what's done is done now, aye?" Amara turned sharply on point and glanced upward at a large, professionally painted portrait of their former Dean, Suzanna Marris. Amara smiled wistfully, nodding to the wise face of her good friend and former mentor. "I told ya not t' work yerself so damned hard, an' ya did anyway, luv. Never asked for help, even after I'd begged ya t' get an assistant. Bedridden an' gravely ill for two whole months 'cos o' that! Now yer off t' Northrend with naught but a letter as means o' farewell. Not before ya asked me to take the mantle o' Dean first, though. Before all o' this, I was merely a Professor 'round this University. I'd made Department Head o' the Sanctum for Healin' Arts soon afterward, aye. But this? Me, as Dean o' Students?! Sure glad YOU saw somethin' in me Suzy, 'cos I'm yet t' find it still, I think..."

Amaranthaea snickered lowly and shook her head a little. When first approached with the prospect of taking over as Dean of the University, she thought Dean Marris was playing a cruel trick on her; payback for all of the headaches Amara had caused Suzanna when she'd first arrived as an Adjunct Professor. However, once the sad reality of the situation had set in, Amara could think only of her friends and fellow colleagues, and the many students still avidly pursuing their education here at Stormwind University. She considered the strong sense of family and camaraderie that she'd exclusively found here. The sheer joy she'd felt each day, seeing the fascinated young faces of her own students as well as those of whom her colleagues were instructing. Sadly, there were some among their now former faculty who no longer shared in Amara's sentiment; A few had lost perspective or even hope in Dean Suzanna Marris' vision for the University. Others decided to pursue different, non-academic opportunities elsewhere. (Much of that also explained the mountains of long overdue and neglected paperwork now sitting atop Amaranthaea's desk, it begs to be noted!) Stormwind University remained, but for how long? Would this sudden and unforeseen lack of guidance and leadership see the University gates close for now a second time in Stormwindian history? Not if the newly appointed Dean, Amaranthaea Crowelley, and her remaining loyal faculty and staff had anything to say about it!

Amaranthaea turned once again to face the window of the office. The earlier bustling swell of students and faculty moving onto their extracurricular activities or making their way toward home had thinned out considerably by now. Amara folded her hands gently behind her back as she'd scanned the campus grounds once more and finally, fixed her gaze on the circular marble-hewn University Seal at the center of the quad. She thought fondly upon the great works that this University had done up to this point, and all of the impressive efforts and advances this fine institution of learning was yet to accomplish. It wouldn't be easy, but with hard work and a can-do attitude, Stormwind University would once again retain its esteemed reputation for academic excellence and maintain it from this day to forevermore.

(cont'd)

A sudden smile, warm and welcoming, spread across Amaranthaea's muzzle as her keen Worgen hearing told her of the approach of her newly appointed administration. She closed her eyes and allowed her mind to find its "peaceful place", accompanied by a gentle sigh born of a newfound serenity. Amara shifted into her Human guise mere moments before Professors Alandi Manablossom and Lhoira, and Dr. Pommie Tappet arrived at Dean Amaranthaea Crowelley's office door, each wearing their most encouraging and pleasant smiles. Not merely colleagues, they were dear friends all, ready to face this challenge head-on and see it through!

((Greetings, fellow Wyrmrest Accordians!

The "new and improved" <Stormwind University> remains a Med./Heavy RP guild, thematically representative of the foremost prestigious institution of higher learning in all of the Eastern Kingdoms! (Debatably, all of present day Azeroth in fact, but we digress! ;D ) We've recently undergone a positive shift in leadership within the span of a month, also adding new faculty - and subsequently, new curriculum! - to our numbers in the process. (We also proudly featured in Episode 47 of Wyrmcast on August 28th! https://soundcloud.com/wyrmcast/episode-47-stormwind-university-and-captain-happys-pom-pom-of-doom - Thanks again, Xortz!)

Stormwind University is currently open to all classes, races and backgrounds, although we do ask that all prospective members are able to reasonably articulate their character's involvement in an academic setting such as this. We proudly boast a friendly, inclusive, mature and supportive environment in our guild! Our current course offerings and curriculum range from the scientific (Biology, Engineering, etc.) to the magical (Healing Arts, Portal Magic, etc.) to the practical (Archaeology, History, etc.)! For our IC (and OOC) thespians out there, we've also most recently added a Performing Arts curriculum! And yes, that's right, we even offer coursework in Fashion Design, for all of the sharp dressers and fashionistas among our fine community!

(While we do have members among us that run *actual* open-to-the-public classes - such as "Pommie's Pet Battles" instructed by Dr. Pommie Tappet - please do note that we don't expect any of our members to run an actual in-game class if they don't want to. :D However, here at Stormwind University, we're all about personal choice and full roleplay options! Every member has the latitude and freedom to be as creative or laid back as they like!)

In addition to academic RP, we also offer plenty of D&D-esque action RP events - both large and small in scale and IC magnitude! - that entail the thrill of excitement and adventure for those so inclined! Despite being a collection of academicians and scholars in our respective fields ICly, many of our number do possess military and/or combat-oriented backgrounds, and are quite capable of defending themselves and those they care about! Especially for the sake of knowledge and academic advancement for all of Azeroth's enlightenment and benefit! ;)

We've also been hard at work (and play!) in getting to know many of our peers in the WrA community, and strive to continue to not only recruit new faculty, but also make lasting connections, friendships and even alliances among many individual RP'ers and entire guilds/organizations alike! Even if you're not a part of our University, our members all enjoy frequent walk-ups and open RP, so feel free to join us if you see us around! (We tend to congregate at the old Argent Dawn office buildings in the Cathedral District or the "Stormwind University Library" inside of Stormwind Keep! Or, after-hours, we can also be found at one of the fine drinking establishments throughout the City! :D )

We're also very receptive to outside plots and storylines if you have an idea and would like to involve our University. (ex. Your character might've found an old, strange looking idol or an indecipherable stone tablet in their travels. Why not have it looked at by a learned expert in such matters? :D ) Of course, we do ask that you approach us IC/OOCly via whisper or in-game mail first, so that we can better accommodate your idea!

If any or all of this sounds good and you'd like to join our faculty, or your interest is piqued and you'd like to know more, feel free to visit our guild website and have a further look!

www.stormwind-university.com

Here, you can get more information about us and what we do through our general access forums, and if you like what you see, feel free to fill out a very brief IC/OOC application for our consideration and review!

For any further comments and/or questions, please feel free contact myself (Amaranthaea) or any of my officers (Alandí [Alt + 0237], Lhoira or Pommie) via whisper or in-game mail!

THANK YOU for your time and interest, and we look forward to hearing from you soon! :D ))
