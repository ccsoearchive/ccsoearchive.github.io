Ok so my earlier post was a bit unkind. Now don't get me wrong, I do really hate raiding.
And yes, I have tried it. I raided extensively on my alliance druid (resto) at the beginning of Wrath. I have discussed this topic at length with a good friend, and believe me when I say I have a LONG list of reasons why I don't like raiding.

That being said, I do not disparage raiders their fun. Also this Dev team has made it pretty clear that they feel raiding is the way the game should be played, and they have never deviated from that. They have earned my never-ending disappointment for that narrow minded attitude. I still like the rest of the game, but I do truly believe that raiding the way WoW does it is an outdated design philosophy that needs to be re-examined.

So yes I hate raiding the activity. But I do not hate raiders as a community. I do very much wish the Devs would reconsider their stance on raiding in general, but I'm realistic enough to know they won't. This just serves to increase my intense dislike for raiding. Round and round we go!
