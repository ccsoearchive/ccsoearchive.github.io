We get new people in our very, very casual guild or people that haven't played in a while that haven't done any Cata raiding or just a couple bosses. We are still having a blast running these guys through to get gear and achieves and experience working with a raid group.

I know it's been nerfed and to many players it would be mind-bogglingly boring to go back to tier 11, but for my casual guild it is still a lot of fun. We did Cho'gall last night with 3 new players who had never done it before and they were so excited. One guy had never raided and he two healed Cho'gall with his poorly geared toon. He was whispering me all night afterwards about how awesome it was.

It's awesome when you see that excitement in a new player and you just know that they are totally hooked. Remember that feeling? Your first raid? The first time you downed an end boss?

Going back and getting the previous tier bosses and achieves with new players or casual non-raiders to me has been as satisfying as anything in the game recently.

Good luck to those that still need and want to do these bosses and get these achievements. I hope you find a fun PUG or raid group to run with.
