Ambiguous gearing question. I've recently hit 90 and am still feeling things out in terms of which spec I'm committed to. For the Engineering Helm, would it just be best to socket the cogwheels with Hit and Expertise until I'm settled on Assassination/Combat, or am I really just screwing myself over not taking a prime dps stat, i.e. haste/mastery?

You should only gem/enchant/socket for hit/expertise over mastery/haste if you fall short of the caps after reforging. Otherwise it's better to reforge lower stats to achieve the caps. The only exception is our cloak enchant, which is a choice between hit and crit, so there we take the hit since crit is valued so low.

Also, what should I be aiming to do right now? I'm not quite sure what to do for gear, just keep running lfr/world raids? (assuming im not doing 10m raids).

For this, the biggest thing is faction grinds (dailies). The golden lotus and klaxxi give rewards all the way to exalted, and the shado-pan and august celestials (unlocked by getting the golden lotus faction to revered) also give gear up through revered (their exalted rewards are more aesthetic, but still pretty cool).

All of that gear costs valor points though, so you'll want to plan your purchases slightly in advance. Then you've got LFR and dungeons, and the lower-ilvl justice point gear as filler. And crafted items, although the 476+ stuff is pretty pricy. Hope this helps a little, I should really finish those gear lists soon.
