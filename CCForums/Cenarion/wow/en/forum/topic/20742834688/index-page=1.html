Bella stood from her small desk hidden in her office with in the bakery. The Grand Opening of her bakery had went wonderful, and the final work and touches on the cafe was done. She held the posters and flyers under one arm as she made her way out of her office and into the main sitting room and counter area for the cafe. Her assistant Kali was hard at work taking orders and handing out boxes and bags of pastries.

The room was thick with the scent of sweetness, the glass counters displayed their goods. Pie freshly baked lay on shelves lined along the back wall behind the counters. While on top of the glass covered counters, trays of freshly baked cookies of all flavors and colors are lined. Resting upon soft lavender cloth. The whole bakery and cafe was done in a softer warm tone of lavender, with white and gold trimming along the upper walls and around the flooring. The flooring was done in a deep oak wood.

Tables spread through out the center of the cafe, each with two to three chairs around them. The table cloth was a soft cream color, with lavender trimming. A soft breeze flowed through the building, both windows opened for the afternoon cool air. After finishing up some last moment things, she steps out of the bakery and cafe and into the busy streets of Stormwind. The new flyers and posters where done, and she had a busy day of handing them out a head of her.

*Flyers are seen through out all major cities. Done in a lavender back ground, written in perfect hand writing done in gold lettering*

New Location :: Fluffy Bun's Baker and Cafe is located in *Just Maces" in the Cathedral Square.

Hours of Operation -

Friday - Saturday :: 3 PM to 6 PM

Fluffy Bun's Bakery is now pleased to offer catering services for weddings and special events. As a professional and full service catering bakery, our specialties include galas and grand openings, fundraisers, weddings and social events. And lots more!
