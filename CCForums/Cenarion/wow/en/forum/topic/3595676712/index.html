There are seemingly only two options to spend talent points in other than imp renew:

Divine Fury - Reduce casting time of heal, greater heal, smite, holy fire by .30...doesn't seem all that useful. I usually can't hard cast...and when I can I'm usually not under huge pressure where the increased casting speed would help a lot.

Reflective Shield - Honestly I do like this talent in some situations...but in the typical scenario where a DK is sitting on me doing huge DPS it's not going to accomplish anything and the loss of HPS from imp renew will hurt.
