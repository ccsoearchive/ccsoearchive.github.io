A few things to bring up about warriors
1. Single Stance combat
2. Defensive capability
3. Mobility
4. [tid bit] Revenge damage [tid bit]

1. Single Stance combat
Stance Dancing was fun, have we thought about bringing it back for arms?
In most cases, arms will only change stances for Disarm, and Berserker for Whirlwind but do we really use whirlwind? Even for PVE I don't think so. (And that's it)

2. Warriors used to have great defense for ranged and melee
Warrior defensive abilities:
  • A. Shield Block B.Shield Wall and C.spell Reflect.

    • Are currently greatly suited for protection (Trust me, i'm prot geared and prot specced) But this leaves fury and arms in the dust due to the fact that prot warriors have around 40% block chance. That said,
  • A. Shield Block gives an additional 25% block chance (So that's 65% block chance for prot! Discluding Critical Blocks.

  • B. Shield Wall is really only used to take a less damage during enemies big cooldowns, which unfortunatly gimps our damage as well. Unlike say Survival Instincts or Ice Bound Fortitude.

  • C. Spell Reflect, nothing wrong with it, it's just the ten second CD was nice.

    • So it's not worth stance dancing for as arms like in Wrath. Swifty does it a lot in this video: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vFQl7JStwu4

    SO! Could we see Shield Block and Shield Wall being useful for nonprot? Using either the two will reduce our damage as well unlike say Survival Instincts (Just a side note, Feral has great tanking potential)
    3. Mobility
    Arms has to be baby sat, agreed?
    Half the time we're chasing our target around while our healers are spamming dispels, and our charges getting jumped. It's silly really to make warriors a wheel barrow. Now i'm not gonna sound like a broken record player with the charge issue, you guys are working on it and finding possible solutions to it. That said, though intercept is inferior to the space bar, could we see it back on arms warriors' action bars? (More stance dancing?)
    (It's okay to have a little self sufficiency.)

    4. Since our damage is reduced while Shield wall and Shield block are up, is there a possibility that Revenge damage could be effected from a talent in arms (new? or possibly added in) (Remember Unrelenting assault?)? :P. And it would also bring back the old defensive stance, shield block combo to tank something out. (Like in these two of swifty's videos:
    http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O4z70qf3fOE
    http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CoEQrIfRLeE
    It just seems right to go defensive as arms
