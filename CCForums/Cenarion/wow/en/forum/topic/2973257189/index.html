I would like to know what you guys plan on doing about hunters?

As a high rated player in arena and RBGs on multiple hunters, I'm not alone when I feel that our class as a whole just doesn't fit well in either environment. Unless you are a monster at this game in general, it is much easier to be replaced in any comp Arena or RBG to yield better results.

As posted in many other threads through numerous other High rated hunters (including some of the worlds top hunters and top players of other classes), this class under performs in PvP.

There are many ways this can be fixed without effecting PvE through use of our glove bonus, 4 set, or by taking a low PvE dps spec and outfitting it with better PvP mechanics. Steady Shot cast times, and application of such in a PvP environment. Traps also come to mind. There could also be changes through our required clunky new Aspect Dancing to further help our PvP performance.

I believe I speak for most of the high rated hunter community when I express this opinion, and I'm fairly well known on these forums for being as unbiased as one can be when playing a class.

Are there any plans to give hunters help in a state of the game they are lacking in? If so, would you kindly give us some hints to further ease our minds?
