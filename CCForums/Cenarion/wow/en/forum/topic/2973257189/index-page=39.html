Hows it going Zarhym? Did you lose some weight. =3

Anyway, a couple things that should be looked at in pvp in my opinion.

1. Rogues. Having played a rogue in pvp for the last couple seasons i've noticed that while a Sub Rogue has pretty good burst damage while CDs are up, their overall damage is pretty low compared to others. So trying to run Sub rogue with a healer class tends to make matches drag on. While I may not be extremely good or anything, I still do not like sitting in an arena match for 45 minutes cause they were able to survive my CDs with their CDs. This kinda does not apply when your running with a Disc priest. Since smite/holy fire = win. Also I use to love playing Assassination, but having a lack of mobility kinda hurt that spec in pvp a great deal. Would be nice if Assassination could get some love.

2. Feral Druids. I've come across to many Feral Druids that can just eat through me. Whether i play on my rogue or warrior. Barkskin is up so often, Survival Instincts while still being able to put out hard damage. then Bear Form and Enrage Regen. I had a Feral Druid in normal 371 pvp gear Crit my Warrior for 20k in Bear for with mangle. And i have around 4k resilience. Overall I think they have to many ways to save themselves and put out some pretty nasty damage.

3. Death Knights. I've always hated Death Knights since they basically have every ability in the game. Maybe only missing a few. Most DKs play frost now in pvp cause they can hit a lot harder then Unholy. Since they play frost they get the Chilbains talent. Which allows their Chains of Ice to STOP the target for 3sec. Now i have to talent into Improved hamstring to be able to stop someone, but it has a 30 second internal CD, and i have to be in melee range to use it. A Dk can basically spam chains of ice, while the stop has its own diminishing returns, it still seems a little unfair that a DK can almost spam slow someone then only have to keep Frost Fever on the target to slow them. I have to use a GCD to either hamstring or piercing howl. Kinda not balanced. This goes along side with the Feral Druid thing. Icebound Fort. Can pop it, take a lot of reduced damage and not be stunned. On top of that. Using Lichborne to death coiling themselves to heal themselves, and summoning their ghoul and consuming it to regain health. Well. I have Rallying Cry and Enrage Regen...... O and shield wall, but i have to equip a shield and lower my damage output. Again I never personally liked DKs.

4. Warriors. We lost some mobility when intercept was put on the same CD as charge. I'm fine with that. We kinda hit pretty hard. But against most other classes we just fall short. While the damage is nice, we really only have 3 ways of healing ourselves. Blood Craze, Second Wind, and Enrage Regen. Enrage Regen is nice, but its a 3 minute CD. Blood Craze is only 3% of your total HP so if i had 100k HP then it would only heal me for 3k. A simple melee swing would do more damage then that. Second Wind only comes into effect when I'm stunned or immobilized, and that is only 5% of my total HP over 10 seconds. So basically, 500 hp every second (if my math is correct). Overall our damage is pretty nice, but our survivability is lacking.

I could probably go on about something about every class and spec, but these were my biggest concerns. On that note I want to add. While AV was fun for a while, and i've heard stories of the 3 day long AVs back in Vanilla, Its kinda grow a little dull. There is no fighting in the middle, very few collecting bloods and armor. Its turned into an "Okay who can cap towers faster and kill a boss!" type of game. Kinda the same way with Isle of Conquest. Its always just a race to see who can get into the keep first and kill a boss. No one attempts to wipe the opposing teams raid, its just "O well the alliance broke our gate first. Guess we lose!" Those battlegrounds normally make me want to afk through them or not even pvp at all.

Well that is all I can think of at this time. If you read this thanks for reading.

O and HEALERS CAN HEAL FINE NOW. MANA ISN'T AN ISSUE ANYMORE. PUT MORTAL STRIKE BACK TO 50%.
or atleast 25%. Seen way to many healers just Nature Swiftness or Fast Cast heal someone for their entire HP. (Sorry about the Caps, the NS full heal thing is kinda annoying lol.)
