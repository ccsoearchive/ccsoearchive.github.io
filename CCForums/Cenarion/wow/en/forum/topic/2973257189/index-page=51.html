I would just like to see retribution get away from this waiting game and into a more active playstyle, because that is certainly more fun and engaging. At the very least, give us more than 2 active buttons to press lol (Holy Wrath doesn't count either, in fact, you may want to revise that whole ability, I don't even know why we have it really).

Zarhym,

I am grateful that you are taking the time to chat with those of us who prefer the PvP aspect of WoW.

My question is one regarding overall design philosophy and may not be representative of the community as a whole. It also contains undertones of class warfare; sorry bout that =(

Why does the development team want to shy away from the split role approach to skills/talents that we saw used with Colossus Smash for Warriors? This approach has been used in the past for CC skills (sap for example) where the skill will do one thing to a mob and do another to a player. It's really hard to not feel frustrated when there is a class balance made to improve PvE balance that then upsets PvP balance. This fosters animosity between the two communities.

From what I understand, PvE is as balanced as it has ever been, and that's great! We want to see the PvP content (Arenas in particular) experience the same kind of evolution. I understand that we are a small part of the consumer base, we just don't like feeling like nobody cares about the competitive aspect of WoW.
