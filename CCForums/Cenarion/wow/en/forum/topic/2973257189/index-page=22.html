Assuming this isn't a troll thread (but ghostcrawler taking a look at lowbie pvp makes me wonder)

I mainly play resto druids so that's what my input will be. I hear resto druid healing is good in pve so my buffs will mainly be focused on survivability/utility. I DO NOT BELIEVE ALL CHANGES SHOULD GO LIVE, just throwing them out there.


Needed
1.Bring back the old natural perfection. 0/3 Increases all spell critical strike chances by 1/2/3% and critical strikes agaisnt you give a buff that reduces damage taken by by 1/2/3% Stacking to 3. I find it strange the old version mirrored priests' inner will in wrath of the lich king while their new version is -20% when stacked while ours got sacked

2. Bring back improved Barkskin, 0/2 Increase armor by 80/160% while in travelform and not shifted. Also increases the damage reduction by 5/10% and reducing chance it's dispelled by 40/80%. Dispell protection is outdated and +armor % is high but the fact the ruthless shield has more armor than my current gear, I really feel it's needed.

3. Bring back the push back reduction talent Natures Focus. 0/3 Reduces the chance your spell will be pushed back by 25/40/70% when casting wrath/regrowth/starfire/tranqulity/nourish/cyclone/entangling roots. I find balance needs this as well especially with the amount of face tanking you do as a moonkin

4. Tree of life being a debuff that allows the use of tree of life for X seconds. Kind of like how avenging wings allows the use of hammer of wrath regardless of health state. This will do wonders when your in treeform and need to avoid manaburns/snares without wasting your cooldown. Right now recklessness and tree of life are the only buffs I can think of that are meant to be good but can put you in a worse state than before you used it.

5. Natures swiftness a 2 minute cooldown. Small change but it will do wonders when conserving cooldown useage so you aren't caught without and defensive abilties.

Optional

1. Balance of power increasing all hit instead of just spell hit. Bash misses are game breaking especially against dk/ferals.

2. I've heard people suggest cyclone being a different tree but I think that would be overpowered. I suggest a damage spell like mindspike/lava burst that would allow some pressure without being susceptible to a full nature lock out (lol wrath)

3. Slight regrowth buff so enemy spell casters actually consider kicking it. Said I wouldn't suggest healing increases but a lot of times I get the feeling people let me cast it unless the person im healing actually drops below 60%


Thanks for taking the time to listen to my concerns
