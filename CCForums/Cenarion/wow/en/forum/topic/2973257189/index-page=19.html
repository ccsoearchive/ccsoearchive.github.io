Team Rating / MMR Rating Needs to be Reset.
The hotfix that took place a few weeks ago regarding MMR was awesome and much needed. Unfortunately, now there is a SEVERE rating deflation - to the point that there is literally 0 incentive for high rated teams to play (as unless they win 100% of their matches, they are going to tank rating). Unless team rating / MMR is reset, the season might as well be over, as the brackets are not going to move between now and the end of the season. If you are not going to reset them, at least do us a favor - end the season and let us start again.

Neglected Moonkin
I love playing a balance druid. I have for years. I'm nothing amazing (~2300 in wotlk playing moonkin/rogue), but I have a pretty good idea on how to play the spec. Unfortunately, moonkin are currently horrible, and it is really taking it's toll on me. It is to the point where I don't really even log on any more.

The only thing that a moonkin can do better than a mage/lock/s.priest is placing solar beam in rated BGs, which most competitive PvPers don't even care about. Outside of that, there is literally 0 reason to bring a moonkin. Our damage is horrible because we cant cast due to the myriad of gap closers and interrupts. Lunar shower was nerfed, so now we can't even run around spamming moonfire (which wasn't at all fun, though it was mildly effective). I am feeling extremely neglected.

Honestly, it's more the little things that are getting to me even more than the "this spec just doesn't feel designed to succeed in PvP." The "this is a nerf because of resto/feral, too bad moonkin - you get no compensation. Examples include the shifting roots nerf (why didn't moonkin get disentanglement like resto?), the -20% lifebloom nerf, the elimination of instant roots, the list goes on and on. I have about a million ideas on how to help fix moonkin PvP, but I'm not a dev, so what I say really doesn't matter. But here are some of my ideas.

1. We need a real defensive cooldown. Barkskin just doesn't cut it at this point. It needs to be an "oh shi-" move a-la ice block, dispersion, etc.

2. We need to be able to actually use our healing spells. A balance druid in caster form is the squishiest target in game. Even when we take the time to use our heals, they are extremely ineffective and take way too long to do anything. WHY do we not have a flash heal / healing surge / flash of light equivolent? Every time I see a shadow priest crit themselves for 30k with a flash heal, while I crit for 9k with a regrowth, it just kills me a little inside.

3. Solar beam needs to have a shorter duration and cooldown. When it happens (which isn't very often) a cyclone->cyclone->cyclone->solarbeam->root->root->root->cyclone->cyclone->cyclone is just silly and should not be possible.

4. We need a way to quickly get to Eclipse. Often a match is already decided by the time I can even start doing damage (because it takes so long to reach Eclipse).

5. Too much of our damage is tied up in to Eclipse. I feel like I'm doing no damage during an Eclipse, and too much damage during Eclipse. Damage outside of Eclipse should be buffed, and the Eclipse buff % should be nerfed.

6. The removal of instant roots was one of the worst and most devastating kicks in the nuts that I have ever gotten while playing this game. As a ranged spec, I am literally unable to peel melee off of myself. How do you expect me to do damage if I can't peel off of myself?

Weapon Gating
I know that by the end of the season it's not a big deal, but why do you insist on gating PvP weapons? Your argument of "well it will make the PvErs feel like they need to PvP to get weps" can be easily turned around. "Well it MAKES PvPers feel like they need to PvE to get weps during the first 2 months of the season." When during the second week of the PvP season, we are facing people with ilvl 391 heroic PvE weapons while being unable to acquire our own, it does nothing but reinforce the feeling of being a second class citizen.

I could keep on going, but this is already at the tldr point... My point is that some specs have nearly always been, and continue to be, flat out horrible for PvP. This is unacceptable at this point of the game to me, and I think it should be a high priority.

Also, PLEASE review frost mage / warlock / resto shaman strength. It's ludicrous how long these classes have been OP.
