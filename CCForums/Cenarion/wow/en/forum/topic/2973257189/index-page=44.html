Hello :)
I'd like to add my two cents in:
Please improve the design of the battlegrounds, specifically Isle of Conquest and Strands of the Ancients- they are both similar to scripted PVE encounters. If I wanted to fight a wall, and down a boss to win a game, I'd que for a random dungeon or raid. When it comes to Player vs Player, I would really like to sink my teeth in some actual combat- not fighting a wall.

Add new battlegrounds, perhaps? Or more world PVP? I know you guys have dedicated yourselves to putting out excellent raids and dungeons, but since Cataclysm has come out there have been numerous dungeon and raid patches, but a serious lack of battleground ones.

---

My opinion on Restoration shaman qqs:
Windshear- Yea, it's annoying in PVP. 6 second cool down, but only interrupts casting spells by 2 seconds. Feral Skull Bash- Interrupts, and increases mana cost of spells for x seconds. There's also plenty of other ranged classes who have 1 or more silences, and other annoying things. Perhaps increase the CD of Windshear? But I find that's what made Shamans unique, and there's many abilities out there that makes a class special. Having a casting class that can interrupt on a fairly regular basis is awesome. It's just like how Warlocks have their fears, and how Rogues have that lovely Recuperate.

---
My QQ:

The passive regeneration of Water Shield is only a little over 500 mana per 5 seconds, even when glyphed (Prime Glyph). In order to survive in PVP, I find I'm using ES more than WS when it comes down to being focused down (makes sense ;P). This brings up the Restoration talent called 'Resurgance' which grants x mana when you crit on x heal- problem? PVP gear rarely crits, and even when gemming for intel+resil or intel+crit, you don't receive a lot of returns from the ratio of heals that crit, to those that don't. It's especially noticeable when you're trying to keep up 1 target that's being focused down by many other players in RBGs, even when you have WS on.

Mana Tide- I don't think the nerf was needed. I hardly noticed a big return of mana when it was at 400% of spirit (especially since I'd use it when I was OOM, and still actively casting?), and even less now that it's at 200%. Note that water shield still only gives passive 500+ mana regenerates per 5 seconds (around 800+ every so often when getting hit). "Providing too much for the raid" was a bad excuse to nerf that, and Innervate from Feral Druids. Also note: spirit on pvp gear is lackluster. Whether it be in a gem, or on the gear itself.

I'm seriously jealous of Paladins who, after three charges of Holy Power, can use a heal (that crits) for no mana. They can also Judge repeatedly in order to gain back mana through, I think, the release of a Seal (of Insight?). >:P
