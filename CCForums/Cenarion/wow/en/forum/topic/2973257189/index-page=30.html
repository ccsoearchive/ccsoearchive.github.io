Overall, the game needs more PvP content. You spoke for us and said that we'd rather have better balanced content then more content, but I would disagree and say were not getting either.

Class balance is really bad ie healers are too strong and too many interupts for caster dpsers, maps are still unbalanced (AB still favors horde with its LM/BS/farm combo of death), AND we haven't gotten any real PvP content in a real long while. When I see the insane amount of content PvE'ers get every patch, It gets discouraging especially when all I get is the another full set to grind in order to not fall behind. And theres nothing unique about the next set I have to get, its just higher stats, while raiders get entirely new gameplay mechanics from set pieces occationally.

Rated BG's dont really solve anything like they were supposed to. Having perfect comps are still too much of an issue and theres a ridiculous amount of set up involved to get just one game going.

Also, I wish there wasn't such a huge gear gap for pvper's. 90% of the time its gear>skill because of that fact. I dont mind that theres always something to get, but the difference is pretty absurd.

another complaint is that once you get your honor set, theres very little reason to do anything but farm CP, which takes almost no time at all to cap each week, then theres not much to do for the rest of the week. maybe if there was more to do in regular bg's, more unique rewards that dont make me super OP, but something useful or interesting to give me something to work towards.

overall, there needs to be a significant more content for pvpers, and hopefully more stuff that brings us out into the open world, rather then going into instanced zones. the pvp game just needs more diversity.

Hi Zarhym,

I won't get too bogged down in class balance specifics, rather I'll provide some general issues that are prevalent in PvP at the moment.

2v2 Arena - Currently healing is too high in this bracket leading to long games. Now simply buffing damage/ nerfing healing would be disastrous for the 3v3 bracket.

Solution: Introduce a damage taken debuff that stacks over time to force players to use their cooldowns effectively as early as possible

Rated battlegrounds & Holy Paladins - Sorry, I have to pick out a class here. Due to exceptional cooldowns such as HoP, LoH and bubble Holy Paladins have an unfair advantage in RBGs are you are severely limited if you don't bring at least one. (Bring the player not the class should apply for rated battelgrounds)


Some abilities that could use revising for PvP:

Desecration (5-10 second internal cooldown is needed, it makes peeling Deathknights near impossible and requires little conscious effort by them)

Feral Cyclone (make it either do a finisher or give an instant spell in PvP, currently ferals do not need to trade off their damage for CC)

Mana Burn (This ability needs a cool down)

Blazing speed and Impact (Frost mages shouldn't be able to pick up both these talents, it is obviously over kill for the spec)

Unstable Affliction (Locks seem to be at a point where they make very little conscious effort to dispel protect a target, rather just throw their dots up on everyone then rotate fears between people. Revise spell to have 1 target and 2 targets if applied through soul swap.)

Rogue, deathknight and feral damage are all too high in general for the relevant amount of utility given to these classes.

Thanks for reading,

I am really looking forward to some changes to the current state of PvP
