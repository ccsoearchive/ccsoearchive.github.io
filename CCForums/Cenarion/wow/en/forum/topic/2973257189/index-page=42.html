Here is the blunt truth about Low-Level PvP :

The Burst - Healing and Damage are too high
Caster BoAs - The spell power given by BoAs is to high
BoA Weapons - BoAs are almost always the best item you could possibly have
Skills - Some Classes get shafted at lower levels for abilities

How to Fix:
____________________________________________________________________________

The Broad Approach - Separate PvE and PvP Damage
This would be a massive undertaking but in the end would really benefit both
the PvE and PvP communities. No more worrying about messing an ability up in PvE
due to a PvP chance or vise versa

____________________________________________________________________________


The Burst - Tone down healing, absorbs and damage and possible Resilience scaling change due to the lower Damage/Healing
Damage is too high, the change to 200% Critical Heals have helped with this
imbalance but it is heading in the wrong direction, excessive burst is something that
makes PvP hard to enjoy

BoAs in General - Damage done while using BoAs in PvE increased by X% per piece would compensate for loss of damage in leveling. Reduce the stats on BoAs.

Skills - Gap closers are the biggest concern for Melee, Class specific dispels should be given at low levels for all classes that receive them (possibly Lvl10)

Specific Skills:
Hammer of Justice - a 6 Second stun (Far too long at lower levels)
Fix: Scale it according to level - 1 second per 25 Levels
Lvl10 - 3 Seconds///Lvl35 - 4 Seconds///Lvl60 - 5 Seconds///Lvl85 - 6 Seconds

Healing Surge - a fast large heal for Shamans learned at Lvl20
Fix: Learn this spell at 16 - Shamans only have the slow efficient healing spell until
20 making it very difficult to heal in PvE or PvP

Intercept - a Warriors in-combat charge learned at Lvl50
Fix: Learn this at a lower level - The biggest problem for warriors at low levels is the
lack of even a single gap closer while in combat

Outdated Skills ? - A specific example of this is "Track Hidden" which grants a Stealth Detection
Value of 30. Nothing comes close to this and is really overpowered, should be changed to a lower value

Thank you for taking the time to read this, also keep and eye out for a thread detailing a list of changes that can help with the problems faced in lower levels by people that play and care for a more balanced PvP experience.

P.S. If anything at all can be taken from this post is reduce the amount of damage in PvP at lower levels.

Thank you :)
