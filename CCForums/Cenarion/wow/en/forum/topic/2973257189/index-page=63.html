Well after much thought about what i feel most strongly about when it comes to pvp, i've decided to make this post about something you're not very likely to reply to, but I think it is an important topic nonetheless.

I really believe the best thing for this game as a whole, would be to change the way abilities act in pvp vs. pve. I understand its been suggested and somewhat shot down already. I dont remember the exact quote but for what i do remember it was explained that it could be too confusing for some players. Really i just dont see much weight in this arguement. There are ways to make tooltips explain things effectivly, and if it were to become the general rule of thumb with all abilities, instead of just certain ones as it is now, there would eventually be enough information about it available that if anyone was confused, they could be corrected by their peers. This has most certainly been the case for almost all pvp experience for me, i didnt learn to pvp because blizz made it easy to learn. I learned through talking about it with friends and pvp forums/videos.

Honestly this one change, though i understand it is not a small one, would open this game up to so much more cool stuff being available to both pvp and pve. So many problems would be cleared up in one fel swoop.

Now I also understand that blizz is in this to make money, and i dont blame the ppl working on this game for having limited resources. I'm sure if it was up to you guys you would have every bug fixed and balance change made to make this game as good as it possibly can be. So I understand that just from a logisical stand point it may be hard to justify something that requires so much of your resources to accomplish, however I feel like in the long run this change would make the game so much better that it is beyond worth it. Imagine not having to make drastic changes to abilities every other patch in order to maintain some semblance of balance. While i do think there would still be some small balance issues in pvp with this change i feel that the solutions for these problems would be much more direct (I.E. X class is not an effective healer in pvp but good in pve, well now you can simply change the pvp numbers with no worrys on pve balance.)


Well thanks alot for your time man, really appreciate you making an effort like this. And I apologize for all the rants and re-posts, including my own, but between the lack of pvp input from blizz lately, and a few lapses in communication regarding patches, we are like starving cubs clawing at their mother's teets, and your the only teet thats giving out any milk.
