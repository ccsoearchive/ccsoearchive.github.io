A guildie signed on for the first time all summer. He spent some honor two s10 pieces, had a few s9, and we made an arena team. It was slow going at first, we got our 8 wins in about 25 games (no prior experience in arena between us, really).

We had a blast. And I got the S10 Conquest helm.

Why is there no equivalent method of small teams getting into RBG? I often "lead" random BG, and thought it isn't measured I post about an 80% win rate in AB and 60% or so for the other skirmish maps (WSG, TP, BfG, EotS). 1) Can individuals finally queue for Rated BG?
2) What if the player base was willing to say "minimum resil to queue"?
3) What if all randoms were rated, for a lower conquest point reward? say 200 conquest points for a random que RBG and 400 conquest points for a premade RBG?
