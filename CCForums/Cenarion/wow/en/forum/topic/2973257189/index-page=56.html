Affliction warlocks are close to the right place at present, it's just the synergy with resto shaman that's the problem. If you're going to nerf resto shaman, please do it in a way that doesn't screw over (or maybe even buffs?) enhancement and elemental.

Now, lock pvp:
Searing pain needs to be castable while moving, I don't care if that's aping fire mages it's a good idea and will bring use to a presently crap ability.
Immolate needs dispel protection, the best solution I can think of is a buff that increases immolate damage by 40% for 15 seconds, stacking up to 5 times whenever it is dispelled. It means you can still interrupt a lock's burst by dispelling but it'll mean his target takes more damage overall, making dispelling still viable but an interesting tactical choice.
Chaos bolt needs to ignore everything. It needs to ignore resilience, resistances, absorbtion, immunity, spell damage reduction, chance to miss everything. That's the point of chaos damage, it'll make chaos bolt actually feared in pvp without being overpowered (chaos bolt only hits about as hard as incinerate).
Shadowflame and shadow burn need to do shadowflame damage. Given their names I thought this would be obvious, but having destruction give a spell that doesn't scale with the innate bonus or mastery is really silly.

Affliction: Single target damage slightly too low, multi target damage slightly too high. Make unstable affliction only affect one target (two with soul swap) and make it always double drain soul's damage when used on players.

All warlock specs: No oh !@#$ button, passive survival a little high. Drop soul link back to 15% but cause it to transfer an extra 60% of damage for 8 seconds when the warlock's health falls below 35%, 45 second cooldown.

I HAVE FIXED EVERYTHING FOREVER.
