My guild leader, an avid and diehard Survival hunter, and one of the most loyal fans of WoW, has finally withdrawn from the game due to exhausted frustration. He believes most of the best talents from Survival have been given to Marksman, and that the best way for Survival to be competitive is to enable some kind of stun or interrupt to give them a chance against healers. I will try to coax him into writing a reply with his suggestions.


That would be me. Thank you for pointing this thread out to me Sam. Very brief backstory, I've been survival since 2.4 and quit a few months ago after being practically forced into marks.

What it boils down to is that marks gets to cherry pick all of the best from both survival and beast mastery, and gives nothing back in return other than a minor focus cost reduction. Abilities like posthaste should be able to be taken by all three specs.

All other pures (and most hybrids) have this luxury. Be it quickening or elusiveness for rogues. Imp. counterspell, impact, improved blink ect for mages. Second wind and blood craze for warrior. The list goes on. All three specs benefit from each other and the top two tiers are improvements to baseline abilities with hunters having no such luck (or disguised luck (i'm looking at your improved steadyshot).

I can't speak much for beast mastery so I'm going to focus on survival:

1) Either a rework or a removal of black arrow, for the high focus cost and low damage and the fact there is no penalty for dispelling it. The long shared cooldown is salt in the wound.

2)A baseline interrupt is needed. Be it pet or silencing shot made baseline. Let marks (or better yet everyone) talent into the silence. That or lower the pet interrupt cooldowns and extend the duration to 4-5 seconds and link them with other pet interrupts and silencing shot to prevent abuse.

3)Survival needs a way to get health back be it from black arrow (could give the full heal if dispelled) or by way of explosive shot.

4) Please do something with mirrored blades, it's a nice talent but needs something. Perhaps along the lines of cooldown reduction for every spell reflected?

5)Have immolation trap take over explosive's job and make explosive a small knockback Ala that daily in sholazar.

I'd come back in a heartbeat if half of these suggestions were implemented.

Thank you for your time.
