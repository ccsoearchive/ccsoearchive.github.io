Season 9 was completely invalidated by MMR system abuse and glad selling, and Season 10 is going to be completely invalidated by a lack of reset. Not resetting MMR has created a serious issue in arenas. There are players at ratings that are currently unreachable, and people who actually have really high personal rating are not going to queue until they are sure they are queuing with others who have the same.

The new system fixes the problem, but only if the inflated players are forced to play or have their MMR lowered or reset.

The reset could be anounced well in advance. Claim you are doing it a month before the season ends (or something along those lines.) People actively queue near the end of the season anyway to make sure they keep their spot, and it wouldn't interfere with gearing much at that point. On top of that people only use RBGs to gear now because arena is highly ineffective unless you are just trying to reduce the number of RBGs you are forced in to playing under the current system.

---

For more of a question, are there any plans to further modify the matchmaking system? At lower ratings gaining MMR can be brutal now, even with ~80% win rate we only went from 2120->2150 MMR over the course of 40 games. It seems like arenas are trying to aggressively match against lower rated players far too quickly, which was a matchmaking change that was meant for RBGs where it makes more sense.
