I play a shadow priest, and for the most part I feel rather well balanced with everyone else except melee. Melee feels like they do more damage, in weaker gear then what I have. Is this just my perception or is this in fact something that's by design?

Another thing that annoys me, and mind you I play a priest, is mana burn. I feel no one spell should be able to change the outcome of a fight by being spammed. Sure it can be interupted, and LOS, but point being, there's no other spell in the game that can either be spammed or if it is being spammed doesn't drastically change fight in favor of the spammer. It's not that I don't like the idea of it per say, as in change targets to the spammer and stop him. It's that it has such no brainer spell to cast. It takes way more skill to stop being mana burned then to cast/spam the mana burn itself. I just feel it needs cooldown of some sort, or have a cost to use it, or be a stacking debuff. As of now, priests typically mana burn as the go to spell. As long as their team mates peel people of the priest, that's half to most what the priest will cast until the enemy mana user is out of mana. Is this intent of the spell to be so indirectly damaging to the enemy team if they don't stop it?
