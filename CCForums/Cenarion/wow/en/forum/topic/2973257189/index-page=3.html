Starting at a completely random place:

1. Healers can survive too much damage, and too many classes have silences/interrupts. That may seem like a contradiction, but the truth is, if you're stuck with a group of people who don't have the ability to time their interrupts well enough, you can't kill a good/geared healer.

2. There really needs to be a better selection of low level pvp gear. Right now you can buy a couple blues every 10 levels, most of which is terrible (bad stats, or wrong stats). Having pvp gear that could compete with heirloom gear would be great.

3. Flight points to battleground vendors (or put those vendors in the main cities).

4. AV needs a change. The towers need to become more easily retaken. As it stands. Putting 5 people in a tower who can hit a cleave button virtually guarantees no recaps.

5. The level restriction on buying gear should be removed, at least for gear below level 85. My level 23 pvp hunter already has enough honour to buy level 70 gear. Which I'd like to do, so it's in the bank when I get there.
