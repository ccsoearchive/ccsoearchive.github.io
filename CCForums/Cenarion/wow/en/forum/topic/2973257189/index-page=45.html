I doubt this will be seen so far down, but I'll try anyway.

I'd like to see elemental become more useful in any pvp scene. Both the mana, and use of spells, and ability to survive, with more cc than simply hex and thunderstorm. I'd like to see earthquake be made useful in pvp, (and pve for that matter)

I posted this in another thread for ideas.

Suggested Changes:

Rolling thunder has been increased to 5 % mana return

Thunderstorm Mana return increased to 16 %.

These changes are small and I see no reason they could not be hotfixed. They also have very little impact on pve.


You make a very good point from a pvp perspective, and I would certainly love it if elemental could work better in pvp, especially considering that's the very reason I leveled this guy in BC.

I genuinely want elemental to be a good pvp spec, but these changes will be counter productive for pve in the eyes of Blizzard.

Dps classes are meant to manage a resource to maximize dps. (Some less than others true) Mana is a little sticky in that regard strictly because it lasts so long. For elemental though, they broke it, and made it more than easy to maintain a full mana bar while dps'ing, and that's without EVER using thunderstorm.

So while I agree mana is a major problem in pvp, and we need several pvp fixes, your suggested change would in fact be counter productive for pve because we would have to manage our mana even less, and Blizzard wants the dps resource to be managed even more.

That being said, Thunderstorm is nearly useless in pve. Earthquake is entirely useless in any serious pvp.


Now, considering that Thunderstorm is the signature spell of elemental, and earthquake is the 31 talent, it would stand a reason that these should in fact be the most significant spells in the elemental arsenal. They need to be alted and improve their usefulness in the style they lack in. IE Thunderstorm made more useful in pve, and Earthquake more useful in pvp.

This is a pvp related topic, but I'll provide my own little solution for both.
Elemental sucks for aoe simply because of the massive ramp up times for flame shock.
Thunderstorm should have a cooldown reset somehow, via talents, or something, and possibly without the knockback, I dunno of the details. But a simple cooldown reset by proc, whether by damage taken, or in conjunction with another spell, should be very useful in maintaining some sustained aoe dps.

Earthquake should also be changed to apply flame shock to all targets, have an initial semi heavy damage added if they insist on having a cast time longer than 1.5 seconds, in addition to a stun, but you could increase the cooldown too. The initial damage could be around 7-10k depending on existing spell power, and be mitigated by armor since it would be physical damage. This would be amazing for pvp, and pve.
(Of course fire nova would need a nerf, but that's okay. Buff its damage via enhancement talents that elemental cannot reach)

The limit of creativity and unique play is beyond question. It's entirely possible if given some thought. As for the question of being OP? Well, ranged classes in pvp can nuke anyway. So don't anyone pretend like elemental is the exception.

It could also be that if earthquake was first cast, the shaman would gain a "ground shield" or buff of some sort related to the earth that either made him take less damage (10-20%?) for 7 seconds, or absorbed an amount possibly equal to the damage done, even to all targets, or just the highest. Intent to keep elemental alive a little longer.

Either way, it could be fun to use. Though clunky I admit. (Here the boss is about to cast something that hurts, let me use my personal cooldown that has a 1.5 second cast, and is an attack)
