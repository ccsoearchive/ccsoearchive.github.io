So Zarhym, I just want to speak up and let you know.. as a dedicated PvE'er, I have found it difficult to break into PvP.

Last season, the time commitment needed for Arena felt perfect for me, for the first time ever, and I felt comfortable overall with trying to PvP just casually. It was fun, and I was able to advance reasonably well. I guess I understood where Blizzard felt the gear was too easy to get, but the changes that were made .. making Conquest take longer to farm and then practically forcing Honor grinding even for those who already had the old (now inferior) conquest gear .. all added up to just not having enough time to PvP with the time investment I was looking for.

The nature of PvP of course is such that if you get behind, you just can't compete. It isn't fun to die repeatedly to lack of gear, but there has to be some way to appease players who aren't dedicated to PvP but would still enjoy the option to be involved.

So all that said, the solution to all this that I could foresee would be some sort of unrated Arena. Perhaps with a Conquest reward that is lesser than rateds.

And I may not see this happening realistically but I could only hope for.. making honor easier to grind if it really must be required.

PvP balance is always hoped for, too, of course ultimately everyone's ideas of what is balanced is what makes their own class do better.. so I have the utmost sympathy for you in trying to decipher what issues are genuine and which are only bias. My only concern specifically for PvP class balance is the issue with interrupts. Players can interrupt so often, and this has made long cast spells far too weak. Nerfing melee damage as a result of melee overpowering PvP just seemed like the wrong answer and it has seriously hurt PvE. It seems like it would be far better if a PvP-related change to interrupts were to go out instead.

It takes 8 wins in arena to reach minimum cap, as opposed to the old system of 10 games total played. This doesn't amount to a huge difference to most people playing in the appropriate bracket, except for when you start out (doesn't matter if you're 2300 or 1300, if you're playing at your W/L should be close to 50%... and hence 8 wins should take maybe 16 games). If you're above minimum cap, then it would seem you have more time to dedicate to arena than that of someone who only does it casually and doesn't care about rating. In any case, one RBG on top of reaching arena cap would push you to 1650... and if your arena cap alone is exceeding 1650, you're definitely not 'casual' in the most common notion of the term.

Also, melee interrupts can be countered... it's called juking or fake casting. When something is readily countered, it does not need to be nerfed, people simply need to learn the counter. That's the nature of improving your play.

Arenas are broken right now. 2s are a joke, seiously. Unplayable. That is at least any comps that have healers. Matches either end in draws, or take so long to kill the heals it is not even worth the time. People are all complaining about Shamans, but Pally's are the worst. It is so ridiculous to try to kill a pally 1v1. Even mana draining them their mana is endless. Now I am a druid/shaman healer and I am even the first to say that the heal increase was a big problem for pvp. Druids are still a bit under powered, but every other class really didn't need that buff.

Also, yes the amount of interrupts etc that are available to every class is becoming a problem.

But the even bigger problem is something discussed already. The MMR system is a real isue. Good players are getting stuck and cannot progress, while less deserving players are sitting at ranks they do not deserve. While I understand it may be too late to reset them this season something either needs to be changed this season to give everyone a chance, or at absolute minimum we need to hear difinitively how this will be fixed for next season. I for one think that everyone should start each season with a 0 mmr. We lose our title for the previous season, why should we be penalized and have less opportunity to progress because of our performance from the previous season. This is the only fair way imo.

As for Rated Battlegrounds it has been said before and I'll say it again. If you are going to require us to do them to get conquest at least provide us with a "RBG Finder" to make it possible to get into a group. I know there are other good players out there looking for rbgs but they are hard to fins right now.

Of course there will always be slight imbalances and people understand that...well, for the most part, but it is so out of whack right now people are getting seriously discouraged. My husband has played for 6 years and cancelled his account today. I am seeing more and more of that. I for one and trying to hang in there, but the longer these issues go on where people feel that they cannot get a fair match no matter what the more people will feel this discouragement. I for one am sticking around, but I really hope to see some light at the end of the tunnel that things will be fixed soon as far as heal balance and the mmr system.
