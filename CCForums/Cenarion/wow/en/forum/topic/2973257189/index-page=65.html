Changes I would be content with for Warriors:

- 10 (or 15 second) CD on Spell Reflection
- Drums of War reducing the rage cost of Spell Reflection
- Lowered cooldown on our Shield Wall (possibly usable without a Shield equipped)
- Enrage proccing from all crits, not just MS crits (nice, reliable boost in damage and also acts to let us save our Berserker Rage to break fear/gouge instead of having to use it for Enraged Regeneration)
- Bringing Intercept back (possibly share a CD with Heroic Leap instead of Charge?)
- Colossus Smash increased to 70% armor reduction

Changes that would be an additional plus for Warriors:

- Some sort of compensation for our damage loss once sword & board are equipped (only class that has to suffer immensely in regards to offensive pressure output once defensive; ie: rogue can stay completely offensive whilst Combat Readiness is up; Feral whilst Barksin and/or Survival Instincts is up; Ret while Divine Protection or even Divine Shield is up; DK whilst Icebound Fortitude or AMS is up)
- Our manually applied slow, Hamstring, is sometimes a nuisance, as it requires constant re-application (ie: only lasts 5 seconds on some classes)
