Lowbie pvp is still borked.

JUST SAYIN

We talked to Ghostcrawler about this yesterday. He's well aware of this, but more importantly, he's not very happy about it either. The class design team's first priority is obviously balance around the end game, but absolute neglect of low-level balance isn't okay. This is something that isn't going to improve much in patch 4.3, but we hope to have more sound solutions coming.


I believe theres a couple of things wrong with lowbie pvp, and the most important is the levels that different classes get their skills.

For example, hunters getting kiting and cc abilities before warriors can charge in combat, topped off with aimed shot scaling ridiculously and crazy long range. Rogues being able to completely control their opponent, going against classes that have no cc, topped off with crazy ambush scaling. A 6 second stun at that level means death, it doesnt matter which way you look at it, and exo spam is deadly, coming from someone thats supposed to be specialized in healing :P

Theres certain skills that are simply too empowering at such low level gameplay that create these gaps, and ways that could be looked into are: Levels where theyre attained (see scatter shot, sap, gouge), scaling issues (pally stun first has a 3s duration, evolves into 6s at a higher level, same with aimed shot and ambush and the like) and lastly, talent point placement. (2 aimed shot talents in the first 2 tiers that makes the skill a 1shot)

Theres a bunch of things really, and I understand that endgame is more important, but getting to experience all the classes should also be important. Problem is, if youre not a hunter or a rogue, your chances of survival at lowbie pvp are really slim in comparison. (Unless of course, youre as leet as me and my shaman! 8B) lolz
