Its late on a friday and 66 pages into a long thread which means the devs watching are likely in skim mode but ill try anyways.

1. Queuing consistency - I'd like to see all queue systems work similarly. If you can do random dungeons 7 times in a day to cap max valor the battlegrounds should be similar. There are other quirks too that I have noticed like if you go linkdead while in the dungeon queue you are kicked out but the battleground/Tol barad queues dont seem to.

2. Tol Barad queue - this is really frustrating to me. Wintergrasp had lots of issues last year with unbalanced sides. I honestly prefer that old system over the current one where you can't play at all. Allowing everyone who wants to play an opportunity outweighs a perfect one to one balance when your realm isnt a good 50/50 split.

3. Ability to find people on your realm easier with a given rating for rated bgs. It's hard for me at least to figure out if someone has a decent rbg rating other than looking at achievements prior to going in.

4. The general character sheet iLevel value is very confusing to most people. It often calculates a higher score for pvp players. The uninformed player often looks at that value when looking for groups or raids in trade chat. Then when you go to look at pve gear its way under the value they described because its counting their much higher ilvl pvp gear. It would be nice if you did it like the website does where it gives the best iLevel count based on gear you have in bags/bank and then an equipped value.

1. Class Balance

The game isn't designed around 1v1 and thats where a lot of WOW's balance shortcomings come from. The game needs a huge overhaul from that perspective and will be difficult to accomplish given the philosophy in place, amount of specs, and how poor balance is now.

2. Give more incentive to PVP

PUGs are what will drive MMOs and generate the community, give more reasons for players to want to play with randoms.

3. More PVP stuff

The amount of BG's and world PVP zones (like Wintergrasp) just seems tiny and often not as polished or cool as new raids and etc. It also seems strange to have ghost town pvp zones like Wintergrap and takes away from the feeling of the WORLD of WARcraft. Feel free to use some iconic NPCs and immerse us in this world of WARcraft. Maybe recreate or create legendary battles.

4. WARcraft!, World of

This game feels too much like a skirmish based sandbox than a world of WARcraft. There are so many ways to go to expand this like sea battles, more BG's, having iconic NPC's fight alongside your team, dynamic game modes, virtual training camps, etc.

5. Equalizer Mode

Maybe implement an option where characters have the same gear for certain PVP instances. That way its a competition mostly based on skill and composition and little to do with items.
