I won't go into class balance too much (well, let's just say I'd love to see some Ret love and leave it at that, hehe). However I hope maybe the devs could offer some insight into their feelings on leveling through PvP. I'd like to direct these questions at them:

1. Has there ever been any discussion on daily quests for battlegrounds? Something to encourage and hopefully guide players toward working toward objectives as a team, while offering a small bonus of xp/honor and money (for lowbies dailies would be unlocked as they leveled and gained new bgs)? Perhaps even awarding dedicated players extra rewards (similar to other daily hubs like Argent Tourney and Molten Front) for completing them?

2. Resilience is such an important stat in PvP and outside of some outliers (players not having all skills yet, overly strong at low level abilities such as Ambush, etc.,), players aren't really exposed to the concept of resilience until 70, if they're even aware of the old season gear. Has there ever been any discussion about retooling the rewards for honor across the board? Stuff like armor sets before level 60, rebalancing battleground rep rewards, etc?
