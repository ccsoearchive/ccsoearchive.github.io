Hi Zarhym

Thanks for coming to the hear the PvP community!

As i see it now, there are few classes that need help in pvp:

Resto druids (very vulnerable to hard switches)
Moonkins (no dispell protection on dots, have to hardcast spells, cannot cast while moving...basically a punching bag for melee classes)
Hunters - needs eyes of the beast back....the most fun spell in the game and its gone :(

And there are a few classes that synergise far too well together (UA lock and resto Sham)

Honestly it would be better if someone who actually played those classes brought up their points, as for me, I'm a die hard elemental shaman, I have a few quick points i'd like to make:

1) Thank you for the ELE buffs in 4.2 it showed that you guys were aware of the extreme problems ELE had in pvp, and now things are much better. A sincere thanks for that.

2) There are still a few things needed: As Novoz and Drainerx have stated in their own 20+page threads (those 2 being the Elemental "mascots" or "superheroes" ) Elemental still needs a defensive cooldown (e.g. dispersion, iceblock, divine protection, deterance, cloak of shadows..etc etc)

As it stands right now, a melee cleave team will just stick like glue to an ELE shaman for the whole match until the healer OOMs and GG.

Solution: bring back Astral shift or some kind of activated defensive cooldown

3) Stoneclaw absorb needs to scale with something (spellpower? stamina?) you can choose! but as it stands right now with people with 391 weps from heroic FL or even just the 378 pvp weapons, one auto attack will get through the absorb...its just not what it used to be (not to mention costing us a major glyph slot)

4) We have the worst 31 point talent in the game right now. It costs us an insane amount of mana, does low damage, knockdown chance is unreliable at best...not to mention cast time.
Solution: Make EQ trainable and put in a defensive cooldown or something we might actually use as a 31 point talent

5) Our mana in pvp has problems, sure if u only cast lightning bolt/Lava burst in pvp you'll be fine but when you start decursing, offhealing..etc...your OOM inside 3 minutes.
Solution: change rolling thunder from 2% to 5%.

OK with all that being said, it can be tricky to balance Ele, since RESTO is already overpowered (i think that much is clear to anyone who pvps)

Last time when you tried to nerf RESTO, u nerfed the healing surge base spell, which really hurt ELE (and enhance) but barely affected resto since they can do a riptide -> GHW at 1.7 cast anyway....

So i'm begging you, when/if you nerf resto shams, please nerf the resto talent tree and not the base spells that Ele and enhance use!

Thanks for reading

First off, ty for starting this thread, i think it makes a lot of people feel better to know you want to hear our concerns.

My thoughts on pvp-

DAMAGE FEELS TOO HIGH ACROSS THE BOARD, though specifically with humans, and id like to see a slight buff to resilience and a change to the human racial. At the moment any class feels viable because any combination of classes can burst someone down in 10 seconds or less. When i pop pain suppression people don't back off, or hold off on offensive cd's until PS is down they just keep zerging because they know they can get a kill regardless.

A LOT OF CLASSES FEEL WATERED DOWN in cata- I feel this way about feral, and while i understand that the amount of ramp up time necessary for ferals in wotlk would make them even less viable in cata than they were previously, it's just...kinda faceroll. I've heard the same thing from warriors and dk's (specifically frost) though i don't play those classes so myself.

Also, a lot of people are UNHAPPY WITH FERALS CURRENT STATE. Specifically instant cyclones coupled with heavy burst damage. Seems like a good time to change feral since neither the people playing nor the people playing against feral are terribly happy with it.

MY PROPOSAL- Reduce berserk duration to 8 seconds and cd to 1 minute, cutting the duration and cd to one third. Obviously this could be tweaked based on how it would affect pve but i think it could remain relatively close to the numbers i gave. This would make feral more fun in pvp because we could capitalize on mistakes like los or hypothermia without the fear that "once i pop berserk i'm kinda crappy for the next 3 minutes so i HAVE to get a kill with it". I think non ferals would appreciate it because it's just not realistic to cc or kite a feral for more than 10 seconds consistently so you end up with 15 seconds of shred spam, and i can't think of any defensive cd outside of CR or Tree Form that can outlast that.

PS- would be changed to "increases haste by 20% and allows nature spells to be castable in cat form" undispelable, the act of shapeshifting removes this buff, lasts 15 seconds. The idea here is to do away with cyclone spam and give opponents a chance to interrupt, but also to make using charge and shift a decision. "Do i want to shift this snare and lose my buff or take an extra second or two to reach that caster?" "do i want to charge now knowing that i may get snared later and have to shift and lose my buff"? I think this active decision making makes pvp more fun =)

The haste buff might also make haste a viable option as a pvp stat, as opposed to stacking 40 agil in every slot after hit. Obviously haste's affect on energy regen would have to be looked at but..../shrug

I'd also like to see our front end damage outside of FB reduced slightly and bleeds increased a proportionate amount but obviously that would affect pve so dunno there...

These things would make me happy =)
