That would essentially be a reset of Season 10. That'd be a rather massive logistical undertaking (season rolls are more complicated than I even know), and we felt the fix was fast enough to normalize this season over time. The system, as it is now, is not functioning correctly. I've been queueing since the fix and the number of queues we get before the eight minute mark has substantially decreased. It seems that all we get queued into now are significantly lower teams despite the cross-bg queues. I know there must be other teams queueing at ~2600mmr, but we rarely see them during a weeks worth of queues. It is substantially difficult, if not impossible within reason, to reach ratings that have been attained prior to the fix. Teams at the higher end of the ladder are able to sit on rank one and gladiator without being challenged because of the aforementioned issue.

Why not implement a rating decay asap to liven up the arena ladders and allow for fair competition for titles, competition in itself, and both a viable and feasible way to allow for the ladders to normalize? Current Obvious PVP Problems:
1) Damage is too high (buff resilience)
2) Healing is too strong with 200% crits (buff mortal strike effects)
3) Interrupts and stuns are rampant - no casting allowed instant cast everything 4.3
4) PVP content is horrible when the core mechanics suck - no matter how cool it is.
5) Game balance is a pipe dream, but changes need to be made faster and be smaller.

Less Obvious Things:
1) Bring back 2v2 titles & weapons, its not like 3s or 5s are more balanced.
2) You lowered warrior skillcap every patch - shouldn't it be increased as the game progresses?
3) Hunters - most played class / lowest representation etc
4) Mages have way too much control for their damage output, one or the other needs to go.
5) Polymorph/fear& cyclone need to share a DR if damage is going to stay WOLK style.

Even Less Obvious Problem:

Some classes are balanced around tournament play in mind, and some are balanced around what happens at 1600 rating. Why? Balance 1600 or balance 2600 just pick one.
