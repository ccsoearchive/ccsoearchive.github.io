Yes. Under is almost always better than over. This goes for Hit Rating as well. every point up to a cap is equally worth just as much as the point before it. So your 1st point of Expertise is worth just as much as your 781st point. But once you hit a cap the value drops, starkly. They become "wasted" points, effectively.

Instead of going over the cap, shifting those last few points to another secondary stat (i.e. Haste) could in general, and why Shadowcraft recommended it, not only make up for the minor gap to the actual cap, but actually be worth more since you are fully utilizing every stat point.

I would say yes. The daggers interact very well with MG. So much so that MG had to be dropped from the Weighted Blades buff. Faster main-hand weapon means more (albeit weaker) MG procs, means more CP procs, means more energy, means more main-hand special attacks, means more MG... So the original buff to MG on Weighted Blades was basically a double dipping buff to Combat's Mastery. As such, no surprise that was redressed.

The 45% should do just fine balancing out the weaker top end damage and lower AP coefficient for SS/RvS. KS takes a hit, but the extra energy from MG is going to mean slightly quicker cycles so greater benefit on Restless Blades reducing AdRush/KS CDs to compensate. By the time we get to the actual legendary versions we'll see drastically reduced CDs.

Then of course there's the stacking agility buff that gets better and better at each stage. Combat loves raw agility.

All to all each stage offers compelling benefits.

Outside of being able to mitigate Black Blood of the Earth through talents and CD rotation on Morchok giving an edge to Sub from the reports I've seen (if it proves true on live I plan to add notes on variant speccing for this fight in Chapter I), PTR strats for T13 are looking like another tier that's going to be very good for Combat. The changes to melee AP buffs and the legendary daggers besides, Combat is going to be very strong indeed.
