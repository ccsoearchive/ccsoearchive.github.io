12/10/2011 11:07 AMPosted by Socio
or are we talking 500
Probably less. Although it's exceptionally hard to quantify the Rocket Jump racial. This is why I recommend going with what you like, not what will give "max DPS." Unless you're min/maxing every conceivable facet of your rogue and play flawlessly 100% of the time, one racial or another is going to be completely washed out. Being a goblin is not going to give you a free pass to the top of a meter, and being undead is not going to relegate you to the bottom. Playing well is what will make or break you. Might as well be happy with your rogue while you're doing it.
