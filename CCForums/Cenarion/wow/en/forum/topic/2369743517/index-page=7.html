We're currently working our way through Firelands and I had someone suggest that I go combat for Majordomo, Shannox, and Beth'tilec. Is this advice I should follow and if so should I reforge for combat or just clean up my current reforging since I prefer Assassination over Combat?


While I haven't done Majordomo personally (That and Baleroc are the only two fights I have not put in any time on yet), I've handled Shannox & Beth'tilac both quite well as assassination. If it's the spec you're most comfortable with, I would suggest you stick with it. But if you've been curious about combat, now is always a good time to make a switch over and see how it feels. I've been out of the game for a few weeks (see internet problems above), but nothing in the preliminary days leading up to 4.2 to some early raiding just after it dropped, or my experiences in the last couple days has me thinking either spec is a 'must' for any particular fight right now. Usually the use spec 'a' for fight 'x' and spec 'b' for fight 'y' and spec 'c' for fight 'z' discussions are relagated to heroic modes and cutting edge progression where every little edge is worth chasing. In regular modes and the progression scale of the majority of guilds, the spec you're most comfortable with and the happiest playing is almost always the better choice.

Sorry for taking so long to reply (again, see internet problems above), Happy rogueing*!
