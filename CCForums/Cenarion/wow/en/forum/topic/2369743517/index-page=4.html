Actually Bloodlust (Heroism... lamesauce =P) is just an attack speed buff, so you'd be fine there. What you need to concern yourself with is any AP or otherwise damage boosting buffs you may gain/lose while attempting to rupture.

No idea if they reverted on that or not, regardless, I couldn't agree with you more.
