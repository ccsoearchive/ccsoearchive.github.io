An example of the current situation:

Outside Eclipse: Nuke hits for 100 dmg.
Inside Eclipse: Nuke hits for 150 dmg.

The difference is huge, 50% or greater with procs.

In PvP, we're almost always outside Eclipse, thus we do -50% dmg. In PvE, Eclipse uptime is ~60% of the time.

My suggested change is to buff Moonfury (baseline), nerf Eclipse so it becomes:

Outside Eclipse: Nuke hits for 115 dmg
Inside Eclipse: Nuke hits for 140 dmg

Mastery scaling is unaffected, thus the stat behaves the current way so its not a bad stat to have. This is the easiest and as some have stated "elegant" solution to help moonkin pvp dmg without imbalancing pve.
