Any class that can't heal well and has rev up time on dps is bad for double burst and classes with stun immunity are that much better suited to it.


The changes to mastery you're suggesting would just straight up be a pvp nerf, an area that we aren't the best in already. If you took away the damage boost mastery provided and replaced it with time spent in eclipse, we would HAVE to cast spells. That would just trivialize boomkins for most classes in arenas. Any class with a fast interrupt or high mobility would roll over us. As it stands now, with mastery and eclipse the way they are... Boomkins actually have decent mobility and no renewable resource that matters.

Sure the changes you talked about might be better and add more balance to PvE. But don't act like it would help PvP at all.

If you change mastery to add time to eclipse instead of damage, we would have the same problem we always did... You HAVE to stand there and cast on something. So any class with any kind of mobility can easily pillar kite us reducing our damage to near nothing. We would also be heavily tied to our mana. So any priest team could outlast our damage for 50 seconds and reduce me to zero mana, essentially reducing dps to zero. The way eclipse works is the ONE thing boomkins have going for them in pvp. If I could, I'd take what ever % moonfury ends up giving, add another 3rd to it and put it all into eclipse. In a 10 min arena match, I spend 9 min in eclipse. Having more damage for the 9 mins, opposed to the 1 min sounds good to me. It'd just be terrible in PvE.
