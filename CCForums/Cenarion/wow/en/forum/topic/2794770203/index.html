I am talking about a tanking cloak.

What tanking cloaks are available, which are viable, from 4.2?

There is the cloak that you get from helping Thrall - item level 365 - relatively good stats.
There is the rep cloak from the Firelands raid - item level - 378 - pretty horrid stats.

Well... that's it. There is no way to upgrade those cloaks to a heroic item level, and there are no other strength cloaks, period (not including the rep cloak which has no viable tanking stats).

So, the best cloak from this patch is the item level 365 cloak with better stats. In my opinion, that is pretty sad. The agility users get a cloak that drops (Dreadfire D@*@% from Lord Rhyolith), and thus they get an upgradeable cloak to heroic item level. I think a strength cloak went missing from the loot tables. (When I say loot tables, I mean valor points, rep, quests – any place where comparable item level gear can be acquired.) They had an agility cloak but not a strength cloak?

I am stuck wearing the 365 cloak until I can convince my guild to return to Al'Akir, heroic, so I can get my best in slot cloak piece.

My question to you all is this.... Do you all feel shorted a cloak in this tier of loot?


P.S. Shield options are quite limited as well… only 1 this tier? Really? ...At least I can take solace in the fact that there are two weapons....
