The comparisions to mage abilties are off base, and presenting then in a simple Mages ability> Hunters ability doesn't accomplish your goal.

Concerning the comparison between Feign Death and Mirror Images. This is not a useful comparison-- Feign Death is meant to remove your threat and remove you from combat. That's why its used during wipes to allow for mass res. Having an ability meant to remove you from combat deal damage wouldn't make sense.

Mirror Images, on the other hand, is used by mages as a damage increasing ability. Its macrod into burn phases. Does it also drop threat? Yes, and it is used this way to lower threat while continuing to deal damage.

Do they share superficial differences? Yes, but they don't play the same, and they aren't intended to play the same.

For Mages, Invisibility more closely serves the role/ design space that feign death is supposed to fill.
Meanwhile, Snake Trap more closely replicates what Mirror Images does-- a short duration added damage spell involving the players own "adds" in the form of Mirror Images/ Snakes.
