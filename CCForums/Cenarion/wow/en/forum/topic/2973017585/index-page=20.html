I honestly wouldn't be happy if, say, Druids could only be Restoration and were just basically totally replaceable in the arena right now. I wouldn't be happy if there was one or two comps a Resto Druid could play, but still be replaced in and everyone talked about those comps like they were overpowered. It just isn't good for the game. I would have friends that would be very unhappy that I wouldn't be able to play with anymore, so many issues.

Blizzard doesn't owe this to me, or to any of us. They owe this to the game. Whether or not Hunters are at 2% or 200%, the fact of the matter is that there's not as much design as there is for other classes. So many things that just weren't updated enough, or weren't updated period. That's just wrong. We're an iconic class that deserves the same design as everyone else, and right now we don't have that.

That is the problem here, not any perceived magical numbers, not any statistics for a facet of the game only a fraction of the player base enjoys.

I would not tolerate this for any other class, either. Not just my own class. I literally stopped playing Warlock after seasons 1/2/3 Gladiator because it was just ridiculous. I remember fighting Warrior/Paladin or anything/Priest in 2v2 and being like, "awesome, auto-win"

The game should be balanced, and I think that is something everyone should agree with. Balance, in this case, has the meaning of design. Design should go everywhere equally. That's it. That's my problem. Why on earth should my Tier 2 PvP bow be a, "2% upgrade?"

I could write a ten page post on poorly updated Hunter spells. In the meantime, compare whatever abilities your class got to Camouflage in PvP. If you find yourself trying to think of a place where Camouflage would be useful, take a step back and imagine a place where Ring of Frost, Smoke Bomb, Heroic Leap or Leap of Faith would be useful.
