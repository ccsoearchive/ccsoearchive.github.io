This is a hard question to answer without a huge wall of text.

I'll start with 'hunters are fine in rbgs'

It's misconceptions like these that illustrate that devs are out of touch with hunters. The communication we get from devs often leaves us scratching our heads. I'm not saying this with anger or angst but to illustrate that the hunter community has little reason to believe blizzard is actively looking at hunter concerns.

It's not exaggeration to say that Hunters have received the least effective changes from exapansion to expansion. Hunters often feel like characters with level 85 stats which use abilites designed for the game at 80 or 70, sometimes even 60.

This is why Hunter mechanics some times feel like Cavalry riding against Mechanised Armor. There are situations where they can win but the other guys usually have to mess up first.

Best way to understand it is read Bandets epistle. I'll warn you it's a 15 post exposition and not a light read. Here is a link to the repost.

http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/2721383344


Edit* I want to add that I'm not trying to say 'Blizzard never does anything for hunters'. It just seems like they make adjustments to them in a vacuum. Like they are turning the knobs on a machine they don't fully understand. Because they are unsure of how changes will actually effect hunters, they are unwilling to risk that hunters would become overpowered.
