The stat priorities I listed are for leveling rogues. That means that you aren't able to cap most stats, because you don't have enough of each stat to cap. It also means that in most cases, you are tanking whatever you are killling, which means that expertise is unusually valuable for dps, since expertise stops parry and dodge.
You must understand that theorycrafting is just that; theory. You still have to apply it to your situation and make sure things still make sense. That still follows at level cap as well. You can't blindly follow EP ratings without understanding why they are listed as such.
Understand your class. Understand your situation. Then apply theory as required.

And concerning heirlooms? Go with 2 scarlet kriss or one kriss and one heartseeker? And is 2 haste trinkets (swift hand of justice) a good idea? I have plenty of crusader seals and honor waiting to be spent for my goblin rogue.

The haste Haste/health trinkets are good if you have a bunch of gear you can get for cheap. With recuperate and kill speed though, its not really an issue.

If you are planning on leveling other characters, it would probably only go with one dagger, a slow weapon and run combat or subtlety. You can use the slow weapon on another class that you want to level as well that way. I say that because not many classes have use for fast weapons like daggers.

If emblems/seals are not an issue; I would run double dagger and mutilate. I say that because I think it is the easiest spec to learn/manage for learning how to rogue and it still performs well at level cap, so you don't have to relearn anything.
