If he is combat, he should be using a slow mainhand weapon. Sinister strike hits harder that way. Combat with daggers is a no-no, unfortunately. If he prefers daggers, he should switch to assassination or subtlety. Thats probably his biggest issue. Other than that, keep up slice and dice and use blade flurry and adrenaline rush often.

I'd go with sinister strike, rupture/eviscerate, and slice and dice. Glyphs are that big of a deal until you start min/maxing at level cap. When he get to 85, he should look up elitistjerks forum and read the rogue guides there.

Macros are great for automating responses... for instance... as a Rogue, our bread and butter is to sap a target and then pickpocket it... instead of having to click on a target, then press your button to sap AND then press a button to pickpocket... You would get something like this...

/cast [target=mouseover] Sap
/cast [target=mouseover] Pick Pocket

Now this is done and you are able to do it by just having your mouse over the target as opposed to clicking on it... All three steps are condensed down to one action.

Another thing about macros is say you are attacking... more often than not we do the same moves...

For example

Ambush>Sinister Strike> SinisterStrike> Hemoragge>Sinister Strike> Eviscerate...

Now... there are 4 different spells here... instead of having to click four different buttons... all you have to do is click one... repeatedly clicking it.... but you would get something like this

/castsequence Ambush, Sinuster Strike, Sinister Strike, Hemorrage, Sin ister Strike Eviscerate

Here is a good site to further explain the uses of macros as well as some that are really good...

http://www.wowwiki.com/Useful_macros

And this one is great for Rogue Macros
