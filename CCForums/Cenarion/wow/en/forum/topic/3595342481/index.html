Hi all,

Pirates In My Porridge is a level 20 guild and the new home to Cenarion Circle's high end PvP players. Although for sure we do not have all of the good players in the guild, we do have a bulk of them. Most of our players play at the 1900+ level in arena, and we run the highest rated Horde RBG group on server, frequently playing above 1900 MMR. Recently we broke the 2000 barrier with several new Champions (RBG rating = 2000) being minted (myself included, shameless advertisement).

If you are interested in joining our community of strong PvP'ers, send me a tell, or contact our GM, Çloùd (not so easy to spell, is it?)

Other officers you can contact or find information from include

Cloutius - DK officer
Granya - druid officer
Pacifika - priest officer
Profearzor - warlock officer

Requirement for full membership is 1800 on your 3v3 or 5v5 arena history, or a strong recommendation from one of the above listed officers.

Look forward to hearing from anybody out there wanting to become a part of our small but tight community.

We are currently looking for:

- A strong frost mage
- A strong disc priest
- A strong shadow priest
- A strong rogue (poor Kurrs has to play all the time because he's like the only one)
