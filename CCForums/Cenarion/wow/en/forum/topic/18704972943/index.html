Much in the same way Kat wanted to get their hands on your junk, I myself am in the market for trunks, stuffed with junk. I'd preferably like to get my hands on as many as possible. For your efforts, I'm happy to reimburse you with gold, however any donations will be happily accepted

What I'm looking for and how much I'll pay:
Vine-Cracked Junkbox (75 gold each)
Flame-Scarred Junkbox (50 gold each)
Heavy Junkbox (50 gold each)
True Steel Lockbox (100 gold each)
Ghost Iron Lockbox (75 gold each)
Elementium Lockbox (50 gold each)
Tiny Titanium Lockbox (25 gold each)

Why do I want these?
Alongside Katecia, Taly is IC'ly an adventurer and something of a treasure hunter. As such, I had the idea they they'd find a good amount of lockboxes and treasure chests that they couldn't crack open. For the purpose of having something to offer at our open guild markets and such, Taly will be accepting barter and trade. You offer some items that they either need or you find might have value, Taly will offer you back an unopened piece of treasure. It's sort of a lucky dip, in that when you open it you'll get (at the very least) what you paid/traded to get it back. At the most, you might get lucky and walk away with more gold than you expected.

How do I get these to you? I am in desperate need of extra riches!
Anything you find, feel free to send CoD to me for the prices listed above. I won't go above these levels, but I also won't say no to donations or lesser amounts if you don't think they're worth that much. I do want you to get something out of your efforts, however, even if you just stumbled across any of these boxes in your travels. Basically if you can't unlock it, you can send it to me and I can still make sure you walk away with a nice amount of pocket money for your troubles.

Now get out there, and don't come back until you've retrieved for me ample amounts of booty!
