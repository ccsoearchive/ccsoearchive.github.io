Madcapmcgee
Profile
Madcapmcgee
88 Gnome Rogue
Elemental Power
2260
Pally was my first. It was great fun and a good starter character. Around level 60 it got insanely boring. So I started trying other classes.

Tried a Priest and died a LOT. Didn't like how they worked.

Tried a Mage, was better, still didn't like the mechanics.

Tried a (Feral) Druid. I loved the idea of it, mostly for the mobility. Cat and Travel forms, Flight and Swim forms, PvP sets (never got to getting one). It was still missing something though.

Tried Rogue and found out what Feral was missing. The toolset. Oh god, the toolset! I mean, Vanish! Hello? BYE! Sap. Distract. Heck, even Pick Pocket (never cared as much for lock picking). And then there were the poisons, options! I think what I really loved though, and was sad to see go were hybrid specs. I know I know, snowflakes were laughed at. But I'm not a hard-core PvPer, I'm a solo-er. When everyone was building Mut hybrids, I was looking towards Prep and Premed and Shadow Step as my primary, and hoping to get something in the poison talents. I didn't really care for the ability Mutilate itself.

Well, those options are long gone, and surprisingly Mut suits me more than Sub now. But the options. To this day I play Rogue for the options.

---------

Can't wait for this stealth run speed in 4.1 though, speed being part of what I liked as Feral. What a QoL change!
