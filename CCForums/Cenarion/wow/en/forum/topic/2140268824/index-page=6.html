I've always liked an agile fighter in almost all fantasy games. Never really played the Warrior type character that wore alot of armor and stuff. Never was my thing. But having a melee fighter who defended not with heavy armor but quickness and agility was my philosophy.

For example when i played D&D I played a Dual-wielding Elven Ranger who fought with 2 bastard swords. So I tried to duplicate that when I played WoW. Going by that you'd think I'd love Combat. But no I've never touched combat. I leveled as Sub and then when I hit 80 back in WotLK I went Assassination for some reason and stubbornly never went combat no matter if it was straight better. That still stands.

Glad I never went Combat since there are no more Agility swords. Dual-wielding axes and maces just doesn't agree to me. Seems like a wanna-be Fury Warrior. :b
