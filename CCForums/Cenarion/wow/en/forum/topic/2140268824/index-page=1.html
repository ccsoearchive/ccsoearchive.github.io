As a child my father and I would watch animal planet before the reality shows kicked in. Our favorite parts would be the lions stalking in the tall grass waitinng to ambush their prey. And the prey would be always on the watch, paranoid, waiting for what they knew might come.

That's why I rolled a rogue. I could be a silent and deadly killer that everyone fears. The one who can sit back and pick and choose who the next victm will be. Unseen from the naked eye until the moment of attack and once I've been spotted, it's too late.

Obviously this sadly isn't reality anymore for cata endgame pvp. Everyone has too much health >_> but I still like to keep my perception about rogues alive. :)
