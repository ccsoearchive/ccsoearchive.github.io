My reason for thinking the 31 talent is weak is simply because as BM, you should already be able to pick up Exotic pets and have a small edge when it comes to your companion. Four talent points isn't substantial compared to Chimera Shot or even Black Arrow.

I get that low levels shouldn't have access to Exotics, thus my thought of integrating. Also, BM needs more control. 30 second, combined trap CDs, 2 min CD on deterrance isn't enough when you're looking at a range, kiting class.

Numbers show that MM is chosen by a vast majority and it's chosen due to control. Silence Shot, double traps, double Scatter Shots, etc.

As BM, when you have to resort to using Scatter Shot, primarily designed to widen the gap distance, for successfully getting a trap over a target then you've taken out a short CC for a trap which will likely be trinketed out (Freezing).
