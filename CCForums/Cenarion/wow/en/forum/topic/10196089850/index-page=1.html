Tired of having to conform to one theme in an RP guild? Have multiple characters and ideas or want to be a storyteller...but can't find a good way to start? Or do you just want to enjoy all aspects of the game in a mature atmosphere?

NOTICE: Recruitment is back in full swing!

Website- http://overture.enjin.com
RP Inspiration / Overture's Tumblr:- http://overtureguild.tumblr.com/
Turwinkle's Interview - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VWq1n9mlma4 WARNING: A little out of date.

Overture is a lvl 25 heavy adult RP guild (18+) that welcomes PVE and PVP players as well. We are dedicated to creativity and the freedom to make your own stories within the World of Warcraft universe and even story-tell your own events. What we are mostly looking for are the kind of rpers who aspire to be storytellers, or are just driven enough to create, whether those creations be realistic and interesting characters or plots of your own. We enjoy using a dice system and some basic stats to enhance roleplay and give it a bit more of a realistic feel, rather than relying on pure cooperation or the luck of a simple /roll. Because of this, some of our events have been commented on as being very D&D like, but members also enjoy normal, casual RP in between. Because we value quality over quantity, we are and probably will remain a small community, but that does not mean that we don't have epic levels of fun.

---
IF YOU ARE NEW TO RP, we will help you out. Just be patient for raises in rank, and the guild will guide you. Your app will still need decent grammar and spelling, but we won't be too harsh in recruitment here by any means.
---

All that we require for membership is this:

1. A good understanding of what RP is and how to do so in World of Warcraft.
2. Maturity. 18+ required with only rare exceptions.
3. A good-natured, drama free attitude.
4. Willingness to cooperate with your fellow guildies. Yes, even in an RP duel. (Roll system provided)
5. Lore-abiding, realistic, non-OP characters.
6. Decent grammar and spelling and readable emotes.
7. The desire to have fun!

If this laid-back style of guild is your thing, do not hesitate to send Miralath or any officer online an in-game mail or message and visit this website. http://overture.enjin.com

Q.A.

Is the name "Overture" IC? - No. We are an RP hub. The tag itself means nothing in the IC world. Having no singular theme, there are several groups of roleplayers all doing different things, and more than a couple storylines going on at a time. This was designed to accommodate any sort of reasonable, lore-abiding character and provide a fun home for alts as well. In a nutshell, Overture is for people who like a lot of different styles of RP.

Something cool that we did (RP): A sandbox storyline that used the entirety of Westfall. Individuals spoke to me first OOC and then would enter an IC adventure, approaching the problem there (Which was a cult uprising) in whatever way they pleased. One particular event ended in a rewrite into story form, found here: http://overture.enjin.com/forum/m/16157866/viewthread/8265959-death-dues-story

Non RP coolness: Lots of farting around in casual PVE content, as well as stuff out of game such as games in our Steam group. Like survival stuff in the vein of DayZ and Nether, Civ 5 and Starbound. ESPECIALLY Starbound.

Something unexpected that went well: During the Westfall events, a player was told to go a certain direction. They forgot which direction and ended up finding a hidden place that I did NOT expect anyone to get to so soon, but it resulted in a dramatic shift in story, which was pretty fun for all involved.

Something unexpected that did NOT end well: There have been a few smaller RP events when Westfall ended that did not go as planned. Little merc missions that either went on too long or just split into multiple parts and were never finished due to player schedules. Nothing major here, however.

Typical day on guild chat: Somewhat lewd and silly sometimes. It's where people blow off steam or mess around. RP is for serious time, and it's not in guild chat. ---

There are several main ranks. Some are self explanatory, but here are the explanations.

Veteran - Proven long-time, helpful guildy who has gone out of his/her way to help out the guild as a whole.

Member - Proven active, attends roleplay (whether it be events or open/random RP)

Initiate - Has just gotten started on the path of Overture RP. Assisted by Esgaroth and/or other officers.

New Roleplayer - Learning the ropes of RP. A student of the guild, here to learn and have a good time. All members are expected to help these people out.

OOC - Non-RP member, whether they be friends/family or just people who want to enjoy the atmosphere. All OOC members must have a character profile looked at before attending events. New guildies also start here before being approved for Initiate rank.

---

The easiest thing to do to rise in rank is to contribute. Be active. Show interest. It's mostly a status symbol, but rewards are planned in the future per rank.

Officers are tasked with keeping the technical/social aspects of the guild running smoothly and without issue. The people who take this up are basically unpaid "employees" who enjoy doing the work for the sake of the guild. Each individual has his/her own tasks, whether it be simple moderation of the guild and its chat, website admin, character profile reviewing, story assistance, etc. These are the gears of the guild. The people who work behind the scenes to make sure drama isn't a thing. Only officers and above can see officer chat, for the sake of keeping everything smooth and to keep issues out of guild so nobody else has to worry.

Storytellers are the main people who help make sure that there is something to RP behind. The intention of this job is to keep things active, and not to make your main character the center of attention. Should you take this job, you should focus on guiding other players through RP during your stories. That is not to say that you cannot RP your character how you like...just that in your stories you should focus on the players more than anything, unless the specific type of plot demands otherwise.

Here's a thread I just made on our official forums to help people make sense of all the things that are happening in Overture right now. Yes, there's a lot, and no, nobody is ever required to participate in all of it, let alone any of it. You're welcome to casual RP and walk-up.

---

J.A.T. (Jack of All Trades) Bounties - Bounty hunters who do simple one-night/day events. These all come together into one slowly evolving, long-term story and currently the situation has them pitted against what seems like another rival company.
Storytellers: Miralath
Players/Characters: Adrienne, Abarice, Agrim, Agalladorn, Managan, Zellorie, Mordekai (The rest TBA)
Dates/Times: Impromptu. Rarely will anything be scheduled as this is meant to be a filler storyline for when there's downtime. To catch up, ask around IC. Rumors and information are up to other characters to give out.
Combat System: Official Guild-Created Rolls and Stats
Status: Available on request.

---

Mercenaries - Good-aligned mercs with a mission to do helpful things around Azeroth and make a little coin in the process.
Storyteller: Destan(Esgaroth)
Players/Characters: Destan, Madigan, Sylladara, Caeir, Gaverick, Gilgamesh
Dates/Times: Wednesdays at 6:30 server
Combat System: Simplified Guild System (Rerolls, no defensives)
Status: ON HOLD

---

The Rusted Mariner - An interesting take on a monster story, heavily reliant on GHI (Gryphonheart Items Mod) and investigation. Involves some out of guild RP.
Storyteller - Managan
Players/Characters: TBA
Dates/Times: Tuesdays, 7pm server
Combat System: Expanded from the original guild roll system.

---

Stories of a Book Untold - A magical artifact lures characters in to alternate realms of sleep where they encounter many larger-than-life scenarios.
Storyteller - Agalladorn
Players/Characters - Varied
Dates/Times: Downtime. Events are one time only and upon request.
Combat System: Official Guild with some small added details.
Status: On Hold

Bloodsports Arena - Come, all you combat oriented characters! Do you want fame and glory? Gold? Or do you want to be the righteous victor and end a realm of horrific, inhumane practice? Talk to a guild representative for more information.
Storyteller - Islar
Dates/Times: Wednesday 6pm
Combat System: Official, with added danger (characters CAN and WILL get injured. Death can happen, but the ST won't force it)
Status: Ongoing
