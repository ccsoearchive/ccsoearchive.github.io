I have one but am currently using it. Sorry Zan.

In other news, there is some pretty cool RP going on in overture!

We got one story running where we are all looking into some mysterious happenings out in Westfall, some kind of crop problem with a shadowy group looming behind it. We've managed to get a little information but only time will tell what we find out there. Death threats, attacks on druids and people killing themselves before they can talk are just the start of this adventures.

Another story going on follows after a few reported murders in Duskwood. The night's watch has its hands full and whoever shows up gets the job. We've found a disturbed tomb, unearthed some mysterious creatures and may have started a war between two factions we do not quite understand. There is magic, bad deals with magical creatures and a cleric who slaps too many people in this tale so far!

Those are two major plot lines going on right now. Pretty fun stuff, though of course limited in participants. Hopefully we will start to have a few more stories run at once but I have to say that so far everything I've done with you all has been quality and very enjoyable.

Best of all I don't need to fit the mold of a specific character type!

aww yeah
