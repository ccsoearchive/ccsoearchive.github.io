Here's a big ol' hunk of stuff. It's a nearly-complete list of every piece of new level-85 PvE gear that was introduced in Patch 4.3 and is specifically intended for use by rogues. (Not included here are hunter bows/crossbows, though they're viable alternatives for Combat rogues in particular. I think I also may have left out some quest rewards or BoE drops in the new dungeons/raid.)

==The New Heroic Dungeons: All Drops/Rewards==
These are all iLevel 378, same as Firelands. "ET" = End Time; "HT" = Hour of Twilight; "WE" = Well of Eternity
  • Axe: Trickster's Edge; Peroth'arn (WE)
  • Belt: Peroth'arn's Belt; Peroth'arn (WE)
  • Boots: Wayfinder Boots; Arcurion (HT)
  • Chest: Time Twisted Tunic; Murozond (ET)
  • Cloak: Cloak of the Banshee Queen; Sylvanas (ET)
  • Dagger (fast): Mandible of the Old Ones; Asira (HT)
  • Dagger (slow): Jagged Edge of Time; Murozond (ET)
  • Gloves: Gloves of the Hollow; any Echo (ET)
  • Helm: Helm of Thorns; Mannoroth/Varo'then (WE)
  • Fist: Clattering Claw; Benedictus (HT)
  • Legs: Puppeteer's Pantaloons; Azhara (WE)
  • Ring: Mannoroth's Signet; Mannoroth/Varo'then (WE)
  • Ring: Alurmi's Ring; quest reward (WE)
  • Shoulders: Desecrated Shoulderguards; Benedictus (HT)
  • Trinket: Arrow of Time; Murozond (ET)


==Valor Gear==
These are all iLevel 397, same as the first six bosses of Dragon Soul, but are intended to be slightly inferior to regular-mode raid drops. Note: Some of the pieces listed below appear to have inferior (Raid Finder) versions and superior (heroic-mode) versions; however, the i397 Valor Point versions won't drop from raids.
  • Belt: Belt of Hidden Keys, 1650 VP
  • Boots: Rooftop Griptoes, 1650 VP
  • Bracers: Bracers of Manifold Pockets, 1250 VP
  • Chest: Shadowbinder Chestguard, 2200 VP
  • Cloak: Batwing Cloak, 1250 VP
  • Gloves: Lightfinger Handwraps, 1650 VP
  • Necklace: Cameo of Terrible Memories, 1250 VP
  • Helm: Nocturnal Gaze, 2200 VP
  • Ring: Emergency Descent Loop, 1250 VP
  • Thrown: Windslicer Boomerang, 700 VP
  • Trinket: Kiroptyric Sigil, 1650 VP


==Dragon Soul Raid==
Each of these has a better heroic version and a worse LFR version, unless otherwise noted; I'll link to the regular version. Note that Deathwing drops have a iLevel (by 6) than other boss drops in the tier.

Weapons
  • Axe: No'Kaled, the Elements of Death; Deathwing #2
  • Dagger (fast): Electrowing Dagger; Hagara
  • Dagger (slow): Blade of the Unmaker; Deathwing #2
  • Mace: Morningstar of Heroic Will; Ultraxion
  • Sword: Spine of the Thousand Cuts; rare drop from regular-mode trash mobs
  • Thrown: Razor Saronite Chip; Morchok

Armor
All tier gear will drop as a "XXX of the Corrupted Vanquisher" token that we have to turn in for the gear; we'll be competing with DKs, druids and mages on the rolls.
  • Belt: Belt of Flayed Skin; Zon'ozz
  • Belt: Nightblind Cinch; rare BoE regular-mode trash drop
  • Boots: Interrogator's Bloody Footpads; Yor'sahj
  • Boots: Molten Blood Footpads; Deathwing #1
  • Bracers: Shadow Wing Armbands; Blackhorn
  • Chest (Tier 13): Blackfang Battleweave Tunic; Ultraxion
  • Cloak: none
  • Gloves (Tier 13): Blackfang Battleweave Gloves; Zon'ozz
  • Helm (Tier 13): Blackfang Battleweave Helmet; Blackhorn
  • Legs (Tier 13): Blackfang Battleweave Legguards; Yor'sahj
  • Necklace: none
  • Ring: Signet of Grasping Mouths; Hagara
  • Ring: Seal of Primordial Shadow; rare drop from first six bosses
  • Shoulders (Tier 13): Blackfang Battleweave Spaulders; Hagara
  • Shoulders: Underdweller's Spaulders; Morchok
  • Trinket: Starcatcher Compass; Blackhorn
  • Trinket: Vial of Shadows; rare drop from first six regular-mode bosses
  • Trinket: Wrath of Unchaining; Deathwing #1


==Leatherworking Patterns==
These are iLevel 397, the same as the first six bosses of regular-mode Dragon Soul. The patterns are BoE drops within the Dragon Soul raid.
  • Bracers: Pattern: Bladeshadow Wristguards
  • Legs: Pattern: Bladeshadow Leggings

Ryan over at Rogue Resource already did a swell job compiling all of these, so I'll just send you over yonder for a rundown: http://www.rogueresource.com/?p=2683

That sounds about right; the Treads of the Craft (tier 12's leatherworking pattern) were clear and away better than anything other than the heroic Majordomo boots, and they're more or less intended to be gold sinks for wealthy rogues who want better gear. I haven't yet bothered crunching the numbers (or seeing what others have crunched) to see just what the difference is between the Bladeshadow wrists and the Shadow Wing thingies, but I want to say it'll end up being notably better (as in, somewhere between 100 and 200 DPS) than the regular raid wrists, and maybe just a weence better than the heroic raid wrists.

There are a ton more gear options being introduced for this patch. I realize we're getting three new dungeons along with the raid tier, and that the raid tier includes three versions of every gear drop. But they've also ratcheted up the number of things we can get through Valor, and I didn't even list the PvP stuff here, some of which may well be comparable to a certain extent if folks are just starting out. Meanwhile, those new heroics drop gear roughly on par with Firelands gear (whereas the Z's dropped gear slightly below Firelands-level), so options for gearing up to Dragon Soul level abound (by comparison to gearing up for Firelands, anyway).

Makes me think a lot more gold is gonna find its way to the reforge dude during the upcoming patch -- and into the pockets of gem and enchant sellers on the AH.
