Goll, I'm finding myself strangely short-sighted when I fight mages, my first priority is to get them in a full kidney after a blink, pressure them without sdance (I use the proc trink, so it works fine) and (hopefully) force a block.
After that point, I'm just LoSing the pet, restealthing, and waiting for another opportunity to catch the mage in a full kidney, forcing cold snapped block, and then I vanish and wait on my cds once more, hopefully scoring the kill.
Though this method works on most mages, I've been duelling a couple higher ranked ones, an arena master fire and a 2k+ frost, and I've been (strangely) having more trouble with the frosty.
It's just a pain in the butt staying on him, and I'm finding it ridiculously difficult to win without prep, a real pain in the !@#.
IDK if my relative ease with the fire was some unlucky blazing speed procs, but in general I'm having a much easier time staying on him, and when I'm on him, his bubble doesnt do squat but piss me off.
on a side note...does shivving crippling on your target remove blazing speed? By remove I mean slow them down to 30% regardless of the 50% increase.

As always, I shall defer to your infinite wisdom in this subject.


If all this was just to ask the question at the end, I know it does slow them. By how much? I don't know.

A deathtard is a Deathknight. Since 95% of them are downs kids who can't play any other class, I refer to them collectively as deathtards, until proven otherwise.

Is saving cloak vs a frost mage still advisable? I duel a 2.2k frost mage quite often. She stacks mastery, and according to my combat log her spamlance hits me for 26k crits without damage trinkets procced. A single shatter can easily do 50k+ and when she uses pvp trinket and her darkmoon card procs it gets pretty rediculious.

I've gotten to the point where she always needs to double block agianst me, and usually has 50k hp or less at the end. I do manage to win some, and I find that all the duels I win have one thing in common. I get a second CoS in. I usually cloak early, because it forces an ice block and gives the chance to have it agian later in the duel.

I felt pretty good the other day when I got her to 50k hp while I was sitting at 100%, only to get stuck in a single deep freeze and lose half my hp to shatter with dps cooldowns procced. This is of course followed up with a cone of cold ice lance for another 30k.

So cold >.>


I never cloak before block.

We can put out so much pressure via effective cooldown usage that we don't need to cloak before block.
