Before reaching the 1937 mark, sometimes I would be doing 3 Cobras 1 AS 1 KC in 7, other times doing 2 Cobras 2 AS 1 KC in 6 based on Focus.

You merely have to be aware but that Haste plateau and shot cycle are the general cycle you fall back into when not having excess Focus. Even when you hit 1937 Haste w/o 2pc T13, you drop your cycle down to a tight 3:1:1 in 6 but you still sometimes end up doing 2:2:1.

Due to Haste modifiers and random Focus gen through Invigoration, you can never maintain a constant rotation. What the OP stated was just the highest DPS general cycle for that Haste level and used as the starting point for which you make your judgement calls and shot choices from.

The second you get the 2pc T13, Haste plateaus go out the window and it's all meaningless. Without it though, it helps to have certain shot cycles in mind.
