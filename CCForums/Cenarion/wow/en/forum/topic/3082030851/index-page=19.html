I'm a little confused on the shot haste plateau wouldn't using more arcane shots and less cobra yield more dps?


No offense meant, but considering what you said, you're giving me the impression that you skipped down to the shot cycles at the bottom of that section without reading what was above. If that's not the case, then you're simply misunderstanding it and confusing shot cycle with shot priority. They're not the same thing.

But to answer your question, yes arcane does more damage than cobra. However, since one uses focus and the other replenishes it, it's just not possible to use nothing but arcane (except while under the effect of BW of course).

Now with all that out of the way, I'll explain what you misunderstood.
Taken from the last paragraph (just above the shot cycles) of the haste plateau section
http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/3082030851?page=1#12
this is what it reads:
Along with the cast time of Cobra Shot, the calculations given here will include the number of shots in that shot cycle. Bear in mind, some Cobra Shots will be replaced with Arcane Shot dumps whenever you have excess focus (but only so long as it doesn't delay a Kill Command). This is especially true for the higher plateaus and/or when you have the 2 piece T13 (Wyrmstalker's) bonus. Also, none of the shot cycles here will include the longer cooldown abilities like Bestial Wrath and Fervor. Remember, this isn't about the Shot Priorities themselves. This is about Cobra Shot cast times (and how many you can squeeze) in a shot cycle.


I hope that clears it up for you.

From my experience, a chimaera should be left on autocast while only a worm should be controlled manually for its AoE (due to its longer CD and limited target zone).

Neither of the Exotic pet AoE abilities are able to be autocasted. They must be manually casted (or macroed) to activate at all.

It may be worth nothing at some point in the guide that using any non-Ferocity pet is a nearly-crippling loss of DPS (around 3000 DPS loss swapping to Cunning, and closer to 5000 if you swap to Tenacity), excluding the two AoE pets for AoE situations.

Socket bonuses are also, in my experience, worth taking for red/yellow sockets whenever the bonus is any amount of agility -- only for blue sockets do you have to worry if it's ~15+ or more agility per blue socket or not.
