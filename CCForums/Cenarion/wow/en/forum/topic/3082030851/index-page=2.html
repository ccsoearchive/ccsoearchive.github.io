First off, I appreciate your feedback. However, I think you're misunderstanding the talents. I'm not sure how I can say this without possibly coming off rude. So I hope you don't take it as such. But I don't understand how you could even think to say "the pet specs were off." Basically, when it comes to talents, it's either required or it's not. If it directly effects damage, it's required. If it doesn't, it's optional. I think that's where you misunderstood.

Everything I have marked as required, indisputably affects damage directly. That's why they're marked required. But as far as optional goes, there's varying degrees. Some things are recommended and some are not. But none are off limits. If you think having lion heart or boar's speed is a good idea, by all means, use them. After taking all the talents that are required (and recommended), there's still a point or two left over. See for yourself.
http://us.battle.net/wow/en/tool/pet-calculator#Gp40!TaaYYadjdeg!!
But since they don't directly affect damage, they're not going to be considered required. I apologize if this wasn't clear in the guide. I'll try to fix that as soon as I get the chance.
