Until you can reach a solid 3 CS > 1 Arc > 1 KC in 6 seconds, Haste isn't better than Crit due to what you give up while floating off in the middle. This makes the Haste "wasted" and losing out a lot of Crit in the process, thus hindering Focus regen for both you and your pet through loss of Sic'em and Invigoration procs, loss of damage through normal crits, and loss of Killing Streak procs

So until you can reach 2,229 Haste

Agi > Hit > Crit > Haste = Mastery

When you can reach 2,229 Haste

Agi > Hit > Haste > Crit > Mastery
