Well, I really disagree when you say the second stat priority for BM hunters. The other things you posted I agree with the most part of them (95%). But what BM hunters are really doing wrong is the Haste stuff.

What we really need is Haste until we get 3 cobra-shots, 1 arcane shot and 1 kill command in 6 seconds. Or in other words:

Kill command - Arcane Shot - Cobra shot - Cobra shot - Cobra shot ---- AND your Kill command must be ready right at the end of this. If you are delaying your next kill command for 1 second, is still ok, but more than that, something must be really wrong.

And until you reach that spot (when your kill command's cooldown finishes right with the last cobra) you must reforge for haste every time!

For BM hunters the second stat priority is: Hit(cap)>Haste>=Crit>Mastery.

You should only keep reforging from crit to haste when you got this rotation in 6 seconds. (I'm assuming you have focus fire and 10% attack speed buff from your party ON)

After all, reforging always for haste doesn't hurt our dps. Just change the order and we are good to go. Haste is never too much and pushes us forward! =)

Another thing: you haven't posted about our long cooldowns yet. I don't know if you agree with me, but the most part of the BM hunters like to put Fervor or Rapid Fire on the same macro as Bestial Wrath and other stuff like Trinkets, Racials, and whatever. This is a DPS loss, since we are better hitting Rapid fire when we need to cast Cobras, or fervor right after Bestial Wrath (if you don't have the Rapid Fire cooldown yet (longer fights)). Call of the Wild works fine with Bestial Wrath and Rapid fire. And if you hit Rapid Fire just when your buff from Bestial Wrath finishes (10 secs after blowing it) you get your cobras/arcanes/kill command really fast and buffed with the Call of the Wild, since it lasts longer than the 10 secs of rage from Bestial Wrath (if you macroed them together, which is fine). It's awesome the DPS increase I won doing that.

The short story is: Put everything on the same macro: Trinkets, Racials, Pet stuff alongside with Bestial Wrath, BUT DON'T put Rapid Fire into this list, nor Fervor! Hit RF right when you finished your 10 secs with WRATH, and the other buffs (Call of the W. and Trinkets) are still on! And if you don't have the RF cooldown, hit Fervor. And continue Arcane Shotting until your next Kill Command. That's all. =)
