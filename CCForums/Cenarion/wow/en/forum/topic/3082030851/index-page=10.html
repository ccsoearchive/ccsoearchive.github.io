Target dummy damage is generally useless as a metric. You're missing buffs, debuffs, and various talents and skills are or are not being used (such as CA ridiculously buffing MM dps and not being able to use Kill Shot). Looking at Female Dwarf with all buffs/debuffs enabled (as I don't know what your group usually has) has you with a potential of 26858. Now, the reality is that fight mechanics will usually bring you down, often by many thousands of dps. On the other hand, FD is not set up to sim the synergistic effect that BM can get with BW combined with other procs.

At any rate, while it can vary a lot depending on the fight and what role you're playing in it (plus buffs and debuffs), you have the potential to be in the low to mid 20k's. At a glance you seem to have about everything set up right except for some refinement of your reforging (try using wowreforge.com, it'll get you much closer to the hit cap than you are now), so it's a matter of working on your skill if you're not pulling that high on things like Chimaeron (since you're not really doing FL).
