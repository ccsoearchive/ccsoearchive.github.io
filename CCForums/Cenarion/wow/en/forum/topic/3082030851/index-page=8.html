"The secondary stat priority for Beast Mastery hunters is:
Hit (til capped) > Critical Strike > Haste ≥ Mastery"

You SHOULD read:
The secondary stat priority for Beast Mastery hunters is:
Hit (til capped) > Haste > Critical Strike > Mastery
^> For PVE

And:
The secondary stat priority for Beast Mastery hunters is:
Hit (til capped) > Critical Strike > Mastery > Haste
^> For PVP

Why do I tell you this? Because based on all the math sites like femaledwarf.com and elitistjerks.com did have shown the best second stat EVER for BM hunters is HASTE, followed by Critical.

You don't have to get all the BiS (best in slot) gear to get the DPS gain from haste. That's bullsh**.

People said: Lol, look at your progress on raids, you don't even know what you're talking about.

Now you can look. Just 2-3 weeks doing FL I got some gear, got some tests, and got some boss kills. In our party, we have all the best dps buffs and two hunters. Why two? Our guild don't have skilled mages/shammans to play FL yet, so one goes MM and I go BM for the Hysteria buff.

Last time we had the 7 boss fights and I surpassed the MM hunter (almost the same gear) in two of them: Baleroc and Majordomo. The others (except alysrazor, cause he is the one who flies), our dps is almost the SAME, maybe 1k of difference.

You may think: How? The MM hunter sucks then.

NO he doesn't, he's the best DPS our guild have (maybe I got the same skill as him). He knows everything about raid encounters as he knows about his rotation and stat reforging for MM.

So, why? Because those fights are almost Stand still DPS for everyone. With a little bit of aspect of Fox in it.

I surpassed him by about 2k DPS, I did 26k and he did 24k. My pve gear is haste over crit over mastery reforged.

I make a challenge with any BM hunter out there. Put in your PVE gear on www.femaledwarf.com, reforge 8% hit, HASTE > CRIT > Mastery, and if you don't get a better DPS than doing ANYTHING else, I'll never post again on Hunter threads.

PS: if you don't believe femaledwarf.com math, you can simply do it on dummies. Yes, you can do some math in BM spec on dummies. You can't if you're talented with Careful Aim, but you probably aren't.

The reason Critical Strike Rating is the most important secondary stat (after being hit capped of course) is not just because of the extra damage from Critical hits, but also because of certain talents that work off of Critical Shots. Those talents being Cobra Strikes, Invigoration, Go for the Throat, and Sic' Em!


Critical strike does not effect Cobra Strikes. It's a 15% chance on hit with arcane shot. When it procs it gives your pet a 100% chance to critical strike with it's next two basic attacks.
