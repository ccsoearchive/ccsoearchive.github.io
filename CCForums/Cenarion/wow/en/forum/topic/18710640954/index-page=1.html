Well I guess the cat is out of the bag and we cannot seem to keep it under wraps - The Moonlit Sanctuary is in fact run by the guild leaders of the Sha'tor. "Why? Isn't the Sha'tor guild enough?" Of course it is - We are not abandoning said Draenei. We love our Draenei and they have in fact been our main characters for some time now. The reason we have created such a guild is because the Draenei within Warlords was the "Flavor of the Month" - Everyone either race changed or rolled new Draenei because of the WoD expansion. Now that it is coming to a close, the Draenei community has started to quiet down and everyone is returning back to their main characters or turning their Draenei back in humans or Night Elves. With that being said, it was brought to our attention by some members that perhaps we can make a very small Kaldorei guild to work with said Draenei lull - With some of our members turning back into Night Elves and getting ready for the Legion expansion but feeling sort of bad for leaving the Sha'tor - We figured, why not just be the same guild but instead Kaldorei? For nights when the Draenei community is quiet - Members can travel on over to the Sanctuary and still be with the amazing friends they met through the Sha'tor. We do not intend to "take over" the other Kaldorei guilds, rather exist with the other Kaldorei guilds. The Sanctuary is mainly based around the random walk - up roleplay that we get in Darnassus.

As for some of the things from Silverwing Sentinels - We had an officer in a guild before the Sha'tor & before this one who created said layout of the guild(s) information. I personally was not aware of such a thing being a little pulled from the Silverwing. I did in fact go ahead and change somethings.
