Roleplayer was running strong for a bit, and then REAL LIFE happened. I am currently looking to get this project back off the ground and need help! I am currently looking for people who want to take on a leadership role in this social guild project!

Contact: Mumsie, Lyir, Isiehra, or spoonymog#1854 for details.

ABOUT <ROLEPLAYER>

What is it?
<Roleplayer> is a casual, social guild for RPers to get together and hang out.

Why join this guild?
  • You have a character that does not fit a theme guild, but you want to find people to RP with regularly.
  • Your character is limiting their RP due to theme, and you want to break away.
  • You want a inclusive, friendly community that shares a common bond (roleplay).
  • You want to have a IC channel that is not trolled.
  • You want to have an OOC chat that is not trolled.
  • You want to find RP events to attend.
  • You want to do content in a nice casual atmosphere, without the hardcore judgement.
  • You have RP alts that don't fit in your main RP guild, but you want them to have a home.


How do I join?
Read the rules of the guild here: www.tinyurl.com/roleplayerwra, then /who roleplayer in game and ask anyone online for an invite. (we are a free invite system for now).

(By accepting an invite we assume you have read the rules, and therefore are willing to adhere to them!)

More info?
The guild is small, we are currently looking for people who would like to take a leadership role so we can get this guild back off the ground.

What does "leadership role" mean?
We need people to help recruit, make the gchat a nice place to be, and encourage RP among the members. Eventually we will be looking for people who would like to take on events creation as well.

Want to think on it? Take a look at www.tinyurl.com/roleplayerwra for more info.

Motion Picture Night

Where: Announced on the ticket when sent.
When: 7 PM ((ST)), Thursday November 12th
Main contact: Isiehra, by in-game mail for invite.

((Join Roleplayer as they host their first ever IC Motion Picture Night with a showing of Neverending Story! This will be a limited invite event, as it is our first one and we want it to remain manageable. We will be actually watching a movie on a united platform (link will be available the day of), as if it were presented in-character.

We at Roleplayer assume that technology in WoW is advanced enough to present a movie on canvas, and as such that there are actors (see Tirisfal Theater Troupe as an example) that would be willing to present such plays on film. Really, it is RP and open to the imagination. If you are also open to these imaginings, feel free to send an in-game mail to Isiehra to request your ticket.
