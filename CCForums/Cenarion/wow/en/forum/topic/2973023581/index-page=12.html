In-Game Name: Tadkins

In-Game Faction: Horde

Race: Goblin

Affiliation, if any, with a short explanation:
Ultimately herself, but can work with almost any organization. She is a mercenary of sorts, a symbol of both goblin greed and a death knight's lust for war.

Alignment, with a short explanation:
Neutral Evil. Tadkins is greedy and lusts for power, but understands honor and recognizes the value of hard work. She can typically be hired as a bodyguard for the more patient, scheming types.

Additional Notes:
In the end, Tadkins seeks godhood and enlightenment. How she actually goes about this is another story. Who can say how her thought process works? She can fit into a story as either an ally or enemy.

In-Game Guild Name: Shadows and Dust

Name of GM or officers: Plagueheart, Hyrdae.

In-Game Faction: The Alliance

Affiliation, if any, with a short explanation: Shadows and Dust is ultimately a Scourge/Cult of the Damned affiliated organization. We pay allegiance to no one but those who run those organizations/factions. We make it a strict policy to stay within the bounds of the Scourge, and never ally ourselves with those who do not pay loyalty to Ner'zhul.

Alignment, with a short explanation: Because we pay allegiance to Ner'zhul, and follow his command, Shadows and Dust is considered a lawful evil guild. Everything we do has an underlining purpose, and that purpose is defined by the will of our God.

Additional Notes: While the Scourge was ultimately defeated, and a new Lich King (whom would have the remains rot from disuse) was christened in Arthas's death, those who were loyal to the ideals of the Cult of the Damned will not simply remain idle, stagnant. Our sect is here to pick up the pieces, collect cultists who have been scattered, and eventually create a new Scourge. Fully realized, and more terrible and frightening than before.
