In-Game Name: Gretig
In-Game Faction: Horde
Race: Forsaken

Affiliation: Twilight Hammer's Cult
Gretig is a long-time member of the Twilight Hammer's cult, although he has become more and more 'detached' from any actual sects. He consider's himself a cultist, and carriers out his own personal quests for power and worshiping of various idols. (From old gods to even ragnaros)

Neutral evil. Gretig is 'loyal' to those whom he serves, but he always holds his own agendas first.

Additional Notes:
Supports the Forsaken blight
Retired Apothecary
Worked Under Putress' Sect (he's an idol to him)
Eager to test out any new ideas or experiments he has
Believes in obtaining knowledge at any cost
Isn't really affiliated with any group to a serious extent.

In-Game Name: Sibelius (Ivan Felsorrow)
In-Game Faction: Horde(?)

Race: Undead Human

Affiliation: The Hand of Vengeance; The Forsaken
Ivan is currently the second head Apothecary of the Eastern Kingdoms division of the Hand of Vengeance, and thus supports usage of the Forsaken Blight.

Alignment: Lawful Evil - Explained in the notes below!

Additional Notes:
The only race that Sibelius does not hate is the Forsaken. He is an -avid- racist.

  • He despises all of the living races equally, Alliance and Horde. He sees all of them as bags of breathing scum, unfit to live on the world in which they were placed, to his annoyance. Despite this, he does not wear it emblazoned across the front of his robe. He harbors this grudge in relative secrecy, only outright speaking against a living creature if they do so to him beforehand.

  • He sees the Horde as a tool, being used by the Forsaken as a means to get rid of their current threat. He believes that after the Alliance is dealt with, the Forsaken will simply wash their hands, disposing of their "tools", so to speak.

  • There is also the increasing number of Forsaken that he does not hold in high regards. Including those who outright speak against the Banshee Queen and the Forsaken as a whole, or those who are simply unlucky enough to aggravate him.

  • He will secretly want to harvest your organs for the Hand of Vengeance's private supply.

  • He, like many of his colleagues, simply wants to see the day when Vengeance rears its head; painting the world in their own image: Green.
