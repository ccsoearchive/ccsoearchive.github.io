In-Game Name: Geslin (Aldaine)

In-Game Faction: Horde

Race: Undead

Affiliation, if any, with a short explanation: Himself. He claims loyalty to House Silvacce, but his own desires come first.

Alignment, with a short explanation: Chaotic Neutral. Geslin seeks self gratification, as well as ways to divert his attention from his...lamentable state.

Additional Notes: Geslin would make a fine antagonist to Sylvanas-loyal Forsaken groups. While not opposed to killing others, the act of resurrecting the dead is unforgivable to him, and he would like nothing more than to destroy those responsible. He is also very ambitious, and would love to see himself on the throne of Lordaeron.

Edit: He is also very subtle, and would lie about his thoughts on Sylvanas. As of now, he has done nothing to warrant suspicion from other Forsaken.

In-Game Name: Alór

In-Game Faction: Alliance

Race: Human

Affiliation, if any, with a short explanation: Kirin Tor. Political affiliation.

Alignment, with a short explanation: Lawful Evil with Chaotic Neutral tendencies.

Additional Notes: Alor is the perfect character if you are looking for an antagonist for a SL that will involve heavy political elements that may or may not include scenes of intense interrogation methods. She makes the perfect political adversary! If you are starting a cursed Gilnean SL with feral elements? Light users with Scarlet tendencies? You may just have your girl. As her player, I am constantly looking to be involved in new and interesting plots. If Alor isn't quite what you are looking for, there's the wonder of the alt...of which I am the possessor of many. Just send a tell, in game letter, or contact me OOCly and lets set something up.





In-Game Guild Name, and name of GM or officers: House DeVin. (Contact Alór, Cydri, Arranax, or Shandruin. We will point you to the appropriate direction).

In-Game Faction: Alliance.

Affiliation, if any, with a short explanation: Kirin Tor. Political Affiliate.

Alignment, with a short explanation: Lawful Neutral - Chaotic Evil. Considering all members of the guild are not actually members of the DeVin family, it is important to remember that they will have varying alignments (however never falling under good).

Additional Notes: House DeVin is seven years old. We've been around the block and know a thing or two. We can play the bad guy and play it well. You will encounter in this guild either the DeVins themselves or their myriad of allies, employees, or acquaintances. Each character able to fill a role that you may need for your plot, each player capable of doing a wonderful job of the request. Spanning from illicit trade to legitimate political aims, House DeVin offers a little of everything for the protagonist to hate and the antagonist to seek out. Feel free to contact our members if you see something that catches your eye! We're usually open to setting up RP that can be a simple scenario to large scale server-wide plots.
