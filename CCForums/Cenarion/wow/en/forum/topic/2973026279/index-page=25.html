Great post Ephineh!!

I can't believe I'm just finding this for the first time. As a fellow player on Chromaggus, and GM of one of the few remaining raiding guilds, it has been quite sad to see our server die during Cataclysm. I have played and raided on both Alliance and Horde since BWL and its definitely the lowest pop and worst conditions we've seen.

With the mass exodus of players over the last 2 quarters, were finally seeing some sort of response from Blizzard, via the LFR option come 4.3. Given the absurd profits Blizzard is making from Server Xfers and Race Changes, I was certain we would never see a server merge or free xfer again so I guess this maybe a step in the right direction for those starting to step into the raiding game.

But as a long term fix, this simply won't suffice. LFR will be great if your pvp oriented and want to dabble in pve a bit, or are a casual one to two night a week player. However for the true raiding guilds out there that strive to down every hardmode while its current, this will only harm us in the long run imo. Its just giving people another easy way to accumulate pve gear without the guild raiding atmosphere.

Hopefully after 4.3 drops and they realize that it hasnt truly helped those of us on very small server, we can look forward again in 5.0 to hoping for a merger. Or I may just give in when the Guild Transfer service comes out.
