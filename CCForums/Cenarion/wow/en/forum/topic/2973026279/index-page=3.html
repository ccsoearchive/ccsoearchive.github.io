"Quit whining and pay the transfer fees for your toons."
- No. We should not be obliged to have to leave our server at all, let alone pay to leave, as we did not cause this population issue. If anything, those who have taken transfers and moved to larger servers are making the problem worse for those who refuse to leave.

"Just quit whining and move already. Why would you play on a dead server when you can pay $100s to be happy?"
- First of all, on principle, as stated above.
- Many people refuse to leave their home servers because they have played there their entire lives and feel bound to the realm's fate.
- Many of us have lots of friends and social connections here (I daresay that it's usually "lone wolves" who only care about themselves who are the first to leave). Those of us who remain are the most loyal, stalwart, stubborn team players in the World of Warcraft. These are not the players who should be (indirectly) punished because so many others are willing to take the coward's way out and transfer.

"You should roll new toons on a bigger server and level a new guild all together."
- A nice thought, but we've already spent months leveling our current guilds and toons, making friends here, and establishing ourselves. The only thing worse than transferring all of your toons is giving up the ones you have. The guild is lost in either scenario but as someone who signed the charter of mine, I won't give it up without a guild transfer.
