Firelands was nice. 7 bosses though. 14 boss raids may be dead but they should have had 2 7's in that case. Perhaps most of the content creators are working on another project and we are seeing the results. I really feel (with the exception of arcane mage) that they nailed the class mechanics down. Sure mana regen could have *really* mattered in this expansion. But the masses ended that back in December.

All their issues have semi-easy fixes (and some very fun ones):

1) Dead pop realms/factions - Free xfer / merges from, Free xfer if faction change, etc.
2) Tanks in 5 mans - Let Demo Locks, Beast Hunters, and Enhance Sham tank. This would be extremely fun for them. You removed threat as an issue. You can add the fun mechanics of mitigation to these classes. And Beast Hunters are tanking quite a bit these days unofficially anyway. Speed up queues and add another fun element to the game.
3) Dead economies - Cross-realm AHs. Allow "Blank Pattern" posts where the customer comes up with mats. Unbind Dreamcloth / Orbs. Allow "Customer Side Requests" on the AH to have suppliers sell to them.
4) Comic relief - Much needed and a golden opportunity to allow transmog of comic style outfits. Tanking with a shovel in an evening gown should be up to the raid/guild/group. If they find it acceptable where is the problem? Some of the funnest pug 5 mans have been with someone doing RP. "This be a mighty dragon". I don't play RP realms or have an interest in it but I was cracking up and it made the grind feel faster.
5) We want to play together - LFG / LFR through real ID is nice. And while I hated Wrath's brain dead raids (minus Ulduar) people do want to play together. In hindsight this expansion needed an i346 raid. And since it would have been i346 who cares about how easy it is? You could have said "Kara hits a little harder". Up the dmg / health and people would have something to do if they couldn't do the i359+'s.

I really like to ramble. It is a curse.
