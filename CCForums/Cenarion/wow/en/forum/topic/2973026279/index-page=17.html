Seems like you're the one trolling. But I'll bite.

Firstly, just like you, we are paying customers. We pay our $15 a month, should that not entitle us to the same gameplay experience as others, without paying extra on top just to get fairness?

Secondly, our gameplay experience has changed, through no fault of our own. Our servers haven't always been this way, but have deteriorated over the past six years of the game. This problem has been exacerbated by, you guessed it, the paid services of server and faction transfers. These paid options should be optional, for changing realm to play with friends etc., not the necessity they have become for people on low realms to simply play the game they're paying for on the character of their choosing.

Imagine for a moment one of the larger servers, let's say Blackrock, suffered something (hypothetical here, and I'm not feeling very imaginative), some kind of technical fault, that made it impossible for players to group up for raids. Assume Blizzard can't fix this problem. Is it then fair to expect these players to all pony up the money for realm transfers or re-roll for something they have no control over? You're right though, this is not a fair comparison. In the case of low population realms, Blizzard can do something.

The question is, will they? They will definitely lose customers over this. They already are. Not wanting to sound like the sky is falling, but we believe we as customers have some very valid concerns here.
