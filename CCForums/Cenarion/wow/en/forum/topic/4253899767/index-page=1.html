I remember forums like these! Loved these things. I've got a few characters so, yeah.
Of course most of these would be Two Steps From Hell, but those are reserved for epic battles only. Since we're just talking themes in general, here's what I've got.:

Kairesh Fury (My night elf warrior) would have This Afternoon - Nickelback and His World - Zebrahead. This is because he's a very laid back, care free kind of individual who would happily beat the crap out of something if it was threatening the world. When he's not saving the world and being drunkenly awesome, he's just... well, drunkenly awesome in a bar and enjoying company with the rest of Feather's and some epic buddies.

Nynra Lirann Lightheart is a tough one because she's so devout and zealous. I'd have to go with Heaven Can Wait - Meatloaf, and this is because though she holds such a passion and respect to the holy Light, she still feels a need to defend the world around her before she can give herself up to the Light. For her, the peace of death and the glory of heaven can wait until other people areound her are happy.

Sathrasa is most likely On The Loose - Saga. This is an easy one to work with; Sath really doesn't care about people around here (save for a choice few) and she just goes around doing whatever the hell she wants regardless of how people feel about her or what the rules/laws of society are. She's basically already off the wall and running around doing things just because she can.

Mialera is, without a doubt, Still Alive - Jonathan Coulton, for obvious reasons; bat!@#$ insane and passionate about what she loves (murder and soul nomming), she is more than happy to destroy lives and people for her own work. And she has a backup plan in case she does actually die, which may or may not work, but insanity makes things like that awesome. And while you're dying, she'll be still alive. Probably.

Emarn Strahn would be Last of the Wilds - Nightwish and The Skyrim Bard Song - Skyrim (Cover by SongeLeReveur). This is because he's such a wanderer and journeyman; a trapper by trade and a poet at heart, Emarn travels the world to learn a good story and ply the trade of furs and meats to the markets. He loves the world around himself, and just enjoys being there.
