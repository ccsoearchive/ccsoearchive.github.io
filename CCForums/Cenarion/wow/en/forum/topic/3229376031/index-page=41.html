The priest bonuses are mixed -- poor for Holy, OK for Disc, and good to great for Shadow. Here are my ratings on a scale of 1 (useless) to 10 (boomin').

HOLY: 2P - 2. Making Lightwell cast a dependency is awkward. In many fights we pre-cast Lightwell, making the mana reduction bonus near useless. In the cases we cast during the fight, there are often timing issues to worry about, and the bonus may or may not help. NOTE: It can't possibility be every time someone CLICKS Lightwell, given that would mean you could keep the 25% reduction up all the time in certain circumstances.
DISC: 2P - 5. Only useable every two minutes (with the CD of PI) but it adds a 25% (stacking) reduction to healing spell mana costs for 10 secs. Could amount to a healthy mana savings in key situations given the hasted casting benefit of PI. Although why not make the set bonus 15 sec also to correspond with PI?
SHADOW: 2P - 4. You do more PW: D dmg, which is good, but don't regen mana when you take dmg from PW: D, which can reduce dmg. Double-edge sword.

HOLY: 4P - 3. Holy rarely uses PW: Shield, and while a 20% reduction to Serenity CD is valuable, the benefit is useless for Sanctuary, as most don't cast it when CD is up already.
DISC: 4P - 4. A buff to one of our key spells -- although only 10% of the time -- is worth something.
SHADOW: 4P - 9. 3 Orbs most of the time? Wow.

I'm sure someone said something about the feral cat set bonuses already, but I figure I add another post to the cause of hopefully changing these bonuses to something more consistent with other dps set bonuses.

2pT13: I won't exactly "complain" about this bonus much, however, while this is marginally good (and honestly, better than 4pT13), it's still not the same kind of damage boost some of the other dps classes/specs get. Ferals already weave in Ferocious Bites above 25%, and while this will be a slight dps gain, it will serve to do little more than smooth out feral dps rotation. For example, if you have 5CP and <7 sec. on Savage Roar or Rip, you no longer have to worry about which ability to prioritize first.

The second thing about 2piece is the wording.


Druid
  • Feral, 2P -- While Berserk is active, Savage Defense absorbs are 100% larger, and your Blood in the Water talent now causes Ferocious Bite to refresh the duration of your Rip on targets with 60% or less health.



    • Proper grammar would dictate that Blood in the Water talent would refresh Rip on targets at 60% health or less regardless if Berserk was active or not, but there is some confusion amongst some in the feral cat community arguing whether or not this is the case; i.e. whether Berserk must be active in order for Blood in the Water talent to refresh the duration of Rip on targets =<60%.

    4pT13: I don't even know how to begin with how bad this is. Feral Charge is a good starter, and a good way to get to/burst down adds, but apart from those very specific things, it's very unusable. A handful of people have already run tests for how this set bonus would work and found that the player would have to have inhumanly accurate response times and be able to time "travel time" (from the current location to behind to boss) to 2 seconds and use Feral Charge on CD in order for this set bonus to be viable. Whatever rotation was eased with 2pT13 is made 100x more complicated with the addition of 4 piece. Not only is adding Feral Charge into the rotation a difficult thing to do and damn near impossible to use to it's fullest potential, but it's also extremely dependent on boss mechanics and positioning. Some bosses (e.g. Sinestra, Ragnaros) are unable to be "Feral Charged," and other encounters (e.g. heroic Baleroc) extremely limit mobility to the point where it's realistically impossible to use Feral Charge except under specific circumstances.

    I understand the new raid, Dragon Soul, isn't Firelands and the bosses therein can't be compared to T12 content, but even from Dragon Soul previews and screenshots, the point remains.

    Because of the nuances of making this set bonus viable and keeping feral cat competitive with other dps classes, I believe these set bonuses should be changed in 4.3 PTR (at the very least). I'm positive the bonuses will yield extremely low numbers in comparison with other dps specs/classes with similar gear and playing ability.
