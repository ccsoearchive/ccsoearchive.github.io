Priest

  • Healer, 2P -- After using Power Infusion or Lightwell, the mana cost of your healing spells is reduced by 25% for [10|15] sec. (10 sec for Discipline, 15 for non-Discipline.)


I have multiple issues with this set-bonus for Holy priests.

1. For the Holy side of it, Lightwell is typically cast pre-emptively to an encounter. This would find the same irritation as if Power Torrent/Heartsong proc'd just before you engaged a boss since you'd have an entire internal cooldown to wait for to even see the benefit again.

2. A 25% healing spell reduction doesn't seem like it would be much benefit at all. The mana reduction lasts 15 seconds. Lightwell costs 6177 mana. It seems that for most of the buff duration you're simply just cancelling out the mana cost of Lightwell and you'd only end up getting a couple thousand mana gain. I don't feel like this is a significant enough mana gain to justify being a Tier-set-bonus.

Let's assume I'm going to be Prayer of Healing spamming just after a Lightwell cast. (Yes, I'd probably throw a Circle of Healing and Prayer of Mending in there, but for the sake of math I'm going to keep it simple with just this one spell.) The buff lasts 15seconds for Holy. As it is now, my Prayer of Healing has a 2.32sec cast time (I use a Mastery build once 12.5% after raid buffs with Haste is reached.).

If we take that 15sec buff duration and divide it by the 2.32sec cast-time I will get 6.4655 casts of Prayer of Healing in that time. The 25% mana cost that is saved from the 5353 mana cost of Prayer of Healing will be 1338.25 mana.
1338.25 mana multiplied by the 6.4655 casts is roughly 8652.478 mana. Lightwell costs 6177 mana. I'd be getting a net gain of 2475.478 mana. How is this a compelling set-bonus for a Holy Priest?

3. Another issue I have with this set bonus is it is dependent on non-mandatory talents. This statement is more directed at Holy. As it stands now, I don't know many Holy priests who opt out of Lightwell, however, we don't know what T13 content is going to be like as far as fight mechanics. As T12 went on, a lot of people began trying many specs we hadn't thought much of before. Who is to say in T13 we aren't going to want to try out specs that would prompt us to take a point out of Lightwell? If this is to happen, we instantly lose a minor mana gain (see my #2 point) or we may be so mana strained we have to stay trapped into speccing Lightwell.

This third issue is dependent on how the mechanics of the T13 encounters are designed and it's impossible for me to know if this will even become valid, but it's still something that's popped into my head and concerns me greatly. I'd be much more comfortable if the bonus was dependant on Penance or Holy Word spells - things you have automatically when you choose either tree as your spec.
