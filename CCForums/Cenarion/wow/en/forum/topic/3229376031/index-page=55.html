Just going to link to a thread I made earlier before the 9/29 updates.. I bumped it after I saw the changes (or lack thereof, in my case)

http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/3278899204#1

For those who wish not to visit another thread, I'll just make my point simply, here.

The Enhancement Shaman 2pc T13 set bonus... fine.. lightning bolts hitting harder, more dependency on spell damage buffs/debuffs - used to it by now. It's not ideal, but we can't always get +stormstrike/+lava lash buffs for every tier. I would've liked to have seen something windfury-related in this slot, though. Perhaps... "Your windfury attacks have a ?% chance to..." or "Your windfury critical strikes grant you ...." or whatever.

The 4pc set bonus, however, is atrocious. We already lose a significant amount of maelstrom weapon procs without hard-casting lightning bolts during ability downtime.. and creating a new maelstrom-generating mechanic (and tying it into our pitiful excuse for a DPS cooldown) is going to force unwanted change into our priority - effectively spamming lightning bolts (like an elemental shaman) during feral spirits (supposedly an enhancement signature ability) Not to mention that even under ideal circumstances, this set bonus (simulated using current PTR mechanics) does not yield a proportionate DPS gain of other set bonuses. In fact, it's far enough behind even our 2pc set bonus that undoubtedly enhancement shaman would opt for 2pc T13 and either 2pc T12(H) or whatever other hit/mastery gear may be available in T13 content.

My suggestion, if you want a 4pc set bonus centered around Feral Spirits.. first make them scale better to do reasonable damage in a raid environment and appropriate for a measurable DPS cooldown... or, as it has been suggested, make their damage 'nature' and have it adjusted by our mastery. Then use the set bonus to reduce it's cooldown (similar to a shadowfiend) or find a way to trigger it to be extended once active.
