    Horrible warrior dps bonuses...

    Inner Rage is suppose to be a way to get rid of excess rage.

    Almost totally useless

    Can't really say this much better but I think I'll add some detail. The 2 pc is actually somewhat frustrating since while less rage cost means a significantly higher Damage Per Rage on HS it's when I am attempting to "dump" that rage. During something like Heroism or Unholy Frenzy effects it could make it more likely that I rage cap thus possibly wasting either as much as or at least some of the rage I was able to "save" marginalizing the gain. It WILL help on CS rotations for an additional HS inside the 6 secs but that, while a definite gain, isn't consistent when I could only burn Inner Rage on every other CS and even then would have to delay inner rage to line up the usage on the 3rd CS.

    The 4 pc needs to be changed.

    09/24/2011 06:00 PMPosted by Zarhym
    DPS, 4P -- Your Execute critical strikes have a 20% chance to refresh the duration of Colossus Smash.


    Problem #1:
    Refresh.......... not adds the additional duration but merely refreshes. So if I am lucky enough to spam an execute, and crit, and proc this poor bonus the GCD after I hit CS I have gained a whopping 1.5 seconds back. Gee thanks for something astounding. Add the full duration back so instead of now 6 seconds after the proc I have 10.5 more seconds to attempt to get another proc.

    Problem #2:
    To try and get the proc this requires Warriors to play outside of optimal performance rotations to attempt at a gain. IF you get a proc, that might have been a gain. If you did not you cost yourself and your raid damage.

    Problem #3:
    Odds. Sheer horrible odds. As stated before lets just go with me having 25% chance to crit. With ZERO latency it could be theoretically possible to get 4 GCDs in during a CD buff of 6 seconds. We however do not play in a vacuum and that isn't possible. So in 3 attacks, assume all 3 are execute, with a 25% chance that it could crit, then to have a 20% chance to proc a buff reset back to 6 seconds, all only during the final and what had better be fastest phase of the entire encounter. Please check my math here but that makes a 15% chance that by ruining your rotation you get a single proc that then still only resets the duration.

    So simply stated it is a buff to the shortest portion of the fight and one that can neither be relied upon or even called a gain in damage since there is no hope of assuring that breaking optimal cycles of DPS would even result in a proc at all.

    Problem #4:
    The lack of synergy between the 2pc and 4 pc is unfortunate. What makes the Hunter set bonus' so incredibly strong isn't just the 2pc or 4 pc, it is that the 1 bolsters the other. More focus allows more time to spend burning Arcane shots with excess Focus. Then those added Arcane shots are the trigger for their haste buff on the 4 pc. The one aids the other.

    Here however you see an exclusion of the 2 bonuses. It simply is not possible to do both at once unless looking at an infinite rage model. With possibly burning as much as 60 rage per GCD in execute and HS at the same time there is not other way to generate enough rage to use them both. How ever on an infinite rage model to allow for this much rage generation there is no or marginal gain come from a lower rage cost on either ability. Thus still nullifying the gain from the 2 pc.
