Restoration, 2P -- After using Mana Tide Totem, the cost of your healing spells are reduced by 25% for 15 sec.Restoration, 4P -- Increases the duration of Spiritwalker's Grace by 5 sec, and you gain 30% haste while Spiritwalker's grace is active.


I gotta say, maybe it's just how I play my resto sham, but it seems like they're tying the bonuses to abilities that are listed under the 'oh crap' catagory of spells. Mana Tide I use in maybe half of raid boss fights, and Spiritwalker's grace..... I think I have only ever used a handful of times since they came out with it. I just hardly ever need it.

I agree that IF I ever do use SWG, it would be helpful, as it allows you to get more out of it. But, in the case of the 2P bonus, by the time mana tide is out, I generally try to spend as much time possible NOT casting...since I'm trying to get mana back. But that bonus would make it hurt less should you have to squeeze a few heals off.

A much better sounding bonus for resto shams (In my opinion, though it may be OP):

2P: Your Resurgence has an (X)% chance to return twice the mana it normally would when it triggers, and your Water shield will regenerate 1 orb every (Y) seconds upto the maximum of 3 charges.

4P: Increases the range your chain heal will jump by (X) yards, and allows it to jump 'through' undamaged friendly targets up to (Y) times, although every jump in this way reduces it's next heal's effectiveness by an additional (Z)%



I have always been a fan of chain heal, but when people are spread out, AE healing as sham really suffers. It would be nice to find a way to boost that somehow. I thought about making Healing Surge castable while moving, but couldn't figure out a good way to balance it out fairly... (increased mana cost, add a cooldown to it, make it add a cooldown to another heal like GHW maybe, not able to proc any talents, I dunno) I never use HS because it's so mana hungry already, but figureed that'd be a good way to add mobility heals.

@Malmilu

I totally agree. The current set bonuses for resto shamans are near useless.

My suggestions would be:

Resto 2p -- After the use of Mana tide totem, a bubble will be placed on all members affected by mana tide that will absorb damage equal to [x]% of the shaman's maximum mana.

I feel this keeps with the idea that I generally try to conserve mana after mana tide totem and If the raid isn't taking damage that would be nice. It also makes mana tide totem a very interesting defensive cd that scales with gear.

Resto 4p -- Your Riptide initial heal has an [x]% chance to spread earthliving to [x] targets, the targets are prioritized based on health.

Riptide is a spell I will use regardless of the situation and when people are spread out I am casting it every available cd.

Other alternatives to the 4p I've been thinking of include:

* It could also be the riptide hot that has a chance to spread, but I feel like that could result in way too many earthliving procs.

* I was unsure if it should spread the riptide hot itself. I feel it would definitely be more powerful, but perhaps unfair.

I feel these are better changes (they may be op), but I feel they are more in the right direction.
