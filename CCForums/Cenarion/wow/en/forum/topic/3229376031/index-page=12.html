Not to sound rude, and I hope that the blues are still reading this and tweaking the bonuses -

As a holy priest main, your set bonuses for holy are off the wall bad. Lightwell has fallen into serious disuse in raids, it's put down at the start of a fight at best. It's an immobile heal that helps in certain situations, and it has a very long cooldown. A cooldown and mana cost that usually doesn't warrant it being used more than once in a fight. (At the start.)

The different between lightwell and, say, innervate for a resto druid, is that lightwell is pretty much a passive, situational spell that rarely gets used. Some raiding holy priests don't even spec into it because it's considered a waste of a talent point. It was decent at the start of cataclysm but it's shown its age as a poor talent that should have been revamped ages ago. And yet you're adding a bonus for it. Innervate restores mana to druids and it's useful in almost every situation. Lightwell is not. I'm not trying to say the druid set bonus is overpowered, I'm just saying that it's better. if you want to have this kind of a set bonus, have it automatically drop the CD on hymn of hope, make it a non-channeled spell, and add the same reduction in mana use afterward. THAT would be on par.

Additionally for 4 piece, there's a reason that most priests have gone disc this late in the expansion. It's because the holy word spells are NOT good anymore. You boosted sanctuary...slightly...but that still mostly doesn't make it worth the large casting cost and the limited healing ability it has. Nobody is going to want to drain their mana for a painfully useless spell even faster.

And then the single target holy word is almost a non-issue. I assume that bonus would be really great in 5 man heroics...too bad it's meant for the upper tier of raiding. I'm a holy priest by choice, but I don't really have a defense when people tell me it's the lesser of the two specs anymore. The 4 piece disc bonus is heads and tails above the holy one, and it's going to be utter laffles in PVP.

Again. These priest bonuses are REALLY looking great for 5 man usage. Not so much for raiding, which I assume is their intended use.

So bottom line, for holy priests these bonuses are terrible. It doesn't much matter to me because I likely won't be seeing the content, but you're doing priests a disservice given how good the other healing set bonuses are. Well...some of them. You futzed a couple of the others up there too.

At least mages don't have anything to complain about. As usual.

Vulgrym

Warrior

  • Protection, 2P -- Your Revenge ability now also grants a physical absorption shield equal to 20% of the damage done by Revenge.
  • Protection, 4P -- Your Shield Wall ability now grants 50% of its effect to all party and raid members.

  • DPS, 2P -- Heroic Strike costs 10 less rage while Inner Rage is active.
  • DPS, 4P -- Your Execute critical strikes have a 20% chance to refresh the duration of Colossus Smash.

Yikes.

Protection, 4P - You guys let raid CDs get out of control in Cataclysm and this just exacerbates the problem. I assume something like this is going to be important for the Deathwing encounter? Either way, I guess your overzealous distribution of raid-wide CDs won't be reigned in until next expansion.

DPS, 2P - Continues the 2P trend of irrelevancy established in the current tier. Also, it's more favorable to Fury (i.e. far more likely to swim in Rage). The T11 2P was similarly imbalanced between the 2 specs, since Fury has free GCDs and Arms can't ever (due to Slam).

DPS, 4P - Just ... awful design for Fury. Let's see. Execute can only be used sub-20%. The window of opportunity is lowered further by requiring a critical strike (let's say 1 in 3 chance, which is optimistic). And then, that critical strike needs to happen within the 3 GCD window afforded by CS? To the point -- usage is too narrowly defined to be exciting or even beneficial for that spec. For Arms? You can have some insane RNG streaks towards the end of the fight since CS windows are more readily available, but again confining the benefit to sub-20% is kind of disappointing (although this insulates it from PvP overpoweredness nicely). Overall - would like an expansion-ending 4P bonus without such narrow usage (i.e. execute range, which is tiny for Warriors, unlike some other specs that start at 35%).

EDIT - just realized, for Fury to even benefit from the 4P bonus, you'd need to spam Execute during that CS window. If you get "unlucky" and don't crit (chances are actually decent that this could happen!), you've tossed out your rotation temporarily in the hope of an Execute crit and suffered a DPS loss in the process. There is no way this bonus can go live.
