For example, now you can pop DG during Shannox burn phase, since it'll pretty much cover the entire raid when the spear AoE happens.

The range increase gives us far more option on when to utilize them, and the CD reduction pretty much guarantees us to have two instances per encounter to use them.

It's a great piece bonus if you stop thinking stubbornly at how it used be utilized, and how it can be used now.
