The priest 4pc is quite bad for Disc, unless it is being accompanied by other mechanic changes that make shield spamming more compelling. Combine the short duration of PW:S and the current expense and you end up with a bonus that, if it procs, likely is only useful on a tank. Shouldn't 4pc bonuses be desirable/exciting?

The 2pc for Disc is not great; even spamming shield (our most expensive spell) with a 1.1 GCD (after gearing and Borrowed Time), that's 9 casts in 10 seconds and around 5400 mana -- so 20% of that is around 9700 mana, which is 400 mp5. But that's the *best* case scenario and rarely do we cast that many expensive spells. In reality it will likely be probably 200-250 mp5.

It would be better to make those kinds of reductions a fixed mana reduction for the next N casts. Then it doesn't matter how expensive the spells you use are or the time they take to cast, making the Healer bonus much more compelling (applies to other healers as well).
