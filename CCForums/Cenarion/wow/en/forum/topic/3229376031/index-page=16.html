I am extremely disappointed in the set bonuses for Holy Priests.

First off, the 2 piece:
To use Lightwell to its maximum efficiency, you place it before the pull starts and BEFORE high damage spikes. You have to have time to announce where the Lightwell is being placed so your raiders know where it is, and it is much better casted before the spike rather than wasting a gcd casting it during. I would really appreciate it if you guys would stop emphasizing Lightwell so much. No matter what you do it will always require player interaction and it hinders us holy priests. All in all, we will get maybe 5-7 seconds of this 2 piece once a fight.

Now the 4 piece:
Holy Word spell cooldown reduction will not help at all. In 25mans you stay in sanctuary for everything. Holy Word: Sanctuary is a good spell, don't get me wrong, but it is VERY situational. We already do NOT use this spell on cooldown, so what would reducing the cooldown do for us? It is extremely mana intensive and holy priests have the worst mana regen mechanics of any healer. Even if you are in Serenity it isn't worth it. Yes, it is an efficient heal, but it heals for almost nothing, and the buff it grants only lasts a very short amount of time.

These set bonuses are good for Disc, but completely take Holy out of the little viability that we had. Completely. Useless.

Priest
  • Healer, 2P -- After using Power Infusion or Lightwell, the mana cost of your healing spells is reduced by 25% for [10|15] sec. (10 sec for Discipline, 15 for non-Discipline.)
  • Healer, 4P -- Your Power Word: Shield has a 10% chance to absorb 100% additional damage, and the cooldown of your Holy Word abilities is reduced by 20%.


The Holy bonuses for both 2pc and 4pc seem to be extremely lackluster and don't solve the issues that Holy seems to have trouble with. The 4pc especially feels like it will be incredibly encounter-dependent. Many Holy Priests already skip the Tome of Light talent (30% Holy Word CD).

I would love to see a 4pc that has something to do with fixing Divine Hymn, or potentially making Holy Word: Sanctuary more appealing to fights that aren't stack-driven (larger radius, etc).

I'd also love to see a 2pc that doesn't get completely wasted on Lightwell. Lightwell is a very specific cooldown, as it must be placed in a certain position and timed correctly. Power Infusion, on the other hand, is an all-purpose haste & mana reduction CD that has nothing to do with positioning. If your raid moves while Power Infusion is up, you haven't lost much (except a few seconds of the buff). If your raid moves while Lightwell is down, you lose the entire Lightwell. Power Infusion is much easier to use "on cooldown" than Lightwell. Putting the two cooldowns on the same level doesn't feel right. Connecting the 2pc with Hymn of Hope - as well as tweaking that spell a bit - could give Holy a good feeling about the set bonuses, while rounding out the spec. Right now, Holy just feels left in the dust.

Edit: Giving Holy a way to get a tiny bit more out of Intellect - instead of nearly ditching it entirely - would be a nice way to welcome new gear that isn't entirely Spirit-driven.
