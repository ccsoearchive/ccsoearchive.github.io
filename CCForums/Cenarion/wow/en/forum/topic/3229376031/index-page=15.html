hunter 2 piece looks OP


I admit at first glance it *looks* OP. But with even a minute of thought you can gather why it won't actually be OP.

It will be a significant boost to survival and BM for sure. It may even be enough to catch them up to everyone else. But the 2 specs are pretty far behind and scale worse then most specs in the game (even MM is dropping to mid pack contesting with other classes worst dps specs) so this will in no way rocket them ahead of everyone. It simply means they may be worth bringing to a raid.

MM will actually get the least bonus from it. Since you will still need to keep improved steady shot going. Which means 4 steady in a 10 second rotation. I'm sure the number crunchers are working on a rotation as we speak to bleed off all that extra focus while still maintaining the imp steady buff. That will be a pretty big battle for alot of MM hunters. Capping focus is a dps loss and losing the 15% haste buff is a dps loss.


But either way, it's a very interesting set bonus. And it may even start a little strong, but hunters as a whole are scaling behind everyone else. So the gap, if there even is one with everyone else also getting a jump in ilvl and scaling better then hunters, won't last long at all.

I really hope it makes the other 2 specs competitive again. The 4k+ dps gap has been a bit to hard to justify running either of the specs, and that gap is even larger when compared to top specs which MM (4k ahead of those 2) isn't one of said top specs. I really look forward to getting a chance to playtest this in anycase.

It looks strong, and if hunters were owning the meters right now it would def be OP. But nothing in wow exists in a vacuum.

The Feral Bonuses are interesting for bears- there are 2 versions of berserk (as a buff), the 3m cd, and the short, 6s mangle cd reset proc from lacerate tick. If it applies to BOTH versions, it would be very good. Otherwise, it turns berserk into a sort of pseudo damage reduction cd, which isn't bad either.

The 4set for bear could be really nice- ideally it won't have the current problem where it alters max hp without adjusting % (in other words, adding to max but not current hp). Being able to shift to bear as a cat and pop that will also be a plus, depending on how that works out in terms of persistence.

Cat 2 set seems kinda underwhelming. Yes, I'm a cat mainspec. First, it makes the dps cycle/rotation/priority/whatever you want to call it far less interesting. Second, altering the blood in the water uptime in a simulation to 60% (assuming even spread, I realize that not turning off 4pc t12 would alter things, the entire point was to see what happened with higher BITW uptime.) upped dps by about 560. The current 2set bonus (t12) accounted for well over 1k dps on its own, for comparison. Making the rotation simpler below 60%, and being weaker than 10% damage from shred as a DoT make the set bonus appear far less interesting and fun. Maybe blizzard could do something similar to rogues, and attach an energy cost reduction to something, but preferably something that either leaves our current cycle/rotation intact, or modifies it in some other way (perhaps allowing shred to extend mangle or rake? Just throwing out ideas). No, my sims aren't an end all, no, they aren't thorough- this was just a quick "What happens if I sim 60% of the fight with BITW up instead of 25% with no other changes), but the bonus being around half the strength of the t12 2pc is kind of surprising for an expansion end tier.

Cat 4set just seems... not sure what to say really. On a single target fight without much movement, it'll be a single extra ravage over the entire fight, which is extremely lackluster. Maybe there won't be any of those this tier... and maybe there won't be any bosses that kill you if you feral charge (fandral- can't feral charge during cat phase when he jumps without going into a fire pool, rag- can't charge period without dying- adds can be charged too, assuming you have another person to stun, those are just two examples). It would be a nice bonus if say, Feral Charge didn't have a minimum range, but they got rid of the few bosses where it effectively didn't a while ago. It is decent for add switches (every 30s). Still, thinking back to t10 in icc, they tested a new mechanic change for feral with the tier bonus- namely, letting rake crit as well as rip. Maybe t13 4pc is time to try letting feral bleeds scale with haste, since we're the only DoT class in the game that does not?

Edit: TL:DR- Bear bonuses look very nice, could use some clarification on the 2set for bears on what exactly counts as berserk (the 3m cd or that and the proc off lacerate ticks). The cat bonuses... please change them. Please?
