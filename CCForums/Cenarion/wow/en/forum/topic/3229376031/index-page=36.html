[quote]

As far as soul shards, what makes you think they are going to fix it? All I see happening at this point is affliction will now be casting soul burn, which is otherwise useless to them save for aoe. So we will now have to include a spell in our rotation that serves no real purpose for us as aff.


Well can you really imagine a 4pc bonus that will only work for 1/2 to 1/3rd of a fight (after we run out of shards)? I give Blizzard more credit than that, thus the potential for a change to the soul shard/soul burn mechanic. Just my opinion.


I could absolutely see them doing something like that, and give them no more credit than I already have. Look at how long Cata has been out, and they STILL haven't created a viable use for soul shards with affliction, save for the occasional soulburn for aoe SoC.

There has been plenty of times where set bonuses did little to nothing for some specs, so I don't see why that would change now. I could care less about our set bonuses personally so much as our dps is up to par.

The only things I don't like about the current set bonuses is that our 4-piece is going to cause us to have to regularly use soulburn in affliction somehow and that giving demo a different time for the Doomguard and Infernal is just going to cause imbalance issues down the road.

Another issue to think about... say you're in a boss fight that has occasional adds. If you cast soulburn too close to when the adds are about to spawn and soulburn is still on cd at that point, you lose out on your soulburn for SoC, missing out on a ton of extra damage (that is if the adds will live long enough for you to do so). However if you hold off too long on using your soulburn, then you are losing out on extra damage overall. It is not just one extra thing for us to have to watch but several more as we'd have to be keeping an eye on spawn timers, phase changes, soulburn cd, along with everything else that goes along with our spec. It's just going to complicate us even more.

One solution to soul shards, at least for affliction, would be for our Haunt spell to award us a soul shard on its return. It would make perfect sense that the spell would be able to do so, but of course would only solve the soul shard issue for one spec out of the 3.
