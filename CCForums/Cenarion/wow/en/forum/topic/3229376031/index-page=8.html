For DPS warriors, the 2pc bonus is pretty decent. Most warriors will likely macro IR onto maybe Colossus Smash or something similar given that's where we want most of our burst, and allowing the possibility of 3 Heroic Strikes under CSmash's debuff is pretty good. You don't even have to use HS on every CD during IR's duration even, but the option is there if you want it. It'll also be much better during burn phases where Hero/BL/TW is up. I'm liking it.

The 4pc bonus, however, is pretty damned atrocious. Not only does it require the boss to be in execute phase (so 80% of the fight is already over and your 4pc bonus has been 100% useless until then), but you need to use Execute in a 6 second window and hope for a crit. Not only do you need a crit, but you only have a 20% chance off of that crit to refresh CSmash's duration. That's a pretty low chance, and it's probably one of the worst DPS bonuses I've seen on a warrior set. Fury warriors currently have a pretty high crit chance, but Arms warriors don't have the same thing going for them.

I do hope you intend to bake the 4p12 set bonus for frost into the spec, because as it stands, that's the only reason it's competitive with arcane before you get Dragonwrath. Arcane pulling ahead by about 4k after both specs acquire it...

The mage T13 bonuses seem a bit underwhelming. 500 haste works out to ~3.9%, and it's a stacking effect that's consumed. There's a reason we didn't like Heart of Ignacious.

AP cooldown reduction doesn't work without evocation to back it up if it's going to continue to increase the mana cost. You already delay using it until evocation comes up as is. Consider making evocation cooldown reduction instead.

IV goes from 13.8% uptime to 23.8%. This means IV's averaged contribution for the duration of it+cooldown length goes from 2.76% haste to 4.76%, and drops us another 3.9% as it expires.

The interaction between the two bonuses should be scrapped. Consider increasing the cooldown reduction on IV. 4p12-frost needs to be baked into the spec.

Edit: A clarification would be appreciated as well. Is the cooldown reduction applied by refreshes of the stacking buff or is it just applied by the stacked consumed at the end? Does this drop IV to 1.4m or does it drop it to what could presumably be as low as 30 seconds? That would make it head and shoulders above what most are reading it as, and would make my above analysis a moot point, as losing 4% haste in exchange for a radically low cooldown IV would make up for it.
