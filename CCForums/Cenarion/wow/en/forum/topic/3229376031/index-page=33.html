* Blood, 2P -- When an attack drops your health below 35%, one of your Blood Runes will immediately activate and convert into a Death Rune for the next 20 sec. This effect cannot occur more than once every 45 sec.
* Blood, 4P -- Your Vampiric Blood ability also affects all party and raid members for 50% of the effect it has on you.

* DPS, 2P -- Sudden Doom has a 30% chance and Rime has a 60% chance to grant 2 charges when triggered instead of 1.
* DPS, 4P -- Runic Empowerment has a 25% chance and Runic Corruption has a 40% chance to also grant 710 mastery rating for 12 sec when activated.


Revised Changes could be:

* DPS 2P -- Runic Empowerment has a 25% chance and Runic Corruption has a 40% chance to also grant 305 mastery rating for 12 seconds when activated.
* DPS 4P -- When you successfully use Death Coil, Frost Strike, or Rune Strike, you have a 45% chance to activate two random fully depleted runes. Runic Corruption runic regeneration rate is increased by 3 seconds.

* Blood, 2P -- Your Will of the Necropolis ability now procs when a damaging attack brings you below 30% of your maximum health. The cooldown on your Rune Tap ability is refreshed, your next Rune Tap has no cost, and all damage taken is reduced by 35% for 8 sec. This effect cannot occur more than once every 45 seconds.
* Blood, 4P -- Your Vampiric Blood ability also affects all party and raid members and increases its cooldown by 30 seconds.
