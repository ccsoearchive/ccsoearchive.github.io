Let me start by saying that I know you all at Blizzard work very hard to come up with these set bonuses and try to make them fair across all classes and specs. We (as a community) do appreciate all the hard work you put in coming up with our new Tier sets with every new major patch that comes out. If we didn't, we wouldn't still be playing and trying to nit pick on the changes that you make. That being said, there will always be those of us that view the bonuses as skewed, and perhaps they are a bit, but it could also be because we (as a community) don't truly know all of the classes and how their game play is like during current content. That all being said, I am now going to nit pick.

Looking at all the classes and their teir bonuses, it looks like tanks get to give a huge bonus to their raid and healers get a huge bump to their healing. Dps, some of it looks awesome (depending on spec) and some of it looks like a complete waste (again, depending on spec).

For example:
09/24/2011 06:00 PMPosted by Zarhym
Shadow, 4P -- Your Shadowfiend and Shadowy Apparitions have a 100% chance to grant you 3 Shadow Orbs each time they deal damage.

This just seems a little OP to me as my Shadowy Apparitions tend to spawn rather quickly. Does this mean that the Apparitions themselves might be changing, or are priests just going to be that much more awesome at dps? (Not that I'm really complaining on this one as one of my alts is a spriest.)

At the opposite end of the spectrum:
09/24/2011 06:00 PMPosted by Zarhym
Balance, 4P -- Starsurge generates 100% extra Lunar or Solar energy while Eclipse is not active.

So, instead of 15 energy druids will get 30 energy while eclipse is not active. This helps you move through the bar a LITTLE faster, but really, not that great in my eyes. Personally, if this remains to be the set bonus, I'm seriously considering keeping 2pc T12 and 2pc T13 as my set. Either that, or switch over to tanking.
