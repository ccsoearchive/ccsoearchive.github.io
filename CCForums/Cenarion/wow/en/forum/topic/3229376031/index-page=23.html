Paladin

  • Retribution, 2P -- Your Judgement ability has a 50% chance to generate 1 Holy Power.
  • Retribution, 4P -- While Zealotry is active your abilities deal 12% more damage.

    As stated by many others in regards to some of the bonuses:

    Blood 2p
    Blood DK bonus is somewhat lackluster in comparison to other tanks gaining an extra absorb; it may very well give the ability to cast another death strike, which in 4.3 will grant a damage shield regardless of it being dodged/etc, but that still requires 1 global after the effect takes place to make use, and can only activate every 45 seconds.

    Hunter bonuses:
    2p is insanely good. 4p is less so, especially with Arcane shot not really part of the Survival rotation. Bonuses would make more sense if the 2p and 4p were switched, and adapted a bit for Survival.

    Holy Paladin 4p:
    It's quite obvious with the aim of all the set bonuses both for tank and healing specs that raid-wide aoe damage will be very extensive, but the adjustment to holy radiance is very small in comparison. I believe the 20% increase is a bit small, 40% probably would be required to make it even noticable.

    Prot Paladin 4p:
    While the bonus is nice, the lack of imagination here is pretty staggering.

    Holy Priest 2p:
    Using lightwell as a damage reduction seems awkward as it is often preplaced before pulls and then maintained afterwards, very difficult to make full use of.

    Shadow 2p:
    As mentioned, if this interferes with regaining mana, it might very well be a liability instead of a dps increase.

    Rogue 2p/4p
    Tying in bonuses to tricks is getting old. Any top end rogue will be using tricks on cooldown regardless of the threat buff for tanks. The bonus honestly feels very little difference between current 4p bonus, activating on cooldown for a passive bonus.

    On 4p, in current standings of things, Shadow Dance is more of an energy reduction or CP/Energy reduction than it is a DPS cooldown; the biggest DPS gain for Shadow dance is from reapplying Find Weakness, which is refreshed upon the last Ambush and lasting after SD fades. I can not even see this being a 100 DPS increase for Subtlety.
    On 4p for Combat, with current energy capping this bonus is once again, almost negligible. Even if activating AR with 0 Energy, with the amount of finishers performed in combination with the Glyph of Sinister strike+Relentless strikes procs, energy capping is almost guaranteed before AR has even halfway faded. Adding duration to this ability is once again not very lucrative, if at all.
    The 4p bonus gives most benefit to Vendetta, but still seems very, very small in comparison to the 2p bonus or even current tier 4p bonus.

    Both of these set bonuses for combat will probably be forgone in favor of a better off-set piece unless set pieces have better substats.

    Elemental 4p
    With the haste capping issues that Elemental currently already has, adding in a random haste proc is probably very detrimental to their play and not as strong at higher gear levels where passive haste is already at a high value.

    Warrior DPS 2p/4p
    Both of these bonuses seem to have very, very small windows of opportunity where they are usable.

    THESE BONUSES WILL PROBABLY BE FORGONE FOR 99% OF RAIDERS:
    Survival Hunters - 4p
    Holy Paladin - 4p
    Shadow priest - 2p IF THEY CANNOT REGAIN MANA WITH SWD
    Holy Priest - 2p, and possibly 4p
    Rogue - 4p, for all 3 specs. Combat : 2p. Assassination/Subtlety will still get 2p.
    Elemental - 4p. Haste capping on proc = not cool.
