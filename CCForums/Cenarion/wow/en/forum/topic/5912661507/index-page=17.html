Basically we are an rp guild dedicated to the Glory of the Sindorei. We are a Fellowship and actually have goblins, and Forsaken in the guild as well as a few others, but mainly Blood elves. The main focus is rp, we have several storylines going right now. We could use a few more good people who are willing to foil the evil rising.

We have romance, intrigue and some good drama, that is good rp when we get together and play it out. The Journal is a way for us to develop our characters. It gives each of us an insight into the minds of the others. Of course it is like a diary, in that most of it is not known IC to the others.

The Journal is also where we are developing our main storyline. You only need to skim through it and see the main villian is Viragona and we have a couple more who are trying to out evil her.

Easiest is to make an alt and /who Rising Sun and message a member, we can invite you to our OOC chat channel and you can ask questions.
