I had a rather amusing incident today and I felt I needed to make a comment. Someone wished to join the Fellowship and asked for the rules and so forth. This person typed very poorly, had a lot of trouble understanding what we were saying and eventually quit, saying we had too many rules and it was too much to keep track of.

That said, I am willing to help anyone who is new to rp to learn it and to help them practice on the intricacies of immersive rp. But this is not for everyone. The person who wishes to rp has to be willing to make an effort to learn the basic rules of rp and the ettiquette involved. You have to have enough of a knowledge of English to be not only typing clearly and making yourself understood, but able to understand what is being said to you. If English is a second language to you, (And I am not biased at all!) this would be a good way to improve your skills. Writing and reading are two of the most important factors in doing rp. But you must be advanced enough to be comfortable taking time to actually learn the skills involved.

This is a good guide to in game rp. We should all be directing people to this who are new to rp. It is very helpful. Yes there is a lot there, but it is important that you learn all these terms and rules if you expect to be taken seriously.

http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/2016793577

I also highly advise getting a few addons to help, such as MyRolePlay (MRP) or TotalRp2 from curse. I also use tongues, and there are others out there to enhance your rp experience.

Hello Deathlyknigh, I do hope you will come up with a better name than that. To help you out I have offered a link here to get you familiar with the ins and outs of in game rp.

http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/2016793577

The guild is Rising Sun Fellowship, the server is Cenarion Circle. Faction is Horde. We prefer Blood elves as most of our rp take splace in Silvermoon City, and the guild's main goal is returning the Glory to the Sindorei.

We have a very complex and intriguing story happening involving a warlock(evil of course) a crazy mage (insane!), lots of romance and kidnappings, rp battles with roll fighting, and a generally helpful and fun group of people who will rp at just about any time of day or night.

If you are interested, you only need to make a horde character on the Cenarion Circle realm and contact one of us for any questions. If you look through the Journal and the OOC threads you will see many names that are very frequent posters.

We have had beginners come into the guild and we are willing to help. But you must agree to rp and Journal your experiences. This is an excellent opportunity to improve your writing skills.
