This is how I've always imagined a RP should go, but the problem with coming up with a story line to start is that sometimes those who get involved may - without meaning to - make choices that throw things off-course from the original idea the starting individual concocted.

It's similar to if the game Skyrim were to suddenly go live and everyone merged into one world - Bethesda would have some massive civil wars break out between the players pushing for their quests to be completed. Hell, don't get me wrong, I'd love to try this out - I'm just being the one to throw it out there that if we make this more player-driven then we need to be prepared to accept that some people may make a decision that causes a dramatic change-up in what was most likely to go down. Though, I've personally always like adaptation to the unexpected, so I'll remain fairly neutral through-out all our aims.
