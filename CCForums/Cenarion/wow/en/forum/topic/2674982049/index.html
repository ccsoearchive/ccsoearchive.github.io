Welcome to Cenarion Circle!

From my own experience, most, if not all, RP guilds here are very welcoming of newcomers. Mostly, just expect an application and an interview as part of the process of joining an RP guild. We have to separate the wheat from the chaff, as it were. Not that anyone will necessarily be judging your worth as a role-player, but most guilds have a theme that they try to stick to, and would like to see if your character is a good fit for that theme. My own guild (AAMS and AAMS Alliance Branch) requires both an application and an interview, although both are fairly casual.

In terms of which guilds are known for helping out newer role-players, my only experience is with <Azeroth College> Alliance side and <Homeland> Horde side. I am sure there are others that I am ignorantly failing to mention, so I would suggest joining the RP channels and asking around. Those channels can be found here: http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/1083609464

I can only really speak for myself (and to some small extent, the AAMS) but I look forward to speaking with you in-game! Once again, welcome to Cenarion Circle, and enjoy your stay with us!
