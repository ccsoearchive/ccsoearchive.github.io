The rider of a bronze drake, covered almost head-to-toe in green-hued armor, swoops down through the streets, barely avoiding passerby as he makes his pass down towards the bulletin boards of all major cities. Orgrimmar, Stormwind, Silvermoon, Darnassus, Thunder Bluff, Dun Morogh...nowhere is safe. Even the goblin cities are bombarded, their bulletin boards victims of cumbersome, messily-written advertisements with an urgent message.

Throm'ka! I am Drakehide, son of Mok'Rukh, of the sons of Rukh. As you may (or may not know), sons of Rukh have GLOOOOORIOUS singing voices. Or so, I am told.

Now, this is a glorious thing, but what is NOT so glorious is our ability to write lyrics. And why should we write, I ask you? We have more important jobs to do! We are WARRIORS! We crush our enemies, see them driven before us, and hear the lamentation of their women...and THEN, time permitting, then we sing songs. AFTER we conquer. Or sometimes, while we conquer. Whistle while you work, as old Mok'Rukh once said.

AHEM. I digress.

I have composed a most glorious piece, but I find myself lacking lyrics. And so, I, Drakehide, son of Mok'Rukh, beg your assistance in this matter. It does not matter if you are scholars, bards, saints, soldiers, brigands, or bums - if you can think of lyrics, your aid would be of great assistance to me.

And when this song becomes famous, and the name of Drakehide is raised up high and exalted [and, of course, GLOOOOORIOUS, and such,] glory shall be yours as well!

As it stands, I require suggestions for the following words:

1. CHARACTER ARCHETYPE

2. OBJECT

3. SOMETHING MUSICAL YOU PLAY

4. ADJECTIVE

5. SOMETHING YOU WEAR

6. PROFESSION

7. NOUN

8. NAME

9. SOMETHING DESIREABLE FOR FREE

10. SOMETHING YOU LIGHT ON FIRE

11. GUILD NAME

12. VERB

13. SOMETHING YOU WISH YOU COULD FORGET

14. ENDEARING TERM

15. SOMETHING NOISY

16. SOMETHING THAT SMELLS BAD

17. BODY PART

18. PLACE

Many thanks in advance for your assistance. Aka'Magosh - may the spirits be with you all!

- Drakehide, son of Mok'Rukh
