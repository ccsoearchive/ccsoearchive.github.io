Hey ya fellow RPers!

In light of recent events (and fun had as a member of the SCW) I thought we really need a guild to represent Protection and Police efforts within Ironforge. So, I have recently created the Ironforge Guard.

I thought I'd announce my intentions to the community, and put a couple of things before you before its finalized.

You're thoughts, input and support is important to me.

1) Support.
As many of you know, IF is a ghost town. All of the action takes place in SW. So RPing in and around IF would naturally be difficult, and with the limited number of Rpers...I can imagine building up the RP scene over there will be slow going. SO, my question i guess would be "Do you think this is a good idea?"

I personally can see the need of at least having an established RP entity representing IF and Dun Morogh...even if the guild itself isn't very active...especially in the beginning. It would open up more doors for creative RP events, and such. But that's just my point of view. What's yours?

2) Organization.
Right now I'm enjoying my RP experience within the SCW, and would like to finish leveling Kael at the very least to 80. I'm notoriously a slow level'er (by choice), so to be honest IFG wouldn't be fully running on its "RP legs" for some time.

I am casually doing some lore research to try and properly 'organize' IFG, so that when it does get running on all four "RP cylinders"...it'll be primed to go! So if anyone want's to chat with me about that aspect and offer advice, I'm all ears.

So here there it is, in all its splendor.

What do you think?
