Adellwyna pulled the arrow from the tree with a grunt and slowly turned it over in her hands. She understood it's meaning, but was still wary. Her people had been invited to these "meetings" before under the flag of truce...to parlay...to make peace...to forgive. But always they had been traps. Instead of exchanging words, they were met with swords and arrows.

But still...there was always a chance that this time might be different. As cautious as she was, she had not yet given up hope.

The priestess drew a small dagger and lodged it into the bark of the tree, tying a small green ribbon on the hilt. 'Yes, we will meet with you. But we will not be unarmed.'

Tucking the arrow into her belt, Adellwyna cast one last glance over her shoulder and melded into the dim shadows of the forest.


Kurshaw took the dagger from her messenger and chuckled. The elves were as they have always been, and she would have it no other way. Turning, Kurshaw tucked the dagger into a belt and tied the ribbon to her horn. "Remember, the elves demand respect. The Horde has made them angry, so it is our duty to prove that we are not a threat. Should any of you misstep, I will kill you myself."
