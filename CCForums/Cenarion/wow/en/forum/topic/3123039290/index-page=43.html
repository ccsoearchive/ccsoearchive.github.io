    • Well I'd like to partake more in Arena and Rated BG's, but Hunters are being sunwelled.

  • What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.


    • Pet rezzing and pet healing. Dismiss pet should be instant. I wish Duel Wield could come back. I'd like to MM Stat allocation simplified or at least made intuitive so that new players can figure out how much of what stats they need. I want to see Pet AI work better (hello pets getting stuck on the bridges in Z axis maps) and I'd like to see Pets able to have more uptime on bosses.

  • What makes playing your class more fun?

    • I've always been a fan of the Ranger class from Pen and Paper games. I like the idea of having light armor, being mobile and being deadly. I like challenges (like soloing old content) and I like saving the day in a Heroic when the tank dies and my turtle comes to the rescue. I love using a bow or a gun and doing ranged damage. I love the idea of being a powerful sniper.

  • What makes playing your class less fun?

    • Where do I begin? On paper the Hunter class seems awesome, but it feels like we are years behind in mechanics and fluidity compared to other classes. I hate that the best strategy to kill a Hunter is to train them. I think of how people setup intelligent CC's or strategic uses of abilities to force CD's. But vs a Hunter, everyone can just train the hunter. It drives me crazy that Hunters are the only class that can't reset a fight through active healing our Vanish type abilities. We have 0 mitigation talents aside from a Glyphed Raptor Strike and a talented RoS from our pets. Healers can solo the Hunter class and that doesn't seem right to me. Hunters have no niche. We need to have something that makes people want us. At the end of the day, I just want to be on the same playing field as everyone else, not running up the hill with leg weights. When I see the Mage class, the Rogue class, I think of how well they are designed and I wonder why Hunters can't be designed the same way. Not saying to homogenize, but the Mage and Rogue class is designed in a way that makes them very fluid. I want that same level of Fluidness for the Hunter class.

  • How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)

    • Currently I feel that in PvE, we're still fighting with Steady Shot/Cobra Shot spam like in TBC. I feel that right now the rotations are not as exciting as they could be. It's all about fitting specials into Focus Regen Spam. I'd really like to see Hunter rotation become more dynamic. Remember when you could chose what special bonus Chimera did based on what sting was being used? I liked that...

  • What’s on your wish list for your class?

    • Feign Death should be given the Glyph Raptor Strike Treatment
    I'd like to see more passive or active self healing that is on par with the other pures.
    I want Disengage to be on shorter CD than everyone else's gap closer. I sure sucks when Disengage is more beneficial to the Warrior Charging me than it is for me using it.
    I'd like to Ice Trap turn into Ice Arrow (targeted CC) and Ice Traps and Fire traps all share seperate CD's. ExTrap and Immo Trap should matter, snake trap should be meaningful as well. If we spend Globals on them and people step in them, it should hurt. (Imagine Immo Trap having a silence attached to it so if Casters are training a hunter, we can drop an immo trap. They shouldn't be in our face anyway.).
    Melee Range should be changed to 3 yards.
    More stable slots (or possibly a way to Void Storage Trophy Pets).
    I'd love to see Spell Pen go away for Hunters.
    Silence Shot should be Baseline. Wyvern should be baseline. 31 point talents imo would be awesome if they defined the tree rather than be a shot.
