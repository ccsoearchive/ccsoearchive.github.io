What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]

N/A

What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.
 - Pets temporarily having low health on summon.
- Dead zone. While I understood this back in vanilla and BC, it is outdated and needs to be removed (as well as any redundant melee abilities).
- Pet class confusion (See "Pet Overhaul" below)

What makes playing your class more fun?
 - Being a melee raider in past expansions, it was nice to be able to go to range as a physical fighter and be completely different from the other ranged classes.
- Focus system is a nice system that isn't too much like rogue energy, while testing a player's ability to manage and control it through a thought-out rotation.

What makes playing your class less fun?
 - Dead Zone. See above
- Tenacity pets need more threat. Taunt is the only thing that keeps the mob on it if another person is seriously beating on said mob.
- Traps as a whole. Freezing trap is the only cc that has both a delay in it being placed and another delay in the trap going off. This is annoying beyond belief when your group leader is telling you to cc faster and you're actually the one being proactive on the cc regardless of if he says it or not. Explosive trap is a mage's flame strike with the same delay. Snake Trap is still useless unless its in pvp (and even then...). This whole system probably needs an overhaul. Snake trap would be a good Army of the Dead equivilant in heroics.

How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)
 - Great. I can get haste caps online for every spec and then test my rotation in game based on what other boosts I have and use it in raids. I'm not blindly listening to what some guy on the internet says about rotation, but instead, I'm testing it out with my gear and latency and seeing if it does do more damage/is more comfortable with my play-style.

What’s on your wish list for your class?
 - Silencing shot for all specs, and no focus cost (same with tranq shot).
- See "What makes playing your class less fun?"
- Exotic beast talent need to go. Needing BM for the tame is fine, but I should be able to use them in any other spec once I've tamed it (logically speaking).
- Pet Overhaul - There are three "classes" of pets. The only real difference between one (tenacity) from the other two at the moment is that one is clearly a tanking spec for the pet.
I could talk about this all day, but I want to get to the brass tacks to shorten this up. Cunning needs all the PvP special abilities (stun, snare, disarm, etc.) and some of them need to be more constant (snare, for example) so that the pet is taken more seriously in PvP situations.
Ferocity, meanwhile, needs to do more damage, and contain most (if not all) of the raid buffs/debuffs that other classes give for raid. This keeps Ferocity as the main raiding pet, and, by giving all the ferocity pets one of the raid de/buffs, it keeps them used in PvE situations unless kiting/some other utility (not damage) based mechanic needs to be used.
- New pet species (Basilisk, Hydra, Bucks)
- Ranged Pets for flying phases (in PvE) (dragonhawk, serpent, wind serpent, chimaera are all viable choices)

What spells do you use the least?
(Not in order, they're all last)
Widow Venom, Wyvern Sting, Raptor Strike, Immolation/Snake trap, Wing clip, Distracting Shot.
