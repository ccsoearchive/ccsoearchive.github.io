Guess I'll give my two cents.

What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]

I'm a heavy PvE player and have focused very little in terms of PvP.


If PvE, what type of PvE? [Heroics/Raids/Other]

I'm a raider, currently 2/7 Heroic FL (as of last night :) ) I don't dungeon all that often unless I need my VP for the week (new raid patch)


If PvP, what type of PvP? [Arenas, BGs, Rated BGs]

Aside from the occasional BG or two, I don't PvP all that often, and never arena.


What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.

There have been a lot of changes in the past few years that have considerably changed the class of the hunter, requiring much less micromanagement throughout the day. The abolition of ammo and pet happiness has been a tremendous help in the day-to-day activities of hunters. As far as my current issues, It's hard to say. I feel that Hunters are overall in a good place right now in the game PvE wise.

Right now pet control is a little chunkly with the new pet stay command.

Focus has been one of the best changes to the Hunter class. Thanks!

Although it's a VAST improvement from TBC and early Wrath, I still find some fights difficult/Annoying with the minimum range. Baelroc comes to mind. Gathering for a melee crystal often makes it difficult to gauge how close I can be without sacrificing dps.

What makes playing your class more fun?

Mobile attacking. Since Cata I've found that Aspect of the Fox has been a very fun, and still today I love Disengage. The ability to get from one place to another quickly in a fight without too much loss in overall dps is satisfying. I do, however wish AotF didn't nerf my damage so much since I can't be in Hawk.


What makes playing your class less fun?

I'd love to raid as BM, I find it a fun spec when faffing about. However it's not viable in today's raid game.


How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)

MM: MM rotation is very fun. Managing to have just enough focus when Chimera Shot comes off CD is a pretty good feeling. There's nothing quite like the love I have when I get Aimedshot proc and T12 procs too.

Survival: This feels much more 'rotationy' than MM. A little stagnant and boring. ES procs are always fun though

What’s on your wish list for your class?
Aspect of the cheetah without daze. Never gonna happen, so I'll give my second.

Disengage without combat. Okay, I always love this, but I know blizzard doesn't want hunters jumping everywhere, so let's hit my third.

Removal of Trap Launcher and replace with individual skills. Now I hate the skill bloat we have (my biggest complaint in wrath) and maybe a couple skills can be removed. However, ever since Cata beta, I have never liked the implementation of the Trap Launcher. It's the clunkiest mechanic the hunter arsenal has. I miss Ice Arrow. It was a much smoother implementation.

What spells do you use the least?

My trap launcher. Use this sparingly in raids. Only if I can't get to a position without sacrificing life or damage do I use it.

Widow Venom is worthless and could be removed as the effect is weak. Maybe it has good PvP uses, I wouldn't know.

Beast Lore: I think I used this a couple times in Vanilla

Everything else has a lot of utility and situational uses (even snake trap)

    I think there should be more weapons similar to thoridal with special shot effects. Besides that I cannot really think of any others.

    # What makes playing your class more fun?
    I could list off basically everything that a hunter can do as what makes it more fun. Ive been playing a hunter as my main since Vanilla, and have had several hunters are one time at max level (in BC). I also have one of every class as it is and i still prefer the hunter for everything they can do, mainly their utility. Im usually the one chosen for any of the "Special mechanics" in Raids since I usually can do them and still do my job since my class allows it.

    # What makes playing your class less fun?
    To be honest, as much as I love focus for some reasons, i absolutely hate it for others. PVP for instance, we have to usually stay in Aspect of the Fox almost 100% of the time. This causes a huge blow to our offensive power by losing 30% of our Attack power. The other issue with Focus is the fact that we are completely out with 2 shots and the time it takes to regenerate it when having pressure put on us feels like an eternity. I am not saying it is impossible but it can be very frustrating at some times. (excluding Beast Master)

    I dont know if it is true but I have been told that a hunters pressence in the PVP statistics is one of the lowest representations. If that is true, I have a strong feeling that it is due to the above.

    The other big thing that kind of bothers me is the Aspect of the Fox/Hawk thing with PVE. Firelands, especially adding the Heroic versions contains an awful lot of movement. There are very very little Stand and nuke situations. Due to this we are either Aspect Dancing A LOT or we are staying in Fox for a significant amount of time. This wasnt a big deal when it was a toss up between Stand and Nuke fights (where we would top the charts) and Movement fights where we would come semi high or middleground for DPS. But now we have classes who can do just as much DPS as we would do on stand and nuke fights, while they are moving. And when there ARE stand and nuke opportunities, those classes and others can do even more.

    The loss of Aspect of the Hawk i feel is a major player in the situation with all of the movement fights, as Raid buffed we lose roughly 15% of our AP by going Fox.

    I number crunch every little thing I can for the hunter, and for our raid comp etc... And I didnt feel this way until recently, as the hunter numbers are lower gear levels were significantly higher than other peoples. (when other raid members were doing 15k, i was doing 20k, when they were doing 20k, i was doing 25k)... However as the gear has increased, it seems like my DPS increase tapered right off while my guildies ramped right up, surpassing me. I really do feel this is due to the amount of movement fights with the heroic versions and less being able to stand still.

Taking the hunter out of play for 10s is as good as a wipe on most heroic raid encounters. Revive Pet should be a 2-3 cast. Not doing any damage for 2-3s is enough of a penalty.
    2. Trap Launcher - Right now, it is not possible to macro Trap Launcher+trap of your choice to a single keybind because of the slight delay in action of trap launcher. So, there is no way to throw a trap without hitting a key twice. This is unnecessarily clunky and should be fixed.
    3. Minimum Range - The min range is a relic of the past, and is a burden in raids. Because of several encounter mechanics, it is often necessary for hunter to be in melee range. While many bosses have been adjusted so that the hunter may keep attacking from melee range, there are a lot of others where this is not so. Removing the min range will be a big step forward.

    What makes playing your class more fun?
    As a pure dps class in raids, besides doing damage it is the situational abilities and talents which make a class fun to play. Hunters perhaps have numerous of these, but rarely put them to full use. On pre-nerf Heroic Beth 10m, the role of a hunter was the most fun I've had for a while, because of exactly this. I could use niches and specific talents to pull off something special. There should be more encounters designed, and more such abilities tuned. Mages and DKs had several of these in T11.

    What makes playing your class less fun?
    Being a burden to my raid makes it less fun to play. Hunters right now have the least defensive capabilities out of all the classes. They do not have any significant self-heal or absorb, they cannot remove debuffs, nor can they prevent ANY damage most of the time. I need to be able to survive mechanics sometimes to make it a fun experience. More defensive tools for hunters is absolutely necessary .

    How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)
    MM - Excellent if slightly demanding rotation. The Aimed vs Arcane choice is very useful, and I love adjusting it on different fights.
    SV - Again, very fluid and easy.
    BM - Kill Command needs to be changed to go off regardless of pet being in melee range of target. This is a very important consideration for BM. KC is the primary ability in BM, and this requirement is very clunky on mobile fights or when we need to switch targets. BM AoE "rotation" should be paid more attention to i.e. AoE should primarily come from adds instead of multishot, and thus those AoE abilities should be tuned appropriately.

    What’s on your wish list for your class?
    1. Traps - One spec (Survival) should have more to do with traps. Immolation Trap is entirely useless right now; Snake Trap rarely used, and even explosive only slightly useful. For a survival hunter, traps should be a lot higher % of damage. A trapper is a pretty common type of ranger/hunter, and it makes me sad not to have the option available to be a trap-based hunter. Adding more types of traps, and making SV use traps more would make the class more interesting to play.
    2. Min range removal
    3. Defensive utilities
    4. Pet fixes (cast time of rez, pathing issues)

    3. Tranq Shot - No real encounters this expansion for the removing enrage part of it. Why? Hunters, rogues and druids have it now. Make them use it.
    4. Deterrence - Sometimes works, mostly doesn't. Needs to be a lot more useful.
    5. Snake Trap - Except for entrapment, pretty useless in PvE. Look at trap suggestion above, make it more useful.
    6. Flare - Useless in PvE. Add some stealthed mobs to detect!
    7. Raptor Strike - Only useful sometimes with the glyph. Since this is the only melee attack we have, and its purpose is clearly not to do damage, make it heal us or something as a part of its base function.
    8. Beast Lore - Useless since pets are homogenized
    9. Distracting Shot - Only used on 2 encounters this expansion. Make taunting by dps more useful sometimes.
    10. Wyvern Sting - Not very useful in its current state for raids. Make it do more damage if the mob is immune. Can become a big 1min cooldown for SV
