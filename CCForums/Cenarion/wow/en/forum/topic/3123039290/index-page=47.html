Firstly congratulations on the game itself, it really is a fantastic achievement and I'm forever in awe of its scope and depth :)

What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]

Both, though more recently PvP

If PvE, what type of PvE? [Heroics/Raids/Other]

Heroics and raids. As much as I enjoy them, I haven't ventured into Firelands raids much because I just can't be bothered committing the majority of my evenings

If PvP, what type of PvP? [Arenas, BGs, Rated BGs]

BGs, Arenas

What are your biggest quality-of-life issues?

Focus regeneration though really only in PvP

Pets that don't behave themselves

But my biggest head ache for all my classes by far is latency/lag in 25m raids or similar sized BG's. Sometimes its 3 seconds, sometimes up to 30 seconds (AV and Isle of Conquest) and almost always results in at least one disconnect per game. Its just not playable. I have a high spec'ed computer and fast internet connection, surely something can be done to optimise/improve data transfer, particularly in battlegrounds where the action is so spread out

What makes playing your class more fun?

Pets are the best part of being a Hunter. Pet tanking in particular. More pet related quests, pet-type specific quests or solo content would be fun. Crowd control is another great part of the hunter tool kit. I also enjoy the use of misdirect and disengage

What makes playing your class less fun?

Focus generation being dependent on cast abilities (particularly when there are a number of low health mobs which die before you complete a steady or cobra shot)

Exotic pets only being able to be tamed by Beast Masters. I PvE in Survival and PvP in Marksmanship. That pretty much means if I come across an exotic/rare pet, I have to respec, tame it, ooo and ahh at it for a few seconds, then stable it and respec back to my usable spec's, never to be able to get the pet out again. A bit pointless really

Aspect Dancing. The animation and sound make it clunky when changing between Fox and Hawk. Aspect of the Cheetah is just plain useless unless running back from a wipe or when there's no chance of combat. Remove the daze effect

How do you feel about your “rotation”?

Great on the whole. I love playing Survival though Marksmanship feels a little clunky and slow at times for me especially in PvP

What’s on your wish list for your class?

Solo achievements and solo challenges. Hunters excel at soloing, its why many of us choose the class. There are also a large number of non-raiding or at least casually raiding players. Provide some solo boss challenges and perhaps some soloing gear that encourage/enhance performance in solo encounters
Class specific quests, like the epic hunter bow of vanilla. They're fun and give you something else to focus on other than grinding rep or running raids.

What spells do you use the least?

Widow's Venom. Useless, even in PvP. Maybe if it increased casting time similar to rogue poison instead. Aspect of the Wild is also pretty much never used

For me its probably still the minimum range that yet rears its ugly head occasionally in PvE. Baleroc is an example of this, where if I have to cover for a melee crystal, more often than not I can't attack. Cataclysm made great strides here, but you simply aren't consistent with applying minimum range fixes in every encounter. After that PvE changes for the sake of PvP and vice-versa really needs to change. Its just not OK anymore. Separate them and be done with it.

■What makes playing your class more fun?
I like knowing I can be competitive if I choose to be. I like that DoT management isn't a large part of hunter DPS with the coming of cataclysm. I know this is a thread for hunters but I'm only filling one of these out. Destruction in WotLK plays very differently from Destruction in cata, and that's a bad thing. I don't have a whole lot of complaints as far as PvE goes and I won't comment on PvP since I do not partake much anymore.
