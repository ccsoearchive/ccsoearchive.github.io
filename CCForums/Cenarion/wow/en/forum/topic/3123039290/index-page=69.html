What type of content do you focus on?
PvE primarily, PvE, solo questing Heroic Dugeons and Raiding

In PVP non rated BG's

What are your biggest quality-of-life issues?
Running out of focus, and no way to buff it in a raid or BG. All the really good drops for mail armor, trinkets, rings etc. seem to be geared for Shamans, hard to get really good gear.

The minimum range issue is huge for me, the pet "assist" doesn't really seem to help functionality, and the talent trees are really pretty vanilla, doesn't really seem to make a huge difference for play style.

What makes playing your class more fun?
the ability to shoot and move and "kite" stand back and see the fight from a perspective where it makes sense, being able to pick a single target out of a mass of meleeing characters.
Hunting a rare or quest target using stealth, traps and long range fire.

Having different pets for different situations is a lot of fun, and the challenge of stalking and taming rare pets.

What makes playing your class less fun?
Focus and focus regen problems, crummy drops for my class, limited range of weapons,
rogues that see trough my "camo" traps that don't trigger and the problems controlling my pet with the new "assist" feature ( he always returns to me after a kill unless it's the worst possible time, then he goes total aggro)

Minimum range problems, no good close range damage abilities and very low damage for traps even in survival.

How do you feel about your “rotation”?
Rotation is very "clunky" with focus, and to many shots burn up way to much focus and leave me "reloading" with cobra, focus was supposed to help with cool downs, it's done the opposite.

What’s on your wish list for your class?
Volley, to many Raid situations where my DpS goes flat ( the eyes in Baradin Hold for example) a return to mana would be nice but I really want / need better drops for my class ( cool Big-Game rifles that don't look like space guns)
Eyes of the Beast was also a cool ability i used quite a bit
Stampede or "call pack" is a VERY cool idea DK's have "army of the dead" we should have an animal version.
Arcane shot should be a casting interrupt ( didn't it used to be? )
Tame my own mounts (as a Tauren, I really want a horse, especially for RP servers.
Dual pistols is also a very cool idea and a return to elite pets, pets with special abilities.

A way to buff focus, a glyph, talent, something, anything

What spells do you use the least?
Immolation trap is worthless, that and counter attack (never used it ) also widow venom is weak and only used in long boss fights as desperation.
Beast lore used to be very useful before all pets were made basically the same (no more Elites )

What type of content do you focus on? I primarily focus on PvE, but am PvP curious...

If PvE, what type of PvE? Solo, 5 mans, raiding

If PvP, what type of PvP? Arenas and BGs - not rated

What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? I feel that hunters are in a pretty good place right now. The only "quality of life" issue I have is our trap-launching. CC was pretty good in Wrath with Freezing Arrow. Perhaps bringing back FA and keeping TL for AOE would be nice.

What makes playing your class more fun? I enjoy being able to shoot on the move, have my choice of THOUSANDS (well... at least 25) pets, and camouflage.

What makes playing your class less fun? Minimum range! Hunters, as compared to other ranged dps classes, suffer when we cannot attack from range as other ranged classes can. This, I feel, would be extremely useful in PvP applications and even in certain PvE encounters that require ranged classes to stack on the boss. There's that mage and warlock casting away while we beat on the boss with our sticks! ; ) Perhaps dual wielding handguns?...

How do you feel about your “rotation”? As with any rotation change, at first I found it to be a bit confusing, but after practice I have become quite comfortable with the current shot priority system. That being said, as a MM hunter, the rotation feels a little snug around the waist and could use a little letting out (i.e. perhaps an extension of the Steady Shot buff?).

What’s on your wish list for your class? Removal of minimum range and bring back Volley as RANGED dps option (I think Volley on top of yourself is a bit unbelievable...).

What spells do you use the least? To quote Frostheim here: "Counterattack and Immolation trap are certainly never used at all. Almost never used are Eagle Eye, Beast Lore, Aspect of the Wild, Widow Venom." I agree.

Thanks for listening Blizz! Keep up the great work!

What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]

Both.

If PvE, what type of PvE? [Heroics/Raids/Other]

Heroics, old content. I raided from early Vanilla all the way to the end of Wrath. I don't raid at all in Cata, I finally burned out on that.

If PvP, what type of PvP? [Arenas, BGs, Rated BGs]

BGs mostly. Sometimes I'll do Arenas with a friend. I'll even get some RBGs in if the guild needs someone to fill a spot.

What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.

The cast time on res pet is unpleasant. A glyph that mimics the old BM improved res pet talent would work, that or just a straight buff to the cast time.

What makes playing your class more fun?

Being able to do my entire dps rotation while on the move. Being so mobile as a ranged dps feels very unique compared to other classes.

The large variety of pets to choose from and seek.

Having spells with uses that aren't immediately apparent. Like Feigning Death to stop something from casting at you or using Distracting Shot as an external tank cool down.

What makes playing your class less fun?

Aspect swapping sucks. Hard.

How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)

I play somewhat exclusively as MM, so that's all I can comment on.

The duration of the Improved Steady Shot buff feels too confining. There are times in my rotation that I'd like to get a couple more arcane shots off before I need to Chimera, or put out CC, or just have time to react to something in a fight. I am often stopped from doing any of that because of how badly my dps will suffer if I don't get two steady shots off every 6-8 seconds.

Using Aimed shot in place of Arcane shot early in fights or when haste is high enough feels gimmicky.

What’s on your wish list for your class?

There are a number of abilities that would be cool to have. But not just something that's a copy paste of another classes abilities. Things like...

An in combat heal.

Desperate Times: You call your pet to your side to protect you while you tend to your most serious wounds. All damage and CC you would otherwise take while healing will instead be taken by your pet. Your pet will only be able to defend you until it has either taken damage equal to 50% of its health or the healing is complete. Neither your nor your pet can attack or move while desperate measures are being taken.

A generic interrupt.

Savage: You command your pet to charge towards your target and attempt to rip the targets face off, interrupting any spells being cast and silencing spells of that school for 4 seconds. 10 second cool down, 20 yard range.

The existing traps belong to one of three magic schools. Each school, save for nature, has a trap for single target (Immolation, Freezing) and aoe (Explosive, Ice). Adding a new nature school trap would be good, as well as a slight tweak to the existing snake trap.

Wind Burst Trap: Tripping this trap will cause the target to fly up into the air and fall slowly for 5 seconds. Due to the 'airy' nature of this trap it is somewhat volatile in flight, making launching this trap impossible.

Wind Burst Trap would be the single target version of the nature trap school. It's main use would be to launch that damnable melee up in the air long enough for you to gain some distance. It could also be used as a sort of 'silence' on casting mobs/players.

Snake trap would become the aoe version of the nature traps. The change is pretty simple. Instead of the snakes attacking only the target that tripped the trap they would attack everything nearby, spreading poisons all willy nilly.

What spells do you use the least?

Widow Venom. I've never used it.

I use Raptor Strike when I want to laugh.

Wing clip is just a melee range conc shot that has no cool down.
