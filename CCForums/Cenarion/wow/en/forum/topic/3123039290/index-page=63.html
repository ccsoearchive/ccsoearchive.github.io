■What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.

Minimum range is horrible. It is bad in PVE but it's even worse in PVP. In PVE at least you are familiar with the fight mechanics and can expect when you'll be in min range and adjust accordingly but not in PVP. Every class uses LoS in PVP as they should but when you mix LoS and min range then it's a nightmare for hunters. For example, if I chase an enemy in PVP around an object (box, column, etc.), the only way to do damage is to find LoS but once I find LoS I still can't do any damage because since I'm chasing around small objects then I'm in minimum range. So the enemy just has to stick to a box or a column and keep going around and I'll either be out of LoS or within minimum range once I have LoS. Our traps are also a nightmare. We can't CC anything that has Serpent Sting or anything that was recently hit with our Aimed Shot or Steady Shot since it procs Piercing Shots and will break our CC. So we have to stop damage and wait for all DoTs to wear off and then apply CC. Also our traps have very poor mechanics in PVP. Most classes can directly cast CC or the CC area of effect is huge. We can just hope that the enemy will not move from where they are (which doesn't happen in PVP) when we launch a trap or we can hope that the enemy will be dumb enough to actually walk over it. The CC is just not reliable and has too many factors that need to be right in order to get a good CC. Lack of heals is also a big PVP problem since every class has the ability to heal or have some sort of shield that absorbs large amounts of damage. Mages for example don't have heals but they have shields that absorb a lot of damage and they also have ice block. Every other class has a descent heal but hunters only have deterrence which has a really long cooldown. Our pets are also a pain right now because there are just too many bugs; the most annying one being having a pet that gets one-shot. In arenas I usually don't summon a pet until the match starts so I know what I'm up against and then quickly Call a pet but he gets one-shot many times.

I like the whole "catch me if you can" strategy that the hunters use. I also like having cool looking pets with me.

In MM it can be extremely hard to keep up a good rotation without Improved Steady Shot dropping off since it only works for 8 seconds and steady shot has a sort of long cast time. In BM it can be annoying when trying to keep a good rotation but the pet is not within melee range of mob so it takes Kill Command out of the formula until the pet gets there.

■What’s on your wish list for your class?

Things I really want: Minimum range gone! I don't understand why every other ranged class can do their full dps from far away and also up-close. There's just no good explanation as to why a ranged weapon would not fire up-close (it actually will do more damage in RL but I'll settle for just having minimum range gone). Please give us freezing arrow back so that hunters can have a CC they can depend on in PVP without leaving it up to luck whether the other player will walk over trap or not. Implement some sort of damage cap or damage type that our trapped target can receive without braking CC such as Serpent Sting and Piercing Shots being able to do damage without braking CC but all other damage will brake it. I'll even be happy if CC automatically removes all of our DoTs from the target when trapped. Give us a self-healing abilitie (spirit bond is too small a heal for PVP as it relies on super tiny heals over very long amounts of time and arenas don't last that long and the Chimera Shot heal is very unreliable since it can be negated by minimum range, the shot being on cooldown, or not having enough focus to fire the shot.) or a reliable damage-reducing abilty (ie. mage Frost Armor) because the only thing we have is deterrence and its cooldown is too long while other classes have damage-reducing abilities PLUS immunities. I just want our survivability to be in par with others classes, not better but not worse either. I would like to see the summon pet bug fixed so that they don't get one-shot. I would also like to see the Aggresive stance back for pets because it seems like those of us that are careful with our pet stance were punished because of all the others that don't pay attention to their pet's stance. Aggresive was an extremely good ability on PVP and it was just taken away to help those that don't pay attention to their pets. Give BM a better tree bonus. I don't play BM but I feel bad for them because their "Beast Mastery" talent doesn't really stand out there as a "powerful-last talent of tree" ability. Maybe give them the ability to summon multiple pets for a short period of time. I also think that Aspect of the Wild be changed so that it increases all the resistances which might make it worth dropping the damage bonus from Aspect of the Hawk.
Things that would be nice to have: the ability to choose cunning, ferocity, or tenacity and choose the buffs for all the pet skins out there so that we can use a pet whose looks we like and not feel forced to bring a pet we don't like or we're sick of (looking at you Cat!). Disable all spells/damage against a beast being tamed other than by the hunter doing the taming unless aggro is dropped because of death or feign death or something like that. The ability to tame beasts as mounts (ie. horses, Rhinos, etc..). The ability to have temporary mind control of a beast allowing you to simply send the beast to auto attack a target until the mind control effect wears off. The ability to dual-wield pistols. The ability to sacrifice our pets and get a percentage of their health. Adding increased swimming speed to Aspect of Cheetah and Pack (after all, we're all about fast moving in the outdoors while hunting down our prey.).


What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]

PvP

 If PvE, what type of PvE? [Heroics/Raids/Other]



 If PvP, what type of PvP? [Arenas, BGs, Rated BGs]

BG, world pvp

 What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.

Biggest quality of life issues:
1. I hate, hate, hate... the fact that PvP gear is obtainable by raiding, but raiding gear is not obtainable via PvP. These should either be completely separate, or equally obtainable by both sides. It's a little lopsided.
2. Not having access to top-shelf PvP gear without having to look for a group. Arena is boring. RBG is essentially Arena that requires a bigger group, hence it takes longer to put one together.
2. A Mage cannot blink off a cliff. A Hunter can Disengage off everything from the Grand Canyon to the Kilamanjaro with a resounding THUD.
3. Nobody kills a Mage's pet, which is arguably more effective than any Hunter pet to date when talking about assisting a class that relies on mobility. But everyone kills a BM Hunter's pet. Why? Because a Mage without a pet will still smack you like nobody's business... but a BM Hunter without his pet is... well... a freaking warrior who dropped his sword somewhere and is sporting a pellet gun.
3. I can't summon a different pet if my current one is dead, and I have to revive it first? Really? Why would I have to revive this pet FIRST so that I can NOT use him, put him away and use a different one? He obviously wasn't very effective in the current situation to begin with. That makes no sense. What better reason could possibly exist to summon a different pet than having a dead one? How about I just keep summoning until all my pets are dead? Then I might take my Beast Mastery build into the Battlegrounds more often.




 What makes playing your class more fun?

"Beast Within" used to be fun. There isn't much that is fun about the class anymore with regards to PvP. Least represented class in Arena? C'mon guys...

 What makes playing your class less fun?

The range deadzone limitation. Seriously, get rid of it. I've heard it before, Blizzard doesn't want to take the whole idea away that "Hunter's are supposed to be a ranged class, so we want to keep that range limitation so that it is at the core of the class' persona."

Well, newsflash. The Mage has no range deadzone, neither does the Warlock, or any other spellcaster. All of these classes can toss up something stupid at point blank range... but has there ever been ANY doubt that these are ranged classes? Or has it changed the way they play their classes? Did they all of a sudden start smacking people with their broomsticks? No.

 How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)

What rotation? There hasn't been a viable Hunter rotation since I don't know when. My current rotation resembles whack-a-mole. Focus was a horrible idea for a Hunter. Rogues can compensate for the DPS loss because while awaiting the energy build up, they are building combo points. So the big finisher at the end compensates for the time spent waiting for energy buildup. Hunters on the other hand have too slow of a mechanic to build focus back up, and there is no big finisher at the end to compensate for the lost time. Once the initial focus store is gone... dps drops like a bad habit.

 What’s on your wish list for your class?

1. Remove the deadzone.
2. Fix Survival tree... this has never been right. Ever. The flavor of the month is either Survival or Marksmanship with regards to viability in both PvE and PvP. This is because neither really shines in their intended purpose... they are both just... ranged dps. Make Survival true to its name... "survival". This should be the PvP tree. It always should have been. Everything in PvP is about survival. This is why PvP gear has the highest stamina, it has survivability as the most important factor. The tree's name is "Survival", some smart guy please put this together already. Here's a suggestion... why not remove "Counterattack", and replace it with "Point-Blank Range"?
3. Remove Counterattack
4. Remove Unsummon pet ability
5. Make Call Pet instant.
6. Should be able to summon a different pet when current one is "dead".


 What spells do you use the least?

Counterattack. Seriously. This skill has never... ever... been... useful.

Dungeons, Heroics, Raids.

What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.

Focus. I don't like it. The more I've tried liking it, the more I realize we didn't need this change.

Minimum range is also not fun.

What makes playing your class more fun?

I like having a pet (but I am NOT a "gotta catch 'em all" hunter), and being a ranged class. I enjoy doing damage. I like being a ranger/hunter/marksman type, overall. I personally prefer guns over bows and crossbows. I like the instant shots we have and that I can fire them while moving, and Disengage is a pretty fun escape ability.

What makes playing your class less fun?

Focus. I really do not like a resource-based system like this. Previously, mana allowed us to be more cooldown-based in how we did damage. Now with Focus, I'm dealing with what seems to be a constantly sliding scale, considering how my resource may or may not replenish more quickly depending on my Haste at a given moment, or abilities that return a certain amount of Focus to me. It's too variable for my liking. Yes, our shots generally always cost the same amount, but when I use one I may be down to 20 focus, or maybe 22 focus, and then regain possibly 30 focus or maybe only 25... no, I don't like it.

I dislike it enough that I've virtually stopped playing this character; one I've played in Classic, TBC, Wrath and the beginning of Cata. I'd stop playing WoW altogether thanks to this, if I didn't have an alt or two that kept me interested.

Putting a more precise focus on things: for BM spec, I dislike how Kill Command works. My pet can spend a lot of time chasing a boss around while I'm spamming Kill Command, hoping it will go off. This feels IMMENSELY un-fun. For BM, Intimidation remains a boring "spec specific" ability. I also miss Volley.

How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)

I don't like how Focus works with our abilities, overall. I dislike that Steady/Cobra Shot is our primary method of regenerating Focus. Coming from TBC and Wrath, where Steady was something we resorted to to 'fill in' when our other FUN shots were on cooldown... having Steady become so critical to our rotation is NOT FUN.

What’s on your wish list for your class?

It's a pipe dream, but go back to Mana.

Also, Traps are very 2004. Trap Launcher is a silly contrivance to make them better. Abolish all of it and give us Grenades that work similarly, but are simply thrown and affect an area rather than requiring the AI (or another *much smarter* player) to wander over our silly trap.

What spells do you use the least?

Pretty much all of them, since this character is shelved for the foreseeable future.
