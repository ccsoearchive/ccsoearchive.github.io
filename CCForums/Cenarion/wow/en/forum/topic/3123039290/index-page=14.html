# What makes playing your class less fun?
The class has become very 'clunky' with some of the latest changes since patch 4.0 and Cata.

FOCUS
While Focus is a huge improvement over Mana (kudos to Blizzard), it is way to easy to become focus 'starved'. This is hugely noticeable in PvP where once we have used our burst we need to switch to Fox to re-gen focus. A melee opponent in our face not only shuts down our damage but also our only real way of recovering Focus. No other class suffers from this problem. On top of that, once we have used our two shots (e.g. Aimed and Chimera), we are done on burst and now must do a lot less damage by switching to Fox to recover Focus.

TRAP LAUNCHER
Trap Launcher is really clunky as well. It has a focus component. It uses a GCD. The trap has an arm time. While Trap Launcher can somewhat be macro'd, it is still very clunky and those not familiar with macros (players new to the class or not in the know on how macros work) are at a huge disadvantage.

HUNTER CC
Hunter CC used to be some of the best in the game. We could have multiple Freeze Traps up. We had traps that were not visible. We had traps that had a larger activation radius. We had traps that had no arming time.

Now to effectively trap an opponent, you must use at least 2 (and often 3) forms of CC. An example is you must activate Trap Launcher first (to use your GCD now), Scatter Shot second (to CC the opponent from moving), and then use Freeze Trap third to actually CC the opponent. Sometimes you need to use another trap (e.g. Snake Trap) as well. This is basically 3 GCD's to CC ONE target. This is ridiculous. Add to the possibility that in arenas, often another DPS (e.g. Warrior) will intervene the opponent you are trying to trap and eat the trap themselves. 3 GCD's wasted and now all Trap Launcher, Scatter Shot and your Traps are on cooldown.

For example, all a mage has to do is /cast polymorph on their focus target with still DPS'ing their kill target with no loss of DPS and with only 1 GCD.

Unfair. No wonder hunters are least represented in high end arenas.

NEW PET AI
The new Pet AI (with Aggressive stance removed) is terrible. Pets stand around for 2-5 seconds before engaging in combat or even changing targets. When a rogue or druid is beating down on you and your pet takes 3 seconds before it does anything - well that is terrible.

TOO MANY ABILITIES
Hunters have way too many abilities. I use almost every button on my bars for an ability or keybind. I use maybe half that many buttons on my rogue, DK, druid or even Lock.

# How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)
PVE - rotation (priority) is fine.
PvP - we need help please.

# What’s on your wish list for your class?
I think the problem with hunter burst has to do auto shot damage. Currently, auto shot damage makes up a significant portion of the hunter's DPS in PvE (raids). I see the problem that if you were to up hunter's damage from their special shots (Chim, Arcane, Explosive, Aimed, Kill Command, etc.) to help burst in PvP, then this increase in special shot damage may scale too well for PvE. maybe the possible fix is to remove auto shots from the game entirely up the hunter damage from hunter special shots to make up for it? This would be more in line of mages, etc.

Besides MM, SV and BM special shots should scale with Weapon Damage. Only MM hunter abilities scale with weapon damage and this is why SV and BM start to fall behind in every expansion.

Hunter's need an easier time to get out of melee range. We don't do damage in melee and we don't regenerate Focus in melee. We need to be able to get out at range faster/easier/more often.

# What spells do you use the least?
Counter Attack - the worst ability of all the classes in the game. Why? Hunter dodge/parry was reduced to nothing a long time ago (to stop hunters from stacking Agility+Aspect of the Monkey) to make them unhittable. Now it is just useless.

Resistance is Futile - What? Terrible talent. Shouldn't even be in the MM tree.

Widow Venom - Too little of a debuff for too short a duration. Not worth the GCD ever.

Also Beastlore, Aspect of the Wild.
