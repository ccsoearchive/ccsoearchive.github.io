■What type of content do you focus on?
PvE

■If PvE, what type of PvE?
Heroics

■If PvP, what type of PvP?
Random BGs (Are hunters even allowed in Arenas and RBGs?)

■What are your biggest quality-of-life issues?
As the only class in the game that is completely helpless in melee range, we should also have more escapes than any other class. We currently have the same or less than others.

■What makes playing your class more fun?
This was my first character, so at this point nostalgia.

■What makes playing your class less fun?
Casted shots, minimum range, lack of reliable CC, no legitimate damage mitigation, complete dependence upon being "not slowed", no top end guns

■How do you feel about your “rotation”?
Hunters don't have a rotation, they have a priority system. We don't have enough resource (focus) to have a rotation. As far as the priority system goes, MM is very boring, SV is fun, BM is too dependent upon a single long CD ability.

■What’s on your wish list for your class?
1. Removal of Melee weapon slot and replaced with an "Ammo" slot that provides similar stats as the stat stick but also allows special Ammo effects (such as incendiary rounds, frozen rounds, poison rounds, etc.) whenever the ranged weapon is fired.

2. Removal of minimum range for all shots. Replaced with "Effective Range". Shots that are in "Melee Range" are out of the "Effective Range" and will do 50% damage but generate a small amount of focus. Shots that are not in "Melee Range" are in the "Effective Range" and will act normally.

3. Explosive Trap now does only 1 tick of fire damage and knocks back all enemies in range of the explosion. Immolation Trap now sets the area on fire for the duration and all enemies who enter the area will get a (renewable) 10 second fire DOT. Freeze Trap and Ice Trap do not share CD. Snake Trap is replaced with Bear Trap which immobilizes the target for 25 seconds (8 in PvP) and causes a 10 second bleed. Glyph for Freezing Trap that allows DoTs to tick without breaking the trap (come on, it isn't like our DoTs are even remotely potent).

4. Aspect of the Wild changed to heal the hunter for 5% of max health every 3 seconds while active.

5. Widow Venom changed to reduce healing received by the target by 30% to compensate for the lack of damage and high focus cost (making it the best MS in the game offset by the high cost).

6. All casted shots are focus free (including Aimed Shot). All instant shots cost focus. This just makes sense since the casting action is the process of "aiming" and therefore no additional focus is needed to be spent since all attention is currently being given.

7. While Aspect of the Fox is active, Cobra and Steady Shots become instant cast with a CD equal to what their cast time would have been. The Focus per melee hit is removed and changed to additional 1-2 focus per autoshot.

8. Aspect of the Cheetah is changed so that being struck no longer dazes the hunter but instead cancels the effect for 3 seconds and during that time all aspects are unusable (but can still be selected for when the debuff wears off).

9. Aspect of the Pack is changed so that all party members receive a 5-10% buff to Mastery.

10. Silencing Shot is baseline.

11. Intimidation becomes baseline. Kill Command becomes the BM ability after getting an appropriate buff.

12. Taming Exotic pets becomes baseline, but only BM hunters get the pet's bonus ability. BM hunters also get a new 31 point talent that is totally cool and awesome.

13. Survival is redesigned to be an amazing PvP spec with better AoE PvE damage than MM or BM. This includes removing completely pointless or broken abilities and giving SV a lot more control abilities.

14. BM hunters should not be able to die until their pet is dead. Pets are given all kinds of survival abilities that never need to be used since there is literally no reason to ever target one in PvP (unless you are another hunter and are getting your face chewed off in melee range).

I'm sure I could think of hundreds of wish list items, but this is a quick list of the most important.

■What spells do you use the least?
Counterattack is proof that you don't have any kind of QA team at Blizzard.
Widow Venom in its current state is a large middle finger to hunters.
Scare Beast has almost no point since it has a cast time and can only be used against a very tiny group of foes.
Immolation Trap does pitiful damage and puts a very useful trap on CD.
Wing Clip does not add a stacking speed debuff and therefore is pointless in many cases (most enemies fighting a hunter are already being slowed by Frost Trap or Concussive shot, so the Wing Clip does nothing to them).
Raptor Strike is only useful when Glyphed, and even then only as an "oh crap" ability.

Please copy the below questions into your reply and answer each one.

-What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]
Mainly PvE, but also PvP for fun with decent PvP gear acquired. Main PvP interest is to get Honor Points and Conquest Points, not high ratings.

-If PvE, what type of PvE? [Heroics/Raids/Other]
Raids

-If PvP, what type of PvP? [Arenas, BGs, Rated BGs]
BGs and Arenas for points

-What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.
1. Focus as an infinitely generating resource is great, especially in PvE. Please keep Focus as our resource.
2. Aspect dancing - it simply leads us to make more and more macros. And with a slightly high latency, those macros do not work a lot of time. Not to mention that forgetting to change your aspect after you have stopped moving is a significant loss of damage.
3. The minimum range, combined with line of sight, is a big problem especially in PvP. The constant need to try to run away from the opponent is probably the least fun part of Hunter playstyle. It also hinders us when we have to stack and do AoE.
4. Tedious trap mechanics - this is really a quality of life issue in PvP. Having to use 3 abilities to have to CC someone is just tedious. Not to mention that another person can eat the trap instead of the intended target.
5. Too many keybinds required to be competitive in Arena and Rated BGs.

-What makes playing your class more fun?
Having a pet over which we have a lot of control and which we can customize using pet talent trees, easy levelling as well as easy time when questing, being a ranged damage dealer.

-What makes playing your class less fun?
Minimum range issues combined with Line of Sight problems, especially in Arena. Tediousness of traps. Lack of focus in PvP, especially when Stead or Cobra shot is slowed. Lack of self healing combined with inability to remove Damage over Time spells. Basically, playing the class in PvP is simply not fun, especially for someone new to PvP like me. In PvE, Hunters are great fun.

-How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)
The rotation is fine in PvE. But, as I said previously, in PvP it is too difficult to maintain in PvP due to minimum range and LoS issues combined.

-What’s on your wish list for your class?
Basically, fixing our bugs, especially pet bugs. And resolving our quality of life issues I meantioned earlier. The class should not be this hard to play in PvP. Please look at the lack of highly ranked Hunter representation in Arenas and Rated BGs and make us more viable in PvP.

-What spells do you use the least?
Immolation Trap
Widow Venom
Counterattack
Snake Trap
