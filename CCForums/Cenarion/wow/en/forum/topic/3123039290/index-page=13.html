■What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]
PVE

■If PvE, what type of PvE? [Heroics/Raids/Other]
All of it

■If PvP, what type of PvP? [Arenas, BGs, Rated BGs]
I used to pvp but hunters were just too annoying to play (pvpd since BC) so now I rarely do it.

■What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.

The minimum range thing is one of the most annoying things about my class. Being rendered almost completely useless because someone is close to me is just a really poor class design for pve and pvp. In pvp it just makes us way to easy to kill.

Trap Launcher is also a very clunky thing to use. I would rather have seen the original Ice Arrow or whatever it was called in LK stay in the game and just added a few others like Immolation Arrow, Explosive Arrow, or something along those lines. If the ability could have been properly macrod it would have been fine but as it stands now you can't just turn it into a one click type of deal. Again, just a very poor design and a headache for pvp.

Freezing Trap as a CC has always been a pet peeve of mine. Ive been trapping since BC so I know how it works and I was pretty good at it. The delay from when a trap lands til activation is really annoying, and having to physically aim my trap say in the middle of a fight when things are moving is near impossible. I'm aware i can use scatter shot to keep them still then trap but even the slightest delay and they usually run out of range of it anyway. As someone else suggested it needs to be a direct shot. No other CC in the game has to be aimed like ours does and it doesn't bring anything awesome to the class. It's just a nuisance.

The new pet stances are pretty awful too. The old ones were alot better.

■What makes playing your class more fun?

I like having a pet. I've been using my wolf, Digby, since I was leveling back in BC.

■What makes playing your class less fun?

The minimum range and my class being the joke of pvp.

■How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)

I really enjoy how SV plays - no issues there. I don't mind MM but it feels a little clunky at the moment. Especially in the 100 - 90% phase. I have little experience with the Cata BM style but I know I did not enjoy how kill command worked, not being able to use it if my pet was like 1 foot away from it's target. I'm not 100% sure if that has been improved at all.

■What’s on your wish list for your class?

I would love to see the minimum range removed entirely. I never really understood how anybody thought it was a good idea. It is more annoying than anything else.

Ive seen the dual pistols thing mentioned a few times. Honestly that sounds pretty bad !@#. I would love that.

Trap Launcher redesigned like how the original Freezing Arrow worked from WoTLK.

■What spells do you use the least?

Widow venom. Just not worth the focus for what it does.
Raptor Strike
Counterattack (was really surprised this didn't get removed when the talent tree revamp)
Beast Lore (I don't think I've ever used this ability)
Eagle Eye is not something I use very often but I like to take fancy screenshots of the game scenery for desktop wallpapers and junk so I would prefer this one not be removed :P
