Focus is a double edged sword. I have no real issues with its mechanics in PvE but it can be a huge issue in PvP. I feel this is in large part due to current PvP gear itemization. In order to fire off Steady/Cobra shots effectively I feel more haste is needed. At close to 2 second casts it becomes an enormous chore to regenerate focus. I feel this leads into some of the common attitude towards Hunter PvP.

We are capable of producing decent damage, but our burst hits an abrupt wall once we dip below necessary focus thresholds. It is in arena's where this issue is most visible. Smart players will simply juke/LOS etc the Steady/Cobra shots thus completely killing our damage output.

So, no, I'm not saying 'MOAR DMG PLOX'. Nor am I asking for instant cast Steady/Cobra's. Just maybe a better balance of itemization in gear to allow more haste. I suppose there's more than way to reach this, but it's just a suggestion.

Only other issue I would mention would be to fix the pet health bug. Even if it's only for a few seconds a pet with 6k health is a real pita.

What makes playing your class more fun?
It's a versatile class. I test the limits of what is 'soloable' or I can jump in and be a dynamic addition to a group.

What makes playing your class less fun?
'Not enough focus'
Being Deadzoned and having my pet die as soon as he spawns.

I have no issues with the current rotations in PvE. In PvP the idea of rotation is a bit more ambiguous other than perhaps the above mentioned issues with focus/haste.
