I guess since the character change-er isn't working right now for me for whatever reason, I'll post this on my warrior instead of my Hunter.

What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]
PvE

If PvE, what type of PvE? [Heroics/Raids/Other]
Raids

If PvP, what type of PvP? [Arenas, BGs, Rated BGs]
What little PvP I do do is random BGs/TB.

What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.

The issue of mid-cast interruptions when a mob dies is quite annoying, and turning the option to auto-target something if that happens causes too many problems on trash and such to be worth it. I'd very much like to have something added to fix that, similar to the fix to Starfall put in not too long ago (Starfall not hitting mobs you aren't in combat with).

Disengage requiring you to be in combat is annoying.

Trap launcher now not being refreshable is quite annoying.

Not being able to keep a pet alive through certain abilities (Whirlwinds on Berserkers in Stonecore, trash for Baleroc and Rhyolith - and the elemental packs all around the area between the first 5 bosses), and them pathing into lava and dying is extremely annoying. Making sure all aoe abilities, or DoT abilities which Mend Pet cannot heal through (as a non-BM hunter) such as the elementals in Firelands have, not able to target Pets, or being counted by the 90% AoE damage reduction pets have would be nice. And lava being the same way would cause me to pull out less hair on packs of trash that, for whatever idiotic reason, decide to stay rooted to their spot half a yard from lava.

What makes playing your class more fun?
Aimed Shot/Chimera Shot, the new Multi-Shot, and being one of, if not THE, most mobile ranged DPS (less DPS loss while moving than any other caster). Largely instant abilities is nice, while some cast-time abilities in between evens it out nicely.

Disengage is fun as well.

What makes playing your class less fun?

I put a lot of this into the quality of life section, and that's really all it is. Hunters are great (in my opinion), we just have a lot of quality of life issues that really do add up.

And obviously, any time we have to be in melee range is not in any way fun.

Aspect of the Pack/Cheetah aren't fun at all either, their daze component is stupid. Especially for Cheetah, Pack I can understand.

Feign Death being resisted...

How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)

I enjoy the new Cataclysm rotations overall. Regenning focus during AoE Multi-Shot spam is quite clunky as MM, though, due to how short the Bombardment talent's proc lasts.

What’s on your wish list for your class?
Melee range utility, reworking of traps so they're all useful in PvE - Snake trap's damage is pitiful, as is Immolation's, the casting slow portion of Snake trap seems to be useless on the majority of encounters, albeit for fairly obvious reasons, but it would be nice if Snake Trap could be used on, say, Maloriak, in place of an Arcane Mage's Slow, or a Warlock's Curse of Tongues.

Unique pet buffs for non-BMs. It'd be nice if, even in a 25man raid, my pet brought something other than a cosmetic change. Sunder Armor application is nice, sometimes, but other than that there's really nothing. It's just 'pick your favorite.'

Having some pet-based abilities that hunters sorely lack, such as a pushback or stun, would be nice for encounters like Ragnaros, as well as for PvP.


What spells do you use the least?
Immolation Trap, Widow's Venom, Eagle Vision (if that's the right name, can't remember), Snake Trap, Raptor Strike on anything but Majordomo Staghelm (and even then, it's useless on Heroic) - even with the Glyph, Flare (It's pvp only really, which is fine, but still), Dismiss Pet in combat, and outside combat it's only really used to avoid pathing fails that pull packs, or to allow me to reset a fight with Feign (that's about the only time I use it in combat).

Aspect of the Pack and Aspect of the Wild. Wild is borderline completely useless now, and Pack has never been useful except for running back to a boss after a wipe.

I mainly play the BM spec, and I think it's a little lacking. I think the 31 talent point should by a damaging ability. I'll explain what it should be in my wish list.

What’s on your wish list for your class?

Well, I'll start off by saying that Blizzard has done a good job so far with the hunter class. In the past, I've always wanted to see mana go in favor of something like a rogue's energy bar, and here we are with focus. And for the balance between specs have been the best it has ever been. But I think there are still some changes are still needed. Below are changes I wish to see.

Minimum range, a tricky issue. In pvp it's a problem for hunters for sure. Two main reasons, melee slowing our focus regen because we’re unable to cast steady shot or cobra shot and pillars. But here is the thing, they can’t just simply remove our minimum range. Without it, we’d be more op than frost mages. My solution to the problem is to remove the minimum range, but add a cast time to our main shots. This will allow us to continue to generate focus in the melee range, but allow other classes to slow our damage down (ie interrupts). So chimera shot and explosive shot would have a cast time of say 2 seconds.

For aspects, I would do some changes. First aspect of the hawk would be a 5% (or something) to agility. I would remove aspect of the fox and just make the ability to cast while moving permanent. My reasoning for this is we already macro hawk to our instant shots, and switch to fox when we move or even just macro it to steady or cobra shot. To replace it, I think they should bring back aspect of the monkey. It would give say 15% to 20% dodge and cast time push back reduced by 100%. This would give hunters a good defense stance and would give logical reason to switch between hawk and monkey. For aspect of the cheetah, the daze effect is just silly, sorta self defeating. So to make it more reasonable to use, yet not op, is while in cheetah, movement speed increased by 30% but cannot attack. Basically would be like travel form or ghost wolf. As for aspect of the pack, it should be just a crusader aura, 20% to mount speed.

Stings and venoms. I would like to see viper sting and scorpid sting come back. And for venoms, we have the 10% debuff, but I’d like to see two more. Maybe snake venom, reduces crit chance by 5%. Maybe (can't think of a name) venom, increases damage taken by 5%. Instead of shooting a sting and a venom onto the target, you would apply the sting to auto shot, and the venom to steady/cobra shot. Basically like rogue.

For the BM spec, I think kill command should be the bonus ability, not the stun, the stun should be either base line, in the tree, or just removed. With kill command as the bonus ability, the damage can be increased to give more a burst like chimera shot or explosive shot do. The 31 talent needs a little bit of work. I think it should unlock a second buff (like how exotic pets have 2 now) and make all pets feel “exotic”. So that would mean that MM or SV could use a spirit beast, but only get to use one buff. The 4 more talent points for the pet is kinda lame. I think it should unlock an offensive ability depending on the pets spec.
