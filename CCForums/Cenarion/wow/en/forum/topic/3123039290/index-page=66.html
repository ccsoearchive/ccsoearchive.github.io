What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]

PvE with some PvP.

If PvE, what type of PvE? [Heroics/Raids/Other]

Heroics, some raiding, although not much.

If PvP, what type of PvP? [Arenas, BGs, Rated BGs]

Battlegrounds.

What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.

Lack of any sort of self heal. It's really irritating to have to waste gold on food.

What makes playing your class more fun?

Honestly, after years of playing this character, I don't know why I do, anymore. Daestrum was my first toon and my first character at each of the level caps. I've raided with him since Karazhan all the way to Arthas himself. I've loved him since the day I first logged into Coldridge Valley, but in Cataclysm?

I did not accept the focus change easily and I still feel it was not a change for the better. Very little about the class is enjoyable to me beyond nostalgia. In fact, Daestrum is my least played character this expansion. The only things I enjoy are the things more based on my skill with the class and knowledge of its mechanics.

Things like being able to off tank with my pet, chain trap a mob indefinitely, and kite mobs like they were never a threat at all.

EDIT: In the desire of sounding less like a bitter old man, I left something out. I do enjoy PvP most of the time. In stand up fights against most classes, it actually feels balanced and I feel like I'm of use to the team. I'm effective at killing clothies like no ones business and I can kite Death Knights around all day.

What makes playing your class less fun?

See above. The Hunter class just feels boring, nowadays. Painfully so.

EDIT: In the interest of sounding more like a bitter old man, I hate being stunlocked. I've hated it since BC. I hate being snared, and I hate feeling like I have no chance at all. That happens every single, bloody time I enter a battleground with a warrior or a rogue. There are not words to describe how frustrating it is to not be able to get a single shot of on someone.

It's not skill based at all, either. One ability (hamstring), or just sodding hitting us (Crippling Poison) wrecks us entirely. We can't just disengage and frost trap away, either. They have ways of closing the gap and completely nullify our ability to fight whatsoever.

How do you feel about your "rotation"? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)

I hate the way that Hunter rotations work since the Focus change. I hate being forced to spam Cobra shot over and over again with "filler" special shots. That seems wrong to me.

Beyond that, Survival feels like it has too many overlapping cooldowns and it becomes stressful to watch a dozen procs, DoTs, control your pet, optimize Cobra Shot usage to keep Focus up, and mind crowd control.

What's on your wish list for your class?

Simplicity, though that's been a desire for years, now. We have too many abilities, most of which are useless.
Actually pet usefulness. After all these years, they still just feel like expendable assets.
A feel of weight behind our special shots would be nice (ie, Death Strike hits for 38k while Explosive shot merely ticks for 8k).
Some form of defense against melee. What you've currently tried to implement has not helped at all. Rogues, Warriors, and Enhancement shaman still steamroll us.

What spells do you use the least?

Any form of tracking, Eyes of the Beast, Eagle Eye, ect. Hunters have dozens of useless abilities that should just be removed. Our spellbook is cluttered with useless crap.

My biggest complaint about hunter "quality-of-life" in the PVP realm is that we have so many abilities and talents which should be huge in the hunter arsenal but instead are completely unused. Here are some examples:


  • Camouflage seems to have the potential to be an amazing PVP ability but is rarely ever used except at the start of a match. There have been numerous suggestions on how to fix this (e.g. usable in combat, or perhaps adopt mechanics similar to rogue's cloak of shadows).

  • Aimed shot was great in WotLK, but I understand that we had a few too many instants in that time. However, I find nowadays that except again in the rare match opening occurrence and or with Master Marksman proc that I never use this ability. Perhaps a shorter cast time / better damage?

  • Widow Venom is not even on my bars for what appears to be a purely PVP ability. Why is this ability not merged with another attack / last longer / undispellable?


Besides useless abilities, I have two other major gripes.

  • Master Marksman. This talent should not be an RNG proc (haven't you learned your lesson with this sort of thing?). Honestly, make it 100% chance for a stack, and up the stack count requirement if you must for balance sake.

  • CC. While I think we have the potential to be very deadly with CC, no other class has to deal with the crap that we do in order to pull it off. Between scatter shot causing the player to roam randomly, having to aim our ice trap with trap launcher, and players being able to stack on each other in order to soak traps, the fail rate is so much higher than it needs to be.

  • Aspects. These have become near pointless. Almost everyone uses macros to switch to Fox when casting Steady Shot and then Hawk when casting Aimed/Chimera. Remove or rework.


What makes playing your class more fun? High skill cap, potential to be deadly with CC, nice burst when we're lucky

What makes playing your class less fun? Having to wait until Chimera's Shot is up to be deadly, having Steady Shot be so prevalent in our rotation (see below), having to deal with spellcleave teams being so ridiculous right now

How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.) Two words: Steady Shot. Between being forced and often unable to cancel it in order to (a) stay alive, (b) interrupt a crucial cast, or (c) getting in melee range of someone, there is much frustration to be had. Our rotation is dumbed down to simply waiting for Chimera Shot to be up, and in between casting Steady Shot or Arcane Shot (to dump extra focus). Also, considering the difficulty of getting two Steady Shots off without having to cast anything in between, Improved Steady Shot is a huge pain in the !@#.

What’s on your wish list for your class? More consistent CC, more interesting rotation, less focus on Steady Shot

What spells do you use the least? Camouflage, Aimed Shot, Widow Venom, Serpent Sting. These should be class-defining abilities, and yet I find myself rarely using any of them.
