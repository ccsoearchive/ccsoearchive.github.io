I have been playing a hunter for over 5 years now. I have seen the many different incarnations that we have gone through. I have persevered. I have become strong.

The following can only be explained as personal belief and preference and any thoughts or ideas expressed should be considered as such.

    What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]
    Mostly PvE with a dash of occasional PvP

    If PvE, what type of PvE? [Heroics/Raids/Other]
    Casual Raiding (it does exist :P) and some heroics. I am also working on the Loremaster title and as such I am doing alot of questing.

    If PvP, what type of PvP? [Arenas, BGs, Rated BGs]
    Mostly just random Battlegrounds. I have done a handful of Arenas and while I find them fun, I have a hard time finding a stable team.

    What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.
    I was actually one of those that did not really like these changes. Removing the ammo and the haste off of the quivers, along with removing the need to care for your pet has made the Hunter class loose a lot of its flavor. Also the change from mana to focus makes my class feel more like a ranged rogue than its own individual and unique class.

    What makes playing your class more fun?
    My pet definatly makes playing my hunter the best thing. I have the same cat that I have raided Kara/Gruul/Mag/SSC/TK/BT/ZA/Nax/TOC (I took a break :D) and finally took the same cat with me to kill the Lich King. More people know my pet than know me. My pet is VERY dear to me and if i ever lost him, I would probably stop playing my hunter.

    What makes playing your class less fun?
    The only thing that makes playing a hunter not fun is dealing with a person who has no idea how a hunter works. Not really a hunter specific issue there. :D

    How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)
    I really like the rotation of Survival. MM not so much, but that has to do with the fact that i have played far less as marks then as Survival. BM feels good. But I don't do much current content as BM, I mostly use it for solo-farming old content.

    What’s on your wish list for your class?
    I wish we could have some of the old hunter things back, like ammo and pet care. These things defined a hunter. These things made us strong.

    Oh! and a new Dragonspine Trophy

    What spells do you use the least?
    This one is easy. Scare Beast. At least... it was.. I dont even know if we still have it.

What type of content do you focus on?
-PvE, finding I enjoy PvP also more often as well

-Random BGs, entering into Arenas and finding them fun as well

What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.
-Pet bar disappearing bug, Client launcher failing to launch the client and having to run the client manually as administrator as a work around (though not class specific). I do appreciate the update to the pet AI and stance changes, however I am finding that when I want somethings specific from my pet it requires more micromanaging of it. As an example I enjoy going in and soloing the Onyxia 10man encounter on my hunter for fun and my pet has a more difficult time staying on the whelps and holding them in the current defensive stance than it did in the previous defensive stance. I find that our shots requiring a ranged weapon and being interruptable but being neither melee or spells for trinket/enchant/etc procs quite annoying personally, yes they do physical damage and some have a cast bar. Currently I do find it annoying that there is not a competitive one-handed weapon enchant favorable to hunters, however only having to collect/win one melee weapon is a quality of life upgrade.

What makes playing your class more fun?
-I enjoy being able to tame beasts in the wild of varying models and skins to help me in combat and the recent changes to pet abilities makes it easy to use a variety of them for different roles. I really appreciate the hunter specific tameables in 4.2, and eagerly look forward to any and all that may come in future patches. I enjoy being able to combat at range and see what is going on around me.

What makes playing your class less fun?
-The biggest thing I find unenjoyable about my hunter is the minimum range mechanic, though from a flavor perspective I see the role it plays, its quite difficult to draw a bow with someone in your face. It may be my playstyle but I find myself easily stunlocked by rogues and feral druids.

How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)
-I miss having all instant shots like back in 3.3.5 (or was it 3.3.2?) and using Aimed Shot on cooldown as an instant shot (playing primarily as Marksman through Wrath and Cataclysm) beyond that I enjoy the new priority system more engaging than the prior press-buttons-in-this-order-over-and-over-till-the-boss-stops-moving that it was in wrath.

What’s on your wish list for your class?
-Quite simply new pets, new models, skins, taming challenges, or easter eggs like my favorite pet the Prairie Wolf that barks like a happy puppy when clicked. Being able to complete as a current content tank with a tenacity pet would be interesting, I am able to save some pulls in heroics with my tenacity pet and solo old content with him as well, though he's had aggro issues since the changes to pet stances. I'd also like to see a more fluid way of using the trap launcher, though I have no ideas on how that I may suggest.

What spells do you use the least?
-In my current spec and playstyle I don't use Kill Command or Cobra Shot very often, and have only recently found the utility of Eagle Eye though love it now that I have. Oh and if I may point out, our shots are not technically spells lol.

Thank you for the opportunity for to give this feedback.

#What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]

-PvE

#If PvE, what type of PvE? [Heroics/Raids/Other]

-Everything. From cutting edge content Heroics and raids to 5-mans and raid content from older expansions, whether soloing or with a few friends.

#What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.

-Trap launcher being on the GCD is quite a pain. It'd be nice to be able to simply macro the whole ordeal into one button, ready to select the target area and fire. Trap launcher itself requiring a global cooldown makes CCing on the fly sluggish.

#What makes playing your class more fun?

-The ability to survive and be a very flexible/adaptable DPS. This is due a lot to being ranged and having a unique rotation with focus. The ability to have 5 pets that provide different buffs to groups or functions depending on the situation is invaluable, on top of taming which I believe if the most innocent fun I've ever had playing a character.

#What makes playing your class less fun?

-Various bugs that still persist, like the pet bar disappearing or pets despawning or dropping all threat for unknown reasons.

#How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)

-I play two specs primarily: Survival and Marksman.
For Survival, I feel the rotation is fluid - there's no complications about how much of an ability to do or what ability should be done. When Explosive shot and Black Arrow are on cooldown, the rotation is filled with as many Cobra Shots as you can and the formula doesn't change much outside of spending excess focus on Arcane Shots.

Marksman, on the other hand, feels clunky to me with the necessity to fire off Steady shots in pairs. The DPS also requires precise use of arcane shots to really increase DPS - which is nice for people interested in the potential of increasing the damage they do on fights but is intimidating for players new to the spec.

Both specs feel full, though, which is a great improvement upon how DPS felt in earlier expansions. You're constantly doing something and for Hunters this is largely due to the unique mechanic inherent with Steady Shot/Cobra Shot.

#What’s on your wish list for your class?

-More stable slots. 1 more page or so is a modest wish. Taming pets, as I've said, is innocent fun in a game of grinding and dungeoning and really makes the class stand out. With that in a growing game, there are dozens of cool pets that people want to collect, and the space they have to hold them is limited. I can only imagine that this would become an increasing concern for hunters and more patches and expansions are released providing more and more pet looks and families.

Also, I wish that Survival had access to some amount of a self-heal, similar to the healing received from Chimera shot in Marksman. Some kind of drain life attached to Black Arrow would be cool.

#What spells do you use the least?

-Widow Venom. Doesn't seem to be too incredibly useful as it's very circumstantial and doesn't do too much.

Raptor strike, when glyphed, is very useful but outside of that I feel it is wanting.

Not sure why feeding the pet is still a button or a function in the game outside of RP purposes.
