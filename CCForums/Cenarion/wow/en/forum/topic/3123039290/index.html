We’re looking for feedback on your class as we work on changes and adjustments for a future game update. While we may be making some specific class changes in 4.3, what we’re looking for in this thread is overall feeling on the class as a whole for more long term changes in the future.

Here is the kind of feedback that is NOT helpful in this particular thread:

  • Concerns that your current damage, healing or tanking is not competitive. While this kind of feedback is often useful, it also is the kind of thing that can change from patch to patch, and so it isn’t as useful for long term vision changes.

  • Concerns that your feedback is being ignored or that we don’t take these kind of posts seriously, or otherwise posting anything that is off-topic or not in the spirit of this thread’s intent.

  • Requests for responses to your suggestions or feedback. The purpose of this thread is to get information to us, not the reverse.

We hope you take these threads seriously, as we’re going to be reading them very carefully. We ask that you take time to think and formulate a useful and objective reply, as this feedback could be used to alter your class in substantial ways.

We won’t be replying here addressing your concerns. This is purely for us to read your answers to the below questions.

Please copy the below questions into your reply and answer each one.

  • What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]

  • If PvE, what type of PvE? [Heroics/Raids/Other]

  • If PvP, what type of PvP? [Arenas, BGs, Rated BGs]

  • What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.

  • What makes playing your class more fun?

  • What makes playing your class less fun?

  • How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)

  • What’s on your wish list for your class?

  • What spells do you use the least?

Great guns, the ability to do almost any older content solo, good CC. Having my pet croc, Bill. My wife won;t let me have a real croc (though I do collect smaller reptiles).

■ What makes playing your class less fun?

A lack of uniqueness among my class and other DPS. Raid spots are based purely on DPS numbers now. Yes, hunters do well at that, but I think encounter design is so "generic" that any DPS can replace any other as long as the DPS # is high enough.

I'm not happy with the new pet stances, though I can't really put my finger on why. Okay, so I miss setting Bill the Croc on auto-slaughter while doing Archaeology. :) But there's something just MISSING; the current "Assist" isn't quite working like the old "Defensive". No self-heal. :)

Let me customize Bill the Crocolisk so I can choose the skin (croc) I like and the ability I want. I like having lots of pets available in the stable, but for personal preference, I want to run with a Crocolisk. That's who I am.

A decent self-heal, ala Rogues. Yes, again. It drives me nuts that my only self-heal in PvE is to chug a potion, only ONCE per fight. Also, more responsibility for staying alive needs to be shifted to DPS.

■ What spells do you use the least?

Most of the "special" shots don't have much use in dungeons, raids or general PvE. It's easier to kill something than silence or stun it.

Generally, I shoot stuff or do CCs. Everything else is gravy that I may not eat.

# What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]

Both

# If PvE, what type of PvE? [Heroics/Raids/Other]

Everything, Heroics, Raids, Old content

# If PvP, what type of PvP? [Arenas, BGs, Rated BGs]

Arena, BGs

# What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.

Poor pet pathing and control, too glitchy. Terribly clunky crowd control for pvp. The inability to do hardly anything to anyone who can LoS, particularly in Dalaran Sewers arena. Aspect Dancing is clunky. Focus is too hard to gain in pvp when being focused by others. Camouflage only usable at the start of arena, unable to feign>camo.

# What makes playing your class more fun?

Soloing old content, when cc actually works.

# What makes playing your class less fun?

Being LoSed into the ground, cc failing because of how terrible it works, glitchy abilities, aspect switching constantly, Survival spec being currently useless both in pvp and pve, being focus starved because our resource is tied to a shot that can easily be stopped. Camouflage being nearly useless outside of soloing old content. Inability to fire in short range makes certain pve encounters tedious.

# How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)

PvE rotation is fine right now, I have no real problems with any spec.
PvP can be terribly focus starved if you are being attacked by more than one opponent.

# What’s on your wish list for your class?

Better cc (we need a freezing shot, not just a trap with loading time that fails.)
Slightly higher natural focus regen, slightly lower steady/cobra gain.
A smaller close range gap (though not nonexistent), so that we can actually hit people who try to LoS up close.
Survival to be changed to work with weapon damage so that it can actually scale with gear correctly.
Multiple bugs and glitches to be fixed, many of which are pet related.
-% healing built into one of our main shots.

# What spells do you use the least?

Widow Venom, short time, low negation, focus cost, not worth using.
Eagle Eye
Beast Lore
Aspect of the Cheetah/Pack, daze makes it pointless in pvp, minimal use in pve.
Immolation trap, low damage, no point. Minimal use in pvp with entrapment.
Raptor Strike, no use in pve, very little use in pvp if any at all.
