The current state of pets with their unreliable nature and buggy AI. Even with using pet attack macros and using Passive, my pet will frequently attempt to run back to me, causing loss of DPS time.


■What makes playing your class more fun?
When I can play the specs I enjoy (Survival and Beast Mastery.) Marksmanship is a clunky mess.

■What makes playing your class less fun?
Being forced into a single spec as a Pure that happens to be the most clunky and unfluid of our specs. If you polled every single raiding Hunter on what their favorite spec is, you would maybe get 5% saying Marksmanship.

■How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)
Survival and Beast Mastery feel extremely fluid and is the reason why they've been so well received by the Hunter community. Priority systems such as those work wonders for adding compelling gameplay, whereas clunky full rotations such as Marksmanship are looked down on.

■What’s on your wish list for your class?
I would like to see Survival and Beast Mastery brought up to cbe feasible options somewhere. Currently, Marksmanship has been built up as the end all be all of our specs. It has far superior AoE, far superior single target, an interrupt, double Rapids for burst scenarios, is our designated PvP spec, and is also the best leveling and soloing spec now with Aimed Shot burst and Chimera self heal. The other specs need to be competitive somewhere or at least have a niche role and strength.

■What spells do you use the least?
Immolation Trap. The DoT effect is not worth the GCD it takes to cast for it's pathetic damage and it is out DPSed by Explosive Trap even on single target for Beast and Marksmanship (Survival excluded here because of Black Arrow.) Runners up go to Raptor Strike, Snake Trap, and Widow Venom.

  • What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]
    At various times, both.

  • If PvE, what type of PvE? [Heroics/Raids/Other]
    Currently just heroics, but I have raided in the past and probably will raid again in the future.

  • If PvP, what type of PvP? [Arenas, BGs, Rated BGs]
    Battlegrounds, battle grounds, and more battlegrounds. No RBGs yet, but that's a function of my life schedule rather than a dislike for the idea.

  • What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.
    Now that the pet spawning has been addressed (especially in BGs -- having a pet die instantly because it spawned with a mere 6k health really sucked) quality of life is fine.

    Maybe making Trap Launcher not so stupidly, unnecessarily complex to use. Why do I have to press two buttons to do one thing? Y u no one button, trap launcher!?

  • What makes playing your class more fun?
    The feeling I can do anything if I need to -- I have the tools to deal with just about any situation (tank dies in a heroic, soloing the Molten Front mini-bosses, escaping getting ganked by that one damn rogue, etc.)

  • What makes playing your class less fun?
    The fact that I can only do anything if I have the proper set-up time. Wrong pet out? Nope, sorry, can't tank that boss. Deterrence on CD? Well, that rogue is gonna eat me alive. Hey, is that a protadin? Only way I can kill him is if I hide behind this row of healers and hope he doesn't notice me...

    I guess the main issue is that hunters have to be proactive, and can have trouble reacting. If I get a drop on someone or something then I win -- heck, if they don't see me while they are doing something else I can even get your average frost mage. Basically, however, my CDs are evenly matched with the CDs of most other classes (except, of course, frost mages, but that's a problem everyone has) but my resources are generally more limited. So PvP encounters usually come down to who gets the first move in. If they get the jump on me, it's often difficult to get the upper hand, because as soon as I almost do I have to strafe and steady shot and hope I don't die.

  • How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)
    It's in a good spot -- predictable enough, but still requires some attention. Focus in PvP is the only 'rotational' problem I have.

  • What’s on your wish list for your class?
    A reliable self heal, or a dot-scrub, or a way to share damage with my pet (in other words, something more generally useful than than the overly-situational Roar of Sacrifice). Hunters are the only class that can't recover health or cope with predictable incoming damage, like a Shadow Priest's dots. It sucks knowing you are gonna die but having nothing you can do.

    Not being able to Cloak or Iceblock or recover crazy amounts of health using Victory Rush or a ghoul sacrifice is a bigger hindrance to hunters than our weakness in melee range, in my humble opinion -- and it's unlcear what we get in exchange for this lack. Tradeoffs are cool, but only when what you get is equal to what you give up.

  • What spells do you use the least? Raptor Strike, times one billion. Yeah, it might be nice when glyphed (and maybe could even solve some of the issues I discussed above) but man, I have to many keybinds as is, I can't fit it in anywhere.

    Oh yeah, and Widow Venom (seriously, it's crap). And the pet Disarm (give up a stun for a situational ability? Um... no.). And all the cool stuff we lost when Chimera Shot lost its chimerical nature.
