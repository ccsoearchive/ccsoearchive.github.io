What type of content do you focus on?

I play both sides of the coin, for PvE i do heroics and raids. In PvP I do mainly bgs, rated bgs and world pvp and although I hate arena with a passion I still participate in basic tier.

PvE
Raids, although the lack of proper build up from quests is a downer, I was more concerned about killing Cho'gall than Deathwing due to his lack of physical appearances or direct influence.

PvP
Primarly World PvP and BGs, there needs to be another popular PvP area like Halaa/Terokkar or Silithus, a reason to entice World PvP instead other than Tol'barad. Or adding more vehicles into the TB battle, the catapults demos and potential promises of Flying vehicles was alurring for Wintergrasp.

What are your biggest quality-of-life issues?

That you guys removed Eyes of the Beast, it was one of the greatest perks of being a hunter. Which allowed for easier positioning for PvE encounters and a fun way to torment players that attacked it and were PvP tagged.

Focus is a minor issue in PvP as well, since our burst and overall damage is lower compared to other classes. Since the generation of the pool is slow and easily gimped with players pillar humping or running into our melee zone after snaring/etc.

What makes playing your class more fun?

The fact that I have a unique companion system and being able to pelt enemies with arrows or lead.

Also that for Battlegrounds I am a force to be reckoned with and using my abilities in unorthodox ways leads me to be a powerful asset.

What makes playing your class less fun?

That I cannot enjoy arena as my class had their melee damage output nerfed, making melee range the new deadzone and lack of a heal not chained to a talent tree.

Also, I do not like being signicantly beaten by a class that can role a healer or tank on the damage meters, my class is made for dealing damage and I would prefer having my primary competition being damage classes, not a hybrid.

How do you feel about your “rotation”?

I think the MMS rotation is perfect the way it is. Survival though feels stale as Lock N Load procs from Black Arrow are slow and uncommon. Beast Master is also in essence a two button rotation, which causes me to die from nodding off during boss fights.

What’s on your wish list for your class?

Hydras are implemented into the game, as they were promised in 4.1 and have the abilities in game from the ghost Hydra.

Finally given a proper heal as EVERY OTHER class has a reliable method that is readily accessible early in the talents or not tied to their talent tree.

A replacement model for Frenzystrike Longbow, as I can no longer have mine restored after I was hacked.

Changing Deterrence to either heal or remove all harmful effects upon activation while retaining the ghetto bubble.

Changing Trap launcher to instead target an enemy rather than the ground, as it is painfully easy to side step our sadly best CC.

Changing the Gylph for Scatter Shot to instead remove all dots on the target, as the Rogue Blind does.

A damage buff across the board for hunters so we don't have to work as hard to compete with hybrid dps classes in similar gear levels.

Giving Trueshot Aura a passive effect for hunters, we're the only class that doesn't get a stat buff from our aura compared to Fury war's extra 2% self crit or Dk's 2% self str buff.

What spells do you use the least?

Counterattack in the Survival tree, seriously, we no longer have the parry necessary to activate the talent. It's embarrassing that this ability is still in the game and a very good reason we hunters joke that Blizzard has dropped us on the priority fix list.

Another is Widow Sting, an easily removable debuff that costs a tremendous amount of focus for something that should be chained to an ability like the priest Mind Blast when talented or warrior Mortal Strike.

Next is Raptor Strike, it's terrible damage output makes melee have no qualms about entering our new deadzone to strike us. I prefer using Wing Clip over it, since i have a better chance at shaking them off. Also I loathe having to stack for encounters in PvE and will gladly risk killing myself to be able to shoot arrows.

Last is Black Arrow, the final talent for Survival, I hate, yes hate, this ability when I tried doing PvP when i rejoined WoW. As casters would simply dispel it and go right on their merry way to killing me with no repercussions what so ever, compared to the Warlocks Unstable Affliction or some of the Shadow Priest spells. Also it pales in comparison to MM and BM's final talents and makes me feel that it does not deserve this status.

I find with some classes it's a regular, somewhat repetitive rotation that is usually somewhat effective, but with hunters you adapt and change your rotation at times to suit the situation, making it somewhat of a mind game. "Do i fire concussive or ice trap. Do i charge up my focus a bit and attempt a aimed shot, or run for it and wait for my chance to try and nuke them"
