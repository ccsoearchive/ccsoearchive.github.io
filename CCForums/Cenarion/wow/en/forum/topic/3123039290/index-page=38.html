# What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.

Please change this whole "I need 3 GCD's to do 1 CC because its so clunky" mechanic. No other class has to target who they intend to CC, then scatter shot them (notice we have to use an extra small CC CD just to get off our main CC, very broken), then use trap launcher (why trap launcher doesnt have a drop down menu to which trap to launch is just insane, have to use an extra GCD), then proceed to use freezing trap and mouse over who we intend to CC (hoping it lands because the area for human error is large, especially in arena when the other enemy is chasing you and your trying to kite as well as CC your target). Remake freezing arrow that freezes our selected target or another way to CC without us having to do 10 things for 1 cc that has a large area of failure.

Please make freezing trap and ice trap off the same CD. it makes no sense that our only real CC is on the same 30sec CD as our big kiting mechanic. Dk's get to lay down desecration all over the floor per Nec/Plague/scourge stike with no CD as long as they have runes, mages get to polymorph as well as frost nova/ring of frost/cone of cold. With the CC being on the same CD as ice trap it mean no hunters use ice trap in arena or risk having to wait another 30 sec before you can actually CC anything again.

Hunters have a huge problem with their min. range mechanic. Please change it in a way that we can be like other ranged/caster classes, that we can actually do something in min. range. If a caster decides to sit and try to cast while a melee is on them, they get to, why cant we? (not at all saying sitting while being tunneled is good, its just we shouldn't be forced to constantly run away, which ruins our focus regen as well). If a healer is running around an object to LoS you (pillar-humping), you literally cant do anything. If you chase them then your LoS the whole time, if you manage to slow them and catch up, then their in minimum range so you still cant do anything, its an unfair Lose-lose situation. Also frost mages and other classes with many snares/roots ruin hunters because they just root after snare after root and we have to sit there because the good players know to stand next to us where we just have to eat the damage if we dont have an open CD, and with frost mages having around 5-6 roots all on very short CD's, having one or two defensive CD's up wont help you much.

Please fix our focus regen. The 5% focus regen on the 4pc set is truly laughable. It amounts to around .1-2 more focus (someone posted the actual data on the hunter forums, don't want to try to find it to post). Please make it useful and not complete garbage. Also steady/cobra shot is a huge problem in PVP when you will get LoS'd every second by a player (namely healers). Make it so you at least get some amount of focus regen'ed whilst casting the shot instead of when it finishes. You will not believe how many times im focus starved and i go to steady shot, and right before it finishes, my target will purposely run behind a pillar/object and cancel the shot, not only wasting my time, but letting me stay terrible focus starved and being of no use to my team mate.

Lastly, one of the most IRRITATING things to all hunters that do arena/rbg's is the problem with snake trap. Please please get rid of deadly poison on the snakes, not only does it tick for ~100 damage with 5 stacks (wtf? that's not lvl 85 damage, that's more like BC level damage, maybe even lower) but it always ALWAYS somehow manages to be spread to everyone, namely the healer (who we are trying to CC). So now we literally cant do anything to stop the heals until the poison duration is up, besides an occasional silence or interrupt, but we all know that's not nearly enough. Just make the snakes spit mind-numbing and crippling poison (Make it 60-70% slow like a rogues poison so it is more useful, as a hunter will normally have them slowed with concussive shot). Make a different trap perhaps, or even better take out the oh-so-terrible immolation trap that ticks for nothing, and make a poison-barbed trap that will poison the enemy that steps on it for some decent damage (if its got a 30sec CD, at least let it be useful, not a few hundred per-tick)

One last thing: fix aspects please, every decent hunter has to macro an aspect switch to each shot which makes the class work not as intended and just very bad in performing in a PvP environment, make these easier and not "i need to switch aspects between each shot to perform decently".

    What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]
    PvE; Questing, dungeons (just starting heroics)

    What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.
    First off, I want to say I enjoy focus much more than mana. Switching aspects repeatedly to regenerate mana was annoying, and I love that many of my attacks are no longer limited by cooldowns anymore. While I was a little apprehensive about it at first when the change was announced, I think I really like it.

    Something that I think could be changed about hunters is the fact that having no way to heal myself really makes life difficult when swarmed by multiple mobs while soloing or when things go wrong in a dungeon. Even something small like the rogue's Recuperate would really help. This was not nearly as much of a problem prior to the pet stance change (will be discussed below). Since then, however, my pets cannot tank as well for me as they used to. So I have found myself taking more damage while soloing lately.

    What makes playing your class more fun?

    I play as Beast Mastery spec, almost exclusively, and the reason I enjoy it so much is the bond I feel I share with my pet. It serves as my tank, protects me, and fights alongside me, making up a good portion of my DPS. While it is frustrating sometimes to be so dependent on my pet--if my pet happens to die, I feel sort of helpless--I think that it helps to differentiate my class/spec from other pet classes.

    I also enjoy the variety of pets to tame, and "hunting down" the perfect pet skin for me. I also greatly appreciate that all pets are generally on par with each other, and each have uses in different situations based on their abilities.

    What makes playing your class less fun?
    I think this latest change to pet stances (Assist, Defensive, Passive) made playing hunter much less fun for me. Assist and the new form of Defensive do not replace the functionality the old Defensive had. While Assist ensures my pet attacks only my target, it really is jarring to my rotation to have my pet running back to me after an enemy dies, then rushing to the next one, then running back, and so on. In addition, if I see the need for my pet to help off-tank in the middle of a battle, it's awkward to have to switch into Defensive first before telling my pet to attack.

    The new Defensive, to me, is even worse. My pet will only attack enemies if they first hit me or my pet. But sometimes, it doesn't immediately react when attacked (or if I'm attacked). I tell it to manually attack, but sometimes the Attack command does not work the first time I hit the button, and my pet continues to fight the same target. I have to mash my attack button repeatedly until my pet finally comes to save me from an add. (This is kind of why I would like a self-heal of some kind as well!) Also, like Assist, my pet sometimes runs back to me even if there are other mobs still in combat.

    Keep Assist in the game, but PLEASE bring back the old Defensive stance. My pet felt much MUCH smarter before, as it would switch targets of its own accord, pick up enemies that joined the fight much more readily, and would rush into battle even if I pulled first. Nowadays, my pet feels stupid, unrealistic, and like a robot that I must input every single attack command into. I have never mashed my Pet Attack keybind so much as when this change came about. :\

    How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)
    I am generally very pleased with my BM "rotation." It's simple, but effective, and I feel like I know it innately. I think there is enough to watch with pets and procs that it stays interesting and fun.

    I guess the only issue is that if there is a fight where my pet cannot reach the boss, it eliminates many of my key attacks/procs from my rotation. I can't Focus Fire if my pet isn't attacking to build Frenzy stacks. I can't Kill Command. As you can imagine, that's a serious loss of DPS just because my pet can't reach the enemy or follow me.

    What’s on your wish list for your class?
    Other than fixing the issues I have discussed already, I also would like there to be more than just staves as the general best melee weapons for hunters at end-game. I just don't see a hunter bludgeoning his/her prey to death with a giant staff. (Yes, I know it's just supposed to be a "stat-stick" but I DO have to look at that staff all the time!) A few more options would be excellent, even if just for transmogrification purposes. ;)

    What spells do you use the least?
    I rarely use abilities that are primarily for PvP purposes (Widow Venom, Flare, Snake Trap, Scatter Shot, etc.), but that's because I don't PvP much.

    Outside of that, I rarely use Raptor Strike, Beast Lore, and Aspect of the Wild.
