What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.

Something kinda silly but while mounted your pet is by your side (at least ground mounts) If you happen to say get close to a mob your pet reverts from mounted speed to run speed... which sucks. Either you wait for your pet to catch up or keep going and resummon when you reach your destination. Not a huge deal for hunters but also happens to warlocks... just kinda annoying.

What makes playing your class more fun?

In WotLK i was Survival for the longest time, then with Cata went BM till... well... it couldnt put out the number of the other specs. Now i'm trying out Marksman which seems to be the flavor of the month. To be honest the switch from Mana to Focus has been a lot of fun I remember the days of WotLK where you had a high front load burst followed by Aspect of the viper... ugh... Also, No ammo, makes all the difference.

What makes playing your class less fun?

As BM : My main nuke (Kill Command) being tied to pet radius to the target. If this became a "On next attack" or maybe where the pet leaps at the target... Pulls out a Death Ray... whatever! something to make it as usable as other specs main nuke.

As Surv : Too little to do, The fun part of BM to me is... many CDs. More CDs means i get to do more! As MM your keeping Imp SS up, you have 2 main nukes, and are watching your SS's hoping for a instant Aimed Shot... Survival feels lackluster... I put up my dots, i hit Explosive Shot... wait for LaL proc... wait... wait... zzzzz... Ohhh! proc!!!

As MM : Quite a dynamic playstyle, lots to watch and keep up... Imp SS duration seems a bit low.. would be nice if it was like... 10 seconds?

How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)

All rotations feel fine except Surv... id love if they had more to do...

What’s on your wish list for your class?

As BM : Competitive DPS. You made Our partner spec (Demo) Viable and in some cases Best lock dps... BM is.... well... not even close. To reiterate my previous point. Our main nuke is tied to pet location to the target. If that were able to be modified in some way to where we can use it as much as possible (as CD and Focus permits) we wouldnt lose out on dps as much... That said with the exception of Kill Command our mastery only buffs our pet's dps... Great but what about us??? Is there anyway to bring us onto the table? Maybe tie it to Beastial Wrath... they gain more dps and we do too... short CD... win / win.

Pets : Awesome! I love that hunters are the most diverse class (raid buff/debuff) in the game... That said there really is only 2 out of 3 usable types... What about Tenacity? I realize that pets no longer do less dps by race... but talents are still an issue... Tenacity talents just aren't there as a raid spec... Thats fine... i mean it is a solo/tank pet... but... wait... why are some of the coolest pets with useful raid buffs/debuffs Tenacity??? Shale Spider??? :( Can you make Tenacity Raid worthy? Doesn't have to be tanking but something that brings it on par with the others.

I had more... but i cant think of it... oh well this is good lol.

What spells do you use the least?

Camouflage... I remember seeing it in Beta or previews... it looked so cool... and useful... AND it increased the dmg of your next attack? (i think) woah... now its a wannabe stealth useful for... well... getting enemies closer to you... wait... im a hunter... Its still a cool ability and I like the Predator feel behind it... but is it useful? not really.

Also, Kitty Stealth... cool idea and concept but why the speed reduce? I dont know many hunters that use it even though it adds more dps on next attack... its a dps nerf because theyre so slow!

What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both] Both

What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.

The biggest negative quality of life issue for me is the clunkiness of trap launcher. I'd honestly rather have freezing arrow back for PVP and give up the other launchable traps than have a mechanic that requires two precisely timed clicks just to get a targeting reticle up.

There have been many improvements in this area such as firing on the move/Fox aura

What makes playing your class more fun?

The flexibility of the pet system is great now and being able to easily move around while keeping up dps

What makes playing your class less fun?

There are still minimum range issues on bosses like Baleroc that affect hunters in ways that no other class has to deal with. I've actually been told as a hunter I should never be on crystal duty which is a bit ridiculous in a ten man with two hunters...

For BM specificly, Kill Command is painful on anything that likes to move around. You may look like you're in range but if the target is moving you might not be and you don't even know until you click the attack and nothing happens... very clunky

The aforementioned trap launcher issue is probably my biggest pet peeve in pvp

How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)

For BM and SV the rotations are fine, the MM rotation is still a little clunky as it requires those steady shot pairs every 6 seconds or so

Edit: After thinking on it, I'd have to say the SV rotation is a bit boring and my least favorite to play. The 3/4 button rotation is just too simple and I think adding some type of dps cooldown to manage (ie not another proc) could make it much more interesting.

What’s on your wish list for your class?

Trap launcher fix (I bet you didn't see that on coming)
More interesting/useful PVP centric glyphs.
Better masteries for BM and SV (% increase to specific damage is a bit boring)
A better special for BM than intimidation
Fix Kill Command to either have more range or not be active if out of range. Maybe give BM a talent to allow pets to 'jump' to targets within 10-15 yards when KC is used?

What spells do you use the least?

Widow Venom, Immoliation trap, beast lore

What’s on your wish list for your class?
-Exotic pets should not be restricted to BM.
-New 31 point BM cd: Stampede. The five pets you have with you all come out together and attack your target. Acts a bit like Army but uses your current pet skins.
-Change the level 10 BM ability. Intimidation is pretty unimpressive.
-Camouflage is a neat concept but I don't really understand why it doesn't work exactly like stealth. Increase the CD but remove the duration. Provide full stealth benefits during movement. Allow Camo from FD.
-Add pistols to the game and allow SV to dual wield them.
-Increase duration of the Improved Steady Shot talent buff somewhat. I don't like feeling as if I'm always casting Steady shot as MM.
-Trap launcher is awesome but clunky. Make each trap have the launcher built in but cost no focus if placed within 5 yards of yourself.
-Pet collars with some new gem/rune/gear mechanic that lets you swap out a pet's special ability to what you want.
-SV at this point is really misnamed. It needs a good self heal and something else to make it feel like you are great at surviving ... maybe a double jump Disengage like the scout in TF2, another defensive CD like shield wall, or some passive aoe damage reduction like the pets get.
-New traps! Immolation never gets used so improve it, and add others. Some suggestions: Light trap - increased lighting adds 3% damage to all attacks. Oil slick - enemies entering the slick fall down for 3 seconds. Net trap: completely immobilizes a target for 8 seconds, doesn't break on damage (less time in pvp).
-SV (and maybe all specs) should get some kind of multi-trap launcher only usable out of combat and configurable like totems. They could choose the traps and the spread layout could be selected from a few choices like a square, a line, or stacked traps.
-Cobra and Steady should be rolled into one shot. The difference is minimal and probably confusing for new hunters
-Roll Aspect Pack and Cheetah into one Aspect. Don't remove aspect while mounted, even if aspect doesn' t affect mount speed.
-Losing a pet during combat really really sucks. Make an ability that acts like the T5 2-piece and auto heals our pet for some amount based on percent damage done by the hunter.
-Beast Lore needs a revamp. Make it a passive or roll it into Hunter's Mark and after reaching a certain number of kills for that creature type you get bonus damage to that type? I dunno.
-Mountable pets!
What spells do you use the least?
Widows Venom, Wyvern Sting, Beast Lore, Counterattack, Feed Pet, Scatter Shot (why have this and Concussive?), Immolation Trap, Scare Beast, Distracting Shot, Eagle Eye, and most of the tracking spells.
