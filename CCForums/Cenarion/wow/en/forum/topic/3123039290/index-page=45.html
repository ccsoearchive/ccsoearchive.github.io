What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]
PVE

If PvE, what type of PvE? [Heroics/Raids/Other]
Mostly Heroics

What are your biggest quality-of-life issues?
Minimum range as many have stated before. It still feels like hunters have more buttons to hit than other classes. That's part of the fun, there's always something to do it's hard to decide what abilities you need on your bar or what you can combine in a macro.

Combining Traps in a macro works really well until you try to use Trap Launcher and the icon doesn't light up then. Gets difficult in the midst of combat to tell whether or not you've waited enough time to hit the trap button. There has been many a time that I've laid a trap at my feet because the trap didn't launch.

What makes playing your class more fun?
I like being ranged DPS and having a pet to help peel off squishies. Before the tank changes I really liked being able to Misdirect to a tank and give him a load of AoE threat at the start of the fight.

What makes playing your class less fun?
Honestly? Waiting in LFD queue as DPS really sucks. I love my hunter and I wish I could play him 24/7 but those wait times for the LFD as DPS are mind numbing at times.

How do you feel about your “rotation”?
I really like survival though I don't like the implementation of Black Arrow. (Seems to me that survival favors traps a little more so why do we have an ability that is on the same cooldown as our fire traps when we could just launch Immo Trap instead?) MM's rotation feels too "fixed" needing to steady shot to often to keep the buff up. I don't have as much experience as BM but its rotation doesn't seem to flow for me.

What’s on your wish list for your class?
Shorter wait times in the LFD queue. Give BM hunters the ability to tank maybe? Make them a melee type hunter that fights side by side with their pets. The pet shares some of the damage with its master to give survivability? Aspect of the Beast could be used as the "tank mode" to activate the damage share and increase threat.

Also it kinda bugs me that hunters are the PET class but DKs seem to have more pets out, ghoul, army of the dead, blood worms, and gargoyle. Can hunters get some temporary pets? Maybe you call a copy of your current active pet out to auto attack your current target as a DPS cooldown or tank cool down for BM. Just something to continue the pet flavor that we already have going.

What spells do you use the least?
Aspect of the Beast, Aspect of the Wild, Flare (I know it's a PVP spell but I don't PVP), Scatter Shot (if a monster gets too close I'm doing it wrong)

# What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.
1) Pet Rez - having a 10 second hard cast pet rez makes ever sending your pet behind a pillar to interrupt a drinking healer a risky venture. Pet gets gibbed, you don't have root/monkey stun/disarm/roar of sacrifice/freedom for the rest of the match. It also makes it very risky to dismiss one pet and summon another due to the pet health bug. A significant portion of hunter damage comes from our pets, and having to do complicated risk management calculations before having your pet actually attack something in pvp is not fun. Give us instant rez on a medium cooldown with a 5 second rez unaffected by haste otherwise.
2) Min Range - Minimum range isn't really the problem. The problem is minimum range, steady/cobra focus gen, and line of sight. In order to land hard-hitting focus dumps, you have to hard cast steady/cobra. The cast doesn't go off if you get min ranged or los'd. if you chase behind a pillar, you get min ranged and don't generate focus. If you don't chase, you get los'd and you don't generate focus.
3) Talents. One in three specs is good for pve. None in three specs is good for pvp. MM is better in pvp than the other specs because of silencing shot and readiness, but is undercut by buggy hunter mechanics and poor filler talents. (See resistance is futile.) Beast mastery has a niche with bestial wrath burst, but has worse damage, utility, and survivability than marks. Survival has bad damage and the worst survivability of possibly any talent tree for any class in the game. BM and survival don't scale with weapon damage. Survival is too vulnerable to dispel in pvp. I'd love to see survival get the subtlety treatment and made into a pvp spec.
4) Useless abilities. Camouflage is awfully weak. Widow venom is pointless. Fox and Hawk get macroed into shots and you get aspect noise spam and animation delay when you play. I'd love to see Fox get 15% damage reduction a la moonkin even if it's with talents.

I like the potential to RP survival as a goblin hunter. Poisons and explosives seem to fit. Unfortunately, it's a bad talent tree atm. (glares at counterattack.)
I actually like the way scatter/trap works. Wouldn't mind having frost trap untied from freezing, though.

Numerous unaddressed bugs that have been around for too long. Death Knights (one chains of ice and all my pressure is gone for thirty seconds without dispels.)

Eh. Rotation is more of a PvE concept.
