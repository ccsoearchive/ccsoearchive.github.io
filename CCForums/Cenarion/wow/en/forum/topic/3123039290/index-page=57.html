Remove minimum range.

Give SV hunters a dps cooldown.

Remove the standard trap behavior and make them launchable by default.
Give BM a better 31 point talent and roll the existing talent into choosing the spec.
Improve focus regen during PvP (maybe something that procs from "ticks" of Widow Venom?)
Make Silencing shot baseline so that all specs have an interrupt.
Add a "Disarming Shot"

Never:
Counterattack, Immolation Trap , Snake Trap, Eagle Eye, Feed Pet, and Raptor Strike.
Rarely:
Beast Lore, Aspect of the Pack, Wing Clip, Aspect fo the Wild, and Scare Beast

What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]
Both. Primarily PvE content, but also Arenas and BGs.

If PvE, what type of PvE? [Heroics/Raids/Other]
Heroics and 10-man Raids. Also soloing older raids and dungeons for rep, gear and achievements.

If PvP, what type of PvP? [Arenas, BGs, Rated BGs]
Arenas, Tol Barad and random daily BGs (for the conquest points)

What are your biggest quality-of-life issues?
  • Mid-fight pet swapping - having a pet die, mid-fight, and then having to half-rez and dismiss the pet just to bring out a different pet takes too much time and kills DPS. If the pet is alive, sure, dismiss it and call a new one. But if the pet dies, we should be able to call another from the stable ASAP to get back in the fight. It's a clunky mechanic.

  • Pet stances - they're better, but still not quite right. In PvP, each time I change targets, my pet changes too. I want to be able to sic my pet on one target and have my pet stay on that target no matter who else I may shoot. I don't want to put my pet in passive just to accomplish this, that seems counter intuitive

Otherwise, I think we've already gotten a ton of quality of life improvements: no more ammo (sorry engineers), no more pet happiness, no more mana, trap launcher, tamed pets automatically match the player's level... those were all huge improvements.

What makes playing your class more fun?
Hunters are awesome! Pets are awesome!

What makes playing your class less fun?
  • Minimum range - being able to use our instant shots at close range would help put us on equal footing with other ranged dps classes. I like that it makes hunters unique, but not unique in a good way.

  • Max range - other ranged dps classes have no min range and a higher max range than hunters. If min range doesn't change, then we at least need to have the furthest possible range than the other classes, possibly for specific shots, like aimed shot.

  • Survival has no self-heals - it's almost an oxymoron, that Survival hunters don't have a skill that self-heals, whereas BM and MM both do... it seems to go against nature of what the 'survival' spec is meant to be.

  • PvP Effectiveness - we're one of the least represented pure dps classes in Arenas. I think there is a general consensus that hunter PvP is fairly weak

  • No Survival Burst Damage - In Raids, as Survival, I constantly have to hold back on my Rapid Fire because of the cool down. I wait until a burn phase and someone popping Bloodlust before I trigger Rapid Fire so I can 'kick it up a notch'... that and the occasional trinket on-use ability or proc.

  • Polearms/Staves - we have access to many different weapons, yet the accepted dps weapon types are polearms and occasionally staves.

How do you feel about your "rotation"?
I really enjoy and feel comfortable with the Survival rotation. It's steady, has some wiggle room for special shots or trinket procs, and provides a consistently high level of damage. I tried MM for a while and the rotation felt less forgiving and my DPS fluctuated wildly. But then I could push burst damage and leap up the meters.

What’s on your wish list for your class?
  • Dual-Wield Ranged Weapons - some classes can dual-wield 2h weapons, why not let hunters dual-wield ranged weapons (aside from the obvious weapon model issues)

  • Pet customization - armor, collars, something.

  • A pet ability that picks up an enemy target or player (PvP) and carries them out of range of the hunter, or a pet mass knock-back ability, perhaps in a certain radius.

  • Wild Barrage - what a great spell for Survival hunters to get a little burst damage, why waste the spell on one mob type? Yet it's not the old hunter Volley... single target spell with some cool visual effect.

What spells do you use the least?
Master's Call - I never use it... maybe I should.
Widow Venom - I use this in PvP, and rarely on mobs/bosses that have a self-heal ability, but it never feels like it ever that effective.
Feed Pet - I never use this any more.
Counter Attack - partially because it is not that useful in PvE and I don't PvP in my Survival spec.

  • What makes playing your class less fun?


    • Minimum range! It feels like the designers forgot about hunters’ minimum range when designing quests or boss encounters. In PvP it effectually gives a disarm to every class while being trained. It is understandable to have against melee as both will have range issues as you are trying to stay away while they close the gap. Yet, it's problematic when a mage, which is a ranged class with better control and equal if not better burst, has no minimum range. Also causes the most trouble with pillar humpers as we must run a wider pattern by a 5 yd radius.

    Requiring focus for non-damaging abilities in PvP (Tranq Shot, Silencing Shot, Trap Launcher). Spamming Tranq Shots into a Ret with Avenging Wrath only to be out of focus without removing the effect sucks; being unable to get off a clutch silence; clicking trap launcher to see your character set a trap at his feet because you were out of focus having to wait another 30 seconds to scatter/trap the healer.

    Unable to call another one without the 10 sec rez. Locks can instantly summon 3 pets, and would like to be a little more comparable to this. Our pets are our defensive cooldowns and we need them just as much as locks.

    Weak Steady Shot dmg in PvP. The shot is a staple of MM rotation to regain focus. You would expect it to do more damage than it currently is, especially against plate wearers.

    We have zero mitigation against DoT's.

    We are the only class without some sort of self heal for PvP, and while BM can use a spirit mend the spirit beast does not have Roar of Sacrifice which is a must have in PvP.

    The synergy of our traps in PvP. Snake trap cannot be used before landing a freezing trap because of deadly poison (please remove this).

    In addition, the way the haste stat affects the hunter rotation is very complicated and not intuitive to many hunters. I’d love to see this stat simplified somewhat — perhaps by letting haste reduce the cooldown of our signature shots (thus removing all our many haste plateaus).

  • How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)


    • With MM I’d love to see a bit more breathing room, perhaps by extending the duration of the Improved Steady Shot buff by just 2 more seconds so it was less punishing of small 1-second errors. Survival in particular suffers from a lack of dps coolowns, which makes it hard to burst on command in raid burn phases.

    The way our damage is structured as MM and Surv is not a good fit for arenas. Kills rely upon the ability to do heavy burst quickly and outside of BM it is not there. Doing tests on dummies hunter sustained damage is really good, but burst over a 10 second span is nowhere near other burst in the game. Rapid Fire is great for the 15 second duration but does not provide the same burst other classes can do in 5-10 sec span.

  • What’s on your wish list for your class?


    • Petless hunter spec!

    Better self-healing in MM, the ability to self-heal as BM without losing Roar of Sacrifice, and self-healing in Survival which currently has none.

    To see camouflage be a real stealth or at least work like a mage's invisiblity.

    Remove minimum range or make Raptor Strike hit like a Mac truck. Give BM hunters the “stampede” cooldown ability to summon a bunch of pets all at once! Give SV hunters a
    dps cooldown.

    I would love to have our reduced healing in an actual damaging ability that isn't cleasnable like Mortal Strike or Necrotic Strike, becuase right now wasting a global for Widow Venom is not worth using the ability.

    Make Aspect of the Cheetah a viable tool for arenas. Perhaps redesign it to work like a sprint with a cooldown.

    I’d like to see trap launching work better functionally — preferably a one-button macro.

    Do away woth AotFox. Steady shot does so little damage the difference isn't great and is laughable in PvP. Many hunters macro in the desired aspect into their shots already, and it would be great to just go ahead and allow steady shot to be cast on the move while still in Aspect of the Hawk.

    I’d love to see some additional talent customizations in the pet talent trees to help give pets of different trees a more distinct identity in their different roles and to provide BM with more benefit from their 31-point talent (currently non-BM hunters can get basically all the pet dps talents, making the extra four for BM less valuable).

    Make MM mastery ARMOR PEN!!!

    • Counterattack and Immolation trap are certainly never used at all. Almost never used are Eagle Eye, Beast Lore, Aspect of the Wild, Widow Venom.

No other ranged class in the game has this mechanic.

    Aspect of the Fox was a big game-changer allowing us to dps on the move without significant loss of dps. The new pet system for cata was also a big plus giving us more raid utility.

    What makes playing your class less fun?
    1)Minimum range (which hunter's have complained about for years now) Improvement in cata with some bosses not triggering it (e.g. Argaloth) but not a consistent application (e.g.Magmaw, Baleroc) so raid tactics have to be adjusted depending on whether hunters can stack or not.
    2)The trap launcher is also very clunky, to range lauch a trap, we're consuming focus and using 2 gcds which can easily foul up because of bad latency or button spamming. Either go back to the wrath 'frost arrow' for all traps or take trap launcher off the gcd so a trap launch can be macro'ed more smoothly
    3)Lack of interupts/silences Only MM has a viable raid interupt in Silencing Shot making encounters that require interupts a lot more painful if the raid has to bring a hunter or 2, especially in 10mans. Compared to mages, another pure ranged dps class, which has a interupt available to all specs (Counterspell) I would suggest adding a silencing component to Scatter Shot. In PvP, it would still daze the target so no change there but in PvE where targets are immune to daze, the silencing component would still work giving hunters a ranged silence that works but is still on a longer CD than melee interupts.
    4) SV lacks a on-command burst ability. Rapid fire is a cd button but with only one castable shot in the SV rotation, it dosen't help very much. A possible alternative would be a crit boosting ability or focus-cost reduction ability for a short burst of extra dps.

    How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)
    No idea for BM but SV is fine and quite fun in the managing of procs and focus level to get off the right shots in the correct order to maximize dps. MM is improved from wrath where we had to renew serpent sting everytime stats changed to increase chimera shot dps but the rotation leaves little room for error. A 1sec allowance would help in stabalizing the rotation, especially for hunters still learning MM.

    Interupts and more cooldown type burst abilities
