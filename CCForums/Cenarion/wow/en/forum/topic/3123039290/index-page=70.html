What are your biggest quality-of-life issues?


The pet bar disappearing.

Minimum range is a real pain in the butt. I never understood why casters could still cast in melee but I can't shoot a gun.

The trapping mechanic is also a pain in the butt. It's easier to get it right in PvE when you can predict mob paths, but aggravating in PvP where you can't target. Also the damage they do is ridiculously little. Please fix that.

Pets spawning with low health is an issue in both PvP AND PvE. I've lost them to mobs before because they get one shot on summons.

A self-heal would be nice, but I’d prefer better focus regen so I can always get Chimera off when it comes off cooldown. BM has very poor heals imo without grabbing a spirit beast. Survival I don’t play, so I don’t know.

Aspects are somewhat redundant since it’s easy enough to macro around them.

Besides all that, I really enjoy playing my hunter. :)

What makes playing your class more fun?


Being able to solo pretty much any content. (Being able to enter raids without being in a raid group would be awesome, btw.)

When I’m not getting locked down into min range, moving and dealing damage is great fun PvP-wise.

I love finding and taming pets with interesting skins. Having my pet be a valuable addition to the party. Pettanks ftw!

Being able to bring utility to my group with either pet buffs or useful shots. (I wish it were both, though.)

Doing great dps when I hit my stride. When I have the focus for instant shots, it’s great.

Getting locked down to min range.

Being unable to escape being locked down to min range. Of the above two, this one is worst.

Missing a CC cos the mob or player avoided the trap location at the last second.

Whenever my pet doesn’t do what it’s supposed to.

How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)


MM rotation is my most used. I wish the focus regen was a bit faster, but I’m generally able to keep track of what dots need to be refreshed on my target (through use of addons) and when I need to steady to have enough focus to get off my next Chimera.

BM I love more for the flavor and exotic pets, but rarely use cos MM has more utility with silence shot as an extra interrupt, chimera heals, and slows. I do try and use it for PvE for the buffs, but a lot of the time I need the interrupts and slows more. The range on Kill Command feels too little, with me having to spam it until my pet moves close enough to hit cos the mob/boss is moving around. Otherwise the rotation feels pretty straight forward and easy to maintain.

I can’t comment on survival.

Camouflage actually having a use besides just looking cool.

Remove min range. Barring that, give hunters a greater max range than other classes or apply min range to all ranged classes.

BM getting some sort of stampede (summon all pets at once) ability. And really, they should be able to tame exotics from the start.

I would LOVE to be able to use our pets as mounts.

Less clunky/easier to use/more reliable traps.

Faster focus regen.

Any chance we can get Volley back? It looked awesome.

All specs getting more interrupts. I’d play BM more if it had the same amount of interrupts that MM does.

And please fix all our many bugs.

No longer having ammo is a big plus. Focus with its current mechanics particularly in PvP is a huge minus. Having a casted shot that's speed can be slowed, can be LoSed or otherwise negated thru our minimum range, that we are completely dependent on to regen focus is a serious problem. It is my opinion that focus and steady/cobra shot were implemented improperly in this expansion.

What makes playing your class more fun? Being able to nuke a target from high above/far away. Having a pet, although it seems many more classes have some version of a pet now as opposed to when I first started playing.

What makes playing your class less fun? Clunky mechanics that half the time don't work. Having to macro multiple things together because there is no way you can press that many buttons without a third hand. Being completely gimped at times in PvP

How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.) Rotation for the most part is fine in PvE, if you can call it a rotation. In PvP it is a nightmare, there is nothing resembling a rotation in that sphere of the game for hunters. I get 2 shots off then struggle to live until I have enough focus to get another one off. Steady Shot is extremely difficult to get off in Arena versus any legit team even with Fox, making a rotation next to impossible.

What’s on your wish list for your class?

For Blizzard to take an honest look at Hunters and fix our broken mechanics and balance us so we can legitimately compete in PvP. Our PvE damage is down in comparison to other classes, a slight buff to that would be nice. Also a revamp to talent trees would be great. There are a few useless talents like Resistance is Futile or Counterattack.

What spells do you use the least?

Kill Command (It is next to useless in its current form, it was better in its previous form.) Raptor Strike, Widow Venom (I mean seriously Blizzard, 10% is too low and all other classes that have such have it tied to a damage dealing ability)

P.S. In case no one has noticed, hunters have the most posts by far in these feedback threads. Just in case you were wondering Blizzard many of us are not happy with the current state of our class.
