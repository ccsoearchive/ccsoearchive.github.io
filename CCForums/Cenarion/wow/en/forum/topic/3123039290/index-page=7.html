Minimum Range - This hurts hunters far more than interrupts hurt casters, as it kills active focus generation as well as damage.

Bugs - Hunters are still an extremely buggy class. Spells going through deterrence, abilities costing the wrong amounts of focus, etc.

Traps - Traps are not fun to use for CC. Trap launcher is far too wonky and unreliable. Freezing arrow in wrath was better due to not requiring multiple button presses.

What makes playing your class more fun?


I enjoy being a ranged damage dealer that is primarily focused on dealing physical damage. I like that my most significant abilities are instant cast. I like having a pet-centric tree, when it works...

MM - I find that MM has a pretty decent rotation. Both hardcast Aimed and Arcane rotations are good. Sometimes maintaining ISS is annoying, but I wouldn't remove it, but maybe extend the duration by a few seconds

BM - In a perfect situation, the rotation would be awesome. A short CD nuke, plenty of focus regen, BW every minute or so. BM's rotation is very fun. It's just pet AI that hold it back.

SV - SV has a pretty solid rotation. Black Arrow costs too much focus, causing some resource starvation issues, but otherwise solid.

General -

  • Make trap launcher a toggle ability like Blade Flurry.


  • Improve pet AI, fix assist stance.


  • Bring back Eyes of the Beast!


  • Roll Widow Venom into Serpent Sting. Or better yet, add further utility to Widow Venom, such as "Your Widow Venom causes you to be healed for 15% of the damage you cause to the affected target."


BM -

  • Increase Kill Command damage and increase range to 10 yards. Perhaps swap KC and Intimidation as the BM tree ability so that buffing kill command does not effect the other two trees.

  • Move Resistance is Futile from MM to BM


SV -

  • Despite being called "Survival," SV has the lowest survivability of the three specs. Replace the useless Counterattack talent with something that increases survivability. Perhaps a casted self-heal that also regenerates focus; basically a healing substitute for Cobra shot.


  • Reduce Focus cost of Black Arrow, limit it to three targets, remove the CD (but still cause it to trigger fire trap CD), make it trigger LnL when dispelled.


  • Make ES scale with weapon damage.


What spells do you use the least?


Counterattack and Widow Venom.

No ways to regen focus in melee situations in PvP (you finally get away and have no focus to shoot stuff). Trap launcher system is terribly clunky and unreliable when dealing with moving targets. Kill Command is also very unreliable when dealing with moving targets.

What makes playing your class more fun? Focus is much more fun than mana. Mobility in raid environment is stellar. The 3 specs are closer than they've been in a long time in PvE aspects. Disengage is the only good public transportation on Azeroth.

What makes playing your class less fun? Pets out while mounted. Trying to AoE during stack phases. Trying to PvP in general (personal taste, prefer to play other classes in PvP).

How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.) BM feels like it needs more "buttons to push." The Frenzy/Focus Fire dynamic is pretty solid, but the duration could stand to be maybe 3 or 4 seconds longer to account for Frenzy stacks not being built up fast enough by pets (This is especially problematic in phases that involve movement. Air phases are even worse.) BM also has very high burst AOE potential, which is nice. Overall though, the spec is boring to play at times and its damage is sub-par when compared to the other two for the most part.

SV also feels like it's missing something. A good portion of the rotation just deals with spamming Cobra Shot. The Lock and Load proc makes it much more interesting, but the fact that Arcane Shot consumes charges is pretty bogus. Removing this mechanic from the proc would prevent focus-capping and would add some nice burst for PvP purposes. Black Arrow still feels weak and uninteresting. Maybe adding in some initial damage upon application would help, as well as adding a talent that procs the cooldown resetting when something happens.

MM for the most part feels very solid. The only complaint I really have is that Improved Steady Shot's duration forces us to use a much more rigid rotation instead of a priority queue. Steady Shot pairs are a necessity at least twice a rotation. Other classes have a priority rotation that involves a filler, such as Lightning Bolt for an Elemental Shaman. Marksman Hunters are pretty much bound to craft a predetermined rotation with set cast times on Steady Shot just to make sure we can use 2 pairs each Chimera Shot cooldown. While the current rotation works (and very well, judging from sims and logs) it's lacking the reactionary playstyle found with other classes. While I'm not a fan of heavy reliance on RNG, it is nice to have to react to procs from time to time. Aimed Shot! hardly fits that bill, while Resistance is Futile is very "BM-esque" and not usually picked up by raiding hunters.

What’s on your wish list for your class? Replace Counterattack with something usable. Make Silencing Shot baseline for hunters. Make Disengage usable out of combat. Another Deterrence rework would be appreciated. A little more consideration for our melee range vulnerabilities during quest and encounter design would be much appreciated. The Molten Front daily "Into the Fire" sucks kodo farts when you have 3 other people doing it as well. When they ignore their adds and your Mend Pet pulls them all, it tends to be a headache.

What spells do you use the least? Aspect of the Wild, Immolation Trap, Widow Venom. Oh, and Counterattack. Nobody uses Counterattack.
