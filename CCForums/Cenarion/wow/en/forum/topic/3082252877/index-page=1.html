1. deadzone sucks.

2. deadzone + LOS sucks. in the "better" spec marks you lose a ton of DPS to the pet being worse. having your pet !@%#d behind a box is terrible. but its viable for them to do that to negate your dps completely while you can do zero due to deadzone / los. Imagine if frost mages had a deadzone? Grats.

3. rogues and ferals demolishing hunters. They can stay on us better than we can get away from them. Flare is a joke and does nothing to prevent stealth. Mark is flimsy at best. This pushes hunters towards BM for burst openers. At this point they should rename feral druids to hunters and hunters to prey. I guess having hunters hard comp rogues hurt too many people's (devs) feelings.

4. pet control is still glitchy at best. LOS to pet for abilities, pet getting stuck (a few matches in sewer it would not come down from the start area no matter what i did), terrible pathing.

5. intimidation working on "next attack" instead of as a cast ability. It already is basically bad to use on any target getting stunned, even worse that you have to jockey macros to selectively intimidate.

6. camo revealing partners via their buffs makes it a poor stealth when you need to move.

7. beast gets talents that are marks centric, marks gets beast centric buffs. The pet buffs and deter cooldown just seem placed poorly.

8. tranq shot dispel is the worst offensive dispel. Please find another dispel that eats up that much of a classes resources? We have no other disarm or interrupt in BM unless we use a terrible pet, we have options in marks but less resources to use them.

9. scatter shot + trap is the least effective CC in the game. No other cc can fail or miss based on the other teams positioning. totems/pets/teammates eating traps isn't exactly rocket science. We have to work the hardest to pull off a CC and its the easiest to get around/out of? What happened to freezing arrow?

10. bizarrely behaving melee abilities. Snaring someone at melee range? We're basically dead if they're at melee range. How about a short duration disarm/autotrap, etc?

11. Snake trap as 2nd entrapment buuuuuuut spitting out dots on healers is painfully terrible.

12. Bizarre abilities to make up for lack of abilities. More haste / moving around to cobra/steady but then its required more.


Basically the ranged that do well apply massive CC and constant pressure. Those are the two things we can't do well at all so any warlock or mage is a perfect standin for whatever the hunter would do. We have no real peels, and our big benefit is that if melee try to stay on us we can avoid them better (while doing OK dps). This is simply not enough when we get jumped 2v1 or classes have more peels/removes than we can supply.


But instead we get "blood dk tanking" looked at when they're invincible in arenas. Hunters have always been the leftover, afterthought due to PVE.
