Homeland might be a good one if you're looking for a Horde-side guild. Consider the following:

A few moments ago, following a quarrel between two former lovers, a warrior in our guild punted a goblin from the front door of the Wyvern's Tail Inn, whom I then took aside for some brief post-punt counselling. While this was going on, our druid was attempting valiantly to reverse an accidental spell that had turned a local orc into a frog. While THIS was going on, a happy couple who recently went away for a week-long honeymoon returned, only to be overwhelmed by all the aforementioned foolishness. And shortly thereafter, I was bodily ejected from the bar from starting a fight with one of Orgrimmar's finest. Then there was a dancing bear. Oh, and a most friendly, polite warlock arrived, and then we all settled our differences and had tea. And there was a maggot with a bow tied around it there, too, somehow.

We're definitely not a raiding or PvP guild, and sometimes we're pretty sparse, or difficult to get a hold of. But we have lots of fun RP, a few zany characters, some brilliant in-progress plot arcs, and lots of random moments of hysteria, fun, and varying other forms of general mischief. If this sounds like fun, hop into the Horde-side "homeland" chat channel and we'll be glad to answer questions.

Well, since everyone else is plugging away, I'll throw in our two-cents'.

The Azure Crusade is small for now, but we're recruiting as we go. The story is that it was founded by two former Argent Crusaders who believe, as the Argents do, that honor and morality are critically important--but who also believe, as the Argents do not, that honor and morality dictate taking a stand against the Forsaken and the Horde that shelters them.

Right now, our RP activity tends to run to PvP-related things, as we've only been on this server for a couple of months. But we have some nifty ideas in mind that are making it through the machinery (and that machinery is very complex and sneaky). Very good-aligned friendly, very neutral- and evil-unfriendly. No DKs or warlocks, either.

As a bonus, we are deeply supportive of leveling and PvE, especially the pursuit of achievements, for which we have an already unearthly and growing number of guides on our forum. (Anyone is welcome to look them over, guildie or not; just keep in mind they're rather Alliance-slanted because I don't play Horde.)

Private OOC channel, seven bank tabs stuffed full, spiffy tabard, level 12.
