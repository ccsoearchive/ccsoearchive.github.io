Given that he's wondering if the speed of the fist weapon (which he's using) is an error by Blizzard it more seems like he isn't aware that's the wrong weapon for his off-hand and he's supposed to be using the dagger and less like a general complaint about using said dagger.

The problem with making all off-hands for Combat automatically 1.4 is that you then do exactly what Blizzard sought to get rid of by making all off-hands for rogues daggers. No other class uses agility daggers, enhc shaman dual wield slow weapons, so even a 'slow' dagger is too fast for them, and our 1.4 daggers aren't even a remote possibility. By making all fast agility off-hands daggers, you assure that 3 specs of rogues value them which makes it at least a little likely that when they drop someone's going to find use for them.

The other issue this causes is that it then flips your priority from the fastest possible off-hand for mechanics to the slowest possible off-hand without consequence due to mechanics, which means Combat would avoid the dagger drops that were designed to be useful for them because they would be far worse for them, and then further make your off-hand have higher white damage than your main-hand since even at 75% weapon damage, you're swinging almost twice as fast. It would also make your weapon speeds completely counter-intuitive. As it stands all rogues want a slow main-hand (taking into account weapon restrictions on abilities for each spec) and a fast off-hand (for mechanics). Slow main-hand/Fast off-hand, it's easy, unified and makes sense, which is what Blizzard generally goes for.

It would finally mean going back to either increased useless loot or convoluted loot tables in PVE, and many extra PVP weapons on the vendors, when the system as it stands is doing exactly what they want. Adding fast agility non-dagger off-hands or messing with off-hand swing timers (and then damage/damage coefficients as a result) is a lot of extra work on their dev/art design departments for what amounts to cosmetics. Which is basically the exact reason why all idols, relics, librams are universally relics, why all fast agility off-hands are daggers, and so forth. Conscious design intent by Blizzard to consolidate and clean up item types.
