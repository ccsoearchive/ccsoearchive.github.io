I realize now that mastery may not even be such a valuable choice in PvP.

Both Main Gauche and Crit can be brought up to somewhat similar levels of percentage (around 25-30% each ) however:

30% crit may be much superior because a crit from your main hand deals the same damage as a main gauche proc from your main hand.
While Main Gauche has a chance to proc Combat Potency and your main hand poison, most likely Wound and deal extra damage, Crit simply has a chance to allow all of your other attacks to deal extra damage, which I think outweighs the benefits of Mastery by far.

(The best would be to have a high Main Gauche proc chance and a high Crit chance but that's not possible with the gear stats we currently have at our disposal )

With the next season, I will probably be changing from Mastery to Crit or Haste.
