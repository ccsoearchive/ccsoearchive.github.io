Using EP values for PvP can be useful to establish a starting point, but it should always be taken with a grain of salt. If you fight a PvP fight as you would in PvE, you're unlikely to kill someone being a Rogue; our damage would be too consistent and sustained, and the pressure would not appear. Certain stats cater more towards bursty, unhealable damage while others tend to be higher sustained damage (like haste).

Typically in PvP you focus on the unhealable damage because it can kill someone before anyone can react. If we had much higher sustained potential with lower burst capabilities, it may have been a possibility to follow the EJ stat values because we could just plow through the heals without much regard. Unfortunately, it's pretty easy to heal our damage right now.
