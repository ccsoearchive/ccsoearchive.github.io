Edited by Kharaz on 3/20/11 3:10 AM (UTC) I made my offspec Combat two days ago and I really like it most of the time.

Sure, I think that Subletly is probably better OVERALL in the hands of a 2k + player, but Combat definitely has a niche of its own. The CONSISTANCY of it's control is astounding, and that, above all else, is what makes it "viable."

Sub has the potential for "what the !#*!" moments, like huge Shadow Dance bursts and lolpro prep resets (double vanish, double dismantle, DOUBLE KICK LOL, double shadowstep HUGE) but it pales, absolutely pales, in comparison to combat for CONSISTENT CC. Assassination isn't in mentioned here.

Combat also can nearly double its damage with blade flurry, which is pro.

I use a very unconventional build built around control - consistent control no matter what the situation.

improved kick
glyph of garrote
blackjack

I'm sure you can see obvious potential for DR and screwups, but let me tell you, nothing beats sapping the DPS, garroting the healer, pounding on the helpless healer until sap breaks only for the DPS to find that he is doing 30% damage for 8 seconds, followed by a blind. (breaking blind with trinket? redirect -- KS) A few moments after KS wears off the DPS, gouge will be ready and off the DR. OUCH. All that CC from one dude.

improved kick for long-term control, and with improved recup + quickening, combat has healthy self-healing. Definitely harder to kill than Assassination, even with the -10% damage.

Basically combat is about control, consistant control. Sub has more CLUTCH control (i.e. prep) but if combat is played well you may just circumvent the NEED FOR THAT CLUTCH CONTROL in the first place.

Honestly combat has less damage potential than sub, which is what puts it befind ultimately.

Sub = "burst" control
Combat = sustained control

Different but both have merit, HOWEVER

DAMAGE
combat < sub

So combat needs a damage fix and it's fine.

The real issue is the offspec talents. Combat gets EITHER assassination damage help OR sub energy + CD reduction help. Both assassination and sub rogues don't have to make a choice like that.

Bandit's Guile shouldn't have that huge ramp-up time.
