Forgot to mention that the guide is excellent, I would however add that hunters are beatable, but only if you use what they give you wisely. For instance, their pet. Well timed with a somoke bomb, you can build points of the pet like 2 or more to get recup up to mitigate any damage you may have taken, then get of one or two more before smoke disipates to get off deadly throw, after you redirect. Before this trick, you should blind if in range of hunter to force trinket and have the time to pop smoke. So then you sprint after you throw, then vanish. Use an opener such as ambush and one sinister strike followed by revealing strike, and see if you can get of the nine second kidney, this should allow you to set up burst. Ofcourse theres ways for this to backfire, and points generation taking forever may lead to a failed attempt, but if you pop adrenaline at the right time, youll be able to do this fast enough for it to work. Use adrenaline as you sprint to the target. It works like 50 % of the time for me, and when it doesnt, then ill probably get kited and killed to death from afar
