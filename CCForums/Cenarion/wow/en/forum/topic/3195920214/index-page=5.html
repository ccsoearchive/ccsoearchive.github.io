Reposting a Hunter Feedback response here. Original by Gamaliels.

Edited by Gamaliels on 9/25/11 1:14 AM (PDT)

First and foremost for me, our dodge needs to be actually looked it.

It seems as if it just slipped through the cracks.

Average hunter dodge in PVP gear is 3-4%, that is ridiculous for an agility-based class. When you guys took aspect of the dragonhawk away, you also took 18% dodge away with nothing to replace it since you guys tinkered with how agility worked for us or something. A quick-fix was just adding a flat 18% dodge to the aspect we spent the most time in. Hunter dodge should be in the 20-25% range, like always it was up until you guys changed it a few years ago.

I just saw the new set bonuses for the PVE set for hunters, and giving a focus generation buff to a PVE set feels like a kick in the teeth to PVP hunters. You know full well that every PVP hunter is going to be sacking 2 pieces of PVP gear just to get that PVE set bonus.

The nerf to flare was a buff to rogues, pure and simple, and it should be reverted back to the way it was. Why punish a hunter for having quick reactions? Its not like rogues need much help in defeating us anyway, at least give instant flares back to hunters.

The angle that disengage throws us back should be a higher arc, or it should simply work like heroic leap does for warriors, except for us it just wouldn't do any damage.

The class as a whole needs a baseline self-heal, accessible to each spec, that is on par with all the self-heals every other DPS class got. My suggestion is to buff Spirit Heal, make it a hunter ability that requires a pet, sort of like Kill Command, and just make it available to everyone, not just Beast Masters. Get rid of Chimera Shot's heal to compensate.

Widow Sting's effect should be baked into Serpent Sting. Every other class' healing reduction ability is passive or baked into another ability, why not do that for hunters as well? Our PVP burst is in the gutter as it is, having to spend a GCD and focus just to have it dispelled makes the ability barely worth using.

FIX THE PET BAR AND PET PATHING
FIX THE PET BAR AND PET PATHING
FIX THE PET BAR AND PET PATHING
