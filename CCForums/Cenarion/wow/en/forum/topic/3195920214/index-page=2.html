- Mixing in Widow Venom's ability with all three specs as passive abilities or with talents that all three specs would take anyways for PVP. ex: Efficiency in Marks

- Instant shots that are ranged

- Tameable pets from outdoor mobs, can store 25 of them in a stable

- Tracking enables me to communicate to others about incs in pvp

- Can solo a lot of older raids due to pet tanking

- Great looking pvp and pve sets

What makes playing your class less fun?

- Minimum range is a severe burden in pvp and pve

- Scatter shotting / trapping someone only to have it break off a dot

- Wyvern Sting having a dot after it wears off

- Black Arrow / Serpent Sting being so easily dispellable in pvp ; not worth using considering its focus cost

- Feign deathing only to find out my threat hasn't dropped due to moving too soon, has 1-2 second lag executing it

- Freezing trap being launched is next to impossible to sucessfully trap with target constantly moving

- Flare glitch (If you cast it and then los the point you targeted as it drops, it does not flare up)

- Snake Trap does damage that can't kill a level 40

- Pet bugs up and stays in one place (why can't a pet just drop down from a platform?)

- Low passive focus regen

How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)

- Rotation is fine but your spec-dependant shot should be doing considerably more damage / focus cost than your other abilities that are baseline, comparing explosive to arcane. In pvp spec as a BM hunter, non-Bestial Wrath Kill Command hits for less damage / focus than Arcane Shot on a test dummy and Kill Command damage is armor dependant.

- What’s on your wish list for your class?

- Camouflage: CD is 1 minute but make it unlimited duration until damage breaks it or you fire a shot. Making camo untargetable by ranged aoes since any class can break camo easily and make it a true stealth while moving. With the change, inflict a movement penality much like the rogue used to have with Stealth.

- No minimum range

- Re-implement Eyes of the Beast, morph Hawk and Fox into one aspect

- Higher passive focus regen

- Better pvp set bonus: Instead of 5% extra regen, increase maximum focus by 20 or more (I say more due to the rogue's energy regen and ability cost comparison)

- Un-link Explosive Trap/Black Arrow CD's as well as Freezing/Ice Trap CD's

- Some sort of health regen for all specs

- Silencing Shot baseline for all specs

- Fervor baseline for all specs

- One click trap launcher macro

What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]

My primary focus is PvE but I enjoy PvP as well.

If PvE, what type of PvE? [Heroics/Raids/Other]

Predominantly raiding, with heroics thrown in when gearing up in a new expansion, obviously.

One of my most favorite things to do is extreme soloing. That is awesome, and I highly recommend it for any class, but particularly hunters.

If PvP, what type of PvP? [Arenas, BGs, Rated BGs]

I enjoy the occasion battleground, but most of my PvP time is spent doing 2v2 in arenas (I wish that was still a "viable" arena team for stats and stuff :( ).

What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.

1. As with everyone else, the minimum range (dead zone) issue is huge. There are so many "group up" mechanics now and there are many where I'm having to step out of the group if I want to dps, or have to simply melee and see my dps plummet. Even little things like being in on the crystal rotation on Baleroc cause problems. The crystals spawn at his feet so I have to either stand dangerously far away (melee must watch where I am) or the tank needs to move the boss (which they don't need to do for any other class targeted by the crystal).

2. This is a little odd, but with my extreme soloing, the "must be in a raid" feature of raid instances is really, really annoying. I ALWAYS have to get some friend to log in an alt, then I group up with the alt, and then they log off. And occasionally no one I know is online and I have to beg someone I don't know to group up with me just to get me into the instance...and god forbid I die and have to get back in again after they've left the group. Not a hunter issue I know but meh ;)

3. Finally, I LOATE being a dps-only class. I know that seems silly coming from a hunter, but this was my first MMORPG and I didn't really realize how much I was limiting myself compared to taking a hybrid class. Now that I'm thousands of hours (tens of thousands?) into this character, there is no way I'm switching my main, but god I regret rolling a hunter as I sit in long queues, or can't run a raid because we're missing a "necessary" class (not often dps!)

What makes playing your class more fun?

Pets are awesome. Being mobile is awesome. Back when aggro was more of an issue, FD was awesome.

The rare occasion where I can offtank something with my pet, and being able to extreme solo stuff with my pet, are both times where I just LOVE being a hunter.

What makes playing your class less fun?

The dead zone. Being dps-only. Being completely reliant on healers if I get into trouble (no self-heal; I play survival, and even chimera doesn't do much). Not having a decent interrupt unless I'm in MM spec.

I actually don't mind camouflage, since the main thing I wanted a "stealth" for was to skip the trash during my extreme soloing, and it works fine for that. What I WOULD like to change is the 60 second thing. I really can't figure out for the life of me why I should have to "refresh" it every 60 seconds. Let me just turn on camouflage and have it turn off when I do something that breaks me out of it. Having to run somewhere "safe" every 60 seconds just to click it on again just seems like bad design to me. We've also wiped occasionally in a raid where a hunter is getting set up in position for trap-throwing and the rest of the raid is taking its own sweet time over something and camouflage breaks...hunter's fault of course but still it just seems like an unnecessarily annoying buff mechanic.

How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)


I've been playing survival almost exclusively since TBC, and I really enjoy the current survival rotation. I'm with Frostheim (and others) that the way haste works is a little wonky and wish it didn't create these "plateaus" we need to worry about, and would prefer if I could just use the same rotation when things like heroism and rapid fire went off (it would just be faster).

I wanted to get into Marksman since I liked the self-heal from Chimera, and I use it when extreme soloing, but I find the rotation really too "tight". I think a rotation more similar to survival would benefit marksman hunters a lot (and not just make it a spec for the uber-players).

What’s on your wish list for your class?

1. Get rid of the dead zone.

2. Change the way haste affects our rotation to a more simple model where everything is sped up, not just some things (and consequently messing with the rotation).

3. This is a wild idea, and I'm sure won't get any headway, but I LOVE tanking with my pet, there is a general shortage of tanks, and I think it would beyond cool if there was an ability or abilities in the tenacity pet talent tree that would reduce a hunter's damage to "tank" levels while at the same time buffing their pet to the point that they could REALLY tank. I mean, like be a legitimate tank in a raid encounter.

I think if hunters could do that it would solve a lot of the "not enough tank" problems, and for me it would just be pure awesome to no longer be stuck in a pure-dps roll. Obviously it would work much better if pet pathing and movement issues were fixed, and it might be cool to tie it into a return to "Eyes of the Beast" somehow (maybe we could switch quickly between pet and hunter viewpoint for moving the pet around?).

Anyway, being able to REALLY tank with my pet would be just beyond fantastic. Maybe a talent at the top of the tenacity tree that let you trade out 10-50% of your attack power for an increase in 20-100% of your pet's stamina (or something that would make sense if that doesn't ;)).

4. I would like it if we COULD use Aspect of the Cheetah while mounted (and it would affect mounted speed). Seems silly that a paladin can ride around faster than I can when I'm the "speed" class.

5. I would love it if we could get a decent ranged interrupt that was available to all specs.

6. For PvP, focus regeneration using steady or cobra simply doesn't work well in a PvP environment, what with all the LoS tactics (particularly in arenas). I like the change to focus and the regen mechanic works just fine in PvE, but in PvP it's definitely broken. Not sure how to fix that.

7. Remove the dot from Wyvern sting after it wears off. It's one of our best CC abilities and is high up in the survival tree but rarely gets used because the CC is often made MORE difficult because of that silly dot (which doesn't do enough damage to really be worthwhile anyway).

What spells do you use the least?

As with everyone else, Widow's Venom, Counterattack and Aspect of the Wild do not get used really.

This isn't a spell, but I don't know of any hunters (although I'm sure there must be a couple) that take the "Pathfinding" talent, and have thought for years that it should be replaced with something more useful.

Trap Launcher... It is so much more difficult to use than every other CC out there. 3) Better self healing(perhaps a buff to spirit bond/chimaera, and giving something to survival).
4) Better armor design ;p

■What spells do you use the least?
Situational spells like distracting shot, beast lore. Also, immolation trap, because explosive is simply better in almost any situation.
