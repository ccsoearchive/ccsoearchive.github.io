I'm going to start redirecting these sorts of requests over here:
http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/2046965585

I'm extremely honored that people are asking for input about their characters, and in some instances I've gone far out of my way to help both on the forums and ingame, but this isn't necessarily a responsibility I want to adopt. I could very easily miss something while scrolling through someone's armory if I'm the only one looking, beside the fact that it somewhat undercuts the purpose of writing this guide to begin with.

This is why I would recommend people make a new threads if they want to be inspected, so that several people can check them out and mutually cover what others might have missed. This is a much more thorough and comprehensive approach, and I check these threads when I have time, as do many other very competent posters. It will also get you information quicker than if someone is waiting just on me.

I'll always look at specific questions though, this is a good place for those.

That said, I didn't notice much out of place.
  • You're using a crit cogwheel, haste would be better (maybe even hit, to help with reforging).
  • Meteor Shard is a comparatively weak MH, try to replace it when you have the opportunity.
  • The socket in your gloves isn't really worth matching, although it's not really worth replacing either.
  • In comparison, you should match your boot socket; this will also get you closer to the hit cap.
  • If you're raiding, I'd want at least hurricane on my weapons; landslide can wait for epics.
