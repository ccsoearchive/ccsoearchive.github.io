I'm not... What exactly are you asking, Rhakzul? If you're asking about socket bonuses, the reason why +20 agility is better than +20 mastery is that agility is about twice as valuable as mastery in terms of the amount of additional DPS it will give you. If you were sacrificing *40* mastery for a 20-agility socket bonus, then there might be some room for debate. But you ain't. :)

I can't speak to why you're not doing as much DPS as you feel you should, but I do wanna point out that your hit rating is a lot higher than it needs to be. You're at 1573 in the gear you logged out in, which is 241 higher than you need to be at the spell cap. Reforging all of that hit into more useful secondary stats (mastery, or haste where mastery isn't possible) will likely net you a few hundred additional DPS at least. Strongly recommend you use a reforging tool to help you through this; it might well recommend changes to almost everything you've done there.

Online load-and-fiddle tool: http://shadowcraft.mmo-mumble.com/

Downloadable reforge helper: http://elitistjerks.com/f78/t115053-complete_rogue_reforger_tool/

More info/discussion: http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/2267487076

XI. Consumables:

Using proper foods, flasks, and potions is easily a significant increase to your DPS, and raiding players especially should always be prepared with the proper consumables. Since Flask of the Winds can be price-restrictive to some players, a number of related elixirs are also listed.

Food 234 Skewered Eel.
156 Tender Baked Turtle.

Flasks/ 780 Flask of the Winds.
Elixirs 315 Elixir of Impossible Accuracy (below the spell hit cap).
270 Elixir of the Master.
247.5 Elixir of Mighty Speed.
168.75 Elixir of Impossible Accuracy (above the spell hit cap).

Potions 325* Potion of the Tol'vir.

* Assumes single-potting for a 4-minute encounter, as a baseline.
Double potting: this involves drinking a potion quickly just before entering combat, and then drinking another after the 2-minute CD has worn off. This is easier on some fights than others, and requires a decent amount of tank-DPS communication (a simple count-down to the pull suffices).

This macro can streamline the process:
/use Potion of the Tol'Vir
/cast Stealth

*** Combat Starts ***
