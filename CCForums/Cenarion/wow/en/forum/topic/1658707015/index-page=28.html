Shadowcraft is putting my projected dps at 28112, but I'm currently pulling approximately 2.2k. Does shadowcraft usually list things higher than reality (lots of movement, target switching, etc;) or do you suggest I re-evaluate my rotation?
2.2K or 22K?

ShadowCraft assumes a perfect raid environment without target switching, etc.; you can realistically expect to see close to your theoretical DPS on Baleroc, but not really anywhere else this tier (you could even beat it, slightly). The idea is that if ShadowCraft says your DPS is higher one way, then that's the right way, even though the DPS number itself would (usually) be incredibly unrealistic to attain.
