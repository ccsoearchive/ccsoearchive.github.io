I don't know, since combat is 3-5% behind assassination minimum in PvE (more at lower gear levels).

For your other question:
Agility: 2.6
Specials Hit Rating: 1.75 (1 - 481 hit rating).
Spell Hit Rating: 1.4 (482 - 1332 hit rating).
Mastery Rating: 1.3
Haste Rating: 1.2
Expertise Rating: 1.1
Attack Power: 1.0
Crit Rating: 0.9
White Hit Rating: 0.75 (1333 - 2763 hit rating).
So after 1332 hit rating, your priorities should be mastery and haste ratings.
