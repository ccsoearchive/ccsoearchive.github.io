Quick question about the rotation for future reference:

I understand the opening, however, I'm a bit confused about the Ruthlessness bit. Why should I not mutilate if Ruthlessness pops? If it does, what should I do?
The only time you should not mutilate if ruthlessness procs is in your opening rotation between rupture and SnD. Since you're building immediately into a 4-5 point envenom right after SnD, putting more than one point into it is a DPS loss unless you absolutely must in order to get it up.

This also lets us get both rupture and SnD up within three GCDs ~60% of the time.
