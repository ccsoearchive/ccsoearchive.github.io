It's not too complicated, you're 255 hit rating over the spell hit cap. From 0-1332 hit rating, it's one of our strongest stats, but after 1332 it's our absolute worst (assumes 2/3 precision, caps are slightly different for 3/3 precision, see this post: http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/1658707015?page=1#5 ).

Crit is also our second worst stat, and the reforger got rid of 44 rating there. So basically getting over 350 more haste rating balanced everything out for a net gain. Reforge like it suggests and proceed to win. :)

Spell hit cap is 17% for raids, Mattyk. See post #5 of this thread:
