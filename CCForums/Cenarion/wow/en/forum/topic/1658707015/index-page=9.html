That's an extremely tight window to refresh SnD with envenom, I'd suggest practicing one/both of the rotations in the guide and seeing if they give you better results (one of them is simply switching rupture and envenom, compared to your opening rotation).

You shouldn't be reforging into expertise, and for heroics you don't need to reforge into hit rating either, as long as you've got 205 with 2/3 precision you're fine. Raiding is where the higher hit caps come into play, so you can concentrate on agility/mastery/haste right now.

Your talents are a mess, honestly. There's two builds in the guide that are viable.

If you have any specific questions based on the reading I can help more from there.
