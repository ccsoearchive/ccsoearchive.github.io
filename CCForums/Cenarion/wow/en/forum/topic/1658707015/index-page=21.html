Did you read anything in the guide you're posting on at all? :|

To elaborate, initiative just isn't worth it. It gives you an extra combo point when you use garrote, which you use a maximum of 2-3 times in a fight. Even in full BiS, that's worth about half as much as an equal number of talent points in precision.

Expose armor also isn't worth the effort. There are several other classes which do the same thing with less of a DPS loss than rogues do, and frankly you wouldn't even be benefiting much from it. Expose armor increases physical damage, while most damage done by assassination rogues is from poisons and counts as spell damage.

Pooling energy is also a recourse, not a requirement. If you're ready to use envenom and the buff from your last envenom is still active, you can wait a second or two for it to fall off. Always pooling to a specific energy level is usually more counterproductive than helpful.

You should also not use shiv except to remove an enrage effect on your target, if you have 4 combo points it's better to go ahead and use a finisher instead of mutilating again or using shiv. The exception is when you're using backstab on a target below 35%, then you should go for a full 5 combo points.

You should also get SnD up with 1 combo point, and refresh it with a 4-5 combo point envenom before it falls off (Cut to the Chase: Your Eviscerate and Envenom abilities have a 100% chance to refresh your Slice and Dice duration to its 5 combo point maximum).

  • Cloak of Shadows cooldown is now 2 minutes, up from 90 seconds.
