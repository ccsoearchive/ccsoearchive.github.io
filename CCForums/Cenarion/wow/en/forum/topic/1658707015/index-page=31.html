I just made the switch to Combat after raiding as Assassination for the entirety of Cataclysm. I got to the point where I was essentially skillcapped for Assassination and could not parse higher. I currently hold ranks as high as #2 on Hagara (10) and have been that high on a few other fights (Madness, for one) but have since dropped off. I started Combat this week and it's a little early for me to tell what the results will yield, but I tend to ignore spreadsheets and always have because the situations they generate for are 100% ideal circumstances that reality cant compare with.

I've noticed an obvious huge increase on anything cleaveable, but I actually parse lower on single target fights right now. Much lower on Zonozz, for instance. It could be the raid makeup or simple performance issues as I'm probably not using CD's properly to coincide with the insight buff, but if I dont see a considerable improvement then I'll be switching back, as I absolutely cannot stand how button mashy Combat is.
