Lol, that is curious. It's not like it didn't do its job though, it found the highest EP value for your reforgings and went with it. Getting the same results by not reforging to complementary pieces just proves that we're still a bit smarter than the programs we create... for now...

// edit //

Two things, though. You shouldn't use that reforger if you have 3/3 precision, since it's assuming you're using the cookie-cutter assassination spec, that uses 2/3 precision. In this case you should use http://shadowcraft.mmo-mumble.com/ to get a more thorough (and smarter) reforging plan.

Secondly, if you're not raiding yet you shouldn't use either one. The hit cap for raids is significantly higher than for heroic dungeons, and all automated reforging resources I know of are designed to go for the raiding cap. With 3/3 precision, you need -zero- hit rating to be capped for heroics.

Just reforge each piece individually until you need to worry about the raiding caps.

And if you are doing dungeons/raids with that character, it'd fix your spec a little bit. Deadly momentum is nice, but 2/2 murderous intent is practically imperative. You can leave a filler point in deadly momentum if you're still doing a lot of questing, or move it to deadly brew or deadened nerves.

3/3 precision is pretty overkill for assassination, too. The hit cap for heroics is 6% melee hit (which is higher than 6% spell hit, which you also need since it's the spell hit cap for heroics). 2/3 precision + 241 hit rating is more than enough, which lets you fill out 3/3 opportunity in the subtlety tree.
