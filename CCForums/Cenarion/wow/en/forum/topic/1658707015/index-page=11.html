Superb guide.

Re: Fluid Death vs. Tier Piece(s)

In my particular case, I opted for the Trinket first, since my guild is not quite ready to raid yet (next week).

I will take my chances that I'll pick up some Tier pieces by luck of the draw while running in a raid. In the meantime, my DPS picked up quite a bit when I equipped Fluid Death and Tia's Grace. When I looked at my in-game character stats, things can be a bit misleading as my overall projected dps dropped since I lost 285 Agi from a previously equipped trinket. However, the stacks from Fluid and Tia's reach 10 each so quickly (720 Agi total), that my dps improved quite a bit. Again, the shorter the fight, the less the improvement, but I estimate at least +150 dps on 85 mobs outside of instances, and quite a bit more than that on Heroic instance bosses (will test this evening for the first time). I love the fact that Fluid begins stacking when you use your thrown weapon as well! Awesome trinket.
