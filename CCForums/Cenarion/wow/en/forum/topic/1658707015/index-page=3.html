A couple things...

12/23/2010 11:45 PMPosted by Verelyse

Leather Specialization: If you are wearing leather items in all the applicable slots, you gain a +5% agility bonus. This makes many of the stated values for things like gems and enchants look strange, since a +40 agility gem is actually worth 109.2 Equivalence Points (40 × 2.6 × 1.05), instead of just 92 (40 × 2.6).


I would bet money that the Leather specialization bonus is already baked into the 2.6 EP weight of Agility. And thus you are inflating the agility portion of gear stat weights by 5%. I can't fathom a reason a rogue would ever have Leather Specialization inactive, so it seems silly that Aldriana (who posted the original stat weights) would require us to make that additional calculation. If you're not convinced I could try and get a response from the EJ crew.


Meta Gems:
    57.33 + damage: Relentless Earthsiege Diamond.*
    48.6 + damage: Chaotic Shadowspirit Diamond.*
    70.2: Fleet Shadowspirit Diamond. (thanks Aurotan).
    64: Thundering Skyflare Diamond.
    * Neither of these metas are worth using until their gem requirements are fixed.



How did you calculate the weight of the Thundering Skyflare Diamond? As I understand, the meta is a 6 second 480 haste proc with ~13.333...% uptime giving us an average of 64 haste rating, which would make it...
64 * (1.2) = 76.8 EP
So unless I'm missing something (reduced proc rate at 85?), I would think the TSD would come out ahead for assassination until the crit meta requirements are fixed.


Anyway, keep up the good work.
