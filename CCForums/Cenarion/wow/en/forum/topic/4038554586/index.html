Drakehide sighs at the notice as he walks by the posting board, near the Orgrimmar mailbox. Another preacher, flaunting a connection to the Light. He had never been fond of the Light, whatever it was, or however it worked - it made the Alliance's priests and paladins very, very difficult to kill. This was a great inconvenience, on a field of battle.

But...Drakehide felt like recently he had tried to address his most recent problem with every other solution, and things were escalating beyond his control. He needed help. Help that, perhaps, mortals could not provide. And so the son of Rukh penned an anonymous message, and dropped in inconspicuously in the message box.

Perhaps the troll would have the answers that Drakehide sought...

Brudda Zalmar...I'm sorry, is Brudda your first name, or a title? And if the latter, am I spelling it correctly...?

I am not a firm believer in the Light. Not by far. In fact, the Light is about as far away from my own personal beliefs as one can possibly get. I find recently, however, that I carry a heavy, deep burden...and if you possess some spiritual gift, some otherwordly grace, or a connection to the divine that I lack, I would ask your assistance, if you are willing to give it.

I find myself caught between a rock and a very hard place. I am, myself, upset by a slaving ring that has recently come to my attention. Some of my friends - friends within the Horde who have connections among the Kal'Dorei - are even more displeased by this, since some of the slaves in question are night elves being used as harem slaves . My companions are even now pursuing a...potentially dangerous, treasonous course of action. A course that could harm many people, destroy many lives, on both sides of this war.

I do not know how long I can keep this at bay, or how long I can prevent disaster. I agree with my friends' cause, and I agree that if the slaving ring cannot be stopped it should at least be addressed in a more ethical, decent fashion...but I cannot condone a course of action that could potentially bring harm to the Horde.

I am doing my utmost to establish a dialogue among the facilitators of this slave trade, and my friends, to see if a settlement that suits us all might be reached, before this turns into an open conflict, but...diplomacy is not my strongest suit. Unless it is diplomacy with an axe. And so, Brudda Zalmar, I come to you with two questions.

One: In my place, what would you do? How would you proceed? If it comes to the point where I must choose between my friends, and my family among the Horde...if one's mind and heart are telling them vastly different things, what should one choose? How should one choose, in these matters...?

Two: Would you pray for me, on my behalf? If you do possess a connection to the divine...I would appreciate it greatly if you would speak to them on my behalf. Right now, I feel like I need all the help I can get.

I thank you for your time. Aka'Magosh - spirits' blessings.
