Here's a neato fact about the guild name--

It was inspired by the work "Leviathan" written by Thomas Hobbes which in-part focuses on the world, and people, being innately selfish and chaotic. The only way to rise above this "state of nature" is to enter into a social contract with your peers-- basically an agreement of preservation. I don't kill you, you don't kill me. With the enforcer of this contract being the Monarch.

Therefore, the "Leviathan" is the body politic that forms from the collective who entered into the contract and the reigning Monarch becomes the "head" of the beast. Of course, in our guild the Leviathan is a many headed serpent!
