Last night the Leviathan pulled what little political strings they had left to take a gander at the "reclaimed" Lordaeron registry. Using this document they uncovered the burial grounds of the Blackmire family, the same Blackmire who had conspired with the traitor Tatris for unknown reasons.

The goal was simple: Use the remains of Blackmires wife to pull her spirit temporarily into the waking world and use it for information. Nothing ever goes exactly according to plan though.. The members of the Coalition had to break three seals Blackmire had laid on the grave before they could access the remains. To break the seals required them to enter into abstract realities to relive parts of Blackmires past. Surreal, to say the least.

In successfully breaking the seals the Leviathan was able to call the spirit to the physical plane. However, before anything could be gained the spirit demanded "permanence". The members then had a choice.. Offer one of their own as a vessel for this spirit, to share with their own mind. Or, betray the spirit and bind it to an object.

The members chose the former option and as a result the lucky volunteer to host the spirit now lies unconscious. Hopefully when Darren awakes the Coalition may get the answers they seek..

Full write up available here: http://www.leviathan-guild.com/#!new-campaign-catch-up/gj9f5
