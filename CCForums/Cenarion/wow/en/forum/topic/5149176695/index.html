Several of these notices have been posted around Orgrimmar, Thunder Bluff, Undercity, and Silvermoon City.

The Black Hand Society presents...

The Black Hand Society Dueling Tournament and Race

The Dueling Tournament and Race will occur on Saturday, June Ninth outside the Gurubashi Arena in Stranglethorn Vale. Five thousand gold pieces will be given away in prizes.

Please sign your name on the attached piece of paper with which events you would like to participate in by June Eighth for the Tournament.

Sign Ups

Dueling Tournament
Vectus
Saronys
Dreejin
Æthelgyth



OOC:

So, as you see we are having a dueling tournament! There will be gold prizes! A total of 5,000 gold will be distributed to the winners.

Please have your character sign up by having them give their signature or something similar on this thread so we know who is interested in participating. You are also welcome to just come and watch these events so long as you are not disruptive OOCly.

Rules for the Dueling Tourney

PVP gear is allowed.
It will be done round-robin style. Each participant will fight every other participant, and the person with the most wins will be the winner. If there is a tie, another duel will take place between those with the most wins.

See to it that this sum is added to the prize monies.

Smiling to himself as he affixed the note to the sack, he spoke aloud and to no one in particular, "Perhaps this will attract more interest. And perhaps the interested parties might actually prove to be a challenge..."
