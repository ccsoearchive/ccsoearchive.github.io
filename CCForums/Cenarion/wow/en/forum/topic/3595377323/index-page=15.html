I don't RP.

I think you'd have a hard time figuring this out in game, though. There are some clues like the lack of MRP/TRP, but not a lot else. Several of my characters have fairly detailed back stories, which I came up with while playing. My Forsaken warlock is rather anti-social, so it's actually in character for her to not speak to many people. My priest was raised on a farm in Westfall, and she's enormously overwhelmed in the big city of Stormwind, so her shyness is also in character. My dwarf paladin is oblivious. He's an archaeologist at heart, so he spends a lot of time trying to do just enough work to fund his next expedition. Chances are that if you speak to him he won't even notice, he's busy thinking about fossil fragments.

Hmm. Seems like just being around roleplayers has gotten me thinking about my characters a lot more than I ever did on a normal PvE server.
