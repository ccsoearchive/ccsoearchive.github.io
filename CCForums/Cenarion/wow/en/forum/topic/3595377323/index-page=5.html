Guys. I'm fairly sure NO one here is saying you're bad for rping, that rpers should gtfo, etc.

However, people ARE saying 'rp or gtfo'. That's the problem. People are feeling the need to defend their right to be on the server. Being told if you don't rp here you don't belong here is hurtful and cruel and there simply is no reason for it. There are other ways to handle non rpers, being welcoming and saying 'by the way this is a role play server, have you considered making your character fit into the world in character? might be fun! Let me help you!' or ANYTHING like that is welcoming and encouraging and may coax people into rping who hadn't intended to in the first place. And if it's someone who used to rp? But got burnt out for whatever reason? Maybe if you were just friendly with them you MIGHT coax them back into trying out rp again with you.

Honestly I'm kind of getting the feeling from several posters here that they would quite cheerfully wish me to gtfo off of this server since I'm not ic all the time, if at all anymore. That since I personally don't fit their requirements for being on this server I'm not welcome. The attitudes that have been coming out are very hostile and intolerant and do our server a disservice.

There are countless reasons why someone would roll on an rp server. Don't make people have to jump through hoops and justify why they should be allowed to be on this server.
