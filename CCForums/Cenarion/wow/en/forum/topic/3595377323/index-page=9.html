As someone who enjoys all aspects of the game (PvE, RP, PvP) but who enjoys RP the most and spends 80% of her in-game time on WRA, I must say that it's a little disheartening to see the vitriol and bile directed at the non-RPers.

If anything is going to kill this realm's RP, it is the attitudes of those on both sides of this debate. The RPers who vilify and blame non-RPers for all of the server's ills are no better than the non-RPers who grief and troll the many who do RP.

This server, compared to the other RP realms I tried before it, has a distinct lack of the griefers and trolls. The non-RPers here tend to be just as polite and well-written as any of the RP crowd, and even in the 9 months I've been on the realm, I have seen several handfuls of those non-RP folks become interested in RP and start doing it themselves. If they had encountered this "RP or GTFO" attitude presented by several of the people in this thread, WRA might not have gained those handfuls of well-written and interesting RPers.

Instead of getting huffy and proclaiming that people MUST RP or GTFO in order to preserve YOUR IMMERSION, try taking the polite approach. You catch more flies with honey, as they say. That also goes for the non-RPers!

As for those RPers who get huffy over PvE/PvP-minded folks? Get over yourselves. I am 6/7 heroic 25-man Firelands, yet I am a fully dedicated roleplayer who takes lore and character concepts/interactions quite maturely and seriously. RP and PvE/PvP are not mutually exclusive, and denigrating those of us who DO raid or PvP competitively will do nothing to make us want to RP with you when we come across you in-game.
