Buffs
1) Shifting on it's own GCD
I want this for mostly a quality of life change. Removing the GCD tax on our utility may allow us to do less damage and still remain competitive in PvP.

2) Berserk on a 1 minute cooldown, 10 second duration.
I really dislike dps cooldowns which are longer than 2 minutes.

3) Predator's Swiftness Proc: Consumed to apply 3 stacks of lifebloom.
We need more appealing options to spend our PS proc on. We also suck at healing. Maybe this would help.

4) Nurturing Instinct: Your melee critical strike rating is applied to healing.
That's how bad I think our healing is. I think our survival has been pretty poor since the root change.

5) Shifting into cheetah removes snares and grants 2 seconds of snare immunity.
I really liked this mechanic back in Vanilla. I also enjoyed the hit and run / utility play style of BC.

Nerfs
1) General shape shifting no longer removes snares.
I think *IF* we were to get shifting on it it's own GCD, breaking snares would no longer have a GCD attack penalty. Power shifting macros could make us essentially immune to snares while being on the offensive.

2) I think feral sustained dps would have to be cut by about 10% in PvP. In order to compensate PvE: I would probably improve haste scaling significantly (it's a bad pvp stat), improve savage roar, reduce the duration on savage roar (to make it harder for feral pvp-ers to afford).
