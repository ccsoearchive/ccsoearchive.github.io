Hi-ho folks of Cenarion Circle! Turwinkle and I would like to thank everyone here on the Cenarion Circle forums for having us! We have enjoyed sharing our adventures through Azeroth with you as well as the fun folks and guilds we have met along the way! We have offered to come and speak with you folks and your guilds as well. We offered to share your wonderful RP storylines and creations with the rest of the WoW community as we have done for WRA, Moon Guard, ED and several other RP servers. We have enjoyed sharing all these wonderful RP concepts and ideas that have come from the RP community within this great world Blizzard has provided for the RPers here!

Unfortunately, no one has come forward wanting to share stories or ideas. Indeed, with the last few weeks we have had our threads taken down for some reason. Perhaps because the series has yet to include any folks from this realm. Perhaps someone here does not like to see what other folks are doing within the game.

So with this in mind, Turwinkle and I will no longer "bothering" the Cenarion Circle forums with our updates. Turwinkle and I would love to still have the opportunity to come and interview any of your guilds or individual's or highlight any of your RP events. If you or your guild would be interested in being highlighted for the rest of the WoW community to see, feel free to send us word! We can be found on the forums mentioned above! We would be more than happy to CRZ over and talk with you folks! :D

With that, Thank you folks for putting up with us for so long! May all your RP continue to be fun and we will be on the look out for you folks on WRA if you are crossed over! *Turwinkle bows humbly*
