[H-RP: Social event] Love is in the Air.

Add a reply

Ah, Love is in the Air: sweet flowers, perfume, and sharing gifts with loved ones!

Love is in the Air is an celebration of love and friendship during which we share gifts and candy, and the air is filled with perfume and cologne. This year, why not celebrate love and friendship with some speed dating, gifting cake with a side order of compliments, and sending sweet nothings with the help of couriers!

--------------------------------------------------------------------------

Event Details:

Hosting:Twisted Notions
With special thanks to Tehluci, Ox, Howl, and Ven.
Guilds/Individuals are absolutely welcomed to contact us for more information.

When: Tuesday, February 16th at 6:00 pm (Server Time).

Where: Eversong (Coord 42.32,29.05)
http://twistednotionswra.tumblr.com/image/139129528676

Event Features:

Secret Admirer: Throughout the event, there will be designated <SA> Couriers. They function as your anonymous letter bearers. Couriers can be whispered with a message and who they should deliver that message to. This will all happen in whisper, so only you, the courier, and the sendee will know. :) Remember, since it's anonymous, it will only work one way, so you won't get to see the sendee's reaction!

Our Wedding Package: Experience the joy of a full on wedding surrounded by new found friends and administered by our very own Pandaren priest TheRev. For 500g, you will receive cake to share with friends, have your attire accommodated by our fine tailor Tehluci, share vows (which you can customize if you'd like to make it extra special), receive a companion to commemorate your event, and you both will receive a Certificate from the priest in the mail. For customization of the wedding please contact Tehluci or Erimond a day in advance.

Your package includes:
10 Minute Ceremony
A Cake to share with friends
Wedding Dress and Tuxedo Set
A companion pet (child) to share
Mailed Certificate from The Rev

Speed Dating: Exactly how it sounds! Chat up or make new friends in a fast paced conversation event, where partners are switched every three minutes, or until one of you say pass.

Singles Auction: Sign yourself up to be raffled off to the highest bidder!
Strip down to your skivvies, dance and auction your arm off, for the evening (rest of the event), to the highest bidder. In this event you sign yourself up, OR your guild signs you up. The highest bidder wins your attention for the rest of the night's events!

We will also be hosting a PvP and Mog contest, so come prepared to impress! Rewards, raffles, scandal, and good fun all around!

Prizes for this event (in various amounts not listed):
[Hexweave Bag] [Void-Shrouded Satchel] [Coalfist Gronnling] [Lovely Black Dress][Tuxedo set][Flask/Pot Set][Random Companions]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------

Get up to date info on events or promote your own on WrA's Discord channel: https://discord.gg/0oC0BbdAAwJ1zjXs

And get the most for you and your guild with the WorldRP channel in WoW.
