How do you do Yo'sahj as unholy? There is a ton of switching and it hurts alot.

If we do go for an aoe build and glyph setup, do we use frost presence? How do we deal with the fact that the faceless adds do not clump instantly, and that by the time the last add closes in the first one is dead. How do you maximize AOE dps?


For normal, you could get away with just staying on top of the boss the whole time. We had a handful of people who would stay in because they had longer transit times than other classes or their dps took a dive if they were constantly moving. On either normal or hardmode, the pet's movement can be an issue too. If you're required to go to the oozes, the standard action is for the pet to be hitting Yor'sahj but then he realizes you're attacking something else so he rushes away from the boss at about the time that the ooze is dead. He can effectively stay on Yor'sahj or, if DPS is tight, have him go with you to the ooze from the start. The less he's divided, the better.

For AoE, it sounds as if the adds are dying rather quickly. If they're dropping at odd intervals, you might as well stay in Unholy presence and keep whatever RP you've accumulated. The goal is just to tag as many as possible; you can delay your DnD & Outbreak until the majority of them have collected together and blood boil as you are able. That part of the fight does feel a little 'whack a mole' since they're bumbling around separately. So long as you get hits in, your pet is getting SI/DTs, and the adds are exterminated quickly, you've done your job.

03/27/2012 09:33 PMPosted by Sábér
PS3: what are all you guys doing to get 2600 haste? heroic kneebreaker, heroic backbreaker, heroic rockhide, heroic glove tier, cloak, neck .... none are giving haste. Assuming 2x Str trinkets, is the 2600 haste a realistic goal until you are totally decked? Has anyone tried out Heroic Ataraxis and found it better/worse than normal gurth?


2600 Haste is a theoretical number which we've set as a cap beyond which the value of Haste for Unholy severely diminishes. I am told that the number was reachable in a mixture of heroic Firelands and DS gear, and so might have been a concern when people were still making the transition from Tier 12 to Tier 13. That's clearly no longer the case, of course. 2600 is simply a Haste number you will never have to worry about, not even in full Tier 13 BiS.
Also, normal Gurthalak trumps heroic Ataraxis. It's just that good.

03/27/2012 09:33 PMPosted by Sábér
PS5: Does unholy frenzy make your diseases tick faster? Do diseases snapshot your haste, or do they update based on your haste?


Haste effects do not affect diseases. Diseases do not interact with Haste whatsoever on Live.
