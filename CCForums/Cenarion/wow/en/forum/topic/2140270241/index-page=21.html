I stopped reading this guide when the OP said to avoid Glyph of Outbreak. At the cost of 1 1/4 of a death coil and a potential loss of DT uptime, you can multi dot. Deathcoil in my current gear, while good, does not do NEARLY the damage of two dots with 30 sec uptime. Situations I've found this glyph extremely useful so far:
Will of the emperor: in between each devestating combo, run out 5 yards (or if youre really pro do it during a DC) and outbreak the other boss. if you cant see how this is a massive dps increase, you should respec frost or reroll warrior.
Elegon: Multidotting the add if you're not assigned to directly dps it.
Garalon: Dotting the far legs if you're assigned to stay on one side of the boss.
Sha of Fear: Multi dotting when adds are out of pestilance range (and reapplying dots on boss when you're out of range)

I won't say its great on every fight, its not, but when used properly in skilled hands, it is extremely effective. OP I would petition for you to consider editing that portion of your guide with this information.

Death Siphon costs a death rune, so you still have to spend 2/6 of your runes no matter what on SS because reaping doesn't convert Unholy runes into death runes. Scourge Strike's mastery scaling is pretty bad to a good portion of it being physical damage. So lets say you have 30% mastery, well your increasing the damage of ~40% of your scourge strike's damage by 30%. Now lets do the math: 0.30x0.40=0.12 or 12% damage increase on scourge strike from 30% mastery which is 18000 Mastery - Base Mastery (30x600, 600 mastery = 1%). On top of that Festering strike, auto attacks, Timmy, and Army dont scale with mastery. Every spell we have can crit.
Haste is just plain too good for Dks
-Faster Rune Regen
-More Autoattacks = More Sudden Doom = Faster/Better Uptime DT and more effective tier 5 talents
-All pets scale with haste aswell

Death Siphon is terrible for dps, death pact costs nothing and heals nearly 70% of your HP.
