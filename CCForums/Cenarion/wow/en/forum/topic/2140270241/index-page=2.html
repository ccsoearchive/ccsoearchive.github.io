Single Target Priority

Diseases > Dark Transformation > Death and Decay/Scourge Strike
> Festering Strike > Death Coil (Sudden Doom)
> Death and Decay/Scourge Strike/Festering Strike > Horn of Winter


Just looks a little ambiguous here, but I think the important distinction here is wanting to keep at least one of every rune type on cooldown at all times so as to not waste rune regen time. As well as making sure to not RP cap for deathcoils.

I'm not one for elegant wording or making it look pretty, but perhaps something like
Diseases > Dark Transformation > Death and Decay/Scourge strike (UU) > Festering Strike (BB FF) > Death Coil (Sudden Doom or RP cap) > Death and Decay/Scourge Strike > Festering Strike > Death coil > Horn of winter

I know its a small difference, but it can really add up over the fight
