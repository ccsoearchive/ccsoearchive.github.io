The sky will be falling to some. Frost wins on something like raid bots more by overall participation than actual performance. It's not a perfect system and each fight and even raid design changes things. But it's too easy to get into a sky is falling mindset. The only real difference is any fights with mechanics that are more anti melee hurts Frost slightly less. Even the ability to howling blast while running out alone lets more damage be logged than dot tics alone unless the Unholy is lucky enough to have Timmy grown for that run out still damaging the mob. Unfortunately balancing specs for every single fight mechanic is hard to measure.

On top of that the logs are all subject to human error. An unholy who does not take advantage of a few extra potential soul reapers alone will lose to the frost. It's definitely not perfect but there is worse options than to use a Unholy DK. Just many Unholy DKs are not as geared as the frost and the min/max guilds would go consistent dps overall higher peak potential usually.
