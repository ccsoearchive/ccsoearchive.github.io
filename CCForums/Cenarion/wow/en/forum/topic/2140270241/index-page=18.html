In comparing specs, Unholy's damage comes from sources which are less affected by dodges and parries (and interestingly, our pets inherit our hit to calculate their expertise). It is important to know that we are still affected by expertise for our swings. It isn't a useless stat, but it's not one we tell people to stack because the others (crit, haste and so on) are so much better for us. So, if you have a few points of expertise, it's doing you some good just not as much another stat might. We will tend to have some low values left in expertise after our reforging is optimized and that's perfectly fine and normal.

Sure! In Hardmode, you'll see that the BiS head item changes from the Visage of Petrification (a non-set piece) to our Necrotic helm (set piece). You can therefore equip Backbreakers in HM BiS without losing your 4pc set bonus. The same can't be said for the Pre-Hardmode where we still use Visage. While Backbreakers would individually be an upgrade of ~7-8 dps, the DPS loss of breaking the set would still make it a net loss. Now, if someone hadn't achieved their set bonuses and were comparing the items 1:1, then Backbreaker would edge slightly by that small amount. Make sense?
