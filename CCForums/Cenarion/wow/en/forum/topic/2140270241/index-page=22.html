Hey there Ely. Now I do not know what play style you use for you off spec but if you are playing Unholy right now your main stat you want is Strength obviosly so that is what you want primarily for Gemming.
As far as secondary stats for Frost and Unholy

Haste increasing the speed at which Runes regenerate.

This is the primary thing that haste dose for a DK. It dose reduce GCD but the fact is the 2 are both effecting the CD of ability use. The more haste you have the more often your runes are available for you to use abilities like Obliterate and Festering Strike. Such abilities give you RP to then use Frost strike for Frost DKs and Death Coil for Unholy DKs. Frost Presence incresses the flat amount of RP generated by abilities while Unholy increases the speed at which Runes regenerate ( it dose not however reduce GCD as this is not a Haste buff).

This means that after an amount of haste it is plausible that your runes may regen fast enough that you could be waiting on GCD. You would however need ALOT of haste to do that.

As far as unholy goes haste is your go to secondary stat so that you can put out more Death coils and get more Dark Transformations.

As far as Frost goes you DW is Mastery as you are doing lots of Frost damage and Frozen Heart increases that. Two handed needs that haste buff as they use only one weapon giving them less procs of Killing Machine.
