However I've noticed there is a trend among most top unholy players - they start reforging haste at ~2100 haste and go mastery.


This is neither accurate, nor optimal by any means.
Lets use a top-ranking Unholy Death Knight and their gear/reforging as an example

Here's Lamperouqe (EU) of Stormreaver:
http://eu.battle.net/wow/en/character/stormreaver/Lamperouqe/advanced

He's in full BiS gear for Unholy, barring the upgraded version of the Spine shoulders. He's reforged towards the Hit cap and then almost exclusively goes for Haste.

The reason you're likely seeing lower haste numbers in these player's gear is because full BiS Tier 13 gear has less Haste on it than BiS Tier 12 did.
Don't mistake that for them "reforging away" from Haste.

The softcap for Haste where its value would diminish enough for Mastery or Crit to surpass it is not attainable for this tier of content (barring the effects of Heroism or UF, of course, which shouldn't factor in), and hence isn't of any real concern to us.

I found for myself that 3/3 in runic power mastery helped alot with forcing fast timmy transforms. You can basically hoard RP while timmy is up, and spam coils the instant timmy is down. I actually cannibalized butchery for it, but still found that it's been helping me do more damage.


Absolutely and utterly unnecessary in most cases. 1/3 in RPM is all that is needed in order to ensure you're capable of spamming 3 Death Coils with a full RP bar. The additional 2 points in the talent do not allow for enough extra RP for an extra Death Coil, and hence have diminished value.

If you insist on running with 3/3 in RPM, sacrifice Improved Blood Tap or AMZ/1 point in Magic Suppression. In a purely single-target scenario, you could also sacrifice Contagion, though Ultraxion is the only fight in the entire tier where this is doable.

But in any case, if you had 28 rp left over after 3 dumps, wouldn't you be able to pump out a 4th coil after 1 SS? You wouldn't be able to do this with 1 SS if you were left at 8 rp.


You could, but that isn't what I'm getting at- we're considering the relative DPS gain per talent point, versus the alternatives. 1/3 RPM for Unholy is so strong because it's a guaranteed additional Death Coil with AMS soaking or hoarding. That means being able to stack up Shadow Infusion all the more quickly when one needs to.
The remaining 2 points simply don't hold as much DPS value as, say, Improved Blood Tap for single target. If you must attain 3/3 in RPM, sacrifice Contagion to do so.

You'll note, of course, that the only fights this really has any worth for is Ultraxion and Morchok. Even for Zon'ozz, assuming you're working on the heroic version of the encounter, Pestilence plays a role during the add phase. Personally, I'd suggest leaving Unholy DKs on the boss (along with rogues and warriors) and letting them cleave onto one of the claws.
