Proper AMS usage is one of the fun parts about being a DK in general. Seeing as Unholy actually gets a DPS increasing from using it, I think being as thorough as you can with it is probably a good idea.

As for Doc's, it tracks runes, RP, procs (Sudden Doom for UH), Runic Corruption, Shadow Infusion stacks, Dark Transformation duration, and diseases. It's visually unobtrusive and you can turn each individual component on or off. It's the best Rune mod I've ever used. I'd recommend you give it a whirl for a day just to see what it offers.
