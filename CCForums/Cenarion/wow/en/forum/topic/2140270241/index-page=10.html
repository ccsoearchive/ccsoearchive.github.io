there's something i discovered about diseases today. the damage of diseases varies depending on what our attack power is the moment they are applied to the target, however, they do not increase/decrease in the middle of it's duration regardless of whether our attack power changes. just when it is applied (it dosen't change if it's refreshed by festering strike either).

I figured that perhaps this little bit of information is something worthy of being noted in your guide, since diseases tend to last about 60-90 seconds (provided we constantly use festering strike on that target) before they need to be reapplied again, and if we were to use any abilities/potions/trinkets that increase our attack power, the dps gain would be carried on through our diseases well after the AP increase has expired.

edit: i'm also wondering if this is the reason unholy dks have a slow ramp up time in their dps.
