I'm not sure if this has been mentioned yet, but as of yesterday (8/30/2012), many of the addons listed in the guide are out of date and may or may not have issues. DDR's author, in particular, doesn't even have an account at the moment, so it isn't likely that it's going to be updated any time soon. For what it's worth, here's a quick list of a few updated rune mods:

RuneHUD: http://www.curse.com/addons/wow/runehud
Very simple set of moveable runes, uses default Blizzard skin. Doesn't appear to have RP bar or timers.

Vex Runes: http://www.curse.com/addons/wow/vexrunes
Similar to RuneHUD, but with DDR-style bars with countdown support. No RP bar or timers here, either.

Umber Runes: http://www.curse.com/addons/wow/umberrunes
Features Blizzard skin runes, RP bar, and disease timer. Can be configured to show basic target information.

mRunes (my current choice): http://www.curse.com/addons/wow/mrunes
Full-featured Death Knight mod, uses bars for everything. Modular with some customization options. I use this for runes/RP, but let my ElvUI handle my class timers.
