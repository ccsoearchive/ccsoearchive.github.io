Let's use your 2k example. Suppose R1/gladiator players are reduced to 2k MMR (legitimate or not). This would affect people who are 2k to begin with, because all of a sudden they're playing people much better than they are. Their rating would drop. However, eventually the R1/gladiator players will build up their MMR from 2k back up to x, where x is a gladiator-range value. What's the harm in that?

To clarify, if x happened to be 1500, the same thing would occur (1500 players would be playing 2500 players etc.), except the system will take longer to normalize simply because high-end players have to climb a greater distance.

The only difference between this scenario and a reset to 1500 is that one affects everyone, while the other only affects the top 2-3% only (only the top 2-3% is concerned with things like MMR exploit).
