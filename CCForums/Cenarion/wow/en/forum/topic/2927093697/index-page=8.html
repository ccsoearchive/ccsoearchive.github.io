For whatever my opinion is worth (which is probably not much), I don't like the idea of the reset.

I just started doing arenas towards the end of last season when I gave up raiding. in PvE I did a server first vashj kill, a faction first kael kill, most of wrath, and most of Cata so far. I decided I wanted a change and arena was that change. It was vastly different and a whole new style of play. With the new game at my hands, I discovered something: I'm terrible at PvP.

Don't get me wrong, I'm getting better. But that is a slow process which will take time I don't have. I currently sit just below 1500 and my goal is to break that this upcoming week. It's hard for me and my team, even though we have a good comp. We just started arenas and are all learning together. It took the entirety of the 4(?) weeks that we've been in the season to push our ranking this far.

I have two months of this game left, because after that I leave for the military. Since my time is limited, I'd like to spend the first month of that trying to hit 1700, instead of having to push back to 1500 from scratch. I don't think all the ladder should be reset. I do like the idea of the ladder being reset up to a certain rating, so that anyone above x rating will drop to x.

If it was, say, 2199, then the exploiters will be forced to play more games and will lose, thereby losing their unearned ranking. Those who deserve 2200+ will surely get there again. I don't think the solution is to punish those on the lower ratings who might not be as skilled, such as myself.
