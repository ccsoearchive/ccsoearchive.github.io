I'm not sure if this has been posted already, but with 5.0, it seems that Misdirection has been removed from the GCD. You can now macro it to your abilities, which is amazing for soloing old content!!

Here's a macro I have for Chimera Shot:

#showtooltip Chimera Shot
/cast [target=focus, help] Misdirection; [help] Misdirection; [target=pet, exists] Misdirection
/cast Chimera Shot
/run UIErrorsFrame:Clear()

This ability will cast Misdirection to your focus target, if one exists, your target, if one exists, or fall back on your pet if neither of those conditions are true. It will then shoot Chimera Shot. This macro can be used for any ability. Just replace "Chimera Shot" with the other shot or ability.
