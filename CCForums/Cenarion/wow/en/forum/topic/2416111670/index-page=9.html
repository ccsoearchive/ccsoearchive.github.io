Hello everyone,
My first attempt at macro writing not sure if anyone can you them but I created 4 macros that have a good rotation, I was able to average 1K DPS at my level so it might scale you pretty nice. The key is that these can be hot keyed and then each tap is a new shot that follow the last in damage and ability.

Beast Master Regular battle (average attacks for working a room)
#showtooltip
/castsequence reset=5 Arcane Shot, Serpent Sting, Concussive Shot, Dire Beast, Bestial Wrath, Intimidation, Kill Command, Arcane Shot, Readiness, Dire Beast, Bestial Wrath,Serpent Sting, Arcane Shot, Concussive Shot, Arcane Shot, Steady Shot

Beast Master Heavy (Boss fight)
#showtooltip
/castsequence reset=5 Arcane Shot, Serpent Sting, Concussive Shot, Dire Beast, Bestial Wrath, Intimidation, Arcane Shot, Kill Command, Readiness,,Dire Beast, Bestial Wrath,Serpent Sting, Arcane Shot, Concussive Shot, Arcane Shot, Mend Pet

Marksman Regular
#showtooltip
/castsequence reset=5 Arcane Shot, Serpent Sting, Concussive Shot, Dire Beast, Rapid Fire, Aimed Shot, Chimera Shot, Steady Shot, Steady Shot, Aimed Shot, Chimera Shot, Arcane Shot, Steady Shot, Aimed Shot, Steady Shot, Arcane Shot, Mend Pet

Markman Heavy (very focus heavy so mangement is key)
#showtooltip /castsequence reset=5 Arcane Shot, Serpent Sting, Concussive Shot, Dire Beast, Aimed Shot, Chimera Shot, Rapid Fire, Steady Shot, Steady Shot, Aimed Shot, Chimera Shot, Readiness, Rapid Fire, Dire Beast, Chimera Shot, Aimed Shot, Steady Shot

The reset actually only starts counting seconds when you stop calling on the macro. If you keep calling the macro, it continues to the next item in the sequence regardless of how much time it takes to go through the whole sequence. I just found this out from testing on dummies. /use Bestial Wrath
/use Rabid
/use Lynx Rush

And yes, /use and /cast are interchangeable for abilities. If Rabid isn't working, verify that your pet is in fact ferocity specced, and that it isn't already on cooldown when hitting the macro (due to autocasting).

The point of the macros is to make things easier that would otherwise take several key presses, eg my pvp burst macro. It is not to make them play the game for you.

Any cast sequence macro will end up getting broken or delaying abilities by a few secs even if you do make it work. That's why I dont put any in the guide or even try to see if I can make the ones posted by other people work. It will always be a dps loss or bad for pvp.

The only reason to use castsequences (and im not even trolling here) is if you are somehow impaired and you physically cannot press the buttons fast enough or have enough reach etc, but it will be a suboptimal way to play the game because it was not designed with this in mind.
