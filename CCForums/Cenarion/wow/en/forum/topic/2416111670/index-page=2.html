It is possible to create much better Aspect Dancing macros if you have two free action bars you can dedicate to tracking your current aspect (so that you only cast the aspect if it is not already up). Set up action bars 1 and 2 to be completely identical, and macro your shots like this:

#showtooltip Arcane Shot
/cast [noactionbar:1] !Aspect of the Hawk
/changeactionbar 1
/cast [actionbar:1] Arcane Shot

#showtooltip Steady Shot
/cast [noactionbar:2] !Aspect of the Fox
/changeactionbar 2
/cast [actionbar:2] Steady Shot

This may not work with some action bar mods.

Ha, everyone comments on high 80+ characters, then the low 66 comes to give advice xP -feels small-

Searching online, I found some pretty simple/good macros that I love to use now, good for PvP and PvE. I can't recall the website that I got it from so I apologize for not giving credit where credit is due . T.T

/cast [@mouseover, harm] [harm] Tranquilizing Shot

Great for PvP! For removing those player buffs without having to constantly click on a person; simply hover over the player and button mash to your heart's content. ^^ Use especially on DKs who know what they're doing, Warriors and Rogues...friggin Rogues...

/targetenemy
/cast Hunter's Mark
/petfollow
/petattack

This targets the nearest enemy and makes your pet immediately follow to attack, not to mention adds the Hunter's Mark (which I often forget to do). A simple T button afterwards will have you automatically attacking as well. However, I find this most useful for PvP, for when a Rouge goes stealth and you can't see them; simply spamming the button will target them eventually and save you from being stunned. ;)

And if you can't tell, I'm not fond of Rogues...>.>+

#showtooltip Disengage
/cast Wing Clip
/cast Disengage

I think that one speaks on its own. As always, be careful though; Disengage is ideal for Hunters to make a quick escape, but using it in the wrong area can often do more harm than good. Take it from someone who was winning in a PvP fight, who then Disengaged off of a cliff and got sniped...Not cool...not cool.

Thanks to everyone who contributes to Macros/read them, as a newbie and a prime Hunter Roller, I need all the help I can get! ^^V

Also, for the MD Macro I suggest using [raid] rather than [help], so even if your focus isn't in party it doesn't return an error.

Here's what I use for MD

#showtooltip Misdirection
/cancelaura Misdirection
/cast [@focus, raid][@mouseover, raid][@pet, nodead][]Misdirection
/script UIErrorsFrame:Clear();
/stopmacro [@focus, nohelp]
/e Kildon has cast Misdirection on %f! 30 second CD.

It lets me hit the same button to both cast and cancel it (if it will end before we pull or I cast on the wrong target), casts on focus, then on my mouseover, then on my pet, then my target and if my focus is a friendly target it announces who MD was cast on in emote chat.
