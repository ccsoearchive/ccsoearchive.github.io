Didn't make this, but I thought it'd help everybody if they knew what it was. Saved my life SO many times, and has given me a great many free kills in PvP when i'm down on focus.
#showtooltip Scatter Shot
/castsequence [@mouseover,harm][harm] reset=3 Scatter Shot, Trap Launcher, !Freezing Trap
/script UIErrorsFrame:Clear()
You have to tap this three times for it to fully work, so it's best to bind it on to an easily accessable key.

-
What it does:

Casts a scattershot at the enemy, disorientating them. The second click activates trap launcher, and the third actives freezing trap, which can then immediately be placed on an enemy.

As the enemy is frozen, you are free to regenerate focus, hunter mark, back off to maximum distance, aimed shot, concussive shot, chimera shot, basically anything to suit your spec.

idk if anyone will actually read this now but since fox was introduced for the aspect dancing ive been using the following macros

#showtooltip Steady Shot
/startattack
/cast [no modifier] !Aspect of the Hawk
/cast [modifier] !Aspect of the Fox
/cast Steady Shot

^ is for pve. most of the time you are going to be in hawk, for those few fights where you cant stutter step or need to move the whole time just hold down a mod and youre good. just make sure its not shift.

#showtooltip Steady Shot
/startattack
/cast [no modifier] !Aspect of the Fox
/cast [modifier] !Aspect of the Hawk
/cast Steady Shot

^ is for pvp. same as before, but when you can get a few hard casted steady shots off just hold down a mod and youre good.

idk if other people like the idea or have tried it but removes the need for 2 separate macros for steady shots.

and then for pvp and pve i make sure that the insta-cast shots are all in hawk so incorporate hawk into those shots and winning.
