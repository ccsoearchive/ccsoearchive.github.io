Holy crap. I've just added a new section in post #3 outlining the changes to come in the new expansion. They will be *huge*.

==Rogues in the Next Expansion, Mists of Pandaria==
  • Rogues are one of the playable classes for the new Pandaren race (read: PANDA NINJAS).

  • Thanks to the monk class, many more fist weapons will be introduced into the game.

  • The monk class will be leather-wearing and will include a melee DPS spec, so expect more competition for gear.

  • Complete revamp of the talent-tree system; rogues will have a single talent tree (instead of three); every 15 levels will unlock a choice of three new talents, of which you can pick one. Talents can be changed like glyphs.

  • You'll still have three specs to choose from; you make that choice at level 10, just like you do now. Your spec choice will determine which additional spec-specific abilities unlock for you as you level.


I bit the bullet and bought the livestream from BlizzCon; this is all stuff that Ghostcrawler revealed on stage just minutes ago. They'll be doing a more detailed runthrough of rogue class changes in an hour or two; as soon as I have time I'll update this thread (or create a new one) with highlights from that discussion.

OK, you win, Kromos. :)

This. Changes. Everything.

  • Level 15 talent choices: Nightstalker; Subterfuge (3 second stealth break delay); Shadow Focus (no energy cost while stealthed)

  • Level 30 talent choices: Deadly Throw; Nerve Strike (KS/CS briefly reduces target damage); Combat Readiness

  • Level 45 talent choices: Cheat Death; Leeching Poison (heals you); Improved Recuperate

  • Level 60 talent choices: Preparation; Shadowstep; Burst of Speed (like Sprint, but costs energy and has no CD)

  • Level 75 talent choices: Deadly Brew; Paralytic Poison (stacks resulting in stun); Dirty Tricks (Gouge/Blind won't break from DoTs)

  • Level 90 talent choices: Killing Spree; Shadow Dance (now allows multi-teleport while active); Vendetta (now provides a ranged attack while active)
