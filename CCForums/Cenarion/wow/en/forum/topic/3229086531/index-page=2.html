Updated this sucker with the most important takeaway notes from the official Blizz blog that was just posted on our upcoming legendary weapons. They've clarified a whole mess of stuff about what the daggers actually do, what those crazy wings are all about, and what you have to do to get them (those of you who guessed "kill Deathwing" win a cookie).

Blog is at: http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/3279039361

Updated "legendary" section in this thread now reads:

The Legendary Weapons: Fangs of the Father
  • Set of daggers, one main-hand, one off-hand.
  • Only rogues can get them. They will be our precious(es).
  • Will be usable by all specs. (“We have a little tweak in there so that Combat Rogues will be able to Sinister Strike with the daggers without it being a big DPS loss.”)
  • On-use effect: Unfurls wings that can be used to slow decent (a la priest's Levitate or mage's Slow Fall).
  • Damage effect: Stacking agility bonus; at maximum effect, abilities temporarily cost 0 combo points and finishing moves temporarily do more damage.
  • How you get them: Long quest chain, which includes the killing of most/all Dragon Soul raid bosses (including Deathwing). You get the daggers early in the chain, but they become progressively more powerful as you move along the chain.
