/sigh.... What exactly is the reason for this? Why can't we have off-hand mace/axe/swords like we did before Cataclysm?


Loot tables, mainly. One of Blizzard's goals of Cataclysm was to trim out a lot of fat. Misc spells, abilities, loot, etc. If only one spec of one class (which happens to also be the least played class) finds a use out of a 1.8/1.4 speed Axe/Sword/Etc, then it's not the best use of a loot slot. Of course, there's a number of alternatives to the simple loot tables we have atm, but none that are likely to be implemented.
