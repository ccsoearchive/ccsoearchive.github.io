On a less silly note, there's the matter of where Malfurion is following Darkshore questing. Though I need to see this for myself (and have rolled up a low level Worgen Druid to do so), according to Wowpedia Malfurion learns that the Twilight's Hammer activity in Darkshore (up to and including the elements literally tearing the region apart) was intended by Azshara to keep him from returning to Hyjal and stopping their activity there.

This leads me to believe that the low level Ashenvale questing and high level Hyjal questing actually happen, if not simultaneously, then very close together (chronologically speaking). In other words, Malfurion couldn't help to deal with the Horde invasion of Ashenvale because he can't be in two places at the same time. The Twilight's Hammer cult is a higher priority than the Horde, at least during Cata.

That may just be speculation on my part, though. It'd help if there was some sort of chart you could reference for when things happened.
