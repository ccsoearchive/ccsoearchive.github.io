With the release of MoP being a mere two months away, I've been bombarded with inquiries regarding if we here at Warcraftier are selling any sort of DS services. I figured I'd address this topic here.

We do not have a price list since we don't typically sell raid services. However, we are willing to work things out on an individual basis. Everyone seems to want different things and have different needs and requirements, and we can all work together to see if we can meet those points. Thus far we've been approached by various individuals wanting different things ranging from pre-MoP Heroic kills to Life-Binder's Handmaiden (which is going to go from 100% current Heroic drop rate down to sub-1% drop rate post MoP release).

So, consider this post "officializing" that if you want a certain DS service, talk to me in game and we can see if something can be arranged. :D
