First I'd like to state, I think Hunter's are pretty balanced; Hunters shouldn't be brought up to the stronger dps classes, but those slightly overpowered/stronger dps classes should be toned down a notch rather than beg for Hunters to be too strong.

Hunter's are the overall weakest class in arena but not by a drastic amount, they're still capable of succeeding with the right players/teams, they don't need massive buffs that will make them faceroll.

Here's a list of balance/quality of life suggestions to possibly make them slightly stronger in arena (without making them faceroll at all, I love the hunter skillcap/play style right now), and to perhaps eliminate some pointless, frustrating things that have no logical reason to be in the game.

I'd like to add that none of these changes would affect PvE whatsoever and are all very minor changes in general.

-Separate frost trap and freeze trap from shared cooldown (might be drastic but with desecration existing I can't see this being that harmful).

-Get rid of the aspect swapping garbage that was forced in, it's clunky and doesn't actually involve choices since everyone just macros aspects to the proper shots and ignores it; the mechanic just becomes obnoxious and clunky (character constantly recasting the same aspect, doing idiotic animation/sound effect over and over for no reason). Might as well just give us Fox + Hawk tied together and call it a day. Aspect of the Cheetah/pack are the only aspect swaps we should ever need to do since the idea behind aspect swapping might make sense but the macro-into-every-shot workaround negates the purpose entirely.

-Pet res still 10 second cast and we depend on our pet so much that this should definitely be reduced, maybe a 4-6 second cast. It's nearly impossible to resummon our pet against a competent team.

-Pet bug when new pet is summoned.....still occurring.....insta-death to anyone who chooses to take advantage.

-Mend pet needs to not suck, maybe just a minor buff because I know it's a free-of-cost heal over time and healers are supposed to heal pets as well.

-Scatter/Trap should have a very small damage threshold so small dots like serpent sting/hemo glyph bleed/rend/frost fever/etc won't break it instantly. Maybe a 4-5k dmg threshold? We don't need a glyph to wipe dots like poly/blind glyph or anything, but now that we can't scorpid sting to delete Serpent Sting dot from our target, this makes swapping focus fire target and CC difficult because Chimera will keep refreshing it's duration, often leaving an 8-15~ second lapse in our CC that pretty much no other class has to deal with. [i]This would also make the stupid 70 dmg poison dot from snake traps not counter our own traps just because we wanted to get an Entrapment proc.

That last one isn't a huge deal other than snake traps, I'd settle for snake poison being removed from the game.

-Attack Slowing effects; they are too powerful against hunters. Waylay/Frost Fever apply automatically, without thought, and are basically up 24/7 if you're the target. It directly and vastly hinders focus regen by making already slow steady shot casts (that deal 1600~ dmg to plate....) PAINFULLY slow, and our focus regeneration rate is already lackluster without these effects. They should be reduced to 10% against ranged instead of 20%.

-MAKE IMMOLATION TRAP WORTH USING EVER PLZ, I like lighting people on fire! This isn't really important, but why have a trap that sets someone on fire be basically useless? :P

ADDED:

-4 Piece PvP Set Bonus should reduce the cast time of steady/cobra shot by 0.2-0.4 (somewhere in that range would be good imo). As of now we're basically going to be having to wear the 2pc PvE set bonus for double focus, this would be a similar benefit without requiring us resorting to PvE gear.

It's not hard to get the 2pc (I already have 2pc from raid finder), it's just I don't think PvE gear being nearly required for PvP is ever a good thing. I like resilience and I prefer to wear a full set of PvP gear. I can't see this doing any real harm, steady shot does meager damage to begin with and it's not like we'll get a ton of extra all-powerful arcane shots in, it just helps us compete with the PvE set a bit.


Okay that's all I can think of, feel free to add/comment/flame/dance.

If you agree with most or all of this list, please up vote this post so it increases the chances a Blizzard rep will see it.
