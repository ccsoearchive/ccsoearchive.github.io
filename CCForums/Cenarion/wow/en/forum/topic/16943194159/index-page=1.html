You might have heard whispers in the streets of major Alliance cities, from a few common folk who seem to have come across some rather interesting news. They might be whispering about a new nation supposedly being formed in Arathi, claiming to be a rebirth of the Empire of Arathor, that great power that ruled over all humanity so long ago. For better or for worse, these rumors seem to be true, and you might later find out that this so-called 'Imperium' has an embassy in Stormwind, where you can speak to them directly about these rumors, among other things that might pique your curiosity.

Or, you might have been travelling through the Arathi Highlands, perhaps to Stromgarde or beyond, and you might have seen patrols wearing red, gold, and/or white armor. All of these patrols seem to share a similar trait: They all were wearing the same tabard, possessing a blood red double-headed eagle on a golden yellow field, with black trimmings around the edge. A few towns even seem to fly a specific banner, sharing the color scheme and the eagle symbol. There is obviously a presence here that recently formed, and they seem to be within their own capacity to defend themselves.

Either way, something is happening in the Arathi Highlands, that you can be sure of. A new beginning for the Arathi people and humankind, perhaps, or instead it could be a bunch of power-hungry vandals, fueled by ambitions of conquest. Difficult to say, but one thing seems certain: These people appear to know what battle is, and exercise their knowledge against the Forsaken and others who would settle in Arathi that are not human. They remain steadfast to the idea of a greater humanity, to the lost human kingdoms returning to their former glory. They praise the Holy Light, and call upon it as a beacon for their hope and wishes of a better future for mankind. They are a new kingdom, a relic risen from the ashes like a phoenix, and they are ready to take action against any who would do harm against the peoples of humanity and the Grand Alliance.

They are the Imperium of Arathor. Their future is bright, and their time is now.

Good day, fellow RPers of Wyrmrest Accord! You may have already heard of us, or you may not have, it's not a problem either way. Basically, I'd like to formally introduce you to <The Imperium of Arathor>, a Heavy RP guild with strong emphasis on Stromgarde and Arathi lore elements, which acts out in the style of Kingdom RP.

What is 'Kingdom RP', you ask? Well, the most accurate description I can muster is that Kingdom RP is a style of RP in which the participants all take on the roles of a kingdom: the ruler (or rulers), advisory council members, nobility, soldiers, clergymen, craftsmen, merchants, peasants, criminals, etc. Then, the group or guild will find a place to act out their kingdom and viola! Kingdom RP. This style is most commonly seem in PuG RPs, but speaking as a semi-experienced RPer of four years or so, I think that this style of RP has a large amount of potential for guilds to act out as well, with a greater level of control on who participates in the RP (which is the point of most guilds), and better organization.

If Kingdom RP isn't up your alley, that's perfectly fine. We consider ourselves to be diverse in the fact that we have multiple branches dedicated to different aspects of the guild, with different RP styles for each branch accordingly. Our second-strongest emphasis is on the 'Military RP' style, with a standing army and troops ready to defend the Imperium's lands, and march forward to battle in the name of the Imperator and humankind. We also offer the 'Holy RP' style, with a well-structured church of our own, to harbor and spread the word of our own special interpretation of the Holy Light. The 'Magic RP' style is also available for any magi who want to research and practice their magics for the good of the Imperium, as well as providing an academic atmosphere to promote the acquisition of further knowledge of humanity's past. Finally, while not a full-fledged RP style on its own, we also offer opportunities to act as part of our Inquisition, to seek out heretics and dissidents within our nation and bring them to 'justice'... whatever you might view that 'justice' to be, muhahaha.

We have been around for some months, and we have shaken up the community a couple of times with a few choice events, or series of events. While we do still plan to be active and involved in the greater RP community, we have come to the understanding that sometimes caution must be exercised in regards to how these events are done. We do intend to continue shaking up the community and keeping it on its toes, though we will do so in a much more respectable fashion, with the understanding that not everyone would be copacetic with our decisions. We've recently reformed ourselves with a much more clear-cut backstory and goals in mind, and we hope to develop further on this in the future. However, as a result of this reformation, we had to do a rather thorough purge of rank, and we're rather scarce on members right now compared to what we used to be at. So, what we need most right now is YOU, that's right, YOU, to help us grow and prosper and bring all of our plans to fruition.

So, simply put, we RP as a semi-independent nation-state of our own, hence the Kingdom RP style, and we consider ourselves an auxiliary to the Kingdom of Stromgarde, stationed in Arathi and supporting Stromgarde and the Grand Alliance as allies, though not formally being a part of either entity. WE HAVE NO CLAIMS, NOR DO WE WISH TO MAKE ANY CLAIMS, TO THE CITY OF STROMGARDE OR REFUGE POINTE, BOTH LOCATIONS RECOGNIZED IN LORE AS BELONGING SOLELY TO THE KINGDOM OF STROMGARDE. We have our own territory, our own military, our own towns, and even our own culture, mostly based on ancient Arathi traditions and ideas (Read: Roman-influenced). We consider ourselves to be the spiritual successors to the long-dead Arathorian Empire, a rebirth of that great and ancient entity which symbolizes humankind's unity under a single banner, and the might and glory it represents. Nothing is more important to us than the continuing longevity and prosperity of the human race, shown by how we aspire to facilitate the reclamation and restoration of all the fallen kingdoms of man.

TL;DR: We kick butt in the name of Arathor and humankind, and run our own country on the side.

With that being said, we definitely have an emphasis on humankind as reflected in WoW lore. If you are a human paladin or priest, you can bet we'll happily take you in, no questions asked (except for those in the interview). That being said, here is our preferences for race and class admission:

Races: From most to least welcome, Human > Dwarf > Gnomes > Draenei = Worgen. Night Elves and Pandaren are generally not allowed except in the most unusual exceptions.

Classes: From most to least welcome, Paladin = Priest > Warrior = Hunter = Mage > Rogue = Monk > Shaman = Druid > Death Knight. Warlocks are generally not allowed except in the most unusual exceptions.

Now, onto the common questions that might be asked.

How often do you guys do RP stuff?
We have regularly-scheduled weekly RP events, such as a military training day, and a day dedicated entirely to recruitment. We have bi-weekly meetings between all the members of the Senate and the Imperator, and anyone is welcome to observe, even if you're not a member of the guild! Usually these meetings occur in the Chapel of Stromgarde.
Also, if you happen to have any ideas for any events, storylines, or plot arcs that you think the guild should participate in, we would be happy to hear them, and we can guarantee that we'll do our best to work your events into the schedule! Anyone is welcome to propose event ideas, even newcomers! It doesn't matter if it's personal or inter-guild, and a lot of our RP, aside from the weekly events, is based on what you, the member, can contribute!

What do you want to do for the community?
Anything that the community wants done, but most importantly, we want to ensure that the community remains diverse, welcoming, and friendly to everyone who participates in it, regardless of how new or inexperienced they are in RP. We want to make sure that everyone understands that the community is only as good as the players make it, and the Imperium of Arathor is an attempt to gather all the best players in the community to work towards this goal. If you or anyone else has a problem with anything that the guild is doing, please do not hesitate to bring it to me or one of my officers, and we will do whatever we can to accommodate you, whether you're a member of our guild or not.

Do you plan to do any RP-PvP?
Actually, yes. We have considered stepping into the wildly exciting realm of RP-PvP, but at the moment, we haven't had the resources to do so effectively. That being said, if anyone has experience in RP-PvP or forming events related to it, we would vastly appreciate your contributions to this end. We've also considered helping to re-stimulate World PvP again, specifically in Hillsbrad, where nostalgic memories of raiding Tarren Mill are fondly regarded, but never quite realized to the scale that they used to be at... yet.
Of course, if RP-PvP isn't your thing, that's fine too! We don't force any of our members to participate in any PvP aspects if they're not comfortable with it, and we will make sure not to shirk our attention to regular RP, treating both RP-PvP and straight-up RP as equal parts of a whole, with neither overpowering the other.

Alright, you've sold me. How do I join?
First thing we'd like you to do is to sign up on our website, marked here: imperium-of-arathor.shivtr.com. Many of the guild's resources can be found here, including a description of how we plan to run our hierarchy, a list of common guild rules, and even our IC Constitution! After you've submitted an application, we'd like to have you participate in an IC and OOC interview in-game, and then after that, you can choose your branch and be a full-fledged guild member!

So please, if you have any other questions about the guild that I haven't answered in this forum post, please ask me in the thread or in-game, and I'll do my best to answer you! My RID is Ricarius#1180, feel free to add me if you need to get something to me pronto!

Lastly, we would be happy to have you with us in the guild, and we would also very much appreciate any like-minded guilds signing alliances with us! Anything to help us contribute more to the community in a positive way! If you like us, show your support, and thanks for reading!
