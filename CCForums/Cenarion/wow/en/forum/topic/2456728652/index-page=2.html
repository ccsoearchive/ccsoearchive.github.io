To take the Avoidance X Mastery example. I tested this past week. I reforged all my gear and regemmed everything to avoidance, got 45% avoidance in raids and around ~70-80% shields. Conclusions?

It felt better on aoe pulls .. like .. absurdly better. Specially when early in pulls (for example BoT trash)my trinket procc'd and i popped DRW (which bumped me to ~70% avoidance) and all i saw was a huge string of parry parry pary parry dodge parry etc ..

On bosses. Spike dmgs were much less frequent, but in the fewer occasions that they happened, i felt i had less control over my survivability (for obvious reasons, absurdly lower shields).

Conclusion ? Heavy physical dmg bosses i took way less dmg than in the previous week with full mastery. Bosses with magic dmg mechanics i took slightly more (magical dmg bosses). The dmg income flow seemed less spiky overall ... I asked the healers if they thought it was easier to heal me .. and the veredict was: "about the same /slightly easier".

So based on those, this week i'm trying to settle in 40% avoidance, and ~110% mastery. Simply because DR on avoidance makes you sacrifice way too much mastery to go past ~40% avoidance.

I think this is the kind of data that people can use to discuss. Pure math .. while usefull to a theorycraft. I see little use to actual concept discutions .. which blood issue was always about. Not about raw numbers and statistics.
