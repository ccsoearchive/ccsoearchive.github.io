As for the Healing bonuses, I don't want to give up the T12 bonuses for these...

TLDR... Healing (Disc) T12 >>>> T13 by a lot imo.
T12 2pc = 410 MP5 (constant, 100% proc with heals)
T13 2pc = 250 MP5 (if used 100% on cooldown with PoH) 125 MP5 is more realistic with PoH used and a 50% CD usage.
T12 4pc = 1.25k HPS (this improves as you heal more or faster with PI or bloodlust etc during heavy healing moments.)
T13 4pc = 300 HPS (this is for a single target constant shielding, it realistically will not be better than this.)


If you shield 12 times in a minute, that means that you are getting over 50k healing per minute from the 4piece, which translates to being the same as our current piece. Any additional shields go above and beyond that. 12 shields a minute translates to roughly one in four casts, possibly one in three, and of course that is counting preshield phases consisting of 10 or more shields in succession. Overall, especially with mana continuing to be less of a constraint, I think that this tier set will be rather exceptional, under the condition that those 80k shields actually break, as they will be subject to a great deal of potential overheal depending on boss mechanics.


T13 4pc: If you get 12 Shields cast in a minute (and this in itself is a huge problem in itself, which I will discuss further on) then with a 10% proc chance, you will get 1.2 procs of the +100% shield in that minute period. Lets say your standard shield absorbs 40k, then on the proc, the shield will be for an additional 40k (80k total). Multiply that 40k by 1.2 to normalize, then you get 1.2 x (+40k) = 48k healing for that minute = 800hps (48k/60seconds).

T12 4pc: Has a 5% proc rate. Over a minute period (using slow cast heals 2 sec each) you get 30 casts. 30 x 5% = 1.5 procs. Each proc smart heals for 50k (5 ticks of 10k). 1.5*50k = 75k. Normalized, this equates to 1,250hps (75k/60seconds).

Important points:
1) Rarely will you be upkeeping 12 shields per minute. The only chance of this would be tank healing (PW:S, GH, GH, GH, PW:S, GH, GH...) with a good amount of haste to make that fast enough maintain a single target shield every 6 seconds.
2) Maybe you might consider shielding 2 targets continuously to get your 12 shields per minute? So you PW:S your tank, and another player, and then sometimes the +40k shield procs on the tank, and other times it procs on another player. Well, if procs on a non-tank, it is going to be wasted most of the time, so what is the point? This is obviously a pointless means (on most fights) of trying to get this proc up.
3) with T13 4pc, any healing spell can proc it. I used slow heals as a conservative example to show how 1.25khps can be achieved. Throwing in penance (3 heals) and flash heals and PoH (5 heals) is going to drastically lift the 1.25khps without even trying. 2khps is quite reasonable. Also, in bloodlust and PI moments, this proc comes out of your ears!!
4) I notice you suggest 50k shield bonuses. I think 40k is much more realistic. But if you must, that brings up the 1,000hps. But hmmm. Top end heroic T13 gear with mastery maxed.. maybe... But regardless. It does not come close to the T12. The T13 should exceed it without trying, or offer utility, but it does not do that either.
5) Precasting 10 shields? That is 60k mana gone. Those days are well gone. If you did cast on many players, one of those will get the proc, and it will unlikely be the tank. But lets say a raid does call for such a niche pre-casting procedure as this, it is terribly situational - and improbable. But hey - I am still working on Rag-regular so what would I know (maybe next week we will see heroics yay).

Summary; the T12 4pc is soooo easy to keep up procs. The T12 4pc numbers far outweigh the T13 4pc numbers. To even try to maintain a high proc rate as you suggest with 12 shields per minute, is going to be rarely achievable due to the dynamics required in healing.
