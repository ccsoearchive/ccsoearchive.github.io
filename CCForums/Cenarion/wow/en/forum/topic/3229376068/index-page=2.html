As far as Shadow Spec goes, It seems as though Blizzard used the Tier 13 set bonuses as temporary "fixes" to problems that Spriests are currently facing in raids.

The 2pc bonus "fixes" the backlash issue which can be frustrating due to fight mechanics, ie Baleroc debuff

The 4pc bonus "fixes" the frustrating Empowered Shadow/Shadow Orb RNG issue, helping to set up better opening rotations for bosses etc.

Anyone else get the sense that these set bonuses are band-aid solutions to class issues? While they will buff DPS overall, they are simply rotation mechanic "fixes" instead of being creative additions to our spec

Imho, Blizz should fix the above mechanic issues AND give us creative set bonuses to enhance our spec, not just to "fix" our rotation

"Fix" is in quotations because i think it remains to be seen if these bonuses are effective solutions to the rotation mechanic issues at all

The only bonus I think is remotely decent of Blizzard to create is the Disc 4pc, and even then its basically adding a crappy crit rating to the spell. Holy got the shaft big time, and the Shadow bonuses are just thrown out there with no sort of regard to the spec/talents at all. I hope they figure out how to read and comprehend their various specs before putting this nonsense live.

Seriously, looking at all the other healer bonuses, and comparing them to ours... I want to hurt small baby animals.

Paladins get 20% to their AoE and their haste/crit cooldown gives them reduced mana costsafter the fact. Druids get a 10% chance to double their hot healing, and get reduced mana costs after using Innervate. Shamans get reduced mana costs after using MTT, and get a 30% haste buff after using Spiritwalker's Grace (which with the 4pc lasts 20s).

So two of the healers get reduced mana costs after using their big mana return ability, and paladins get it after using a throughput ability. We get it after using something that already reduces mana costs and increases throughput on us or another player, or for Holy priests, using an ability that you usually place before fights initially and off cooldown after that. Useful.

As for the 4pc, we get 10% chance to crit on our PW:S, which Holy and Disc can benefit from, and Holy gets the added benefit of getting their Holy Word cooldown reduced by 20%. Whooo. Holys usually stay in Chakra: Sanctuary, which means they get to spend 9k mana 20% sooner. Cool. Discs on the other hand, have been scolded for using PW:S this entire expac, and yet the last 4set bonus of Cataclysm encourages us to use it more to get more benefit from it. Again, good move Blizz. I'd rather the bonus be something along the lines of every time you use Inner Focus you get X increase of a stat for Y seconds on a 45 sec cooldown to prevent those of us with ToT to benefit more. Either that or leave it without the 45 sec cooldown. If you really wanted Holy to use their HW spells more by reducing the cooldown, you could add a mana cost reduction to the 4set, or just change it entirely. Add a Surge of Light type ability with a decent uptime, or everytime you use Chakra you get X stat increase. Yes, it might be boring, but it is also way better than set bonuses that suck.
