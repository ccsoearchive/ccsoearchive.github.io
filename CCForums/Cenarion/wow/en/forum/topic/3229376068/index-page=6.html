The SPriest 2P bonus looks great. SW:Death can be part of the rotation. Especially as a filler when a dot refresh is upcoming shortly, an SW:D can replace a tick or two of mindflay. I guess the 4P bonus is to help reduce the RNG complaints people have with orbs?

As for the Healing bonuses, I don't want to give up the T12 bonuses for these. The 2pc T12 bonus is effectively a constant 410 MP5 forever - which is a fantastic mana return. For the T13 2Pc bonus, It is going to depend on your playstyle (which I may need to adjust because of this) but I save PI for when I need it. Some fights it is not needed at all. I treat it as a CD for oh crap moments. So the mana saving with this for me (as Disc) is going to be minimal - Unless I use it on every CD every 2 minutes:( For example, Following a PI, you have 10 second window for 25% reduction in spell cost. Lets say you can fit in 5 PoH casts (@4.8k mana cost each cast (after PI 20% cost reduction)) then with 25% reduced cost with the 2pc bonus, thats 1.2k x 5 = 6k mana saved. With a PI cooldown of 120 seconds, that equates to 6k/120 * 5 = 250 MP5. If it were flash heals that were being cast during the 10 seconds, then that would be 500 MP5 equivalent savings for a very very best case usage scenario - which is unrealistic.

As for the Holy 2pc bonus, Most times the priest places the LightWell out ahead of time for when they expect people to need it (even before the raid starts). If the priest has any interest in making the most of the 2pc bonus, they are going to have to drop it only when aoe !@#$ happens, not before (or at least not too soon before). Furthermore - if a priest does not normally include the lightwell talent, they are now being told by blizzard - "you must now use it!" - which is taking away their freedom of choice for optional talents.

The 4pc bonus for Disc... 1 in 10 chance of a +100% shield. So, assuming tank shielding mostly (I rarely shield anything else as they are unlikely to consume the shield) then assuming an average shield (of 30k absorption) at a rate of one every 10 seconds on the tank, then every 100 seconds a shield will have an absorption of 60k. So, +30k/100 = +300hps. The current T12 4pc bonus procs very often with a 5% proc chance, and does 50k smart heals. In a 100 second period of heals (lets say Greater heals or heals at ~ 2second cast times) = 50 casts. This is 2.5 procs per 100s. 2.5 x 50k = 125k heals per 100seconds. This = +1.25k HPS. Keep in mind that if you heal faster (flash heals etc) then this will proc even faster! With the T13 4pc, you can not shield your target faster than the Weakened Sould debuff allows.

TLDR... Healing (Disc) T12 >>>> T13 by a lot imo.
T12 2pc = 410 MP5 (constant, 100% proc with heals)
T13 2pc = 250 MP5 (if used 100% on cooldown with PoH) 125 MP5 is more realistic with PoH used and a 50% CD usage.
T12 4pc = 1.25k HPS (this improves as you heal more or faster with PI or bloodlust etc during heavy healing moments.)
T13 4pc = 300 HPS (this is for a single target constant shielding, it realistically will not be better than this.)

Edit: 1) added numbers. 2) Forgot PI already reduces the spell cost by 20% already before the 25% 4pc bonus, so I reduced the rough cost of Flash Heal and PoH to 4.8k each cast instead of 6k. This results in the T13 2pc bonus having even less relative usefulness!
