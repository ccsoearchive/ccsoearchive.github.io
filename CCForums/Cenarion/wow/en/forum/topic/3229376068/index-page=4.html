Very underwhelming bonuses all around. The Tier 12 bonuses are more fun and engaging, as well as being more powerful (at least for us healers). Here's a fixed version:

Rework Divine Hymn and Hymn of Hope (through talents and/or Holy Mastery). When a Holy priest casts the spells, they don't have to channel them, they stay active as a buff on the priest. Both Hymns have a 3 min CD. HoH is off the GCD, DH now has a cast time.

Healing 2pc: After using Power Infusion or Hymn of Hope, the mana cost of your spells is reduced by 25% for 10/15 seconds. (10 seconds for PI, 15 for HoH). HoH effect doesn't work if you're Disc.

Healing 4pc: Casting (X,Y,Z) spells gives you a % chance to have your next Holy Word healing for 100% extra or Power Word: Shield absorb 100% extra damage.

Shadow 2pc: Shadow Word: Death deals an additional 55% damage and the backlash damage can't exceed more than 5% of your current health. (SW:D no longer kills us, Masochism still works).

Shadow 4pc: I'm fine with as is. This probably will result in Shadow switching to a Mind Spike rotation when Fiend is out. If this isn't intended by the devs, then the 2pc should be changed to grant 2 or 3 Shadow Orbs, while keeping the "doesn't kill you" effect. 4pc should give us a stacking buff that makes Mind Blast alone hit harder.
