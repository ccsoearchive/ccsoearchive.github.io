I'm just wondering if there's anything we can do about it? We put in tickets, and are waiting on responses...
The GM was someone we all trusted, and cared for, we put in our time and effort to make raids run smoothly, and we all contributed to the guild leveling up as quick as it did. The guild bank was full of about 50k and countless mats we all spent out money and time on.

See... I understand the selling of bank guilds, and guilds that have been abandoned by it's members, I really do. But ALL of the people in this guild were on not 45 minutes before this ninja sell, raiding and leveling the guild even more. Then... as soon as everyone signed off, he kicked everyone out of the guild, ninja'd the guild bank and sold the guild to some random guy in guild chat. Then he proceeded to message me, laughing and harassing me as well with his little friends.
It sucks... I would have happily bought the guild from him, all of us did... but it seems he decided to hurt us where he knew we couldn't fully fight back on the issue. It was OUR guild, not HIS guild. He was barely on, never contributing and never helping out even when we asked him to. It's all a joke to him, he doesn't care, he just wanted to hurt us.

I'm just wondering... I have read the forums, and I see posts saying both sides can be argued. Can it be at all possible for us to get back the guild we worked months on leveling and recruiting people? The hours we spent working out butts off in dungeons and getting 6/7 in Firelands for hours on the weekends?

I see some people saying the guild is over, and we can't get it back from this stranger. And to that, I say bull...! A guild belongs to it's members, not just the GM. The GM may make the rules, ranks and play around in the guild bank, but in the end, it's all the players in the guild that make it up and that put their hearts into it to make it better.

So... I'm asking, we all put in tickets, trying to get our guild back, so we can get back to raiding and learning from our mistakes of who to trust. Is there any way this will work? Can we get the guild back? Or is everything we are trying for in vain?

Blizzard may not be able to do much about him selling the guild, however they can action his account for stealing the guild bank.

http://us.blizzard.com/support/article.xml?locale=en_US&articleId=20459&pageNumber=1&searchQuery=Guild+Bank+Theft

Guild Bank Theft

This category includes:
• Removing goods from a guild's bank with malicious intent

If a player is found to have perpetrated a guild bank theft, he/she may:
• Be temporarily suspended from the game
• Have any money or item gains removed


The key words here is: "malicious intent" as such it applies to the Guild leader as well.
