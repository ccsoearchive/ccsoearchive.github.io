No, if you do the math on it you will find that you spend 85% more energy to keep SnD up using 5 cp's as opposed to using 1 cp to refresh. take a 2 minute fight. You need 5.22 refreshes of a 1 cp SnD and 2.93 refreshes if you use 5 cp SnD. For the sake of simplicity lets use 5 refreshes using 1 cp and 3 refreshes using 5 cp's.

So that's 5 x 40 energy for the SS's and 5 x 25 energy for the SnD. But Relentless strikes says 1/5th of the SnD will restore 25 energy so really its
5*40 + 4*25 = 300 energy used to keep SnD up for 2 mins.

For 5 cp refreshes using SnD will never cost energy so we can leave that out. We just have 3 refreshes using 5 cp's each. 3 refreshes x 5 SS x 40 energy = 600 energy used.

That's twice as much energy wasted using 5 cp's. I did round so its not quite double it actually comes out that it costs 85% more energy to use 5 cp's than to use 1 cp to refresh SnD. Thats 2 additional 5 cp evis you could use instead. And don't bring up the glyph proc that only favors the lower cp's and for 5 cp's it could waste the glyph completely. This is a pretty bare bones calculation so I am sure I haven't covered all the variables. Unless someone can point out a huge factor I have not included, it will take a lot to over come the energy saving using 1 cp instead of 5.
