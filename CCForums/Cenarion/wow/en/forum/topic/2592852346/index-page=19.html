06/07/2011 11:39 AMPosted by Daxxarri
Generally speaking, we don't set out to provide perfect itemization for every spec in the game for every tier of content (though we don't want to leave anyone totally out in the cold, either). Sometimes the item a particular class or spec wants is going to be a bit tougher to get. While that’s not an ideal situation and some specs are more prone to the effect than others, it is the kind of thing that is going to happen sometimes - otherwise every desirable weapon would be sitting on the first boss of every dungeon. We like to put weapons on end bosses because they feel exciting. I know some of you are frustrated about where it ended up, but we particularly wanted to put the axe on Nef because he also dropped one in Blackwing Lair.


ok, this is an acceptable underlying reason for general lack of conscious effort. I would like to point out, though, that certain disparities exist that make some weapon (gear in general) options a total failure in comparison to other available options. Case in point: Gatecrasher is vastly superior to Shatterskull Bonecrusher. Why? Expertise. Not simply the expertise on the item, but the (frustrating and easily fixable) racial expertise offered to the various races who would benefit from the use of these weapons. I will specifically reference the difference between Orcs and Dwarves; The racial expertise bonus awarded to a Dwarf for wielding a mace (Shatterskull Bonecrusher) is less than the sum of expertise itemized on ONE Gatecrasher. This nullifies the benefit of choosing the mace and makes the axe a much more valuable option. Maybe the internal engine values haste/crit/hit/expertise/mastery all the same - but maybe that's something that should change. Perhaps they should have internal values to help the developers piece together a more logical set of gear rather than just randomly slapping ratings here and there.

06/07/2011 11:39 AMPosted by Daxxarri

We've done some stat streamlining to make things easier, but we don't want to do so much that every weapon in a tier seems to be the same item with a different model.
