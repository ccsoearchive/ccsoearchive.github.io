There are a number of Shaman's in lore the have wielded swords, so it's not a lore issue.

The issue really stems from the caster weapons. Giving shaman's the ability to wield swords would make them the only class in the game to be able to wield every type of spellcaster weapon (Mages and Locks can't use Maces. Shamans, Priests and Druids can't use Swords. Paladins can't use Daggers or Staves). Now, the obvious fix for this is to make the ability to use swords a spec specific ability. However, no other class has a specific weapon allotment by choosing a specific spec so it would be interesting to see the repercussions of that.

Don't get me wrong, seeing that damn agility sword drop almost every week while i've still been rocking the PvP weapons has driven me half insane (yet to see BoE's drop and i have other prio's with gold).

Giving swords to shamans is a complex issue, and i would love to see it happen to at least alleviate the problem of a 'rogue niche sword' but the spellcaster problem would have to be addressed.

Now, the obvious fix for this is to make the ability to use swords a spec specific ability. However, no other class has a specific weapon allotment by choosing a specific spec so it would be interesting to see the repercussions of that.


Well, TG does let you treat 2H weapons as 1H ones, even to the extent of mainhanding a 2H while oHing a shield or something. And shamans used (still do?) gain the ability to dual wield through speccing enchancement before (used to be a talent, is it a passive for the tree now?), so there's certainly precedent.

Generally I just don't like how certain classes are pigeonholed right now into looting only a small selection of weapons per tier. Back in Vanilla and even in BC, the various 1H weapons used to be shared between warriors, rogues, shamans and hunters, with particular ones being ideal for particular classes/specs, but all useful for everyone. Now we have rogue daggers, warrior/dk swords, shaman axes, and hunter/druid staves/polearms. It's great when you're the only person in the raid who can use something that drops and get to win it without competition, but it's annoying when the same thing drops next week and you have to shard it because no-one else has any reason at all to equip it. Then you spend the next 2 months getting similar 1-off items. No options for sidegrades, no looting a weapon you don't need now but which would be ideal for your other spec, or which will be useful once you've got some other items, etc - just one item per weapon slot per tier, unless you happen to be a 2H plate dps I guess.
