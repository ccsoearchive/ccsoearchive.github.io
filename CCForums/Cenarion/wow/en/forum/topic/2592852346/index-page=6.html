This kind of itemization isn't that unusual, nor is it peculiar to this situation. As others have pointed out, there are slow off-hands available which aren't broadly useful.

There's enough room in itemization for some flexibility, and that allows us to create niche items like slow off-hands or 1-handed swords with agility on occasion. Of course, if a particular item has a comparatively narrow niche, then it's more likely that fewer of those items will be introduced. Or, perhaps we'll place an item like Stormwake, the Tempest's Reach which potentially appeals to several different classes.

The issue in this current tier (and now it looks like it will be the same in the next tier) is that enhancement shamans do not really have much of a choice. For example, 2h strength users had 3 options (4 if they wanted to grind archaeology). Combat rogues have 4 slow weapons - one of which comes from a lockbox.. and so on and so forth. Enhancement shamans were limited to 2 random boe weapons (one fist and the off-hand axe) or the axe from Nef - which is only taken if you actually managed to kill Nef on heroic purely for the dmg and dps boost as it is itemised so badly for us. Two of the 3 are not even one hand weapons but very restrictive and random. And that is it.

Therefore in principle, we are restricted to 2 boe random drops (we clear BWD every week and we are still to see more than 1 MH agi fist weapon drop) or an axe off the last boss on HM. When we look at the sword off the Twin Drakes, we see that it is perfectly itemised for us, but we cannot use it. Then we look at the normal axe from Nef and it's meh to be honest. The stats on it are not what enhancement go for.

This has been the same in Wrath and the trend continues now. Thus far, enhancement are again being left out in the upcoming tier since we know we won't be able to have a weapon crafted for us, so we will have to rely raid drops. Having just looked at the 1h axe that drops in there off trash, it is strength.. I facepalmed. Like the lovely strength fist weapon that only 1h fury can use... Is there another that I may have missed?

But we apparently (again if MMO are correct) get a lovely 2.10 speed agi mace.

So I am living in hope that Blizzard will either add a recipe that will allow Blacksmiths to make an agi weapon that Enhancement shamans can actually use and I live in hope that MMO is incorrect and that the mace is actually 2.60 speed. If my hope is in vain, then yeah, what options are there for us as a class when it comes to weapons?

We get Gatecrasher (I am unsure of which boss this is from? - and it seems to be much further in Firelands) and that's it... So unless the 2.10 speed agi mace is fixed, we are down to only one choice this time. So no crafted weapons for us.. nothing off earlier bosses.. But hey, shamans can boast they get a caster axe apparently.. (again I facepalmed).

I am sure you get where I am going here. I agree with previous posters. Blizzard should either abolish Agi and Strenght on these weapons and make it a flat out AP stat - so that all can use them. Or allow Enhancement shamans to use swords.
