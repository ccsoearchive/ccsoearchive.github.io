Nah, but really, the agi sword dilemma is a bit strange to me. I know we're not suppoused to have perfect itemization in our gear. That's the point, actually. To make equipment that is a bit off, so players have to mix and match different pieces of equipment. However, agi swords in particular strike me as an oddity.

Take Venerable Dal'Rend's Sacred Charge, for example. I might be a bit off, but if I recall correctly, it was designed in a time when Fury Warriors ended up using two two-handers, DKs were not supposed to dual-wield, Combat and Sub Rogues could take advantage of it and there weren't any tanking heirlooms. So the design looks ok, considering this. However, as time keeps marching forward, this weapon is starting to look a bit outdated in that it is not a strength option for the plate dual wielders, not a tanking weapon and only fits completely in Combat's gearing. However, Rogues can also use the Venerable of McGowan, which is better.

Lore-wise, a shaman wielding swords looks a bit weird, but when you think about it, a rogue using a mace is also quite strange - oh, fear my stealthy glorified bat ;P

Let's see what happens with this. Hopefully, it will be looked upon Soon (:
