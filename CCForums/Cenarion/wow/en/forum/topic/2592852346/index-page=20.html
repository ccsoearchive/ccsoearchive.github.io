ok, this is an acceptable underlying reason for general lack of conscious effort. I would like to point out, though, that certain disparities exist that make some weapon (gear in general) options a total failure in comparison to other available options. Case in point: Gatecrasher is vastly superior to Shatterskull Bonecrusher. Why? Expertise. Not simply the expertise on the item, but the (frustrating and easily fixable) racial expertise offered to the various races who would benefit from the use of these weapons. I will specifically reference the difference between Orcs and Dwarves; The racial expertise bonus awarded to a Dwarf for wielding a mace (Shatterskull Bonecrusher) is less than the sum of expertise itemized on ONE Gatecrasher. This nullifies the benefit of choosing the mace and makes the axe a much more valuable option. Maybe the internal engine values haste/crit/hit/expertise/mastery all the same - but maybe that's something that should change. Perhaps they should have internal values to help the developers piece together a more logical set of gear rather than just randomly slapping ratings here and there.


Quoting myself, here, because I realized in hindsight that I am making a somewhat invalid claim. The basis is there - itemizing gear with hit and expertise can greatly inflate the value of the item due to the nature of secondary stats and their relativity to eachother. This can also make particular items much less valuable once caps have been met - and thus items with crit/haste/mastery maintain a relatively high value across the board.

Also, racial expertise is most definitely a factor in weapon choice for physical DPS classes and not catering to those nuances of character is a major itemization malfunction. The Gatecrasher should not have expertise, period. That actually makes dwarves (mace expertise racial) more inclined to choose the axe over the mace... and it makes orcs (axe expertise racial) more inclined to choose the mace, because of the over-abundance of expertise in choosing the axe. It's a flaw I'm concerned about - how did that go unchecked?

http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/2592646740?page=1#1

I'd appreciate some feedback there, with regard to some of the concerns which have popped up in this thread. Also, a 'Like' would be nice, to get the OP upgraded to that fancy 'other' shade of brown.
