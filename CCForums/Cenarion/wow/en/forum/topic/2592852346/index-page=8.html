since the blue post, the view count and responses here have been ridiculous...

also, since this thread has been derailed and hijacked by enhance supporters, I'd like to reiterate couple suggestions pertaining to agility weapons for people to ponder.

- Convert all agility/strength weapons into agility and make strength users benefit more from agility.

- Convert all agility/strength items into attack power based unified weapons. Settle the difference in agi/crit elsewhere in itemization. Tank items clearly defined by at least one avoidance stat may be kept with strength BUT could use the extra stat itemization on stamina. Nobody says no to more stamina.

- Allow player conversion of strength to agility and vice versa via the reforging system or a separate available system in-game. This could (perhaps should) be limited to weapons.. but this system seems altogether less tidy than any external conversion option.

- Allow particular classes to benefit more from the opposing stat so that choosing strength over agility or agility over strength isn't nearly as taboo. To specify, I'd go with warriors being able to make better use of agility (not so much that all agility gear becomes desirable vs strength.. perhaps just giving haste rating based on agility) and shamans being able to make better use of strength (simply allowing 2x attack power from strength would do just fine)

- Just put more damn weapons (items) in the game. I don't want to hear about how time-consuming or expensive it is to have an art department come up with new ideas. There are plenty of people out there who can't catch a break to make a dime on their brilliant artistic ability and Blizzard is a multi-billion dollar company. You'll figure something out.
