This seems to be a problem with warlocks specifically.

Pre cata if you had the 70% pushback protection talent you lose .15 sec of cast time, max of two hits. .5 sec per hit without the talent.

When cata went live these talents went away but the effect was made standard for pretty much all casters, though mages still had to talent it.

Before this patch, my warlock would lose .15 sec per hit, max twice, casting. With concentration aura there was no pushback at all.

Now I'm getting the full .5 sec pushback per hit on my warlock.

My balance druid is unchanged however. It is still -.15 sec on wrath, starsurge, starfire, or any healing spell.

Basically they broke whatever hidden effect was giving warlocks pushback protection. This is a very serious issue, especially for pvp, but even in pve there is tons of pushback in dungeons and raids. This needs a hotfix asap.

Channeled spells are also broken, losing 25% of the channel duration each hit, same as zero pushback protection.
