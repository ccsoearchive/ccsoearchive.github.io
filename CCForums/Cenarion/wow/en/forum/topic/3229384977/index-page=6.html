2) Include a faction balancing criteria for the free transfer
- Any Low Pop realm eligible for outgoing free transfers must choose the lower Pop

Mergers won't happen. They've said as much quite a few times before in the past, I remember at least two threads on the old servers where a blue stepped in and said it wouldn't happen. Naming/guild issues for one example of the headache that will ensue. Three threads and no response are probably due to the fact that they've answered this question numerous times. I'm sure they'd like to solve the issue, it just isn't as easy as pressing a button and combining two servers on a whim.

I would be all for some sort of transfer package, however. Maybe pay the $25 dollars and be able to move all characters (from the same realm only) to another target realm. Best bet would be to have this as a limited time offer, say for a week or two. Those who take the offer can get off their dead servers and breathe a little life into their characters. Those who don't take the offer will have to pay full price if they decide they want to move after the time is up. Sort of how they lowered the prices of the game/expacs around Christmas last year. Those that acted got it cheap for friends/alt accounts, those that didn't.. didn't.

Something like this would keep the naming issue more fair (they know the risk they're taking if they move) and help people get off dead servers (thereby helping Blizzard realize which servers they can actually shut down without affecting profits *as much*). And we'd still be paying for the service, just not the ridiculous amount it takes someone with four or five alts to accomplish normally. The transfer system would be pretty busy for the week they enabled it, but it would shed a hell of a lot of light on server balance that needs addressed.
