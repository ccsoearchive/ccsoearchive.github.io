In Old AV you had a choice between any of these:

- Collect armor scraps to buff NPC defenders
- Collect blood/crystals to summon Lokholar/Ivus
- Rescue Wing Commanders and collect flesh/medals to summon air strikes
- Capture mines and turn in supplies for an infantry assault
- Tame rams and kill wolves to outfit a cavalry charge
- Assault enemy towers/bunkers and graveyards
- Defend your own towers/bunkers and graveyards
- Rescue a goblin and build a shredder to attack your foes

Currently, the options are:

- Follow the zerg to the enemy General
- Stand in an enemy tower "just in case" they try to recap it
- Get slaughtered trying to stop the enemy zerg
- Start working on the turn-in quests, only to have your efforts cut short by an incoming zerg or even the reinforcement timer
