We've been active for just over a month now I'm happy to say that the guild has exceeded all of my expectations at this point. We've recruited a lot of fun, active, respectful people, some less than desirable ones, lots of veteran roleplayers and a handful of new ones. Overall it's been quite the experience. And now it's time for an update.

What is the GRI?

The GRI is the Gryphon's Roost Initiative. It is the program in which the guild is founded on. ICly, it's an organization created by the House of Wrynn. Due to Stormwind being the epicenter of the Alliance, it is where the metaphorical gryphon roosts. It is a concentration on the home front, easing the tension that has built up the last few years as well as repairing what damage has come with the straining relationships and costs of war.

The program is structured around the needs of Stormwind and extends to the rest of the Alliance. By providing a better, stronger, more established infrastructure the city can serve as a better example within the Alliance, proving to our allies that Stormwind is stronger than ever and serves as an example to our allies. The goal is to provide jobs for the citizens of Stormwind and encourage trade and cooperation in the Alliance.

The three organizations under the G.R.I. are the Kingsguard, the Civic Services and the Lionheart Corps.

What are the three organizations about?

Please see the post below. This information took up a huge chunk of the maximum character limit.

What are the guild's objectives?

Aside from becoming a stand alone entity in RP, PvE and PvP, we want to spread across the Alliance RP community and be a beacon of hope, stability, camaraderie to those who see us. Eventually we want to host all sorts of events for Wyrmrest Accord to promote those aspects and return Wyrmrest Accord to its former glory.

We will be doing this by giving out adventurer packs, hosting crafting fairs for people to get goods on discount, as well as providing RP etiquette and character development classes, fairs, story nights and other similar events.

We welcome both veteran and inexperienced roleplayers who are willing to learn and adapt. The guild has been designed with RPers in mind. We cater to many roles, jobs and styles and themes of roleplaying.

What else can you tell me about the guild?

I could go on telling you about the guild all day. I could go into great detail of my plans for it, what we want to accomplish, etc, but I can only post so much here. Instead, I will direct you to our website (which is manditory to join prior to be invited to the guild)..

The original post is nearly maxed for characters so I'm posting this down here.

The Threee Organizations

The Kingsguard is the restructuring of the Stormwind Guard. Eventually we plan on having patrols set up around Stormwind and its surrounding territories - Elwynn, Redridge, Duskwood and Westfall. Our guards will have thorough training on how to go about roleplaying the role properly and with etiquette. We want them to do their job but not disrupt anyone's roleplay session, within reason. We also want their interaction with the community to be fun, engaging and memorable. In the future we will be offering the same training to those who roleplay as freelance guards, making them unofficial members of the GRI.

Recently this organization as been given a section of SI:7 agents who fall under the Captain of the Guard.

The Kingsguard is only for humans and Duskwood based Worgen. This is due to the fact that Stormwind needs to prove that it can still stand on its own and protect itself and its territories without having to supplement its defenses with guards from other nations. If we can't protect our own, how are we expected to protect and lead the Alliance?

However, SI:7 is free to join no matter the race as it's an intelligence agency made for the Alliance. If your character is an agent, informant, likes undercover work, investigating, etc, then this branch might be for you.

The Lionheart Corps is a joint special operations force that was designed to be quickly deployed and to finish their task no matter the cost. Those who prefer a more militaristic setting or enjoy mercenary work should look into this section. It is made up from all members of the Alliance.

Additionally, currently all Shadow Priests, Death Knights and Warlocks are being directed to the Lionheart Corps, though they are given a case by case outlook. This was implemented to keep the ones who could potentially harm or corrupt Stormwind away from the main cities but keep them useful for the needs of the Alliance. Plus, what happens in the wilderness....stays in the wilderness...

The Civic Services recently had a complete overhaul. Originally, it was a cluster of citizens, allies and just general non-military/guard roleplayers. That's still the case, but now it's a lot more organized. It has been broken down into four subsections, each with its own leader (some roles are still vacant) and possible RP events. These are the Arcane Sanctum, Cathedral District, Embassy and Merchants Guild.

Essentially, if your character is not wanting to be a guard or join the Corps, the Civic Services has a place for you.

What we're currently in need of:

Although anyone can join in and join any faction/section that they want or that will suit their character we are in need of the following.

Guards - Both for Kingsguard and Cathedral Paladins
Ambassadors - We're looking for roles like Vindicators, Sentinels/Druids, Dwarven Thanes/Clansmen, etc to fill these roles. Though anyone from the other Alliance races are welcomed. They will be able to host events which brings people out of Stormwind to experience other cultures.

Some character jobs we'd like to see join:

Bakers
Artisans (Blacksmiths, tailors, leatherworkers, etc)
Flower Girls (Or boys...if that's your thing)
Shopkeepers
Tavern Workers
Tradesmen
And just about anything else you can think of!

Basically the CS is designed so that you can still RP your character the way you want but still be part of the initiative as a representative of your faction. If your character doesn't have an archtype the CS should be considered if you just want to be an ambassador or want to work around Stormwind. For more details and examples please send me a message in game.
