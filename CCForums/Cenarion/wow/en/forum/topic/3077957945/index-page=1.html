The devs are currently working on tanking. They're updating the DK tanking mechanics in such a way that they can port the features over to the other tanks so they can introduce a brand-new take on tanking.

Basically they pinned the failure of the LFD on a few things, the biggest being the difficulty/demand of tanking, which through natural selection, pared down the pool of willing candidates to a mere handful.

They identified the need to make massive, sweeping changes to tanking to make it as accessible and enjoyable (read: less stressful when dealing with bad dps/heals) for more players. Most people who like to dps consider it fun and do it because it's fun. Far less tanks thinking tanking is fun and more probably do it out of necessity or convenience.

Disclaimer: This is all speculation, but after almost six years this stuff isn't hard to decipher. Be on the lookout for GCs next Watercooler about a new tanking paradigm and how tanking will soon become the domain of any class with a dps spec.
