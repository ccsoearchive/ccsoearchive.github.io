I'd like to make a compendium for the financial (and e-peen) benefit of all Rogues.

Please list all old content you can farm, what spec you use (and if it makes a difference), a link to your armory, any special notes on particular bosses (eg. the difficulty, certain abilities that require mention, consumables used), how long it takes on an average no-death run and how much gold you make

Also looking for links to resources and youtube videos showing bosses/dungeons/raids etc

Ultimately this will hopefully be usable by all rogues as a benchmark and a way to determine suitable content farming strategies and a way to generate some weekly gold


The Spec
http://wowtal.com/#k=k1t7r3tzP.aei.rogue.3ySrJw
Combat 7/31/3 is arguably superior for a few reasons:
Imp Recoup, Lightning Reflexes and Reinforced Leather for survivability
Additionally combat excels standing face to face with the mobs as you already have a high EP value on expertise (so stack it) and such will have far less impact than other specs. Assassination has the benefit of Deadened Nerves but on the downside some bosses are immune to poisons.

Glyphs
Prime - AR, SS, SnD
Major -CoS, Evasion, BFlurry
Minor -Distract, Blurred Speed, Safe Fall

Additional Info
Mending enchant on both weapons, probably not needed for some content but good to save your older weapons and throw these on them for your farm suit

Reforge: Expertise will have a very high EP value as you are a. combat and b. facing the bosses so any boss with parry haste can be an issue.

Rotation is to keep Recup and SnD priority, dump excess cps into rupture/evis. (Thanks Fadedvici)

SSC
-Hydross
Value: 250g
Notes: Simple, tank and spank until the debuff starts to hurt (50%), then pull him out or back into the beam to make him change damage types. Each time you do this he spawns 4 adds but they go down easily. (Source: http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/811748-Rogues-vs-old-content-V2)

-Leotheras the Blind:
Value: 250g
Notes: Soloable, just save clos for demon form chaos bolt spam, and save dps cds to burn him down from 15% when he splits. Mirror of Broken Images from TB rep is decently useful for this, syncs up nicely with his demon form. (Source: http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/811748-Rogues-vs-old-content-V2)

-Lurker
Value: 250g
Notes: (Thanks Stardragon)
1) Stand just inside the water where he is, while still on the wooden platform so you don't aggro the frenzy. Getting closer to him like this means you won't be knocked off the platform when he Whirls, you'll just land on the platform and you can run back into position.

2) When he does spout, simply keep behind him and continue to DPS. Should you be caught in a bad position, it is cloakable, or you can use a teleport like Killing Spree to save yourself. The main reason you don't want to get hit by this is it knocks you 100yds away, often landing you in the water. You waste a lot of time swimming back and getting eaten by frenzy, and he'll be spamming 10k Frostbolts at you all the way.

3) Periodically he will submerge and spawn 3 sets of adds, 2 ranged and 1 melee each. You want to kill them asap so you can get back into position when Lurker surfaces again. If you're late though, he'll usually start off with a Spout so you can cloak through it on your way back to the platform.

PS: Glyph of Blurred Speed highly recommended.

TK
Magtheridon
Value: 500g
Notes: (Thanks Kamikazestab)
-Don't try to burn the adds straight up, wait for them to gather
-Use blade flurry when killing them, and make sure it is hitting another channeler
-just keep recup and snd running while blade flurry is on cd
-Killing Spree does not help vs adds, it just messes up positioning
-Feint ftw vs mag

Kazzak
Value: 500g
Notes: You can cloak the first and third (should be off cd by then) Twisted Reflection, saving Evasion for the second one to minimize his chance of hitting you and healing himself. Even with the debuff, he hits slowly enough that it will keep his health roughly neutral with your DPS (I was doing about 10k), so you'll whittle him down eventually (Source: http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/811748-Rogues-vs-old-content-V2)

Got a little strategy that usually lets me clear Chess on the first try (about 80% success rate for me), though I usually skip Chess + Malchezzar for my Kara clears now since I'm rather rich and I don't feel it's worth the time spent (Chess drops 0 gold btw, only epics).

You have a good amount of time before Medivh sets fire to any squares, so use it to move a couple of pawns in front of your king and queen forward, they make good diversions and give you room to move the King if you're on fire. After that I just hop in the King and start cleaving stuff attacking the pawns.

Regarding Netherspite, he is actually rather easy to solo. Simply tank him against the far wall/window, directly opposite the door you came in from, with you inbetween him and the door. In this position you will absorb all three beams, you don't even need Recuperate.
