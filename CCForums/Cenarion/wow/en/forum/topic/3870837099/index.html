PUBLIC NOTICE

The New Year - the Lunar Festival - this is the time of year for celebrations no matter what calendar you use.

The AAMS, in its grand tradition of multicuturalism, has simply split the difference. We are scheduling our public end-of-year party between the different calendars' new years.

JOIN US THURSDAY, JAN. 19th FOR A CELEBRATION OF THE OLD AND THE NEW!

Horde and Alliance are both invited to join us at the Caer Darrow Renewal Project, located beneath the ruin of the Scholomance on the Isle of Caer Darrow, beginning at 7:00 PM on Thursday the 19th.

Refreshments!

All the finest food that can be put on a stick, freshly imported by our Same-Day Freight Service.

Entertainment!

Hear the Shortest Speech Ever delivered by the AAMS CEO. See the awards for the year -- who had the Nicest Smile? The Most Dangerous Delivery? The coveted Worst Customer Award?

Better yet, return the attached form (see below) with your very own award categories and nominees. We'll hand out all the awards on the night of the party!

Big Prizes!

Ever fancy yourself a new motorbike? How about an exotic pet? Enter our Year-End Sweepstakes to WIN! (( http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/3870807110 )) Drawing will be held on the night of the party.

...and Much, Much More!

Will the AAMS unveil their latest in message-delivering technology? Will the entire staff be forced by administrative decree to wear a silly hat? Will it just devolve into a Horde vs. Alliance bloodbath at last? There's only one way to find out.

Join the Anytime & Anywhere Messaging Service in welcoming the new year -- and bidding a fond farewell to the old one -- at Caer Darrow on Thursday, January 19th, starting at 7 PM!

Peace, by any means!
