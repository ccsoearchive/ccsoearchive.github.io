# What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]

PVE at the moment.

# If PvE, what type of PvE? [Heroics/Raids/Other]

Primarily 10-man Raids.

# If PvP, what type of PvP? [Arenas, BGs, Rated BGs]

I used to arena a fair bit. Cata BGs have been pretty awful.

# What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.

Mostly Throwing Weapon requirements on FoK I guess. It's a bit annoying given Throwing Weapons have had inferior stats to bows each tier thus far. Having to weapon swap to AOE and such. I suppose you could also count the crippling damage loss we have to take to use any of our utility (Non-damage poisons, EA).

# What makes playing your class more fun?

Being able to be the DPS to bring when you absolutely have to kill something was pretty fun in the past. Past that, the rhythm of getting into a fight can be pretty neat. Being able to survive things nobody else can with exceptional timing has always been a nice perk as well.

# What makes playing your class less fun?

The poor target swapping and heft ramp-ups that we've had this expansion are pretty tiresome. My survival cooldowns working on some fight mechanics and not others is also a bit lame.

# How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)

Mutilate's rotation is overall okay--could probably use a bit of spicing up, but not bad overall. Combat's is just plain boring. There's no timing or really thinking about it, just hitting Sinister Strike entirely too much with high levels of haste. Energy capping is horrible.

# What’s on your wish list for your class?

-Removal of Bandit's Guile and a return of Combat being the spec for quick target swapping. It was a nice niche to have. And in general lay off on the ramp-ups and penalties on swapping, these are not parts of the Rogue class in any expansion but this one.

-Poisons revamped. Not only do they do too much of our damage, but they lock us out of too much of our utility at the same time. Instant Poison could be removed altogether and replaced with a choice between Crippling, Wounding or Mind-Numbing (all doing the same damage)--additional poisons could even be added. A Vampiric Poison, for example, would be a much more Rogue-like form of self healing than Recuperate ever will be.

-Along with that find a way to make EA not such a ridiculous DPS loss. Heck, bake it in to Rupture.

-Combo Points on the Rogue. Should've been done this expansion already.

-More active damage, less passive, higher skill cap. It sincerely doesn't matter that our passive damage is triggered by using specials--it is still passive damage that requires sitting in one spot autoattacking. Rupture is the easiest thing to buff, but really anything would be nice.

-I want Grappling Hooks. Anything that makes us more like Batman is a good thing.

-Speaking of? I would like cloaks that cover more. You have to redo player models one of these days anyways, so give us some nice cloaks while you're at it. Capes aren't very rogue-like.

-More uses for the sneakier aspects of the class. Give us a reason to sneak around in instances/raids once in a while. I've always thought it'd be neat if mobs in instanced areas could be pickpocketed for special consumables. Stuff like being able to sneak around and distract/sap things to make life easier has always been fun, but hasn't really been a part of our core gameplay for a long time.

-A more tenuous PVE rotation. Playing Combat in TBC at the upper levels was all about trying to eke out every single rupture tick you could--with just SS, SnD and Rupture the rotation was more involved than Combat or Mutilate these days.

-Something to do at range. Deadly Throw being spammable (can even remove the pointless slow), or even Throw being useable while moving would be nice.

-I really wish Crit would become a viable stat again. It feels like it should thematically be a nice Rogue stat.

-More rogue-like survival stuff. We shouldn't survive PVP because of self-healing and damage reduction, we should survive by avoiding stuff, and being slippery and annoying. Vanish is a good rogue survival tool, very Roguey. Combat Readiness and Recup? Those are basically Warrior abilities. How about something that lets us dodge while stunned? Unconcious Evasion or something. Would be much more Roguelike anyways.

# What spells do you use the least?

Expose Armor. Don't even have it on my bars anymore.
Deadly Throw. Terrible. I have this on my bars only because I use it when I am pissed off that someone is kiting me so I throw at them to vent my rage.
Combat Readiness. Should never have really been in the game to begin with, and it effectively isn't now with the shared CD.

What type of content do you focus on (PVE, PVP, Both)
I do both, often every day, but I'd say I actually focus on PvE. PvP isn't fun (or balanced) enough for me to give it my full attention.

If PVE, what type of PVE?
Raids, hard modes, and pretty much anything else in game with an achievement attached. I only step into a cata heroic if for some reason I'm lacking some VP.

If PVP, what type?
Mostly BGs. I love RBGS but I can count on one hand how many groups actual do them on my server. My guild actually ran a great group but it ended in too much rage so it fell apart. I can't stand arena and use it mostly as a means to an end (CP).

What are your biggest quality of life issues?
Not a whole lot to complain about here. We got a lot of nice stuff in cata including a 1 min sprint, recuperate, and a run-speed stealth. Down time on the rogue while soloing basically doesn't exist anymore. A lot of solid QoL changes indeed. I'd love to see poisons last longer than an hour though. With all the other things we need atm I don't really like asking for such things, but if you ARE asking, manually applying poisons every hour and having to buy more from my mount gets kind of annoying.

Reading another post reminded me: Expertise for mutilate. It's a huge, huge, huge quality of life killer. I would love to be balanced around *having* expertise, instead of not having it. Many of the bosses in cata have been a recipe for absolute frustration due to them facing us. Even when I'm behind a boss I can get two very unlucky dodges and it leads to a blown rotation and massive amounts of energy capping. Melee need expertise, and mutilate should be no different, unless of course you get rid of the need for expertise altogether.

I agree with other posters too, if thrown weapons are really meant to be more meaningful than stat sticks, they need to actually be more meaningful than stat sticks. As you can see I use a heroic bow because what it does for my single target dps is very significant. It's HUGE pain in the butt right now for me because I have to use my macro to switch my thrown back in any time I want to FoK. This invokes a GCD and resets my swing timer. The FoK change was not a good idea. Thrown weapon stats need to be brought up to other ranged weapon stats.

What makes your class more fun?
Stealth has always made the class fun. I love not having to fight every living thing on the planet in order to reach my objective. Having a resource that never truly runs out is great for soloing old content and the like. Feint is a cool ability to me because it feels meaningful to negate so much raid damage. Heroic mode damage gets pretty out of control, and I feel like I'm contributing a lot just by not taking damage. It's feels "smart".

What makes your class less fun?
Unreliable stealth, and it's a huge problem. You can only be warrior charged out of stealth so many times before you /throwchair. It's very hit or miss in cata, with stealth being reliable most of the time but still having plenty of "WTF!" moments. I'd love to get stealth increasing talents back. It's been way too easy to find rogues this expansion. Also sitting behind hybrids in dps is never fun. I ditched my 19 warrior and rolled this pure five years ago because it was supposed to be *just a little bit better* than hybrids when it came to dps.

How do you feel about your rotation?
My mut PvE rotation is ok I guess. My spec was made more interesting, but with that came a whole lot of problems. I'd prefer more energy to do more abilities than to wait around while my energy pools/my envenom buff does it's thing. I love having an "execute" phase, but I hate having backstab be my button to pull it off with. Argoloth, Chimeron, Al'akir, and Rag all refuse to play nice with such an ability. It's not fun to not be able to backstab because a boss turned to shoot a laser. The ability is necessary for pvp, and I don't wish to see it disappear, but I can't control a boss like I can control a player. I sincerely hope backstab is replaced by another ability in the next expac for my "execute" phase. Shiv could work, but I'm not opposed to just a "stab" ability.

Also, it shouldn't even need to be said at this point, but mut's ramp time has been insane pretty much all expac. Nothing, and I mean nothing has caused me more frustration in Cataclysm than mut ramp time. Even worse, should I have to switch targets and my stacks fall off my main one? Oh the horror.

What's on your wishlist for your class?
Most of the things I've covered: Expertise valuable for mutilate, reliable stealth levels, a new or different ability for mut's execute phase, and a massive increase to Deadly Poison proc rate (or lowered required stacks). Wrath aoe (doesn't require a thrown weapon) should probably come back into play.

What spells do you use the least?
Distract and Expose armor. I'm not bothered by this, sub already has enough to do in pvp.
