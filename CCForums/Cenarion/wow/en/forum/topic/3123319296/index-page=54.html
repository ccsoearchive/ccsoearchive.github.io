What type of content do you focus on?
- PvE, some PvP

If PvE, what type of PvE?
- 10 man Normal/Heroic raids, Heroics, Solo

If PvP, what type of PvP?
- BG's and limited arena

What are your biggest quality-of-life issues?
- Keeping stacks of poisons in my bags. A spell to apply them would be nice.

What makes playing your class more fun?
- Doesn't seem to be an overly popular class that every person plays one
- The stealth factor
- Overall feel of the class as a damage dealer
- Combo point builder/finisher system
- Energy as a resource
- The rogue community seems to be more tight-knit than other classes, i.e. I've never been scoffed at for asking advice from another rogue, whereas any other class responds with EJ or L2P.

What makes playing your class less fun?
- Consistently not being able to pre-pot due to stealthed Tricks pulls.
- Having to rely on a spreadsheet for every single gearing option.
- Swapping specs/gear/reforging for a particular fight over and over, or between Mut and Combat depending on which performs best for an encounter.
- Synapse Springs triggering in a macro pulling me from stealth while right behind Domo. :(

How do you feel about your “rotation”?
- Mut feels fairly smooth, just a little too easy to maintain. Target switching is an issue with ramp-up time being so long. Feels a little spammy with only one builder and two finishers. I know poisons are the overall gist of the tree, but should they really contribute 50% of our total output? Mastery stacking is beginning to feel like Combat ArmPen all over again.
- Combat feels even more spammy, almost hectic at points trying to not cap energy. Rupture is basically useless. Bandit's Guile needs reworking, takes way too long to ramp up after a target swap, and usually doesn't even get utilized on fights where adds die in seconds. Restless Blades is a nice talent, although sad that my 31-point talent is almost situational with current content, and sometimes too dangerous to use at all, like on a dps burn style fight Baleroc. The damage for it is horrible, even timing it with maximum buffs/procs.
- Sub is very difficult to maintain in PvE, but offers great utility and control in PvP. Still feels like a gimmick spec for raids.

What’s on your wish list for your class?
- Reworked Killing Spree.
- Poisons as a spell not a consumable.
- Deadly Poison stacking revamp.
- Combo points stored on player instead of target, the way it already works with SnD and Recup.
- Combat Readiness baked into Evasion (normal dodge buff plus minor dmg reduction if hit).
- Vendetta being a player buff rather than a target debuff.
- Smoke Bomb having more use outside of PvP.
- Reduction on major glyphs. Many are so situational they don't even justify using.
- Ninja costume, perhaps a glyph to change appearance?
- Reworked Redirect and/or better target swapping for all specs.
- Shadowstep or a form of it as a baseline rogue ability.
- Prep for Combat/Mut, throw it on a 10 min cd, but it would be nice to have in emergencies.

What spells do you use the least?
- Combat Readiness - Not using this to waste a Cloak.
- Expose Armor - Always brought by another class that can apply the debuff easier.
- Shiv - Very situational for removing enrages, mostly Arena.
- Throw - Mainly because of better stats being found on bows.
- Deadly Throw - Situational in Arena.
- Rupture - Literally only good for keeping another rogue from restealthing.
- Pickpocket - Now that 359 Thrown are obsolete, no incentive to farm lockboxes.
- Wound/Mind-Numbing Poison - 10% healing reduction is useless with health pools reaching over 200k now.
- Disarm Trap - Last time this was fun was in ICC.

Poisons could be a pick-list/separate tab on abilities page for Main Hand, Off Hand and Ranged

What makes playing your class more fun?

Playing a Rogue as its role was truly intended. Which is to be an overpowered assassin. If we are to be disliked by the general WoW population then allow us to be truly despised.

What makes playing your class less fun?

Becoming Level 85. New skills from L81-L85 have provided Rogues with minimal functionality. Here is an example of what Mages gained from L81-L85 compared to Rogues.

Rogues
L81 Combat Readiness Minimal Utility
L83 Redirect Minimal Utility
L85 Smoke Bomb Minimal Utility

Mages
L81 Flame Orb DPS Increase
L83 Ring of Frost Major PVP function
L85 Time Warp Serious Raid Buff

2) Complicated/Unforgiving Rotations. A missed rotation or 2 could drop us way down in damage meters for the raid.

3) Removal of stuns. Stuns/Fear/Frost/Snares have been provided to all classes which has removed the general advantage Rogues used to have in PvP. The equalization has made much less fun to play a rogue.

4) Survivability In PVP. The increase in hit points has made it almost impossible for a rogue to kill most classes. Even after a stun or 2, it requires a lot of skill and technique to kill most good opponents. 1) Make Combat a Tanking Spec for Rogues. This would create more tanks and off-tanks for WoW in general and give Rogues a better chance to gear up and improve our overall population numbers in WoW.

2) Give us a Kill/Assassination Ability on a 3 or 5 min cooldown. Automatic Kill of any class (if stealthed and behind the opponent) not mitigated by anything. (Not to be used in arenas) but for fun and make up for the several rebalancing issues we face in our class in BG’s. But on a long cooldown as to not make it overpowered.

3) Give Raid Leaders a reason to bring Rogues to a Raid. Mages have Time Warp. Shamans have Bloodlust. DK’s have Hysteria. Create a Rogue Whirlwind ability - like 10% damage increase to raid for 10-20 secs.

4) Reduce the Kick cooldown from 10 to 5 secs to give us more interrupt utility

5) Make the Rogue class easier to enjoy. Remove cooldowns in general and reconfigure the class. Like if Slice N Dice is required for all PVE and PVP then just incorporate that into our basic skills. If it becomes mandatory for all rogues to maintain in our rotation then why not just make it automatic. Like the Blade Furry button give us a Slice and Dice button.


What spells do you use the least?

Distract, Deadly Throw, Combat Readiness (after shared cooldown) , Smoke Bomb, Expose Armor, Detect/Disarm Traps, Redirect, Shiv, Gouge

Tab target never selects the proper target. Always targets pets and minions first, when I should never have to target a minion.


There are times that targetting a pet or minion is useful. Perhaps you want to kill that earthbind totem? The game provides some targetting options to help you be more specific.

Background:
Tab target starts from your left front and cycles through the available targets within a given range. It's very easy to target the wrong thing when switching targets, so I use a macro. The full macro isn't relevant (I use some console commands to shorten the targetting ranges and change back), but 2 particular commands may help you:
/targetenemyplayer : This targets only players, no snakes, totems, pets, or other NPCs of any kind.
/targetlasttarget : The guy you were on before your target switch...

so my macro works fundamentally like this:
/cleartarget
/targetenemyplayer
/cast Blind
/targetlasttarget

Face your blind target, hit the Blind button and go back to killing the hunter. Be aware that this isn't 100% because there may be times that you wish to blind your focus (/cast [@focus] blind) or you wish to blind the target under your mouse cursor (/cast [@mouseover] blind), or a few others. The above macro will just pick the guy closest to you and blind him. If you need to blind a specific player (like when doing 5v5s), this may end up wasting your blind on the wrong guy. For 2's and most other PvP situations, it's clutch. I use multiple macros like this for the same reason.

Just be aware that mechanisms are at your disposal to handle these frustrations.

enjoy...

What makes playing your class less fun?

- Feeling underpowered against plate-wearing classes
- Not being able to switch between multiple targets and keep dps at a high enough rate
- Not being able to aoe competitively (rogues are a single-target DPS class in a world of AOEing)
- Being a slow burn class (can start off hot every few minutes, but overall it's slow to build up dps and it's a steady stream of low numbers (3k, 1.7k, 5k, 8k, etc) with not very powerful finishers (32k average, 50-60k if you're extremely lucky and we're talking PvE not PvP)
- Combo points, combo points, combo points
- Being out-dpsed by most other classes when single-target, slow DPS is the only thing you really bring to the table.
- Being a leather wearer in a world filled with plate and mail.

How do you feel about your “rotation”?

With multiple targets, our rotation feels broke due to the slow combo building. Other than that, it's easy and predictable. Feel more than a little useless against mobs immune to poison damage.

What’s on your wish list for your class?

- A true finishing move. Why do warriors get execute instead of rogues? That is to say, why wouldn't rogue have a move like this?
- A lower cloak of shadows cooldown again
- Reworked poison/combo system

What spells do you use the least?

- Shiv (only try to remove enrage and only then when I remember it)
- Expose armor (feels underpowered and someone else can usually do it better)
- Deadly Throw - our only ranged with any potential for doing damage, but usually only used to throw a knife at a blinked mage after an ice armor/ice blast
- Pickpocket - Nothing special obtained through pickpocketing (the drop rate for anything decent was way too low and now is out of date)
- Open lock - now lock boxes can be opened with other professions
- Disarm Trap - really only used for hunters and they're able to put them back down way too fast
- Distract - PvE only. Would be great if this works like distracting shot for hunters
- Redirect - this is only used to boost someone else's dps. Why not just give a buff instead?

Gear Scaling
Ramp up times

What makes playing your class more fun?

Having a lot of tools to deal with different abilities, cloak/evasion/feint/vanish (when it could dodge spells) were great because with good play we could abuse things others couldn't

What makes playing your class less fun?

Being energy capped and having to spam abilities with no taught. Rogues should have to time their abilities right with low energy regen so that they maximize their DpE. Right now we are merely hitting buttons to keep our energy low.

The difference between a decent rogue and a good rogue is extremely small, as long as you know the rotation you can play a rogue at pretty much 95% efficiency, there should be a higher learning curve on the rogue rotation.

We don't scale at all with gear, while classes can see a huge difference between tiers rogue dps is pretty much at the same level overall. This is actually so bad that we're getting buffed roughly every patch so we can keep up with others, our scaling needs some serious work.

How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)

All specs are currently unable to effectively keep their energy down in a rotation. We shouldn't have to clip the envenom buff every time, we shouldn't have to spam an ability every gcd and still hit 100 energy. Those things are not the way the rogue class was like, if i wanted to just spam abilities when its up I could just play a mana user.

What’s on your wish list for your class?
Better scaling
The poison system also needs a look at, just putting it on a dagger and forgetting about it is not that great, being more active with poisons would be great.
Better ramp up times (and as a result better target swapping)

Based on procs rogue dps also changes a lot. During 2 pulls, while doing same rotation, I pulled 33k dps, and 22k at the 2 minute mark for both (this is also on baleroc). The only dif between those 2 was RS, VW, and ruthlesness procs. A few procs shouldn't be able to make an over 10k dps difference. I should be able to consistently pull relatively similar numbers assuming i do similar things.

Better utility (a taunt into 100% evasion for 3-5 seconds for example would be great)
Better AoE

What spells do you use the least?
Smoke bomb
Combat readiness
Evasion (because i vanish for the extra dps boost, so I'm not high enough on aggro to use this)
Recuperate (it doesn't have a large enough frontload of healing, i find myself in positions where using it is either a dps loss while death isn't imminent, or its too late and the recup doesn't do its job)
Deadly throw
Expose armor (the dps loss is not worth it considering how easy other classes bring this)
redirect (poisons are a big part of our dps, while it looks good on paper our rampup time is still terrible even with this)
pick pocket
