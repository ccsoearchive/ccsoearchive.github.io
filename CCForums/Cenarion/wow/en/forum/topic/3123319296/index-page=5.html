    ■What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]

    PvE > PvP, but I enjoy both aspects of the game.

    ■If PvE, what type of PvE? [Heroics/Raids/Other]

    Raids when I have time, heroics if not.

    ■If PvP, what type of PvP? [Arenas, BGs, Rated BGs]

    BGs more than arena, even though I find arena more interesting.

    ■What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.

    For raiding, I would like to have some more utility and a reason for the raid leaders to bring a rogue. Currently, I'm only as good as my dps in PvE which can suffer from fight to fight with varying mechanics.

    For PvP, I'd love to have the old stunlocks back, but I think that's just being greedy.

    Also, just from an aesthetics perspective, I'd love to have fast OH swords back. I hate the look of sword/dagger combo.

    ■What makes playing your class more fun?

    PvE: I like the fact that we have a smaller number of abilities to use, but they all (more or less) serve a useful purpose. I can't say the same about my warlock where I don't use half the spells. I can't really think of much else. Topping damage meters I guess...

    PvP: Mobility, mobility, mobility. I love being able to swtich quickly between players, being able to keep multiple opponents locked down and frustrated. I don't even care if I do a ton of damage. I love being an annoyance. I like being able to get in and out of combat quickly, I just wish I could use vanish a bit more frequently!

    ■What makes playing your class less fun?

    PvE: Bosses that only face forward. DPS tanking because of parries/blocks. Inability to use certain ability (i.e. Killing Spree) because of boss positioning (just think of Rags here, but other bosses as well).

    ■How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)

    Mutilate: love it. I wish I had better daggers so I could play it instead of combat.

    Combat: I liked it better before revealing strike. I find this ability clunky and un-natural. Killing spree as I said before, is fun to use, but there are a few bosses where it's not possible which is a buzzkill.

    Sub: I only use it for PvP, but it's probably one of the most fun specs to play in the game (even amongst other classes).

    ■What’s on your wish list for your class?

    PvE:

    1. More raid utility
    2. An instant ranged attack that doesn't require combo point. Having to stop to use throw, or not having combo points to redirect/deadly throw is annoying. Even if you did have combo points, you can only redirect so often.

    PvP: Take combat readiness off shared CD with cloak. Bring back luffa pl0x.

    ■What spells do you use the least?

    EA, Feint (should probably use it more though), Shiv

    What makes playing your class more fun?

    One thing I especially enjoy is how active and involved our defensive abilities are. It's fun to pop Cloak of Shadows and barrel through a void zone, or chill in a stomp radius while every other melee has to get out. And, believe it or not Greg, popping Feint every time there's a meteor, or an electrocute, or whatever, is actually a blast.

    As for our offensive abilities, well...

    What makes playing your class less fun?

    +% Damage abilities and talents are boring. If I want to do more damage than Joe Schmoe, make me think about it, don't just give it to me.

    How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)

    Okay, here's what I like - I like that Muti is not a GCD-spam spec. A lot of people are all "oh, I don't like being energy starved as Muti" but I think they're missing the point. What I like about my rotation is that it's more of a rhythm than a rotation. Not clipping Envenom, knowing not to cap energy, knowing WHEN to hit the buttons as opposed to "oh god it's button hitting time which button do I hit" - that is cool. Please keep that in the design.

    What I don't like about it is that it's simple, dull, and kind of convoluted. I don't feel like I'm thinking enough. The only spells Muti really has to hit to do well are Mutilate, Envenom, and Rupture (with Vendetta on cooldown, of course) - nothing else has a big impact on our performance, and it feels lacking. Okay, yeah, actually using Rupture is great. And you threw us a bone with switching to Backstab for final 35%. You're on the right track - but making sure I'm hitting 3 instead of 2 after a certain health point doesn't involve much thinking.

    What’s on your wish list for your class?

    I want an element, be it through talents or otherwise, that makes me think more. I want to use two combo builders. I want something - random proc, maybe? - that makes one combo builder better than the other when active, or something that doubles our combo point pool, or gives us variable critical strike chance depending on how many combo points we have on the target, or - well, you guys are the game designers, you tell me.

    I want cool masteries. I was so excited when I heard about masteries and the talent rehaul with the intent of taking out everything passive. And then you throw us bonus poison damage, chance to proc bonus auto-attacks, and bonus finisher damage. Snore, snore, and snore - they all read "bonus damage" to me, and that does not change my playstyle at all. I find myself looking at Shadow Priests and Arcane Mages with envy - please, give me a mastery that makes me think more. Level 80 is very high level, and a very good time to add another layer of intrigue to the playstyle without intimidating newbies.

    Vendetta is a boring cooldown. Assassination could use a bursty, Killing Spree flavour ability - give it to us!

    I want you to listen to Aldriana concerning ramp-up time and Deadly Poison. Please. I could go over what he's gone over in his blog here, but I know you guys are capable of using Google and it'd be redundant. That man knows a lot about what's wrong with the class and you guys really should listen to him.

    Shiv. Expose Armor. Pick Pocket. Half the poisons. Gouge. Disarm Trap.
