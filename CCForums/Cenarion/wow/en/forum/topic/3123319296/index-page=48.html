What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]

I've done my fair share in both as a rogue, Combat PvE since Wrath, Sub PvP since Cata's launch.

If PvE, what type of PvE? [Heroics/Raids/Other]

Raider, generally only normal content though.

If PvP, what type of PvP? [Arenas, BGs, Rated BGs]

All three as often as I can.

What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.

I think poisons should have their own rogue specific container and last until cancelled, so they don't take up bag space, you don't have to invest in more poison, and you don't have to keep track of your poisons. Also I think there should be more incentive for using pickpocket, if that applies here.

What makes playing your class more fun?

Feeling like a swashbuckler, a killer, an agile, lightly armored combatant that trades thick armor for more freedom of movement, building up combo points, a visceral and fast paced rotation.

What makes playing your class less fun?

Poor sound effects on abilities, IE, the sound of a heartbeat while using Adrenaline Rush would be fantastic, targets dying too fast to use finishers, enormous penalties with a target switch, and stealth bugs.

How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)

I think it's fairly bland in combat, far too cooldown reliant, Adrenaline Rush for example can be extremely punishing to DPS if you lag for a few seconds, or if you need to suddenly switch targets. Not a lot of flavor, really, too much spamming.

What’s on your wish list for your class?

A built in sunder armor effect, expose armor being tied into combat at least so that we get a bit more utility, more diversity between specs, making Assassination more poison oriented, with an AOE ability that drops or tosses poison vials, perhaps, and Sub perhaps taking a more shadow and mystical tone similar to the Assassin's Shadow talents in D2, and maybe more kick oriented abilities in combat, maybe a kick that knocks the target back somewhat, or more abilities that display the martial prowess of Combat rogues. Insight was good in theory but it translated into the game as just another ability that I look at to time my killing spree. Maybe Revealing strike might add additional effects to the next finisher depending on what level of insight the rogue is at? Just a few ideas, regardless I think Rogues could use a total revamp, I think Slice and Dice is fairly outdated and should be replaced with something more interesting, OH and more ranged abilities.

What spells do you use the least?


Never use expose armor, pickpocket, Shiv very rarely since other classes tend to do that better, I DO use deadly throw and gouge, but not often.
