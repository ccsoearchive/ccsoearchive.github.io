BGs

What are your biggest quality-of-life issues?
Poisons not having a timer would be great.

What makes playing your class more fun?
Being able to move and jump around while dealing damage adds alot of character to the class. Being able to stealth and detect other stealthed characters better used to be fun (not anymore now that it's all baselined).

What makes playing your class less fun?
The inability to get back quickly into a fight after kiting/running from orbs etc.

How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)
Rotation seems ok right now, but finishers are rather gimmicky, and no longer hold the meaning of 'hard hitting finishers' anymore. The concept of building up combo points to unleash a hard hitter is no longer present.
Killing spree as a final last level skill is rather anti-climax. It often causes more problems in fights than it's worth.

What’s on your wish list for your class?
A better ability to quickly move back on target (sprint has a rather long CD, shadowstep isn't in a raid viable spec).
A unique raid buff, or at least something that gives a good reason to bring a rogue to a raid.
Removing combat readiness and cloak of shadow's shared timer.
Combo points building on me and not my target.

What spells do you use the least?
Expose armor, shiv, combat readiness (after the nerf)

What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]

PvP only.

If PvP, what type of PvP? [Arenas, BGs, Rated BGs]

Arenas with the occasional Rated BG. Normal BG's are just terrible.

What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.

Poisons in the bag. I usually carry around a lot of poisons so that I always have it ready, this means carrying 200 Crippling Poisons and 100 Wound Poisons takes up 15 bag spaces. While not a huge concern due to the current bag sizes, it would be nice to have poisons built into the actual interface.

What makes playing your class more fun?

Stealth. I love it. The stuns are also nice and with diminishing returns it doesn't seem so overpowered. Controlling a battle using my various abilities and tricky little Rogue tricks is also something that is really unique and fun, and I think it fits the rogue class perfectly.

What makes playing your class less fun?

The shared cooldown on Cloak of Shadows and Combat Readiness. The way I see it is this, a Rogue is meant to control the battle with our various abilities and tricks, while having so-so damage so we're not overpowered. It's all about control. Nerf our damage, and give us control and survivability. The enemy should be screaming because they can't cast anything or move while I'm on them, not from me destroying them with backstab.

How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)

Rotation feels fine, even if in PvP there is no "rotation" in a sense. However, from what I can tell, the rotation feels very, very solid. The only thing I would like to see change is have Rupture actually be used and used seriously. Right now, ticking at 800, I don't even think about using it, my Glyphed Hemo does more damage and lasts longer.

What’s on your wish list for your class?

More control and survivability while keeping competitive damage. Control & Survivabilty > Damage.

What spells do you use the least?

Redirect - It's great when I need to make a quick target switch and Kidney, other than that, I rarely use it. With a one minute cooldown I save it for the target switch kidney. Hemo builds combo points fast, very fast, so it never feels like I have a NEED for it.

Tricks of the Trade - Feels very clunky in PvP. Even with focus or target macros the ability just feels clunky.

Rupture - As I said before, very low damage. Using Rupture by accident usually leaves me crying and screaming at my monitor.
