# If PvP, what type of PvP? [Arenas, BGs, Rated BGs]
All of them, but i prefer arenas.

# What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.
Having to stack poisons on my inventory sucks. Perhaps making a poison tab for rogues only like the title and currency tab would solve the issue.

# What makes playing your class more fun?
High pace rotation, feeling that you're a deadly killing machine, Stealth.

# What makes playing your class less fun?
As a mut rogue, what really makes me feel bad is that the results of your work only show on logs and parses, not really in game. The only way for me to see big numbers are some 50k envenom when perfectly timed and my backstab spam in the end. Also too many finishers are needed to keep up when i rarely pvp(as sub, of course) so rogue have to actualy build up too much of it's burst. Also i miss fast one handed weapons in both hands as combat, and that's a big issue for orcs and humans, because we never get to use our race bonus as a rogue for expertise, because it won't cap if you're using a dagger. Also, the feeling of the Swashbuckler is lacking now because of the dagger usage. Perhaps making we use 2 slow weapons and making sinister strike/Eviscerate hit with both weapons would solve the issue. It would buff it's damage, you can tune down the poisons for combat and buff it for the other specs.

# How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)
As mutilate, it's fast, so fast that sometimes i have to clip a dot like rupture, because i play with over 300ms, and that makes my stuff hit a little bit slower than it should.
As combat it's fine. It's fast, and you gotta use everything you got. I just find killing spree a little bit too random, and i often prefer not to use it, because it can break a cc or something like that.

# What’s on your wish list for your class?

- I wish combat felt more like a swashbuckler, the way it was back in the day
- I wish i needed to build up a little less my burst on pvp. Sub needs too many finishers to be really effective
- I wish that results showed better on screen for mut.

# What spells do you use the least?
Expose armor, distract(nowadays), disarm trap.

What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]

PVE,
If PvE, what type of PvE? [Heroics/Raids/Other]

raids, dungeons and solo playing

What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could
be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.

I agree with some of the other posters about the poison issue. perhaps making poison a buff that you can put on your weapons as opposed to something you have to reapply periodically would be a sufficient fix. this still gives the option of choosing your poison without being in the middle of a boss and suddenly realizing your damage has dropped 20 percent because your poison has worn off. plus, it would free up bag space that has dwindled since i now have to carry more junk in my bags.

What makes playing your class more fun?

stealth is a lot of fun, more so in solo play than in a raid. being able to slip past mobs to kill a primary objective quickly while robbing them has always been one of my favorite things about playing a rogue. I like the smoothness of the rogue rotation without having to watch for procs and playing whack a mole.

What makes playing your class less fun?

I do think that one of the pitfalls of playing a rogue is the lack of bringing something extra to the party. being included in a raid either means that people have to be familiar with you personally as a player or you have to be very competitive on the damage meter. considering our damage output is based on smaller hits in quick succession, damage can vary between boss to boss if there is a lot of target switching and wasted combo points or just generally chasing down mobs or ads in boss fights as opposed to a ranged that can switch targets without restrictions of distance between them and the next target. perhaps a raid wide cool down ability like hero or even a defensive ability that helps the party mitigate damage would be a welcome addition and provide more incentive for rogues to be sought after.

one previous poster mentioned global cool downs and i think they make a good point. I think as a whole it is unnecessary because of energy management and only serves as a frustration when you are hyper spamming SS during hero and your energy bar never dips under 80 percent because of global cool downs.



How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)

I am a dyed in the wool combat rogue so I can't speak much for other specs. but as i stated before what i like the most is the smoothness of the rotation. I already have to keep an eye on combo points and energy regen so the relative simplicity is welcome so i can keep the other eye out for interrupts and boss mechanics. for those wishing for a proc perhaps it can be applied to killing spree for the combats. it would feel like a satisfying little bonus to be able to use it while the energy refilled and since you do have to be cautious of your surroundings before using killing spree I don't think it would overly effect balance issues. reducing the cool down on redirect would be excellent as i know rogues treasure their combat points. and removing the choice between battle readiness and cloak of shadows. I understand why it was implemented for the PVP'rs, but us PVE'rs have died countless times because we pop BR when things go downhill and then cloak is unavailable when they go completely swirly making vanish worthless.


What’s on your wish list for your class?

other than what has been previously mentioned the only other wishes I have would be game based. more traps to disarm would be fun and make us feel useful. class specific quests would be a lot of fun. we are rogues, go let us be thieves, assassinate targets be spies and be the ninja/pirate, ne'er do wells that we long to be.

oh, and every rogue needs a motorcycle. it is the roguiest mount we could have. =D

What spells do you use the least?

feint. I have found much better ways to mitigate my threat through ToTT and vanish

  • What makes playing your class more fun?

    • When I am a valued raider because my class is invaluable to raid composition, and in pvp, when I am preferred in a comp over a hybrid class, for instance, a feral.

  • What makes playing your class less fun?

    • When hybrid classes take the preferred spot over a pure-dps class such as rogue, this makes me unhappy. For example, I cannot choose any spec that does not do damage. I am a damage dealer, only. Therefore, I would assume that if played properly, I would do the highest damage vs hybrid classes or contribute something invaluable to the raid or arena team that would not be overlooked for a hybrid class. For PvP it is not much of an issue, since smoke bomb is a great tool and is often preferred over feral druids cloning. Though in raids with ferals survivability and incredible damage, a lot of low-end or non-heroic raiders typically pass over rogues to choose ferals. Perhaps rogues could contribute something useful along the lines of a druid's battle rez to make them more sought after in PvE raiding. Not saying we should get rez, but consider bringing back Tricks of the Trade threat generation, or something.

  • How do you feel about your “rotation”?

    • I am a fan of both rotations for PvE, and I think they are fine. Mutilate's rotation is simple. Combat's rotation is more erratic and fun, but requires more attention to detail and reactive skill. I would consider making Sub more competitive, perhaps adding some third tier PvE talents to it's tree, and adding fights which Shadowstep and Shadowdance would be preferred, and PvE situations where Smoke Bomb can be used defensively and offensively, and without using it, could create raid problems, to add more variety to the rogue's rotation, playstyle, and desirability in raid composition. I think the PvE rotations themselves are fine, and I don't mine Revealing Strike.

  • What’s on your wish list for your class?

    • Being able to refresh either recuperate or slice-n-dice in PvP, once it's up, Envenom style. Perhaps attached to another ability like Eviscerates refresh Recuperate to it's maximum duration. More usefulness for Subtlety in raiding environments, not just the survivability of the spec, but the abilities in PvE, such as Smoke Bomb, Shadowstep, and Shadowdance.

  • What spells do you use the least?

    • In PvE -- I never or extremely rarely use Expose Armor, Pick-Pocket, Gouge, Shadowstep/dance, or Smoke Bomb. Though all of these abilities are used in PvE, like Smoke Bomb on certain AoE packs, Expose for padding meters, Gouge on non-kickable interrupts... I find that these are definitely the least used in PvE combat. I also find it's generally a waste to use Recuperate in PvE. Perhaps that ability can find more PvE usefulness in the future.

    In PvP -- I rarely use any spec outside of Subtlety, therefore a lot of the non-sub talents are never used. It is a shame that mutilate received so many nerfs to mobility that it can no longer be viable in top end arenas or RBG's. Specifically, I rarely or never use Envenom in PvP, because it's never preferred to use Deadly Poison.

    Thank You for taking our suggestions seriously.

    What makes playing your class less fun?

    Energy capping and being forced into a GCD based resource model. This is really the only tragedy that can occur, in my opinion. Being energy starved isn't fun, but you can still make the same decisions around when to pool and when to deplete energy. Energy capping, on the other hand completely destroys the energy management aspect of our class, and we're forced to spam our rotation in order to use every GCD. I set my lag tolerance to 400ms (though my latency is only 100ms) because during these moments of energy capping, not using every GCD on cooldown is a dps loss.

    * Remove things like Tricks/Adrenaline Rush/Feint from the GCD so that we don't have to waste a GCD not spending energy.
