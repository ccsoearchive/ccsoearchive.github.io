What type of content do you focus on? [PvE/PvP/Both]
PvE

If PvE, what type of PvE? [Heroics/Raids/Other]
I do heroics for VP, and Baradin Hold each week for the VP and hopes of winning the leggings. I would like to raid Firelands etc, but it just doesn't seem possible for me to do that stuff anymore, despite the fact that I used to raid ICC and Wrath raids all the time back it WotLK.

What are your biggest quality-of-life issues? For instance, no longer requiring ammo could
be considered a quality-of-life improvement for hunters.
As far as quality of life goes I am very happy with the Rogue. The way we work is, in my opinion, very functional. Having to refresh our poisons every hour is not at all a big deal :P. 100% movement speed while in stealth was a superb choice XD.

What makes playing your class more fun?
I really like being able to sprint around while fighting bosses etc, especially as Combat; my Sprint is basically available whenever I want to use it. As a Rogue I just feel maneuverable and... functional. I love the way we deal our damages. Killing Spree is awesome btw.

What makes playing your class less fun?
I feel as though I am in the minority who values aesthetics. As a Combat Rogue not being able to dual-wield one handed weapons actually makes playing my Rogue slightly less fun. Now, I understand some of the reasons why you at Blizzard Entertainment decided to exclude fast-speed one-handed swords, axes, maces, and fist weapons from Cataclysm, but as a Combat Rogue I would be so terribly happy to be able to dual-wield a pair of swords (again).

How do you feel about your “rotation”? (Rotation is the accepted order in which abilities are used to maximum efficiency.)
I love my rotation. I play Combat without using Rupture. I love managing my combo points to make sure I have the right amount to do Slice and Dice at the right time and still fit in my Eviscerates. I love Bandit's Guile and waiting till it is in red before popping Killing Spree, using Shiv to get that 5th stack of Deadly Poison but only when I already have Revealing Strike up and I have 4 combo points on my target and it just feels right to do. Blade Flurry plus KS plus Fan of Knives for massive AoE, Blade Flurry when I am fighting just two equal-health targets and seeing my DPS numbers skyrocket. It is all very good.

What’s on your wish list for your class?
Number one on my wishlist is being able to dual-wield one handed weapons like I used to be able to. Second on my wishlist is every spec being able to use Shadowstep. Not a huge huge deal but.... I'm not sure you realize how amazing it would be to be able to use Shadowstep as Combat or Assassination. Give Subtlety a talent that reduces its cooldown lololol.

What spells do you use the least?
I do not PvP ever so I never use Gouge. I also never use Expose Armor. I don't use Rupture either but that is just my preference, I know some Combat Rogues will still use it.
