The following notices are posted in all major cities of the Alliance:

Come one! Come all! To celebrate the expansion of the Conclavengers! Conclave will be hosting a tournament of champions this next First Day at noon. Champions are asked to come forth to test their mettle in hand to hand combat.

Please contact a member of Conclave to sign up. Prizes will be awarded to champions.

Craftsmen and Merchants are encouraged to peddle their wares during this time.

----
OOC INFO:

Conclave just hit level 25 and to celebrate we will be holding a tournament in one weeks time on August 12.

What: Conclave Tournament
Location: Gurabashi Arena
Time: 12pm server time
Brackets: 1v1, 2v2, 3v3 5v5 and Battle Royal, New Bracket: Ridiculous
Prizes: Pets, gold and possibly mounts.

Please sign up here so we can get the brackets created!
