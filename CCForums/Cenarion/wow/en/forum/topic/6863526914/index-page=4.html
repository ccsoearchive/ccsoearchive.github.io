Only hit and expertise don't scale down, I would assume spirit works similarly in that the hit on your character sheet wouldn't change. Enchants and gems are important, of course, gem slots are a good value relative to item budget, which is important when your gear is being scaled.
The hit cap is no different than raids: bosses are 93 (raid boss level) and trash is 92 typically. So you need much less hit for trash than bosses.
