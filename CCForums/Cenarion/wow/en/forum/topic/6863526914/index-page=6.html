I think it's no secret at this point that having a DK, tank or otherwise, makes CMs a lot easier than without one, lol. Also just found out about killing Lillian really fast recently, to skip the rest of the fight. Huge timesaver!

I thought it was some sort of bug at first, but Voss does say "end it now, before he can"

Assume you'd need hero there for that to work, as the DPS requirement does seem high. I get close in LFD groups saving hero, but that's with better gear. Once in awhile I do pull it off. My question is whether the RP for transformation goes off at a set time, or it starts at like 50% or something.


It starts at 60%, and you definitely need heroism or something akin to it. Luckily, you have that. :P

I am not a big forum dweller, but I would like to thank Marza for the videos and tips, as well as the opportunity to talk on ventrilo. The videos were a nice way to preview the trials ahead, although we had a way different group composition and needed to make our own strats; but, that was half the fun.

On a more positive note, you don't need the "ideal" group to do these; just play to the best of your abilities and work as a team. Yes, I know, teamwork among players on wow. Shocking. The only thing I consider necessary is a heroism of some kind.

For anyone who is curious, we had:
    Protection Warrior
    Retribution Paladin
    Holy Paladin
    Windwalker Monk
    Elemental Shaman


We were a little melee heavy, and although a couple instances did not favor melee as much, others did. You learn to adapt and do what you can during those encounters. All in all, it was probably some of the most fun I've had on this game, and I encourage others to at least try them some time if you haven't yet.

Forgive the ramble. *bow*
