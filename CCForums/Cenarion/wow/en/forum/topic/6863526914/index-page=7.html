PvP Power/PvP Resil are no longer included in the stat budgeting of the item for its ilvl. That's why the PvP gear is a viable option. It is a lower ilvl than comparable PvE gear for similar content levels, which is why PvE gear is generally better for PvE. With Challenge Mode gear scaling, this is no longer a factor.

Your gems and chest enchant, however, are another story. Those would provide zero benefit in Challenge Mode. It probably wouldn't hurt to grab at least a 463 PvE chest, and possibly a PvE trinket to replace the Badge.

Yes as far as I understand PVP gear is equivalent to PVE gear if they are the same ilvl. PVP gear has full secondary stats and the PVP Power/Resilience are free extra stats to make the PVP gear superior for PVP. The major problem here is your PVP gems are worthless, including chest enchant, and your trinkets even moreso because they do not have decent secondary stats effects like PVE trinkets.

So what I would recommend primarily is replacing those trinkets. Other then that you can either change all your gems/enchants to maximize DPS or just roll with what you have. It's technically not necessary but it's definitely not optimal and will hurt your times. Personally I'd just get a PVE set and use that for CMs. A final consideration that makes me highly suggest a PVE set is you need to make sure you're Hit and Expertise capped for PVE. The cap for PVE is way higher than the cap for PVP so gemming/reforging all that PVP gear to hit the cap for CMs is a real waste for PVP.
