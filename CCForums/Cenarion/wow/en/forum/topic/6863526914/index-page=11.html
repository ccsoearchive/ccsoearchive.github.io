Replying to save OP's excellent post for later!

Edit: I realize I do have a few questions. OP, how quickly should one expect to progress through the times? That is, does it take a three weeks of 5-night-a-week attempts on a single dungeon to get down into gold? Or 5 nights? Or 1 night? I'm well aware that it's fully dependent on the group comp, group gearing, group skill, group dedication, and a whole other slew of factors. However, in your experience (for your group), how long did it take? Do you hear of other groups and how long they take?

I ask because I'm thinking about beginning the slow process of building a team to tackle some of these. It's going to be incredibly difficult to find people who are going to be dedicated enough to want to watch some of these videos, learn the fights, and then wipe/try over and over. I just want to know what I might be getting into.

Also, are warrior tanks pretty solid? I have to imagine a warrior with disrupting shout and shockwave to be a serious asset to a challenge mode team. At the same time, we don't have the kind of absorption and healing that deathknights do so we're pretty dependent on a good healer.


None of them are remotely as hard as your questions make it sound like you think they are :) Warriors are good tanks. They have a lot of interrupts and stuns as you've noted, and those are the best kinds of cc. At this point the knowledge of how to do CMs has disseminated enough into the userbase that golds aren't that difficult at all once you find some like minded people, and most of the classes have only received buffs rather than nerfs, making for some seriously easy runs (monks are really good for instance).
