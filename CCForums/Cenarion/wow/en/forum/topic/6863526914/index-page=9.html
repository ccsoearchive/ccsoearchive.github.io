I did all mine as prot because it was much easier to find other roles. Prot is my offspec, but I reforge for 15 exp and 7.5 hit for raids. I didn't respec or regem any differently for challenges.

Sockets will obviously be better since they don't get normalized, but other than that, gear isn't the deciding factor with these things. It's group synergy, practicing the pulls, and using your CDs. You'll need to figure out which pulls are hitting you like a truck so you can use demo shout, shield wall, demo banner, last stand, etc. AoE stuns rotating with others in your group is very important, as is knowing when to stand and tank vs when to kite.

Bottom line, gear won't make or break you. But you'll need a group that knows their CDs and how to use them with each other.
