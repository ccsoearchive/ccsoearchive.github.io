I just wrote about a very similar topic over on Blizzard Watch last week; you may want to give that a gander:

http://blizzardwatch.com/2015/03/22/encrypted-text-do-secondary-stats-really-matter/

However, the short answer to your question (how much of each secondary stat you should have) is: There is no hard "cap" for any of your secondary stats. For instance, if you're Combat, there is no point at which multistrike suddenly stops being valuable.

Every one of our secondary stats is valuable. When it comes to gemming and enchanting, the question we need to answer is: Which of our secondaries is a more valuable than the others? The column I linked above explores that question in more detail. But if you just want to know which stat is your "best" secondary at any given time, hop over to ShadowCraft, import your armory and poke the "Optimize Gems" and "Optimize Enchants" buttons.

Secondary stats can definitely seem overwhelming. The nice thing is, they're not important enough that you have to worry about them too much unless you're pretty committed to high-end PvE such as raiding.
