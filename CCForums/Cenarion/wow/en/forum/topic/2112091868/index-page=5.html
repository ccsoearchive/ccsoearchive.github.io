We could tell you, but then we'd have to kill you.

Oh, wait. We already did. OK then:

We've got a few abilities that allow us to move like the proverbial wind, but my guess is you're referring to a Subtlety rogue's bestest friend, Shadowstep (http://www.wowhead.com/spell=36554/shadowstep).

Shadowstep
25 yd range
24 sec cooldown
Causes you to step through the shadows and appear behind an enemy target. Your next Ambush or Garrote delivered within 10 sec will deal 30% additional damage, and your movement speed is increased by 70% for 3 sec.


Combat rogues also have a similar-ish ability you may have seen called Killing Spree (http://www.wowhead.com/spell=51690/killing-spree), which is also bunches of fun for us (and perhaps less so for you).

Killing Spree
10 yd range
2 min cooldown
Requires Melee Weapon
Step through the shadows from enemy to enemy within 10 yards, attacking an enemy every .5 secs with both weapons until 5 assaults are made, and increasing all damage done by 20% for the duration. Can hit the same target multiple times. Cannot hit invisible or stealthed targets.


Both abilities make it appear as though the rogue is far away one moment and then suddenly behind you the next. Killing Spree is perhaps even more impressive-looking in that it actually keeps going for a full two and a half seconds, with the rogue popping about and stabbing people repeatedly from behind. But Shadowstep is arguably the more deadly of the two. :)
