You heard correctly! Another month, another dwarven smashin', crashin', drunken, minglin', duelin', honest-to-goodness Clan Moot!

Lots of changes this month: with the title of Grand Thane dissolved, Clan Moots are now being cycled between the various guilds and organizations that make up the dwarven community. It's the Bouldershoulder Brigade's great honor this month to host the Moot on May 5th, 2015 in lovely Thundermar, at 7 PM PST! WILDHAMMERS REJOICE! Summons will be available for dwarves of all levels to attend.

We will be featuring a PvP duel tournament open to all who sign up before the cutoff date of May 4th, a great celebration and prizes for the champion, and of course plenty of time for the clans to simply socialize in the bright and positively dwarfy atmosphere of the Highlands. As always, dwarves from all realms are welcome, regardless of clan or political affiliation, to join in and meet more kinsmen, speak their new developments, desires, or concerns, and have a great time.

FOR THE DWAAAAAAARVES!
