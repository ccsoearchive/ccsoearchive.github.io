So, you're thinking of transferring to our server? Well, I hope you join us! Please look over this thread, which includes a brief glance over at our channels, our wiki, and our rp events!

To all Rpers, if you see this NOT at the top over any inflammatory or non-constructive posts, please give it a bump. The intention of this post is to keep it top on the post list as a shout out to any RPers who are thinking of transferring to CC. I want this to be one of the /first things/ they see, please, not anything else not related to RP or community related events.

For starters, I suggest logging onto your respective interested faction and joining our out of character channels, /allianceooc or /hordeooc and start off by chatting with us! On either channel, we are always welcome to new potential transfers and happy to answer any questions relating to concepts you may have, or anything about our lovely RP guilds. I really cannot emphasize this enough, because really, there are big differences between our factions, and we want you happy and you should be as informed as possible.

Alelsa wrote a very helpful guide on breaking into the RP scene on CC located here: http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/6308621370

The respective IC/RP channels are Hearthstone for Horde, and HearthstoneIC for Alliance. Both Ally and Horde essentially treat them as vocal means of communication through the Hearthstones.

For a more in depth post on our various channels, please go here:
http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/1083609464

If you are looking for a guild or a very in depth description of all the events that happen on CC, Araceli does a wonderful job maintaining a thread with all that information: http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/1072437309

If you're curious about our most active RPers and where we try to focus for RP related communication, I also highly recommend going to our RP wiki with the easy to remember name(The dotOrg) : CenarionCircle.org

For those of you interested in the down and dirty of our RP, these are our most active events. It'd actually be a help if people from Alliance side let me know of the most populous events you have that I missed.

Horde Weekly RP Events
Da Doctas Clinic, Tuesdays 7st, located by the portals in Orgrimmar: It is exactly what it sounds like, a clinic! Come have any injuries looked at by the well-trained Doctas, or drop by to keep them company in between seeing patients.

Weekly Fishing, Thursdays, 7st, location varies : Hosted by Dernes and Raoul, come be social and join us while hopefully procuring some dinner! See http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/5271778115 for specific locations.

Lounge Night, Saturdays, 7st, Hardwrench Hideaway in Stranglethorn Vale: Hosted by AAMS and Da Doctas, one of our free-for-all social nights where you can come and hopefully meet someone new, and meet up with familiar faces, all with a drink and great view of the water. Please note, every 2nd Saturday of the month, Lounge night is Lore night, and will be held in a different location and have a specific topic of the night, meant for in depth discussion and the open exchange of ideas. Refer to http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/5270833100 for specific Lore night information.

Alliance Weekly RP Events

Troubadour Nights/Pia Presidium Clinic, Mondays, 5st, Explorer's Library in Ironforge: On alternating nights, come and hear(or tell!) a story or get a patch up from your adventuring.

Feathers of Iron Tavern, Tuesdays, 6:30st, Booty Bay Inn in Stranglethorn Vale: Come in for a drink or a bite, stay for the company!

The Light & You, Wednesdays, 6st, garden behind the cathedral in Stormwind: Come and listen to a sermon, and discuss what exactly the Light means to you.
