Here is a a SUB ROTATION GUIDE FOR DUMMIES that I wrote for another thread. It's for the novice and beginner PVE sub rogues to get a basic understanding of the logic of a sub rogue rotation.

For someone who is having problems elsewhere, and for the more advanced rogue please see: Sub PvE Guide (4.3) Version 2.0 by Haileaus on the first page of the rogue forums! (http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/4139869493)

This guide only deals with the basics of the rotation. For macros, gearing, and other more advanced questions please see that same sticky.

Please also note, this guide assumes the use of the Rogue Tier 13 2-piece bonus.

THE OPENER:

It is always best to start off with a 5 Point Slice and Dice in stealth. To do this you can either redirect your combo points from trash, OR you can get your healers to proc Honor Among Thieves for you! Once you have slice and dice going, it's time to get on that boss! Use TRICKS OF THE TRADE, use your on use trinket ---> Shadowstep ----> Premeditation Ambush!

Look at that! You already have 5 combo points! Amazing!

Use these points to RUPTURE!

"But Ratatat, what about recuperate?!"

Well, Recuperate is important, but due to MASTER OF SUBTLETY, it is more important to get that rupture up within 6 seconds of your first ambush!

If you're pro, you can watch for a landslide proc and you can pot before rupturing! This rupture is going to stay on your target for the rest of the fight, so the more attack power you have when you put it up, the better. If it does not stay up during the entire encounter, you need to practice keeping it up. Remember, it refreshes with EVISCERATE!

So, you have Rupture up! Everything is going great! What's next? Well now you can RECUPERATE!

Recuperate increases your energy regeneration, and thus is very important. After you get your rupture up, you will begin using backstab as your main combo point generator! HEMORRHAGE will also be used, but only every 24 seconds because of GLYPH OF HEMORRHAGE!

So, after rupture, you will want to: Hemo x1, Backstab x 2. Let Honor among thieves get the rest of your combo points! At 5 points, RECUPERATE!

Now let's take a step back and look at what we have going on... we have slice and dice up, we have a rupture ticking, and we have a recuperate going! We also have a hemorrhage ticking! This is great!

Slice and dice is going to wear off next, so you will want to refresh that after your recuperate! To do this you will want to backstab x 3, and let honor among thieves get the rest of your combo points! Getting a feel for HONOR AMONG THIEVES is crucial to the overall flow of a subtlety rogue. At 5 combo points, refresh SLICE AND DICE!

"Oh, so there's a pattern?! I get it now! Next is refreshing that rupture, right?!"

WRONG! Or well, you're kind of right. But let's dance, shall we?

SHADOWDANCE:

Tricks of the trade should now be coming off cooldown, so use it and SHADOWDANCE!

Premeditation should be macro'd to your AMBUSH! So when you dance, your first ambush will get you to 5 combo points! EVISCERATE! Your RUPTURE is now refreshed! Cool! SHADOWSTEP and AMBUSH again! And again! EVISCERATE! Ambush again! And again! AND EVISCERATE AND AMBUSH AGAIN! THIS IS AWESOME! DAMAGE IS AWESOME!

Yes, yes it is. Nothing beats a good waltz. Or futuristic slide, or whatever you kids are doing now a days.

Regardless, your dance is done and your recuperate is about to come off. Refresh it. Now your slice and dice is going to come off in a second! Refresh that! Watch that hemo bleed! AH! MY RUPTURE IS ABOUT TO COME OFF! I DON'T HAVE ENOUGH COMBO POINTS TO GET IT UP IN TIME!

No problem, dude. It just so happens that your FIND WEAKNESS is just about to come off too. Vanish, and use PREPARATION to refresh your SHADOWSTEP if it is on cooldown! Shadowstep and use that premeditation AMBUSH macro! You now have 5 combo points and it only took you 2 seconds! EVISCERATE to get that rupture refreshed!! Saved by the bell.

What's next? Recuperate? Ok! Slice and dice after? Awesome! Find weakness is about to come off? Vanish again! Keep that rupture up! Shadowdance is almost up again! I have tricks up too! It's like blizzard designed it to play like this!

They did. It's a really nice fluid spec.

Keep practicing, keep watching thoses buffs and debuffs. The more you play it, the better you will get. Keep in mind, not everything goes as planned. This is just a basic guide for people to get a grasp on what things should look like.

Hope I helped! Please feel free to offer advice or to give me comments. If you see anything blatantly wrong, please feel free to correct me. Thanks
