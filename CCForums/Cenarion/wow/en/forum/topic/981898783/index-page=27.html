Personally I'd shoot for about 150k hp unbuffed before stacking mastery.

Your choice of gems may at times be affected by what you need for your meta, and what the socket bonus is. I can't think of any examples where you wouldn't want the socket bonus, but I wouldn't be surprised if some exist.

Yellow slots- mastery gem, or stam+mastery if needed for a meta
blue- stam+mastery assuming you're not stacking stam
red slots- parry+stam
