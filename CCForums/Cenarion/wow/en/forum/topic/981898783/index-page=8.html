Generally, in terms of rating, once you're at ~2kish(approx 10-15%) each for dodge and parry you'll want to be focusing mostly on mastery. The breakpoints are rather flexible depending on how much overall rating you have to work with though, so going through a spreadsheet if you're looking to absolutely min/max your reforging would do you well. AFAIK, a couple people have been making them, and I'll link it here when I see a finished one.

EDIT: This is mostly applicable at 85. At 80, just do whatever. It doesn't really matter.
