Math behind Thunderstruck (In my current tank gear):

Thunderclap
-----------------
1026 damage x 6% = 61.56 damage increase

Cleave
---------
1863 damage x 6% = 111.78 damage increase

Rend
-------
570 initial, 1060 DOT x 6% = 34.2 damage increase for initial hit, 63.6 damage increase for DOT

Shockwave
--------------
2478 damage x 10% (one stack)/20% (two stacks)/30% (full stack) = 247.8 damage increase/495.6 damage increase/743.4 damage increase.

Summary
------------
Thunderstruck's greatest threat increase lies behind Shockwave, requires a full three stacks for the optimal amount of threat, and still proves relatively lackluster. The passive damage increases Thunderstruck offers are hardly noticeable.

I'm still using a fast tank weapon, which may skew some of the data (but only in the case of Rend, as the rest are AP-based). It's still a poor talent.
