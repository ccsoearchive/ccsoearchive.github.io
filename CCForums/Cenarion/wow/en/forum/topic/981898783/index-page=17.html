Small tip.

You should change your Warbringer macro listed in the guide to...

#showtooltip
/castsequence [nohelp] reset=15 Charge, Intercept, Charge; [help] Intervene


[nohelp] instead of [harm], that way even when you are between fights and don't have a target, it'll continue to show the correct cooldowns as if you were in combat so you know how many seconds are left until you can charge again.
