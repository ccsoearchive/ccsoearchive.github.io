I see it as more of a litmus test.

Even back in Wrath when stam stacking was king, no one would claim that the group wiped because the tank had a +20 agi gem instead of a +30 stam. C'mon, that's 300 health, and bosses were hitting for 20k, there's no chance it would make a difference. Even multiply that by 5-6 gems, it still won't make or break the encounter.


But if you put the time in to make sure you're hitting the cap values, gearing appropriately, and enchanting your stuff, it means you probably take the role seriously. You've done the research, so you probably researched the encounters too. You're likely more reliable than someone who doesn't feel like rep grinding, so they use a PVP neck because "it's basically the same".
