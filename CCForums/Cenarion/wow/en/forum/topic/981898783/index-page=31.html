Reasons tanks suck:

Like you regurgitated, mastery > parry > dodge. Stamina comes on all of our gear, and you should not be gemming for any pure stamina, so that makes it kind of a passive stat. Gem Mastery/stam or parry/stam, but never pure stam.

Expertise and hit are not important as a warrior tank. You should be prioritizing your gear according to Mastery > Parry > dodge. Never ever make the decision of one piece of gear over another because of hit or exp. Always make your decisions based on the 3 main stats. That make sense?

So in answer to your question about the gear, choose the one with +mastery because mastery > dodge. The +hit is just a bonus, so to speak.

[p.s. I know people will disagree about the hit/exp prioritization, but you are not maximizing effective health by not maximizing your mastery, parry, or dodge. Since effective health maximization is the most popular and useful way of prioritizing your stats, I'm advising according to that method.]
