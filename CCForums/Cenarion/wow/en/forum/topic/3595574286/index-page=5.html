First off, who I am and what I do, I've been playing a rogue for what will be 5(6?) years in just a couple weeks, in that time the only time I haven't played sub is for one month when my guild made me raid as combat and another 2 months at the start of 3.0. I love the spec, I love the toys, I love how versatile it can be.

First what I think about the talents
Tier one:
I love the talent subterfuge, staying in stealth for 3 seconds after attacking, how can that not be amazing.
I also love how Shadow Focus combos with burst of speed (assuming its supposed to work like that).
And of course Nightstalker is reliable and good.
Tier two:
a good solid tier
combat readiness for melee defense, deadly throw for caster defense, and nerve strike which can be useful to combo with shadow dance in a multiplayer situation.
Tier three:
Another good one, all about defense, and with leeching poison, something rogues have been suggesting since the dawn of time practically.
Then there is Tier Four:
This one has probably gotten the most complaints. Shadowstep and Burst of Speed are good for different playstyles and specs, but prep has been a staple of rogue pvp for a long time. No matter what after coming from sub where you have sstep and prep rogues will feel nerfed. It also doesn't seem like that apples to apples comparison you said you were going for. Prep is extremely versatile and on a long cooldown while sstep and bos are short cd abilities that just cover mobility.
Tier five:
This tier is ok, good choice between Dirty Tricks and Deadly Brew, but if Paralytic Poison doesn't share a DR with our other stuns it will be a must have in pvp, and if it does, no one will pick it up.
Tier six:
Again, this feels like a nerf, having to go up to level 90 just to get the same abilities wer have now, it doesn't exactly feel right, especially when each of them are pretty specific to the spec they came from unless something is changed.


Now, I'm going to assume the tooltips and specialization bonuses aren't final yet as we don't have our elusiveness based cooldowns (a huge nerf in pvp), backstab and ambush are at 60 energy and sinister strike is at 45, there is no energy regen in the sub build, and there is no crit bonus for backstab or mutilate.

just my feedback on the situation.
