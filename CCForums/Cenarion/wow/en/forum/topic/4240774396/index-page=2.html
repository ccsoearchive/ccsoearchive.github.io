Name: Wallan Hardcastle

Age and race: 45 Forsaken

Reason for joining: Wallan was hung for his crimes and his body was carelessly thrown into a ravine in Silverpine. When the Forsaken scavengers came through they scooped up his mostly intact body and hauled him to Undercity. His reanimation was mostly uneventful until he started recovering his memories.

Eventually he remembered he was a very powerful warlock in life and his powers seem to have been left intact. Learning from his mistakes, Wallan was careful to keep his dark experiments to himself, enlisting the aid of a very few apprentices. He found willing students and very knowledgeable trainers in the city of the Undead. His efforts to find the knowledge of returning to his once glorious life style were encouraged by some and laughed at by others. He soon learned he could not rejoin the living, but his new "unlife" gave him a lot of benefits.

He was always right on the edge of discovery and the nervous tension often made him restless. He learned some spells and utilized some ancient scrolls to make him even more of a Shadowcaster than most would believe. The spells were fleeting however and he could not maintain the form he wished for more than an hour at a time.

His goal at the moment is to find certain souls that are adept at Shadow magic. He has an ancient tome that describes combining these souls into a certain crystal orb. The spell that can be cast with the aid of these captured and tortured souls will enable him to maintain a Shadowform for as long as he wishes. He is adept at convincing others to do his will, only those who are strong in the powers of the Light and have great faith will be able to withstand his efforts. He is not always successful, often his coaxing works against him as the strong willed and wary realize what he is doing.

His last foray into Alliance territory ended rather badly, he is now in Icecrown. Seeking to coerce a few more souls into the glowing purple Orb he has in the pouch at his waist.

History: Damion Ace was a name every young calf had heard tales of, the told of his accuracy unmatched by any other Tauren and of his fearlessness even in the art of melee. When leaving his home village, he decided to break his ties to the horde, he boarded a boat bound for stranglevale in hopes of joining a goblin cartel. Upon arriving in Booty Bay he was recruited for the bilgewater cartel within the week. He spent years upon the vessel, the maidens fury, until a young adventure convinced him to travel with him. He spent the next 6 years learning from the young warrior and in turn teaching the warrior. The grew to be great friends and amazing duo.
When one afternoon they accepted a job as to retrieve a stolen necklace from a night elf thief. He let the warrior go alone, he had other matters at hand. When the warrior did not return, damion grew worried. When the next day he had still not returned he went after him. The Tauren traced the warrior back to an old burrow and reluctantly entered. As he reached the burrow end he was ambushed and vigorously mauled by a Druid gone feral. He laid there and bled to death within the cave.
The Tauren opened his eyes and rose, what he saw was a factory of sorts, a factory for the forsaken! The Tauren scanned his body and seeing what marks the druid had done to his body. He was horrified and ashamed and he was mutilated. He ran, running into a large metalclad figure. He introduced himself as Arthus.
He explained to the newly formed death knight what he had become and told him the he had his eyes on this legendary hunter for a long time.
Damion soon made his way up the scourge ranks, till he became a valuable general and servant for the Lich king. He fought by Arthus side through till Arthus death, when Arthus died he tried to return to his to his village but was shunned and hated. He was confronted by a little blood elf girl who's father had died in service of the horde, but he had no answer for why he did what he did but he could only feel shame.
He went into seclusion in the far of lands of blood elf home.
He learned great bounds of shadow magic and excelling in it's ways, so when he heard of the blood elves journey he jumped at the opportunity for justification for the travesties he had commited
