The combined damage of bleeds and direct damage is way too much for PvP. I currently have ~3800 resilience and the average feral with full pvp gear and maybe 1 PvE trinket (Unheeded Warning seems to be the choice for ferals for alot of dmg) is 12k periodic damage from bleeds along with 19-23k shreds and 30-34k Ferocious Bites.


You imply you're getting hit or hitting a target with 3800 resil -- I'm seeing these numbers only on a level 85 dummy, in full 371 vicious, so that is probably what you mean by these numbers, you hit those on a target dummy. It's my gathering alt so I have the dps neutral professions of herbing and mining. I have 15.2k ap and about 5400 agi with UW (10 sec proc, 50 sec icd), 14,3k ap and 5k agi with the vicious badge of conq, 1700 agi on click for 20 sec every 2 mins. This is how a session with the target dummy looks like (tiger's fury was popped, 15% damage increase, when UW procced or badge trinket was pushed):

With UW:
rip tick crits: 10-12k
shred crits: 23-37k. Indeed quite the spike when UW procs
white melee crits: as much as 7.4k
Ferocious bite crits: 30k

With badge:
rip tick crits: a little higher but not quite popping to 13k.
Shred crits: 30k
white melee crits: as much as 5.3k
Ferocious bite crits: 35k

So although the shreds go up quite a bit in the top end with UW, the badge has a longer burst period and also affects the burst finisher by quite a bit. Nerf UW? probably.

The damage overall though in pvp when affected by resil of feral in general compared to classes such as a warrior or a mage seems in a pretty balanced spot. My arms warrior still hits freaking hard with far less setup in far lesser gear. I've seen MS crits of 42k nearly out of the blue. Against geared healers the feral needs more of a setup and a strategy of what moves to use, else that healer isn't going down. Warrior on the other hand can cheese a lot just by virtue of throwdown and charge.

Maybe the conclusion should be that damage is far to high in general for a lot of specs compared to the philosophy set out in the beta phase in pvp, but that's another, but very related, discussion. Don't know if there's a way back with the healing crit change either, because most healer specs are very strong at the moment. That resto druid isn't one of them probably does add to the problem of AHHGHHH MORE KITTIES ON MY FACE!! feeling right now.
