It seems some missed what was posted on the last page: http://raidbots.com/dpsbot/. The numbers are showing ferals as doing less than fury warriors according the the current parses. It should also be pointed out, Unheeded Warning was recently nerfed. It has happened already. We won't scale nearly as well with it since it is attack power based so we should be back to a reasonable state with higher PVP gear.

The comparison of bear form being equal to bubble is a stretch. When we take NO damage and can heal full then I will consider this a reasonable statement. The fact is, bear form requires talent points in armor which will take a way from important dps selections. If we don't allocate talent points into armor, we aren't buffed nearly as much as one would think and if we do take them, we will not have nearly as much damage output. We are additionally wearing leather so the modifications are necessary to make tanks compatible to plate wearers in damage mitigation. If you take this away, you will loose even more tanks.

As far as peeling, we are constantly being peeled in PVP. Yes, even our warrior friends get Intimidating Shout which fears us. They can, however get out of fear with their flavor of berserk (Berserker Rage) which we lost as of 4.0.6. Warriors also have Charge and Intercept which are quite effective at stopping us in our tracks.

All said, Konradcurze, the personal attacks are unwarranted. We are simply asking to be competitive as ferals. If we came into a thread about nerfing warriors into the ground and did that, particularly while mischaracterizing the state of PVP balance, I imagine you would be pretty insulted and upset.
