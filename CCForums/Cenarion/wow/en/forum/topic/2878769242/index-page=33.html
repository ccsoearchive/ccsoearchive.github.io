Disclaimer: I am a PvE bear tank, not a PvP cat, so i can not speak from extensive personal experience on this subject, but I do feel the need to point out a few things and ask a few questions. Also, I have not read all 33 pages of this thread, so I might be repeating points made by others.

The claim is that Feral DPS is way too high in PvP: Given that the most recent patch didn't give any real buff to Feral Damage (an estimated 1% increase after all buffs) and Feral PvE damage does not seem to be topping the charts, we have to ask 'why'?

The only logical conclusion is:
Feral DPS is NOT too high.
Feral BURST DPS is too high.

So, we need to nerf the burst while at least maintaining the overall DPS. I have a few ideas at the moment:

The Sarcastic Solution:
    Shift some of our Direct Damage to our Bleeds,
    Allow Ferals to shift out of roots (improving mobility to allow enough 'uptime' to get bleeds going), and
    Add a fear immunity to 'Berserk'


The Non-Sarcastic ideas:
    Convert 'King of the Jungle' to be a constant energy regen (2/sec fully talented) rather than a 60 energy every time Tiger's Fury is used
    Decrease the Duration and Cooldown on Berserk to reduce it's effectiveness in short fights (i know this has already been suggested in this thread)
    Nerf Berserk but compensate by stealing the 'Bear' version bonus: 'Your Bleed ticks have a chance to make your next Mangle(Cat) cost no energy.'

How can people really be QQ'ing about Feral damage when an AMPLIFIER is equipped (Unheeded Warning). If Ferals were doing in the ballpark of Arcane Mage damage without such a trinket, then they would need to be nerfed. However this isn't the case, therefore its Unheeded Warning that needs to be nerfed. As a matter of fact i believe that Ferals may need to be buffed or tweaked; let me explain:

Energy regain is garbage. Energy regen should be buffed either by talent, or some mechanic that allows savage roar to boast energy regen, much like recuperate.

Burst damage may be high with such a long Berserk duration, but likewise sustained damage is lacking. The fix for this has already been proposed; reduce the zerk duration, but also reduce its cooldown to 2 minutes. This will bring it in line with Zealotry, avenging wrath, etc. That would reduce burst, and increase sustained.

I don't find the insta clones from predatory strikes to be OP. It takes 5 combo points and a finisher in order to be able to cast it; which is alot of work as well as alot of energy to do. Not only that, it leaves a feral vulnerable in caster form, and reduces up time. If predatory strikes were removed (not sure why since they've had it for years), then the ability to shapeshift out of snares should be restored. The mechanic of removing snares by having to waste your sprint is really quite stupid.

Since we are on the topic of OP classes, we should be having serious discussions about Holy Pally's and Warlocks. THE FEARS- whats the cooldown on that OP CC, like 1/2 a sec (sarcasm). But really though, the bubbles and Fears have got to be nerfed.
