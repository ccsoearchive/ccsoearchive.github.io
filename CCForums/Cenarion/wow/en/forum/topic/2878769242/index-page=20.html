A mascot, that's what we've been for seasons on end.

One thing I will mention that does irritate me is yes, instant clones suck. Deal with it. Being chain feared because I can't break fears sans trinket sucks, but I deal with it. Getting chain rooted by a frost mage before he globals me with ice lances sucks, but I deal with it. Getting gibbed in a reckstorm sucks, but I deal with it because I've been true to this spec for too long to just reroll. Ferals were viable for a bit in Wrath, being the only class/spec that could keep up with the Highlord Frost Mage, but as soon as frost mages complained, suddenly a class mechanic that had been a staple of druidism in WoW since launch was removed. Locks popped up complaining about fear immunity and we lost zerk fear breaking. But you know what? We f***ing dealt with it.

We've been looked down on for too long and now that we're finally FOTM and skilled ferals are shining, everyone is out for blood. Granted, it does annoy me that all the work I put into getting good at feral is being written off to scrubferals cheesing their way to 2.2k but I deal with it. People have already mentioned how to stop us through cooldowns, etc. Personally I don't even use UHW anymore because skilled players know how squishy we are and will train us into a bear form cower, so I use a +resil trinket.

TL;DR: There is always going to be a FOTM, deal with it. Don't take away our tools just because people aren't getting their 2.2k handed to them. Work at it, theorycraft, practice against ferals in duels, watch videos. Don't just give up and gripe about us on the forums.
