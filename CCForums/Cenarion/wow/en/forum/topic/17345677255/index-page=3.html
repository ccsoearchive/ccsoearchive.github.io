Updates!

Site: After thinking about what Morteloff said about maps with long travel paths, I've been working on a solution for this. It won't be ready for a little while - still holding out for some better graphics. The next version of the code will probably be the "last" unless it turns out we need something else specifically. Once this one's done it'll support multiple maps, storyline highlighting, and will show a list of the storylines we've had along with short synopses of the events when clicked on them. My only regret is that because there's no custom database, it has to all be hardcoded into the Enjin module, making it less than accessible for others to add to the path. :/

Addon: I'm gonna post a screenshot of it later today, once I get a few more buttons added to the player interface. After chatting with Taly about it, the current plan is to have the DM be the only one who can manipulate the player's hitpoints, with the one exception to the rule being our free-hitpoint trait which would grant one hitpoint and become unavailable.

Events: The Winds of Fortune will be moving on from Lakeshire on Thursday, June 11. Taking a route up through Burning Steppes and along the eastern highway, we'll be heading into the Swamp of Sorrows and be making camp just far enough outside of Bogpaddle that the Goblins can't charge rent.

We'll also be having our first public event - On June 14th we'll be having a Caravan Campfire event, which is a general social meet 'n' greet event. If you want to get to know us, or just hang out outside of the major cities, join us at 8PM server time in the Bogpaddle inn.
