I made a macro, with my friends help, that allows us Hunters to Aimed shot! while moving. (instead of the macro buggin out)

WITH BARETENDER
1) Type /FRAMESTACK
-- You'll see a box pop up
2) Put a Aimed Shot on a separate button by itself and mouse-over that button, you should see something like <2> BT4 Button XX
3) Make the macro (In my case)
-- #showtooltip Aimed Shot
--/cast !Aspect of the Hawk
--/click BT4Button51

DEFAULT UI NOTE
-- You will see "<X> ActionButtonX" instead of "<X> BT 4 Button XX"

If you want to cast your instant aimed shot or even just hard cast an aimed shot, this macro will work perfectly.


i.imgur.com/fWCKr.jpg

You'll see my Aimed shot all by itself on the bottom bar, and the macro bound to number 5

Anyone know a way i can make room so that the pet abilities cast on your target if no mouseover exists?

Try this:
#show [pet:spider]Web;[pet:bat]Sonic Blast; Bad Manner
/petattack [@mouseover,harm]
/petattack
/cast dash
/cast dive
/cast [@mouseover,harm][] Web
/cast [@mouseover,harm][] Sonic blast
/cast [@mouseover,harm][] Bad manner


You could also consider removing dash and dive and just set them on autocast.
Or you could get the addons Bindpad or Super Duper Macro to extend macro limit beyond 255 char.

One more note is that your mouseover Web for example, will still cause your pet to abandon its original target and rush for the new Web target. I don't know of any short way to get around this except with /focus /clearfocus macros.
