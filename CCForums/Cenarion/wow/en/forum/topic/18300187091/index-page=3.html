With running AVs and such, they have a policy of "every night is open mic night."

This means that while you get some really cool leaders (Plebian, Majika, Zeeleraj, Sugfoot, Arceris, etc) you sometimes get folks who aren't the best. Thankfully most AV groups have different leaders every time so if someone leading really irks you, you can always /afk out and wait until the next one rolls.

I do recall we had a drunken leader go full metal jacket on us one night, and by the end of the first game the party I was in was 110% done and so were several others in the raid. When he stepped up to lead another round, a whole bunch of us just politely excused ourselves and went to the break room. We sat the game out. I don't recall if we didn't queue or if we afked out. But the AV team disbanded early for the night after that game.

Drunklead actually came up and chatted in the break room and folks politely mentioned that he was drunk and his behavior wasn't pleasant. I told him he was going full metal jacket on us.

The next day he posted a public apology on the Facebook group, which took an incredible amount of maturity and testicular fortitude to do. And because he had the courage and decency to give an apology, folks did decide to let bygones be bygones. He learned that he isn't a good leader when he's drunk, we learned that we should just tell him "Go home, ___, you're drunk" if he starts acting a fool like that.

You're missing some stuff like this if you're not watching the FB group. There's also discussions on how to deal with trolls and stuff, when Hydra was growing rapidly at the start of Ashran runs, there were some young guys hitting on anyone that sounded female. This was brought up and the idea of 'letting them have enough rope to hang themselves' then removing them by force once they were proven to be a dirtbag. A majority of the regulars seem to frown at people making advances on lady players.

For a while they were discussing trying to move everything over to the publicvent TS3 server where people can create their own channel and they have better control of stuff like bans and channels, but we were told there was some new announcement from typefrag (who provides the vent) about something.
