Generally they are multiplicative, you could quite easily reach a 100% damage reduction if they were simply added together.

To test this, I put a barrier and Pain Suppression on myself and cast Shadow Word: Death at a dummy, dealing 2484 damage. I took 1044 damage from the backlash, and had no other sources of damage reduction (Dont worry, I didn't have shadow protection up).

2484 * 0.7 * 0.6 = 1043.28.

Focused Will would factor in for another multiplier of 0.8 and your hypothetical resilience for another 0.7.

Mana regeneration

There are several different sources of mana regeneration:

Mana per 5 seconds, which is directly increased by Spirit. Spirit is associated with blue gems. Intellect (Red gems) also increases the amount of regeneration that Spirit provides. Base regeneration at level 85 is ~1029

At level 85, Spirit regen works off the following formula: 0.016725 * spirit * sqrt(intellect)

With Discipline or Holy Masteries, you retain 50% of spirit based mana regeneration during combat. Holy priests can spec into Holy Concentration, bringing their mana regeneration in combat up to 80%. For combat regen, the formulas are:

No Holy Concentration: BaseRegen + (SpiritRegen * 0.5)
Holy Concentration: BaseRegen + (SpiritRegen * 0.8)

I've noticed at 85 in 4.1 DoTs and HoTs are now updated with spell power procs as well as bonus haste effects(buffs, haven't experimented with procs yet).

I.e. I apply a full row of dots, they tick every 2.42 secs, then heroism is poped, i do not refresh them, volcano + power torrent + lightweave all proc together, all the dots continuing to tick update to ticking every 1.94 secs and are hitting about 140% harder (ES does not update existing dots they must be reapplied however). Just thought I would post that haste and SP gains(from static temporary effects like Lustism/Jindo's slow, etc) dynamically update(no longer a need to reapply) for the duration.


EDIT: Oh and I should also post the diminishing returns cap on haste rating to haste drops DRAMATICALLY at 90%+, @ 70 with heroism + borrowed time, + haste procs + moonkin present I was at about 93% haste i procceded to drink a 500 haste rating pot(normally about 25% haste) and gained .03% more haste. So the haste hard cap from non gimmick fights is around 92-95% at best(even thousands of haste rating won't get you 1% more haste above that). Haste rating seems to update dynamically with your current buffed haste(static mechanics can still raise this higher of course), but after 93% haste from any effects additional haste from haste rating is under such DRs as to be of almost no benefit whatsoever. Keep this in mind when choosing to activate trinkets/procs of haste rating as well as considering your own base haste from haste rating accordingly under gimmics/lustism. If you are gaining closer to this break point.
