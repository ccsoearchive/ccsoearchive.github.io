I agree with this. I can't imagine actually saving Shield Block for burst unless the burst occurs once every 30/60s (in which case you're not actually "saving" anything, you're basically just hitting it on cooldown, perhaps delaying it a second or two to adjust.)

The cooldown is short enough and the duration long enough that I suspect it's a wash in terms of effectiveness at best if you save it. You might save your healers more mana per Shield Block by saving it for when burst happens, so that you don't need as much throughput to top you up after, but you get more Shield Blocks if you don't save it at all. On top of that, it's linked to Heavy Repercussions, so you're giving up threat and damage to delay it.

Honestly, the truth is that we really just don't have an equivalent for the 60s cooldown. Whether that's balanced or appropriate or what is a separate discussion entirely. But Shield Block is too dissimilar in effect to treat the same way, in my opinion.

I realize it only swings the damage a minor amount either way, of course, so it's not as if it matters much, but behaving as if we'll be saving it for burst doesn't accurately reflect how I think the ability will mostly get used, in my opinion.
