the point of this post is basically just to try and give a bit of insight on feral, and some of the reasons people are complaining about Feral so much. there has been a history of Feral being overnerfed anytime it comes close to being competitive, and i want to prevent that from happening in the near future

Anyway, here are some of the big reasons people are complaining about Feral

1) Berserk's duration in short matches: this is probably the biggest reason out of anything. Ferals tend to play setups where they can end matches quickly, because the longer a match goes on the weaker Feral is. so if a feral opens, pops berserk shortly after, and the match ends within a minute, the Ferals damage output and the amount of Clone procs we get seems over the top, because they have berserk active for half of the fight.

if you DO decide to change Berserk, a very easy way to fix this issue is to change Berserk to have a short CD/Dura, like Sdance does. this is an important change because as it is right now, people tend to think that Feral damage/Clone procs are regularly as high as it is during Berserk, since it lasts so long

2) Unheeded Warning: nothing much needs to be said here, this trinket is increasing shreds damage by a pretty absurd amount when the proc is up (35%~). sure, it will affect PvE to nerf this trinket, but the reality is that Feral's shouldn't be hanging on to an ilv 359 BOE trinket for the rest of their PvE and PvP careers this xpac, so just nerf it

again, this is an important change because most people tend to think that most of the "big numbers" they are seeing is Ferals regular damage, which isn't at all the case. UW inflates shreds damage by a ton and is giving people the impression that Feral can just toss out these crits all the time

3) Lack of Experience: let's be honest, 99% of players have never had to deal with Feral like they do now. Resto and Balance are relatively weak, so they've respecced Feral, and there are alot of us. but they've had years learning to fight other classes, and have no idea how to fight back VS Ferals yet

whenever people fight against a Rogue, and he Dances, everyone is like "lookout, he's dancing!" and they adjust accordingly. same with a warrior bladestorming or recking, ETC. NO ONE does that when a Feral Berserks, no one does that when a Feral pools energy, or Tiger's furys with bleeds. they simply haven't learned yet

Endzzz

anyway, my HOPE is that you'll will read this, and take my advice and just hold on a little before you nerf Feral in a big way. you know as well as i do that Feral in 4.2 is practically no different then Feral in 4.1 in PvP. you made a scaling change and increased Berserks duration by 5 seconds, thats pretty much the only thing that has changed, and the scaling change has hardly done anything in PvP yet because we dont have our 391 weapons

right now Feral is the target of 4.2's "nerf bandwagon", but there are much bigger issues in PvP right now you should be focusing on, so please be careful with how you decide to handle Feral, because it will pass if you give it a bit of time ^^
