This will probably only hold any value or meaning for the nerdiest among the 5 or 6 people who follow this thread, buuuut, in case you missed this little tidbit nestled within one of GC's posts today to the Beta Class Balance Analysis thread: In Mists, you'll be able to clip the last tick of our Envenom and Revealing Strike buffs without being penalized.

the benefit of this functionality (being able to clip the last tick with no loss) has been extended to a few additional spells. These spells have been turned into periodics, despite not really doing anything periodically, so that you can take advantage of this same ability to clip the last couple seconds with no loss. Inquisition, Haunt, Revealing Strike, Envenom, and Savage Roar work this way. For example, Savage Roar uses a 3 sec periodic tick rate under the hood, so that if it normally lasts 18 sec, and you recast it after 17 sec, it will have 19sec remaining. Total of 36 sec for two casts of it, despite clipping it by 1 sec. We hope this is a nice little quality of life change (and should obviously have no downside).

http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/5889309137?page=123#2456

Anyone know how long an Envenom buff "tick" lasts?
