"our block chance is decreased and you wind up with wasted block chance that does nothing"

I'd point out that for warriors, the extra block transfers into critical block. Extra block above unhittable is not wasted.

As I understand it, the statement is still correct:

Shield Block's additional block chance converts into Critical Block once you pass unhittable, but raw block chance as gained from Mastery Rating does not.

That is, if you are unhittable without Shield Block, and you gain some more avoidance, some of the block gets "pushed off" the attack table for no additional benefit.

At the same time, if you are unhittable without Shield Block, and you gain some more Mastery, the amount of Critical Block chance you gain is still the same as it was before you were unhittable.
