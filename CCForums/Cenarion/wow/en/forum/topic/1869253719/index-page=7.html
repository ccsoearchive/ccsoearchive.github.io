x' is avoidance after diminishing returns
x is avoidance before diminishing returns
c is the dodge/parry cap (65.531440% for warriors, paladins and DKs, 116.890707 for druids)
k is a constant equal to 0.9560 for warriors, paladins and DKs, 0.9720 for druids[/quote]
