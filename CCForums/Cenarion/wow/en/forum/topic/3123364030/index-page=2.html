Pretty much. Survival was the top by a large margin when everyone was in blue gear and just starting raiding because of its high base damage with poor scaling. Blizzard's solution to the issue was to gut Survival and buff Marksmanship. All that buff did though was flip flop the specs as MM was doing the exact same DPS Survival was doing prior.

Now this wasn't so bad as Survival had a pretty good niche. It had a massive AoE gain on MM and also had superior mobility to its MM counterparts.

Then 3 days after the hotfix, MM had Aimed Shot nerfed and thus switched to Arcane for the primary shot, causing MM to have higher mobile DPS than Survival.

Then in 4.1 Blizzard buffed MM AoE through the roof overtaking Survival.

So now you have spec breakdowns that look like

Beast Mastery
Mobility: **
Damage: **
AoE: *

Marksmanship
Mobility: ***
Damage: ***
AoE: ***

Survival
Mobility: **
Damage: **
Aoe: **

As you can see, there's no reason to play any of the other specs unless specifically needed for a single buff. MM can do everything the other 2 specs can do and more efficiently. That's incredibly poor game design. The other specs have no strengths or incentive to use over MM. Surv is only kept usable in 10m content because of Hunting Party and has been written off as one of the usable specs in 25m. Beast is just useless due to mchanics. Hell, MM is even the better leveling and soloing spec, knocking BM off its throne after this many years.
