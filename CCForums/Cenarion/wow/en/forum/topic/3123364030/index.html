Kill Command: Now can strike form 8 yards, up from 5.

Kindred Spirits: In addition, after casting Multi-Shot, your pet's next 1/2 Basic Attacks will strike all targets within 8 yards. Basic Attacks triggered by this effect cannot activate Invigoration.

Killing Streak: Also increases Kill Command's damage by 5/10% at all times.

Sic'em: Now can proc from Kill Command critical hits.

Dash and Dive now increase movement speed by 100%, up from 80. Now cost 20 Focus, down from 30.


All of the above would be simple ways to fix BM's mechanics without making it OP or using any off the wall modifications.

-Kill Command change corrects the issue of using Kill Command on moving targets and it bugging out
-Killing Streak change is just for a little extra oomph on the damage
-Kindred Spirits corrects BM's AoE issue and brings it up to par with the other specs while staying true to spec flavor.
-Sic'em would help counteract the large nerf BM received when they fixed Focus in 4.2.
-Dash and Dive would alleviate BM target swapping pains as much and make it a more mechanically sound spec. The more complicated fights are, the worse BM does.
