Possibly, from what I can deduce, is that since the remote gchat app does not require an authenticator to log in, our guild rank settings prevent people without authenticators to be in a raider or officer rank. My guild master has it set to not allow any promotion higher than member, since this became a feature prior to the Cata xpac.

Not sure if this is "as intended" but definitely an inconvenience to anyone who gets demoted, and then has to have the GM re-promote them. Basically, I can't use the remote gchat as an officer. It could also conflict with any DKP or EPGP loot systems, as well.
