Ceridwen, while the app won't continue sending notifications of incoming chat while you're idle, the app actually does have message playback built in. Once you return to the app, you should receive any messages sent in guild or whispers to you while your character was in an "Away" status. There may be an issue right now with whisper-specific message playback during Away statuses that we are working on, but for the most part, what you're asking for is already in place! :)

As for disconnecting after idling for X amount of time, that is the same functionality that exists in-game, and it's also a good thing for your phone's battery life!

Put me down with another "keep me logged in when the phone goes idle". It's a pretty standard chat program feature (and this is basically a chat program), and a chat shouldn't really be all that battery intensive. Additionally being logged in just for chats shouldn't be hitting your servers very hard (which I imagine would be the other concern). Lets face it - $3 a month is a pretty premium price for a chat service - it should be enough to cover the minimal bandwidth and load.

The other one that is a pain is that it disconnects (Or maybe even crashes? It looks like the whole program quietly quits) when the network connection changes: like for instance if I move from a wifi to a cell network or vice versa, or even if I lose reception for a moment. I understand that for the purposes of stopping cheaters that behaviour is necessary for the full game (although still pretty annoying sometimes), but for a chat program it shouldn't be.
