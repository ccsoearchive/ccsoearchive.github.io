I agree with most of this. Removing Steady Shot, though, would be too much of a physical damage loss. That being more of a PvE problem than PvP. As for your Master Marksman suggestion: Making it proc 100% but with 8 stacks would make it proc no more often then it does, on average, now. And if it did cause a PvE DPS increase, removing something like MM's spec perk would add a problem of having to replace it with something else, because MM can't just go without one (although knowing Blizz...).

Now as for something you might have overlooked, Hunters are the only class without a sufficient self heal. Putting aside the hybrid classes... Evocation, Drain Life, Death Pact, Enraged Regeneration, Recuperate. All other non-healer classes have a way to get health numbers back up. Why don't we? Blizz gave us a 5% health heal on Chimera Shot, probably because they put it there to have a reason to remove its more useful disarming effect. Even after all of that, only MM get it, and in PvP a 5% heal on a 10 second cooldown amounts to being as good as us using level 70 bandages. We then have Spirit Bond. BM tree but not too high as to keep every spec from getting it; It heals for only 2% every 10 seconds and it requires your pet to be active. In any way you look at it, its the 80 year old running the marathon next to the 20 year old, Recupercate, who just passed you only a mile in.
