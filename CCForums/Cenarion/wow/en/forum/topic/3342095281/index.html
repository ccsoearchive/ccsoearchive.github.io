Hi I'm making yet another post about hunters with suggestions of how to fix our class so that we're even remotely balanced. I'm a top rated hunter, Highest hunter 3v3 ever in the world in season8, and highest rated of whatever comp I play in whatever season, with the relentless and wrathful gladiator title on multiple hunters and battle groups in EU so I am clearly extremely qualified to know what the problems are with our class.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Disclaimer: We have been carried by the top classes throughout this expansion, any other class is better in the team than us, the hunters. In season 6 it was believed that hunters were very weak at first, yet I could still outplay people if I executed things well, however the state of hunters in season9 has really been the last straw for me, with the rise of warlocks and has given me the impulse to actually write a forum post. These are my proposals of how to fix the class in arena and tournaments. I've spoken to a lot of rank 1 and tournament players and they all agree that hunters need a lot of help, that they are a sad class and easily out playable in arena due to lack of, well, just about everything and they are arguably the worst class by a nice margin as of now (except ele shamans, although that's a spec).

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Scatter Shot - MOST IMPORTANT CHANGE: Introduce a major glyph that removes all dots on the target when you use scatter shot - why do mages, rogues etc have this when we don't even have it for scatter shot which we can't even spam like polymorph, fear etc (it actually takes some sort of dynamic play and skill to execute unlike spammable cc)

Scatter Shot - update it to 25-30 yards, the target should not wander as much when scattered, it makes it incredibly easy for people to take traps whereas the only requirement to eat traps as of now is: Having a pair of eyes.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Steady Shot - Remove it from the game, apply cobra shot to the master marksmanship and improved steady shot talents and reduce the cast time by possible 0.5 second via maybe a talent, or the Hunter PVP 4-set bonus (face it our 4-set bonus is dog-awful, it gives us a whopping 0.3 extra focus regen per second) Best of all that change won't affect PVE almost AT ALL. This will fix our extremly poor damage versus any plate class (especially warriors and dks) when I face them in arena my main tactic is to pray that they are bad. It basically normalizes our damage.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Master Marksman - Make it 100% chance to proc and 8 (more or less?) stacks to get a free aimed shot. This would allow us to control our burst instead of this RNG that we currently put up with.

PVE DPS - if that change occurs in a too much of a pve damage boost, then the solution is simple - remove the 15% auto shot damage mastery perk from MM.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Scare Beast - Reduce the cast time to 1 second. It's so easy to shapeshift this and there's no way to catch a druid (with half a brain cell) off guard to pull it off. Give it some use or give us a glyph similar to the paladin turn evil glyph. This would make us much more versatile and it would reward people with good reactions versus all druid specs.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Resistance Is Futile Talent - A Simple fix. Apply a hefty damage modifier to this when it procs to say, 200% more damage(?). Then it would actually be worth taking the talent and this would increase the skill-cap of hunters as they have to manage their focus better, keeping 40 focus for the proc, when it procs.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Post Haste Talent - Increase the speed you gain from this to say 40%. It's stupid when mages get a 70% sprint after blinking. Maybe this is overkill though. 8% boots run speed + 30% from talent which don't stack resulting in a whopping 22% movement speed increase.
EDIT: Hotfix in 4.3 now makes this talent stack with boots minor speed enchant etc. It is still a bit weak imo though
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Readiness - Most classes like Rogue, Mage, DK, Warrior etc have nice on-demand burst (hi shadow dance) and we also had great on-demand burst back in WOTLK where our resource was mana, by the use of readiness. Now I propose a simple solution: Give readiness the ability when you pop Readiness to reduce the focus cost of all abilities by 100% for X seconds (something like 2-4 seconds?). That would give us the on-demand burst we had in wotlk and it would not affect pve at all as it's on a 3min CD. It would also increase the skill-cap slightly.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Wing clip - Turn this into a more Cooldown like ability, atm it's pointless as concussive shot is far superior and Wing clip is usually parried or dodged. The only time I use it is on cloaked rogues. It could possible be a 45-second CD Immobilize. People would have to be careful with this as it would most likely share diminishing returns with entrapment or web. It would give us a nice, reliable peel versus melee to help our healer etc.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Raptor Strike - This needs to scale properly with attack power. To give hunters a competitive tool it could also be redesigned into a gouge-like ability with a reasonably higher cooldown as suggested by a fellow hunter. This would increase the skill cap massively as it would stress the importance of positioning in arena as a hunter furthermore.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Rapid Fire - Give it the cata rogue sprint treatment. Lower the CD but lower the duration and haste bonus.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Camouflage - Redesign this, turn it into a dot wiper, but with no immunity like cloak thus making it balanced and not overpowered

it really needs a re-work as it's almost totally useless
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Edit:
Our WOTLK range (niche)! - In wotlk we could outplay casters by out ranging them. I would advise giving us a range advantage (say 45 yrd on our shots) and updateing scatter to 30, and tranq/silence to 45 along with it. This would really make us shine in arena

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Items:

4-set Bonus - Like said above I strongly thing a 4-set bonus such as steady shot and/or aimed shot cast time cast time reduction would make our class much more optimal. Or even allow aimed shot to be cast while moving (maybe overkill).

Most importantly, Hunter's have no immunity or breaks to any CC (such as lichbourne, zerker rage, shapeshift, blink, tremor totem; list goes on) however I like that they are based 100% of positioning and movement although I propose to either rework warlock fear so it's more skilful OR give us a fear breaker - SPAMMABLE CC IS OP.

Bugs: I'm happy that trap resists are being fixed however the range on TRANQ AND SILENCE SHOT RANGE STILL HAVEN'T BEEN UPDATED TO 40 YARDS

Thank you for reading.
