Hi Brain, i like your suggestions. As one would expect from a top hunter. However i think we also need to address problems with the other specs. I will propose some changes here for Survival as it is the spec i use now for Pvp. I am not a top hunter like you so some of this may sound retarded. I ask you to use these as ideas for some improvement on Survival tree. This is mainly concerned with bgs since i dont do much arenas.

An existing problem with Lnl procs and Ice Trap. I have to wait 22 seconds AFTER using my Lnl procs to use my Ice Trap again if i want my procs. I understand the concept of burning the procs as soon as they come but in bgs target can die or proc Lnl from an existing ice trap which means i got to wait more than 22 seconds to use my trap again for proc. If i use my trap for survival, i loose my damage burst.

The talent Counterattack is useless to say the least. I get the proc if i parry an attack which would be extremely good if i had parry more than 5%. This talent is an obvious joke.

Immolation trap or Black Arrow has pathetic damage and Explosive trap is not so useful for Tnt procs when you are kiting someone. Would i really want to use Immo trap or waste points picking up Black Arrow for this damage? No. A simple solution would be to reduce the chance of proc and tie it to Serpent Sting, seeing as how it already plays a big role in Survival dps.

If i choose anyother spec, i get a cc AND a self heal. If i choose MM i can even get entrapment which means Survival only gives me one cc which BREAKS on any damage and on a 1 min cd. If i had my immo trap on it, its over. But common sense for Survival play style tells us that we should always have Immo/Black Arrow on for Tnt procs. We dont get self heal or ability to recover any HP like the other 2 specs.

These are some basic problems i have identified with Survival hunter play so far in Cata. I hope you also include sections for both the other specs too in your post.
