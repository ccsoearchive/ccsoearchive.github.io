    The Legends of Pandaria Vs The Warlords of Draenor

“When faced with opposition, be like the sea, which washes over all without doubt or hesitation. It will topple the unsecured immediately or shape the land into its own image with persistence and patience.”

The Steelpaw Shaodin is a Heavy Pandaren RP guild. Made up of a union of Huojin aligned and Mainland Pandaren, united under the creed “Perfection through Victory”. Staunch adherents to the ideology that bettering oneself is wrought through pursuing challenges and overcoming opposition, the Steelpaw band together under the values of honor, strength, and their vigilant desire to see the Pandaren people reach new heights of power.

Martial, determined, and eager to find wisdom through success, the Steelpaw operate within the Horde as a predominately militant order. Through the sword or quill, the clan's mission aggressively revolves around advocating Pandaren interests, along with their own. Recognizing that the current state of Azeroth is in proverbial disarray, the clan strives to train warriors, gather artifacts, empower their allies and be at the forefront of a cohesive union of the Pandaren people.

Pandaren that are enthusiastic to master martial disciplines, eager to enjoy the thrill of victory, and wish to capitalize on the shift of powers on Azeroth- returning their people to a height of power, then the Steelpaw clan wants you!

________________________________________
The Steelpaw as a concept started with just a few friends from Moonguard and Wyrmrest that enjoyed playing as part of a rough housing family together. After gathering enough popularity from roleplay and guilds that helped evolve what started as a loose/improvised concept, eventually there was enough interest to form a guild centered around Pandaren Heavy RP.

That, and we wanted an excuse to dress our Pandaren in the most fearsome mog combinations and not feel bad about it.

We roleplay all throughout Azeroth. From Orgrimmar to the Jade Forest, members of Steelpaw and their allies can be found adventuring just about anywhere. The diverse lot of characters that make up the guild ensures that their pressence is felt everywhere, pursuing the troubles of the world or pushing the agenda of the Pandaren people. From hardened warriors to shrewd diplomats, you'll find that the clan brings in all types, each with a role and purpose, all working in an effective tandem to flesh out the end-game of the guild.

Pandaren of all walks of life and disciplines are welcome, but not all may have what it takes to carry the burdens that the Shaodin faces. The Steelpaw's vision is a unified Pandaria, restored to an era of witnessed only during the legacies of their late emperors. It is a goal far too precious and meaningful to shirk responsibilities or for the faint of heart. Those in the Steelpaw are expected to be disciplined, committed, and willingness to act whenever necessary to oppose those who act against them or their allies.

To reach us, whether to join, coordinate RP, or anything else feel free to contact the following officers or any of our members. By in-game mail is or signing to our site prefered, as whispers are often lost in the chat spam our officers and the GM tackle on a routine basis.

Emeraldstorm - Clan Leader
Chenju, Genglei, Mieliu - Tiger Generals(Officers)

There is only one rule to any conflict.
If they strike first, defeat them.
If you strike first, defeat them.
If you both strike at the same time, defeat them.

By overcoming challenges by your strength and determination, by pursing the chaos of this world, you bring harmony to yourself and others. The realm is, by it's nature, wanton and reckless. It is up to you to calm the randomness of nature.

No matter how wild the rapids, they will not spill beyond a study embankment.

F.A.Q.

What types of roleplay can I expect?
Our spectrum for roleplay is pretty wide, with the one constant is an adherence to quality. We have sermons at monasteries, missions to quell the various dangers that ravage the world at large, and diplomatic ventures to establish alliances and politic avenues. This can range from casual meet ups to more structured DM events. We also enjoy playing the game, so IC instance runs or PvP is a big allure to us.

What types of characters would I find in Steelpaw?
Studious monks, sly ambassadors, cunning rogues and fierce warriors. The constant of Steelpaw is our separation from what is expected of general Pandaren, seemingly pacifist role play. Though not to say the clan's theme is just short of war mongering, Steelpaw's principles attract those who are willing to act and commit to upfront means of garnering influence or protecting their values. Steelpaw recognizes the vital roles of a diplomat as well as a standing army—and both are used liberally.

Are the Steelpaw Neutral?
The Steelpaw always put Pandaren interests first, as they are staunch nationalists and are far from subtle about their priority agenda to aiding their people. Though the Steelpaw, unlike many Pandaren, express interest in integrating and supporting the Horde proper, climbing ranks and securing allies. This can be seen as an interest to ensure power abuses from time old never happen again, though a few can see that there is a larger scheme afoot.

What non-RP related things do you participate in?
We have guild LFR and Transmog run twice a week with regular PvP/Arena games roughly all week. Given the nature of our story direction and the types of player's we've gathered, Steelpaw can be seen as RP/PvP. This is not to discourage those who do not like or wish to participate in PvP whatsoever, though we are patient and encourage anyone to give the thrill of snuffing out the lifebars of other players a try.

Do you take Non-Pandaren?
Yes, though on a limited basis, usually to out of character alts or in character allies. Very rarely is a non-Pandaren considered part of the clan proper and even those are only after a set of strict trials and long term commitment.

How do I Join?
Contacting any of the officers mentioned earlier by website application. Our recruiting process is different in that we do all of our interviews in-character as to get one familiar with the guild and what they're exactly joining. This helps maintain our standard of RP and what we expect from our member base.

But I'm Blue-side.
It is never too late to fix that mistake! However, should you feel tethered by the Alliance out of some moral obligation or what have you, the people at Jadepaw Dynasty are an excellent bunch...for Tuishi.

Recent Events.
The savage world of Draenor is a realm of strife, conflict and turmoil. The very land is a pit of many tests of survival and wit. Besieged on all sides by threats of the Iron Horde and the very enviroment they've trusted themselves in, the Horde continues to press on, standing proud and true against the threat of the Iron Horde. For if they fall, the rest of Azeroth follows.

Enter Operation: Emperor's Fist.

Made up of stunch defenders of Pandaria and their allies, Emperor's Fist is a plan with one agenda in mind. Victory. Mainland, Huojin, Shado-pan, Hozen, Grummle merchants, assistance from the Black Market, and more, the full fighting force and resolve of Pandaria storms through the Dark Portal, bringing the strength of their homeland to an unknown world. This time, the fight is on their shores now. This time, the Steelpaw and their trusted allies are once again pursuing the conflicts of the world, hoping to bring peace through fists Stronger than Iron.
