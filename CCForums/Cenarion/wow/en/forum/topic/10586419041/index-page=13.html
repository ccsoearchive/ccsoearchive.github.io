Front Page Updated! The guild is recruting once more and Steelpaw is happy to announce the start of a new series of plotlines in Draenor! With our new events, we're hoping to continue to deliver and engaging narrative, one that focuses on the Pandaren role in a new, rebuilding Horde, while also fortifying their involvement with the events surrounding Warlords of Draenor. Many challenges await, from Ashran to Nagrand, but the Steelpaw Shaodin is ready to face them all, being a beacon of strength in the shadows of the Iron Horde!

Operation Emperor's Fist is an extension of the Horde Campaign into the Iron Horde controlled realm of Draenor. Collaborating with the separate constructs of the Horde's military agenda, Operation Emperor's Fist will utilize the unique skill assets. of the Pandaren allies among the Horde to secure a foothold on the realm of Dreanor.

Participants

      [*]The Pandaren Coalition of the Steelpaw Shaodin
      [*]Warcheif's Hammer Battalion <Horde 41st>
      [*]Pandaren Blackmarket
      [*]Grummle Caraven Merchents
      [*]Shado-Pan Dispatch Unit
      [*]Grimtotem Ravagers, Grimtotem Loyalist Company
      [*]Bligewater W.R.E.K T'D <Weapons. Recon. Kills. Tactics. Division


Operations Commanders:
    [*]Steelpaw Shaodin Commanding Leadership
    [*]Gorthusk Bloodfury, Legionaire of Warchief's Hammer


Objectives:

    [*]To find a means and stop the Iron Horde invasion into Azeroth.
    [*]To discover and neutralize any potential or present threat to Azeroth on Draenor.
    [*]To accumulate resources to expedite and fortify the Horde's efforts on Draenor.
    [*]To discover any artifacts or potential magics of note and secure them for the Horde.


These objectives are to be met by any means necessary.

Base of Operations
The Steelpaw unit and other participants of “Emperor's Fist” will be given an extension to Fort Frostwall. This district, “Xuen's Keep” will serve as the rest, rearm, and strategic hub of the operation in addition to facilitating the specific interests of the Pandaren forces allied and/or supportive of the Horde's war efforts. Xuen's Keep is to be kept up to the exceptional standards and qualifications expected of soldiers of the Horde in this important operation. Failure to meet appropriate checks will result in disciplinary action from the Frostfall's commanding officer.

Xuen's Keep is equipped with all of the necessities in maintaining a functioning and prepared battalion among the Horde. Such facilities include but are not limited to:

    [*]-Great Hall
    [*]-Barracks
    [*]-Tavern
    [*]-Training Grounds
    [*]-Mine
    [*]-Enchanter's Study
    [*]-Portal Relays


And more. Architects have been granted to assist the Steelpaw Unit in expanding the needs and grounds of their district, should it be necessary to assist them in their war efforts.

Collaborative efforts to maintain upkeep for the district is imperative. Those who fall behind in their duties will be met with the appropriate disciplinary action and within the highest penalty applicable of military law.
