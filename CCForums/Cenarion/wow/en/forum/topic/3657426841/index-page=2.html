Steamrolled through it - took 5 mins

Picked sub going through the patrols, vanish immed if they spot, prep and have another vanish on the ready.

Hard to explain route without showing a picture but, go straight across bridge, to the left, zig zag between the 2 houses, jump into water, from there its much easier...

It IS doable tho. Some of the earlier routes, like jumping off the initial bridge into the water, doesnt work so well anymore, also going straight across the first bridge and staying on the outside wall doesnt work really either anymore since the guards from above on the top ledges will spot you now.

you will have to HUG walls around the houses on the inside... no room for error really, but make sure if you agro you IMMED vanish, or itll port you out if you remain in combat for more than literally like 1 second.
