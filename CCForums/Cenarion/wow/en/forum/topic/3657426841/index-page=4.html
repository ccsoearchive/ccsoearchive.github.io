This was not very difficult at all, and one of the best quests I've done in the entire game.

I actually took the long way through the entire place, sweeping around the far right and cutting through the training dummy area. It was a lot more difficult than "cheating" it, like a lot of people are doing, but it was fun.

The boss itself I did as Sub spec, and never got me below 90% HP. Interrupt the shadowbolt, dont stand in the cone or AoE, and keep Recuperate up.

Overall very fun imo, and not even close to being "impossible" like people are saying. Just because you fail to understand the most simplistic of Rogue mechanics and design (Stay behind targets, use CC tools, imp stealth from pre-Vanish) doesn't make it impossible, it makes you unfit for the quest until you learn your class.
