Did it rather easily. Fat fingered my first try but got it on the second.

Jump off bridge, swim to the left. SS to the pat going up the stairs and sap.

Sap the left most mob to your right hand side then sneak past the other ones..just skirting the ones to your left.

Jump down stairs..did it over the two patting. Go along the waterway's wall until you reach the Third entrance on the left hand side with the 2 stationary guards and 1 pat. Let the pat walk away and sap the right most mob. Hug the wall to cut your circle in half..get as close as you can to that next group of 3 and be aware of the previous pat. Distract the SECOND group of mobs and SS to the outermost one and sap then immediately sap the next one all while hugging hte wall to the right.

Distract the last 2 guards to the left..sap the other most one and sprint by into the courtyard.

Just take your time. Surviving is more important than burning him down quickly. Use all of your abilities. If you keep him interrupted, and stay out of the bad stuff, you can easily survive long enough for your blind cooldown to reset. Then, it's just a matter of bandaging up to full and waiting out the duration of blind, so you get more uptime on cooldowns.

If it looks like you're doing badly, and blind isn't ready - vanish. Start over. Don't let him kill you.
