I didn't find the stealth mini-game in any way fun or challenging. It was just frustrating having many mobs detecting you well outside the range of the stealth "circle", and outside the range of being able to sap them even while they are distracted. Since I don't have a subtlety spec it probably added 5-6 attempts to getting through to the courtyard.

I really don't understand why Blizzard would add this mini-game that completely changes the rules of how stealth works after people have been playing with and refining their use of that system for many years.

It's like being assigned the most important task (a legendary) you've ever had at your place of employment/school. However, instead of using the system you've used for the entirety of your career there you're given a completely different set of tools and expected to have the same results.

I guess I'm out of touch with today's game design, as I can't stand 90% of the console/pc action games put out lately either. IMO it's unfortunate that MMO's, at least WoW anyway, are looking to copy that.

I... kinda cheated...

I went in turned right then went to the first courtyard... I was in sub spec making sure vanish and prep and sprint were off CD. At this point I popped sprint, and rushed through, waiting until I agroed. At the moment they agroed on me, I popped vanish and kept going.

While running I popped prep, then hit sprint again and waited until I agroed again. Vanished again and jumped off the bridge into the shallow water part.

AFK 5 mins, rinse repeat until Creed:)

Also, a quick guide for Creed, I found him to be really easy.

Put up mind numbing and crippling poisons. This will slow his casts and keep him snared. All I did was chip at him with recup up, stay in motion and kick his casts. Remember that he can be CCed with all our tools, gouge, kidney, blind.

When I got low on HP, I blinded him and bandaged.

Hope this helps team.
