This took me an hour or so of attempts. There were three other horde rogues doing it at the same time. We didn't mess each other up, but we had our fun sapping each other when we were next to one another on the bridge. It took me a while to figure out the optimal route to get through the first district, the second one took much less time but was more risky.

One of the dogs in the courtyard in the military district that was supposed to be asleep wasn't, so I had to waste my sap on him instead of one of the 3 guard packs. But I distracted them so they were facing away from me and I managed to sneak past them (I was holding my breath, it was quite nerve-racking).

The boss for me took one shot, after about 30 seconds of close calls (recuperate keeping me alive), I could completely avoid every single one of his abilities. And my auto-attacks dazed him, allwong me to kite him really easily and recuperate practically all of his melee damage.
