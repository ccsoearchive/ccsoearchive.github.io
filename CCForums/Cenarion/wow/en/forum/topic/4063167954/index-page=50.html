Some questions and feedback on Warlocks:

  • Is there a new design for DoTs? Some spells increase the rate in which DoTs tick (Malefic Grasp, Shadow Trance), so I assume that means the time between ticks will be able to vary on the fly, i.e. without recasting or refreshing the DoT. And now the 1 million dollar question: What about their damage? Will it update on a per tick basis and not retain its stats from the time it was cast or refreshed? If yes, this would be awesome. Not only we wouldn't feel cheated if a SP buff procced for not recasting our DoTs but also we wouldn't be able to create a super powered DoT just because we had all the procs aligned when it was cast, which would provide a lot more balance and sense.
  • What about cast times? Will they also be able to change mid-cast? Based on Kiljaeden's Cunning, I'd assume yes. Would this affect Curse of Enfeeblement?
  • Where are Pandemic and Everlasting Affliction in its current form? Are we always supposed to manually refresh all 3 Affliction DoTs?
  • I don't get the current Hand of Gul'dan or Gul'dan's Grasp. For instance, what do the charges do and how are they generated? Please elaborate on it.
  • What's the design on Soul Fire exactly? Its interaction with Destruction seems more or less understood considering Burning Embers and Destructive Influence, but what about Demo? Are we supposed to cast it once every 30s while the enemy is above 25% health? This would be Improved Soul Fire all over again...
  • Will the time between ticks of a DoT cast while under the effect of Kiljaeden's Cunning be penalized at all or does it affect only cast and channel times? If yes, does the penalty last for the DoT's whole duration or only until I stop moving? Will cast times be updated on the fly as I move or stop moving? (See also second question)
  • Archimonde's Vengeance: Will the passive portion of this talent work with bosses' non-targeted damage dealing abilities (damage auras, AoE, etc)?
