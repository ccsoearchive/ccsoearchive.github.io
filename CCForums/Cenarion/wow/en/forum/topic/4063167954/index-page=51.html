Dark Soul:

"Increases <Stat> by 30% for 20 sec. Passive: Increases <Stat> by 5% while this spell is not on cooldown."

Right now, the passive vs. active effects would be a wash in a Patchwerk sim when not taking activated trinkets or other burst buffs into account. (the passive and active bonuses both provide 600 stat%-seconds over two minutes). This makes it very, very favorable to use the activated ability instead of holding onto the passive bonus. Stand-and-nukes don't exist anymore, so activating the burst buff is always going to be the right play except towards the end of a fight. Have you considered increasing the passive bonus to a point where the passive bonus might be better than the burst-damage effect against an almost-patchwerk like Baleroc or Ultraxion? As-is, the passive bonus doesn't seem good enough to factor into PvE gameplay.


Locking out the passive buff just doesn't really make sense to me. Either you're going to pop it on cooldown and never get the passive benefit, or you're going to want the passive benefit and never pop it (though that seems less likely). Just let them both coexist.

Having MORE taunts, not less seems more fun when you are having to deal with the actions of other people to do your basic job-keeping mobs on you and off everyone else.

Maybe had this design been worked since BC, when people knew how to target the tank's target and work that way without much asking them to. But nowdays groups just want to go in and AOE down everything or single target burn any mob they want-even if it's a ranged caster the tank has barely any AOE plash threat on.

This just doesn't seem like a fun move to make. Right now I am sortof withholding judgment on how things will work with Mists gameplay but I am kinda getting tired of the actual mechanics changing so much with each expansion.

Blizz seems to be dumbing down the talent trees (they seem fun, but honestly I like more choice, not less) and yet expecting players to have good enough skill to actually focus on the tank's target since they will have only single target taunts.
