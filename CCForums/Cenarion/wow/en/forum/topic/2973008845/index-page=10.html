Absolutely love the idea of more World Pvp. When the next expansion "Mists of Pandaria" comes out (which most likely will be an Island expansion where you have to sail to different islands) I would like to see Blizzard make an entire island or small group of islands exclusively for World Pvp or simply have them set aside for Battlegrounds.

Id like to point out that when a battleground can be won in different ways it creates diversity. If they create a large World Pvp area with say 3-5 different ways to win. (capturing a total of 25 flags, collecting 10,000 resource points from controlling areas, having a kill cap where you get say 10 points for a kill and once your team hits say 5,000 kills the match is won. This would emphasize survivability. And last idea, if both sides have a reputation bar. for doing certain quests in the zone and killing certain bosses when you control an area you could gain rep for your team and your team then hits max rep the Npc's in the zone literally pushback the opposing faction for say 20 minutes where they cant enter the area and you can do dailies etc in the zone). Oh and no seige vehicles except for say transporting you faster around the zones you control to the edges that the opponent controls.

I would very much enjoy larger versions of AB, WSG. Combined. I know that Blizzard already has kind of done that with Eye of the Storm but they have it on a point system. If they changed it to getting flags OR holding areas. Some may think that battles will end up with no conflict where one faction goes after flags and the other holds the zones. But what if there were 6 zones set up in a Hexagonal shape with a spot in the middle where the flag rests. The teams have to have a way to unlock the flag so that both sides can go after it. But it would make for a very interesting Battleground.

I love Pvp but would like a little more content. Not saying any of these ideas are fixtures and are certainly flexible. But by having larger amount to be contested over would make a battle longer and more dynamic with many chances for a team to comeback and try different things. This would help a well lead group while a group of renegades would struggle.

Feel free to like this post and bump it up. I think some of these ideas will spark some conversation and that some will just be left in the dust but if Blizzard takes a look and likes something Ive said then they may use it in future content if they see the community backs the idea.

Thats all, Zilnagra Out.
