"A unit from Ironforge's Siege Brigade should be arriving in three hours."
Every hour, a different unit comes to assist the attackers.

1 hour - Airborne Medic unit arrives: Friendly player corpses become interactive, by clicking on the body of a fallen player (while out of combat), you shoot a flare into the air. A goblin/gnome Airborne Medic parachutes down and shocks the player back to life. The use is instant and the rez is instant because it takes a short time for the Medic to land and he can be killed.

2 hours - Something :D

3 hours - Siege Unit arrives: Attackers can receive an on-use item from the Siege Unit leader which can be used to place a ground-target AoE or circle, which will call in artillery from the Siege Units. Destructive if attacking players coordinate it's use. Increased damage vs enemy defenses such as turrets.

4 hours - Elite Unit arrives: When a section is taken, a special elite unit will protect it.. for example, for the Horde: Gob Squad, or some elite Forsaken unit. This would allow for an outnumbered attacker to attack other sections, as the NPC's would be sufficient guards for already taken sections.

These are some suggestions to add/fine-tune balance for some already mentioned ideas that I FULLY support....


Someone mentioned the parachute-in goblin/gnome medics. If I may suggest, those rezzed by said npcs would get a 5-10 sec immunity buff, something like "Renewed Vigor" or "Realized Purpose". I say this, because let's face it, the "Honorless Target" buff/debuff doesn't matter for crap in the heat of battle. "If it's red, it's dead." BUT, while said player has this 5-10 second immunity buff, they cannot perform ANY heal/spell/attack. They can get out of enemy reach, LoS, but cannot become "OMGPWNGODS." I say this, because every pvper has at some point in a BG/WG/TB, been THE walking bullseye (I've been that MANY times..... *Evil Grin*) for the opposite faction, and simply being auto-rezzed without said temporary immunity would just be a new form of "OMG, it's back!! KILL IT, ASAP!!"

Someone mentioned actual battle formations for npcs in this post, and I LIKE the idea. If I may make a suggestion, have a quest to actually LEAD a small army (and I'm talking more than 5-6 members of Gob Squad or SI:7, but more like 20 npc soldiers), and actually be the spearhead of this small army to obtain an awesome quest objective, such as taking down another player's army and capturing an outpost, hold off an enemy advancement, or something like that. Yes, I'm very inspired by "300" atm for this idea. "SPARTANS!!! This is where we hold them!! This is where we fight!! This is where they DIE!!"

My last idea for right now.... Someone mentioned using/capturing Submarines. Why not have a quest (BUT, if you complete another quest, it becomes disabled for you, like the Shadow Wardens/Druids of the Talon quest branching in the Molten Front), where IF you capture said Submarine, and you can use a form of missle salvo where said missles drop some form of chemical agent payload (Forsaken Plague for example off the top of my head) into say, District A, B, OR C (you can only choose one, so choose wisely), where the enemy faction (players and NPCs) get a debuff. This debuff could be something as benign as puking for say like 3-5 seconds every 20-25 seconds, to something as severe as that "Creeping Paralysis" debuff, but a debuff that's medical/chemical-oriented.

And say IF this payload is dropped into, say District B, players from your same faction trying to take District B, will get a quest to arm themselves AND the attacking npcs with chemical warfare gear (gas masks, radiation suits, rebreathers, etc) before entering District B, otherwise they get the debuff as well.

Also, I heavily Ba-BUMP this thread. /salute to all those contributing so far.

EDIT: Bolding an item here or there.

EDIT 2: These suggestions are to try to make the suggestions of this and the previous thread have a much grander scale to where you actually feel like you are in a FULL-ON WAR!! :)
