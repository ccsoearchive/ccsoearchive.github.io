Having nodes there to encourage PvP is a brilliant idea. If I could have a nickle for every epic story I have of node wars I'd be a billionaire!

I have an idea of a system that kind of works like a killing spree.

A player that has gotten a decent amount of kills (I personally think it should be whether said played did the most damage to said enemy and not killing blows. killing blows promote kill stealing and = noskill) gets a killing spree. So after maybe 5 kills, for each kill he gets X amount of gold or X amount of honor and increases with the more kills he gets. At maybe 25 or something it could be announced server wide that *Player* is on a killing spree.

At this point he will be track-able by all players of the opposite faction which would be really cool, sort of like a bounty hunt where when killed, all players involved could get a reward (gold or honor whatever). The longer the player survives while being hunted, the bigger the bounty is, also as a reward for trying to stay alive the player receives gold per X amount of time.

I think this would be an awesome addition not only because it would fun times, but skilled world PvPers would get their name out in the world and it could be like it was in vanilla. Like, "Oh dude did you hear how *Player from opposite faction* got 35 kills before he got caught?"
"Yeah broski, but I heard *Player from same faction* got him in the end" ect ect ect.

tldr:
Killing spree/bounty hunt
