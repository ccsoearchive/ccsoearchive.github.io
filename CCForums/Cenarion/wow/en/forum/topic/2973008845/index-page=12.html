Love this thread. Such great ideas coming in all around. After reading this and the previous thread I just had to add something to it. Just my random thoughts after reading it all.

The city would be split up into the four sections. Killing the miniboss for each sections would activate the slider allowing the other side to capture if they could fight and hold on to it long enough. A miniboss would then spawn after a section was captured to be killed if the opposing faction wanted to take it back activate the slider again.

There would be gates at the main entrances to protect each section that would have to be destroyed by using the seige vehicles on the outside of the city. Glaive throwers, Dwarf tanks for alliance - Catapults, demolishers for the horde. There would be some ways into the city for players to enter without destroying the main gates to stir up some trouble buy the offence outside time to take out the gates. Some cannons on the walls for the defense to use to take out the seige weapons soften up the npc or players trying to enter. They could act like the cannons in isle of conquest on the keeps not that strong but destroyable and repairable.

To keep one faction from holding the city 24/7 the attacking side would get reinforcements in the form of npc's coming into the city. They would only enter after a gate was taken down by a player, but would begin to flow into the other sections after the current one was capped. The longer a side held the city the npc's would get a small stacking buff. After so many hours an airship could show up giving the players another way to enter the city or attack the gates from the sky. The gates would stay down for each section till the entire city was taken over then get repaired. Npc's would continue to pile in making it a little more difficult for the defending faction to take back the sections but not impossible unless they have held the city for say 10 hours.

Could be like halaa and wg holding the city would give a buff to that faction on both kalimdor and eastern kingdoms upping exp, honor, or rep gained and vendors to buy usefull items say mounts or pets with tokens can earn by pvp quests and other daily qsts outside the city wall. There could also be daily quests in the city preparing for the attack to come. Gather so many items to help repair the damaged walls, kill so many players in the city or npc's outside the gates. After taking the city completely over everything would reset so there isn't 100 mobs in a single section. They would still hold say half the buff they had when attacking but it would quickly fall off after an hour or so.

This all was just my random thoughts on how I see the battle going down if it was implemented.

Alright I have been thinking of some things to make each section a different way to capture. First off the Merchant Square could be captured by lets say having five flags in five different buildings throughout that area. Once you capture and hold all five flags for lets say five minutes you then get the option to capture a large flag. If you can capture the large flag and not have an opposing member defend the flag like you can in Arathi Basin then you get Merchant Square.

The Military District could be captured by having to gain so many resources by the attacking faction and then once the amount needed is turned in you have to keep the area while a slider bar goes making sure to keep the defending faction out.

The Cathedral Quarter could just be a simple slider bar.

The Greymane Court could be a general first then cap a flag.

The Light's Dawn Cathedral would be a capture then flag then slider bar.

All of these places would spawn a mini boss once that is complete. Upon defeating the final mini boss to cap the entire city a large raid boss spawns in the Cathedral Quarter. It would have the different phases like a normal boss etc etc. Look for an earlier post by me for a discription of this boss.
