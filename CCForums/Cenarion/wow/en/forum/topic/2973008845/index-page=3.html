It seems like a great idea!

My ideas are probably already posted in the other thread, but i guess it cant hurt to post them so..

After each battle there should be a 10 minute period before the next battle can start. During this time (and any time a battle isn't ongoing) there should be a sanctuary outside of the city (for the faction that doesn't hold gilneas) and one inside for the faction that does. In the outside sanctuary there should be an npc that can be talked to in order to begin the battle, and after it is started, a 5 minute warning will broadcasted to all players on the attacking faction.

The defending team will not get this warning, instead they would have to rely on players in the zone to warn them, or a message that would broadcast only after the attackers had begun attacking the city.

I think it would also be nice to see some good strategic elements included in this, like ambush locations that have a chance to spawn guards (based on the numbers on each team) with these locations being optional but providing shortcuts , the ability to call for NPC reinforcements (again, based on numbers) and also a gradual weakening of defenders after each battle. Something like a 4% decrease to run speed, and 5% decrease to damage and health. This could stack up to 5 times.

Again, great idea. I sincerely hope they implement this at some point. If not during cata, in the next xpac

outskirts crews' job: To shoot down the enemy airship patrolling the outskirts, and flanking incoming enemy siege machines.

Each time Gilneas switchings controlling hands all buildings and morale and damage is reset.
