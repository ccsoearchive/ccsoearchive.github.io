((This feels difficult to summarize, but I'll try my best.))

Kaeev hails from a collection of Winterhoof and Dawnstrider families that have made Mulgore their home. He is an only child, and both his parents--who were well into old age at the time of his birth--passed on when he was still quite young. Notwithstanding this loss, he had loving family to take care of him, and lived an otherwise normal childhood among his people.

His shamanic gifts manifested themselves relatively late, and remained fairly dormant until a chance meeting with an old bull named Grennan, who was then chieftain of a small traveling band of tauren shamans known as Stormspeakers. Kaeev was inspired by the old chieftain, and Grennan happened to be seeking a student. Under the tutelage of Grennan and the Stormspeakers, Kaeev spent the next several years learning and harnessing his gifts.

Shortly after coming of age, Kaeev put his gifts to further use and enlisted into the ranks of the Horde. He served in that capacity for several years until near the conclusion of the Horde's Northrend campaign. From there, he continued on as a free agent, lending his aid wherever the Earth Mother led him. It was in the midst of such efforts that his aging mentor Grennan, much to his surprise, passed on the mantle of chieftain to him.

The Shattering occurred not long after Kaeev became chieftain, and much to his sorrow, Grennan was slain in the events immediately following the cataclysm. Without his mentor to look to for guidance or support, Kaeev frequently finds himself feeling inadequate in his leadership. Now, in a world that has changed more than ever, the young shaman struggles to continue on the legacy that Grennan left behind.
