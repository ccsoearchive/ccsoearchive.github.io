I really doubt blizz is nerfing shadow priest damage because of dark intent.


They are. Dark Intent is best on Spriests (highest benefit to raids dps), and Spriests are the best targets for Dark Intent for the Warlocks uptime - so Warlocks give Spriests Dark Intent. Consequently whenever you see a top end guild with a spriest doing really good damage, its their damage plus 3% more haste, which is our strongest secondary stat by far.

You don't see the boomkin or the mage or the hunter with it, so their damage isnt 3% higher haste than it should actually be without it - so all top end spriests parses are skewed higher than their actual damage would be, were it not for Dark Intent. What's more - the same is true for Power Infusion - so guess who usually gets Power Infusions? The Shadowpriests.

What does this mean? It means without a full retinue of people handing the spriest their single target ally buffs, our damage is actually pretty much spot on average, if possibly slightly Lower on single target fights. However, many current fights are dot cleave fights - guess who does better than single target damage with two long-term persistent targets around that both need to be taking damage? Shadowpriests.

This is pretty much a 10% nerf to our total dps - that's Massive (with a capital M).


Don't forget that DI is not just 3% haste but also 9% more DoT damage (up usually 100%), which is about 85% of our damage.
