1. Get rid of the dead zone.

I realize the hunter dead zone was removed like 4 years ago, but I just hate Stephen King so I figure every other hunter also hates him.

2. Show our ranged weapons on our backs.

Okay, I really like nothing better to do than to stare at my character so I really really really want to see my gun!

3. Duel weld pistols

Come on now, I can do it IRL with my water guns (I am 12 years old btw). So if it can be done IRL it should be done in a fantasy game with orcs and dragons and people shooting fireballs out of their ... nvm.

4. Let me ride my pet

Okay I gotta admit...whenever I need to use the restroom or go out for dinner I call sparky over and he gives me a piggy back ride. I tried riding my kitty cat but it just screams at me till I get off it.

5. Turn BM or SV into a tanking spec

You can tell by #4 that I'm in love with my pet so I think my pet should just be as strong as a PC tank.

6. 5,000 stable slots

Seriously now - I want every single pet in the game with every single skin so I can match the color of my pet with my purse while staring at my gun and riding my turtle into battle that he is going to tank.

7. Give me back mana

Okay it's been like an entire expansion and I still can't get used to focus (I'm a wrath baby) so they should just ditch focus.

8 Make exotic pets not exotic

I really like some random pet I tamed as BM but I don't want to play BM. Just don't ask me why I tamed it, but it really does look cute and have the cutest eyes so I'd like to explore all the lands with him.

9. Hunters need a self heal!

I mean we have none! Spirit bond? I forgot about that. Spirit beast? oh...hmmm. Chimera shot? Shut up already! Okay survival doesn't have a heal. Wait, blizzard gave self heals to melee type classes to help them level? So that kinda means preventing my pet from taking damage while leveling via mend pet? Shut up with logic! This is the forumz!

10. Gimme back eyes of the beast

Why did blizzard take away my fun of ganking lowbies into attacking my pet? Blizzard just hates fun!

11. And gimme back volley!

I especially loved spamming volley in boss fights. Just look at all them arrows hitting that big boss guy! It's no fair!

12. Let me shoot in melee range

Why should I have to try to get ranged while I'm a ranged class? I just want to turn with my arrow keys and pew pew anyone around me. It's no fair that I can't.

13. Bring back pet aggressive

My pet has anger management issues and gets pissed off at me for always sending it in to die so it should be able to attack a stealthed rogue while BGing when my window is minimized as I check the status of my !@#$ download.

14. Fix tame beast

I really want that pokemon and it's unfair someone coming to steal it so blizzard should let me phase out, become immune, let only me see the pet, and when the pet is tamed have it bring me a beer. Speaking of which.

15. Let me bond with my pet

Once tamed, pets should start at level 1 and go through the bonding process with the hunter. Hugs and kisses and nice petting and cuddling. These are all essential.

1. No. Please just no. I understand a lot of people hate this, but no.

2. Don't care either way.

3. No. You know why. No.

4. Eh... It would be unnecessary, and I'd rather have them fix current abilities than add in new cosmetic abilities (I'm looking at you, new rare pets).

5. As nice of a feature as this would be, along with the suggestions of Demo Locks tanking, no. Leave actual tanking classes to tanking. We've always been and should remain a ranged DPS class with a pet.

6. 5,000 is obviously an exaggeration, but they get the point. I have tons of pets from when I was BM and a pet collector. I've run out of room for new pets and am having to drop my old "collectibles".

7. Focus is a better resource than Mana. You may not think so because of the trouble you're having it, but it's miles ahead.

8. Beast Mastery is a master of beasts. Let them tame Exotics. It's one of the only things they have left.

9. We need something that won't be overpowered, but allow us to squeeze out in tight situations. 5% of our health (10% if crit) is simply not enough.

10. Blizzard took this out because it was an unneeded spell. It was a cosmetic thing and had no real value. I did enjoy having it as a scout, though.

11. Volley was bad. Volley is terrible compared to Multi-Shot. Keep Multi-Shot, keep Volley gone.

12. That was the same thing as #1. No.

13. The current pet stances are broken. We need to revert to the old changes and let the people punish the Huntards that leave it on Aggressive in a dungeon.

14. Tame Beast isn't broken. You roll on a PvP server, you take the chance of getting your tame stolen. It's competition. Rare pets are rare for a reason.

15. No. This is the exact definition of reverse progress and reversing all QoL changes we had. We had a system like this and removed it.

Overall, it seems like you just scrounged through every Hunter nostalgia thread and just added every ability or wish you saw to this list.

No.
