Even in my craptastic gear I find BGs to be fun and I do not get rolled as often as I do on my shaman who is substantially better geared (enh). I have always liked rogues, this is my 3rd rogue in my WoW life which started during vanilla beta. The stealthiness is what draws me. Even in crap gear I'm managing to kill or hold off most classes till help arrives.

Arena will be a totally different beast with a unique set of issues. It is harder to be successful as a rogue in arena than it is as many other classes.

I feel rogues need a damage buff. My mage in the same gear has just as much survivability (probably more realistically) and higher damage output from range. Since my dps is meh esp in PvP I tend to work more technically on my rogue. I'll go after objectives over a kill whenever I can. My fav BG is AB. Nothing better than sapping some poor slub and capping the flag only to disappear again and hold out till reinforcements arrive.

Rogues have changed since vanilla... we no longer seem like assassins. We are more like the sneaky thief due to the changes blizz has implemented. I use to love killing other players but blizz changed the formula so I changed my game style for my rogue. I realize blizz no longer wants me to eat people alive like I use to. This seems to have turned off many of the rogues who were in it for the kills. This is why the population has dropped year after year as blizz pushes us further and further down the DPS list.

I'll never stop playing my rogue, I have however split my time more with other toons. Which I had not really done as much before.
