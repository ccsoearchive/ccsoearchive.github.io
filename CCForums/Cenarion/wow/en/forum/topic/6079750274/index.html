Welcome to Warcraftier's Cenarion Circle "All is not as it seems" contest!

The idea here is fairly simple, we would like to see everyone's best effort at an awesome transmog/RP outfit, cosplay, or unique application of in-game transfiguration. The winning individual or team (please specify in your submission) will win 100,000 gold. While only one grand prize winner will get the big prize, runners up and winners for most humorous, most transformed, best copy of another class, most original and some other specialty categories will receive other prizes, including but not limited to 25K gold, in game pets, and massive amounts of odd junk from Warcraftier's mysterious guild bank.

Entries can be submitted on our website at www.warcraftier.com in the thread entitled "Transmog Contest Entries" in the Forum "Fun Stuff". Entries should include an image capture (in game screenshot) of the transmog/RP outfit/Cosplay or transfiguration, a reference image showing you in "normal" gear, a note indicating if it is a group or individual submission, and any explanation for the submission that you want to make. Please use the thread for entries only. There will be a seperate thread for comments and questions titled "Q&A for the Transmog Contest". Please be aware that this contest is open to everyone on the server including Warcraftier members. The judges however are not eligible to enter ;P The deadline for contest submissions is 07/31/12.

Relevant links:

Transmog Contest Announcement (same info as this post):
http://warcraftier.guildlaunch.com/forums/viewtopic.php?t=8514825&gid=96291

Transmog Contest Entries go here:
http://warcraftier.guildlaunch.com/forums/viewtopic.php?t=8514879&gid=96291

Q&A for the Transmog Contest post here:
http://warcraftier.guildlaunch.com/forums/viewtopic.php?t=8514875&gid=96291

*Please note that you would need a guildlaunch account to be able to post on our site. You do not need to be a member of our guild to be able to post in the thread. If you encounter issues making a submission or posting a question please let me know.

Also, for those of you creating guildlaunch accounts for the first time, please verify your accounts, otherwise you cannot use BBCode to display images. You just need to go to your e-mail and look for the validation e-mail.

Good luck all and we look forward to seeing your entries. :)
