Hey folks! This is Folkvar Brightale. He's my first real attempt to get into RP and really flesh out a character. You may notice that he's on a non-RP realm, and this is because he's one of my favorite alts on my home realm, and I really wanted to unlock his potential as a character living and breathing in Azeroth. I made a character named Brightale on Moon Guard to act out Folk's ingame RP, but I'm not sure which realm would fit me best; MG is kind of a placeholder until I can find my own home server with a community I enjoy.

Since I'm relatively new to RP, I want my "RP main" to be how I personally would act in a perfect world, and lived in Azeroth. So some features of Folkvar will be traits I have outside of the game (generally kind and merciful to those who wrong him), some will be exaggerated (slower to anger than I am, but much scarier than me when he reaches that point), and some will be flat-out "I wish I was like that" (very family-and-country-oriented, social, etc).

Is this unrealistic? I'm not a horrible writer (had a decently popular fan fiction of the Pokemon universe on another site for a while), so I'm sure I can make things sound nice, but how do people respond to self-projections like this? Maybe they won't care, since most people I interact with won't know me in real life. I don't know, this is all new and exciting to me.

Also nerve-wracking, because in starting to form ideas for a character backstory, I begin to realize just how many small details and nuances go into a chatacter's history, and I'm afraid that I'll mess up and ruin the fun for others who are role playing with me. I know more about lore than the average-Joe WoW player, but tiny trivia tidbits really stump me sometimes.

So I guess I'll just have to jump in and find out? My major question isn't really a question at all, but a request for a breakdown of various RP servers and the communities within. I could make a character and scope out each realm, but with the amount of new people that must pass through, I feel like a lot of my questions would be met with scripted replies or being too nice just to get me to stay (it's happened before). I haut want real talk from real people on the servers c:

Thanks so much, guys! I'm excited to try this new experience out!
