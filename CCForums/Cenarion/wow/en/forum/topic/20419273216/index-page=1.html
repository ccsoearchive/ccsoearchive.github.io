Hello there and welcome. We of the Stonehammer Clan would like to invite you to a very special event for us. Due to recent events we have changed the name, but it still has the same heart.

We are hosting the Gund’Harg, or as it translates into common, the Stone Festival. This is held in the spirit of the Kosh’Harg of the old orcish world. The reason we do not like to hold it under the shadow of Oshu’gun is due to the mass of hostile mobs around both of those available. This would make it rather difficult for lower level players to join us in the event.

Information on the event:
Time/date: This will happen every last Friday of the month, unless holidays or other events get in the way. This month it will happen Friday, May 27th at 7:30 pm realm time.
Location: The Valley we host it in is ICly sacred to the Stonehammers. It is in the Northwest Stonetalon Mountains. With the coordinates add-on you can find it at 24:12.
Who to look for: Those who are hosting the events are those within the Stonehammer, notable names are Crogart, Kusnel, Talre and Yulrica. Guest moderators include Ruhkor (Might add more later)

We welcome all who call the Horde their family. We come to feast, and be merry. Keep your hostilities, hatreds and grudges at the mouth of the mountains before you enter the area.

Please note: The valley in question is difficult if not impossible to enter without a flying mount but does not contain any hostile mobs. If you need a ride please whisper any Stonehammer and we will give you a hand.
