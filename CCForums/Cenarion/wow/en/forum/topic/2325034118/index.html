Looking to find a raid to experience the new content with when it launches? Here is your chance!

As the title says; Infusion, formerly known as Team Bloodlust is currently recruiting to expand our roster and fill in a few key roles.

We believe in casual schedule but not casual raiders. There's a big difference between the two. While we won't fault you for having a life and not being able to maintain 100% attendance two out of the three raid nights a week are a requirement to remain in good standing. While the days set are set at a casual level, we do not want that mentality to carry over to the raid team. When we're raiding, we need to be focused and get what we need to get done accomplished. Being as we have such a small schedule we expect everyone to come to raids prepared. At this point, guild membership will be mandatory to hold a raiding position. We do expect as main raiders on our team that your raid lockouts are saved for our raids. I'm not your mother, but I do get disappointed when my raiders clear content on off nights on locks that were to be extended! :)

For main raiders, flasks, food buffs, and most enchanting mats are provided.

Gear isn't necessarily a deciding factor when we review applications. However, a base line class knowledge, and effort should be apparent in your gear. We do expect you to have access to both your helm and shoulder enchants by this point in the expansion. You must know how to listen to directions, not sleep in fire, know how to switch dps to adds, dodge sound discs/poop/tornadoes, and just generally have a pulse and half a brain! We are a fun team, we love to joke around, harass each other, and have fun. Standing in fire so ruins the mood, so don't do it!

Current Progression:
4/7 ~ 6/7 T12
12/12 T11
3/13 HM T11





Oldschool Progression:

ICC 25 12/12 11/12 (Heroic) with drakes and all that
ICC 10 12/12 (Heroic). Glory of the Ice Crown Raider 10 complete! (Horde #1) Yay drakes!
25 TOGC 4/5
10 TOGC Insanity Tribute (Horde #1)
Ulduar 10/25 complete with drakes on both levels (lol)


Raid Times:

25 man Tues/Thurs/Sunday 7-10:30 with invites starting at 6:45

Current Main Needs:

1-2 Warlocks
2-3 casters of any variety
1-2 melee of the rogue/warrior variety

All exceptional players of any class/spec will be considered! (unless you are a hunter or ret paladin. We still love you, but we already have lots of you!)

We also are always happy to accept social members, as long as you aren't a jerk!


Please visit http://infusion-cc.wowstead.com/recruitment to apply, feel free to speak with myself, Destriel, Sasorina, or Ronnin in game with any questions!

Also, Destriel accepts bribes in the form of fruit baskets and pie...
