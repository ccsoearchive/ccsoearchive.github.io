To add on this:

About us:

Will of the First Wolf is a Goldrinn and Ancient heavy themed guild, as is it's RP. Our site of operations is currently at the Shrine of Goldrinn in Mount Hyjal, along with other various lore important sites throughout Azeroth. Our goals are Neutral for the most part, Not really affiliated with the Alliance, our members are simply limited due to Faction restrictions. We are led by Elder Talandael, one of the ancient Kaldorei Worgen. He functions as the overall leader and spiritual guide to our order. Often willing to teach others about druidism and the lore of Goldrinn and his chosen children. Then there is myself, Cardley, who is more combat leader and general day to day instructor. I am Talandael's second in command.

What we do:

As in above, we focus primarily on defending Goldrinn, his children along with the other Ancients. Protecting their shrines, holy sites, retrieving important relics, etc. Our current storyline of events is battling a bit of Twilight Cultist incursion in Hyjal. What started out as a small threat has turned into something much, much worse. As time goes on, we'll expand into new stories, revisit some, work on character development, go to different area's etc. We have -many- different story and event ideas, big and small. All we need to do is set them in motion (And you can help!)

What are the Requirements?:

We do have some restrictions/requirements on members joining due to the nature of the guild. You do not need to be a specific race, for our order is open to all races both OOC and IClY. To become an IC member of the order, your character MUST (And this is important) Share a strong faith in Goldrinn. It's the core of our guild, we are his Protectors and instruments of his will. If they do not, they cannot become IC members of the order. It's a rule that is broken for no one. If your character does not share this this faith, they can become an OOC member. That basically means they are called upon for support, supplies, etc. They are allies, but are not part of order. (You are still in the guild of course :P)

For Warlocks, Death Knights and Demon Hunters. Like Tal said, it is a case by case basis for IC reasons. For warlocks and Demon Hunters, it's a lot to do with Fel Magic, Demonic Magic, and just demons in general. There is an uneasy trust and built in bias against them. Very, very rarely would the be allowed in, at most they would be a support role (Ooc member). For Death Knights, it's a similar situation. The undeath and use of necrotic, blood and general 'evil' magics. Plus the way some Death Knights have carried themselves left a bad impression of the leaders.

Anything else?

Just a few OOC things about the guild and joining.
-While there is no age limit, we hope that you carry yourself in a mature way and handle mature topics that can arise in guild chat from time to time.
- If you do not like/support Gay/Lesbian/Transgender/Bi then do not apply (For our benefit and yours. We like to keep a friendly and relaxed atmosphere for everyone.)
- Use of mental disabilities (Like Autism etc) In a negative way will -not- be tolerated.

So yeah, I think that's about. I might be missing something but if so, I can always add to it later. Our events are usually at 6- 6:30 server time. We are looking to do some more (maybe small ones to fit with others scheduals). Our Events are all DM'd by our great friend and guild member Nevan ( So much <3 there)

If any further questions, you can leave them here or send them to us in game.

Have a great day and may the Great Wolf Spirit guide you
~ Cardley, Guardian of Goldrinn.
