None of those are going to happen. I think you've correctly identified problem areas, but your suggested solutions conflict with Blizzard's previous statements about class design.

Aspect switching is a problem, but the correct solution isn't to turn the macro'ed choice into a complete non-choice. Once the correct answer is always be in your suggested merged aspect, why bother having an Aspect bar at all? The hybrid Hawk/Fox may as well be passive, with Cheetah, etc. turned into a cooldown... but now you're homogenizing the classes because you now have Dash. Aspects are meant to work like DK Presences (as they were originally modeled after them); you should have to choose the correct one for the correct situation. The better solution is to redesign AotFox so it's worth sacrificing the AP from Hawk and worth using for longer than a Steady Shot cast at a time.

Replacing Steady Shot with Cobra Shot defeats the purpose of introducing Cobra Shot in the first place. Cobra Shot gives the devs a balancing tool that, if necessary to use, doesn't screw with Marks. Here's a hypothetical situation: ignoring all the other problems with BM/SV for a moment, say Blizzard buffed KC/Explosive Shot as much as they could without creating "overpowered" burst, but still needed to make up some more damage... a good place to do it would be Cobra Shot.

As for Steady/Cobra being instant with a 1.5 sec cd, I think Blizzard is reluctant to make that change because it would further devalue haste. Now you could argue for a class ability that allows haste to lower that cd, but they're gun-shy about that idea because it hasn't really worked out when they tried it with Ret Pallies. For now, the first step is to get Slow/Frost Fever/Infected Wounds to stop affecting Steady/Cobra. Even if that's ultimately not enough on its own, it's a step in the right direction.
