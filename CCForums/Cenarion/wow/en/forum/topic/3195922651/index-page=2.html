My guild is having the same problem with recruiting. We can barely find enough people that can come every week, and have the skill/gear for FL raiding. And when people can't come, we don't have the replacements. It makes it very difficult to progress weekly. And although 25m raiding is something we would all like to do, it would be impossible for us to find enough of the right people on Hakkar. We even tried merging with another guild for some fights, but it didn't work out.

In addition to that, the economy is well... Terrible. It's impossible to find anything in the auction house, and things end up costing so much more than they are worth.

The scary thing is, since I joined, big guilds have transferred off to higher population servers. Don't we want more people from higher population to transfer HERE? It's only going to get worse. The people that can afford it will leave (and have left), and what will happen with the rest of Hakkar?

I think a merge would be helpful, but would it really fix the problem? If we had free transfers from any other server, could we convince people to come?
