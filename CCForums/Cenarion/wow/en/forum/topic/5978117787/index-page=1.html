I just want to start off this post with saying that Cenarion Circle has been my home server for over 5 years and I've seen a lot change over that time. My main was Rojan on Alliance side and I had just came back in preparation for Mists of Pandaria to come out. In the one month that I've came back to this server I've noticed something, that high-rated arena is absolutely dead on this server. When I used to play I could occasionally see a forum post with an aspiring individual wanting to get serious about arena and such, but now I can sift through 10 pages of CC forum posts and not find one. There used to be a serious strive to be the top rated team on server and it usually ended up being around 2400 to 2600 depending on the bracket, but now all I see is one inactive 2k rated team?

I guess what I'm trying to say here in my rant is that I want high rated arena to come back to this server. I understand that this server was never the top rated or anything close to Tich or KT, but come on. I am asking for some help in bringing back competitive / high-rated arena on CC. Let's try to get some ideas flowing in this thread and I'll edit in the ones posted. I don't want this server to die in the arena scene, but right now it isn't healthy at all and we need to try our best to fix that before MoP is released.

Edit: So far we've implemented an Alliance side channel (CCPvP) geared around trying to help out aspiring PvPer's or just a place for seasoned veterans to discuss topics of PvP. In the future we're hoping to see this channel being used for arena team recruiting and other things similar to that. I'd also just like to throw out a quick thanks to everyone that has been supporting this movement.

Proposed Solutions:
  • Arena Channel
    • CCPvP (/join CCPvP)
  • Arena Guild
    • NAACP Gaming (Now accepting any aspiring or veteran PvPer's)
