Events and people seeking RP partners to be found!

You may have heard of it...then again, maybe not. But now we're singing it!

cenarioncircle.org

The dotOrg is website for all your Roleplayer needs, mon. Half-wiki, half-forums, ALL community, this website has been put together to serve the Cenarion Circle RPer base in whatever capacity they may require.

What features do we have? Well, let's see, mon...

Interactive Character, Guild, and Non-Guild Organization Profiles
What makes a profile interactive, mon? Via the comments system, you may post your IC thoughts about other characters and guilds...and they're encouraged to post their own thoughts about yours! Now you can see two sides to any guild or character: it's creator's intent, and what everyone else in the game world thinks, too...

Character Blogs
What is your thought du jour, mon? These blogs can also be password-ed should your extra secret diary be, er, extra secret.

Calendar
While it's not auto-updating yet, we now have a calendar for any server-wide events you wish to post. If it's on this calendar, it's free for anyone to stop by and participate in!

Story Archive
All kinds of stories! Ya, even those ones. Well, okay, maybe not THOSE ones. Put a warning on it if it's a little heeby-jeeby or ooh la la, mon!

Forums
You can never go wrong with a good pair of forums, mon. Or even just one.

Handy Links
Links, links, my company for a buncha handy World of Warcraft links.

Twitter Support
We've also got a Twitter feed, mon. Check us out!

These not be enough for you? New features are being added all the time! Here's just a sneak peek into some of the "To Do List":

Auto-Updating Calendar
It's always been a want for the myriad of server events, but how to make it most convenient? It's on the thought process, mon!

Hearthstone Chat
Oh, woe is da chatty roleplayer when the server is down for maintenance! With the dotOrg's hearthstone, you'll be able to chat whenever you like. Trillian and ingame support anticipated.

Bug Squashing
It's never OFF the To Do List, ya know? If ya see one of these nasty buggers, we've got a support forum and contact page just waiting for you!

So, have I convinced you yet? Don't make me get out the totems and the lava burst things, mon. <sly wink>

The dotOrg. It be the new rage!

You, yes, you! We have need of you!

The dotOrg is currently looking for beta-testers. A lot of features have been coming out in the past few months, but with not enough time to really streamline them and make them pretty-ful. And that's where you come in!

Beta testers will:
--poke everything on the site to see if they can make it break
--suggest improvements to already existing features
--test features for user-friendliness, ease of access, and other such things
--post on the forums with their observations and suggestions: we can't fix it if we don't know what's broke!

To become a beta-tester, just register at the dotOrg and get to exploring! Also please let your intents be known to either myself or Alelsa, so you can get your shiny beta tester badge and access to things not yet available to regular users.

Please in keep in mind...we're not a business or an official of any sort, so this is purely volunteer, just-for-the-heck-of-it work, to put your own mark on a community site that's growing up fast.
