What: Greetings WA! Clan Steelgrasp and some of the other Dwarven clans wish to host a crafter's festival to honor centuries of fine Dwarven craftsmanship!

When: We plan to host the event from 6pm to 9pm server time. Opening ceremonies will take place outside the "Cask and Anvil" next to the flight path at 6pm.

Where: The Great Forge of Ironforge, of course!

What you can do:

  • As a patron, you are welcome to roam around the festival grounds, enjoy the night atmosphere and some good casual RP! Come by and shop around, purchase fine goods from craftsmen and women around the forge. Enjoy great music, drink, and food! Even register to participate in our fighting pit sub-event.
  • As a crafter, you can register for a booth to show and sell your wares! We plan to spread out along the outer ring of the Great Forge, allowing tons of space for different players to form small gatherings. Blacksmiths, engineers, scribes, even musicians and cooks are welcome!

    If you can craft something, you can register. Don't worry, there are no fees or anything! We just want to keep track of how many people want to fill this position. Please get your booth approved beforehand! You can message me in game at any point or send a mail!
  • As a host, you are welcome to participate in either roll, but you are also volunteering to help out where/when it is needed. If you wish to get involved in planning the event, please contact Bathildis in game! I look forward hearing from you!


PS: If you use the addon GHI, you'll also be in good company at this event. We'll have special wares available, as well as at least one bard playing live music throughout part of the event.

PPS: I'll keep this post edited as events unfold!

Currently:

01. Modarin of the "Cask and Anvil", and Bathildis of "Steelgrasp Brew Works" are teaming up to provide you with the finest Dwarven food and drink available in the inner ring.
02. Bigeasy will be bringing some musical talent.
03. Furgas will be hosting a blackjack/gambling table. He will also be taking registration for the 'fighting pit' sub-event.
04. Halflan will be hosting an engineering booth.
05. Gleita will be helping out at the fighting pit.
06. Drakehammer will be presenting his blacksmithing wares.
07. Ulfourogan will also be hosting a blacksmithing booth.
08. Jiaoshen will be providing Bubble Tea via "Bubble Delights".
09. Nori will be hosting a gunsmithing booth.
10. Khorun will be holding a weapons table.
11. Breaska will be hosting an alchemy stand.

We still have a handful of maybes, possibly offering alchemy, blacksmithing, woodworking, and inscriptioning wares.

Fighting Pit Brackets! Talk to Furgas in game to sign up!
