I have an idea. And of course its a team death match type in a way, put not fully. Theres more to it. It also implements true flying mounts, not like the ones u cant control. No idea for back ground or how the map is, but i got a simple idea of it.

Ok. There are 2 teams, one is defenders of a fortress with 3 4-sided walls, a gate on each side of each wall... and an attacker that uses siege weapons and flying mounts to destroy the enemy team.

The defenders: have infinite reinforcements. 5 flying mounts (used to drop bombs on enemies and attack enemy flying mounts at melee) max at 1 time, 50 players, turrents and machine guns on the walls. The turrents are unreachable by the enemy until the enemy breaks down a gate of that wall. Defenders get a debuff to their turrents for every gate that falls, stacking up to 3. In the middle is a small building, that if destroyed by the siege weapons, will give a limit of 50 reinforcements to the defenders. Thats where the deathmatch starts.

Attackers: 150 reinforcements, 10 flying mounts (same as defenders cept more of them) siege vehicles, 50 players. Get a buff for every gate that falls, stacking up to 3. Destroys small building to create death match.

Flyers: Can carry 1 bomb, must go back to base to retrieve another.

Siege Vehicles: Used as main source of gate, building and vehicle vs. infantry dmg. Can not be attacked by player spells (this would result in players not using turrents to destroy them if not enforced). Limit of 5 at a time.
