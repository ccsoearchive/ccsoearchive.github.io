TF2-style payload maps, please. Isle of Conquest would be perfect to convert for that. The terrain would have to be touched up and smoothed out a bit in a few areas, namely just one gate/wall per base. Near the Alliance base there would be a "payload cart" (cart full of bombs) on a railroad headed toward the Horde gate, traveling along the western part of the island (near Docks) and another one for the Horde near their base that goes across the Hangar mountain toward an Alliance gate.

The carts work similar to a Tol Barad base in how it's captured, where you need to have more people standing around it to control it. When Alliance "captures" their cart, it moves forward, but when Horde captures the Alliance's cart, it moves backwards slowly (and vice versa). If the cap meter is neutral the cart is stopped.

This means the Docks and Hangar bases/flags would be removed, but the Quarry, Workshop, and Refinery would stay as regular bases. Capturing any of those three bases helps increase your carts speed when you own it or decreases it's speed when moving backwards.

I would probably lean towards removing the mini bosses inside the building too, and just have a win happen when the gate is blown.
