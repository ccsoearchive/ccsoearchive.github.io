  • Is this still the case as of 10/9?
  • Is the buggy nature of our casting bars associated with this change simply the result of PTR lag in large cities and will it work well outside of them and on live servers?
  • Is the new shot speed, combined with two quite good set bonuses going to be enough to keep hunters viable in the next tier?
  • Will the fact that making 1.5 seconds on cobra being more of a buff than 1.5 seconds on steady make this a slightly bigger buff for BM and Survival than it is for MM? (After all, a bigger number able to be put up more often is going to mean more than a smaller number put up more often.)
  • Will the fact that we now get twice as much focus .5 seconds faster let us cast more arcanes, leading to more arcane crits, lead to enough pet focus regen that we can turn back on 2/2 of Wild Hunt thereby further upping BM DPS?
  • Will slightly higer iLevel also help add in the crit percentage to further help in this regard?

    • My personal opinion is that all of these factors, taken together will go a pretty long way towards helping BM climb a few places. We might break out of the bottom three specs for raid dps for the first time in three years! We still won't be above average but things will get better. I still feel pretty bad for Survival hunters though.

    So here is my challenge to other players. List one or two changes that can be made in a patch that will finish the job if all of the above are true. The rules, however, for my challenge are as follows: You don't get to remake the class. You don't get to design any new abilities. And you don't get to change the mechanics of how any existing abilities work. I want to give Blizzard a list of TWEAKS that they can make with numbers only. (Literally, things they could do in a half hour of work on Monday morning.) Here are mine:

  • Change the cost of pet basic bite, claw, and smack from 25 to 22.
  • Change the cost of dash and dive to zero.
