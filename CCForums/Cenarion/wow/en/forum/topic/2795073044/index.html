PUBLIC NOTICE: RENEWAL PROJECT AT CAER DARROW

Priests, shaman and other clergy sought to help in putting the unnamed dead to rest!

Cross-faction effort to begin at 7:30 local time, at the Isle of Caer Darrow in the Western Plaguelands.

Every week the AAMS invites members of the Horde and the Alliance to join us in helping to renew the abandoned settlement at Caer Darrow a little bit further. It is our hope that, in time, the Caer Darrow Renewal Project will offer a haven for peaceful members of both factions. But at the moment there is much left to do!

This week we are putting out a special call for priests or other religious leaders able and willing to help us put the many nameless bones and bodies that litter the island to rest. It is our hope that proper rites will help quiet the ghosts that still whisper about the empty city.

More practically, we hope to discourage the necromantic practices of the Scholomance by removing one of their primary sources of bodies for experimentation. Anyone not comfortable with performing funeral rites is invited to join us in digging and filling graves instead! The AAMS will try to have shovels and other tools on hand.

As with all CDRP events, we also welcome any business ventures to the island and highly encourage anyone to join us as a "vendor for an evening." The AAMS has materials and start-up cash to donate if you wish to establish a market at Caer Darrow.

The CDRP is a neutral, peaceful event. Translations will be available through the AAMS.

Andelia Windtouched
Branch Manager, AAMS Horde

The AAMS extends its thanks and congratulations to everyone involved!
