Citizens of Stormwind!


To celebrate the public opening of our new headquarters in the Cathedral District ((represented by the city hall building)), we will be hosting an open house on November 19th from 1pm - 5pm ((server)).

Keeping the peace isn't just making arrests, and we want the entire community to have an opportunity to experience the wide variety of work we do. Officers will be present the entire afternoon to answer questions and pass along crime prevention and safety tips. Food and drink will also be provided.

In addition, several special demonstrations are planned:

1:00: Official welcome
1:30: The medical unit will demonstrate how to conduct an autopsy
2:30: The K9 unit will demonstrate how dogs can be used to locate and capture suspects.
3:30: The riot unit will demonstrate how its mammoths are used to break up riots
4:30: Special medal presentation ceremony

Attendees will also be able to win prizes throughout the afternoon! One of the prizes we'll be giving away is a Guardian Cub.

If you are interested in learning more about how the Watch protects the kingdom or want to know more about how you can help keep yourself and your loved ones safe, we hope you will attend!

- Officer Chambliss Acton
Acting Public Information Officer
