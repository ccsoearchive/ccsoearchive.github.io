Mark your calendars! On April 14th at 7PM (server time), Grim Fate is hosting a fabulous fashion show to kick off this spring with a bit of pizazz!

Come celebrate springtime with us and have a little fun as we goof around and dress up! Everyone is invited to watch, and anyone can enter! It will take place in Dalaran! If your character can't get to Dalaran, don't worry. We'll have mages hired to port you to the event!

The show will be judged by a panel of 5 members of Grim Fate and hosted by yours truly! We're all super excited to see what everyone comes up with! We have two categories with some amazing prizes totaling up to 20,000 gold!

Category One: General Best Dressed. Our judges will pick which outfits they like the best. (1st place wins 5k in cash, 2nd place wins 3k in cash, and 3rd place wins 1k in cash!)

Category Two: Booby Outfits! Your whackiest, craziest, ugliest, or funniest outfits! There will only be one winner in this category. (A disgusting prize is awarded to this winner!)

The rules for entering the contest are pretty simple: All outfits must be a valid transmog outfit on your character. This means you can't mix armor types. We will allow you to wear legendaries, though!

To enter, just show up about 15 minutes early!

((This event is not a standard RP event as many people probably won't be in character, but you're welcome to RP if you like!))
