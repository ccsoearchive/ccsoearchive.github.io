Massive post incoming. You have been warned.

Death Knight
  • Disciple of Acherus: The death knight summons a loyal disciple from Acherus to fight alongside him/her. Depending on your spec, the disciple will behave differently, and bestow different buffs. Disciples of Blood will increase his master's stamina by 15%, and will fight using physical attacks. Disciples of Frost will increase his master's strength by 10% and the amount of Frost damage dealt by the same amount, and will attack using frost magic. Disciples of Unholy will increase the death knight and his ghoul's strength by 5% each and increase the damage dealt by diseases by 10%, and will fight with shadow magic. Disciples are considered guardians, so they will not override Unholy death knight ghouls. They will fight alongside you for one minute or until killed.
    1 Blood/Frost/Unholy (depending on spec); 1.5 Second Cast; 5 Minute Cooldown


Druid
  • Gushing Wounds (Feral): An instant claw attack that inflicts physical damage and causes the target to bleed. If the target moves, the duration of the bleed is reset. This cannot happen more than once every three seconds.
    45 Energy/Rage; Instant Cast; 15 Second Cooldown

  • Seal of Sun/Moon (Balance): Places a seal on the target, inflicting Arcane or Nature damage over time and increasing Arcane and Nature damage taken depending on the seal. The type of seal placed depends on which Eclipse it is.
    8% Base Mana; Instant Cast; 5 Second Cooldown

  • Refreshing Wind (Restoration): Summons a cool breeze that heals all allies within 20 yards of the druid. Players who remain in the area of effect for more than four seconds receive the Refreshed buff, which increases the amount the player is healed for by 20% for 20 seconds.
    8% Base Mana; 2.5 Second Cast; 25 Second Cooldown


Hunter
  • Trailing Shot: An instant shot that inflicts damage and causes the target to bleed. Additionally, a trail is left behind, allowing the hunter to track his prey.
    45 Focus; Instant Cast; 15 Second Cooldown

  • Keen Senses: Instructs your pet to sniff out stealthed targets, revealing to the hunter his foe's location.
    40 Focus; Instant Cast; 2 Minute Cooldown


Mage
  • Summon Phoenix (Fire): Calls a phoenix from the sky that bombards the mage's target with fire. Lasts for 1 minute or until canceled.
    12% Base Mana; 3 Second Cast; 2 Minute Cooldown

  • Summon Roc (Frost): Calls a roc from the sky that bombards the mage's target with frost. Lasts for 1 minute or until canceled.
    12% Base Mana; 3 Second Cast; 2 Minute Cooldown

  • Aura Bolt (Arcane): Flings a bolt of magic at the target, inflicting Arcane damage.
    4% Base Mana; Instant Cast; 1 Second Cooldown


Paladin
  • Holy Strike: Strikes the target, inflicting 225% weapon strength as holy damage. The damage inflicted is increased by 33% for each amassed point of Holy Power, but does not consume it. Additionally, the target will be burned for 110% of the damage inflicted over 12 seconds.
    26% Base Mana; Instant Cast; 6 Second Cooldown


Priest
  • Aegis of the Mind/Body (Discipline):[/b] Places a protective barrier on the target that lasts for one minute. These barriers can absorb an infinite amount of damage for their duration. Once used, the target receives a debuff preventing them from being shielded by the same Aegis for three minutes. Additionally, Aegis of the Body only protects from physical damage, and Aegis of the Mind only offers immunity to magic. Neither Aegis can be active on a target that is already protected by the other.
    20% Base Mana; 1.5 Second Cast; 15 Second Cooldown

  • Holy Ray (Holy): Rays of light shine forth from the priest's fingertips, healing all allies caught within it's 8 yard cone. Additionally, the healed targets are blessed, and will heal for 25% of their total health over 10 seconds.
    15% Base Mana; Instant Cast; 20 Second Cooldown

  • Mind Burst (Shadow): Fills the target's mind with shadow energy and causing a debuff called Psychic Overload, which increases shadow damage taken by 2% for 15 seconds and stacks up to 5 times. After the duration on the fifth stack, the target's mind explodes, causing shadow damage to all targets within 25 yards. Once this occurs, the target is afflicted with Psychic Fatigue, which reduces attack haste by 20% for 10 seconds.
    7% Base Mana; 1 Second Cast; 10 Second Cooldown


Rogue
  • Assassin's Rush: Propels the rogue forward 20 yards. Doesn't break stealth, but cannot be used indoors.
    35 Energy; Instant Cast; 10 Second Cooldown


Shaman
  • Whirling Flame (Elemental): Summons a great tornado of fire that roots the target in place and inflicts fire damage over time. This effect lasts for 10 seconds. Additionally, the target is burned, increasing fire damage inflicted by 10% for 20 seconds.
    18% Base Mana; 2.5 Second Cast; 10 Second Cooldown

  • Thunder Strike (Enhancement): Strikes the target with both weapons, calling down a bolt of lighting that inflicts 210% weapon damage as Nature damage. Additionally, the target is shocked, reducing overall speed by 10% and inflicting 110% of the damage dealt by the initial strike over 10 seconds.
    22% Base Mana; Instant Cast; 20 Second Cooldown

  • Tsunami (Restoration: Summons a great wave that heals all allies caught within. This ability consumes all stacks of Tidal Wave, increasing the amount it heals for by 25% per stack.
    32% Base Mana; 3 Second Cast; 30 Second Cooldown


Warlock
  • Eyes of the Demon (Demonology): Allows the warlock to take control of his demon for 1 minute 30 seconds, plus an additional minute per Soul Shard. If the warlock takes damage, it is canceled.
    30% Base Mana; 1 Second Cast; 20 Second Cooldown

  • Meteor (Destruction): Summons a great meteor from the Nether that inflicts large amounts of fire damage to all targets within a 20 yard radius and stuns them for 8 seconds.
    30% Base Mana; 3 Second Cast; 2 Minute Cooldown

  • Consuming Plague (Affliction): Afflicts the target with a disease that causes shadow damage over time. Each time the disease ticks, the amount of damage inflicted is increased by 25%. The disease is highly contagious, spreading to other targets within 8 yards. Every time the disease spreads, the overall damage it inflicts is reduced by 20%. It cannot spread more than 5 times per initial duration.
    10% Base Mana; 1 Second Cast; 1 Second Cooldown


Warrior
  • Titanic Strike (Arms): An instant weapon strike that inflicts physical damage to all targets in front of the warrior and knocks them back 10 yards. Additionally, all targets impacted by this ability are dazed for 4 seconds. If used against a target that is casting or channeling, the spell or ability is interrupted.
    30 Rage; Instant Cast; 15 Second Cooldown

  • Adrenaline Burst: The warrior enters a heightened state, increasing melee haste by 40% while it is active.
    25 Rage; Instant Cast; 5 Minute Cooldown

First off, don't be dissing my Arcane Shot. It adds some much needed flavor to the admittedly bland Marks hunter rotation, and I use it extensively while questing when Aimed Shot and Chimera Shot fail to take down a target. It's godlike for kiting.

Anyhow, Aura Bolt is roughly along the same lines as Fel Flame for warlocks: a cheap, quick, instant cast ability that would fit snugly in between larger, more powerful spells offering extra DPS. Also note that it's 4% of your base mana, i.e. the amount of mana you have before Intellect from gear and such is taken into consideration. It's really not a lot.

And on the subject of the phoenix/roc abilities, it wouldn't make much sense for a fire mage to use an ice bird or a frost mage to use a fire bird, now would it?
