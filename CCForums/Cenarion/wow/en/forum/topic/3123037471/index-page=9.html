4 Class final abilities down, 6 to go:

Death Knights:

Blood
Sanguine Harvest
2 Blood 1 Unholy
Cooldown 30 Minutes
Channeled (5 Seconds)
The Death Knight begins siphoning blood out of every enemy in 10 yards, dealing damage equal to 5% of their max health every second, and healing the Death Knight for 50% of the damage done. Once the Death Knight finishes channeling, 1 Blood-Gorged Fleshfiend will spawn for every X damage dealt. The Blood-Gorged Fleshfiends will assist the Death Knight by attacking his target, reducing all damage he takes by 10% and healing him for 20% of his max health on death. Blood-Gorged Fleshfiends will last for 2 minutes. This spell cannot be Interrupted.

Frost
Arctic Dungeon
2 Frost 1 Death
30 Minute Cooldown
Instant Cast
Freezes every enemy within 20 yards of the Death Knight for 10 seconds, inflicting Frost Fever and creating a patch of Black Ice in a 20 yard radius beneath the Death Knight. Black Ice deals X Frost damage per second to every enemy standing on it and refreshes the duration of Frost Fever each time it does damage.

Unholy
Judgment of the Reaper
2 Unholy 1 Blood
30 Minute Cooldown
Instant Cast
Transforms your Ghoul into an Avatar of Death for 30 seconds. While transformed, your Ghoul becomes immune to crowd control, deals 60% increased damage, is immune to damage and gains new abilities. While your Ghoul is transformed, you gain The Reaper's Blessing, increasing damage done by 30%, increases your Rune regeneration by 150% and makes you immune to crowd control.

Paladins...

Holy
Holy Song
20% of Max mana
30 Minute Cooldown
3 Second Cast
Sings a blessed song, increasing all party and raid members with 40 yards damage done by 25%, healing done and healing received by 50%, makes them immune to poison, disease and fear effects and reduces damage taken by 25% for 30 seconds. Players affected by this spell cannot be affected by it again for 30 minutes.

Protection
Shining Bind
40% of Max Mana
30 Minute Cooldown
Instant Cast
Creates a ring of light at your feet, stunning all enemies within 20 yards for 6 seconds, dealing X Holy damage and pulling them into melee range. After 2 seconds, the ring of light calls down a column of divine fury, dealing x Holy damage every second for 4 seconds.

Retribution
Exterminatus
3 Holy Power
30 Minute Cooldown
Instant Cast
Empowers you with divine fury, making you immune to crowd control, increasing damage dealt by 50%, reduces damage taken by 50%, reduces the mana cost of all spells and abilties by 100%, reduces the cooldown on Templar's Verdict and Divine Storm by 100% and allows you to use all abilities as though you had 3 Holy Power for 30 seconds.
