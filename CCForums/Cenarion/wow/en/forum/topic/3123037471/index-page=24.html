Shaman/Elemental:

Death Knight/Blood:

Path of blood: the death knight leaves a trail of blood in their wake, if the death knight stands on this bloodied ground, he/she regenerates health by an additional 100% normal healing, this effect also regenerates the health of nearby party members with every strike that requires a Blood rune(so spamming heart strike will add HP regen to the group, this will help healers in big battles), this effect does not stack with other death knights present(to make it not so OP in PVP). 5 minute cooldown.
