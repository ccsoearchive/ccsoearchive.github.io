Mages...

Arcane:
Spatial Distortion
30% of Max Mana
30 Minute Cooldown
3 Second Cast

Fire:
Summon Phoenix
40% of Max Mana
30 Minute Cooldown
Instant Cast
Summons a Phoenix to assist the Mage until it is killed. The Phoenix will attack the Mage's target and has Burning Feathers, which deals X Fire damage to all enemies within 5 yards of the Phoenix every second. When the Phoenix is killed, it will leave behind a Phoenix Egg, which deals X Fire damage to all enemies within 5 yards every second and exploding after 10 seconds, dealing X Fire damage to all enemies to all enemies in 15 yards and spawning a new Phoenix. The Phoenix can only be permanently killed by destroying the Egg. The Phoenix and Egg will fade after the Mage spends 1 minute out of combat. This spell's cooldown does not start until the Phoenix disappears or is killed.

Frost:
Tidal Wave
30% of Max Mana
30 Minute Cooldown
Channeled (5 seconds)
Creates a seething vortex of frigid water around the Mage, dealing X Frost damage to every enemy in 30 yards and Freezing them for 15 seconds. The damage from Tidal Wave cannot break your Freeze effects. In addition, while channeling Tidal Wave your Water Elemental gains Frozen Supremacy, increasing its casting speed by 200% and damage done by 50%. This spell cannot be interrupted.
