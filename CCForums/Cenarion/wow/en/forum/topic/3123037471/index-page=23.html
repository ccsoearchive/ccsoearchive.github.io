Thirdegree
Profile
Thirdegree
85 Blood Elf Mage
Battle Rezzed
9920
Evolution (30 min CD, no mana cost.)

Molten Evolution
Requires molten armor.
Gives up all hope of survival in return for the chance to kill the opponent.
The mage becomes infused with the flame of his armor for 20 seconds, absorbing ALL DAMAGE TAKEN. The mage’s Spell power is increased at a 1:1 ratio by damage taken. Crit chance is increased by 50%. All enemies are dealt 3k damage per second, increased by spell power. At the end of this effect, all damage dealt to the mage over the duration is instantly applied, times 100 (basically, it kills him unless he was dueling a level 1). In addition, all armors are put on a 5 min CD, and on the off chance the mage doesn’t die, his damage is reduced by 50% for 2 min.

Frost Evolution
Requires frost armor.
Moderates attack and defense.
The mage becomes infused with the ice of his armor for 20 seconds. All enemies that attack him are slowed by 70%. This counts as half of a freeze for damaging components, and a full one for the shatter effect. (If a frozen mob would take 100 increased damage with a normal freeze, with this they’ll take 50 increased damage.) Using this evolution chills the mage to the bone, reducing movement speed after the effect is done by 50% and damage by 50% for 2 min or until death. In addition, all armors are put on a 5 min CD.

Arcane Evolution
Requires Arcane armor.
Gives up all chance of a kill in the hope of escape.
The mage becomes infused with arcane powers, instantly making him invisible and invulnerable to all mobs and players, and all players invisible to him. Lasts until canceled. For every min spent in the arcane realm, all stats are reduced by 10%. This effect CAN NOT KILL THE MAGE. However, his HP, mana, spellpower, and all other stats can be reduced to 1. This reduction lasts as long as the mage was in the arcane realm, or until death. In addition, all armors are put on a 5 min CD.

Note: NOTHING can clear the debuffs after the use of this ability, not even ice block. They are not dispellable in any way, other then death. The damage from molten evolution can not be mitigated in any way, including immunities (ice block) and will not trigger cauterize, bypassing it entirely to simply kill the mage. The cool down on armors applies even after death (obvious imo, but it should be noted explicitly.) Also, the debuff timers start when the mage is OUT OF COMBAT. These abilities are for FUN. Clearly for balancing purposes they couldn’t be allowed in arenas or rated battle grounds, and the damage debuffs after use should be enough to discourage use in serious pve encounters. Again, I was not thinking of balance, pve, pvp, or any other aspect of this game but FUN when I came up with these.
Side note: I do think every class in the game should get “fun” abilities such as this, but mage happens to be the only one I have experience with.
