Discipline:
Prayer of Divine Protection
30% of Max Mana
30 Minute Cooldown
Channeled (10 Seconds)
The Priest invokes the gods of healing to protect them and their allies from harm. All friendly party or raid members within 40 yards gain a shield protecting them from all damage as long as the Priest is channeling. This spell cannot be interrupted and may be cast while Silenced. Targets affected by the spell cannot be affected by it again for 30 minutes.

Holy:
Sacred Shine
30% of Max Mana
10 Minute Cooldown
3 Second Cast
Unleashes a burst of shining holy energy, healing all friendly targets within 40 yards for a minimum of 25% of their max health, and dealing x Holy damage to all enemies within 30 yards, and disorienting them for 3 seconds.

Shadow:
Violet Pain
15% of Max Mana
10 Minute Cooldown
3 Second Cast
Stuns and immobilizes the target for 6 seconds and creates a Well of Shadows at their feet, lasting 6 seconds. Enemies standing in the Well of Shadows take x Shadow damage per second. After 2 seconds a group of Shadowy Tendrils will rise from the Well of Shadows and blast the primary target and all targets with 5 yards for an additional x Shadow damage over 4 seconds. Targets immune to stun and immobilize effects will instead take x Shadow damage.

Warlocks...

Affliction:
Nether Plague
20% of Max Mana
30 Minute Cooldown
2 Second Cast
Inflicts an otherworldly plague upon the target, causing them to take x Shadow damage every 5 seconds until the target dies. Nether Plague cannot be dispelled and spreads your Nether Plague, Corruption, Unstable Affliction and Bane spells to all enemies with 10 yards every time it deals damage. In addition, every time Nether Plague deals damage, the durations of your Corruption, Unstable Affliction, Bane and Curse spells is refreshed.

Demonology:
Gift of the Legion
Passive
Transforms your Metamorphosis into Demon Form, removing it's cooldown and duration, but armor bonus gained while in Demon Form is reduced to 300%. In addition, all of your demon minions take 20% less damage, deal 10% more damage and have their health increased by 15%

Destruction:
Towering Inferno
40% of Max Mana
30 Minute Cooldown
Instant Cast
Engulfs the Warlock in a column of demonic fire, dealing X Shadowflame damage every second to all enemies with 30 yards. In addition, the area of effect from Hand Of Gul'dan is increased by 100%, its damage is increased by 30% and its cooldown is reduced by 5 seconds and Incinerate has its cast time reduced by 100%. Lasts for 20 seconds.
