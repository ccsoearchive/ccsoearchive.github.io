Made about 3 spells per class.

=WARNING, EXTREMELY LONG, Class names are bolded and underlined, skip to the ones you're interested in seeing.=

Most of the spells are powerful ones with large drawbacks (like costs or a debuff), which would make them more of a strategic decision rather than one that they could use to stomp everything to dust.

Some are rather simple ones that would serve a nice purpose.

Hunter

Stampede
Spec Focus: Beast Mastery
10 minute CD, 80 focus cost, 8 seconds channeled.

Calls every single pet you can currently call (Randomly will summon a pet from your stable if your unable to control exotic creatures and they are on one of your call pets/You don't have 5 pets on you.) to attack for one minute. Any pet summoned can share in pet + master buffs (Such as beastial wrath.). Any pet that isn't your primary pet that is killed while stampede is up will not require revival. (So no stampede popping, then someone kills your pets and you need to go down a list of 5 pets to revive them all.).

While stampede is active the hunter takes 20% less damage but personally deals 20% less damage. Kill command will only register on your primary pet. (So no 5 pet kill command trololol one shots.) Non-Primary pets will be unable to cast special pet race abilities.

Improvise (Would have an icon of an angry tauren.)
Spec Focus: Survival/Any
3 minute CD, 30 focus, Instant

The hunter improvises to the situation, regenerating 5% health per second. Lasts 5 seconds.

Snipe
Spec focus: Marksmanship. (Still works well with any spec.)
3 minute CD, 65 focus, 3 second cast time, 50 yd range, 25 minimum range.

The hunter snipes a far off target for 175% ranged weapon damage, as well as causing a bleed effect that does 20% of the damage inflicted by Snipe over time for 10 seconds. Cannot be used in close range.

Death Knight

Rage of Death
Spec Focus: Unholy
4 minute CD, Costs one of each rune and 40 runic power. Instant cast.

The Death knight goes into a murderous killing frenzy, enraging them for 15 seconds. While enraged, all runes become death runes, and the death knight receives a 10% attack damage and attack speed boost as well as runic regeneration increase. Runic power cannot be gained through abilities while active, rather it regenerates 5 RP per second. Any damage the death knight receives during the enrage has 5% absorbed, once this ability has ended they receive 3% of the damage absorbed.

Engorged Assault
Spec focus: Blood/Any DPS spec.
10 minute CD, Requires two blood runes, Channeled assault.

The Death knight flies into a blood fed rage on the targeted enemy for 10 seconds, doing constant thrashing attacks for 110% weapon damage or 55% main hand weapon damage + 55% off hand weapon damage if dual wielding, The Death knight will also be healed for 5% of the damage caused per swing. While using this ability the death knight is unable to attack any other enemy and cannot use abilities. Engorged assault causes the death knight to have a 50% reduction to all damage done to the death knight. The target is rooted in place for the duration of the assault and diseases (not including spec disease specials such as ebon plaguebringer.) are automatically placed on the target for the duration of the assault.

Once over the death knight will receive a 25% increase to damage received for 10 seconds. Blood death knights will not receive this reduction, instead it consumes any blood shield currently active and prevents the DK from gaining one for 10 seconds. The Death Knight will be slowed for 4 seconds if interupted.(So people don't just bum rush people in PvP with it without repercussions.)

Chill of the Grave
Spec Focus: Frost/DPS
10 minute CD, Costs two frost runes, 2 second cast time. 25yd range. Duration of 20 seconds.

The death knight radiates an aura of frost, causing any enemies within a 25yd range to have 5% increase to all magical damage received by them as well as being slowed as long as they remain in range of the death knight's aura. Alternatively, the death knight can use the ability on one target, causing them to be stunned for 3 seconds and taking 135% (180% main hand damage for DW) weapon damage. If the target is immune to stuns they will be silenced for 3 seconds instead.

Paladin

Righteous Sacrifice
Spec Focus: Retribution mostly. Still affective for Holy, moderately affective for prot.
5 minute CD, Costs 25% of the paladin's maximum health, Instant. Lasts 15 seconds.

The paladin sacrifices a part of their own life force to empower their attacks with the power of the light. While activated the paladin enters an enraged holy state, increasing all damage by 10%, attack speed/casting speed by 5% and causes all abilities requiring holy power to be usable without. The paladin cannot gain holy power in this state, and any ability that uses holy power while used in this state does their effect equiviliant to if the paladin had 2 charges of holy power.

Casting Guardian of the Ancient Kings while in this state will cause the paladin to transform into their guardian, Causing an additional 2% increase to attack and casting speeds, and taking a reduced 5% damage.

The paladin's health will not go above 75% while in this state, Divine shield and Avenging Wrath cannot be used in this state. (The paladin will receive forbearance when this ability is activated.). The paladin will also receive 5% reduced healing.

Aegis of Faith
Spec Focus: Holy
5 minute cooldown, instant cast, Costs 20% of the paladin's maximum mana. Lasts 10 seconds. 25yd range.

The paladin calls upon the light to protect themselves or a targeted ally for 10 seconds. While protected the target has all mind controlling or movement impairing affects removed, and receives a 50% damage reduction (30% for the paladin) to themselves. If used on a target other than the paladin, the target will also gain 30% increased movement speed and will be healed 5% of their maximum health per second while active.

Cannot be used in conjunction with any ability that would give Forbearance. (It too gives forbearance). Lay on hands will only heal the target for 40% of the normal amount if casted on a target with this ability. Grants two charges of holy power if casted on someone other than the paladin.

(Basicly would be good for high damage situations in pvp/raids or when things need to be avoided/move quickly, such as people getting out of the fire in a boss fight/escaping from hairy situations in PvP.)

Eternal Paragon
Spec Focus: Prot
10 minute CD, Costs 3 holy power charges and 10% of the paladin's maximum mana. Lasts 30 seconds, 3 second cast.

The paladin infuses themselves with the power of the light, while empowered the paladin is immune to movement impairing affects, gains 10% damage reduction, receives 15% increased healing to themselves, and any ability that would strike one target strikes an additional nearby target. (Including judgement.). The paladin also gains 5% additional health, this health is lost once the effect is over.

Mage

Brilliance Aura
Spec Focus: Arcane
Costs 20% of the Mage's maximum mana, Aura radius of 25yds, lasts two minutes.

The Mage empowers their very presence with their wisdom, causing the mage and all nearby allies to regenerate 5% of the mage's maximum mana every 5 seconds for two minutes. The Mage regenerates an additional 1% mana for every ally in the area of the aura, up to a maximum of 5% additional mana regeneration. While active, all of the mage's spells cost an additional 4% mana.

Brilliance aura does not stack, and everyone who receives the aura receives a debuff preventing them from gaining the aura's affects again for 7 minutes. (No mage aura spam.)

Engulfing Phoenix
Spec Focus: Fire
Cost: 20% of the Mage's maximum mana. 3 second cast, 40yd range. 10 minute cool down.

The Mage engulfs the targeted enemy with flame, causing 10,000 damage over 15 seconds (Plus an additional 5% of the mage's total spell power.). After the duration is up/the affect is dispelled, a phoenix will burst from the enemy, engulfing them with another 10k worth of fire damage. The phoenix will then come under the mage's command, doing passive AoE damage to those nearby, as well as casting Phoenix fire, which is similar to fireball.

The Phoenix lasts for one minute, If the ability kills the target, the phoenix will last for an additional minute. If slain the phoenix will turn into an egg for 10 seconds. If killed in this form the mage regains 5% of the mana required to cast the spell, if not the phoenix will be reborn. The reborn phoenix inflicts 50% reduced damage, cannot be controlled via pet bar, loses the AoE damage, and lasts another 10 seconds. It cannot be reborn and the mage receives no mana if the phoenix is slain.

Fire spec can also gain the ability to make the phoenix into a permanent pet. It loses 5% of the damage it'd cause as a summon. Engulfing Phoenix will be turned into Summon Phoenix, which does no damage. The AoE affect must also be activated and is a channeled spell. The phoenix does however gain blazing fury, which causes it to attack faster and the mage to cast faster for 5 seconds by 5%.

Frozen Impalement
Spec Focus: Frost
Costs the mage's Ice Block ability, Instant cast, can only be used while ice blocked, 45yd range.

The Mage unleashes power from within their ice block, shattering it sending empowered frozen ice shards out at all enemies in a 45 yd radius. The shards do 15,000 damage (Plus an additional 10% to frozen targets.) and leave patches of empowered frost on the area where the enemy was struck. The patches are about 10yds wide, and while standing on them enemies are slowed, as well as taking 450 frost damage per 2 seconds while standing on the patches. Patches last 30 seconds. If a mage stands on a patch, they gain 5% additional damage with all frost spells.

Frozen impalement increases the duration of Hypothermia by an additional 2 minutes.

Priest

Divine Will
Spec Focus: Holy
Costs 20% of the priest's maximum mana, Cast time of 2 seconds, 35yd range. One minute thirty second duration, Cooldown of 10 minutes.

The priest taps into their strongest abilities, transforming into a spirit of redemption. Similarly to spirit of redemption, The priest can cast any spell free of mana cost. In addition, the priest enters a state of unending chakra. The priest cannot move while divine will is active. This ability cannot be used in Shadow Form, and non-holy priests will not receive the benefit of unending chakra, As well as suffering a 10% reduced cast time for all spells following the usage of this ability.

Once over, The priest is significantly weakened. They receive an additional 25% damage taken debuff for 30 seconds, are slowed by 30% for 30 seconds, and are unable to gain the chakra affect for 30 seconds. Divine will also prevents the ability to activate spirit of redemption upon death for 10 minutes. (The priest would receive Faltering Will for the 30 second debuff and the 10 minute debuff to prevent recasting of this spell/spirit of redemption would be weakened will.)

Shield of Divinity
Spec Focus: Discipline
Cost, 15% of the priest's maximum mana, Requires a Power Word: Shield to be active on the target, Will consume the shield.

The Priest embues the target with additional energy, empowering the Power Word: Shield on a target. The shield now lasts 2 minutes, absorbing 5% of all damage caused, and reducing damage the target takes by 15%. The shield is not consumed and cannot absorb full damage from attacks. In addition, Weakened Soul's debuff time is raised (Or reapplied) for another 2 minutes.

Descending Darkness
Spec Focus: Shadow
Cost, 15% of the priest's maximum mana, Requires 3 shadow orbs, consumes the orbs. Consumes Shadow form if active.

The priest empowers their spells with the darkest and strongest of shadow energies. Depending on the next spell casted the affect varies.

The next mind spike casted will inflict 150% additional damage, as well as leaving the target stunned with a DoT (Engulfing Shadows) that inflicts an additional 5% of the damage caused over 10 seconds.

If Mind sear is casted on an enemy target, They will become surrounded by engulfing shadows, dealing AoE and slowing damage to all nearby enemies in 15yds, Causing Mind sears original damage with an additional 50%.

If disperse is casted, Any enemy near the priest is knocked back, and the priest regains an additional 20% mana, and 10% health.

If Shadow Word: Death is casted, The target will take 50% of the priest's maximum mana as damage. (Shadow Word: Death will do no original damage.). The priest will take 25% of the damage caused. This damage cannot crit.

Once any empowered spell is used, the priest is unable to enter shadowform for 30 seconds, and receives a 5% movement speed reduction. Non-shadow priests do not require shadow orbs, But all affects do 70% reduced damage.

Shaman

Elemental Empowerment
Spec focus: Elemental
Costs, 15% of the shaman's mana, consumes a specific totem. 3 second cast time. Requires totem corresponding to the element used to be up.

The shaman empowers their next shock spell (including wind shear) with the powers of that element's corresponding totem. Depending on the spell used the affect varies.

Earth Shock: The target will be crushed by an extremely large boulder, knocking them back and stunning them for 3 seconds. In addition to the spell's original damage, 25% additional damage is added. Fulmination damage does not add to the additional damage. Any earth element totem is consumed and the shaman cannot place another for 2 minutes. If the target is immune to stuns or knockbacks, an additional 5% damage is added.

Fire Shock: The target is engulfed in searing flames, causing damage equivalent to that of a Lava surge over 20 seconds in addition to the original flame shock damage. Lava surge can still crit. Any fire element totem is consumed, and cannot be placed for 2 minutes. Searing totems cannot be placed for an additional minute onto that.

Frost Shock: The target is bombarded by the elements of water in addition to the cold, rooting and stunning the target for 3 seconds. The next spell will be a guarenteed crit on the target, and will do an additional 30% damage ontop of that. Any water element totem is consumed and cannot be replaced for 3 minutes. 5% additional damage is added if the target is immune to stun affects.

Wind Shear: Rather than damage the target, An air elemental will be summoned to fight by the shaman's side for 5 minutes. The air elemental can be controlled via pet bar. Wind shear will still interupt. The air elemental can cast lightning bolt, tempest fury (An AoE slowing affect), chain lightning, and harsh winds (A DoT that does Lightning bolt damage over 5 seconds.). Air element totems are consumed and cannot be replaced for 10 minutes.

Non-Elemental shamans will find all damage affects reduced by 25%, Frost shock's affect will not stun. Elemental shamans can also spec to have a permanent air elemental companion. Wind Shear is turned into Summon Air Elemental. The air elemental's harsh winds affect loses it's dot, instead being replaced with an interupt similar to wind shear to compensate for the loss of the interupt spell.

Lava Shield
Spec Focus: Enhancement
No mana cost, instant cast, can only be cast on self.

The shaman calls upon the element of fire to shield them, while active, any affect Lightning shield may have had from the shaman's spec (Not including Fulmination from Elemental) is applied to Lava shield. Lava shield does not have any charges, when the shaman is struck the enemy receives fire damage, and any enemy in the area of the shaman will take AoE fire damage.

Ethereal Form
Spec Focus: Restoration
20% of the shaman's maximum mana, Instant cast, Lasts 2 minutes. 10 minute cooldown.

The shaman embraces the spirits of the elements around them, entering a spirit form. While in Ethereal form, the shaman takes 20% reduced damage, can cast any healing spell while moving. Spells also have their cast times reduced by 10% (Only resto spells.) and their mana costs cut in half. While in ethereal form, the shaman can only cast Restoration spells or non-damaging spells.

Warlock

Consuming Embrace
Spec focus: Affliction
Costs 30% of the warlock's maximum mana, 3 second cast, 10 minute cool down. Consumes present minion, consumes a soul shard.

The warlock consumes part of their mana and the life force of their summoned minion to unleash the force of all present DoT spells on the target from the Warlock. The DoTs must be shadow in nature (Immolate does not count), and will do 50% of the total damage of each DoT instantly. All DoTs are consumed from the target, in addition, the foe will receive damage equal to 40% (25% for void walkers) of the minion's health. This ability also takes a soul shard and cannot be empowered by Soulburn.

Touch of Sargeras
Spec Focus: Destro
Cost: 20% of the warlock's mana, Consumes their present minion, Takes one soul shard. Consumes any fire related dots on the target. 10 minute CD.

The warlock consumes as much destructive energy as they can, including their minion's life, and empowers fire spells. Depending on the spell the affect varies.

Immolation will engulf the target in searing flames, causing original DoT damage instantly, as well as placing a DoT on the target with an additional 5% damage increase.

Chaos Bolt will deal 50% additional damage.

Incinerate will leave a DoT that does 50% of the damage caused over 10 seconds, in addition to causing 10% additional damage.

Unholy Pact
Spec Focus: Demonology
Costs 20% of the warlock's maximum mana, Consumes their minion and a soul shard. Cooldown of 10 minutes. Lasts 2 minutes.

The warlock empowers their own soul with that of their demonic minion, transforming them into their demon.

Imp: The warlock turns into a large hellfire imp, all fire spells have their damage increased by 30% (10% for non-demonology warlocks.). They also move 20% faster and take 30% additional damage.

Voidwalker: The warlock turns into an empowered void walker, granting an extremely large boost to their health and defense capacities while active. The warlock has increased stealth detection and Mana tap causes a shield to appear that absorbs 20% of their maximum mana in damage. They also move 10% slower.

Succubus: The warlock turns into an empowered succubus, Increasing the damage of all shadow spells by 20% (5% for non-demonology warlocks). DoTs also last an additional 5 seconds. The warlock's detect invisibility spell turns the warlock invisible for 10 seconds. Fear now charms the target, causing them to become hostile to their allies for 5 seconds, or stunned for 5 seconds.

Fel Guard: The warlock turns into an empowered fel guard. While in this form, the Warlock's cast time spells become melee activated and instant, increasing damage by 25% for all attacks. While in this form the warlock moves 10% faster, and takes 25% reduced damage.

You cannot summon a new demon until the spell is over.

Druid

Raven Flock
Spec Focus: Balance
Cost: 30% of the druid's maximum mana, Can only be cast while not shapeshifted. 5 second cast, 10 minute cooldown, 30 second duration.

The druid calls upon nature, summoning several ravens to ruthlessly assault the druid's enemies for 30 seconds. The ravens will disarm and attack any nearby enemy, as well as taunting those tauntable. They are uncontrollable by the druid. When the spell's duration is over, the ravens will leave a debuff on nearby enemies increasing magical damage taken by 5% for 2 minutes. The ravens can be killed, if all are killed no debuff will be given.

Serpent Form
Spec focus: Feral
Cost: 5% of the druid's maximum mana. Instant cast.

The druid takes on the form of the serpent, allowing them to cast various spells using their focus. While in serpent form, several spells are transformed and affected by agility to increase their attack power, in addition the druid will gain a snake companion while in this form. Snake form increases the damage of nature spells by 10% and allows the druid to move 5% faster.

Wrath is transformed into Serpent Strike, which deals nature damage.
Moonfire is turned into Venomous Bile, which deals nature damage over time.
Starfire is turned into Raging fangs, which causes your snake companion to attack for 120% weapon damage.
Insect Swarm is turned into marked for death, Which increases the damage of all attacks in serpent form by 5% on the target.
Faerie Fire now slows the target.

Embrace of Ysera
Spec Focus: Resto
Cost: 20% of the druid's max mana. CD of 1 minute, instant cast.

The druid draws upon the power of the emerald dream to absorb X amount of damage on their target. The shield lasts 30 seconds, or until it has absorbed X amount of damage. The target gains Touch of the Nightmare once the shield is gone, which reduces the affectiveness of Rejuvenate on the target by 5% for 30 seconds.

Warrior

Might of the Titan
Spec Focus: Arms
Cost: 50 rage, instant cast. 4 minute CD.

The warrior unleashes a devistating blow to the enemy. While the blow it's self only does 50% weapon damage, it refreshes or applies all available bleed DoTs on the target with 30 seconds, and causes the next Heroic Strike to do 150% more damage. Alternatively, the Warrior can activate Bladestorm, which causes the target to take 80% more damage and slows them. Might of the Titan can only be cast in Battle Stance and also allows for heroic strike or bladestorm to inflict damage through any sort of invulnerablity affect. (Such as divine shield or ice block.)

Grip of Destruction
Spec Focus: Fury
Cost: 50 rage, instant cast.

The warrior lunges at the enemy, causing 100% (200% if one handed) main hand weapon damage, as well as throwing the enemy back. Once the enemy lands they are stunned for 3 seconds and receive a bleed affect which does 120% of the damage caused over 20 seconds. If the target is immune to knockbacks or stuns, an additional 20% damage is added to the bleed affect, which is applied once used. Requires Berserker Stance

Pillar of Might
Spec Focus: Prot
Cost: 50 rage, Instant cast. Melee range.

The warrior entrenches himself into his position, making him immobile but reducing all damage taken by 75%, as well as immobilizing his target with him. The warrior can attack while immobilized and cannot be affected by any other form of immobilize for 30 seconds. Once the affect is over or has been canceled the warrior's next devastate or sunder armor will cause the target to receive a bleed affect that does 150% weapon damage over 30 seconds. Requires Defensive stance.

Rogue

Mercy Killing
Spec Focus: Assassination
Cost: 80 energy, requires 5 combo points, instant cast.

The rogue unleashes a quick but extremely deadly attack that deals 350% main hand damage. The target will then receive a bleed debuff for 25% of the damage inflicted over 20 seconds. If the attack fails to kill the target the rogue deals 15% reduced damage for 30 seconds.

Unfair Advantage
Spec Focus: Combat
Cost: 50 energy, instant cast, requires the rogue not be in combat.

The rogue analyzes all weaknesses in their opponent before striking. The first attack the rogue makes on the target will be a guarenteed crit, with an additional 100% damage. This attack cannot be over 25,000 damage. The target will also receive a 2 minute debuff that reduces their physical damage made by 5%. The hit must not be one made from stealth.

Shadow Stalk
Spec Focus: Subtlety
Cost: 100 energy, instant cast, requires stealth. Duration of 10 seconds.

The rogue stalks through the shadows of their opponent, using all of their energy they become untraceable for 10 seconds once they enter combat. While active, The rogue will not regenerate any energy, and all abilities gain a 2 second cooldown. The rogue will not gain combo points while active as well. Shadow stalk keeps the rogue stealthed while in combat, and all attacks do damage equivalent to 2 combo points on the target and all abilities require no energy.. Once over, the rogue comes out of stealth with 20 energy and is unable to vanish or enter stealth for another 30 seconds, even outside of combat.

Non-subtlety rogues deal 25% reduced damage while shadow stalk is active.
