-Warlock
-Demonic Evolution
-Temporarily evolves your active minion, giving it new abilities and making it more powerful. Lasts 20 seconds. 30 minute cooldown.

Imp - The warlock's imp evolves into a Fel Imp, increasing its fire damage by 20% and critical strike chance by 40%. Blood Pact heals everyone with the buff for 5% health every 4 seconds for the duration of Demonic Evolution. If the warlock has talented into Burning Embers, Burning Embers will stack up to 3 times for the duration of Demonic Evolution.

Voidwalker - The warlock's voidwalker evolves into a Voidcaller, increasing its spellpower by 30% of the warlock's intellect and increasing its stamina by 50%. The voidcaller casts shadowbolt volley, which generates a large amount of threat. All spell damage done to the voidcaller is decreased by 50%. If the voidcaller is dealt damage via magic, all damage that would be dealt by that school of spell will instead heal the voidcaller by the amount it would deal in damage. Shadowbolt Volley will then change its school depending on the damage it the voidcaller absorbs.

Succubus - The warlock's succubus evolves into a Temptress, increasing its attack speed by 20%. The Temptress's Seduction spell is replaced with Improved Seduction, which places the target under the control of the Temptress for 15 seconds. If the target is immune to Improved Seduction, the target will be afflicted with Temptress's Scorn, which deals 10,000 damage every 4 seconds for 20 seconds.

Felhunter - The warlock's felhunter evolves into a Fel Ravager, increasing all magic resistances by 60% and increasing its movement speed by 20%. The Fel Ravager is immune to taunt and will ignore all targets that have no mana and focus on attacking spellcasting targets, unless there are no spellcasting targets within 40 yards the warlock or the Fel Ravager. The Fel Ravager's Devour Magic is replaced with Improved Devour Magic which will not only remove a beneficial magic effect from the enemy, but will also give that magic effect to the warlock for 20 seconds.

Felguard - The warlock's felguard evolves into a black Felguard (with more spines coming out of its back), increasing its attack power by 30%. Its Felstorm ability, in addition to its current effects, afflicts the target with a bleed effect, which deals damage every 4 seconds for 20 seconds, dealing 20% of Felstorm's damage total. This bleed effect stacks 3 times. Its Axe Toss ability also stuns 2 additional targets in front of the Felguard. If the target is immune, Axe Toss deals 12,000 damage.


EDIT - I don't know where our health will be at 90, so I played it safe with the hard (non-percentage) damage.
