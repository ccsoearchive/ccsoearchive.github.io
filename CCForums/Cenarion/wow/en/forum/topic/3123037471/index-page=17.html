Shaman

Elemental Empowerment
Spec focus: Elemental
Costs, 15% of the shaman's mana, consumes a specific totem. 3 second cast time. Requires totem corresponding to the element used to be up.

The shaman empowers their next shock spell (including wind shear) with the powers of that element's corresponding totem. Depending on the spell used the affect varies.

Earth Shock: The target will be crushed by an extremely large boulder, knocking them back and stunning them for 3 seconds. In addition to the spell's original damage, 25% additional damage is added. Fulmination damage does not add to the additional damage. Any earth element totem is consumed and the shaman cannot place another for 2 minutes. If the target is immune to stuns or knockbacks, an additional 5% damage is added.

Fire Shock: The target is engulfed in searing flames, causing damage equivalent to that of a Lava surge over 20 seconds in addition to the original flame shock damage. Lava surge can still crit. Any fire element totem is consumed, and cannot be placed for 2 minutes. Searing totems cannot be placed for an additional minute onto that.

Frost Shock: The target is bombarded by the elements of water in addition to the cold, rooting and stunning the target for 3 seconds. The next spell will be a guarenteed crit on the target, and will do an additional 30% damage ontop of that. Any water element totem is consumed and cannot be replaced for 3 minutes. 5% additional damage is added if the target is immune to stun affects.

Wind Shear: Rather than damage the target, An air elemental will be summoned to fight by the shaman's side for 5 minutes. The air elemental can be controlled via pet bar. Wind shear will still interupt. The air elemental can cast lightning bolt, tempest fury (An AoE slowing affect), chain lightning, and harsh winds (A DoT that does Lightning bolt damage over 5 seconds.). Air element totems are consumed and cannot be replaced for 10 minutes.

Non-Elemental shamans will find all damage affects reduced by 25%, Frost shock's affect will not stun. Elemental shamans can also spec to have a permanent air elemental companion. Wind Shear is turned into Summon Air Elemental. The air elemental's harsh winds affect loses it's dot, instead being replaced with an interupt similar to wind shear to compensate for the loss of the interupt spell.

Lava Shield
Spec Focus: Enhancement
No mana cost, instant cast, can only be cast on self.

The shaman calls upon the element of fire to shield them, while active, any affect Lightning shield may have had from the shaman's spec (Not including Fulmination from Elemental) is applied to Lava shield. Lava shield does not have any charges, when the shaman is struck the enemy receives fire damage, and any enemy in the area of the shaman will take AoE fire damage.

Ethereal Form
Spec Focus: Restoration
20% of the shaman's maximum mana, Instant cast, Lasts 2 minutes. 10 minute cooldown.

The shaman embraces the spirits of the elements around them, entering a spirit form. While in Ethereal form, the shaman takes 30% reduced damage, can cast any healing spell while moving. Spells also have their cast times reduced by 10% (Only resto spells.) and their mana costs cut in half. While in ethereal form, the shaman can only cast Restoration spells or non-damaging spells
