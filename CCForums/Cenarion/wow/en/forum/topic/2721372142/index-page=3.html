Q: Are the Warcraft and World of Warcraft RPG books considered canon?

A: No. The RPG books were created to provide an engaging table-top role-playing experience, which sometimes required diverging from the established video game canon. Blizzard helped generate a great deal of the content within the RPG books, so there will be times when ideas from the RPG will make their way into the game and official lore, but you are much better off considering the RPG books non-canonical unless otherwise stated.


As somebody who collecys the RPG books specifically for the lore, I find this disappointing. I'm sure, however, that this question alone was the reason for the delays in getting these answers posted. I can't imagine the Creative Development team would make an announcement like this without first mulling over all the books to find what is of value and what is not.

As far as my personal philosophy goes, anything in those books is cannon unless seemingly contradicted by other sources, at which point I will come up with an elaborate theory that allows bother versions to coexist.
