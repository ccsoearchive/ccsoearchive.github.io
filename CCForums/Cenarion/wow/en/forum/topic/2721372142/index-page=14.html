Hmm... descended from Trolls?

Not hard to believe given the physical similarities between the Night elves and Trolls. Also, when environmental factors make for such distinct physical and cultural differences between individual tribes of Trolls (ex. Frostmane, Gurubashi, Amani, Witherbark, Drakkari...) it's not a stretch to theorize that exposure to the energies associated with the Well of Eternity could have mutated a race that was already prone to mutation.

Further, the mutation did not stop with Troll to Night elf. High elf, Naga, and Satyr are all derivatives of the Kaldorei, each exposed to different environments/energies. Additionally from High elf to Blood elf is yet another mutation.

In closing, the Trollish Peoples (and their derivative races) seem to be the most adaptive on Azeroth, found in all climate and terrain types.
