NEWS 12/26: Going to be tough for me to get in-game over the next week but would like to finish up recruiting! Please post in this thread or send me in-game mail if you'd like to get together with me, and I'll try to arrange a time that works for both of us.

The Stonebreaker Clan is a new orc-focused leveling/RP guild with the goal of providing IC company to those who wish to quest and run dungeons for a new Horde alt (some race restrictions, see below) during typical U.S. business hours (when it is often hard to find company and RP). We are trying to keep as much as possible to a compatible level band as we "grow up," though this is not mandatory.

*Please note in the first paragraph: "typical U.S. business hours." This is a very important fact that often gets skipped over by readers! This guild is active mostly between 9 am and 3 pm Pacific time weekdays. That's between noon and 6 p.m. Eastern time. So we're looking for people who are no longer in school and whose jobs are not during standard U.S. business hours.

We quickly filled out a core group of 5 class-balanced dungeon-buddies, but we are still looking for more people who would like to create a new alt and join the evolving story (which can be read in full on the Cenarion Circle realm forums under the "Lost Clan" heading. http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/3657282666 ). We also need people willing to step in when one of our core members can't make it to our regular dungeon runs (this happens virtually every run - juggling five schedules is hard!).

What we would love to have in the guild, in order of preference:

*An orc rogue willing to RP as another orc born to the "lost clan" (see storyline)

*A backup orc warrior/tank and backup orc shaman/healer for the core group's dungeon runs, RPed as other "Lost clan" members.

*Tauren, trolls, or goblins of the unavailable orc classes (Druid, Paladin, Priest) RPing as devoted friends of one or more members of the clan. Though not orcs, these characters will be given honorary clan status and full membership privileges due to possessing skills that are needed but not native to orcs. Those specced to heal or tank will be most likely to get in on the IC dungeon runs.

*Additional orc DPS (RPed as other orcs born to the "lost clan")

The guild "level band" is still well under 20, so there is plenty of time to catch up, and then you should have no trouble keeping up with our very leisurely leveling speed that will give you plenty of time to play your mains.

If you fall ahead or behind of the current recommended level range you will still be welcome to join our RP, you will just not be invited to our dungeon runs.

If your main is on a server other than Cenarion Circle, providing the guild leader with your RealID information will ensure that you are invited more often to dungeon runs, spontaneous guild RP, etc., though it is not 100% mandatory. Your information will never be used for any purpose other than to give you a heads-up about guild activities you're welcome to.

If you're interested, post here or contact me in-game on Egar or Edson (both on Cenarion Circle Horde side).
