So here is the approximate plan for the next couple of weeks:

I am going to continue my recruiting efforts for another week or so in the hopes of finding a compatible and available class-balanced group of 5 to do IC dungeon runs and occasional quests (the most fun-to-RP ones) from level 20-85! It would be nice to have some "spares" to keep us from having to PUG fill-in when one of the main group can't make it, and also to broaden our RP storyline base. But I do intend to keep the guild very small, casual, and laid-back.

By New Year I'd like to know who our main group of 5 is and what schedule we'd all like to use for our dungeon-running. If you can post availability info in the forum thread it will help (It's been really hard to get people online and available at the same time to have discussions).

My preferences: I would like to meet 3x a week for IC dungeon runs (one dungeon per meeting unless they are really short and we all want to do a 2nd), with unscheduled RP/questing in between as people feel like it. I would prefer to start the dungeon runs as early in the morning as possible (anytime after 9am), to free up my afternoons for RL stuff. But if need be I can schedule them starting as late as 1pm server/Pacific time.

Those who want to be part of the guild but do not want to run dungeons 3x a week, that is also fine! We are open to very casual part-time players as long as you want to RP with us and be part of the story.

If you're interested, please respond to me in this thread, via in-game mail, or in game before January 1st!
