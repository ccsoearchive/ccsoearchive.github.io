Keep in mind this is all my opinion.

I love it here. Having personally played on MG and WrA for several years I prefer CC as my go-to realm for all things. The server has been around for a long time and so have many of the people.

There are many prominent, well-respected guilds that populate the server both Alliance and Horde-side. Guilds like the AAMS, a neutral-faction translation/messenger service, Lluchduu Ocheliad, an Alliance-side guild of crazy cultis- I mean wonderful evil people. And of course there is Pia Presidium, a militaristic religious order for do-gooders.

This is a very short list of tons of wonderful guilds that make-up the CC community.

I will say that there is not as much random-RP as there is on WrA. Its everywhere on WrA... ev-ery-where. Though there are plenty of regularly hosted events dedicated to Role-Playing.

I love it here though, and there is definitely something here for everyone.

"Allow me to regale you a harrowing tale about a lost Mage. A powerfully talented Mage as matter of fact. You see, she discovered how to jump from one dimension to another."

"It was a marvelous ability!" He waves his hands wildly in the air. "Truly wondrous! And as fortune would have it she discovered this ability not a moment too soon."

"Her home realm, you see..." His expression growing more grim. "... it had become far too violent and oppressive. Why, an attractive female mage such as herself could hardly take a step outside the city walls without the constant molestation of the vile sneaky undead."

He clacks his tongue with disapproval. "And if it wasn't the undead, then it was an oafish green Orc or one of those.." He gestures horns on his forehead with his fingers. "... those silly looking moo-cow monstrosities. Taurens, I think they're called."

He pounds a balled fist into his other hand. "She lived in constant fear for her life! Now one should be so deathly afraid to venture forth from their very own city!"

The old gnome sits back, twisting his mustache in profound recollection. "Yes, those were troubling times indeed." A grin slowly forms from within his beard. "There came a day in which she decided that there had to have been a better place, another realm in which she may call home. And so she began peeking hither and thither, opening small windows through space and time until finally she decided to come here."

The old gnome pats the padded arms of his own chair. "Yes, here she found her home. Warm and welcoming. Oh sure, things are never perfect, but here she found her niche. She grew in power and stature until now she is more marvelous a Mage than she ever could have been back in that other dimension."

The old gnome takes a sip from his own mug of superbly brewed coffee. "So, what do you think? Oh! True story! It was told to me by a most reliable source, and it was told to him from another reliable source."

He holds his right hand up at the square and crosses his heart with the other. He grins. "Honest to Gnome!"

I just packed up and moved to Cenarion Circle maybe a month ago. My old guild on Area 52 had died off and I was the last one to move on. I could find silly guilds that spam invite and hardcore raiding guilds, and that's about it. The server was huge, which meant dealing with a proportionately huge population of... not so nice folks.

I had always wanted to try RP in WoW, so I meandered on over to Cenarion Circle after testing the waters in other places. And you know what? I found a completely different community. Lots of folks who were welcoming and friendly, but a lot more of a community feel. Maybe I could have found that on my other server if I had looked hard enough, but I didn't have to look at all here - it found me!

So, sample size of one, but I love it here.
