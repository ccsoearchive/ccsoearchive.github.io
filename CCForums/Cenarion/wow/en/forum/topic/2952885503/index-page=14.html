I came into WoW towards the end of BC. I never was able to witness the old Alterac Valley, so I cannot truly say whether I would prefer the old battleground to what it is today. However, I do empathize with many of your points. The OP post was extremely well written and the history lesson on WoW's trend in PvP was quite interesting.

I am happy to say that WoW is more balanced than ever. However, with what could be a golden age of PvP in WoW, it truly is just getting worse. Battles are short. Every PvP player I know abhors vehicular combat (here's looking at you WotLK). My subjective view points on Tol Barad and Wintergrasp are pretty dismal. The shift of focus from Blizzard from permanent attlegrounds to transient "epic" (in their view) world battle zones have been failures. Wintergrasp is remembered as unbalanced (how many realms are 50% horde, 50% alliance?), and a complete lag fest. Tol Barad becomes instanced during combat, which basically causes it to become another battleground. Pvpers care less about the dailies, they really just want a place to PvP for however long they please. Yes we want proper rewards to go with the amount of time spent, but the fact that nothing has been implemented to help is painful. Blues constantly post that they are listening but I just haven't seen them responsive to the WoW PvP community. We have asked for a persistant War zone in Gilneas with hundreds of members of the community posting their thoughts. We have asked for a return to Alterac Valley. Yet, the responses we are getting are not helpful. It's basically "We really don't know if that's a good idea, so we aren't going to take the risk in development time and will just develop ____ instead" (normally some transient war zone that is only relevant in the current expansion) or "We are working on a battleground builder so it is easier and less time consuming to make a battleground". Well, I finally said no, and instead of waiting and paying my 15$ a month, I am leaving. I paid my last monthly fee and on October 6th I will not be returning unless they truly show they care about the PvP community. New MMO's are coming, I only hope Blizzard realizes that they need to do something (and no not a raid, transmog, new Darkmoon faire) but something "new" and innovative. If not, then I am afraid we might see the slow, painful death to a game that gave us years of fun.
