Q: Are there any plans to redo Alterac Valley with more quests? Some of those quests were truly awesome and it feels like they became obsolete but still lurk around for years now.

A: I don't think we'll revise Alterac Valley anytime soon. This was before my time at Blizzard, but the original idea was that everyone would be questing while PvPing, but that fell short. Eventually people simplified it and found one good way to win. Now, Tol Barad is another example of questing while PvPing that has worked better. We don't have plans yet for an elaborate questing PvP hub at the start of 5.0, but we definitely want to step it up mid-expansion.

I do miss the old days. PvP/WPvP was a lot better back then. It actually was fun to go explore the world and possibly get into a huge fight as you got ganked, called in help to counter gank, only to have 50+ people duking it out in Southshore/TM where you couldn't escape without
"res sickness" Now, you just have to time it right, and you can mount up/bird form away before they can get you back in combat.

AV though, was by far some of the most fun I had. It wasn't as personal as WPvP, but you got well known in those fights and even made friends across servers. You actually got the impression that this fight was actually worth winning and you had to do whatever you could to make a difference. Now, well like everyone said. Its a Zergfest with no regard to the other fraction. WSG nets more honor then AV and is usually shorter which is pathetic. It is, in its current state, a joke for both honor and PvP combat considering you can do WSG faster, get more honor and get a ton more actual combat in it.


Personally I would love to see the old AV back, or at the least remade to some form of what it was instead of a zerg fest.
