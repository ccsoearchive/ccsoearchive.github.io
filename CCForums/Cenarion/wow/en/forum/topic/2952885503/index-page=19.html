Didnt read the entire thread, but will post what I miss most about Classic AV:

- Back in those days the BGs were server only, mainly due to there not being as many servers online, so each server was more populated (mine was atleast). Having said that, it was way easier to get in the same game with guildies or friends. I miss the team work of old AV.

- Ninja'ing flags: Nothing better than myself (resto druid) and a few rogues going and killing a tower marshal and capping the flag then holding it. That siren would go off and then EVERYONE in the game would zerg that tower and the fights were amazing.

- This was kinda cheap, but the summon-to-the-back-of-the-map was fun just because the reaction it caused to the opposing team lol.

- 4 Mages + 3 healers + Other team turtling = HAHAHAHAHA OWNED!!! That was back when AoE was actually good hehe.

- Once you capped a tower, the NPCs stopped spawning, which made capping towers A MUST back in those days. Killing Galv/Bal was a must back then as well.

- The epic games. We had a game last almost a week on Mannoroth.... well it was several days. Lets just say almost a 'work week'. Id log off that night and back on the next day and be in the same game!!!

All in all, I miss it. However I'd warn that with the cross server queues I dont think it will EVER be as epic as it was. Back then you knew everyone on both sides in the game, or most of em. I think that added a little to the magic.
