NPC's attacking at every corner... matches lasting days upon days... summoning ele-freaking-mentals... yes, yes, YES!!!! God yes!!! Original AV all the way!

Back when AV first was introduced, I remember my guild and I running to the glorious entrance of AV. There we queued and waited... and waited... and waited some more. The anticipation driving us beyond excited for what glories were instore for us. After over an hour of being in queue finally we entered. Not truly understanding what we were getting ourselves into and then, there it was... Alterac Valley.

The gates opened and all players rushed to explore this strange new land. We headed south (I was Alliance back then) passing our bunker and graveyard. Rode past our forward fortress into the Field of Strife. It was there we encountered the Horde. Without a seconds thought, both sides rushed at eachother. Emotes filled the chat window and the first drops of virtual blood met the frosty ground...

While busy killing one another a giant emerged and joined the fray, Korrak the Bloodrager. For a moment it seemed that both sides would join to kill this common enemy, but instead each side would join the creature as it ripped appart our comrades. As one side would begin to fall back, reinforcements would arrive. Our formally fallen comrades would rejoin the fray and push the opposition back.

This would go on for hours, a day... days even!
/sigh
I want this back :)
