There has been so many great suggestions on how to remake it or fix in this thread it just boggles me why Blizzard refuses to do something about it.

I guarantee if I was a Dev, I know I could fix this BG and make it the masterpiece it once was in less then a day. It's very simple.

Heroic Alterac Valley

1. Eliminate Reinforcements.

2. Add named elite Lieutenants back in strategic locations with elite npc guards tied to them. Just like it was before, if the named is killed, the normal mobs do not respawn.

3. Make all normal guards and archers elite again. Add a "net gun" ranged attack to ALL elite npc guards that roots the target in place. This stops people from kiting them away or killing them with 1-2 players.

4. Make Captain Galvangar and Captain Balinda Stonehearth much more powerful, so it actually takes an effort to kill them like it did back in the day.

5. Drek`Thar and Vanndar Stormpike are only able to be attacked once every opposing tower has been destroyed. Their power also will be boosted.

That is really all there is to it. Take it out of the random BG queue so only people that wish to play it can. Everyone is now happy. Please Blizzard....please make it happen.
