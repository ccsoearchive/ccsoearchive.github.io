Once upon a time, back when the Horde and the Alliance mounted campaigns against the forces of the Legion in Outland, a young orc hunter hit level 60 and wanted a faster mount. But this young orc was terrible at farming money, and fast mounts were horrendously expensive.

But wait, what's this? There's a mount available that costs no gold, but instead costs marks of honor, earned through blood and sweat in Alterac Valley? This shouldn't take nearly as long as it would to scrimp and save and hoard gold!

So the young orc threw himself into the meat grinder, and fought tooth and nail for those marks of honor. Skirmishes all over the place... in the Field of Strife, in the mines, at the graveyards, back at the Frostwolf base, up in the Stormpike base... so much battle. It wasn't the same as the days-long engagements his friends had spoken of in the older days, but it was challenging, and fun, and when the time came that the young orc had earned the reputation and marks of honor to buy that Frostwolf Howler, he felt a tremendous sense of satisfaction and accomplishment. And he even chose to stay just a bit longer, to earn the marks needed for a tabard, to show off his dedication to the fight in Alterac Valley.

Today, that young orc is older, more mature. Still terrible with his money, but not so much that he's not able to afford himself nice things. His collection of mounts and tabards has grown large since those days, but he still fondly recalls the Frostwolf Howler and Frostwolf Tabard that he fought and died for, and even today still uses them when he feels like it.

But he dreads returning to Alterac Valley. Not because he's burnt out... but because there's no longer any glory or fun to be had there. Just a mindless rush from one side of the map to the other, ignoring the enemy as they charge past. No PvP. No battle. No fun.

I stand with the Alliance mage who began this thread, wholeheartedly in support of his proposal: return Alterac Valley back to the way it was, in the glory days. Return Alterac Valley back to being a BATTLEground and not just a strip of land for players farming honor to Zerg through.
