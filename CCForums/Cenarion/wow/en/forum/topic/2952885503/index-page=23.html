For those that never saw it, the Goblin Shredder vehicle was actually a lot of fun in old AV and purely optional. You built it yourself with your own mined metal bars, and then picked up a blade within the zone to put the shredder together. It was pretty powerful, and wasn't used against buildings, but to fight other players. A good player controlling it with some decent heal/support helped break stalemates and could give your team a push in gaining ground.

http://www.wowwiki.com/Quest:Zinfizzlex%27s_Portable_Shredder_Unit_%28Alliance%29

But...that was part of the charm of old AV....every class had a role, and there were multiple ways to attempt to push the opposition back. Each time you played, things could unfold in a totally different way, which always made the game exciting and dynamic.
