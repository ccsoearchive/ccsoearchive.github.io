I only began playing WoW at the latter end of Wrath. I only began my nervous initiation into serious pvp and battle grounds when I hit level 75. I was instantly hooked.

I've only known the "Alterac Valley Lite" version of AV. And it is still one of my favorite BG's.
But I do remember one time while stuck in a turtle, that all the sudden the horde were coming throught the valley of strife with some gigantic thing that was killing Alliance with ease, and would not die. We sent wave after wave of Alliance heroes against this monster, all like lambs to the slaughter. After a time, it seemed to bore with merely killing us in the field of strife and moved on to slaughter our hold in the Valley. We lost. And it was AWESOME!

I had to google what the hell happened to us, because I had no clue.

Now, after that experience, combined with your great introduction to the true history of AV I really see what PVP is missing in WoW. It seems the dungeon finder, and immensely powerful NPC guards in cities have choked world pvp to a trickle. The objectives in BG's leave very little time to actually go and test your skills against others in the battleground.

This Newb Alliance is 100% for the old AV. Where do I sign?
