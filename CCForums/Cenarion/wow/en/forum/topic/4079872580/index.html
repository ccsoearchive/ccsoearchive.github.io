Circle of Chaos is a mid-size casual raiding guild. We raid two to three nights a week, currently Monday and Wednesday 6-9 pm and we always have some fun doing it.

We're looking to rebuild our raiding ranks after an epidemic of Real Life (such a terrible disease).

In particular, we could really use:
  • 1-2 healers: A paladin or a shaman would be super, but a druid or a priest would also be welcome.
  • 1 melee with a strong tank off-spec: Be warned, you may find yourself as a tank with a melee off-spec.
  • 2-3 ranged dps: Any class, just know how to kill things.


We are happy to take people who still need a little bit of work gearing as long as you are committed to staying with us for a while. Those interested in raiding with us who do not wish to leave their current guild will be considered on a case by case basis.

If you are interested in joining us, please contact myself (Anikah) or any other Circle of Chaos officer in game. (Brakk, Beholder, Gentatsu, or Himreborn)
