Overall, I like it. I admit, the first class I tried it with was my Death Knight (morbid curiosity, if you will, seeing how much the class has changed since its introduction) and I wasn't too impressed.

The next few looked better, but still had the cookie-cutter feel to it. Some branches really had the "these have the same overall effect but different means or aspects to them", which is great. Others, though, felt like, "why would I ever choose that ability?" or, "I would choose that for my PvP spec/alts, and that for my PvE"--ie, cookie-cutter.

Then I looked at the Warlock tree. My hat's off, Blizz.

However, I suspect it's going to fall into the Death Knight revamp syndrome. Just like in LK with the Death Knights, with no clear spec being either Tank or DPS, I suspect this new version of the talent trees are going to face that. "Which abilities do I take for tanking?" Blizzard, to help make these decisions less confusing, with revert to what we have now (more or less): Tanks pick this, DPS pick that, Healers pick that other thing.

But, still, I'm pleased and excited to see this in-game.
