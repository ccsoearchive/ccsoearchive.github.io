I'm not sure I can express how flustered I am with these trees. I understand that this is preAlpha, but what on earth is this?

Conceptually, I hate this change. The 31 point talent trees are something I identify with WoW and have been in game since at least launch. I feel the talent trees are one of the defining features of WoW, and they're just gone. I don't understand what the devs are trying to accomplish on any level here.

In terms of evaluating the trees, I also hate them. Looking over most of the tanks it is clear that there are so many numbers that have to change. Also, I don't like the proposed changes to skills I've had since BC in some cases. The rotation looks clunky and not fun. I don't want to be a death knight. And why do I lose shockwave for 5 levels? I've had this ability for 2 expansions now and I'm going to lose it for 5.0->leveling? That's garbage Blizzard.

Yeah, some of the new abilities look interesting. The holy star thing for priests really stands out. The mage tree's are just nerfs. Grats, you can't have access to abilities you have currently. The druid trees (the level 90 'talents') and new ability read like something out of a druid's dream they are that over powered.

Overall, I'm really glad I wasn't planning on going forward with MoP. This is just not what I expect from Blizzard. I expect better. And for the record, I have more meaningful choices on both my priest and warrior currently than I will post 5.0. I have ONE decision with these new trees and it is only if encounters favor 'charge more often' or 'charge twice'

Disbelief is the term that best describes how I feel about these trees.

Less options = less interest from the players, imho. I did NOT like the talent changes in cataclysm (ic failure) either.
