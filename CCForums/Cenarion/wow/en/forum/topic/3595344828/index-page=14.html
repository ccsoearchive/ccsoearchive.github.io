I don't like it at all. It seems like every time a new patch or expansion comes out the game is made just a little bit more simple. I liked the complexity of the game before, it's what drew me into the game and made it a world that had a whole bunch of content to explore. I especially like the original talent trees where you could spec into all 3 if you wanted, it gave us more to work and play with. There was also a certain pleasure when i finally figured it all out when i knew it wasn't too easy. But now, it seems like anyone who plays is going to have an easy time figuring it out, which bothers me. One of the biggest elements of the game that kept me playing was the pride in my character. i know that sounds corny, but we've all had those moments where we just stand in stormwind trade district knowing people are inspecting us and envying the gear and mounts we have. There's not going to be much to envy anymore....:(

I like the new tree design but I do have a couple comments for the Hunter one in its current state:

1) Hunter tree has too many obvious choices because some talents are total crap and are placed on the same tier as other awesome ones.

Level 15: Frost Arrow vs Arcane Arrow.
30% chance to reduce speed by 30% on autoattacks, vs 2.5focus per autoattack. Why would anyone use Frost Arrow when Conc Shot can be used on cooldown for a -50% speed reduction?

Level 60: You cannot possibly compare CT/HC with a -15% dmg reduction and a 600hps HoT, especially not when your disengage can heal you for 15% of your HP or provide a short Deterrence.

Level 75: Fervor and TotH are quite underpar to Readiness. I'd be a tough choice if Fervor had a lower CD and a TotH had a much higher proc rate.

2) Not a single pet related talent? I'm sorry but Intimidation and Spirit Bond don't count.
What I'd do is put Beast Mastery as a Talent. Put it on the level 75 tier (replace either TotH or Fervor) and give something else to the BM spec. Fervor would be a pretty good candidate. For instance this would allow BM hunters to choose between a superior pet and godly burst in the form of double BW+Fervor.


Beast Mastery as a talent for all specs? Oh, ok, that makes perfect sense...
Just no. I'm tired of hearing that, just sacrifice a little dps and play Beast Mastery if you want the pets!
