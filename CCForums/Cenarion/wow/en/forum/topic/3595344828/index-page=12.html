Bashiok,

I have a quesiton for you regarding Paladins in MoP. From the compilation (arguably still a work in progress) done by MMO-Champion, it appears that Holy is losing exorcism and just about any other form of damage that has any meaningful impact (Holy Wrath etc). Their judgements do the least of any judgement both in terms of proc and likely in terms of damage as well.

Then we have the Speed of Light ability. It seems oddly backwards in what is done with that ability. Holy gets damage reduction (something you'd probably think Prot would get for survivability's sake), Prot gets a damage increase (something Ret would normally be thought to get), and Ret gets the oddest of them all: a version of Holy Radiance. Considering the above regarding Holy Paladins losing both Exorcism and a meaningful Holy Wrath (unless you make it instant cast minus the anti-crit feature for Holy) and Ret paladins getting what appears to be gobs of damage tweaks, but both it and Prot losing ALL healing spells minus Flash of Light (which Supplication is now tied to), the state of the Paladin seems very much like you have absolutely zero idea what you really want to do with the class other than water its core utility down.

I have to say, the Paladin changes (which I know aren't necessarily final) really have the feel of "to the ground", and not in a good way. Please at least tell me Holy is getting some serious damage output boost to Holy Shock, otherwise leveling as Holy is going to suck supremely badly (as in "Holy Priest having to wand during vanilla WoW" bad). And assuming you stay on the current healing model, Flash of Light as the ONLY heal spell available to Prot/Ret outside of WoG = OOM. Fast. There's not even any middle ground. Any possible utility to save the group or an individual member goes down the tubes with this change.

Priests are in a similar boat with regard to spec specific heals. Holy has the look of feeling similar to its current form, but Disc is losing half its toolbox. No binding heal and you're low and so is the tank? Have fun with that. That cheap heal (yes, Heal) feels worse than before but you have to use it in order to maintain mana for when you need it? Forget renew helping to leverage it. It's Holy only now. Holy and Disc already have decidedly different strengths and weaknesses as it is - this only compounds things, especially on the Disc side. Hymn of Hope is Disc only? Um...Disc wasn't the spec having mana issues. And shadow priest losing all but Flash Heal. So much for being a good player and knowing when to offheal during a critical turn of bad luck - with only FH at our disposal we'll go OOM just as fast as a Ret paladin attempting to use their FoL.

These changes have me very concerned that you have absolutely no clue what makes healing even remotely fun for us (hint: it's having the skill to know which tool to use from your set to get the job done). Dumbing down our sets really feels very, very....well, it doesn't encourage me, somebody that has loved to heal up until now, to bother much with it in the future.

You say you want our talents to be utility choices, and then for what appears to be the core specs that keep everybody else alive, you water down our utility. I'm honestly baffled by the changes. This isn't making the specs "flavorful", it's literally pigeonholing them. And I gotta say, if this is the state of things to come, I'm not really looking forward to the next expansion, new content or not.

I think we could realy use some insight from the devs as to why they're screwing up healers like this even more than they were planning to previously (the INT change posted a while back). The more I see of MoP, the more I feel that the annual pass I bought into is going to be basically the last stand for my WoW days. I'll be playing WoW until I can get ahold of D3, and since I get D3 essentially for free with my locked WoW subscription, if I don't like D3 I can drop it no loss.

I'm really, really saddened and angered by these changes to the healers. If this is your "balance" for eliminating the hybrid tax, then for the love of all that's good, leave the tax in and don't neuter our utility like this. Please.

/DiedALittleInsideJustNow
