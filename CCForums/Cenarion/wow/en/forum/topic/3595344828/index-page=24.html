Sure you have 6 tiers of 3 unique talents that seemingly give you a choice in what to spec into. But this is an MMO and will always be an MMO.

Pretty much how I feel about it. Regardless of how hard Blizzard tries, I don't think we will ever get much choice outside of our spec, and 2 or 3 cookie-cutters within the spec. Due to the competitiveness of WoW, anyone in a serious raiding guild, or high end pvp will be expected to get the most out of their talents.
