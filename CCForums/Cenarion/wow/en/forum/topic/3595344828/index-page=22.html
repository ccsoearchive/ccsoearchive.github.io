For a PVE resto druid it looks like half the talent tiers have nothing, tier 30 seems to have two highly useful items, and the other tiers only have one. Something I am not seeing or are PVE resto druids hosed here?


I was able to find something useful for resto in every talent level...

Level 15 talents: Feline Swiftness would benefit resto druids every time they have to "run out" of something, such as when Sindy would suck in players in ICC, or the second boss in ZA/ZG.

Level 30 talents: All 3 talents are decent for resto. I use Nature's Swiftness with Healing Touch when tanks take a huge hp hit. Disc and Holy priests can pick up a talent like Renewal currently (I've been raiding w/my disc priest this xpac, and the priest version of the talent is really handy to have in a variety of pvp/pve scenarios). Cenarion Ward will be beneficial for tank healing.

Level 45 talents: I'd pick up typhoon for the knockback for fights like the add phase on Ragnaros.

Level 60 talents: I'd pick up Incarnation for Tree of Life (pretty much a no-brainer for pve)

Level 75 talents: Ursol's Vortex = an aoe death grip! I'd probably just use this to help out the tank when adds need to be picked up quickly (again, this would be a big help during encounters like the add phase during Rag). Bear Hug and Demoralizing Roar are decent add control options.

Level 90 talents: this one is a bit tricky. I'd probably pick up Disentanglement for pve for the 20% self heal, or pick up Master Shapeshifter for the increased magic damage as a raid buff (standing in with melee healing, and shapeshifting and autoattacking the boss for the buff).

Anyway, looks like a lot of stuff in the tree for pve resto druids...just my two cents.
