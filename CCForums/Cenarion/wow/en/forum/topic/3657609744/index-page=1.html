PUBLIC NOTICE

The Anytime & Anywhere Messenger Service is happy to announce the introduction of its newest project, the All Anonymous Merchandise Swap, in tandem with this year's Winter Veil celebrations. In its continuing mission to promote better communication between all peoples, the AAMS introduces this program as a means to potentially connect people who would otherwise never have reason to meet.

The concept of the All Anonymous Merchandise Swap is simple, yet elegant. Participants will submit their name to the AAMS for inclusion in the merchandise swap. Once the list is filled, or the sign up deadline is reached (whichever comes first), the AAMS will then randomly assign participants as merchandise providers for other participants. All participants will then be notified of their randomly selected "partner" . The participants will then have a predeterming amount of time to select merchandise they think appropriate for their randomly selected "partner". All merchandise will then be turned in to the AAMS, who will deliver the anonymously provided merchandise to its assigned participant during the final days of the program.

Sign-ups for the All Anonymous Merchandise swap will begin immediately following the distribution of this notice and end on the first day of the official Winter Veil celebrations, December the 15th. The AAMS will then distribute the randomly determined participant assignments on or before December the 18th. Merchandise will then be collected and stored until December the 26th, when delivery of the merchandise will begin. All merchandise is scheduled to be delivered before the new year begins in earnest, on January the 1st.

Included with this public notice is a sign-up sheet. Please feel free to sign up directly here, as others will certainly be signing up at the other public notice distribution locations.

The AAMS is proud to serve you, our community, and hope that you will find the time to participate in the spirit of giving this Winter Veil season.

Safe Journeys!
