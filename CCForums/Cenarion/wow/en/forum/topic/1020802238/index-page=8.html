It can now be noted that we have an increased ability to sort of "base jump" off of cliffs.

With us being able to move faster while stealthed, you could potentially get a nice running head start and jump off a cliff or something (usually in pursuit of a Mage or some such who is trying to get away), and make a semi safe or low damage landing if the drop isn't life threatening.

Tie in a parachute if you're an Engineer or some other slowfall mechanic and you can make it pretty far. Fun thing to do is having Vanish+Sprint on a one click macro then activate that as you jump/run off a cliff. You'll go flying off the cliff and no one will be able to see you land and contiue your nearly dead quarry.

Edit: as to the above post on Jumper cables - they fail more than they work.

Edit to the edit after the edit:

As many guilds are now level 25, or close to it, it's possible to mass rez people. This gives everyone (in a lv. 25 guild) a rez ability.
