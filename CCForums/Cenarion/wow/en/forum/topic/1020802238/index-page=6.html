Back in mid Wrath I was levelling engineering and had plenty of leftover Boxes of Bombs and Global Thermal Sapper Charges.

I never did test using Cloak right before the sapper to absorb the damage or using Cold Blood to see if it would cause more damage (or using both *Evil Grin*), but the GTSC is perfect for taking out unsuspecting low levels...completely by surprise too.


If you're and Engineer, just find a group of flagged lowbies hanging out, stay stealthed and waltz right up to them and clicp the sapper for an instant mushroom cloud of death. You might even macro FoK with the Sapper for a nice shrapnel effect.

I did exactly that to four level 30 something Alliance at Nessingwary's Camp in STV one night, they were standing there and I snuck up on them stealthed. I then one shotted all of them with one Sapper. I bet the look on their faces was a kodak moment.

The current CATA bombs are fun too, Big Daddy it's called. If your target hasn't taken any sort of damage prior to your attack with them (hence being unaware as the tooltip says) they take even more damage.

For Rogue miners and dealing with those pesky ore thieves that try to steal your node if/when you aggro a nearby mob, just vanish ( I use a Cloak+evade+ vanish macro) you will drop all aggro onto your wouldbe thief and then you can safely mine the node while the other guy has to deal with the mobs.

If it's opposing faction, you could stealth and wait by a node and then jump your target.
