Rogues may be the most feared and / or hated class out in the world. They are built for ambushing the unsuspecting, and can easily punish a target relentlessly. Here are a few utilities you can...utilize...to ensure your quarry doesn't flee prior to satisfying your sadistic tendencies. Don't get caught unprepared!*

*Originally written by Io (http://us.battle.net/wow/en/character/maelstrom/io/), but I was unable to locate the thread so this is reconstructed from Google, memory and suggestions. I'll do my best to keep it updated.

An unprepared Rogue is the most vulnerable player in the game.
...
A Rogue's Perspective on Ganking
For 85 levels of the game, a Rogue learns to strike at the most opportune time. This often entails waiting for enemies to path away from a desired mob, or tediously tiptoeing through a cave of elites to assassinate a heavily guarded target and sap their bodyguards. Against players, the same mentality will come into play. Be wary of performing an excessive act in PvP - repeated emoting, corpse-camping or reinforcement overkill will only serve to help the Rogue remember who you are and what you did to them. -WoWwiki.com/Rogue_ganking

Blackened Worg Steak
Track Humanoids, self explanatory.

Elixir of Camouflage
Prevents anyone from tracking you on their mini-map, including Druids, Hunters and Worg Steak users.

Tracker Snacks
For Druids in Cat form, etc.

Rogue's Draught (Pickpocketing)
Healing potion exclusive to Rogues.

+Stealth Effectiveness
Sneaking Potion
or
Stealth of the Stalker (Quest)
and
Various items with +Stealth attributes:
http://shadowpanther.net/stealth.htm

+Stealth Detection
Catseye Elixir (+1)
or
Elixir of the Searching Eye (+2)
or
Distilled Stalker Sight (+3, Quest)
Use to find another Rogue or Druid.

+Invisibility / Detection
Elixir of Detect Lesser Invisibility
and / or
Invisibility Potion
or
Invisibility Field (http://www.wowhead.com/spell=84424)
Use to see invisible Mages; the latter two actually bump you into the same realm.

+Anti-CC
Living Action Potion
3 sec. immunity to slows, snares & stuns; usable while stunned. Extra trinket.
or
Free Action Potion
10 sec. immunity to slows, snares & stuns; not usable while stunned.

+Ranged CC / Interrupt
Embersilk Net (Tailoring)
or
Big Daddy (Engineering)
or
High-Powered Bolt Gun (Engineering)
or
Gnomish Poultryizer (Engineering)
or
Discombobulator Ray (Engineering)
or
Arcane Bomb (Engineering)
Frost Grenade (Engineering)
Gnomish Net-o-Matic Projector (Engineering)
The final three reportedly do not share a cooldown with the others. There are also many options for pure ranged damage, such as Hand-Mounted Pyro Rocket (Cataclysm version is "Tazik Shocker" http://www.wowhead.com/spell=82180).

+Decoys
Cardboard Assassin (http://www.wowhead.com/spell=84425)
Can take a pet off of you; especially useful with Vanish.
or
Explosive Decoy (Engineering)
Explosive Sheep (Engineering)
Self explanatory. Great fun in lowbie towns.

+Diversions
Fetch Ball
Send your favorite pet running to possibly divert attention from your location. Breaks stealth.
or
Blue Smoke Flare
Green Smoke Flare
Purple Smoke Flare
Red Smoke Flare
White Smoke Flare
Can be thrown from stealth!
or
Rogues Deck (Quest)
Archmage Vargoth's Staff (Quest)
Highborne Soul Mirror (Archaeology)
These will summon a semi-transparent stationary unit that may distract your opponent. Useful with a Vanish or to divert attention while already stealthed. The mirror creates a replica of your character, but it will face your direction, possibly giving away your location.

Critter Bites
Lure your opponents into a false sense of safety. Amass a critter army, stealth, profit.

Blue Crashin' Thrashin' Racer Controller
Crashin' Thrashin' Racer Controller
or
MiniZep Controller
Usable while stealthed! Allows you to explore the unknown from a safe spot. The toys themselves do not stealth, but can serve as a sort of Eye of Kilrogg. New in 2010: The MiniZep allows for air to ground reconnaissance!
As an added bonus with the Racers, enemy players who attack a Racer will become flagged for PvP (apparently the Zep can not be targeted). Also, the Racers can aggro NPCs, but the NPCs will not transfer aggro to you after killing the toy if you are in Stealth.

Ornate Spyglass
Because it has the word spy in it. Also, it allows you to see players beyond your normal max rendering distance. Some Engineering goggles have the same effect, but this item is usable by everyone.

+Slow Fall
Flexweave Underlay (Engineering)
or
Noggenfogger Elixir (Quest)
or
Evonice's Landin' Pilla (Quest)
or
Skyguard's D@%%! (Reputation)
Follow Mages and Priests off cliffs.

+Speed
Glyph of Sprint
Increases your sprint speed to that of a ground mount.
or
Nitro Boosts (Engineering)
Faster than a ground mount!
or
Swiftness Potion

+Water Walking
Glyph of Blurred Speed
and / or
Elixir of Water Walking
Use for chasing Death Knights and Shamans over water.

+Water Breathing
Elixir of Greater Water Breathing
or
Nagrand Cherry (Quest)
or
Deepdive Helmet (Engineering)
or
Hook of the Master Angler (Fishing)
Use these with Swim Speed Potion.

Note - Water: Water makes an excellent stealth spot (suiton-no-jutsu). Rarely will people search here. When they do look, they won't find you.

Note 2 - Bushes: Bushes that fully conceal your character model are a great place to stealth. Even if someone gets within stealth detection range they probably won't see you, though they may hear you. Nameplates do not show up over stealthed characters.

Note 3 - Flying: Flying very high can also remove the threat of your prey getting away. Most players flee to the nearest perch to drink while frantically surveying the ground, but rarely will they look up.

Note 4 - Shields: Damage-absorbing trinkets like Grounded Plasma Shield (http://www.wowhead.com/spell=84427) can allow you to re-stealth. If the bubble absorbs damage for 5 seconds, you will exit combat.

+Debuffs
Bag of Marbles (Quest)
or
Glowing Wax Stick (Pre-Cata Quest)
or
Discombobulator Ray (Engineering)

Mirror of Broken Images
Spell resist trinkets will give you an edge against casters.

Bouquet of Red Roses
Bouquet of Ebon Roses
Use one of these on your enemy and they'll leave a trail to follow (assuming they don't remove it). Your target may confuse the red and pink petals for an act of diplomacy or kindness, but black is obviously the mark of death.

Dark Jade Focusing Lens
Shadow Jade Focusing Lens
Shadow Crystal Focusing Lens
These shoot directly at your target and face you to them, so you'll know exactly where they are. The player being focused will be able to see this, but if timed correctly you can do it fast enough that they may not notice it. The macro for this is:
/target <player name>
/use <item name>
