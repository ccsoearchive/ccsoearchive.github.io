The front forum page sickeningly overflows with QQ. We are Rogues, not Rouge wearing pansies. Any new recruit to the class who comes here has my sympathy. I love my Rogue, and if I can spread that feeling to others, or rekindle a lost love, all the better.

Vanish is not broken, it's different. Kittens don't have Vanish. Nor do they have Cloak. Nor do they have the coolness factor. Cuddly, maybe. Stunlock is not dead, at least not with the Garrote glyph against casters.

Rogues are not useless.

Above all else, Wold PvP is back! Zones new and old alike are filled with people of all levels questing and farming. However, adventurers have forgotten the open world belongs to us. It is our domain. The adage of safety in numbers has gone by the wayside in our perceived absence. Lone quest seekers no longer keep an eye over their shoulder. I provide this thread in the hopes of offering a remedy. Go forth and awaken dormant fears in those now accustomed to ignoring their surrounding shadows.

The day - strike that - the night shall come again when the Rogue forums' front page is filled not with the QQ of our class comrades, but of our felled enemies.
