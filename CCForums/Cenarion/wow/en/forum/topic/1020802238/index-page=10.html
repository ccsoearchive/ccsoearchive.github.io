RE: Org kills -- those are fun! Nothing like one shotting an 85 just as he is about to lose a duel, or has just lost one -- lol.

Technically, you could log out in an enemy city, like Org, but you have to make sure you are out of sight and aggro range of guards - and of course players. I sometimes log in an empty hut.

In the villages it's easier, and you probably could just walk away from the guards while killing them -- once you get out of town they stop re-popping so if you kill the one on you and are out you won't need vanish or sprint. the problem is when you are in town, you get multiple guards on you and they can take your health down -- and don't aggro the flight masters...

One fun thing in Org is in the Cleft of Shadows, people port in flagged and you can kill them and then run into Ragefire Chasm to get away from the guards. Only problem is if you walk out the guards will aggro and attack while you are loading in. We use that for our last kills of the night and just Hearth home after.
