I used to be Captcrunch (in earlier posts) so check out our videos in the previous posts for some funny stuff. Some scenarios we enjoy employing against lowbies below -- some are in the videos; some were not captured yet:

-Garrote...watch them run away slowly bleeding to death.

-Snare a lowbie with Gnomish net; launch Cannonball Runner to shoot them in the face until they die. Best with a fourway gang each with a Cannon.

-The above but with the Gnomish Flame Turret – or both!

-Find Lowbie fighting mobs. Hide behind a tree or other world object. Use lowest level Thrown weapon to slowly tick down their health. Watch them die by the mob and think they were killed by it. Repeat ad inifitum.

-The above but with the Gnomish Lightning Generator.

-Find Lowbie. Equip largest gun you have (recommend: Fury of the Raging Dragon); go to minimum distance to fire; shoot them in the face for one shot kill.

-Find Lowbie fighting mobs. Launch one of many combat pets (best when they don’t see you and think it’s a world mob). These include Barov’s, Tiny Voodoo Mask, Battle Chicken, Explosive sheep (for really low lev), etc. Watch them panic as they try to run away and agro more mobs.

-Go outside capital city; find duelers flagged for PVP; wait until end of duel; one-shot loser of duel (usually they are down to minimal health).

-Find Lowbie; use Gnomish Poultryizer to turn them into a chicken; launch Gnomish Battle Chicken to kill them.

Numerous MC scenarios (using belt or Gnomish helm):
-Find a high level player helping a low level player: MC the higher level player to kill his low level friend.

-MC a low level character in town and watch the guards kill him.

-MC a Hunter to kill his own pet.

-MC an escort in an escort mission to attack the players escorting him
-Variant: MC a player in an escort mission to kill the escortee

-Of course, MC a low level player off a cliff

-MC a low level character to agro numerous mobs.

-Find an AFK lowbie and MC them into water so they drown.

More to come…

After 6 years of ganking in this game I thought I knew, had done and had discovered it all but came across two awesome scenarios this last weekend.

Here is how they work:

-Find a lowbie questing in the wild. Doesn't matter where, really, as long as there are mobs alive around them -- and a Lowbie helps so that you can beat them without question in a duel -- so could even be a high level player (though I haven't tested that). They need to be in the wild though with mobs around. Challenge them to a duel (many lowbies accept as they know you cannot kill them in a duel) and as you duel, cleverly move them toward a mob so that they get aggro (this is where it helps that they are low level as the mob won't aggro you, but you have to be careful not to end the duel too soon by hitting them), then, once they aggro something defeat them -- when the duel ends, they have 1 hit point and ONE hit by the aggroed mob will kill them -- HILARIOUS stuff! It works in either contested or enemy zones. Lowbies work best as they can hit you numerous times (which makes it fun for them) as you lure them into a mob and you won't lose the duel -- as long as the mob can hit them without missing it will work.

-The second scenario is similar, and is also easier with a lowbie, but possible with higher levs too: A buddy and I pulled this one off outside Gadgetstan (so can be done just outside a neutral city with guards (Gadgetstan, Booty Bay, Winterspring will be best I suppose, maybe Shatt too)). We found a lowbie quite far out of town and ganked him in various ways several times. He started getting mad and attacking us by stunning us so he could get away (he was a hunter) and get back to the "protection" of town. So we had him backing up moving to town attacking us, though he could not kill us -- we were not attacking at this point. We backed him up to the outside of the city and -- LOL -- he aggroed the guards without us pulling aggro. The guards killed him almost instantly!!

Both of these are great because they are extremely humiliating/embarassing and the player gets gear decay from dying from AI.
