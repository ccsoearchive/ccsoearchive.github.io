I would like to make an addition to the ganking list. I discovered this somewhat by accident while messing around the other day with engineering toys

The Gnomish Gravity Well. While it cannot be used in combat, it does something very interesting. The well eliminates all of your forward velocity upon use. How is this relevant? Wait and see.

Aviana's feather, and the flying toys acquired from the Goblin workshop/gearworks, function by launching you into the air and giving you a super slow fall speed plus tremendous forward velocity. If you use the gravity well, while using Aviana's feather or the Skyterror, it eliminates that forward velocity. You will fall very slowly.

Now presume you have a N.U.K.U.L.A.R. device handy. The device has tremendous range and damage. You can in fact, fire it while falling. It is therefore possible to overfly a location, kill your forward velocity, and then hit the ground with a tremendous blast of damage before you land. Since you presumably kept 2 uses of the Skyterror or an unused feather handy, you can now vault off into the stratosphere again.

Goblin Air Strike
  • Fly over target location
  • Use gravity well
  • Descend slowly
  • Fire Nuke.
  • Use flight-toy again. Escape.
  • Bonus points if you have multiple people in a raid group do this.
  • Additional bonus points if you're a mage and deployed arcane crystal prior to firing the nuke.
  • Even more additional bonus points if you use a coordinates add-on and some macros to have everyone hit the same location in succession
