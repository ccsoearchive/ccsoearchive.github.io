I was always under the general rule of healing, don't shield the priest unless you HAVE to, because then they don't get hit and don't get enough rage. So, as the game has progressed and things have changed, does shielding prohibit a tank from gathering rage, has that changed, or is it less relevant now?


I'm going to have to write one myself eventually! Anyway, rage generation was fixed during Wrath so that absorption has no effect on it. You can shield as much as you want. Avoid reforging per Mrrobot unless you're going there to find the most efficient way of meeting an exact stat plateau/threshhold which you have already determined is what you need. Mrrobot's default settings will have you do strange things, such as equip trinkets which are not particularly good for Disc, or reforge into Spirit when you already have enough, or... whatever.

If you use PW:S heavily (and I'm talking like >20% of your total healing done on most fights), reforge Mastery at the expense of Haste (in reality, most of your cast-time spells will have Borrowed Time buff if you use that much PW:S)... otherwise, you're probably pretty well served by reforging for Haste, especially as AA/A spec.
