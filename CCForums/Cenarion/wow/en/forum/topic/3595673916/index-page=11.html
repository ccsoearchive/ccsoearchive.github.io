There isn't really a difference on 10 v 25 man raid size. Its more of "what spells do you cast."

In 10 man you may be more likely to PW: Shield your entire raid to mitigate damage, thus you stack Mastery and high Spirit to reduce the penalty of casting so many shields.

In 25 man and 10 man you may be more likely to use PW: Shield only for Rapture while focusing on Prayer of Healing (and thus Divine Aegis as well) as your main spell, which benefits greatly from a Haste/Crit set up.

Or you can take the middle route and use Haste/Mastery with equal usage of PW:S/PoH.
