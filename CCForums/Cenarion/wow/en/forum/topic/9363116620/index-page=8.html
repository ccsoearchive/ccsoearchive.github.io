Well, he thought of it. So it was up to him to handle the preparations this time.

The auction had brought in a lot of traffic, and word was starting to spread about the library. So, he decided to go all out, and in a big way.

Having retrieved the list of frequent guests from Archivist Dawnsong, Fastice Blackblade set to work with a quill and ink, which would happen to be more difficult than he initially anticipated. But once he finally had a final draft, he gave it to a scribe, to be copied dozens of times and sent off among the usual mailing list, a few new names he had added himself, and to be pinned up around Silvermoon and The Undercity.

The Royal Library Presents,

A night of fun, companionship, and friendly competition in the form of a scavenger hunt throughout Eversong Woods and The Ghostlands. Festivities will begin at 5 P.M. Directly underneath Thallasian Pass, the tunnel which leads from The Ghostlands to The Eastern Plaguelands.

Participants will receive a clue from Guardian Fastice Blackblade, which they must decipher, and follow to the next location, and so on and so forth, until all have the chance to solve the mystery, and unearth the final clue.

The first lucky soul to decipher the mystery will receive a grand prize of precisely One Thousand Gold pieces, in one lump sum, at the end of their journey. Other prizes may be awarded for runners-up as well, so give it your all regardless of if someone has already completed the task!

When: Sunday December 1st, 5 P.M.
Where: Thallasian Pass in The Southern Ghostlands
Dress Code: Casual, though an emphasis on Comfort is recommended!
Refreshments will be provided at the gate until it seems we have plenty of participants, at which point the hunt will commence.

We look forward to seeing you all there!

A tentative knock came at the basement door, breaking Poesy's intense concentration. With a snarl, she threw up her hands which were drenched in paste. The sudden motion stirred a flurry of shredded paper around her.

"Much open!" Poesy shouted.

As an intern tip-toed down the stairs to the archivist's office, Poesy turned back to her paper mache ... snowman? Goat? Poesy had taken up the delicate art of paper mache after a visit from Archivist Dawnsong, who gently suggested that Poesy ought to pay more attention to the collection of goblin business ledgers in her charge.

Poesy was sure that Archivist Dawnsong would be much impressed by her series of handmade pinatas.

"Um... That's a nice... Uh... Dolphin?" The intern canted his head at Poesy's latest creation. Poesy scowled. The intern gulped and said, "Sorry! Sorry. Um. Uh. I'm just here to deliver an invitation to this week's salon, archivist! Pleasedon'teatme!"

Poesy gasped, spotting the scroll between the intern's hands. "Ah! Ahhh! Very thanks!" Nodding rapidly, she flashed her razor-bladed teeth at the intern with her most winsome smile. The intern dropped the invitation on Poesy's desk and ran off, overcome by joy, perhaps?
