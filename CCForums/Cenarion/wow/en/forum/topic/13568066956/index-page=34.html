I also had this reply.
Howdy! This is Game Master Strybur from customer support. WoW Armory was never designed to be a real-time update as the expected update part is usually within 24 hours. Most of the time it's significantly faster than that, but there are no guarantees. With that being said, I can go over some possible tips to consider to see if it helps to force the refresh a bit faster.

1a) Make sure that you're exiting completely out of all Blizzard games, applications, and websites, so that the battle.net account has a reason to attempt to resync with the website.

1b) Log into the character using the battle.net desktop application and move around for 10s or so.

1c) Repeat step 1a and exit completely out of all Blizzard games, applications, and websites again.

1d) Repeat step 1b to log back in.

1e) Check the armory to see if it updated.
