Don't mind Sophie (Flambe,) he's just a very opinionated troll that likes attention :)

1. We have a healthy enough mix of races. Belfs are the most populous, but we have a decent amount of others too, and most are entirely fine with rping with those of other races.

2. Melting pots. We don't have any race specific guilds, really.

3. Nope! Alliance side is actually more RP-PvP driven. While most of the Horde players do actually enjoy PvP, a lot of our mains aren't actually interested in the war. As such, you're more likely to see the Horde doing TB/BGs OOCly while ICly defending and drinking tea rather than burning and pillaging a town. (Or ooc attacks of boredom from alts.)

4. I'd say you can find most of your RP from that, actually! We're far more interested in chatting about things and drinking tea over here than slaying our enemies and blood and honor :) (We're terrible at being savages, aren't we?)

5. A lot of our RP happens at events. We're more likely to be randomly doing a BG or something than idling in a tavern, so a lot of the day to day RP happens over the Hearthstone channel or other channels unless someone actually opts to meet up with someone else.

As for that thing about new ideas, we're entirely open to new ideas - if they're good ones. We just won't hesitate to point out when something is a /bad/ idea. Honesty and bluntness =/= being doodoo heads. We're /more/ than happy to help try and improve said bad idea to be a good one instead, but if someone makes it clear they don't actually care and are throwing lore/ect, then there's no point in helping.

Feel free to hit me up in game sometime if you do make an alt over here.
