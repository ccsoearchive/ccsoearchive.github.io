Finnaeus

Name: Finnaeus Peverley
Race: Worgen
Gender: Male
Age: ~45
Guild: None (Yet)

Anything you can add:

Finnaeus Peverley was a popular Gilnean soldier turned farmer when the Greymane Wall closed. A staunch supporter of the wall and Greymane's attitude of isolationism, Finnaeus nurtured a budding druidic talent while tending his crops, living on his farmland with his wife Claire, his dear daughter Lydia, and his elderly father Frederick. Once good-natured and a doting husband and wife, everything changed when his father was bitten by a worgen while hunting. At the behest of his wife Claire, and his own sentimentality, he refused to hunt his father, even though Finnaeus suspected it was his father biting the horses and stalking his farmland. That changed when, one night, his father attacked the home, biting both Claire and Lydia before Finnaeus finally put him down. Before his family could turn, sick from the bite and curse, Finnaeus was forced to make the terrible choice. Following the duty of Gilneas, and sworn as a budding druid to protect the balance of life, Finnaeus murdered his family before they come become cruel, vicious worgen.

Having suffered the bite himself years later when the worgen invaded, suffering from the burden of his deeds when a cure finally came to those with the bite, Finnaeus is now coldly analytical when it comes to life. Though he fought alongside the Night Elves in Kalimdor to repay his debt for them saving the nation that he loves, he stands on the brink of an unknown future. With no ties to his former life, and a seething hatred for all things corrupting, Finnaeus seeks to forge a new path for himself, all the while fighting those things that threaten the balance of existence.
