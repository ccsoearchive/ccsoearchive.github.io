No. No to basically everything you said.

You do not always 5CP Rupture, that's going to either leave gaps in Rupture uptime or you're holding energy and CP and sacrificing other things to do it. Any way you cut it you're losing DPS.

Energy capping is a massive loss of DPS every second you're capped. Can be something like 5-6K DPS. It all depends on your gear and procs and a number of other factors, but the point is it's a very big loss.

If you have 4+CP, SnD is up and Rupture is up, you Envenom. No question. Clipping the Envenom buff is a DPS loss, but nothing compared to capping out. Anything else you could do is equally detrimental to your performance. One Mutilate has nowhere near the damage of a full 5CP Envenom, nor the beneficial buff, nor the energy/CP return from talents.

Lastly, it shouldn't even matter that you can't overwrite Rupture. You don't want to, it's only ever even permissable to do so if the current Rupture has less than 2 seconds left which means the last tick will be rolled into the next Rupture. Anything else it's a DPS loss, again.

so when if ever is it beneficial for a 5pt rupture ?


If you happen to have 5CP as Rupture drops, do it. If you can within your rotation, without sacrificing Envenoms, or anything else, get to 4+CP in time for Rupture to drop, that's great. But it's not something you can do reliably, there's too much RNG in the way to predict how many CP you're going to have and what the state of combat and where your Buffs/DeBuffs are going to be at with any accuracy 10-20s in the future.

Higher CP Rupture better than lower CP Rupture. But gaps in uptime, any gaps, worse than all else. It's ice cream night and you're all out of mint chip kind of bad. And I don't have to tell you, that's bad.

Edit: For your second question. Don't ever let Rupture not be up. You want as high an uptime on Envenom as possible, but it's not going to be 100% so between Rupture or Envenom you restart Rupture first then Enevom the next time you get 4+CP, your goal for Rupture is 100%. Only exception is SnD if you get in a position where it's going to fall off and your choices are SnD or Rupture, Envenom immediately regardless of CP, hope for a Ruthlessness proc and get Rupture back up, or Mut then get Rupture back up. But try not to let yourself get into that kind of a bad situation.

To try to be with the clarity, even though this is a bit more than you were asking for, here's the general priorities I consider when running my rotation:


From Stealth: Garrote (otherwise Mutilate) > Rupture > Mutilate (If Ruthlessness didn’t proc CP only) > SnD > Mutilate to 4+CP > Envenom (unless because of bad procs SnD is going to fall off then Envenom early) > Mutilate to 4+CP > Envenom or Rupture if it's about to fall off (wait for last tick, so not to overwrite, 1+CP is all that's required for a Rupture) > Mutilate to 4+CP

Finisher Priority to follow during fight:
SnD > Rupture > Envenom
-Always refresh SnD with Envenom
-Always Envenom at 4+, unless SnD is going to fall off
-Pool to no more than 70-90 energy before Envenom (haste dependant) to get x2 Mutilate or 4+CP (whichever comes first) during buff and not cap energy due to procs or dodges
-Do not clip Envenom buff; wait until it wears off to re-apply Envenom
-Do not energy cap, this rule supersedes the previous rule
-Higher CP Ruptures are better than low CP Ruptures, but not at the cost of gaps in uptime, always Rupture with whatever CP is available as it is about to fall off

Sub 35% Execute Phase:
Replace Mutilate with Backstab, and always Envenom at 5CP, continuing to use 1+CP Ruptures

For added DPS boost:
-Tricks of the Trade as the tank makes the pull, and again whenever the CD is finished and you have a GCD open (Do not TotT and delay a more important action, use it while waiting for energy regen or energy pooling)
-Cold Blood at the first opportunity of a 5CP Envenom, if fights are long enough do this again on CD
-Vanish to restart Garrotte/Overkill, co-ordinating if possible with other group buffs for maximum potency
-Vendetta around the start of the sub 35% execute phase using any DPS trinkets/other CDs you may have here as well, again co-ordinating with group buffs for best DPS increase (i.e. Bloodlust), for longer fights Vendetta at the first available GCD once your rotation is stabilized, if you know you will have the CD available again for the Execute Phase
-On fights without interrupt requirements Arcane Torrent can provide early energy boost and get a full stable rotation going faster and apply an energy boost on CD throughout the fight (Blood Elf only)
