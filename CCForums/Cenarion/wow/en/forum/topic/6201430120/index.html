Pia will once again be hosting a box social open to all.

In case you missed the previous ones, here's what happens.

Ladies (the last one was men) prepare a "box lunch" to be auctioned off for charity. The ladies keep the food for the picnic, but they give the auctioneer a description of the decorated box the lunch would be in.

The auctioneer sells it to the highest bidder and the lady has lunch with the person who bought the lunch.

Funds are used to promote rp on CC. We use it to give prizes for contests, donate to new rp guilds starting up, donate to existing rp guilds when they have fund drives and just generally support and expand rp on the server.

This is a roleplay event, but it's open to anyone who wishes to attend, including horde. There will be a translator there to repeat what the auctioneer is saying. We'll also hire a caterer for people who don't bid on the lunches. Since opposing factions can't share food, it will have to be faction specific bidding.

All money raised from horde lunches will be donated to horde rp guilds to promote events there. I am thinking of donating it to Da Doctas and they can allocate it to the other guilds. If someone from Da Doctas sees this, please respond if that would be acceptable.

Warcraftier contacted me long ago asked to combine their transmog fashion contest with a roleplay event and we decided a box social would be the perfect venue. I hope everyone who entered the fashion show will attend in your transmog outfits and even participate in the box social. It's a fun way to socialize and meet new people even if you don't normally roleplay. Who knows, you might find out you enjoy it.

For those who don't know, Warcraftier has been a supporter of roleplay for a long time. Whenever I send out a call to help, such as when AAMS held their fund raising drive, they are always very quick to respond. This is one of CC's finest guilds and I hope people will recognize the contributions they make to the server, even if they are never in the rp spotlight.

Warcraftier is sponsoring a faaaabulous 100k gold grand prize transmog contest with several smaller prizes as well. If you haven't entered, please do so. There are several categories, so don't let the great submissions already in stop you.
