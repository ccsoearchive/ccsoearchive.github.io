I haven't read all the responses so I apologize if I'm repeating something someone already posted here.

It would seem that you simply don't have the time to cap points on several alts given your "strict" real life schedule. There's nothing wrong with that, it's just the way life is. There are only so many hours in a week and only so many of those you can reasonably spend in game. Given those limitations it makes sense that you will be faced with difficult decisions such as which toon(s) to work on this week. We all are limited by the same finite amount of time between cap resets and have to make that time work for us however we are able.

That said, it is not Blizzard's responsibility to compensate for your busier schedule outside of WoW. It isn't reasonable to expect otherwise. It simply makes sense that someone who is able to commit 40 hours per week to this game will be able to accomplish more than someone spending less than half that amount of time. That's pretty much how it works with all aspects of life, not just here. Get used to it.
