About us:

Eternal Sorrow was formed in September 2005, and still remain an active guild when it comes to progression. We're mostly all dedicated gamers, but don't take ourselves too seriously. We still try to provide a high level of play to be able to go through the content, and kill ze bosses. We strive to be a group of exceptional players focused on finding challenges to overcome, and the right players to meet those challenges. Our current goal is to progress through Cataclysm strong (or as Nerdos puts it, to be awesome-er).

We are currently recruiting select classes to strengthen our roster. This means we are looking for very skilled, knowledgeable players who desire to play well. We hold our members to competitive standards. We look to pick up players who enjoy maximizing their character and fulfill their raid role at the highest level.

Wanted Classes:
- Resto Druid
- Warlock or Shadow Priest
- All exceptional players

We are accepting extremely skilled players regardless of class needs. If your class is not posted and you're an exceptional player feel free to apply. We are accepting realm transfers as well as faction switches.

Our raid times are Monday to Thursday from 7:00 to 10:00 server time (PST). If we finish earlier, we won't force you to stay (nobody wants people to feel burned out). We're also focusing on 25man new Firelands stuff and heroic modes.

If you are interested in applying please visit our website at www.eternalsorrow.net/phpBB3 or speak to Nerdos.

Added bonus:
- A level 25 guild with all the goodies
- Have our own Minecraft and Minicity
- Double rainbows
- Awesomeness
