I just simply refuse to PVP beyond the odd Tol Barad in our current state. It gets really tiring to be killed by melee and have almost no control of my character. I remember in Vanilla being able to fight and kill 3 people at once (in raid gear). Succubus, fear, banish, and decent damage and survivability. We could still get chewed up, but we actually had a chance. I'm not asking to go back to that, but to be able to have a reasonable chance at winning a 1v1 against any melee class.

DK's simply destroy us. I had a DK once fight me, and at one point in the fight, he stopped and literally /danced with me while I unloaded all my dots, etc. After he generously let me cast all I could cast, he finished me off and went on his merry way with over 90% health. It's just unbelievable what they've done to this class.

Rogues, simply kill me 100-0. I trinket the KS. They vanish and start again. I consider myself fortunate if I have some range and can *try* to cast a fear. Or sometimes there is the possibility that I can get an instant cast corruption off. Super fun.

Warriors simply tear through us like we are dressed in toilet paper. The 1 in 100 chance that you have a portal up to teleport away is simply one charge, intercept, or heroic leap away anyway.

Paladins...I actually managed to get a paladin to use lay on hands a few days ago. He was killing a hunter and I jumped in to help. He killed the hunter (naturally) and then came at me. I managed to survive for 8 or 9 seconds, which was pretty impressive.

Shadow Priests...they do what we do but infinitely better. Far more dot damage, mind flay, stuns on their dots, dispersion, all kinds of cool tricks.

If I'm in a 1v1, I generally now just type /sleep and wait for the graveyard trip. That is of course, unless I find someone at 40% fighting three mobs. Then I feel like I might have a chance to kill him/her before they annihilate me. Otherwise, it's just an absolute joke.

We are totally de-fanged, de-clawed, and left with broken, gimmicky mechanics to deal with.

It's certainly a fun class, and I love it, but the PVP is just broken far beyond repair.

(Except 3's apparently, and somehow we miraculously perform at a decent level with two other competent people babysitting us).


You have a raid spec and raid gear. PvP is balanced around people with PvP specs/talents and gear.

FWIW, as Affliction you should be able to 1v1 ANYTHING except possibly a DK or maybe a rogue.

The biggest issue as I see it is that all of our counters are extremely hard counters, and there are lots of them. While you take a look at what we once countered, mages and palys, who are doing rather well against us when compared to how we are doing against ours. I would just love to be able to get an equally skilled/geared rogue or warrior below 50% before being hammered into the ground. If you can dispel or melee your a good candidate to be a warlock killer and the sad fact is, that's the majority of classes. We have no defenses against stuns or snares, burst melee kills us far to fast and our tools to gain distance are.. hinky.. with Deathcoil being the only reliable "oh no" button and it only only gains us enough room for 1 or 2 instants at most. That combined with only supportive damage makes the class very weak by itself, and if you like doing random battle grounds alone, that is a very bad thing. Plus, with our slow dot ticks, I've even had rogues pop vanish without cloak and catch me with a stun between ticks.

The fact is, what makes us "bad" in pvp needs to be addressed through survivability mechanics and distance gainers. Not through damage mechanics that would more then likely make us over powered in PvE. CoExhaustion, for example, needs to either be increased to it's former usefulness or removed and replaced with something else. As it stands it's pretty much useless due to abilities like charges, gw, cloak, etc. etc. etc. Replace it with a reliable single target snare imo. We also need an ability to either break free of a stun or an ability that reduces damage significantly while stunned, usable when stunned.

Now tele is great, don't get me wrong, I love the spell. But alone it's not enough to get us the distance we need and it's so predictable. A melee can easily get on us, then force us into a bind and force the port only to intercept us easily with one of their many distance creators. Charge, then intercept the port, AMS then DG the port, stealth then sprint, fs then gw.. you get the point. The only good uses are the LoS ones, while still predictable can at least get us a portion of a bandage and a few dot ticks. But we can't really do any more then that. We all know the dot ticks aren't going to kill them and anyone will easily be able to find us in seconds.

I also understand how it was addressed that stuns were going to be less effective come cata. That it wasn't desired that we would be killed within a stun lock. While it has slightly improved, a stun lock still means death, even after trinketing the KS. After all that they still have all of their cooldowns, and we are severely maimed. Unless the rogue really doesn't know what he's doing, we are going down with hardly a fight. Burst has already gotten much higher then survivability.

All I want is a single button to push, that doesn't require a specific pet or any sort of pre requirements that I can push when I get pulled into a pit of piranha unexpectedly that will help me survive the initial powpow of melee. And I don't mean a stun or another unbalancable fear. A single defensive mechanic that helps us against melee. It's our biggest issue and I really don't see why this blatantly obvious flaw has been neglected to be addressed for so long.
