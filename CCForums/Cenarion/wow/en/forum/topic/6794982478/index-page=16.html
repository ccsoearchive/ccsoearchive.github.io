Solorin, Auxilia, and Someone vs. Alexander ‘The Butcher’ Yuri (8 Shadow Knights, 8 Rangers, 4 Sorcerers)

Kel’tira and Cyaer vs. Targus Cloudstriker and Tychus Malcan (Tychus summons Shadow Beasts and Sha to fight for him)

Shadow and Ratheron vs. Tyranus Firehawk and Selethen Dawnrunner (5 Shadow Knights, 5 Sorcerers, 5 Rangers, 5 Blood Knights)

Tislina, Xalander and Someone vs. Darius Malcan (10 Shadow Knights, 5 Sorcerers, 5 Blood Knights)

These will be the pairings. I’ve left room for people to throw their alts and other characters into the groups. The only two groups that are set and completely unable to be joined are the Kel Cy and Shadow Rath groups. These are for plot devices that the characters involved feel like are great elements for the RP as a whole and for their character development. If you would like to discuss with me such things or see if I have an ideas for you let me know and I shall endeavor to help.

I am also willing to make a fifth group for those who feel “left out” I can easily just have Tychus go up against Kel and Cy and have it work out as well. The simple fact of this section of the RP is its a battle for survival. The Fellowship is caught off-guard and unprepared. They are outnumbered and out-skilled in a lot of regards. The consensus is to split up and force the enemy to split up as well. This way the strength of their numbers weakens as well as their combined “hero” strength as it were.

As you can see each enemy group has been given a number of troops, the Fellowship forces are not there as they were sent out on different tasks and missions, nobody expecting this attack. These are just smaller numbers than what the actual force consists of as there is a rear-guard and troops moving to hem in the Fellowship from using the sea as an escape route. They also sent troops out to isolate the Fellowship’s other forces to defeat them and further destroy the group.

As you can see the Fellowship is in a crappy position. This does not mean people have to die, nay I don’t want anyone to die. What I want to see however is injuries, fear, panic, dissension. I want to see people pushed past their breaking points or pushed to them. I want to see our characters challenged beyond anything they have been before and I want you to fail. The only reason the Fellowship survives past this is because it receives aid from the unlikely allies of Tyrael, Shadowless, Eve, Nicias and Raina. If they didn’t show up then this would all end badly for the Fellowship and its supposed to be that way. Read the bit I started this post with and you will see what the idea is.

Everything becomes strained and is held together by a single strand and this pushes us into the next stage of the RP. If you don’t like it please make your feelings clear here in the OOC thread or in the channel so that we can talk to you about it and either help you understand or work to compromise with you.

You have free creative license below are each of the bad guys (besides Tychus who I will be controlling) with their details and fighting styles and info about them so that you -can- control everything about the fight. However if I find your attempts to be “lacking” and yes I do get to make the call I will come in and RP it out with y’all. This is a simple matter of trying to keep the plot on the line of where it needs to go while still giving you the freedom to creatively write it and to fit within how you want to write it. Unlike a lot of RPs you have free reign of the combat. The only thing that is set is the tempo and then the ending. None of these characters will die, the troops can be killed and beaten, not easily mind you but they can be killed, however the “champions” cannot.

----

So I am just posting this a bit early. It is up in the old one as I didn't feel like posting it to the new one when we aren't yet ready to have that section of the Arc begin.

For those needing further explanation just let me know.
