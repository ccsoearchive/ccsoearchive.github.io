Abilities.
Ambidexterity (Passive)
Increases the damage you deal with your off-hand weapon by 75%.

Blindside
Performing a successful Mutilate has a 30% chance of leaving you in an advantageous position, enabling a single use of Dispatch regardless of the enemy target's health.

Combat Potency
Gives your successful off-hand melee attacks and Main Gauche attacks a chance to generate 15 Energy. Slower off-hand weapons have a proportionally higher chance to trigger Combat Potency.

Crimson Tempest
35 Energy Melee range
Instant
Requires Melee Weapon
Finishing move consumes combo points on any nearby target to slash at the flesh of all enemies within 8 yards, dealing Physical damage and causing victims to bleed and suffer an additional 30% of the initial damage over 12 sec:

Crippling Poison
3 sec cast
Requires One-Handed Melee Weapon
Coats your weapons with a Non-Lethal Poison that lasts for 60 min. Each strike has a 50% chance of poisoning the enemy, slowing their movement speed by 50% for 12 sec.

Deadly Poison
3 sec cast
Requires One-Handed Melee Weapon
Coats your weapons with a Lethal Poison that lasts for 60 min. Each strike has a 30% chance of poisoning the enemy for 0 Nature damage over 12 sec. Subsequent poison applications will instantly deal 0 Nature damage.

Expose Armor
25 Energy Melee range
Instant
Requires Melee Weapon
Weaken the target's defenses, applying the Weakened Armor debuff. Awards 1 combo point.

Weakened Armor
Weakens the armor of the target by 4% for 30 sec. Stacks up to 3 times.

Fan of Knives
35 Energy
Instant
Instantly whirl around, releasing a spray of knives at all targets within 8 yards, dealing 1,246 Physical damage. This attack has a chance of applying your active poisons at their normal rate. Generates 1 combo point if it strikes your current combo target.

Fleet Footed (Passive)
Your movement speed is increased by 15%. This does not stack with most other movement speed increasing effects.

Hemorrhage
30 Energy Melee range
Instant
Requires Melee Weapon
An instant strike that deals 100% weapon damage (145% if a dagger is equipped), causing profuse bleeding that deals an additional 40% of the direct strike's damage over 24 sec. Awards 1 combo point.

Replaces Sinister Strike.

Kick
15 Energy Melee range
Instant 15 sec cooldown
A quick kick that interrupts spellcasting and prevents any spell in that school from being cast for 5 sec.

Killing Spree
10 yd range
Instant 2 min cooldown
Requires Melee Weapon
Step through the shadows from enemy to enemy within 10 yards, attacking an enemy every 0.5 sec with both weapons until 7 assaults are made, and increasing all damage done by 20% for the duration. Can hit the same target multiple times. Cannot hit invisible or stealthed targets.

Master Poisoner
Increases the spell damage taken by any target you have poisoned by 5%.

Mind-numbing Poison
3 sec cast
Requires One-Handed Melee Weapon
Coats your weapons with a Non-Lethal Poison that lasts for 60 min. Each strike has a 50% chance of poisoning the enemy, applying the Slowed Casting effect.

Slowed Casting
Clouds the target's mind, slowing their casting speed by 30% for 10 sec.

Relentless Strikes (Passive)
Your finishing moves have a 20% chance per combo point to restore 25 Energy.

Revealing Strike
Melee Range
An instant strike that deals 0% weapon damage and exposes the target's vulnerabilities, increasing the effectiveness of your offensive finishing moves on that target by 0%, and giving your Sinister Strikes a 20% chance to generate an extra combo point, for 18 sec. Awards 1 combo point.

Shadow Blades
Instant 3 min cooldown
Draw upon the surrounding shadows to empower your weapons, causing your auto-attacks to deal pure Shadow damage and your combo-point-generating abilties to generate an additional combo point when used. Lasts 12 sec.

Shadow Walk
Instant 60 sec cooldown
Requires Stealth
Significantly increases Stealth effectiveness for 6 sec.

Shiv
20 Energy Melee range
Instant 10 sec cooldown
Requires One-Handed Melee Weapon
Strikes an enemy's pressure point with your off-hand weapon, dealing 25% weapon damage, dispelling an Enrage effect from the target, and applying a concentrated form of your active Non-Lethal poison. Awards 1 combo point.

See individual Non-Lethal poison tooltips for details regarding the concentrated effects of each.

Shroud of Concealment
Instant 5 min cooldown
Requires Stealth
Extend a cloak that wraps party and raid members within 20 yards in shadows, concealing them from sight.

Slice and Dice
25 Energy 100 yd range
Instant
Finishing move that consumes combo points on any nearby target to increase melee attack speed by 40%. Lasts longer per combo point:
1 point : 12 seconds
2 points: 18 seconds
3 points: 24 seconds
4 points: 30 seconds
5 points: 36 seconds

Stealth
Instant 6 sec cooldown
Conceals you in the shadows, allowing you to stalk enemies without being seen. Lasts until canceled.

Swiftblade's Cunning (Passive)
Increases the melee and ranged attack speed of all party and raid members within 100 yards by 10%.

Throw
30 yd range
0.5 sec cast
Hurl a dagger at an enemy target.

Wound Poison
3 sec cast
Requires One-Handed Melee Weapon
Coats your weapons with a Lethal Poison that lasts for 60 min. Each strike has a 30% chance of poisoning the enemy, which instantly inflicts 0 Nature damage and reduces all healing effects on them by 0% for 15 sec.


Talents.
Anticipation Rank 1
When one of your attacks generates a combo point on a target that already has 5 combo points, you gain an Anticipation charge, up to a maximum of 5.

When you perform an offensive finishing move on an enemy, any Anticipation charges are consumed to grant you an equal number of combo points on that target.

Burst of Speed Rank 1
Increases movement speed by 0% for 4 sec. If you are afflicted by any movement-impairing effects, activating this ability will instead remove any such effects and grant immunity to their re-application for 4 sec.

Cheat Death Rank 1
An attack that would otherwise be fatal will instead reduce you no lower than 10% of your maximum health, and damage taken will be reduced by 0% for 3 sec. This effect cannot occur more than once per 90 seconds.

Combat Readiness
2 min cooldown
Enter into a state of heightened awareness, deflecting enemy weapon strikes with increasing effectiveness. Successive attacks will deal 10% less damage per application, stacking 5 times.

Lasts for 20 sec, but if 10 sec elapse without any incoming weapon strikes, this state will end.

Deadly Brew
Whenever you apply one of your Non-Lethal poisons to a target, you also apply Crippling Poison.

Deadly Throw (Talent)
35 Energy 30 yd range
Finishing move that reduces the movement speed of the target by 50% for 6 sec. If performed with 5 combo points, also interrupts spellcasting and prevents any spell in that school from being cast for 6 sec.

Dirty Tricks Rank 1
Your Gouge and Blind no longer have an Energy cost, and no longer break from damage dealt by your Poison and Bleed effects.

Elusiveness Talent
Your Feint ability also reduces all damage taken by 30% for 5 sec.

Leeching Poison Rank 1
3 sec cast
Coats your weapons with a Non-Lethal Poison that lasts for 1 hour. Your melee attacks have a 50% chance to poison the target, and all your subsequent weapon strikes against the poisoned target will heal you for 0% of damage dealt.

Nerve Strike Rank 1
A successful Kidney Shot or Cheap Shot also reduces the damage dealt by the target by 50% for 6 seconds after the effect fades.

Nightstalker Rank 1
Increases your speed while stealthed by 0%.

Paralytic Poison Rank 1
3 sec cast
Coats your weapons with a Non-Lethal Poison that lasts for 1 hour. Each strike has a 20% chance of poisoning the enemy for 15 sec. Stacks up to 5 times on a single target, and upon a fifth application, the enemy will be stunned for 4 seconds.

Preparation
Instant 5 min cooldown
When activated, this ability immediately finishes the cooldown on your Sprint, Vanish, Cloak of Shadows, Evasion, and Dismantle abilities.

Shadow Focus Rank 1
Abilities no longer cost Energy while you are stealthed.

Shadowstep
25 yd range
Instant 24 sec cooldown
Step through the shadows and appear behind a target. Movement speed is increased by 70% for 2 sec afterwards.

Shuriken Toss Level 85
30 yd range
A ranged attack that deals 0 Physical damage to an enemy target. Generates 1 combo point.

Subterfuge Rank 1
Your Stealth breaks 3 sec after dealing or receiving hostile actions, rather than doing so immediately.

Versatility Rank 1
Your Redirect ability now has no cooldown.

Glyphs.

Glyph of Blade Flurry
Your attacks have a 30% higher chance of applying Non-Lethal poisons while Blade Flurry is active.

Glyph of Decoy
When you Vanish, you leave behind a brief illusion that very closely resembles you.

Glyph of Debilitation
Your Eviscerate and Envenom abilities also reduce the target's movement speed by 50% for 6 sec.

Glyph of Detection
Focus intently on trying to detect something.

Glyph of Disguise
When you Pick Pocket a humanoid enemy, you also copy their appearance for until cancelled.

Glyph of Expose Armor
Your Expose Armor ability causes three applications of Weakened Armor.

Glyph of Feint
Increases the duration of Feint by 2 sec.

Glyph of Hemorrhage
Your Hemorrhage ability only causes lingering damage over time to targets that were already afflicted by a Bleed effect.

Glyph of Killing Spree
Your Killing Spree returns you to your starting location when the effect ends.

Glyph of Recuperate
Increases the healing of your Recuperate ability by an additional 0.5% of your maximum health.

Glyph of Shadow Walk
Your Shadow Walk ability also increases your stealth detection while active.

Glyph of Sinister Strike
Your Sinister Strike deals 25% more damage and costs 25% more Energy.

Glyph of Smoke Bomb
Increases the duration of your Smoke Bomb by 2 sec.

Glyph of Tricks of the Trade
Your Tricks of the Trade ability no longer costs Energy, but also no longer increases the damage dealt by the target.

Glyph of Vendetta
Reduces the damage bonus of your Vendetta ability by 5% but increases its duration by 10 sec.

I don't know. The combat ones seem give benefit for haste capping scenarios. Looking at some of the changes, and some dev comments, they may be exploring options for rotations via glyphs.

The glyph of inquisition for instance makes it so that it has higher uptime for players who want it easier to keep up, while still allowing players who like the challenge to have slightly better dps. The tradeoff comes in just how optimally you play. If you play really well, the glyph is bad, but if you find yourself having trouble, you can glyph it to make the rotation more stable at the cost of theoretical dps.

We have something similar. Glyphing rupture and slice and dice makes subtlety much more manageable, especially while learning the rotation.

I think we'll see players using things like the sinister strike glyph in high haste scenarios when the energy would be wasted anyway or for questing where burst is more valuable.

I have no idea what they are doing with some of them like tricks.

And the net nerf to recuperate along with the removal of survivability talents is going to hurt soloing.

If you're a player who's just joined us within the past year or two, please keep in mind the cardinal rule of beta: What's datamined from beta doesn't always stay in beta. :)

Datamining often only tells part of the story or leaves out important context for what appear to be changes in certain abilities, items, you name it. For instance, as of yet we don't know the entirety of glyph choices we'll have as rogues, or even how many of each type of glyph we'll definitely be able to choose from, so with something like the Smoke Bomb glyph we have no idea yet what other glyphs we might have to give up in order to take it. Indications are that Blizz wants our major glyph choices to be about as tough as our talent choices, and our minor glyph choices to hardly affect actual gameplay at all.

So please, especially in the next few weeks as we just begin to see our new virtual world, carry a lot of salt with you. And sprinkle a grain of it as you read what we all write about apparent changes. :)
