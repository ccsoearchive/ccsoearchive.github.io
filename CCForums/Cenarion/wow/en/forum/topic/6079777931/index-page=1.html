First off, thanks for checking us out! Since from what you've said, you seem to already know a bit about the server in general, so I'll skip right to answering your questions.

Background-wise, don't start with a complex story for your character, start with something simple and elaborate upon it if you wish.

As for professions commonly used with Rp, the most common I've seen are engineering, tailoring, and alchemy.

Addons are something I personally opt not to use, but I know quite a few that use My Roleplay and Total Roleplay 2.

And last of all, welcome to the server! Be sure to check us out at out chat channels, allianceOOC (Or hordeOOC) and hearthstone (HearthstoneIC? I dunno, I'm not in that channel.) Have fun, and beware of frying pans! ;)
