In the beginning, the great titan, Blizzard, searched the world for those who were destined to be heroes and found nine great souls. To these souls Blizzard offered power that would be limited only by their imagination.

Blizzard gathered these beings before him to find what powers he should bestow. This is how said meeting went.

Blizzard: Hail mighty adventurers, your souls and those of your descendants will be key in the protection of this great world so in my infinite wisdom I will offer you power, all you need do is ask.

Warrior: I WANT STRENGTH IN BATTLE! AND WEAPONS THE SIZE OF MOUNTAINS SO THAT I MIGHT DISEMBOWL MY ENEMIES.

Blizzard: Settle down Warrior, you are not first, no, Warlock is to be first.

Warrior: SILLY DISEMBODIED VOICE, YOU DO NOT COMMAND ME! I WILL CRUSH YOU!

Rogue: Good luck with that mate, crushing a giant disembodies voice that has the power to grant wishes can’t be that difficult.

Warrior: SHUT UP YOU MONGREL PUP OR I WILL RIP OUT YOUR THROAT WITH MY TEETH!

SMACK!

Warrior: WHAT THE HELL WAS THAT? WHO IS SO SCARED THAT THEY MUST STRIKE ME FROM BEHIND?

Blizzard: As I said Warrior, settle down or I will do much worse than that. Now as I said, Warlock will go first.

Warlock: I want the power to control the energies and denizens of the fel plane. I want to be able to suck the life force from my enemies and corrupt their souls with fire. I want to be the master of all that is darkness and be able to strike fear into the heart of my enemies.

Rogue: A little over dramatic don’t you think?

Warlock: You, thief, will be my first victim (shoots burning glare).
