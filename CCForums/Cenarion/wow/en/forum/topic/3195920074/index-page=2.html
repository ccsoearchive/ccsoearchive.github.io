Many people in the thread have stated that Skullcrusher is actually listed as a medium population server I can assure you that on the alliance side the server is almost completely dead. As of this week we do not have any more 25man guilds left on the alliance side. The horde side has one 25man guild left.

Over the last three weeks I have been scanning the alliance side with an add on called 'Census Plus' probably 4-5 times a day to see how many players we have on the server. As of right now the add on is telling me we have 702 different lvl 85 characters. Now that isn't 702 different people who are playing on the server. That is 702 different level 85 characters. Considering most people have at least 2+ level 85s you can safely say the active player base is less then 300 on skullcrusher alliance at the moment.

This does not make for a very enjoyable server to play on and something needs to change. This makes recruiting for guilds extremely difficult, gives us a very poor economy on the server since there is not much demands for items that would sell quickly on other servers. This also limits our auction house of new content BoEs, recently I spent two weeks spamming trade chat and constantly checking the AH before it was possible to even get two obsidium cleavers for my warrior.

People are leaving and quitting because of the conditions of their servers. Blizzards needs to do something, but they won't, because the bottom line is server transfers and faction changes make them too much money and they aren't willing to lose that income. Merger servers would be the best possible thing they could ever do for the game right now.
