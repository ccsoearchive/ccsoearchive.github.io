Drakehide stretched, and glanced over the balcony at the glorious Dalaran sunrise. His beloved was still sleeping, in the room they'd rented out for the evening while they were here, traveling. Kol'than and Mogg had finally stopped harassing them, near sundown, and...everything was calm, now. Peaceful. It was a glorious new day, and Drakehide felt glorious.

...until a stray, floating piece of parchment, carried on the breeze, slapped the warrior cleanly in the face.

He snatched it up, and inspected it.

A WHAT party - ?

He breathed a sigh of relief, as he read the message. He was not the intended target for this "stag party". At least, he was fairly certain he wasn't. However...notices like this presented a major, major threat. The last thing he needed was Keedriel, Fearow, or Aminara getting their hands on notices like this - given current events in his life - and getting any funny ideas.

He checked that his obsidium bolt gun was loaded, and that he had a full complement of saronite grenades.

Drakehide was going hunting.

Every single one of these parchments in Horde or neutral territories needed to be destroyed, NOW, before they made their way to Ratchet...
