How about you just address the real offender for Warrior burst in PvP: Colossus Smash.

You're trying to balance the entire class around one ability, which is frustrating enough to use effectively with how much CC is in this game at the moment. I rather have our mobility work reliably, and put out steady pressure rather than have a 6s window every 20s in which I can kill someone. Our damage should be more consistent outside of Colossus Smash. There are too many forms of CC and escape tools that can counter this ability as is, and we pay too high a price when we can't properly utilize it.

Heroic Leap needs to work everywhere in BGs/Arena. I'm tired of seeing the "You can't use that here." message due to uneven terrain or a slight incline. I can understand the "No path available." message in most cases, but there needs to be a little more leeway here as well. It should work the same way that Life Grip and Death Grip work. I can Charge straight up a cliff or in midair, but I can't Heroic Leap up there? Just make it work properly, please.

Hamstring needs to be looked at as well. There is a talent that allows us to root someone in place if we use the ability twice, two global cooldowns, and it can be overwritten by just about every other snare in the game. Chains of Ice snares 10% more than Hamstring and has a 20-30 yard range. Crippling Poison snares for 20% more, and is automatically applied by the Rogue's attacks or can be applied on demand with Shiv. Why is Hamstring so archaic when compared to other snares in the game?

I like having Fury be viable in PvP, but the ability to remove Enrage effects is crippling to Warriors who are trying Fury in competitive PvP. Shiv, Soothe, and Tranquilizing Shot can already easily counter Raging Blow. If the damage is being lowered, then can we at least retain the ability to use this somewhat reliably in PvP without losing our Enrage effects, or at least remove the Enrage requirement from Raging Blow?

Also, can we expect any sort of compensation for the damage lost from Raging Blow?
