As someone that is into heroic 25 cataclysm raiding, this moonkin nerf is both justifiable and ridiculous at the same time. At the moment, I'm constantly first or 2nd in 11/12 fights, usually without the effects of DI (I sometimes have it, but most of the time it goes to our spriest). With DI, I just completely dominate damage and dps, which isn't a problem with the class, it's a problem with an ability that directly affects a major source of our damage, dots. Before actually going through with the nerfs, Blizzard would first have to look at a 25 man raid without a warlock, and observe balance and shadow priest damage accordingly. I think they'll find that we're actually balanced compared to other classes.

Sure, with DI, both specs dominate. With these nerfs, they are basically saying is with DI, you can be balanced, and without it, your damage is crippled. This is not the way to go about it, especially with your bring the player not the class moto. Realistically, how many 10 mans are going to contain a warlock? Some, yes, all of them, no. Balancing 2 classes around a buff that only one class can give is completely wrong.

However, on a positive note, I can see where Blizzard are coming from, atleast from a boomkin perspective. Our damage is high, but it isn't single target. A high majority of Tier 11 fights have add phases, or council type encounters (Ascendent/Halfus/Valiona and Theralion) where our multi-dotting and use of bugged starfall and buffed wild mushrooms blows all other dps out of the water. In these cases, I can see nerfing us acceptable, but crippling single target damage over AoE type encounters is, in my opinion, wrong. What happens on fights like cataclysms Festergut type encounter, or even BH, where it's just a single target DPS race? We'll lack behind because our damage got nerfed due to AoE encounters.

Overall, I'll take this nerf on the chin as I am certain I can remain competitive and in the top 3 for 25 Heroic raiding. Even without DI, a well played boomkin, with buffs to mushrooms, starfall and mastery, can out damage and compete with fellow DPS specs. Balance druids can't really say, from a PvE perspective, that Blizzard hasn't shown us some love this expansion, even if it's to do with the type of raid encounters we face, or our spec as a whole.

By the way, sorry to Spriests, Warriors and PvP balance druids. I can see this hurting you guys and the last thing I want is for our overall raid DPS to suffer due to mistakes from blizzard. Hopefully you guys can some compensation like we did with it going from 6% - 10%.

Tl;DR: Read it or move on.
