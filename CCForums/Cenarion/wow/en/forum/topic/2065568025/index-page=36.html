Here are some reasons a broad nerf to ALL of shadow priests damage is a really bad idea.

Mind Sear: You buffed mind sear because no one even cast it anymore it was so underwhelming - this nerf undo's that buff

Dark Intent: Without this buff, we do not do too much damage in PVE. So you are doing a broad nerf to ALL damage based on a buff?? That is the same as saying "Well, there is this one trinket in the game that you may or may not have, but if you do have it, you do too much damage, so instead of fixing the trinket we are just going to reduce all damage you do by 10%, just in case". How is this logical at all?

PVP: You fully admitted shadow was in a bad state in PVP prior to patch 4.06. The mastery buff you felt would remedy that. Now you nerf all damage based on "burst potential"? Why would you nerf my Shadow Word: Pain because of "burst potential"? Why would you nerf my Vampiric touch because of "burst potential"? Why would you nerf my mind flay because of "burst potential"? Please explain this logic?

Shadow Orbs: You buffed the amount they benefit from mastery by 15%, but now reduce the amount of damage overall by 10%. Why are you changing things in one manner only to remove them immediately back in a different way?

Shadow Word: Death: This spell as an execute was already very situational and felt somewhat inherently unfair in comparison to other classes execute abilities do to the back lash effect. In real raids, often priests are too low in health to use this ability at all, (Chimereon, Halfus during stuns etc etc). We do not always get to use it, or it kills us. Now it has been nerfed by 10%?? None of the buffs in 4.06 affected SWD. so did you always feel it was too strong?? Even though it was so situational at times?

In general, broad overall damage reduction nerfs such as these come across as very lazy. You are not looking at the root cause. You are looking at meters and instead of asking why shadow priests moved up, you are simply saying, we want you 10% lower.

Well guess what. Haste scales linear for us, so if you don't want to address the real issue which is Dark Intent and Power Infusion, then it's only a matter of time before our gear pushes us back up for those that get the buff. What then? nerf us again? That makes me feel bad for all the shadow priests out there not receiving DI/PI, as the gap between them and the priests that do will widen.

Please think this through further and address the root cause and not blindly hack away 10% of all damage done.

If you have been playing WoW long enough, you remember all the Shadow Weaving nerfs (a Shadow Priest target debuff was making Warlocks do too much damage so they left Warlocks alone and nerfed Shadow Weaving).

This is very similar but even worse (a Warlock single-target buff is making a single Shadow Priest in the raid do too much damage), so they nerf Shadow Priests instead of the buff.

Dark Intent has no place in the current raiding paradigm anyway (it goes counter to bring the player, as you'll need a Warlock for every Shadow Priest you want to bring).

Not that Shadow doesn't need some innate nerfs, but first they have to get rid of DI. The best way is by making DI a self-buff on the Warlock (maybe a 3% haste buff with a raid-wide 1%/2%/3% buff to DoT damage/HoT healing when the Warlock's DoTs crits, which would only be a minimal nerf to Warlocks and Blizzard could buff their damage some other way).

After that, the Shadow Priest fix would be smaller (probably reducing VT by ~15%).
