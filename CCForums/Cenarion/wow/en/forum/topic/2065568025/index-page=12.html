Arcane Mana Costs:

The problem Arcane is having with mana is in regards to the design of Arcane Blast itself.

Presently:

Each time you cast Arcane Blast, the damage of Arcane Blast is increased by 13%, the casting time is reduced by 0.1 sec and mana cost is increased by 150%.

What this is doing:

Arcane Blast for me at (no buffs procs or intel, but Arcane Tactics talent 3% dmg buff):

initial cast: base dmg 13582 for 1219 mana. ...Ok very generous
1 stack: base dmg 15347 for 3047 mana ...This is where the mana increase gets rediculous.

In comparison to the Fire spec Livingbomb spell:

17938 dmg over 12 secs for 2961 mana. ...Now I added the explosion dmg in which was
2826dmg. This can be spread to 3 targets, but I only accounted for a single boss in this
case.

2 stacks: base dmg 17113 for 4876 mana. ...Again the mana cost is still rediculous, but it's not more than the Flamestrike (a fire AoE which turns to instant cast via talents) for 5225 mana. It's also less than Blizzard which is 12889 mana.

3 stacks: 6704 mana (hard to give you true dmg b/c procs are hitting by this point) but the base dmg should be 18878 dmg.

4 stacks: 8533 mana (hard to give you true dmg b/c procs are hitting by this point) but the base dmg should be 20643 dmg.

The full ramp up on the spell is an increase of 700% of the initial base mana cost of the spell by the time you are casting with 4 stacks on a target. I'm certainly not advocating that we should get super cheap 20k spells.

However, the mana cost design of Arcane Blast is the problem. What should be changed is the cost multiplier of the stacks. This might want to be changed to a 40% increase of mana per stack. The costs would look like this (now this is from my mana pool with ilvl avg of 355):

initial cost: 1219 mana (100%)
1 stack: 1706 mana (140%)
2 stacks: 2194 mana (180%)
3 stacks: 2681 mana (220%)
4 stacks: 3169 mana (260%)

I truly think this would bring Arcane on par with Fire.

Now, the only other thing Arcane could use a placeable AoE that we can. Arcane Explosion is not good to use on PvE encounters, so this forces Arcane to use Flamestrike and Blizzard abilities for AoE. I was thinking maybe the Devs could gives us a spell similar to the one of the bosses in Halls of Origination (think it's Setesh). The boss drops small AoE bubbles on the ground that does dmg to nearby targets. Something similar to this could be useful to have. :)
