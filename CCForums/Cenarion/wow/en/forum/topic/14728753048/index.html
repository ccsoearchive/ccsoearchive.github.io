[A-Server Event] Hallow's End Carnival!

Add a reply
Gloree
Profile
Gloree
100 Human Priest
The Order of Goldrinn
9865
Located all throughout Stormwind are fliers posted upon stone walls, light-posts and pinned to wooden trees. Upon the fliers are illustrations of not only the Gilnean Nation Insignia, but an odd picture of a metallic shaped wolf's head.

The words Come One Come All! are etched at the top while the rest reads:

Come celebrate the ending of a bountiful harvest season, and welcome in autumn's tricks-and-treats, with the second annual carnival hosted by the Gilnean group, The Order of Goldrinn. Food, entertainment, games, prizes and a costume contest with the reward of a small fortune all await within the Stormwind Cemetery, Saturday October 18th at sunset. Wander down and enjoy!

OOC INFO:

The Order of Goldrinn will be hosting their second annual server-wide faire on October 18th starting at 7 server. Our members will be hosting small games, booths, stories and much more! Thank you so much for the support and encouragement after last year's event, we appreciate that you had just as much fun as we did! So excited to share in this distinctive experience with you again this year!

Costume Contest Rules:

The Costume Contest will be judged by my character. All outfits MUST be In Character and fit with the World of Warcraft Lore. That means, while your Mario and Superman costumes are super awesome, they are not feasible to judge.

Armor sets are allowed. Such as, wearing Paladin Grand Marshal Gear for a Bolvar outfit, IS acceptable to judging. The more creative and impressive your costume is, the more likely you'll be chosen as a finalist!

Costume Contest Prizes:

1st Place: 15,000 Gold

2nd Place: 10,000 Gold

3rd Place: 5,000 Gold

For any questions concerning the event, please contact Esmond or Ovan in game.
