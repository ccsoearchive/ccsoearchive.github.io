Can't resist chiming in - lol :)

This idea is a *great* one - for many ppl :)

The potentials for abuse - hacked accounts - guild ninja'd in the night [as if - that's when most ppl play - lol] - or auctioned on <insert-auction-place here> are all at least *somewhat* addressable though...

The concept is a "guild transfer"... so to stop hacked/ninja'd:

1. Look for recent activity - such as mass kickings - not toons voluntarily dropping - to detect possible hack [perhaps no transfer eligibility until no members leave for 7 days]

2. Require a confirmation from multiple ppl in the guild - not necessarily officers [ppl can be promoted/demoted with hacked account] but ppl who have been in for a certain length of time or have a certain rep with the guild [honored/revered/etc]. The number of ppl required would be left to blizzard - but either some % of remaining members [20%] or a hard number [5 minimum?]...

3. In event of "bank toon guilds" - ie 1 member - where a hacked account = the whole guild - require a 7 day waiting period before transfer will commence - hopefully allowing for hacked person to discover and recover the account...

Actually having a 7 day transfer period for a guild - in which a guild message comes up saying "transfer in X days" [on login] - and an action to take if the account is being hacked - would be another way to stop the hack/ninja'd thing...


Insofar as the "selling" of a guild for RL $ or wow gold... perhaps requiring "voting" toons to transfer will make that unfeasable - ie - they will sell their characters off with the guild.

Example: "Goldsellers R Us" decide to post on e-bay and transfer: get $2000 for a 25 guild with 6 tabs and perks... because of the guild size - maybe 100 toons - blizz decides 10 ppl need to transfer with the guild - and they need to be 85 and revered with the guild. In order for the guild transfer to proceed - they require that on the new server - they transfer - log in - and "activate" the new guild - they've paid whatever cost for transferring - and they're stuck on the new server... and then they have no guild - and perhaps few or no friends on the new server...

If they're *that* committed - willing to spend all that collective time levelling to sell it off [not just one person here either] not sure what blizz can do - perhaps make it when the "transferrring" toons leave the guild - it disbands?

Not too likely :(

Also - perhaps having a guild "split" function later would be cool - ie - guild gets in a fight and decides to split. Currently - half the guild drops and forms a new guild or joins another - all guild perks are lost - rep - etc...

Splitting could function half like a rename - but to prevent it's abuse - maybe have the "new guild" be <old guild level> x .5 or -3 or whatever - to prevent the abuse of said feature - perhaps even "de-leveling" the original guild in the process - and such a process would again require a voting system based on guild rank/time with guild/whatever...

Anyway - sorry for the wall of text - just my 2 cents - lol :D
