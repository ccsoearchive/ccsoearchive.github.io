To combat the "GM gets hacked and makes off with the guild" scenario, I propose the following:

Implement an optional rank of "advisor" which is:

- A rank type consisting of one or more characters, all of whom must be online at the same time and confirm the GM's decision to perform certain actions, such as to disband the guild, or to transfer it.

This allows the GM to protect the guild. Much like the 'require authenticator' rank type, it is optional. It should also be able to be stacked with 'require authenticator' for best protection.

Looking back at the core issue, if your GM doesn't have an authenticator, you're much more likely to be a sitting duck than if the GM did have one.
