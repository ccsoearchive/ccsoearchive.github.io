Moon Guard is the largest, without a doubt, if you're looking for massive amounts of RP out in the world instead of in guilds.

Wyrmrest Accord has a pretty tight-knit community that spans both sides, active RP on both sides, and a lot of guilds to suit anyone's needs. You're going Horde-side, so I would suggest looking into the race-specific guilds we've got going on. I can only speak for the Tauren tribes that are on Wyrmrest Accord.

If you're planning on rolling Tauren, look into the Stormtotem Tribe, Snowhoof Tribe, Earthspear Tribe, and the Shadowhoof Tribes. These are the biggest Tauren RP guilds out on Wyrmrest Accord.

But other than that, make your decision based on your own observances rather than the plugs of everyone on this thread. You can't choose a server simply because someone told you it was awesome.
