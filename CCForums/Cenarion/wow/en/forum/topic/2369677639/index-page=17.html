It depends on you honestly. Most of the random on our server happens in hubs and in the evening. So the Blue Recluse or the Salty Sailor for instance. The more you know people in the community, the more randomness opens up for short bursts. For instance, I ran into Gentyl (leader of Pia Presidium) at the Brawler's Pit and we had a short interaction. I've run into people at the Auction House and carried on a conversation for a bit as well. But it becomes more common the more you get to know the people on the server.

Most of our guilds don't require a complete interaction in the story, just a connection to it. Like for Wellspring School, you're a teacher or a student. For Pia Presidium, you're a guardian of the Light. For Conclave, you're a pursuer of knowledge. For Lluchuu Oeliad (I think I mispelt that but Imperon can correct it), you're part of Imperon's 'Family.'

Some of the RP guilds on the server have pretty extensive storylines that you can belong to, but we have a fair allotment of events and open RP interactions to keep it casual and friendly. There's a list of events posted on the forum that's pretty accurate still even though it was last updated more than 6 months ago. If anything, this server is consistent.
