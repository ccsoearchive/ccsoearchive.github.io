Gentyl needed to get the word out about some things and was looking for a better method than she currently used. Frostfall seemed to have good luck contacting Imperon by sending him leaves on the wind with runes on them. Perhaps something like that.

Gentyl glanced over at Frost. The leaves seemed to work for her, but it was probably a druid thing. What were paladins known for?

She took out her blacksmith hammer and beat out a little piece of steel then stuck it back in the forge for a bit to finish it. When she was done she had a lovely miniature shield. She polished the surface and the edges, paying close attention to the four barbs surrounding the center of the shield. Then she carefully etched the words on it, "Lord Imperon. Need to speak."

When all was done she said a prayer over it and dedicated it to the Light. Looked like a perfect divine shield to her. She tried tosing it into the wind as she had seen Frost do with the leaf, but it just dropped like a rock, or a little shield. She sighed at Frost who was watching with the curiosity of a cat, which she was at the time.

Gentyl picked it back up and chunked the divine shield as hard as she could, then watched with much satisfaction as it flew through the air. THUNK The shield hit Guard Jaxon square in the forehead and just kind of stayed there, like a little shelf.

"Ooops. C'mon, Frost. We better go."

Roleplay on Cenarion Circle is seldom what you expected.
