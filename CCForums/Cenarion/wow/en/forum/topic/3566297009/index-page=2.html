A: Yes, in 5.0. Block capping and mastery in general is currently too good for warriors and paladins. We think tank balance is close enough in 4.3 that dramatic overhauls could make matters worse. In 5.0 we will change things.


OK, so I asked a question about this and did not get a reply. I am fine with that as I know you guys were bombarded by players. The question I asked was basically, "In pre-heroic raid gear, only a paladin can reach unhittable. In the past raid scene, it became a requirement to get a raid invite. Will you make this goal attainable to keep the tanking classes balanced". Now I get an answer. Even though I have raided FL weekly on normal, doing the best that my guild can at 6/7, I am now told I am OP and my mastery must be nerfed, even tho as a warrior I cannot hit unhittable. This makes me sad. You guys give us tier pieces with stats you KNOW we do not want, making unhittable impossible, and then tell us we are OP even when we cannot reach the goal any tank wants to hit?

Pre-cata when defense was the tank stat, you never made it so we could not attain the goal every tank needed to reach. Why the shift? There was a time when crazy gear setups could make a player untouchable. Now if we get close to a mark that allows us to peform our intended role pre-heroic, we are labeled as OP?

Please, allow us, as players, to reach the milestones that are important to our class with due diligence. If we have put forth the effort and time to gain the upgrades available to us, in a setting that warrants such stats, make them attainable. You can certainly tune encounters around any stats widely accessible. Why would you choose to deny the threshhold, then tune accordingly? Being 3% from an important milestone for a long time does not feel like a win. It feels like defeat. I, and perhaps many raid leaders, am starting to feel pressured to be a Paladin.
