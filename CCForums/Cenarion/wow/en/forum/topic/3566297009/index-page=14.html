Queueing as fury and tanking in DPS gear are two ENTIRELY different things.

A tank in DPS gear is still insanely durable pre-80, and can handle anything a low level instance can throw at them.

When was the last time you leveled a tank, Xurk? On my prot paladin, I soloed half of the instances from 40-75. In DPS gear. At the recommended level. Warriors can probably do the same thing for trash pulls with Victory Rush, although bosses might give them trouble.

This is theorycraft 101: you get the stats you need. If you're dying to burst, you get stamina. If your healers are running OOM, you get avoidance. Otherwise, you focus on damage output to speed things up. That's why people recommend hit/expertise if you're only running 5-mans.

At low levels, healers don't run OOM when healing people in tank specs (unless you're talking about really low level druids, who have no real defensive abilities yet). Tanks don't die to burst damage.

Dodge and parry don't do ANYTHING useful for low level tanks. DPS stats do.
