You're in luck! CC's got a nice balance of Alliance and horde RPers. Several RP guilds also run the occasional raid, both current and old content. We have RPing hubs on both factions (Blue Recluse in SW for Alliance, Wyvern's Tail in Org or broken Keel in Ratchet for Horde) and a great, mostly troll-free community.

Be sure to check out either faction's OOC channel (AllianceOOC, HordeOOC) and CC's website for more info. Welcome to the server!

Jump out of trade and into allianceooc. You'll find more help there since that's our RP OOC channel. We also have HearthstoneIC if you'd like to try and introduce yourself IC and find guilds through a RP moment instead of just asking OOC.

There are a plethora of great guilds out there. Going evil? Check out Imperon's guild. If you're an assassin, try the Assassin's League. Feathers of Iron is a mercenary group of bar owners that are holding an expo on June 9th so you can check that out as well. Conclave is a Light and law abiding group of people. A little more scholarly but friendly all the same. You have Pia Presidium if you want to join an Holy Guard group. Then there's... as you can see the list goes on and on and caters to a lot of different types of players and stories. Your best bet is to join one of our channels and get to chatting everyone up. If you don't match our particular guild, we'll point you to one that maybe you will. We're friendly like that.

Oh and there's an event tonight. Troubadour night is hosted in the Explorer's Hall in IronForge. You may want to check that out for some fun stories and songs and goofy jokes.
