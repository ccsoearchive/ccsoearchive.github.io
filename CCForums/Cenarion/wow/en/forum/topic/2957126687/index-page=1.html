Alterac Valley

Broken quests, NPC's are too easy to defeat, quest requirements too high, games are too short... this is a short list of what is wrong with this battleground. What is needed to fix this BG and to make it truly awesome is to give it some attention.

Implement achievements for some of the cooler events in the battle. Make every NPC a powerful opponent. It's irony, sure, but stronger NPC's force PvP to happen. Implement periodic honor games and rework some of the quests. WoW is missing something that is a persistent, raging battle which was meant to be one of the prominent feelings in Cataclysm. We need a battleground that lasts longer than 30 minutes.

Think of all the achievements that could be added to AV with the removal of the abomination that is 'The Alterac Blitz' - one to summon Ivus or Lokholar, one to get the cavalry out...

With the removal of marks of honor and the addition of two world PvP zones on a timer and 7 new battlegrounds, there is no reason we can't have a longer battleground. Something that someone can dive into in between raids and feel they are making a difference.

Where are Draenei, Worgen, Goblin and Blood Elf units in the battle for Alterac? With the introduction of elite tower guards, you could implement some new in the old too.

Still waiting for the teleport functionality to be moved on to the tabards instead of a level 60 trinket. It would never need to be updated and you'd never hear the '25 levels of ilvl difference just to try and help our base out' argument again. :)

Isle of Conquest

The two commanders and vehicles in the 80-84 bracket are too powerful and too weak in the max level bracket. This needs to flip around.

Warsong Gulch

The new graveyard changes haven't really gone over well. You have fixed the 'stay at the graveyard with the flag and then cap when it's dropped' issue, but gy camping is now more prominent than before. The same thing could have been achieved by putting the graveyards on ledges impossible to jump up onto - thus making graveyard camping a strategic move while also preventing flag carriers from standing where everyone resses.

World PvP

We need some quests implemented with a generous honor reward to help promote activity in the world again:

- For the Alliance/Horde: Slay ____ (faction leader or world boss, such as Jaina or Rexxar): weekly quest, boss selected is random
- Slay x players in (enemy & home city chosen at random - both would need to be the same city, so that offending and defending players collide)
- Plant X explosives in town Y/extinguish X explosives in town Y (those darn gnomes/goblins!)

I'd like to see a 100v100 AV style BG (but with more than 1 choke point). Blizz may have a few lag issues with this one though.

Or how about a city based (hostile 3rd party) 1st to capture X (#) of flags or 1st to capture throne. Allies start at one side of the city, Horde the other, local reinforcements flow in from 3rd side (think west, east and south). May even be fun to have random start sides (North, West, South & East). Imagine something 4x the side of Org or SW with canals, sewers and buildings to hide/ambush from. Actually smaller may be better for this (area wise) as it would be harder to hold off the reinforcements.

OK another 1. 50v50 (or whatever - drooling on the concept of a 200vs200 + kill fest version) last person standing BGs. Think giant arena. Short game time, would actually require a plan to be used. No bosses and truly random because of the real player only aspect. Should be easy to design a few random locations or even use empty existing dungeons / BGs (with a few exceptions of course). Invisible could be an issue.

Race against time. Set up 2 parallel areas (that the other side can see the progress of their opponents). Horde and Allies never actually fight. Many bosses / flag captures (whatever) and the team that gets to the end (or farthest if you just set a time limit) wins.

Phased start time BG. Now this could be a pain. Say 20vs20, but starts 5vs5 with an additional 5 (every 2 minutes) for both sides until it's 20vs20. Once all 40 are in, whatever side has the most X in 5 minutes wins.

From the "just nuts" column. How about a class only BG. So only warriors or only DKs. Would be interesting to see how 1 spec works against the other with the same inherent limits. Druid & pallies would have a wide range of options and the other classes less so. Just the image of a 20vs20 rogue BG fills me with ... fear (no) ... confusion (closer) ... glee (yep).

WSG is an easy fix. You have a set time from when you capture the flag until it auto-resets. Now more sitting with the flag for 5 minutes (+) as each side tries to take out the opposing flag carrier.

AV. Tabard teleport (as above post). Maybe allow the tabard to port to home camp and the farthest away secure point / graveyard. May actually encourage some D. But you would loose those choke-point battles with almost everyone stuck at one spot. How about getting the bosses (Ivus the Frostlord or Lok'holar the Ice Lord) back into common use. That could be interesting if they were properly buffed.

AB. I don't mind this one.

EotS. No more getting pushed off the side to your death would be nice, and maybe make flag running worth it.

SotA. Sigh. I'm just not sure of this one. Guns with full arc firing, so you can hit the demos right below you. Maybe a nice HP reward for using the cannons, since at 85 they don't do much dps (or so it seams). Maybe a few quests / achievements - killed in 10 (seconds of landing). Blood on the beach (kill 1 of each class / race in 1 game on the beach). I'm just not sure.

IoC. Again, just not sure. More damage from guns. Maybe make a streaming gun (like a machine gun) instead of the fire (and hope they don't move) ones. Everyone (at least when I get in) always goes to the same gate. Are all 3 destroyable? If not, Why? Maybe it's just a size issue for me. Maybe 20 or 80 per side would be more interesting. 20 would force a focus on the gates and everyone to be at the boss at the same time. 80 would allow for some D and some fun mass combat (Wait is anyone doing anything about the gates? Who cares, get this guy off of me!)

Wintergrasp. Scale it down (critters, demo/gun damage and damage needed to take down walls) and lower the starting level to 10 (or even 5). I think this would make a great starting BG (like WSG & AB). Maybe run it every hour only. On this server, nobody (OK, I exaggerate) every seems to que for this. A wider range may help this.

Lastly just introduce a handicap system or limit BGs to a set iLevel range. So you don't put 10k GS (i371+) in with i300s. Would be easy to get a few more "levels" out of the 85 only range. Not sure how the handicap system would work (maybe just H.P. rewards) but that would allow something else for 85 level PVP players to work towards (either to get into i370+ range or to get a zero handicap). Plus you could have several bank bags set-up just for gear ... I feel like doing a few i350s now but wouldn't mind a few i365s in a few hours.

Now, if you could que for multiple ranges if you had the gear with you and you had 1 minute from joining the BG until you had to have it on (and no re-gearing during the BG) or get punted. That would push the adoption of very large bags and a nice bit of randomness.


That's just off the top of my head blizz. I know it's hard to come up with new BG ideas WSG=TP, Basin=BfG, Wintergrasp=Tol Barad but come on. 5 minutes and I've given you a few possible ideas. With 11 thousand (I mean million) players, we should be able to help you out with a little creativity if you need it.
