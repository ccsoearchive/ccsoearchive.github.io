Welcome! IRL, I've been playing WoW for coming up on 7 years now. I spent all of Burning Crusade as part of the Horde, all of Wrath over on Wyrmrest Accord, and I returned to Cenarion Circle, where I first started playing, about a month or so before Cataclysm launched.

IC, Ragefang is the leader of a very small guild (as in, about 3 semi-active people at this point) called the Ebon Sanction; its RP concept is an Alliance-focused branch of the Knights of the Ebon Blade and other allied individuals, but the reality is we were having more success recruiting when we were just a casual guild of friendly players, some of whom RP'd. If you join us in the AllianceOOC channel, it's a near guarantee I'm going to steal your pants/lower body garment at some point; there's also a strong chance I'll stuff a gnome down your pants. If that occurs, a couple of people may dive in after the gnome.

As has been mentioned, the best way to get to know folks is to dive right in. There isn't much walk-up RP on Cenarion Circle, as most of our RP is guild and event-based. There are some tremendous storytellers involved in our realm on both sides of the war. Being one of World of Warcraft's charter servers, our realm also has a fairly large non-RPer population. Our Trade is as off-topic and OOC as anywhere else. No ERP however; our Goldshire is 100% safe. We haven't even seen Moon Guard people showing up there now with the new cross-realm zones. We mostly see people from Sisters of Elune.

There aren't really long lists of people to avoid; we have our occasional kerfuffles, and a couple of people who of late seem actively antagonistic towards the Alliance RP community, but I suppose that's to be expected. For the most part, we have no more or less drama than any other RP realm.

Welcome to the server; pop in and say hi.
