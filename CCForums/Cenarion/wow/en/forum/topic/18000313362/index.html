After much planning, we've decided to make a few changes and officially launch the first Silvermoon Sunday Market this upcoming Sunday. So, here's the skinny. By the way, feel free to make in character advertisements in this thread for your chosen shop, service, or show. I know I will.

When? Sunday, 6/14/2015, from 3:00 PM Server/PST to 7:00 PM Server/PST

Where? The Bazaar district, Silvermoon City

What? Merchandise, entertainment, and plenty of walk-up roleplay. Pick up a souvenir, chat up and meet other roleplayers, or hire a mercenary or two. Plenty of potential plotlines to start at a marketplace.

How? Walk up and talk to somebody. Derp. Most merchants will approach you if you've got an MRP/TRP or see you in RP gear. I know I certainly will. You can't miss us. The vendors will be positioned all over the place in The Bazaar.

Why? Great chance to meet new players and bring RP out of the Garrisons. Plenty of transmog gear, alcohol, and entertainment available. At worst, a chance to mingle. At best, some real cool stuff to show off to your friends.

Attendance to the meeting added a few things to do, but we're always looking for more booths or individuals to set up shop. You name it, we want it. Currently, the following is available.

Malzane's Mognificent Munitions Looking for a shiny new weapon? I sell style and slaughter. You can find me near the bank in The Bazaar. Also, every purchase comes with a free gun. Higher ticket items mean more expensive guns...

Drunken Dragon Raffle Who doesn't love a good raffle? Buy a ticket or two, possibly win something worth far more than your ticket. I don't know the current prize list, but I heard they're planning to add a Garn Nighthowl, and there are plenty of nice battle pets.

Blaxie's Racing Exhibition The Blaxie Racing Association is looking to tear up the town with an explosive show. They've been quiet about their exact intentions...but really, a bunch of goblins packing gunpowder and fast bikes can't go wrong.

Mercs! Various mercenaries have been paid to attend and advertise their services as well as provide security for this event. If you're looking to hire some muscle for an event of your own or swap war stories with a veteran, keep an eye out. My mercs have been authorized to accept any contracts they wish even under my employ, so don't hesitate! And I pay actual gold, for those looking to add to this list.

We're excited for the event, and I hope to see plenty of smiling faces there. Hit me up in game if you have questions or wish to set up a booth. It is free of charge, though I like advance notice so I can help you pick a good spot. If I'm in my CM gear, I'm OOC. Otherwise, keep an eye out for "Uncle" Malzane.
