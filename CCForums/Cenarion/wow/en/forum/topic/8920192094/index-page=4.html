quick question. Do you switch your poisons prior to an encounter? or do you keep the constant throughout the whole BG?

For the most part your lethal poison will stay the same. If you're a fresh 90 or lower, it'll stay as Wound Poison. If you've got some gear and stacked mastery to around 55-60% or higher it'll stay as Deadly Poison.

Your nonlethal poison can change based upon the opposing team you face in arenas. Mind-Numbing if there are casters, and Crippling (or Paralytic) if there are only melee on the other team.

If you use Glyph of Poisons you can probably switch poisons while stealthed if you wanted to, but for the most part they'll stay as they are through the entire BG.

I generally use Deadly/Mind-Numbing for every BG except Strand of the Ancients (I have AV and IoC blocked). I use Deadly/Crippling on Strands because its shiv bonus will help slow demolishers. Paralytic is a decent choice too for Strands.
