Agility > any secondary stat

I cannot imagine why you would want trade a 2-bonus enchant--one that contains your most valuable stat, mind you--for 0.51% hit.

If ever you were to consider an alternative to Enchant Boots - Blurred Speed, the closest thing would be Enchant Boots - Pandaren's Step which contains mastery in lieu of agility.

Any reforging wiggle room you'd gain from using Enchant Boots - Greater Precision would be roughly equivalent to the mastery bonus of Pandaren's Step, and as a net result you'd be robbing yourself of the 15% movement speed after breaking even.

The speed bonus is relatively moot if you're running with Burst of Speed, but it's rage fuel trying to catch people otherwise. Paladins running Pursuit of Justice come to mind.
