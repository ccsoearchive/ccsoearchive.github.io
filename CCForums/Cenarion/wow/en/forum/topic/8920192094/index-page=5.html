09/02/2013 06:52 AMPosted by Onikagi
What I don't quite understand is, I see a lot of rogues on here using both garrote and rupture in there openers and I'm wondering why?

Comes down to math.

Garrote ticks every 3 seconds as opposed to every 2 with Rupture. Garrote lasts 18 seconds, providing 6 chances (18/3) to gain 10 energy.

Rupture lasts 8, 12, 16, 20, and 24 seconds at 1, 2, 3, 4, and 5 combo points respectively. Opportunities to gain 10 energy with Rupture are: 4, 6, 8, 10, and 12 as your combo points increase. Two combo points is a break-even with Garrote, and using one or the other becomes a toss-up.

While Garrote may provide the same chances to proc Venomous Wounds as a 2-CP Rupture, it does so over a longer period. It becomes personal preference as to whether you'd like to have a slower or faster VW proc rate. The drawback to using Rupture at 2 CPs and below is the increased risk of not regaining 25 energy through Relentless Strikes for using a finisher.

I personally do not enjoy gambling with my energy. I will not use Rupture at 3 or less CPs unless I know the target is going to die soon and I'm just trying to harvest energy.

MfD is nice for burst, but not as nice as it was for subtlety. It's also good for an instant, full cheap shot
.

That depends on your opener. A huge % of assassination damage comes from poisons and so opening with a cheap shot becomes viable. 'Here, stand still while I inject lethal poisons and make you bleed, sir'. If you open with CS and use the pts on SnD you can instantly use MFD for a full Rupture while they are stunned and move to Mutilate, Mutilate, Envenom at that point. A quick Kidney shot at this point and you can either continue the attack or vanish into another cheap shot while they wither away.

Point is the original MFD/Rupture is refreshed with the later Envenom and by this time the damage from that is significant. Subtlety is the way to go for rated BGs and teams but any rogue just starting out in random BGs does well with assassination.

In fact: A brand new assassination rogue with ilvl 463 blue PvE gear and not a stitch of PvP Malevolent, Tyr or Grievous gear that will 'tap' for MAYBE 5k per hit with weapons can still do significant poison damage since it's mastery based and not dps based. Reforge all to mastery early on and wean your way out of it as you get gear, to 80% or so.

Oh to reply to why I would use garrote and rupture is because of the dot damage from both at the same time. Yes the silence is nice but having more poisen damage is even better imo

Garrote + Rupture will not give you any additional poison damage. Tooltip (@lvl50):

Each time your Rupture or Garrote deals damage to an enemy that you have poisoned, you have a 75% chance to deal 685 [+ 16% of Attack power] additional Nature damage and to regain 10 Energy. Garrote will not trigger this effect if the enemy is also afflicted by your Rupture.

The poison damage and energy proc are inclusive. If Rupture is the bleed providing you with energy, it is also the only bleed providing you with additional poison damage.

Using both Garrote and Rupture together only confers additional bleed damage, and honestly Garrote's damage is pretty humble.
