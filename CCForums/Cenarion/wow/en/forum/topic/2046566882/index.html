People complain about rogue stealth being worse than druid stealth due to reading info on wowhead and other datamining sites. I'm here to tell you that you read them wrong. Default rogue stealth is three levels better than default druid stealth, and talented druid stealth is equal to rogue stealth.

From where does this confusion come?

Rogue Stealth:
http://www.wowhead.com/spell=1784/stealth
Apply Aura: Stealth
Value: 40

Druid Stealth:
http://www.wowhead.com/spell=5215
Apply Aura: Stealth
Value: 50

People look at those two abilities, see the 40 for rogues and 50 for druids and incorrectly conclude that rogues are worse off. What you missed, however, is the fact that this aura value is just the base amount at the level the skill is first obtained. Each subsequent level adds +5 to this amount.

What that means is that since rogues get stealth at level 5 and druids get stealth at level. 10, a level 10 rogue would get an additional 5 levels of stealth skill over the base of 40, for a total of +25 points. This puts their total stealth skill at 40+25 = 65. That's 15 higher than the druids, or in other words, they have three more levels of stealth than druids. Feral druids can spec in a talent to give them this extra +15 points, meaning rogue stealth is always at least as good as druid stealth if not better.

From Kotori, weeks ago:

Stealth is a lvl 5 ability, Prowl is a level 10 ability.

Each level provides a player with +5 stealth to the base value mentioned.

at level 10, a druid has 50 stealth, untalented.
at level 10, a rogue has 40+25 = 65 stealth, Master of Deception has been rolled into base stealth (MoD = 3 levels of effective stealth, or 15 stealth rating; the equivalent of the feral talent +15 stealth)

Master of Deception talent has been quoted by Ghostcrawler in saying: "Every rogue has the best stealth built in -- they have Master of Deception for free. Druids still have to talent Feral Instinct for it because we don't want every druid (read Balance and Restoration) to have uber stealth. "

Note the disparity of 15...

At level 85, a druid has 50 + (5x75) = 425 stealth, 440 feral talented
At level 85, a rogue has 40 + (5x80) = 440 stealth (same as feral talented)
