We can no longer reclaim, for Azeroth is not a lost thing to be coddled and nursed back to health. It is a living, thriving place of champions, and magic, and wonders beyond belief.

And it is being threatened. Threatened by the machinations of a would-be replacement of the Lich King, and by the thundering warmonger who sits on Orgrimmar's throne, throwing the support of his Horde behind her.

And the Ebon Sanction is come to right this wrong. We call now on the heroes of the Alliance, those who work in shadow and light, those who worship nature and naaru, those who cast spell and wield blade, those who swing mighty hammers and those who twist curses from the Twisting Nether. Join us, and we will take the fight to the places where others cannot... or will not. We will see our world's lush, thriving ferocity maintained, and we will do so for the glory of not only the Alliance, but of life itself. Find me, champions. Speak with me, and join the Sanction.

Home of the casual player, no level or class requirements, all races of the Alliance are welcome.

Even gnomes.
