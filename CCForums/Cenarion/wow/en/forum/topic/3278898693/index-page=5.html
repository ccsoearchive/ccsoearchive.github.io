It seems awfully childish to keep trolling a class that's clearly hurting, but I'm hopeful that's all he's doing.

You can make this argument to other ranged classes, but you realize that:
a. We have pets, we lose a ton of DPS when they can't melee.
b. There are fights that our pets can't even attack due to pathing issues and/or bugs (Magmaw head vuln phase my pet would attack like 50% of the time, AlAkir P3 the pet despawns, Sinestra if you try to move the pet in P2, it tries to run up towards the eggs, then into the lava and despawns, can't attack Alysrazor in air [even if we try to fly], gets stuck at the top on Beth'tilac then spends all of P2 not attacking, runs so far behind mobs that we can't even use our pet's abilities to buff ourself [Roar of Courage] unless we run to a completely unrealistic spot, it runs behind mobs to attack, even if that means running into the lava on something like Rhyolith).
c. We still have a minimum range. You may not realize this, but it's true. For us to help soak a shard on Baleroc, we either have to clear out an extra 10 yards around the shard of all melee, so we can be outside his stupidly large hitbox to shoot, or we have to stand there and melee. Again Magmaw head phase, we had to be insanely far away to shoot. All sorts of movement heavy fights require a mob to be moved, and it will go through our minimum range. Healers want people to stack tightly for something like Bethtilac P2, yet we have to back up and make healing harder for our Holy Pally to get healed.

No offense man, but hunters still have positioning requirements, still have a portion of our DPS as melee (and worse than melee at that because it's a pet), and on top of this -DO NOT DO MORE DAMAGE THAN MELEE CURRENTLY-.

As people have said, hunters are very strong with low gear...this is due to the fact a weapon doesn't even buff some 50% of a survival hunter's damage, we get 4000+ AP as passive buffs, and we have the lowest "hit" (read: hit+expertise) requirement in the game. But this !@#$ doesn't scale. Most classes have hit to cap as something like 2, or even 3x the value of other stats. This isn't even different for us, but we cap in blues, whereas casters continue to climb.

All that being said, I agree it sucks to be a melee - but that doesn't have to do with your damage output. When people don't want another melee, it's not because they think they will fail an enrage timer, it's because it makes other %^-* harder on the raid. They should be compensating melee with utility, mobility, a few ranged attacks (something like Howling Blast, that isn't bad dmg, and can be used while running/approaching a mob...make heroic throw a viable GCD for warriors to use while not on target, maybe get 2 back to back once every 45sec or something, IDK), and maybe more usable defensive CDS (I specifically mean warriors here, DKs are pretty fine in this department).

Right now, I'm not even that unhappy where MM hunters are. We aren't top of the pack, we're middle though, which means I generally am at/near the top of my raids. Giving a flat 10% buff to all melee though is beyond unfair to us. Also, our 2/4pc bonuses have been quite overstated in use (new 2pc is a sidegrade to current 4pc as far as focus generation goes, 4pc is potentially a DPS loss, depending on exact implementation), and Surv/BM are going to be falling even further behind MM next patch because they both scale significantly worse than MM does with added stats from ilvl.
