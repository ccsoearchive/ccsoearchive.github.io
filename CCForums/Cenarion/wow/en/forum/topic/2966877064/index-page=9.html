We think battlegrounds work best when people are able to get into them quickly.

While the matchmaker starts out being stringent it fairly quickly loosens up its search requirements to get people into games within a reasonable amount of time. We could absolutely make it so limiting that it won't place you into a game until X, Y, and Z standards are equal and you could sit in queue for ... maybe ever, but as you still receive rewards for losing, and due to the (intended) quick nature of games, we don't feel that makes sense for how battlegrounds flow.

Getting people in, playing the game, win or lose, and then quickly into the next match is what most players tend to want - and not long queue times or drawn out matches.


Rather than spamming us with battlegrounds maybe we could focus on quality? As it is I frequently get into 3-4 BGs in a row where my team is severely outgunned, and then I will have one match even, and then maybe 1-2 where I outgun them severely as well.

The even matches are the most fun, where overall team strat and coordination is the most important thing.

Too bad I have to sit through all of the terrible losses in order to get to those games. If I'm in a WSG and I see we have 2 undergeared people I just PRAY that they have at least 2 undergeared people as well, because if they don't its damn near impossible to adjust for the massive difference in power levels. Someone in season 9 honor gear (the non-epic variety) is very difficult for someone in T11 epics/blues to kill. If said person is also wearing PvP epics then it goes from "difficult" to "impossible". What makes matters worse is that you become target numero uno, thanks to your low health and resilience level. I mean, why not? If I see someone in 100k hp in gear thats obviously not PvP I'll always target them first, because it takes so little effort to get that HK.

In short, its not fun gearing up in PvP, but its also very not fun playing the BGs these days. Team gear disparity wins most matches. Also spec disparity in many cases, because as I mentioned earlier, if you are in AB and have 1 healer, and they have 4, your team had better outgun them severely or else you aren't going to be getting any kills.

Final Thoughts:

I truly loved BGs in WoW from the get go, my stupid self actually leveled from 17-30 by turning WSG and AB marks in for XP (instead of rep.....this decision haunts me to this day!), and in TBC I still had a blast. To a lesser extent I even had fun in Wrath. Nowadays though I really feel powerless some matches. Knowing that no matter how well I perform, I can't make up for the undergeared players on my team really burns.

Matching iLevels, matching specs, or maybe even giving a hidden "MMR" type rating to players would be a nice step. As it is right now I queue up to get my ghetto conquests each day on my warrior and call it good. Even that much is quite painful these days.

At peak game times queue times are about 2-4min on my BG. I would gladly double that time to increase the quality of my average match.
