I helped run a Draenei only guild on another, unmentionable, server.

We made our base camp in Azure Watch, moving as a group into darkshore, and again into Ashenvale. We had an amazing time ICly raiding into the Barrens and attacking Orc camps, villages and towns there as retribution for the war on Dreanor.

My character personally lead the IC raid that burned the Cross Roads with our Night Elf allies and slaughtering their militia and civilians (we even ICly captured a few players in the area and took them back to our camp as prisoners). It was a fairly significant server event at the time as it sent a clear signal to the Horde guilds, especially the two Orc guilds, that the Draenei meant business.

From there, we entered into some negotiations, and a ceasefire, which ended when one of the Orc guilds attempted to destroy what was left of the Exodar. Our guild went into full offensive mode and with the help of our human and dwarf friends arriving from Theramore and Ratchet we burned everything Orc related right up to the gates of Orgrimmar over a four week period.

Finally, they decided to submit to our demands.

That was an amazing RP experience, and if we got a group of Draenei and N.Elves together to attempt something like that, I would certainly be interesting in it!
