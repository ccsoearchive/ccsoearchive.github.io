Kalen, CC is a great rp server. One thing I love about it is most rp stories try to include as many people as possible. Even someone unguilded and new can get involved.

Fortunately, or unfortunately, things do happen later at night also. I suppose your point of view depends on whether you need to get up and work the next day. The Blue Recluse is our rp hotspot, so if someone wants to just drop in to visit or possibly get involved in stuff, they stop by there. Much of the time, people will be discussing ongoing storylines.

From what I hear, WrA has a lot of walk up rp, which we're short of. That may be a turn off to some people coming here and hoping to find that. It does happen, but not as often as WrA I think.

I think pve we are pretty balanced. PVP might be slightly tilted toward horde. There are some great horde pvpers, but Alliance also has them. Plus, we've got quite a few people working on world rp/pvp things now.

CC is very forgiving for new RPers, and has a fair bit of depth for the more experienced crowd. Level doesn't matter much to anyone. Just a few days ago I seen a level 13 Dwarf Pally and a level 85 Draenei DK in an RP fight/argument.

The players here don't seem to have an elitest complex, so if you want to play pally who uses a sword vs the iconic hammer, you wont get called for it like on other servers.

The guilds get a long well, and there seems to be enough flavor to go around. Good, evil, and a everything in between. My own AAMS is probably the most unique concept I have ever seen on any server. We have those that focus on RPing through PvE content, and some that like to mix things up in RP based PvP. The grim maw clan's recent offensive to take Halaa comes to mind, or the all out war in the barrens a few months ago. Back when I was more active it wasn't uncommon for RP raids to form and attack each other's capitals.

As Gentyl pointed out, our walk up style RP isn't that plentiful, but our guild, interguild and event based RP can be amazing. A lot of the threads on here reflect what is happening ingame, such as when the AAMS was contracted with deliverinng 100 exploding sheep as a dowery from a gnome to a forsaken. A little bit silly, but it was an amazing story (and yes, we physically had 100 exploding sheep to deliver ICly).
