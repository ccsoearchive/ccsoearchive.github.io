Hello everyone!

I'm from Cenarion Circle and I'm trying to reach out to all of the servers that are connected to us through CRZ. I think CRZ presents a wonderful opportunity for roleplayers, and especially those of us on smaller servers. I know Shadow Council has a mostly underground RP scene, and I think CRZ is a wonderful opportunity for RPers to find RP while maintaining their communities on this server.

I made a large post on the official US Roleplay, forum, World's End Tavern, and got little response, so I'm switching focus. I think to start I should get a list of events in CRZ zones going, and expand from there. I have ambitions to have a grand list of CRZ RP hotspots, areas people use for personal/guild RP and lists/descriptions of
characters who are open for CRZ RP.

You can read more about all that at my WET post http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/16858195691

What I want from this thread is to gather a list of CRZ friendly events from every server and gather them in one place. I made another post in the WET forum, http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/16953835379#1

If those who run events in CRZ accessible zones are interested in bringing in people from other server, post a response here with a short description just like you would for a server event list, with the following format

Event name
Time and location
Host (if applicable, can be guild or individual)
Brief description

I also want to try and have a list of upcoming, one off events if possible.

Hopefully I can do more in the future, but I think this is a good start. Contributions are greatly appreciated!

The main one I know about in a regularly CRZ'd zone will be in Silvermoon City. Alliance side, Braghaman is restarting the Shrouded Dawn patrols of Duskwood on Monday nights.

The others are currently being held on Draenor or in Stormwind, neither of which are currently CRZ'd.

Horde:
Fancy Cakes
Place: Silvermoon City, Royal Exchange
Time: 6pm Mountain time on Sundays
Hosts: Braedyn and Xuihteena
Desc: drinks, pastries, and conversation on the patio outside the archaeology trainers.

Alliance:
Shrouded Dawn Patrol
Place: Duskwood, starting in Darkshire
Time: 7pm Mountain time on Mondays
Host: The Shrouded Dawn (current GM is Braghaman)
Desc: Ridden patrol of Duskwood, making sure the citizens remain safe. Anyone who wishes to attend to learn about the Shrouded Dawn, or learn about Duskwood--or think it's an opportune time to cause trouble--are welcome.

Non-CRZ:
Inkwell
Place: Varies around Draenor
Time: 7pm Mountain time on Tuesdays
Host: Darlain (or the player's various alts)
Desc: light dinner, drinks, and conversation. Used to primarily take place in a library in Stormwind, but since going through the Portal, Darlain finds places around Draenor to meet up.

Dancing Dragon
Place: Stormwind, Mage Quarter
Time: 7pm Mountain time on Fridays
Host Bao Dai (a Darlain alt, and various other alts)
Desc: Tea service by a pandaren master, around the little well outside the Slaughtered Lamb.
