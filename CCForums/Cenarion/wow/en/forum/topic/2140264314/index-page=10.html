I rarely ever check my DPS during a fight. Threat, yes, but not dps.


This is, honestly, the biggest reason I would recommend Skada over Recount + Omen.

Set it up to show threat during fights, and then check your damage after the fights.

Recount is a much more detailed logger, which makes it optimal for a raid-leading position. As for running a meter, just so you can check how your performance was on a fight, Skada is a better fit, imo. It doesn't offer the same robust features and in-depth logging Recount, but the reality is that 3 out of 4 people using Recount just want a damage meter, and not an on the spot overview of a parse.

Recount is a powerhouse of an add-on during fights, and it uses enough memory and processing power to reflect that (And yes, it can single-handedly gimp your FPS if you aren't on a decent machine). Skada is the meter for any casual gamer that simply wants a ballpark idea of what their DPS is for a fight.
