With my ever-so-compact stacked unitframes~

http://i.imgur.com/XDQob.jpg

Important buffs appear above the EH window (AR, for example), less important buffs/debuffs appear to the sides. Castbars overlay onto the power bars, and the upper bars of each chat/meter window serve a purpose in combat (left/chatpane = Starcatcher Compass internal cooldown bar, right/Skadapane = Lust/Timewarp duration)

The lower bars of the UI, underneath the actionbars, are set up to track the stacking agility buff from the daggers in the center, as well as to track the legendary proc itself once available. The bars adjacent to it bubble orangeish-yellow when blade flurry is activated, for aesthetic purposes and to make it very easy to tell when it's enabled or disabled due to the animation.

Example of the BF and dagger bars being active, as well as Starcatcher's ICD bar: http://i.imgur.com/2YXnN.jpg
