In combat:
http://img98.imageshack.us/i/wowscrnshot022411130445.jpg/

I gave an in combat example with absolutely everything up that could be up, usually it displays less at any given time. My HUD from left to right displays health, recuperate duration, and slice and dice duration (all on the left side of the hud) and envenom buff duration, rupture duration, vendetta duration, and energy (all on the right side of the hud). The health bar has black tick marks at 25%, 50% and 75% health. The energy bar has black tick marks at 25 energy (SnD/rup), 35 energy (envenom/FoK), and 55 energy (for mutilate). This lets me prepare for using abilities and knowing exactly when I can use them.

I also have two hidden bars on the right that only appear on mouseover. They don't hold any abilities used in combat, only things like picklock and professions.

Holding down Ctrl to show hotkeys:
http://img821.imageshack.us/i/wowscrnshot022511204222.jpg/

In combat:
http://img812.imageshack.us/i/wowscrnshot022511204254.jpg/
Meters hidden, bars on the right on mouseover.

First White Hud bar on left is SnD.
Second Red bar on left is Deep Insight (30% Bandit's Guile)
First red bar on right is Rupture.

There is also bars for Recuperate, Expose armor, Hemorrhage, but I didn't have them. (obviously).

Killing Spree and Adrenaline Rush pop up and spin when off cooldown, Rupture and SnD also spin when not up (and are slightly more transparent).

Blade Flurry icon on the right there is static and disappears when I toggle it off.

5 Green orbs are stacks of DP
4 White orbs are combo points.

There is also a power aura at the top of the HuD that shows a crescent when my Deadly Scheme procs ( will probably remove it as it is more annoying than helpful )

Yes all my abilities are hotkeyed, some do not have the hotkey shown.
Yes my monitor isn't widescreen, sue me.
