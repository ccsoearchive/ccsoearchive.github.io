Confirmed. Tying back to an earlier description of how/why:

08/18/2015 10:31 PMPosted by Glaxigrav
Some locations, like your Garrison, exist on an Instance (the same realms that run Raids, Dungeons, and Battlegrounds that don't necessarily exist on the same realm as a World).

So, if the Instance Realm that you're on is good but the World Server you're on is experiencing issues, when you try to leave the transfer between the two will fail and keep you in place to prevent you from getting disconnected and then stuck on that bad zone.


As a heads up, I'm nearing the point where I have to leave the office. Don't fret - those who are actually working on the issue are still working on the issue and will continue working until things are fixed.

They do use our forums and social media to check how progress is going in concert with their metrics, so if you notice an improvement please do post it here to let them know.
