OK, as a rogue im looking for juicy quick cast Macros. Most of you probably know these, so no negative comments. I'm here to help the rogues out, so listen, pay attention, and learn, My shadow-lurking friends.

Death from above or "Drop":
/dismount
/cast stealth
This ones good for dropping down unto unsuspecting foes and enemies.

Escape Artist "Master Vanish":
/cast sprint
/cast cloak of shadows
/cast vanish
Saved my ass more times than i can count. You wanna survive? this is the macro for you!

Shadow Sap "TrapSap"
/targetenemy
/cast sap
Works miracles on stealth targets or camouflaged targets. tested and highly recommended (spam it and you'll eventually land your target)

Tango Down "Signal Sap"
/cast sap
/p Target Sapped
Confirm your sap to your party and or raid members. Communication is key!

Desperate Escape "Camo"
/cast smokebomb
/cast Shadowmeld*
/cast stealth
Sorry folks, this ones night elf only. Effective a good percentage of the time.

The Noob Dropper "Night Blade"
/cast ambush
/cast Vanish
/y ZOMGWTFPWNED
a fun little macro for pvp servers to down unsuspecting Lowbies. Feel like being a ###!? enjoy this quick instant WTFPWNED macro! :)

Rogue Tank "Defensive Discipline"
/cast evasion
/cast combat readiness
/cast cloak of shadows
If you get any damage for 5 secs you can Nerd Rage. This Macro will make you god. For about 5 sec* (some restrictions may apply time will vary)

Contract Killer "Locator"
/target *Enemy player's name*
Got an anoying SOB running around killing you while your skinning? Stalk the jerk with this fun macro. with this you can locate and stalk your target, and pick him off while hes "Jerking" off. :P

Move out "Ninja"
/cast Shadowstep
/cast premeditation
A great quick macro for sub rogues. If you Specced for blackjack, be sure to add a sap in there for good measure.

Tricks of the Blade "Poison Macro"
/use Wound Poison *or any type of poison*
/use [btn:2] 17; 16
I'm in love with this macro. if your like me you forget poisons sometimes. Of course since i discovered this gem Ive never forgotten. If you left click the macro, the selected poison will be applied directly to your main hand weapon. NO CLICK DRAGGING! if you right click it the poison will be applied to your offhand. isn't that just great? :P Ive made one for every type of poison, and recommend you do this as well.

Chip n Dip "Throwing poison"
/use Crippling poison *or any type of poison*
/use 18
Dip your Throwing weapon into some nice hot poison without the hassle of click dragging with this fun little macro. Same as Tricks of the Blade, but for your throwing weapon :P

Thanks a lot for your time guys, enjoy these wonderful, tactical, and deadly rogue macros. Feel free to add your own macros to the list. United we stand. Lets Show the World of Warcraft how to fear rogues again :P

I hate to be 'that guy', but there's a few macros here I feel deserve a bit of attention.

Death from above or "Drop":
/dismount
/cast stealth


Just hitting Stealth will dismount you anyways, so you could save your macro slot.



Escape Artist "Master Vanish":
/cast sprint
/cast cloak of shadows
/cast vanish
Saved my ass more times than i can count. You wanna survive? this is the macro for you!


Be sure to have all three of those abilities bound separately and stray from using this. While it's okay for a "panic" situation, locking yourself into blowing three incredibly useful cooldowns every time you want one of them is a huge waste. It may save you in the immediate situation, but if you don't let some of those cooldowns reset before opening again you may find yourself in a bad situation.



The Noob Dropper "Night Blade"
/cast ambush
/cast stealth
/y ZOMGWTFPWNED
a fun little macro for pvp servers to down unsuspecting Lowbies. Feel like being a ###!? enjoy this quick instant WTFPWNED macro! :)


Think that should be a /cast Vanish; you won't leave combat immediately!

Rogue Tank "Defensive Discipline"
/cast evasion
/cast combat readiness
/cast cloak of shadows
If you get any damage for 5 secs you can Nerd Rage. This Macro will make you god. For about 5 sec* (some restrictions may apply time will vary)


While it will make you invincible, using evasion and combat readiness at the same time is a little wasteful. If you are good at keeping your enemies to your front, you may expire your own combat readiness! Just like the other cooldown intensive one, this macro could be more effectively used if you space everything out individually. Once CR hits 5 stacks, it's going to take a lot of damage to take you out; the evasion could be saved for later for better use.

The rest can be useful tools or just for some fun times, just thought I'd point out the ones that can lead to bad habits.

And to add a few that I use:

#showtooltip Shadow Dance
/cast Shadow Dance
/swapactionbar 1 2

Gets 'rid' of the shadow dance bar. I don't know if it still breaks when you vanish, but this cleared up the problem. Just remember to switch back to your primary bar (hit the macro again) at the end of shadow dance.

#showtooltip Cannibalize
/cast [nostealth] !Cannibalize;
/cast [nocombat] !Stealth;
/cancelaura [modifier:shift, stealth] Stealth
/cast [stealth] !Cannibalize
/cast [modifier:shift] !Vanish

Cannibalize in stealth. Only for undeads; if you're in combat, or don't want to leave stealth to cannibalize, hold shift for a vanish cannibalize.

#showtooltip Cloak of Shadows
/cancelaura Hand of Protection
/cast Cloak of Shadows

Sometimes you need to use cloak of shadows while Hand of Protection is on you. This will cancel the aura and cast cloak. Useful on a few other abilities as well.

#showtooltip
/target [target=player]
/use Dense Embersilk Bandage
/targetlasttarget

Target yourself to bandage without losing your primary target.

#showtooltip
/cast [modifier:alt, nostealth] Rupture; [modifier:ctrl, nostealth] Expose Armor; [nomodifier:shift, nostealth] Recuperate; [nomod:alt, stealth] Cheap Shot; [nomod:shift, stealth] Recuperate; Slice and Dice
#showtooltip
/cast [modifier:alt, nostealth] Rupture; [modifier:ctrl, nostealth] Expose Armor; [nomodifier:shift, nostealth] Recuperate; [nomod:shift, stealth] Cheap Shot; [nomod:alt, stealth] Slice and Dice; Recuperate

Keep my finishers cleaned up. I keep this macro on '3' for easy access to all modifiers. I can use all the modifiers to cast different finisher abilities. If I'm in stealth, it defaults to cheap shot; holding alt will allow me to Recuperate, and holding alt+shift allows me to slice and dice. I posted two of them because of PvP/PvE. I prefer recuperate on alt in stealth for PvP, and slice and dice on alt for PvE.
