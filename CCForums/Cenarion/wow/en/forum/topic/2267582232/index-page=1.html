(Edit: oh the pains of switching from needing DPS to healing. Let's see... what can I change...)
Hey, interested in raiding? Sure ya are. And I bet you like big groups, too. Because you're one of those touchy-feely empathy types who likes to kiss booboos and give hot cocoa. Or maybe you're a tough-love gruff veteran with a scowl and a fairly sterile soldering iron.

You know what? Either way, we're just happy to meet you. Well, no, we'd also like to be healed by you. That's really what we're after. First meet, then heal. Then beer.

For a freakin' long time, Outlandish Fortune has been wandering into dungeons and carving our names into anything we find. We keep a reasonable schedule, namely Tuesday, Thursday, and Monday from 5-9 server, and while we want people who know how to do well in their class, we also want people who like being witty and debonair. Or maybe sexy sometimes. Or just plain weird in a not-too-creepy way. Ok, maybe creepy too. We have a pretty wide set of tastes, to tell the truth.

If you do too, and if you like the idea of 25-person raiding with a fun group that loves to push progression but doesn't get really full of itself, then you owe it to yourself to come and check us out. We've cleared three heroics as of this updated post, and we are still hoping to clear more but we're up against summer. And yes, as soon as Firelands is out we'll be wiggling our private parts at the biggest hottest fire-covered spider-giant thing we can find, because... well, don't ask why. It's complicated.

Come join us! It's fun. Honest. If you come and don't have fun, I will give you a puppy. A live one. Covered in TWO TYPES of bacon.

Check us out at http://outlandish.guildlaunch.com or contact any guild member if you have a question - most will just act dumb and tell you to contact me, though, so if you dislike middle men, I guess you should do that. I should mention here that in particular we're looking for shaman or paladin healers, but we'll happily consider any healing class; we're also open to DPS applications and give extra points to anybody who references 80's television shows in their application.
