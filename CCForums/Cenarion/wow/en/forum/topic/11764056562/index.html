Now more then ever is the time to let our server hospitality shine. With our server connection in just under two weeks we're bound to have some major shakeups in how we do things as two worlds become one. What I am looking for are dedicated guilds willing to put aside a guild rank in order to host members of Sisters of Elune who may wish to come over a little early to get to know the people they will be sharing this world with. What does it entail you ask? Simply that, if you do not have a rank already then simply creating a rank that they can join and be willing to show them around what makes CC great, beyond all that it's a chance to make new friends and learn some about them in return.

Long story short! Are you willing to accept members who are coming to get to know the server and may or may not stick around after the merger? Are you willing to show them around and help them find the things that make us great? If so drop a post either here or to me in game with the following.

Edit: Please stick to just the following information. This is not a thread for guild recruitment.

Guild name, Guild level, Point of contact, and the guild focus AKA are you a PVP guild, a PVE guild, or a PVE guild?
