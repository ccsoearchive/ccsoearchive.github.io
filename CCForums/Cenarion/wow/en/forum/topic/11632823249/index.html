Saints of Exodar is a level 25 guild located on the PVE server Bladefist (now CST time). We are very active, friendly, helpful & welcome all new people. We are a stable, laid back, semi-hardcore raiding guild that dabbles in PVE & PVP. SoE has been under same leadership for the last couple year with many of the Officers dating back to then as well. We do not tolerate drama within the guild.

Saints of Exodar MoP raid progress:

T14 - 16/16
T15 - 12/12
T16 - 13/14 normal and 14/14 FLEX

READ THE BELOW CAREFULLY. IT HAS ALL INFO.

Please read schedule carefully to make sure that you can make raids.

Raid Days for 25 man core:

Fridays-9:00 pm CST
Sundays-7:00 pm CST

Raids are 3 hrs in length. Please plan on staying 3 hrs so you don't disrupt the other 24 ppl on raid team with leaving early, etc.

Current Needs:

1-2 TANKS
3-4 DPS

We are looking for RELIABLE, exceptional raiders that are item level 550+ and have their legendary back or are working on their legendary back. If you can pick up on mechanics quickly, show up on time for scheduled raids, take constructive criticism and have a team player mentality then we are interested in you :). No elist dicks, loot wh*res or drama causing jerks please. I got a low bullsh!t tolerance level.

**WE CURRENTLY ARE LOOKING FOR ALL CLASSES/SPECS FOR CORE TEAM FILL IN/10 MAN RUNS/FLEXES AND LOOKING FORWARD TO WoD**

Loot Distribution: Loot council. No loot !@#$%s. We want to benefit team overall and make us stronger so we can slay online dragons together in a timely manner :)

What we expect from you:

- Be 10 minutes early to group up.

- Have exceptional raid attendance. Real life does come first and stuff comes up just give advance notice. Raid times and days are pretty clear cut. Why join a raid team if you're consistently going to miss raids?

- Be properly gemmed, enchanted and reforged for your class spec in PVE gear. (Still sad I have to mention this but I do)

- Be prepared, grab your flasks, etc (We will provide in our raiders tab IF people contribute to the farming of mats for flasks, potions & food)

- MATURITY & RESPECT! No whining or finger pointing over mistakes. Sh!t happens and if it is consistently happening the proper measures will be taken to address it.

- Be able to take constructive criticism.

- Be able to listen and not interrupt raid leader while they explain fights or distribute loot. Have a question? Type in raid chat and it will be addressed. We don't want talking over others.

- Research fights, doesn't take long to watch a video or read up on it.

- Know your spec to maximize your performance.

- Have vent along with a working headset and mic - communication key to successful raiding.

- Have patience. Progression takes time and wipes. No elists or ragers please. If you're looking for a "carry" then move along. We want loyal, dedicated raiders to be a part of our guild family.

- Have fun while killing bosses :)

Outside raid nights you can often find us doing something whether it be Ordos/Celestials, Flex raids, transmog/mount/achievement runs, challenge dungeons, scenarios, arenas, RBG's, etc.

RBG Team:

We are setting up a consistent RBG team that will run twice a week. So far we run Saturday & Monday evenings but this may change. Need a 3rd heals and some RDPS.

We welcome all players, even if you aren't interested in being on a raid team, we can provide a place for you to hang out and have friendly people to play with. We always have people just chatting on vent. Just remember we do not tolerate drama. It is a game, we all have enough stress in real life, let's have guild a place to kick back in and have fun.
