I have a question. What totem would Kaldorei druids go by if they specialized in the feral combat of the wild feline? I tried looking up a totem for my character but could not find one for this. I saw that fang was for snakes, talon was for birds and claw was for bears. Antler was for Malorne. Druids of the wild were balanced all around, I think. I could not find much on the grove druids, but I don't think they are for felines. Is there another totem I could not find, or would my druid be lumped in sort of with the claw totem?

Edit ~ I know that most druids are well balanced with healing, plants, animals and even weather sometimes, but what I mean is that if my character had to engage in combat she would definitely take on the form of a wild cat. I know there is a spirit of the feral feline in Moonglade, just not sure what my totem is.


A druid who takes on a feline form is either a Druid of the Wild (one who takes many forms) or a Druid of the Claw (one who just takes bear/cat forms).

Druids of the Claw have now been confirmed to be able to take bear or feline form due to the Insane Druid questline in Ashenvale.
