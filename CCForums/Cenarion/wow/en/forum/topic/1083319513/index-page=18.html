I've a question that I didn't see already. Direct me to it if I missed it, please.

While Night Elves have a natural resistance to nature, poisons, etc (see their racials), do they have any specific tolerance of other climates?

See what Ilthilior is wearing now? The pants, single shoulderpad, gloves and boots? That's his RP set. Let's say I want to go into...Dun Morogh. It's freaking cold there. Would Ilthil have to bundle up a lot, or just put on some simple clothing so he would'nt freeze over?


It isn't particularly mentioned, however Night Elves do inhabit places like Winterspring. I'd honestly say they'd have a cold resistance greater than humans but lower than dwarves. So I'd say medium clothing would be fine... but thin linen clothes or going shirtless would probably not be the best idea.

Also remember that cold resistance isn't entirely physical. Depending on your character's willpower they may be able to resist the cold.
