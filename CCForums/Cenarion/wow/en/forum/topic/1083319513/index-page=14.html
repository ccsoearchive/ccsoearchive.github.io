I think it's worth mentioning that there are a few exceptions to the rule: Not every Night Elf is going to pray to Elune. There are a few Night Elves (mostly male) who have joined the Argent Crusade and have presumably adopted some beliefs of the cult of the Holy Light. There may also be some atheist Night Elves who don't believe in the gods. As long as you can justify your character's beliefs, you can do a lot more than RP a "cookie-cutter" Night Elf, but it does require a basic understanding of what a typical Kaldorei would believe.
