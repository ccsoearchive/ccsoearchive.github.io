Given Deathwing's physical size, there's a scaling issue that keeps the encounter from working very well in terms of visual impact. For the most part, in traditional instances and raids, tanks beat on boss's feet with their weapons as if this matters. It's a game so it doesn't and for the most part we've suspended disbelief on that.

Deathwing is a little different in that he's so large that if you could manage to work out a way to get him all onto the screen, the players would just be tiny dots and you couldn't see anything. In itself, it's a little ridiculous, even for a fantasy game.

So Blizzard compromised and made it so you fight him a piece at a time. It's a little incoherent and not surprising that it's a bit of a letdown. I think the storyline works OK but the previous cinematics going all the way back to the trailer raised expectations as to what the fight might look like; probably raised them too much. It's a little sad but pretty much a symbol of how the entire expansion pack has gone.

EDIT: "Bigger is badder" is not always better. (TL:DR)
