I started mid-BC and wasn't really into the raiding game until Wrath. I loved Wrath and the fact that the story was full and easy to catch whether you read every single quest or didn't read them at all. Climbing the epic ladder through Icecrown to get your personal whack at the Lich King for being a douche canoe and ruining so many lives was and still is my best memory of this game thus far.

I was sketchy about Cataclysm when I heard of it's development, and so I gave the beta a look over. Hmm... not too bad I must say. After release, there was still a little hindrance on my part while entering BWD and BoT, but after battling my way through, I realized ... hey, this is actually really nice content. It was put together well, and I was actually given the desire to want to go after these tough cookies on Heroic mode! It was an -enjoyable- experience, in my opinion, and the mechanics were pretty awesome. And I don't lie when I say Atramedes stole my heart.

When Firelands was introduced, I feared for my poor little paladin life! It was awesome, it was scarey... and Ragnaros was a pretty good challenge. And here also, the mechanics were fun. Run here, dodge that... now do this! Oh crap... we missed something. /dead. I didn't fall asleep, let's put it that way. :)

Then 4.3 happened.
I do like the idea of the LFR and it's being there for people to get a look at the so called "story" that has been laid out for the seemingly lame "climactic" ending of Deathwing's terrible reign. But as for the normal mode, I'm sorry Blizzard, but I'm not buying it. Sure, the more -awesome- things and endings are reserved for those that delve into Heroic Deathwing, as it should be. However, I must say that the way this patch was put together doesn't make me want to work for anything. It was a huge let down and I personally wouldn't object to just killing this expansion off now while there is still hope for the interest of WoW's dedicated players.

"So you want to see a real ending? Play Heroic mode." - Sure, maybe if this new content made me actually -want- to work for it. But as of this moment, I'm not seeing anything that is pulling my attention in any direction other than just going back to leveling alts and being done with this whole deal.

Hopefully MoP will be as good as it promises to be. Minus the new talent system of course. = /

So to my fellow players/raiders, there may be some hope left for the future. Until then, let's go old school and enjoy some low level noobness fun. :D At least that's more exciting. :3
