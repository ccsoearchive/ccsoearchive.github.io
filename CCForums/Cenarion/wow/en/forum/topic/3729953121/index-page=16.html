Again, you're completely ignoring the story then complaining that the story sucks.

Just to clarify a few things:

1. Not every boss fight is going to be the same or follow a specific formula. That would be bland.

2. The fight with Deathwing wasn't just about players vs deathwing. It was about the fact that Deathwing is such a huge threat and a behemoth of a boss that you couldn't possibly beat him without the aid of Thrall and the other dragon aspects.

Had those characters not been involved, NOT A SINGLE PLAYER WOULD HAVE KILLED DEATHWING.

3. The story telling is good because it takes a completely different approach to downing this boss. In the past, you just storm the keep of the Big Bad Guy, kill all his minions, and then engage in an epic battle vs the boss himself.

There is no 'oh this boss is so difficult that we needed to enlist the aid of other in game characters JUST TO HAVE A CHANCE AT POSSIBLY MAYBE DEFEATING HIM.

So for Deathwing, they decided to create a fight that couldn't possibly be beaten by players themselves. And they did a good job of it. You can sit here and say 'omg it was boring, we just fought a whimpering dragon and he died like a pansy' but you'd be forgetting everything it took to get him to that point:

- You had to help Thrall and the other dragon aspects find a weapon that could take down Deathwing.

- You had to help Thrall and the other dragon aspects create an opening in Deathwing's armor so that the weapon could be used.

- Even after all that, with Thrall and the other dragon aspects channeling ALL THEIR POWER though this weapon, Deathwing STILL didn't die.

- He was greatly injured and almost forced back into the Maelstrom, but he was still strong enough to hold on.

- That's where us players come in again to help finish him off for good.

You guys need to understand that without Thrall, without the dragon aspects, we would have never killed Deathwing to begin with. That's the magnitude of Deathwing. He's been flying around since he Sundered Azeroth, setting entire zones ablaze. Anyone in his wake, level 85 or not, geared out to the hilt or not, DIES IN ONE HIT JUST FROM HIS FLAMES.

I'm sorry, but if you expected to fight Deathwing head on when he is at full strength, you're simply not appreciating just how powerful Deathwing is. Let's say that just out of curiosity, he landed and engaged you and your raid group, while at full health, without the aid of Thrall or anyone else.

He'd crush your entire raid under one foot, then eat you like Skittles, and you'd do absolutely zero damage to him.

You guys need to get a clue.
