1) Does anyone know the content of the next raid?
2) Do we know if one spec will be better than the other?
3) Do we know if we have multiple adds to deal with?
4) Based on current content have any of actually sat and figured what would be the potential DPS gain\lose if these were the current bonus?
5) Everyone that is anti tricks, wtf is your problem? The rogue players have complained and complained about lack of raid utility and here we have a unique ability that allows us to trade with other rogues which gives us a dps boost or to give to another DPS helping the raid progress. Blizz putting a reward for ToT is just rewarding a player that is managing their abilities.

1) Given the lines T11 & 12 have gone along, we can infer a good deal about the general aspects
2) Assassination and Combat have remained fairly equal across 2 raid tiers, Sub has taken a (not surprising in the least) up-shot
3) Given Blizzards design goals, it's an almost certainty on at least a couple encounters if not the majority
4) Aldriana did, and on paper they look pretty nice; see my earlier posts for factors it seems he's not taken into consideration
5) It's a button you have to press every 30sec to boost someone else's DPS by ~2%. That's not particularly fun or engaging to me, and for GCD locked combat often to your own detriment to maintain especially if its flaws aren't hidden under yet another TotT-based set bonus. We also have to spend a glyph on it just so it isn't impacting our DPS under even ideal circumstances. When it was part of our active aggro management, it was an interesting and useful ability. It has been a casualty of the changes to tank threat, and it's time they put it out of its misery. Now that the threat is a non-issue it is entirely not unique it's a lacklustre DI or FM. Unless by unique you mean must be constantly babysat and mircro-managed. That's not the good kind of, "unique."

As for energy capping as combat, yes it does happen during rush but at no other time will you have capping. I find that I will easily get the message not enough energy during the fight and do we even know what stats we will see on the next gear? Maybe it will be haste starved meaning the added energy regen will be much weaker than current BIS.

That's just completely wrong. While there are periods of time when energy will be low there are also (frequent) periods where you will be at or near enough to energy cap that any GCD spent on a non-energy dump is going to cap you. If you never experience that, you're quite frankly doing something wrong.

Even if next tier were, "haste starved," as you postulate this is the third tier of the expansion through reforges alone the stat budget of the next tier is going to push our haste forward. We're already getting to the point where we are spell hit capped just from gear as combat. Next tier we'll need to actively reforge out of Hit, the best place to put that will be Haste. Which under your theory will be an option on every piece (since they'd have to lack haste to create a haste starved environment). Unless of course you also think our gear will be both Haste and Hit starved. In which case we'll be flooded in so much mastery Combat and Sub will be dominated by Assassination doing its happy dance.

Yes, it's still very early and this is just preliminary information that is both incomplete and still open to change. However, after two full raid tiers of this expansion a lot can actually be inferred about design intent and general aspects of what we're like to see in the next raid tier. We're not going into this completely blind either they have been giving small tidbits of information about 4.3 already as well.
