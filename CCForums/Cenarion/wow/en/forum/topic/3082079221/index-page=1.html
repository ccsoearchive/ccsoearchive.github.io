An interrupt/silence shuts down 80+% (depends on caster) of a caster for a short time.
A disarm shuts down 50-70% depends on melee) of a melee for a short time.

Both of those cost resources, and sometimes a GCD.

Standing on the hunter shuts down 60-80% of the hunter, no GCD, no resource, and in the case of melee, that is where they want to be anyways.

Question, do disarms work on Ferals? If not, I doubt interupts/silences do much. But, they want to be in melee, hunters can't use a majority of their spell book in melee range.

That is the crux. Yes, we can Raptor Strike you, but that hasn't really been a threat since S3 (maybe a little later).

Those challenges really aren't THAT difficult to achieve. Not only that, but there is no repeatability to them. Unlike fixing one of the dozens of issues that make playing the class truly a pain in pvp (replayable) they gave us a bunch of pet challenges.

The pet challenges are a good idea, however they put all of their eggs in one basket. Rather than give us 1-2 pet challenges per patch, with a couple coming each x-pac, they gave us all 10 at once.
