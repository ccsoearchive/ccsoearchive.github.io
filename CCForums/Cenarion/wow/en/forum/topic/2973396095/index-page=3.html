I have full respect for the ESRB rating and our Terms of Use. That said, when I'm playing WoW on my own time, I'm generally not interested in helping police things. Unless I witness some really severe conduct violations or exploits, I don't get involved in issues of harassment. My profanity filter is turned off, as I can handle what people are saying. :)

I don't really enjoy filing tickets or reports to GMs while playing. In the end, I'd just be another player adding to their workload. Now, if another player is offended by someone harassing or trolling others, I encourage them to file a report. I've played video games for years though and have pretty thick skin, so I just try to focus on being a skilled, polite player. If I don't get that in return, I tend to shrug it off and move on.


Just because of this, I want to have the honor to play with you even more :D
