The rotation I posted is what spriests universally use. Using SW:D when the boss has more health than 25% is a DPS loss as MF's DPET (Damage Per Execute Time) is higher than SW:D's. The only time you should be weaving SW:D into your rotation is when you're slightly undergeared and running OOM quickly. Archangel and Shadowfiend should be popped once all your DOTs are rolling and you have x5 Archangel stacks, so generally in the beginning of every fight. The only exception for this is if you are undergeared and running OOM, then save these for mana CDs rather than DPS CDs.

For max dps you should be using MB on CD, As long as you won't lose your DP / VT, and it won't cause you to lose your Empowered Shadows buff. Even with no Orbs, you can still gain dps by using MB, as long as your ES buff will last until the next MB CD (and you gain an orb in that time).

Personally, I choose to MB on every CD with a minimum of one orb; that way I don't run the risk of losing my buff.

Long story short: No, don't wait for 3 orbs.

Question:
- Mana is such a problem, how do you manage it?
Answer:
It really depends on one thing for this. How is your gear? This is mainly the problem I'm guessing or you're doing something incredibly wrong. After freshly hitting 85, mana is going to be a huge problem/annoyance for spriests until you get about full heroic 5 man geared. However, after this gear threshold, mana doesn't become a problem whatsoever unless you're multi-dotting like a madman (IE - HM Maloriak).


Mana shouldn't necessarily be an issue, even with low ilvl gear since we have plenty of mana-regening spells (i.e., shadowfiend, archangel, sw:d). Because it buffs the damage of sw:d, I use sw:d on every cd when archangel is on. Not sure this is worthwhile (Haotududis, you might have to correct this behavior if it's counterproductive), but it's great in desperate times, seeing as archangel gives you 5% mana back as long as you wait until you have 5 stacks on evangelism, plus sw:d giving you back mana. Obviously, on fights like chimaeron you don't want to be using spells that can kill you during massacre, but other raid bosses are typically fine to use this in.
In any desperate mana situation, dispersion is availiable.

Also, Sin and Punishment is hot for the decreased cd on shadowfiend, which gives you even more chance for an increase in dps while the fiend is up, and lots of extra mana regen.
