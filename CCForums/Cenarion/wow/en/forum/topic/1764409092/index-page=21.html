I guess I don't understand why people are applying dots before stacks of evangelism. Your priority should be to apply your dots at their maximum potential for dmg output (evangelism/empowered shadows) without wasting time. I understand that fishing for an orb can be a waste of dps, but taking 2 seconds to apply evangelism still gives you a high chance of proc'ing an orb and prevents you from having to reapply your dots. I'll explain my opener:

SW:P -> MF till 5 stacks of evangelism (1 and a half casts). At this point I've found that I proc a shadow orb at least 80% of the time -> MB -> DP -> VT

By using that opener I've got all my stacks and empowered shadows buff in about 5 seconds. I only have to apply VT/DP once and then I can move straight into AA. It doesn't make sense to apply your dots, MF till 5 stacks, MB w/orb proc, THEN reapply your dots. While you're busy reapplying, I'm doing 20k+ in the first 10 seconds of the fight (w/ shadow fiend) every time.

If I don't proc an orb at 1 the 1/2 MF mark, I'll finish my second MF cast and apply DP/VT like normal.

Just posting this as a head up, In my current(pre-raid gear) I find mind spike to be very situationally useful(though ONLY under the duration of Dark Archangel), mirrioring dot dps with flay spam when a boss (or very large healthed add) is at 25% and less, this is due to the 90% crit rate/instant mindblast, the rotation for this is as follows(and assumes mind blast just went on cd in order to maximize mana returns/procs) DA > Mind Spike x3 Mind Blast, SW:D(once), Mindspike x3 Mind Blast SW:D SW:D. This is the end of most heroic boss there is a 4second window remaining that you can use inbetween there(perhaps squeezing out another SW:D with careful timing)to regain Evangelism and set VT up on the target to combo your final instant Mindblast to procing returns again(this is all easily done in the window of ES duration), the gain is slightly more dps than dot spaming and considerably less mana + more returns. However this only works on fights where you won't be required to move very much during this 25% and under burn phase, and assumes you are not under lustism. The main source of this dps is appropriated timing for 3-4 SW:D that you would normally be tempted to double tap in, so you will gain the 40-48K crits from 2 more SW:D boosting your dps quite a bit, if you have marvelous timing skills(or are skilless using an addon to track such timings) however you can significantly increase your damage(still popping DA) by timing a 3 orb(assumes you are lucky) Mind blast in between a flay +re-dot channel of the same theory for SW:D (getting 3-4 in the same duration you would only get 2 normally). However the latter is based on luck of proc with Shadow orbs and luck with critting with a 3 orb Mindblast, while the former is a CONSISTENT dps rate for 12 secs, and lower mana cost. Of course I am a tailor and plot my simultaneous procs of Power Torrent + Lightweave to coincide with this maneuver(though the dps gain is consistent as described) for maximum mana returns(I can go from 1K mana to 91,000 in the 12 second duration, without the use of shadow fiend or dispersion of course) and damage gains, resulting in SLIGHTLY superior to low crit(or less than 35~40% ES effect/mastery) dot rotation gains. However if you are using this same moment to maximize gains with volcanic potion as well WHILE under heroism you will see vastly superior numbers from refreshing your dots as lustism is poped, but this is a perfectly ideal situation.

So I make this post for those in cata heroic gear or under(including ZA) and for those unlucky with their Shadow orb procs =D Mind spike IS part of your rotation, though only on certain bosses or adds.

If you want to see awesome dps in heroics, get an addon to track your DoT's, and Multi-DoT on all of your pulls. 3 is a good number, Put Sw:P and VT on about 3 different mobs, and keep those dots up. Keeping your dots on one or more adds will make your dps go way up. What I do, is I always keep Sw:P on all 3 targets, by using a full mind flay on one of them, then mind flay the next one, then the next one, and just keep rotating between each one. You should refresh VT, if the mobs still have quite a bit of health left.

Also, use Shadowfiend and Archangel off cooldown every opportunity you can. (If you can, time it so you use both when you get any int or sp procs, it stacks)

DoTs are full of win, always keep DoTs up on as many targets as you can, as long as you can.
