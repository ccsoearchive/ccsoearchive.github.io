Everything's been updated accordingly. Main changes are as follow:

--------
Glyph of Mind Flay – 10% extra base MF damage - Changed in 4.06 to deal the increased damage even without SW:P on the target.

--------
(Scale is based off the scale from Simcraft BiS 372 gear, ran on 02/09/12)

Intellect = 1.00 (Everything compared to Int)
Spirit = 0.21
Hit = 0.21
Spellpower = 0.80
Crit = 0.49
Haste = 0.59
Mastery = 0.44

--------
As of Patch 4.06, Mastery has been buffed significantly. Even though there is a 0.05 difference between Crit and Mastery, you want to try and balance the two out as best as you possibly can.

--------
Meta gem – Burning Shadowspirit Diamond; +54 Int and 3% increased Crit damage

--------
Brilliant Inferno Ruby -> 40 PP
Reckless Ember Topaz -> 31.8 PP
Purified Demonseye -> 24.2 PP

---------
Off Hand – [Enchant Off Hand – Superior Intellect] - +40 Int

---------
Casting MB should only be based on two things – Refreshing Empowered Shadows or casting it on CD knowing that Empowered Shadows will not fall off before it is off CD. With 4.06, Mind Blast does significant damage and from a pure DPS view, is a DPS increase casting it on every CD.


---------
Simcraft values (mainly the value of Hit, I can't see as to why it would have dropped about half its value due to 4.06 going Live) are subject to change.

New Shadow Priest hotfix notes out on MMO.

Shadow Power (Shadow priest passive) is being changed from 25% to 15% of Shadow damage.

To further explain this change, the post I found most clear and to the point was this one found here: http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/2065778165#15

The post is wrong that it is mastery, Shadow Power is one of the passives you get for selecting the shadow tree. (Shadow power, shadow orbs, Shadow orb mastery and mind flay are the passives we get for choosing shadow.)

It's basically a completely flat 8% damage nerf and shouldn't affect any scaling factors at all.

There is a close to BiS list out compiled by the members of Shadowpriest.com, though its not perfect yet, its probably the best thing we'll get as of now.

http://www.askmrrobot.com/wow/gear/priestshadow#v0-o50300222-v,EDC,05GH1G080R270SBb010102ob0e2I,B9,AE7H85G12469B0F

Just incase you wanted to check it out. Pointing this out mainly to show that this setup has a few pieces of spirit/haste which helps you with your hit dilemma.

But for your current gear, what I can suggest doing is the following:

    Reforge boots - Mastery to spirit/hit
    Reforge chest - Crit to spirit
    Replace Stump with Theralion's Mirror, I'm pretty sure the Int bonus will be worth it unless you have a hard time noticing misses
    Replace the Flame Pillar Leggings with T11 legs when possible, maintaining your 2p bonus then replace T11 gloves with Flamebloom Gloves and reforge the Mastery into spirit/hit


Other than that, you seem to be fine gear-wise. You have a good amount of crit already, sacrificing some for hit and then replacing your hit trinket with Theralion's Mirror would probably be ideal.
