Well from the stat scale, it shows that Haste and Crit are pretty close, definitely closer than it was in WotLK. Even though they're close, haste is still the superior stat.

To answer your question though, haste can add ticks to your DOTs, though it only adds another tick at certain haste levels, and yes, it does affect channeled spells. And considering how everything we use is a DOT, including MF now even though its still channeled, haste is our superior stat.
