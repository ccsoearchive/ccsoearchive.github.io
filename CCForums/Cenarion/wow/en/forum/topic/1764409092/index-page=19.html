Well latency really does play a huge part in playing a shadow priest because of the fact that we have to channel Mind Flay. While that is an issue, I'll go over your current gear right here real quick, though there are of course upgrades out there in heroic instances over what you have.

Helm: Good
Neck: Reforge Mastery to Haste
Shoulders: Reforge Mastery to Haste instead of Crit to Haste
Back: Reforge Crit to Haste instead of Hit to Haste, never reforge out of Hit/Spirit when you're below cap
Chest: Should have two Brilliant Inferno Rubies, even in the blue socket. Reforge Mastery to Spirit
Bracers: Good, +50 Haste enchant however, its relatively cheap as well
Gloves: +50 Haste as well for the enchant, relatively cheap. Reforge Mastery to Spirit
Belt: Good
Legs: Replace gem with a Brilliant Inferno Ruby
Boots: Replace gem with a Brilliant Inferno Ruby. Reforge Crit to Haste instead of Hit to Haste
Ring 1: Good
Ring 2: Reforge Crit to Spirit
Trinket 1: Reforge Mastery to Haste
Trinket 2: Good
Weapon: Good
Wand: Good

2 Questions

1. Can you explain the reason why Mind flay clipping is so bad?

2. Does Mind Flay's "Shadow word: Pain Refresher" ability allow shadow word pain to benefit from haste/intellect procs?


Mind Flay's DPET (Damage Per Execute Time) is based off getting a full cast off. Of course, if you clip it for no reason, you're losing the tick(s) that you should have gotten and just starting up another MF for no reason. The only time clipping is fine is if you have to move obviously as staying alive is > DPS.

SW:P refreshed by MF updates SW:P all your current buffs, including haste and int/sp buffs.



"Meta gem – Burning Shadowspirit Diamond; +54 Int and 3% increased Crit damage"

There is no design by that name at the JC Design Vendor. There is also no shadowspirit cut with those exact stats. Is it a design that you get in a drop somewhere? Or, perhaps you meant Chaotic Shadowspirit Diamond with +54 Critical Strike and 3% Increased Critical Damage or Bracing Shadowspirit Diamond with +54 Intellect and 2% Reduced Threat.


It's a random world drop, if you can't get your hands on it check the AH. If its still a bit costly on your server and you aren't too serious about min/max'ing, the Chaotic Shadowspirit would be your next bet.

Very good guide.

One point I would add is that it's always worth recasting DP if you get bloodlust/heroism. Simply because DP (due to improved DP) does marginally more damage than MF so you might as well refresh it immediately and take advantage of the 30% haste buff.

Recasting VT on the other hand is a bit harder to judge. Because you don't do any instant damage when recasting VT, you are always wasting some cast time by recasting it early. I'm not sure whether the extra 30% haste is worth the loss of the cast time.
