Your DoTs will only receive a damage boost if they're cast while ES is up. If you cast a DoT while ES is up, and ES falls off shortly after, the DoT you applied doesn't lose damage. How much damage the DoT does is calculated at the time of application and doesnt change until it's refreshed.

You should add in the hit cap for human spriests. The extra spirit bonus really tunes the amount of hit actually needed. Great guide btw. :)


It isn't quite the same for each player because the amount of hit rating you get out of the human spirit isn't a static value, it's a 3% modifier and therefore entirely dependent on the amount of spirit you have on your gear. If you're using a Book of Binding Will instead of a Scepter of Ice (127 spirit vs 127 hit rating) then the remaining amount of hit rating you need is not the same. It isn't very complicated to figure out though. You just have to factor in the human spirit buff when choosing how to tune your hit cap.
