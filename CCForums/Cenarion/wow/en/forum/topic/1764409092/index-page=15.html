1) Should I refresh VT and DP after 5x Dark Evangelism, Empowered Shadows? or other buffs as Bloodlust, Volcanic Potion, DA?

2) Which buffs affect Shadowfiend and how and when is the right time to use it?

3) Should I clip MF if VT, DP or Empowered Shadows is about to fall off?

4) I don't understand 'scaling' at all.


1. The ideal time to refresh your VT and DP would be once you have x5 Evangelism and ES active. However if the RNG on orbs is not to your liking, it doesn't create all that big of a difference. During Lust, you want to recast VT and possibly even DP, though I haven't heard any talk about refreshing DP for a DPS increase since Cata dropped.

2. Shadowfiend scales from how much Int or spellpower you have at the time when you pop it. Since I'm running with Power Torrent (Weapon enchant) and DMC: Volcano (Procs 1600 Int), I wait about 5 seconds in before popping SF since those buffs usually pop at the start, and if I pre-potted a Volcanic Potion, thats another bonus.

3. Don't clip MF for keeping ES up, just wait out the second or so duration of how much left is on your MF and cast MB with an orb (hopefully you have one within 15 seconds after gaining the original ES buff) to gain the buff back. If you can time the clip perfectly, I would say that refreshing VT/DP would be worth clipping MF.

4. Scaling is pretty much the value of each stat. Since Int is obviously our most important stat and we benefit most from it, everything is compared against it. So like on the main post, Int is 1.0 and following that, Haste is 0.5, Crit is 0.42, etc etc.

So for example, lets say you're trying to pick between Gear #1 that has 100 Int and 25 Haste and 50 Crit and Gear #2 that has 100 Int and 60 Haste.

Gear #1 = 100 Int, 25 haste, 50 crit
Gear #2 = 100 Int, 60 haste

Gear #1 = 100 (Int) + 25 (haste) * 0.5 (Scaling number) + 50 (crit) * 0.42 (Scaling number)
This would = 100 + 12.5 + 21 = 133.5 PP (Value of the piece of gear overall)

Gear #2 = 100 (Int) + 60 (haste)
This would = 100 + 30 = 130 PP (Value of piece of gear overall)

So Gear #1 has a value of 133.5 and Gear #2 has a value of 130. Therefore, the one with the higher value is the better upgrade.

Emtuberheal..

Item Enhancements (Reforging, Enchanting, Gemming) -
Helm: Mastery to Haste
Neck: Mastery to Haste
Back: Crit to Haste
Chest: Mastery to Haste
Bracers: Mastery to Haste; Swap the spirit enchant for haste if you can manage the 0.1% hit discrepancy, it would be technically a DPS boost
Wand: Mastery to Haste
Belt: Switch Reckless gem with Brilliant, socket bonus isn't worth it. Reforge Mastery to Haste
Boots: Crit to Haste instead of Hit -> Spirit
Ring 1: Mastery to Haste

Get rid of the Core as well, even though yes it is an epic and has 321 Int, there are many other trinkets available from heroics that are much better.
