As of right now, with all things equal (Gear, knowledge, skill, etc.) we should be close to always topping meters on double/multi DOT fights such as V&T or at least a close second. However, with the DOT nerfs coming in 4.2, I doubt that will be the case anymore.

For a Patchwerk, single target, rarely moving fight, since our single target direct damage spells are getting buffed for compensation, our position won't change and we're looking at around top 5ish maybe, meterwise.

Shadow is a real DPS spec, don't let anyone convince you otherwise. Sure, its a bit more to read up on and whatnot, but in the hands of a learned player, its a competitive spec. Our utilities such as our Hymns and being able to avoid damage with Dispersion are just bonuses.

Even with the nerf, our DOTs are still a big part of our DPS and the nerf does not give for us to disregard them. This is a big mistake I see people assuming since the patch and their DPS suffering from low DOT uptime as a result.

I really don't see how the priority or rotation can change though, as VT and DP are single applied for a timed duration and we just filler with MF and MB on CD.

MB and MF are just doing overall greater damage while our DOTs are a bit weaker. Like for example, on a single target fight my top three spells will most likely be VT, MF, and MB coming in third instead of SW:P or DP as would be expected.

Edit - As the beginning line says, the entire guide is updated for 4.2 with maybe a slight mistake here and there that I overlooked. However, the really important things such as stat weights and rotation/priority are always up to date as that's what I prioritize on keeping current.

Solkeera, you're missing a ton of Haste that you could have. Haste should be your highest secondary stat so I'll do a quick rundown of your current gear. Off a quick look, I'd suggest picking up some pieces with less hit/spirit as I see you're reforging out of it on other pieces.

Helm: Reforge Crit to Haste
Neck: Reforge either stat to Haste
Shoulders: Reforge Crit to Haste
Back: Good
Chest: Good
Bracers: +50 Int enchant would be better. Reforge the Crit to Spirit (See Belt notes)
Weapon: As you said, the enchant needs a change. Even Hurricane will make a pretty good difference and isn't that expensive.
Offhand: Good
Wand: Good
Gloves: Replace with a Brilliant Inferno Ruby
Belt: Switch both gems to Brilliant Inferno Rubies and reforge the Crit on your Bracers to Spirit to make up for hit cap.
Legs: Switch yellow socket to Reckless Ember Topaz.
Boots: Switch gem to Brilliant Ember Topaz. Enchant with either +50 Haste or Lavawalker
Ring 1: Good
Ring 2: Good
Trinket 1: Good
Trinket 2: There are alotta other trinkets that would suit better for Shadow. Refer to the BiS gear list I linked at the Sources part of the guide for more info.

Regarding your rotation/priority list, it seems pretty solid. However, you should only wait on an Orb before MB'ing during your opening rotation. However, it is a straight DPS increase to MB on CD, regardless of having orbs or not but you have to be smart about not letting Empowered Shadows fall off. And you should not need the spirit scroll as you are already above Hit cap right now.
