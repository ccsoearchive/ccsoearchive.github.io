First of all, you need to get to 17% hit (1742 hit rating). If you can't get your spells on your targets it really wreck your DPS. You don't have absolutely to get perfect hit cap (1-2% less is not that bad) if you have to sacrifice to much for it.

Second, all out haste. Yes, as you said, more haste = more dots, and we are 70% dots. For an example, I was doing about 5.5k dps with an ilvl of 332, I reforged all crit /mastery to haste (got from 3% to 7%) = 6.3k dps average.

Gear : If you have the choice between +sprit and + hit for gear, get spirit. 1) You get hit from spirit. 2) Mana regen (out of combat) and 3) You may even use same gear for your healing spec (holy = 12.5% haste). Gems as Haotududis said.

Next patch mastery will be a bit better than crit, so you may save a bit of mastery, but it'll still be way less than haste.

Talents : Some questionnable choices there.
Get : Harnessed shadows 2/2 because you want to always cast MB with 1-3 orbs (and next patch mastery gonna be buffed a bit, so the talent is even better)

Drop fear and silence : Highly situational for PvE. Mainly for pvp.

Get veiled shadows : More shadowfiend = more dps and mana and fade is becoming a must-use tool, especialy on pugs. (you can drop one point on mental agility to get 2/2).

For other questions, check shadowpriest.com.
Note : http://www.shadowpriest.com/viewtopic.php?f=73&t=29791
