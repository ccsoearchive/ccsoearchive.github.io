Ok lets brain storm about game improvements in a way to easily read them. Post your ideas in two ways. First as it would look in a offical patch note and then as a more detailed description about what it changes and why. I'll keep this top post with the patch note form and a link to your post.



Professions
Fishing
-Your fishing line has a higher chance to land in a pool if your fishing skill is higher then the pools level. #1
-You have less chance of depleting a pool if your fishing skill is higher then the pools level. ----When hovering over a fishing pool the pool will show a bar in the tooltip showing how many more fish can be pulled up #1

Items
-Several items in game are now bound to the guild in which the person who made or acquire the item belongs. This items can now be stored in the guild bank, and mail or traded to people of the same guild. Items include but not limited to Cauldron of Battle and Seafood Magnifique Feast. #1
- Trinkets that that have cosmetic use and no stats or special effects on them, are no longer trinkets and can be used without needing to equipping. #1
- Items that generate purely cosmetic "fun" effects on long cooldowns have had their cooldowns collectively reduced to 10 seconds. #7
- Items that generate purely cosmetic "fun" effects will no longer break when the user shapeshifts, casts an appearance-based spell, gains a quest-specific appearance, or other such things that would previously cause the cosmetic buff to be removed. Shapeshifts, spells, etc. are prioritized higher than purely cosmetic "fun" effects, and will suppress the "fun" effect without removing it. #7

Creatures:

High Priestess Tyrande Whisperwind has received a new model.
Malfurion Stormrage will no longer stand by during a Horde raid on Darnassus. He will now aggro with Tyrande Whisperwind, whom he loves dearly.

PvP
Battlegrounds

Alterac Valley:
A large number of changes have been made to Alterac Valley, for an extensive list please see our blog article here.

* The damage and health of all NPC's within Alterac Valley has been increased significantly. Several NPC's have had a few abilities added, to make them more challenging.
* All NPC's are now at or above level cap. The exception to this are the Wing Commanders (which are now levels 84, 85 and 86 respectfully) and graveyard guardians (who start at level 84, are level 85 at seasoned, 86 at veteran and 87 at champion)
* The 'Alterac Blitz' acheivement has been removed.
* The Stormpike and Frostwolf Insignia's awarded from the Proving Grounds and subsequent quests no longer have teleport functionality but have updated stats.
* Frostwolf and Stormpike Tabards, available from the respective quartermasters, now have the 'teleport to base' functionality.
* Reinforcements have been increased to 1000, up from 600.
* Mines now provide 3 reinforcements every 30 seconds, up from 1 every 45 seconds. Teams will receive increased reinforcement gains if they control both mines (7 every 25 seconds).
* Elite guards, representing missing races have been added to towers and bunkers. These guards are immune to crowd control effects and can see through stealth.
* Alterac Valley is no longer available through the random battleground queue.
* Periodic honor gains have been added.
* Quest turn ins have been reduced. Quests that were unable to be completed have been fixed.
* The Frostwolf base has been fortified. No more pesky Alliance wall hopping!
* The amount of time it takes to receive the periodic buffs granted by surviving captains or generals has been reduced.


Captain Balinda Stonehearth

* Fireball and Cone of Cold base damage has been increased to match the values of Frostbolt and Arcane Explosion. The duration of Ice Block has been increased to 6 seconds, up from 4.
* Added a powerful Blizzard ability, that will snare and deal damage to opponents who choose to stand in it.
* Now immune to the gripping effect of Death Grip, all stuns and knockback effects.
* Frostbolt and Fireball can no longer be spell reflected.
* Casting time of all abilities have been reduced (Arcane Explosion is now instant, Frostbolt & Fireball are now 2 second casts and Blizzard is a 1 second cast, non-channelled ability)
* Will now reset if she is taken beyond her wall, rather than taken outside of her fortress. This is to prevent line of sighting her casts.


Captain Galvangar

* Now immune to the gripping effect of Death Grip, all stuns and knockback effects.
* Heroic Leap added, dealing weapon damage and stunning targets caught within it.


Vanndar Stormpike

* Knock Away has been added.
* Two owls have been added to the Hall of the Stormpike, linked with Vanndar and the Stormpike Marshals. They are also affected by the bunker buffs, similar to Duros and Drakan.
* Thunderclap now deals weapon damage as nature damage to prevent scaling issues.


Drek'Thar

* Now fights from the comfort of his wheelchair.
* Has had a chain lightning ability added, dealing increasing nature damage to each successive target (up to a maximum of 3 players).


Achievements

Several achievements have been added to compliment the new Alterac Valley play style. These are not required for the 'Master of Alterac Valley' meta achievement.

The Ultimate Unit (Reward: Mini Ivus/Lokholar non-combat pet
Successfully summon Ivus the Forest Lord/Lokholar the Frost Lord in Alterac Valley.

Lumberjacked/Melted!
Slay Ivus the Forest Lord/Lokholar the Ice Lord.

Grounded
Slay an enemy Wing Commander before they reach their base.

CHARGE!
Summon a cavalry attack.

Death From Above
Win Alterac Valley while you have all wing commanders in the air.

To battle!
Send an infantry assault into battle.

Yes, Captain!
Win Alterac Valley while your captain is still alive.

Not in my base
Slay an enemy player within 30 seconds of using your tabard to teleport to base.

Justice and Valor Points have been removed

Death Knights have been removed as we have just realized how stoned we were when we made them in the first place.

Made honor gain back to every week rather then instantaneous.

PvP gear now has its honor value increased along with tokens re-implemented.

Removed Battle of Gilneas Battleground and Twin Peaks Battleground; we already have something like that and thought it wasn't creative at all.

Changed Talent Trees to old ones that we had in The Burning Crusade.

Made Enhance a Tank Spec again.

Deleted all heirloom items.

Removed Exp from Bg's

Made brackets back to their original formatting (10-19...etc)

Finally listened to PvPers arguments on the Classic AV content and have finally brought back this epic experience.

Brought back 60 naxxaramas and removed the 80 version due to lack of activity and creativity.

Added a baseline silence for hunters.

Added a revamped viper sting for hunters.

Removed all Cataclysm classes (such as tauren pally, troll druid etc...)

Completely revamped Tol Barad and put more thought into it.

It is now like a DotA map where Alliance and Horde must fight to bring down the main base of the openents with help of NPC's. As the fight progresses more and stronger units come into play.

Starting Units for Horde/Alliance

First Tier Units

Grunts/Footmen
- Basic Melee Unit

Troll Spearmen/Dwarven Riflemen
- Basic Ranged Unit

Second Tier units

Tauren Druids/Night Elf Druids
- Druids can heal/damage/tank

Gnomish/Goblin Engineers
- Can make a factory, throw bombs/explosives, and plant tramps.

Undead/Worgen Warlocks/Priests

Priests
- can heal or damage depending on the spec
- able to cast physic scream

Warlocks
- able to cast fear and summon demons.

Third Tier Units

Blood Elf/Draenei Paladin

- Either Retribution or Protection talent spec
- Able to Hammer of Justice other players

Orc Wolfrider/Human Calvary

- Much faster then a grunt with lighter damage.
- Has the charge ability.
- Wolf/Horse has a seperate health pool.

Fourth Tier Units

Wind Rider/Gryphon Rider
- Flying Units
- Players are also able to mount up on Wind Riders/Gryphon Riders if they have no rider mounted on them.

Undead Plague Catapults/Night Elf Ballista

- Heavy damage
- Controlled by NPC's, however players can replace the NPC.
- Effective on enemy towers/buildings

Players Must Advance through the middle or sides in order to destroy the main enemy's base.
The amount of kills a team has will be the influence on when the next tier will occur. As well as looting player's corpses and turning in the Armor Scraps, medallions etc...

There will be 3 towers on each side of progression (Left, Middle, Right) that must be destroyed in order to progress to the main base of the opponent.


Main Base is a Fortress/Keep that will have to be destroyed and then kill the enemy faction leader inside.

Player cap of 500.

No time limit, must be lvl 85, no reinforcements.

The Tol Barad idea was just a quick typeout of my idea of a true PvP battle.
