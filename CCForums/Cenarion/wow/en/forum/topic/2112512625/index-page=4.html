ty aranann for a lifesaving thread.


for those of you have a spare action bar, some guy posted a very clever solution to constant aspect switching. you basically create a duplicate of your main action bar. except one bar has all your hawk shots macroed to hawk, and the other has cobra/steady macroed to fox.
every shot with an aspect macro has a /changeactionbar to the other action bar.

that means that only switch aspects when you need to. when you're in fox and hit chimera shot, it will be bound to AoTH. but then it changes to a bar where all your hawk shots are not macroed to an aspect switch. hit your steady shot on this bar and it changes to a bar where your steady isn't macroed to fox. nifty nifty.



I ended up doing this. works extremely well as long as you memorized where your binds are. thanks for this, saved me a lot of file editing and what not

For those of you that are interested, here's how to do it with actionbar swaps (http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/2522464642):

#showtooltip Arcane Shot
/cast [noactionbar:1] !Aspect of the Hawk
/changeactionbar 1
/cast [actionbar:1] Arcane Shot

#showtooltip Steady Shot
/cast [noactionbar:2] !Aspect of the Fox
/changeactionbar 2
/cast [actionbar:2] Steady Shot
