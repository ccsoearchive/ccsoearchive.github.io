Rated BG rank achievement idea

I am not sure where to post this, upon some consideration this seems to be the best forum.

Let me preface this by saying that I am a member of the military, and I really like the way the PvP ranks in this game are set to mimic military rank structure.

However, I find that the current implementation of the PvP ranks to be a bit cold. What I mean is that you get titles the same way as every other title, you get it when you get an achievement. This is somewhat insipid and sometimes even absurd (for example, I got my Legionnaire title and Veteran of the Horde title fighting as 'alliance' in rated BGs...), given the current structure of rated BGs. It also feels a bit disappointing that Garrosh sent me my wolf and letter congratulating me for my valorous service to the Horde (after my Veteran of the Horde title) in a letter, instead of having some sort of face time.

What I propose, is that there should be an actual promotion ceremony implemented in the game as a phased quest. One possible implementation of this would be if you hit the requirement for a certain rank, you get a quest summoning you to the faction leader's room or the PvP gear room for a promotion. The area would be phased, and upon entering you have a specific NPC giving a speech and then giving you a promotion. What would be nice is if you get an insignia for your rank, say a flavour item that you can equip to denote your rank. And upon completion of this quest, you get your title.

A more specific detail would be that ranks 1-5, ranks 6-11, and ranks 12-14 can be promoted by different NPCs. Ranks 1-5 are 'enlisted' ranks (for example Horde is Scout, Grunt, Sergeant, Senior Sergeant, First Sergeant), so it would be fine to be promoted by some relatively lower ranking military official in the faction (say Rexxar for Horde), and ranks 6-11 are junior/senior officer ranks, so it would be fine to get promoted by a higher ranking officer (say High Overlord Saurfang for Horde), and the final 12-14 ranks are flag officer ranks and should be promoted by the faction leader (Garrosh/Varian respectively).

As well, I believe a similar 'ceremony' should be implemented for players who have achieved the Veteran of the Horde and Warbringer titles respectively, in a similar fashion as ceremonies awarding medals to service members.

A similar implementation could be added for arena players as well, for challengers/rivals/duelists/gladiators.

This would bring flavor to the PvP side, and gives a much better sense of immersion. This makes PvP players feel like they're actually part of the game instead of just using WoW as a platform to bash faces in and calling people scrubs.
