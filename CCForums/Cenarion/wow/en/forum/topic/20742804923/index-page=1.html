Hey all, after having a talk with a couple of folks both in and out of the game, and a discussion on the forums, I've decided to pitch this as an actual 'thing', and I hope people will help me get the ball rolling on.

So, we've all had the frustration of using Blizzard's 'Looking For Group' system which has, unfortunately, created a mentality where you can be an absolute bastard to people outside of your server with little-to-no repercussion because the odds of you running into them again are almost negligible?

So you've either got to run with a tyrant's fist on the Master Loot, which can work well with a fair-minded person, or terribly if you're running with somebody who just wants your additional DPS/Tanking/Heals while they hoover up everything, or you run in the desperate hope that you've assembled a PuG with some small semblance of courtesy and intelligence.

Hence, /OldRaids.

Maybe you just want a hand with Pandaria Raids. Maybe you've just hit level 60 and want somebody to help you run Blackrock Spire for a chance at a sweet axe or piece of transmog. Maybe you're just lonely and want somebody to gab with during your weekly gold-farming without having to weather the /chan/-isms of the LFG channel. A player-made channel where you can pop in, advertise your needs or wants for a raid and negotiate freely with players of our own realm without having to risk the toxicity of the LFG system.

As well, if you're looking to ravage some raids, pop a request into this thread, get yourself a small team of fellow Wyrmies and go forth to fabulous transmogs, raid achievements and possible glorious mounts!
