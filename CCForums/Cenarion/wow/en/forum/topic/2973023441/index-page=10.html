I'm going to come at this from the perspective of someone that really really wants to see this work well and succeed. Because I am. I play on a slower progression server and am in a guild that spends very little time raiding. I have attachments to the people in my guild and to the server and while I've considered changing servers I just couldn't do it in the end.

My guild does Firelands two nights a week sometimes only for an hour. It would be great if those of us still wanting to raid could just que up to fill the one or two slots we need with a fairly competent player so that we could continue a while.

On the other hand I LOVE killing the big guys and have a shaman alt that I would love to get geared up but just can't with the way things are in my situation. Thats my problem but if I could que up and find a decent raid that would be awesome. I have mumble/vent, research the fights ahead of time, can listen to instructions, and have the BEST possible gear I can get my hands on outside a raid so I'm not holding back and think I would be a great asset to any raid.

When using this tool to find people to fill our slots I would like to see people that are geared enough, can jump on our vent and are ready to go. When using this for my alt I would like to be matched with a raid that is as ready to go as I am. Prefferably an organized group that needs a few slots filled mostly because raid groups need decent leadership and I don't get excited about jumping in somewhere with other random people and expecting everything to come together.

Those are just my quick thoughts on what I would like to see when using this tool. How to go about it I don't know, or if what I expect are just unreasonable I don't know but I do want to see it work.
