http://mop.wowhead.com/item=85112/malevolent-gladiators-greatsword

http://mop.wowhead.com/item=87176

So comparing the two (fair comparison; considering right now T2 weapons are compared to heroic raid weapons), we see that there is a difference of 224 strength + socket as well as 818.3 weapon damage, or 19.367% more weapon damage. Let's just conservatively say that the heroic weapon (without accounting for socket) does 20% more damage than the PvP weapon. 2235 PvP power would have to give 20% more PvP damage to make it better than the raid equivalent.

Toss in a +600 strength gem (it's one of the sha-touched weapons; hence eligible for the legendary gem) and you're looking at an even bigger difference.
