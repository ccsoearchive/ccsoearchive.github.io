The Cloak & Dagger Compendium sticky addresses this (as have a number of other recent threads in the forum), but the bottom line is: yes, 8% hit (the "special" cap) is the only "must" for a raiding Combat rogue.

What you may be seeing on other Combat rogues is a natural result of gear inflation: At this point in the expansion, there's so freaking much stats on gear that a rogue with 3/3 Precision can easily end up with 14% spell hit without even really wanting to.

Also keep in mind that spell hit doesn't actually suck for Combat; it's more valuable than crit and mastery, but it's less valuable than haste. This means that if there's already haste on a piece of gear, and they're already expertise capped and special hit capped, a Combat rogue will reforge *into* hit simply because it's better than the other options available.
