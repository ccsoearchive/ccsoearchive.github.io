Fully agree with all that was said, I did PvP a long awhile ago on my hunter and just didn't find it fun like I did with my alts. Granted I never got high rating but noticed the hunter problems early on and said "hell with this" and rather pvp on my alt.

As others have said, we basically lost the few advantages we did have and got nothing in this expac. I mean we were teased with several different types of Venom skills to replace the loss of scorpid and viper stings. What did we end up getting, widow venom, a worthless spell with the MS nerf and its focus/GCD use.

We used to have a further range then all other classes 41 vs 35. Cata hits and we lose our 6yard talent and everyone gets set to 40 yards. We change resource mechanics (from mana to focus) which was a good thing as we were no longer reliant on a certain classes buffs yet they neuter our focus regen so bad and tie 60% of the regen in a spell that can be min ranged and affected by haste effects.

I mean look if you slow a rogue for example, mostly it will just affect their base energy regen slightly. But if you slow a hunter, not only do you slow the base focus regen, you also cripple the spell thats required to cast so we can even use our abilities. 4 focus per second base is too low and having an additional 6 focus per second coming from a casted ability is a terrible setup. If anything I needed to be flipped, 6-8 focus regen per second base and 2-4 focus per second casting steady/cobra so getting LoS'ed or deadzoned doesn't cripple the class.

Not to mention the other bugs that affect our class. Things like abilities costing more focus then given, pets spawning at 5k life and getting 1 shot before they get scaled. Yet blizz decides to "Oh, hunters were having a fire pet, lets fix that". Hell for Wrath, if anyone remembers quite a few of use fought blizzard for a year to get serpent sting to work on physical crit damage value (200% instead of 150%) and posted up logs and data yet nothing was done even after blizz had mentioned that serpent sting should work with physical crit damage. Even after it finally got fixed, it still was busted as the 3% meta caused issues with it and that was not long before 4.0.

Funny part for cata was the whole, we are decreasing the cast time for aimed shot to 2.4 and steady/cobra to 1.5 yet they come back saying there's a "Hidden" 0.5s cast time to our shots.

We basically got nothing this xpac after being teased by the dev's early on in beta. Only thing we have going for us is our damage in PvE but thats starting to fall down the charts as classes gear up and our terrible scaling becomes known.
