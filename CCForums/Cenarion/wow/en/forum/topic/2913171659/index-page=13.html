BG's aren't about how much damage you do. It's about how effective you are helping your team succeed in winning the game. While doing damage is awesome and fun, it's not a dps race to win a BG imo. If you can help your team secure nodes, secure flags and prevent the other team from doing the same, you're doing your job.

Weapons are weapons. Meh, the other gear is just as important and if you hit up the BG that is having the BG Holiday, you can get honor pretty fast. You can also convert Justice Points into Honor if you want to run a few instances.

Overall, you should not be "put-off" but the honor grind. We've all had to do it and if you can forget about how much honor you need and just play, your experience will be a lot better. =)
