Well, the big moment I've been waiting for finally came last night....

The team my Hunter usually does RBG's with has been moving up steadily in the ratings. I've done my bit to get them there. Defended nodes solo like a champ.... Stopped and killed I don't know how many EFC's with my Shale Spider/Chimaera on them.... Pulled out the Corehound for Ancient Hysteria in enemy flagrooms to take down EFC's.... Had great awareness - was where I needed to be when I needed to be there....

So last night I log on at the usual time, ask if RBG's are starting soon, and am told by the leader that, "just for the night" they want to try running two Frost Mages. Ok, no biggie I say and log off.

But the writing's on the wall.

I don't Raid. I don't do Arenas except for Points. But I love running BG's. Always have. Naively, I was excited when Blizzard introduced Rated BG's into the game. Little did I realize that the same insidious neglect Blizzard shows Hunters with regard to Arenas would carry into the Battleground, where most DPS classes have mechanics/cc/utility which are superior to ours.

The developers at Blizzard sit in their little cubicles oblivious to the fact that real people play in their pixelated world. This real person will either play Rated Battlegrounds with a good team or, within the month, sign onto Battle.net to cancel the account. I put a lot of love and work into my Hunter - as much love and work as other classes put into their toons. No where did it say on the Hunter creation screen - "And, by the way, we will entirely neglect your class in PvP once you reach endgame."

As far as I'm concerned, Blizz has sold us lemons. I'm tired of trying to make lemonade with mine.
