How many times do you read in trade "looking for hunter for serious arenas" or "looking for a hunter for rbgs"....Every serious pvper knows hunters arent as good as other classes in pvp.

I spent the last 40 minutes reading every comment on this thread. I even read the serious discussion on http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/2721383344?page=2

Im filled with mixed emotions about this subjects. I love my hunter, but iv never had a harder time trying to do any kind of competitive pvp...Iv researched my specs, added 40 macros and downloaded a bunch of addons. Iv done more research and practiced so many more differant layouts and specs then i have ever had to on my pally or my shaman. Yet even with all my dedication im never taken that seriously by other pvpers.

Sure i can win some duels and do great in regular bgs, but what does that really say about me. Regular bgs arent were the geared pvpers go, its where the players who need to farm honor before they can do serious pvp go. Beating them when I myself have all the gear i can get from honor doesnt feel like an accomplishment nor do i feel it accurately displays my skill level to anyone.

I wanna keep this in context and not turn into my own personal QQ comment but im trying to use my problem to try to give other classes context on the current hunter situation even outside of PvP. Im in a full pvp guild with mostly 2k+ rated players and with several multi glads and 'heros of the alliance". In my entire guild of 300+ pvpers we have 2 hunters over 2k and that is from rated bgs with all 2k+ players.

There simply is no reason to pick up a hunter from a rated bg when we cant out cc a mage/lock/rogue, cant shutdown a player as well as a rogue/warrior/dk/shaman, and cant survive longer then...well...mage/lock/rogue/pally/warrior/dk/priest/shaman/druid.

Hunters have always been great in PvE. In fact I can get into a raid in full pvp gear with no experience easier then a rbg. I can even do competitive dps with a pvp spec and pvp gear. Iv said it before in other threads but this is exactly why we suffer in PvP. Blizzard cant buff us in pvp without buffing us in pve..and lets just face the facts...blizzard cares more about pve. Even though they wont openly say it, they respond to PvE bugs and issues must faster then pvp bugs and issues. They fix raid encounters must quicker then bg or arena problems. I cant blame them...they put alot of time into raid instances and they want players to enjoy them, but i cant help feel that pvp has definetly been thrown in the back seat. They know its there and that it needs fixing...but lets do that after we make Firelands easier for higher end raid guilds.

As long as I play WoW i will be a hunter, and maybe one day it will be something to be more proud of. I dream of a day when there will be more "nerf hunter" threads then there will be "buff hunter" threads, just like every other class has. Its very easy to write a thread about how somebody owned you and you want them to not be able to do that again. Its much harder to write a thread saying that you cant win encounters that you should.

My suggestions, that I think are well thought out (I will explain my reasoning with a third post):

-Deterrence now clears all DoT effects, roots and magical debuffs.

-Trap Launcher now has two options:
A) Trap Launcher: Same tooltip, but has a longer range than TL (P).
B) Trap Launcher (Precision): Launches the trap directly at your target (and works in conjunction with focus). The trap wouldn't be avoidable or eatable.


-Freezing Trap now clears all DoT effects on the target (if it already doesn't). In addition to its current effect, Freezing Trap now also immobilizes the target in place for two seconds if the trap effect is dispelled.The root does not share diminishing returns with Entrapment, but does share it with roots such as Frost Nova.

-Snake Trap no longer applies a DoT effect in any way.

-New minor glyph: [Insert name here]: Disengage now propels you forward.

-Disengage no longer requires combat in an Arena battle or Battleground.

-Steady Shot and Cobra Shot are no longer affected by Frost Fever, Slow, or any other similar debuffs.

-Silencing Shot is now baseline for Hunters.

-Master's Call is now a buff casted by the Hunter (you would do a roar maybe?) that executes its current effect, removing the need for a pet to be active to have this ability work. In addition, Master's Call works through line of sight.

-Kill Shot's damage has been increased by 5%.

-Revive Pet now has a 3 second cast time, and its focus cost has been reduced by 50%.

-Wing Clip has been removed.

-Raptor Strike, in addition to its current effect, reduces an enemy's movement speed by 50%.


MARKSMANSHIP:
-In addition to its current effect, 'Termination' now causes Arcane Shot and Chimera Shot to do 5/10% more damage to targets with 25% or less health.

-New talent (to replace Silencing Shot): [Insert name here]: When you are hit by a melee attack, you have a 40% chance to make your next Steady Shot an instant cast and generate 3 additional focus, and cause your next Aimed Shot cast time to be reduced by 50% (would have to find a way so that the AS effect would not overlap/overwrite the Master Marksman proc).

-Chimera Shot now heals the player for 8% of their health, up from 5%.

-In addition to its current effect, 'Efficiency' now reduces the focus cost by 50%/100% of both Trap Launcher and Trap Launcher (Precision).

-'Posthaste' now reduces the cooldown of Rapid Fire by 30 seconds/1 minute, down from 1 minute/2 minutes.

SURVIVAL:

-In addition to its current effect, 'Hunter vs. Wild' now reduces all damage taken by 3/6/9%.

-In addition to its current effect, 'Improved Serpent Sting' now increases Serpent Sting DoT tick damage by 10%.

-'Counterattack' is now renamed to [Insert more fitting name]. It now immobilizes the target for 2 seconds, down from 5 seconds (root now shares DR with Entrapment) but when the root effect is gone, the target is disarmed for 3 seconds (if the root is completely diminished and doesn't go off, the target is disarmed immediately). Counterattack no longer requires the hunter to parry an attack to be useable. However, Counterattack now has a 45 second cooldown.

-Explosive Shot damage has been increased by x%. Explosive Shot also has no minimum range requirement.

-Black Arrow is no longer dispellable. In addition to it's current effect, Black Arrow heals you for 3% of your maximum health with every tick.

-In addition to its current effect, [Fitting talent for addition] now increases Cobra Shot damage by 15%.

THIS IS A RESPONSE TO MY POST WITH THE ACTUAL CHANGES.

-My Trap Launcher suggestion (baseline) I feel would smooth out Hunter play in general, making Scatter Shot have the potential for more creativity, rather than setting a trap up on another player (almost always a healer).

-Ice Block and Divine Shield both remove all debuffs and DoTs. Both classes that have these abilities are in a better state than Hunters. I don't think I need to expand upon this.

-Freezing Trap should act as a reliable CC.. given the current circumstances to land one on a player via Trap Launcher, it shouldn't be broken with DoTs.

-Snake Trap applies an awful DoT that does nothing useful. If the above change was to be applied, this DoT would still affect other CCs (example: Repentance) and be very annoying. Indirectly buffs Hunter kiting and control.

-QoL change for Master's Call.

-The other two specs are both in a bad spot. One is only viable in one comp (Jungle Cleave), and even then it might be better to play MM. Survival has no use in PVE or PVP. This is a start.

-I just personally feel Kill Shot could use a small buff in damage. That is all.

-The Revive Pet cast time is just disgusting, and the focus cost is pretty harsh too. Killing a Hunter's pet shouldn't be SO devastating, and would help fix the clunkiness of the class overall to apply this change.

-Disengage can't always be measured when you're going backward, especially in the heat of the moment. They could fancy it up to avoid homogenization, and make it a cooler ability. Also, the combat requirement is really a hinderance in a PVP environment. Extremely annoying, and doesn't need to be this way. However, I say in a PVP environment because you don't want people using Disengage to glitch things outside of it, so the combat requirement is good for PVE.

-The cast times are bad enough in PVP, don't need a Mage applying a debuff, or a DK applying it too for that matter, that completely ruins the ability. Also, the ease of application with these debuffs is uncalled for in the first place.

-The name 'Wing Clip' really doesn't make any sense. We're not playing Aeon. Also, baking this into Raptor Strike improves the usefulness of the ability in conjunction with the glyph, frees up GCDs and helps with the clunkiness of Hunter play in PVP.

MARKSMANSHIP:

-The Termination change would certainly be a slight buff in PVE, which isn't really needed for Marksmanship at the moment, however, in a heavy movement fight, Hunters do have a bit of trouble, so buffing instant cast shots would indirectly buff their movement DPS in a very small way, which is a good thing for the class and wouldn't be overpowered. As for PVP, this change would overall just make Hunters more efficient in an execution of a player.

-Would not affect PVE in any way. However it would help PVP quite a bit to make Hunters not so useless in PVP when they are being trained.

-I just feel everyone else is getting their awesome self heals. As a Hunter, you get a extremely bad Spirit Bond heal, Spirit Mend for only BM Hunters, and the 'okay' Chimera heal. This would help the situation out a bit, without being too overpowered.

-Efficiency change would help all Hunters of all specs, and not make the Trap Launcher glyph as desirable, freeing up a glyph spot for some Hunters who grab it.

-If you look at my Glyph of Rapid Fire change, the nerf to Posthaste would be to balance it out, so the ability wouldn't become overpowered. This would improve DPS for all hunter specs in PVE, and give Hunters more burst opportunities in PVP as all specs.

SURVIVAL:

-Hunters who pick up Entrapment for PVP almost 9 times out of ten pick up 2/3 or 3/3 Hunter vs. Wild as a filler point to progress into the tree. This would buff hunters who go into the Survival tree quite a bit.

-Survival needs help in both PVP and PVE, and the Serpent Sting change would be a start, a very small buff, but a buff indeed.

-I feel like the Counterattack change would make Survival have more, well, survival :P. A disarm for Hunters is a godsend and would really help out the spec in addition to the other changes I have suggested. However, giving it a cooldown and reducing the immobilization effect a bit would balance it out so it isn't overpowered.

-Removing the minimum range requirement for Explosive Shot would kind of make sense, help Survival hunters that are being trained be more productive, and the damage buff would help them in both PVE and PVP, where they are really lacking.

-Black Arrow grants a large portion of your Lock and Load procs, and more importantly, your damage. Dispelling it can really kill a Survival hunter's damage, so this needs to be undispellable. Also, BM and MM get self heals, but Survival doesn't, really. Survival is about survival, so this would be a pretty strong heal. Could even go further and increase BA duration by a couple more seconds.

-Again, Survival needs buffs across the board, so this change is warranted for Cobra Shot, without making BM too high on DPS, although it probably wouldn't make them much higher.
