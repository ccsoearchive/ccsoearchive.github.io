The Stonehammer Clan is a Horde rp guild with a welcoming community and helpful players dedicated to the rp craft as well as growing a strong community not only as a guild but with the server as a whole and enriching the playing experience.

Our cause is a simple one that we uphold with honor, respect, and cunning and that is to protect the Barrens. This includes core main storylines weekly as well as various outposts used to attract those of special skills and rp playing styles to those of a like mind weather it be engineering, or political and trade negotiations, or simply spying and acting out of the shadows against those that would seek to harm the Barrens and those living within.

The Stonehammers are a close group of brothers and sisters born of blood and strength. Following with traditional ways of both the orcs and the tauren while calling in those other races within the Horde to add to the culture, story, and family of the Clan for it is the duty of all to protect the Barrens. We use the tight community born out of the conflicts we face to build one another ic and ooc to become stronger characters but also closer friends.

We have almost daily events at various times as well as random rp with one another at the drop of a hat as well as with our allies and friends including the Southfury Watch, the Whitehorn Tribe, and the Wolfmane Tribe as well as others and we are always looking for more members to our cause as well as allies to bond with.

In the ooc side of the guild we have a pvp team doing Battle Grounds as well as arenas and we are looking for more skilled pvpers to join in and due to the nature of the guild in protecting the barrens we do participate in world pvp rping out the outcomes of these battles and training sessions to further the development of both character and player. More about the pvp side of things can be found here: http://thestonehammerclan.enjin.com/forum/m/35623721/viewforum/6918572

We also have a small budding pve side of things, currently looking to form a solid 20 man from scratch so all specs and classes are needed as well as junior raid officers! More information about this may be found on our webpage here: http://thestonehammerclan.enjin.com/forum/m/35623721/viewforum/6766425

Send anyone in the guild a whisper if you have questions, send them to me here and check us out!

Our monthly server wide event known as the Gund'harg is a testimant to our goals of bringing the serer together and building a strong, enriching, rp experience! Details for which can be seen here: http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/20419273216?page=1

So in the words of the Chieftain Kus'nel, "Walk tall friend, for it is together that the Clan will defend this land, the families and caravans within, this symbol of unity between so many different cultures and peoples of the Horde. Such is our honored duty and I expect you all to stand tall and face the enemy with a battle shout loud enough to shake the ground and fight with the honor of our ancestors!"

Guild Webpage: http://thestonehammerclan.enjin.com/home
Guild application: http://thestonehammerclan.enjin.com/recruitment

My Battle tag is GhostDragon#120840 if you have any questions you don't want to put below or just want to get together on events!
