Who enjoys the playstyle of modern AV, rather than the honor/hour it generates? In other words, who would queue for modern AV if there were no honor rewarded at all, if it was just for fun? My guess is very, very few.

In fact, I'd hazard a guess that more people would queue for classic AV with no honor rewarded than would modern AV with no honor rewarded. And therein lies the problem. Blizz has allowed AV to be turned into an honor machine that very few enjoy actually playing, they just put up with it to grind their gear. Is that consistent with Blizzard's philosophy?

Now this can be nothing more than a thought experiment, since I don't foresee Blizzard ever turning off honor to see which BGs people actually want to play. But it is interesting to think about in my opinion.
