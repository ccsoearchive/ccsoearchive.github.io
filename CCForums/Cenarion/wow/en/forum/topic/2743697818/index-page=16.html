While the honor gain from a quick victory may feel good, a lot of players want something more than a quick skirmish. We want to feel like we're part of the story, as though what we're doing really truly matters. A mounted race just doesn't cut it.

One of the suggestions listed in the Compendium of Requested Changes to AV is to remove the diminishing returns from honor kills in Alterac Valley. You can farm your precious little numbers in any of seven other battlegrounds.
