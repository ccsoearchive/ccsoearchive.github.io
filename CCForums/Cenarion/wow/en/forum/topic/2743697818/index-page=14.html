I remember the good ol' AV. Back in the Vanilla times one could enter AV, battle for hours, go sleep, come back the next day to the EXACT SAME BG still raging. It was incredible, and it was amazing to feel like a solid member in an actual Azeroth war. Personally I would prefer it to be back to the days long battles that next to never end. Where the players work with their NPC troops to win the day. All Blizz would need to do was just ensure that the honour/hour is balanced with the other short BGs. Create an AV mark system that awards additional honour for those lucky or determined enough to be there at the win.

Then even if you don't finish the BG, your time isn't wasted, you've gained all the honour you would have anyway if you HAD been chugging through random BGs and those that want to helm an epic pvp war can do so. Alternatively if AV awards less honour than a normal BG, have it award a lot of gold through repeatable pvp quests. Make it the pvp way to money if it isn't there for honour.

Remove the deserter from AV so people could come and go as they please and you're good to go. Everybody wins.
