Hello Foury and all other excited Classic AV picketers!


the plea for this classic AV is just. Though it has the potential to work and play as you intended with recapping, it is 'too quick' to have any extended fun with it. so finally, a middle ground proposition to even get close to Classic AV:

1. on top of the massive damage buff for each tower up, a part of this stack should massively decrease damage to the boss (think quicksilver armor, but not as extreme as 99%), or working to summon druids or an icelord or a cavalry would make the boss "vulnerable". taking the pve race out of the pvp is a good thing.

2. 600 deaths/reinforcements in a 25 vs 25 is still too quick. even an increase to 900 would be a great start to test it out

3. most of us wouldnt care if honor gained from player kills was completely taken away if you would increase capping/recapping honor and/or summoning bosses for honor. frankly my tabbed kill count is alot more desireable. and its not like enough action happens in here anymore anyway to actually farm honor...

4. if someone HATES a bg with every fiber of their being, let players drop the bg work the debuff like res sick and lower your stats, so you can still jump in but not be as effective for a couple minutes.

and most important...

5. capturing mines should be VERY competitive vs downing towers or killing players. "oh yes took out IWB! damn! they took a mine." other faction ticks up reinforcements by lots. not by 1 every 45 seconds, just take the mines out then if they basically do nothing. for example in WSG one swift kill and outsmarting players in a heated tie game, with very little time left can switch up a win or a loss. The mines should be able to function the same way to be the "oh SHHHHHIII----" moments we need. the losing team SHOULD be able to fight back and switch it up at the last second. 50 kills left? still one tower up? "GG all. pile in remaining tower for a win guys."

pvp is ultimately about engaging the enemy faction, so when people reach the right level to do AV because at one time it yielded the most kills and honor, that should be okay. arenas and rated bgs yield the best rewards anyway. Why bring down the amount of fun everyone had in AV when it was longer, had more diversity and more challenge?

'New' AV has potential to be great, but new AV is waaaay too quick. Too many regents are need to use the really cool systems in place never get used anymore. Its like Eots without having the middle flag. strand without driving demos. or conquest without walls to take down.

sustaining the battle to be longer helps everything thats inside the bg be part of it
