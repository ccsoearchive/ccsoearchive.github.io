it had been a long day. finn hadn't slept well and that had kept gentyl awake most of the night. turncutt was gone and she had dreamed of epic storms at sea where the mary celeste, and her crew, fought for their very lives. that dream was replaced by the one of the man, standing in her bedroom holding a lit bomb from which there was no escape.

she finally got up and padded to the tower kitchen to make some hot chocolate. on either side of the kitchen entry were warning signs--GENTYL KEEP OUT. she was pretty sure the eyes above the wording were following her. she walked this way and then that. yep, the eyes were moving. that was impressive.

finished admiring the technology, she moved into the kitchen only to be scared out of her skin by a gaggle of alarm bots going off. WARNING WARNING GENTYL ALERT.

'i'm not amused,' she grumbled and cast consecrate to silence them.

sara wandered into the kitchen, rubbing her eyes. 'sepha, do you need something?'

'yes,' gentyl grumbled, 'peace, quiet and a cup of hot chocolate.'

'oh, let me fix that for you. i think the gnomish alarms refresh automatically in a few minutes if you remain.'

'lovely. yes, please, if you don't mind.' she needed to talk to the gnomes and direct them back to the important things in life, like finishing imperon's girlfriend.

they day hadn't gotten any better. the cart pony got stuck in a cart. how he got in the cart no one knew, but he had managed to wedge his head through the wicker and they finally had to all but destroy the cart to get him out.

she decided a trip to town to check with quill about printing the wanted posters might be something safe and relaxing.
