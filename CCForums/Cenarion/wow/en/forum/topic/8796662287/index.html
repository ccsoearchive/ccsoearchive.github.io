Hello everyone!

After two plus years as a guild focused almost entirely on portraying law enforcement officers and guards, we have decided to dramatically expand our RP boundaries in order to give more people and types of characters an opportunity to join.

The guild will henceforth be known as:

THE STORMWIND UNION

What does this mean?

The guild is now open to any good/law-abiding character that lives within the kingdom of Stormwind. I know many people like to have flexibility playing their characters, and by opening the guild to civilians of different professions and individual goals, that will allow people to have the RP flexibility they want in a Stormwind-focused guild. What ties the members together is the guild's dedication to keeping the kingdom strong and prosperous. From an in-character perspective, the guild is like a volunteer community association.

Of course I have to say that the Watch you know and love will continue to exist in its current form, but as a subgroup within the guild. They will be differentiated from the civilians by their uniforms and RP flags/titles.

I want to encourage everyone who is interested to contact me and see how your character can be part of this new adventure!

- Orwyn
