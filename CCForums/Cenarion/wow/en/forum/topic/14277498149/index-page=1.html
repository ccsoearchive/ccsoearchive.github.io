What is the Conclave?
The Conclave was founded as an academy devoted to the study of magic.

They existed that way for almost two years until a life changing event altered their course and now they find themselves under the political protection of the Grand Alliance as a chartered military unit, specializing in handling darker magical matters. All this occurred when they chose to stand their ground on their laws to accept -all- forms of magic and all members of the alliance; the only sanction being Necromancy.

The Conclave has changed their mission to reflect a more militant aspect over the last year and a half. As they handle one of their primary goals, that of the search and destruction of dangerous artifacts, cults, rogue mages and dark entities. This group employs an elite cadre within the martial structure called Spellbreakers as well as a structured military group, a full support staff, including medics, and healers, scholars and archaeologists. These individuals are the backbone of the group and proof that every walk of life is welcome within their walls.

***As of 8.16.15, in addition to standard applications, we are actively seeking IC healers and medics to join our ranks.***

What You Can Expect With Us:
  • A mixture of magic and military guild management..
  • Immersive and frequent world, dungeon and raid-driven RP events that will take you all over Azeroth and Draenor.
  • Dungeon Master-style event management system that turns every event into a story-arc.
  • 2-5 Events per week, Mission boards for random adventure, organic open RP with fellow guildies.
  • Magical gatherings for learning, research, debates and artifact destruction.
  • Monthly Community events such as our Soup Kitchen, Server-wide classes.
  • Multi-Guild Events, thanks to our much appreciated alliances.
  • An Officer Core committed to helping veteran and new rper's alike.
  • Cookies. Lots and lots of cookies
    • Important OOC Notes:
    We are an 18+ guild. No exceptions will be made.
    We do not allow IC Unholy Death Knights as necromancy is banned.
    Website applications will be answered within 48 hours of submission.
    IC interview required before entering guild.

    Visit us at www.conclave.shivtr.com.

    What is the Conclave?
    The Conclave is a role-playing guild founded in 2012 as an academy devoted to the study of magic, the cataloging and creation of spells and the recovery and safekeeping of magical artifacts.

    Since then the Conclave has followed its path with precision. Until a life changing event altered that course and now they find themselves in Ironforge, under the political protection of the Alliance and Council of Three Hammers, so they may operate outside the clutches of some Kirin Tor representatives that would do them ill. All this when they chose to stand their ground on their laws to accept -all- forms of magic and all members of the alliance; the only sanction being Necromancy.

    The Conclave has changed their mission to reflect a more militant aspect.

    The members of the Conclave may find advanced teachings in magic through Apprenticeship to more experienced casters. General study is found on a peer to peer relationship. We have a knowledgeable research group in charge of tracking down dangerous and interesting artifacts in a race to do so before the Horde and other parties.
