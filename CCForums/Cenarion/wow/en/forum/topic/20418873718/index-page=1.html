"Clan Steelgrasp has been some of Khaz Modans top crafters since the War of the Three Hammers! In fact, we have stood valiantly in defense of Khaz Modan for damn near five-hundred years! An now...

We take steel, an iron and bend them to our will! Fires roar, and magma boils as heat purges impurities from both our steel... an' our foes.

The steel fist of Ironforge now bares it's massive weight down on the foes of the Dwarven Kin, as we fight to ring in a new age. An age of glory for our people once more!" ~Furgas Steelgrasp



-Who we are IC!

Clan Steelgrasp is a Dwarven Clan which is focused icly on securing the borders of Khaz Modan. They believe that since the borders were weakened in the Second War, by the sacking of Dun Algaz and Dun Modr in the Wetlands, we well as the breach in the Twilight Highlands, that they must fight to restore this!

The Clan struggles to push across the Thandol Span now to secure a path through the Arathi Highlands to further unite the Wildhammer and Bronzebeard Kinsmen in their home cities of the Aerie Peak and Ironforge. Keeping open a secure trade and supply route to the Hinterlands.

All in all though... Clan Steelgrasp acts as a very particular role in any militant group.

Steelgrasp is a Clan of Blacksmiths, Miners, Engineers, Runesmiths, Leatherworkers, Lumberjacks and even Chemists. All focused on one thing.... creating weapons and armor of war by combining their skills.

The Clan Steelgrasp, acts as mass weapons, siege weapons and armor manufacturers. Hellbent on supplying as many Dwarves as they can with, the best of the best.



-Who we are OOC!

We try our best to stick to an RP, RPPvP and RPPvE setting. We are not one of those guilds where gear don't matter! We are not a guild of weak members! And most of all, we are not a guild for the typical -CHILD- to join. WHAT SO EVER!

We try to be heavy in the RP scene, as well as in the PVP and PVE scene. As of late we have been trying to branch away from so much PVP to condone more Raids and Heroics with the Guildies.

Keep in mind though we still try holding a strong PVP presence so, a set of at least honor based gear should be obtained.

THOUGH!

-Keep in mind, you are not required to take part in any of this. We do a lot of RP still for members only interested in that. We do weekly pub crawls, and plenty of /roll based events to keep those said players happy and interested.

-We do condone a custom, YET, fully lore-abiding story-line with the Clan and plan to take it a bit further.

-We also are in the middle of an RPPvP event chain with many, many more installments to come!

-Clan Steelgrasp as of late has been facing off with the a few H-Side guilds who's RP opposes ours. So we RPPvP against eachother.

-So if you enjoy RPPvP and RP events, regular PvP and all that, then this is the guild for you!



-The Rules!

-"NO PUBLIC ERP!" The Clan DEMANDS that it be in party or whispers, as well as inside a building owned / used by your character. Failure to abide this rule will lead to removal from the Guild, as well as a Blacklisting from ALL Dwarven guilds on the Wyrmrest Accord server. As, we are allied with 90% to 95% of them.

-"NO TROLLING!" You can act goofy and OOC, but do not purposely troll RPers when they clearly don't wish for it to be so. If you are to be OOC, do not do so in main RP hubs as it will irritate players and make them look upon Clan Steelgrasp as trolling rabble. Which we are not!

-"NO RANDOM IC ASSAULTS!" I know that many of you love to RP fight! But, remember that the standards in the Clan is held much higher. There are ways! -Challenge the individual to step outside the city gates where a duel is within legal rights. -Call into the Thane or a Council Member to check the situation to see if we have justified grounds to go after somebody. BUT NO RANDOM ASSAULTS! Your character will be punished accordingly.

-"ONLY TITAN-BORN!" We are a guild that is Dwarven at it's core. Though, we allow all Titan-born races in within limits. Gnomes are beloved in Clan Steelgrasp, of course. Humans are also allowed, but they have more strict guidelines and have a REQUIREMENT to be adept in Dwarven culture, lore, and craft icly.
