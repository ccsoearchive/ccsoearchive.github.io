Taking a glyph that improves the 4th thing on the list compared to the second thing on the list is kind of stupid. Even moreso considering longer slice n dice means more evis.


Yes and no.

The glyph of SnD is devalued when you're playing against teams that are kiting you or where you often swap targets (RLS, specifically) because of the time you have down in between the targets where the seconds of SnD aren't being used.

Eviscerate is being used by some of the top rogues in the game (Original, for example), because it's a guaranteed damage boost every time you eviscerate.

SnD = Good for sustained damage in a vacuum and when training a target

Eviscerate = Not as good as SnD if you stay on a target to train, but better for swaps and when you can't stay on a target


Because of this, it's entirely up to personal preference, imo.
