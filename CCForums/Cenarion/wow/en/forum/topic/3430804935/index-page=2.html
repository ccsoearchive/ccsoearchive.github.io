It's about context. If the question was about heroic modes or other cutting edge content then BM damage isn't even "pretty good" from a theoretical standpoint. But he was talking to a guy who was complaining that he couldn't use certain pets unless he was BM, and he wished he didn't have to. GC was just saying, "yeah, one one hand that kind of sucks, but on the other hand it's not the worst thing in the world if you switch to BM and use those pets. Your dps will be pretty good."

In this context, "pretty good" means "it will get the job done." From my own experience (only 6/7 normal mode FL, so I'm not bragging by any stretch) BM does that and more, even with the mechanical flaws it has. It's worth noting that I often raid with a slightly better geared MM hunter, and I'm usually no more than a few percentage points behind. Sometimes I even come out ahead. Neither of us is near the bottom of the pack in dps (usually battling for 1 and 2), although on some aoe fights we both get smoked by some multidot spec.

But the bottom line is that stuff dies. What a lot of people miss is that a ton of people don't just think that BM underperforms. They seem to think that they will go from being at the top of the meters to hanging out with the tanks or that holy paladin that keeps judging for the haste buff. Those are the people who need to be reassured that their dps will be "pretty good" as BM, because for them, it will be.
