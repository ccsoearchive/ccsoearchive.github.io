Kindred Spirits: When you use Multi-Shot, your pet's next 1/2 Basic Attacks strike up to 4 additional enemies within 8 yards.

Anyone who believes a word that comes out of Greg Street's mouth about hunters or BM is an idiot.

He is the one that in the beginning of Wrath stated BM hunters were doing too much damage. And right at release with BM having access to double BW, it was a bit high, though mostly because it had double BW and because it required little gear to perform well.

However the spec did have scaling issues, and it wasn't over the top against other specs once everyone had raid gear. Yet he continued to declare it was even as data and testing showed it was not, and showed that his proposed changes would gut the spec.

Of course he implemented his changes and BM went from competitive with a few bust issues to absolute garbage. SV immediately replaced BM as the go to spec for progression and was able to do more damage than pre-nerf BM.

His comments after the changes went live and it was clear BM had been gutted and that SV basically replaced the spec AND was now doing more damage than BM was to insinuate that BM was nerfed not because it was too high, but because it was too easy.

Greg Street probably tries to do a good job and probably doesn't have anything against any particular spec or class. However, he and the people working with him do not have a good track record with achieving balance. And he can and will be very disingenuous in his statements about changes when they screw up, which happens more often than not.
