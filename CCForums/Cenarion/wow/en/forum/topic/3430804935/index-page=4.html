That's the point several of us have been trying to make in this thread. Most of 5-man bosses do not require a lot of movement or target swapping. You probably don't do too well on fights like Vanessa Van Cleef or Jan'alai, but do fairly well on fights like Ozruk and Umbriss. There's absolutely nothing wrong with playing BM (or SV or MM) if your focus is on 5-mans and non-raid content. However, because of fight mechanics, BM is a poor choice for most raids.
