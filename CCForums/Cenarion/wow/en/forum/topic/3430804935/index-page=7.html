@Koregaz

The reason I "flamed" you is because you have an incredibly ignorant PoV on the subject and are trying to preach the casual mentality in a discussion built around progression oriented raiding.

Any time specs have been within 5%, variety becomes the fun of Pures. History has proven that if the specs are within 5%, people WILL play those specs. It doesn't have to be over a 1% difference that everyone flocks to a new spec. The problem is that they don't appear to be hurting themselves to make that a non accidental reality.

If Surv or BM were realistically within 5% of MM people would play them. However, with the 10-20% gap between specs based on the spec and the fight, the bad specs have NO place in any even semi progression oriented raid. 5% is fine. 10%+ is broken.

No one cares what you play in a 5 man or reg Firelands. I could melee Hunter through any 5 man and reg Firelands but that doesn't mean it's a feasible choice. Numbers matter when your class has "options." You might classify yourself as a Survivalist or a Beast Master, but in the game design and raid makeup, you're a Hunter first and foremost and need to do anything in your power to capitalize on that aspect. Blizzard seems less concerned about my yearning to be a Survivalist, and it's what many of us have been campaigning against.
