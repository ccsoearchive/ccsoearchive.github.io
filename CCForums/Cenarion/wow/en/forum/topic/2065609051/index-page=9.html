I started playing about midway through Wrath, before ICC came out, but not too much before. I didn't know how to use stealth for the first 40 levels. Leveling with a friend, she told me to stealth into an area and grab an item I needed. I ran headlong into the area and aggro-ed about twelve red mobs before bringing them all back to her. I discovered and began using vanish and stealth after that.

Didn't know to use poisons until level 80, had no idea how to play combat for the first six months I played (I didn't raid or dungeon much, so not a *huge* issue at the time).

Didn't know how to glyph until level 80, and even then it took me a while. Gems and enchants were very strange and foreign things to me up until last September (when I switched to assassination and began learning how to play my class for realsies).

Learning what the radar sign over mobs' heads meant (I was trying to stealth to the Elder for Lunar Festival achieves). At least no one was there to witness that particular fail.

Forgetting to re-stealth before heading off to sap a mob in BoT was pretty bad, too, though hilarious.
