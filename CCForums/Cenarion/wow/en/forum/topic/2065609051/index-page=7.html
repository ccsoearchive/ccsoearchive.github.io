Still adore this thread.

In a WSG not terribly long ago (not long enough, anyway), two bitterly opposed factions were locked in a tie with less than a minute remaining on the clock. We had the tiebreaker advantage. They had our flag, and the carrier plus four hangers-on were two-thirds of the way up the ramp to their flag room when I caught up to them -- and actually went a bit ahead of them -- via stealth and Sprint. Nobody knew I was there. I decided to try bursting the carrier down before anyone knew what had happened, and let Crippling Poison keep him within range.

I failed to take into account that Crippling was dispellable.

I also failed to fully realize that their own flag was sitting right there, ripe for the plucking.

With about 20 seconds left, they capped their third flag. And, as coincidence would have it, simultaneously certified my status as a truly awful PvPer. (Though thankfully, nobody else from my side was around to see what I had done... or failed to do.)
