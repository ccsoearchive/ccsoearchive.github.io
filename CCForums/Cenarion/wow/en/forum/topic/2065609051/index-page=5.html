Oh man, where to start?

Back when I was a wee little rogue, I accidentally fell into the canals in Stormwind and I didn't know how to get out of the water. Seriously. I was panicking seeing the breath bar go lower and lower and I was thinking "HOW DO I GET OUT OF HERE?!?!?" I was alternating between letting myself die and spirit rezzing and "no, I will survive this!" mentality. I finally discovered the space bar while pushing keys franticly; the breath bar had run out and I was starting to lose health. Oh how I cried tears of happiness. It still took me ten minutes to actually get myself out of the water afterwards (couldn't find the steps to get out).

Another I-was-so-stupid-moment-they-shouldn't-let-me-play-this-game moment was where I thought that cooking, fishing, and first aid counted as one of your two-professions limit. So all I had in the beginning was first aid and enchanting (I still don't know why I chose enchanting). I was in Westfall selling all the cooking recipes I got because I thought I couldn't use them ever.

I managed to rope my sister into the game during my time of ineptitude and she became a NE hunter. Of course I wanted to hang out w/ her in the game but I didn't know how to get there so I ran all the way from Theramore to Darkshore. With no horse. Usually in Stealth, at level 20 thinking that I could avoid all the ?? skull creatures lurking about. Yeah. >_>

It also took me the longest time (level 40) to actually use stealth and openers in combat. Until then, I used throwing knives to aggro creatures and fight them. Silly me.

You'd think I've gotten better through time but I'm still as bad. I just hide it better. ^_^
