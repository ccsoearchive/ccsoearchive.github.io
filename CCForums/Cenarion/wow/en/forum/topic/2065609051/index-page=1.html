First pug ever was normal Utgarde Keep, I was beyond terrible (like, half the DPS of the healer, terrible). The warrior tank told me to google this website called Elitist Jerks and things got a lot better very quickly.

Last week on Al'akir I missed solo'ing the last 400K-ish for our first guild kill because I got the lightning rod debuff while cloak was on CD and forgot I had a healthstone, which would've kept me alive along with chaining feints and recuperates to get through the debuff and finish off the last 185K. Also realized after wiping that I'd been using my solo'ing spec for the last half hour of attempts, increased damage from my raiding spec would've probably also tipped the balance for that kill. Instead two of our priests finished him off our next attempt. I always forget I have a healthstone.

Btw, 10m Al'akir is infinitely easier than 25m. Just throwing that out there.

Ignoring that last bit for the moment entirely, with the level difference he obviously saw me coming from a mile away, and seemed rather amused. Then he 1-shot me with something and rode away.
