I started doing the arena thing a couple weeks ago which was also right around my first cata pvp experiences. So I'm still gearing up and relearning the rogue. My arena partner is a paladin that had also never done the arena thing and had absolutely zero pvp gear (not even a trinket). Yeah, we were bad but we got our wins in for points.

Usually, I wound up doing most of the legwork since my pally was really bad at pvp (standing around casting without trying to get away from opponents, spam cleansing stuff that didn't need cleansing and ooming himself, not using pillars, doing all this in his pve spec, etc) but I didn't mind...I suck too and we were learning.

The biggest moment was trying to get our 5th win for the week to cap out points. It just wasn't happening. I couldn't put out enough pressure or damage to take anyone out. When we finally got the 5th win I found out why. I hadn't had any poisons on for the past several games.

I didn't tell my partner that was what was up though. I just said good job, about time, and silently thanked bliz for giving me an opponent so bad I didn't need poisons to kill them.
