The way I see it, your job as a Rogue is to hang out behind enemy lines and pick off the cripples. It is a time honored tradition amongst our kind, one that sadly seems to be falling by the wayside in this new 1 v 1 mentality so many have taken. Still, even with bad gear if you can find that Mage just barely clinging to life with 21k hp left trying to eat, I promise that you will know joy as few others have. You will be rewarded for stealing the KB from that Hunter with a timely shadowstep/ambush with honor which will allow you to buy gear and become stronger. Oom Priests are a favorite delicacy of mine and the way I see it, I'm doing them a favor by giving them a full mana bar upon rez. Warriors and DKs require patience, you have to wait for your friends to beat them to within an inch of their lives before timing the kill shot. Just sit back, watch and wait for the chance to steal the KB. Hey, we're thieves, it's what we do.
