Okay so I haven't been lvl 85 for too long but I'm focusing on PvP.

I bought the AH bloodied set and I'm slowly collecting the Blue Bloodthirsty set with a couple of arena conquest items.

The problem is. I'm dieing so much. I'm doing little to no damage. I am sub specced. On people with 99k health I can destroy but most people I fight are in decked out in vicious gear, have at least 112-145k hp and it's no fun. I can't kill anyone.

What the heck am I supposed to do? I get /laughed at by almost everyone I encounter in PvP. I lose most 1v1 encounters. I feel terrible. And I know how to play Sub to. I have enough experience to know when to do what.

My brother is also with me a lot. He's an 85 rogue too, we have basically the same exact gear and it's pathetic when 2 rogues take 2 minutes to kill a dk with 142k hp.


PLEASE give some tips. I know my problem has a lot to do with gear but I have got to be doing something wrong here. Should I go Mutilate spec? My bro went Mut for a little and it was TERRIBLE. He was doing about half the damage he did as Sub.


Are rogues supposed to go Sub AFTER they get good gear? What am I supposed to do please help me and my bro! Please.

If I just had the gear it would be a different story probably. I should also note that I will probably just stop going for mages all together. I know all the tactics to kill one but against a skilled fire mage it's game over.

What did you "pro" rogues do when you were fresh 85s? How did you stand getting honor with crap gear?

Please help a fellow rogue out here.

At your gear level it's best for you to not try to be the BG hero that everyone wants to be in a battleground. Help your team win by making use of your cc. Garrote healers, blind important targets, make use of smoke bomb when your team is trying to kill a flag carrier. You have many abilities to make yourself useful no matter what your gear is. Also to be less of a burden on your healers, keep recuperate up at all times when in combat. Just keep going at it until you get enough honor for the blue gear, and once you got that you'll do just fine ;)
