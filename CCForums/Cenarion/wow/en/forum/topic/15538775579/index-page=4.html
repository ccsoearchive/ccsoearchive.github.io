We are indeed! In fact we recently had an influx of recruits and I have not pushed our recruiting very hard lately, to give the new folks time to settle in.

Last night the Servitors captured a ship belonging to their old nemesis Brog Bloodeye. It was one of those nights:

1. Water walk from the mainland to Fray Island, where Bloodeye and his Iron Knuckles have taken up residence. Everyone fails to take out the lookout. Lookout raises the alarm.

2. Commander says time for Plan B, run directly to the ship. Knuckles pour out of their barracks, ready for a fight, looking all around for the invaders and don't see any. That's because the invaders are still out on the water, now running for the ship at dock.

3. Anybody got a rope?

4. Thanks to the first good roll of the night, we have rope. We also have an orc sailor on deck, pointing us out to his buddies and now they're firing pistols down at us. The Servitors take cover under the dock while Jocastia, an old hand at piracy, climbs through one of the open hatches on the gun deck.

5. While the orcs reload their pistols, the Servitors run out from under the dock and manage to hit all three of them at range. We are no longer being shot at. Yay! But the gunfire attracted attention from the orcs on the island. Uh oh.

6. The rest of the Servitors follow Jo onto the gun deck and take out the rest of Bloodeye's sailors, and capture his shaman. Let's get this ship a-sail before we get boarded!

7. Orcs swarm the dock and try to get onto the ship. The Servitors manage to hold them at bay while the ship slowly lurches forward.

8. Brommidor hurls an incendiary potion at the orcs on the dock and rolls a 1. The ship is now on fire.

9. Driven by the same shaman-summoned wind that fills our sails, the fire spreads. Everyone fails to put it out until Aarond rolls a 100 and Thunder Claps the flame into submission.

10. Brog Bloodeye waves his fists in rage as we sail away with all of his personal belongings, including his copy of "Hot Paladins In Bondage."

It was not the sort of victory Eth was hoping for, but we got away with intel, a ship, and our lives.

To get in on the swashbuckling action, go to www.servitors.org and hit the "click here to join" link on the left!

The Servitors faced an unusual challenge in Draenor yesterday as they sought out wisdom from Shadowmoon orc ancestors to help them fight the malignant, soul-devouring Unnamed. Shaman Izarre contacted the spirit of an ancient Shadowmoon elder, who agreed to teach her if the Servitors could prove their wisdom, courage, and strength. The spirit tested us with a trial of riddles and combat, and in the end we proved our worth. Hopefully the knowledge Izarre gained brings us one step closer to defeating the Unnamed!

Even ancient Shadowmoon wisdom, however, is unlikely to be of any use in facing a new threat that's emerged. The Servitors have been contracted to hunt down and destroy the persons responsible for a series of murder/mutilations. And it appears to be a crazed, oblivion-worshipping cult.

Soul-devouring undead, murderous cultists, and a creatively malignant orc commander with a personal vendetta...are you sure you want to join the Servitors now? Of course you do. Go to www.servitors.org and send us your IC letter of interest!
