Mutilate no longer requires poisons. Deadly is going to a single stack. Envenom no longer requires deadly. Utility poisons will fulfill the poisoned requirement on venemous wounds.


Mutilate no longer requires poisons, the comment was that Hemo requiring a bleed is a lot like the old Mutilate that had increased damage on poisoned targets.

Deadly is going to a single stack which will make ramp-up more forgiving, and Envenom doesn't require poisons ... but that also sucks some of the flavor out of Envenom. It's now closer to being a poison Eviscerate.

Utility poisons can fill the poison requirement on Venomous Wounds, but keep in mind that in PvP utility poisons have a duration of 8 seconds. Compare with Deadly that has a duration of 12s. PvP Paralytic and Leeching may have a duration of 15s. Compare with 35+ second duration of SnD (Sub's energy regen), which is a buff on the Rogue (not a dispelable debuff on the target) usable with HaT/premed procs at range.
