We had a nice long, 3 part thread in the MoP forums. It eventually died. Still, there are some lingering concerns I'd like to share with the Rogue community at large. Please, share your thoughts.

First off: rotations.

Sub: Sub looks to finally get to use both Hemo and Backstab throughout a fight and depending on positioning. Not bad, right? Here's the issue, Rupture is auto-refreshed and SnD lasts 25-40 seconds before mastery. In other words, between refreshing SnD every 40+ seconds, Sub will essentially spam Eviscerate. It's not very compelling gameplay.

Combat: Probably the best off, Combat has 2 combo point builders (Sinister Strike and Revealing Strike) and 2- 3 finishers (SnD, probably Rupture, and Eviscerate. A 5 button damage rotation with the possibility of other moves as well (Faint, Tricks, even Expose Armor or Shiv). To be honest, Combat's rotation doesn't bother me, and is used here only for comparison purposes.

Mut: Mut has a similar finisher issue as Sub, SnD is auto-refreshed leaving Mut with just two finishers to apply manually throughout a fight, Envenom and Rupture. Fortunately (for PvE Rogues), Rupture's duration is shorter than SnD's, so Mut won't see quite as much Envenom spam as Sub sees Eviscerate spam … but it still boils down to just two finishers. Mut's rotation is further dumbed down however by only one cp builder. Yes, one. Mut ALWAYS uses Mutilate until mob health drops below 35%, then it ALWAYS uses Dispatch. This is effectively a 3 button damage rotation.

Think about that a minute folks. With Sub, you use two cp builders and spam Eviscerate except once per 40+ seconds (then SnD). With Mut, you use one cp builder and spam Envenom except once per 20-ish seconds (then Rupture).

Even with Faint/Tricks/etc thrown in, would you call that compelling gameplay?

-------------

Second: these rotations create another problem in PvP.

- SnD is a 25-40s (before Sub mastery) self-buff that can be cast at range with combo points generated at range (HaT & Premed).
- Venomous Wounds procs off 18s Garrote (requires stealth) and 12-20s Rupture, and still requires a 8-15s poison on target.
- Vitality is always running, but Combat Potency requires melee range.

So who do you think will more easily fit Recuperate into their PvP rotation? Sub is currently set to continue enjoying a significant PvP survivability advantage over the other two specs.

------------

Third: We're still missing Ruthlessness. Though "optional" for Sub and Combat PvE, Ruthlessness is a staple of the Mut rotation. That one extra RNG combo point after a finisher helped vary Mut's simplistic rotation. Sometimes you'd Mutilate twice, sometimes only once. Without it, is there any longer a question?
