So I am going to do a background for a human priestess of the Light. Any interested in doing the paladin as a starting character and joining her in start zone, post here.

Traesa Haluman was born in Southshore 20 years ago, she moved to Stormwind when she was 16. Her parents had sent her to train in the big city. When she choose the Light and became a priestess they were happy for her.

She finished her schooling and became a regular at Northshire, teaching first aid and helping to fight off the orcs. The sacking of Southshore came as a shock to her. Luckily her parents escaped injury and were able to make it to Stormwind.

Recently she has become more active in her training. She wishes to become an archeologist some day as she enjoys natural history and is fascinated by cultural differences in the races.

She is friendly and outgoing, actively seeking diplomatic avenues to relieve the tensions. The war has touched her personally, and the loss of her home has affected her, but she is sure there is a way for it to end peaceably.

Ok if you want to join in the story, post your background story here. Then we can discuss meeting in game. Traesa is on Moonguard at the moment. Or can be done on any rp server if someone wants to start a different server.
