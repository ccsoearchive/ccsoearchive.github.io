Would I be significantly hurting myself and my fellow raiders if I tried to raid as Assassination for heroic SoO? Right now I'm combat but I really enjoy Assassination far more.


It's hard to say. If you have the gear (Notably, the 4 set, good weps for Combat and Assurance of Consequence) and can master the fast pace, then Combat will very likely outperform Assassination. The 4-set and trinket have fantastic synergy with Restless Blades, reducing the cooldowns on Rush and Spree to near zilch. If you don't have good weapons or the trinket (The 4-set bonus, while very good, doesn't have as much of an impact without those two factors) then you're probably better off as Assassination.

Short answer, yes, but only if you have Assurance of Consequence and good weapons.

If gear isn't a factor (That is to say that you could have best in slot for both specs) than Combat will pull ahead on single target fights but it won't be a large difference. However, many of the fights in SoO, especially Heroic, are cleave or AoE heavy: Immerseus has new adds on Heroic, Protectors is the cleave fight, Norushen still has adds, Pride still has adds, Galakras of course has adds... About the only notably single target fights on Heroic are Juggernaut, Thok and Paragons. (And even Thok has an add on Heroic, although the mechanics of it allow it to be ignored or planned around.) So it should go without saying that Combat has a very large advantage.

So, yeah, if your guild is hurting for the DPS and/or you want to bring all that you can to the raid, you're probably better off as Combat.
