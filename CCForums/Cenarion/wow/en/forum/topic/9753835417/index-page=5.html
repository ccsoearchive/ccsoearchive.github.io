Generating Anticipation charges won't affect your finishers as they only consume CPs, not Anticipation, so even if you have 5 CPs and 3 Anticipation charges, finishers will consume 5 CPs and then your Anticipation charges will become CPs. So, yes, Mutilate/Dispatch and don't let your energy cap.

The changes in 6.0.2 such as Venomous Wounds now being a 100% proc rate and the removal of reforging giving us more Haste then we need means that you may sometimes get yourself into situations where you have 5 CPs, 5 Anticipation charges and near capping on energy. In those situations, you can clip Envenom so that you don't cap energy/waste CPs or you can give yourself a little defensive help with Recuperate or Feint, which is very useful if you have the 20% more healing while Recup is active glyph and if the raid is going through a heavy damage phase. You can also refresh a Rupture with 30% or less time on it (7 seconds at 5 CPs I believe) and the new duration will be added to the old one.
