I don't know if anyone uses Icy Veins as a guide - I like it because it's a well organized site - but their stat priority is AGI > Crit > Multistrike > (Versatility) > Mastery > Haste.

Do you find their guides to be a reliable source of info?


Icy Veins definitely has their head on straight and I tend to trust them. It's Noxxic that you should watch out for!

Stat values tend to change as you get more of one stat. At my point, for example, Shadowcraft alternates between crit gemming and mastery gemming every time I hit the optimize button with mastery coming out slightly ahead. (Probably because I no longer have my 2 set.)

At any rate, neither stat value is really correct nor wrong since, as I mentioned, they change often. Both priorities are really just an average from across the 6.0.2 ilvl range of what stat tends to give the most benefit. The differences between our priorities are also just conjecture since there's only one way to get Multistrike as a Agi class currently, and that's from Haromm's Talisman which is a BIS trinket.


Ah, thanks for the clarification and info! Great guide. =)
