The note hasn't been added yet, but we haven't forgotten. Here's what you're likely to see in the patch notes:

  • Meta gems with the Chaotic and Relentless prefixes now have a requirement of 3 red gems.

  • Added new meta gems: Agile Shadowspirit Diamond (Agility/3% critical damage), Reverberating Shadowspirit Diamond (Strength/3% critical damage), and Burning Shadowspirit Diamond (Intellect/3% critical damage). These new recipes are unbound and can drop from any Cataclysm creature. The new meta gems have a requirement of 3 red gems equipped.




I am concerned about the fact that it will be some sort of "World drop."

It will truly be a pity if such desirable metas have no reliable method of acquisition. I would really, really prefer that they be added to the loot tables of certain Heroic bosses, much like in WotLK, and were made BoP for JCs. All other metas are easily obtainable through JC dailies/tokens. Why should these desirable cuts be so vastly different, especially when they are dps-specific, and tank and healer metas are readily available?


I understand certain green and even blue quality niche cuts being world drop and more of a "lottery" to obtain, but is it really a good idea to have such HIGHLY and WIDELY desired cuts as a world drop that some extremely low population servers/factions won't even see for some time?


Please also take into account the fact that, while Wold Drops may not be an issue for higher populations to find in larger number more quickly, some low pop servers will suffer from a method of RNG acquisition because they will have fewer JCs and fewer people 'farming' the pattern. Making something that is arguably going to be 'required' for competitive dps based on RNG, and putting low-pop realms and factions at a disadvantage for getting them out into the market quickly just doens't seem "fair," or like a good idea from a "player enjoyment" standpoint.
