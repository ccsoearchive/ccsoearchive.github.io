Hello! My fiance and I are looking for a guild.

About Our Characters:
I have 2 level 85 healers (Druid and Priest) and he has several level 85s (Tank/DPS/Heals). I have experience healing raids in early Cataclysm and LFR (yuck!) and he has experience DPSing since vanilla, including early Cata content and later LFR. He is interesting in gaining experience in raid tanking.

About Us in RL:
We are 21+ and friendly. We enjoy helping our guildies out and are NOT jerks. We don’t enjoy playing with bratty children (but under-aged mature players are fine). We both have busy schedules during the work week, but do usually find time to play almost every day. I also go to college full time. We both like to goof off and have a good time, but prefer a more mature, socially aware group.

What We Want:
We are looking for a guild that does 10man raids and intends to continue through MoP. We're not looking for a hardcore group but rather one that we can enjoy playing the game with as we progress. We prefer weekend raiding, but other times that fit our schedule are fine too. We are looking for LIKE MINDED PLAYERS. This means: Casual, laid back and friendly, but also goal-oriented. Maybe, "Progressive Casual" is a good way to put it? We want to get our money’s worth of this game and see the content while it is still hot (although we also like going back through old content as well). Any guild PVP is a plus. We like to PVP on the side – Battlegrounds, Arena, Rated Battlegrounds, but this is not necessary. Just a plus.

We were on hiatus until earlier this summer when we returned and joined a great group of players. However, we're finding ourselves a bit more ambitious and the guild just doesn't quite fit anymore.

We prefer to find something on CC-Alliance, but please do not hesitate to post if you aren't on CC-Alliance. We are willing to relocate for the right group.
