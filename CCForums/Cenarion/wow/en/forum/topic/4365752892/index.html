CC is and always will be my RP home.
It's smaller than some of the big ones like Moon Guard, and Wrymrest Accord, but I find that makes everything so much better when you're interacting with fellow players.

These are people you are going to play with extensively, people that you'll get to know both IC and OOC. Not a single one I've found to be a person I'd rather not talk to. CC is small-ish, but so much more like a close knit family.

If you're new, I can tell you through experience that these people will not scorn, ignore, or troll you.

Just hop in to AllianceOOC or HordeOOC and say "Hello! I'm new to CC! or RP!" I guarantee the welcomes will be warm and truly heartfelt, the excitement will be tangible and the number of tacklehugs, smacks with the frying pans of doom, and roguish tricks will make you smile and want to stay here.

The raiding scene is here on CC, many of the RP guilds with a plethora of 85's have groups that raid. Pia Presidium, Ocheliad, Conclave, etc... They all raid, and are happy to bring extra members of the community that wish to go. They're not elitists, they're RPers and raiders who take both casually, and just go to have fun.

I'd advise poking around and trying to get random filler spaces with a few groups before choosing a permanent home. Find out which guild fits your personal style best :)
