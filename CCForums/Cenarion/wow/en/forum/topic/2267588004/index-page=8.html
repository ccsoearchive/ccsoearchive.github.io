I still want that macro for 1 button application for all my poisons including thrown. I used the one earlier, just not working for some reason, could you re-post it or so?

#showtooltip
/use [button:1] MH Poison; [button:2] OH Poison; [button:3] Thrown Poison
/use [button:1] 16; [button:2] 17; [button:3] 18
/Click StaticPopup1Button1

Puts the selected poison on your MH with a left-click, OH poison with a right-click and thrown poison with a middle-click. The bottom line gets rid of the poison-override confirmation.

There was a typo on the original, which I fixed, so you might have copied it wrong. I also changed it from a castsequence so Im not quite sure if it will work as well as the original.

Poison macros do serve their function and all, but I can't recommend this enough:

http://wow.curse.com/downloads/wow-addons/details/poisoner.aspx

Puts all your poison options into a collapsible UI button. Left click - MH / Right click - OH / Middle click - Thrown

Not only that but has options for different types of audio and/or visual warnings for poison expiration, and can be set to automatically restock poisons to a pre-set level anytime you visit a vendor that carries them (or a repair bot, for that matter).
