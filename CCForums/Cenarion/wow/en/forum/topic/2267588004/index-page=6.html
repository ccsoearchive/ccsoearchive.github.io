Ooc: stealth (if not stealthed), premed, ss, garrot, hemo, kidneyshot, backstab, backstab, rupture

#showtooltip
/cast [nocombat] !Stealth
/cast Premeditation
/cast [stealth] Shadowstep; [combat] Shadowstep
/cast [stealth] Garrote; [combat] Backstab

Stealths whenever you are able to, casts Premeditation whenever you are able to, casts SS when you are in stealth and in combat, and casts Garrote while Stealthed and BS while not.

I could add modifiers to the macro, but it would just complicate it further, and its not a good idea to have such a bloated macro because it can bug out and not work the way you want it to. I would use this to Stealth/SS mainly because you dont want to waste a SS while BSing.

Well, it wouldnt be a good idea to put all of that into one macro. However, I could combine a few of them to simplify things.

You can combine Premeditation to all of your openers so that you have one less button to worry about:

#Showtooltip Ambush
/Cast Premeditation
/Cast Ambush

Then:

#Showtooltip
/Cast [mod:shift] Kidney Shot
/Cast [nomod, stealth] Ambush; [nomod, nostealth] Backstab

Ambush while Stealth and Backstabs otherwise, just hold shift to use Kidney Shot. Splits what you were looking for into two macros.

Adding Backstab/Ambush to Shadowstep is also a good idea; lets you get off the attack the moment you are behind your target:

#Showtooltip
/Cast Shadowstep
/Cast [stance:1/3] Ambush; [nostealth] Backstab

You could also move your spells around so that your opener switches to your finisher when your bars switch.

However, if you really want what youre after, this may suffice:

#Showtooltip
/Cast !Stealth
/Cast Premeditation
/Cast [mod:shift] Kidney Shot
/Cast [nomod, stealth, combat] Shadowstep
/Cast [nomod, stance:1/3] Ambush; [nomod, nostealth] Backstab

It should do everything you asked for. Just hold shift to cast the finisher, and swap out the spells for your other spec.

05/10/2011 09:29 AMPosted by Hottaco
Could say exactly what this does. I am new to Macros. Also. do you have to hit the button to complete each sequence ie. Stealth mash, Kidney shot mash, Shadowstep mash etc..

Well it looks kinda complicated, but its actually pretty simple. Clicking this button out of Stealth/combat will Stealth you. Premeditation is off of the GCD meaning that it can cast simultaneously with other spells, so this is one less spell you will have to worry about. The mod portion of the macro is to simply cast Kidney Shot while holding shift. Shadowstep is also off of the GCD, and it will only cast if you are Stealthed or in combat. The last line is for getting off an Ambush or Backstab the second you Shadowstep behind your target. However, this macro isnt for Ambush/Backstab spamming because if you were to use it as one, youd probably be wasting a Shadowstep CD when you might already be behind the target, and thats why I mainly use it to Stealth/Shadowstep.
