No, those types of qualitative decisions by design do not function in a macro, in fact to write a script that would is considered botting and bannable under the ToS/EULA.

Macros are meant to efficiently handle complex actions but always at the judgement of the user. If a macro evaluated the game state and chose the best action to perform as a result, then there is no decision to be made by the player, the game is playing itself in effect.

Just to pitch in heres a more easier to manager posion macro.
#showtooltip Wound Poison
/use [nomod, button:1] Mind-Numbing Poison; [nomod, button:2] Crippling Poison; [mod:shift, button:2] Wound Poison; [nomod, button:3] Crippling Poison
/use [button:1] 16; [button:2] 17; [button:3] 18
/click StaticPopup1Button1
Left clicking it does Mind numbing on mainhand, right clicking it does crip on offhand, mid mousing it does crip on throw wep. Shift right click does wound. Another perk to that is you don't have to hit yes for every weapon.

For wep swap macros to have Mindnumbing/crip/crip on throw too as well as keeping up a MS for certain comps you can get another offhand and put wound posion on that weapon and macro it into shiv. Sadly only one offhand dagger and no diff names so In order to macro it for the wep swap I use the equipment manager and use 2 different sets.(for my orig set Wep1, the swap I named Wep2 just preference whatever you wan't to call it.)

#showtooltip Shiv
/equipset Wep1
/cast shiv
/cast throw (just preference if im in range it will shiv if im out of range it will throw saving a keybind.) For the wep swap it's the same macro except for It being Wep2 instead of Wep1.

Another useful addition for PvP Would be to macro cheapshot/kidney into stopwatch to track DR. Of cource there's always gladius or some type of DR tracker but to have the stopwatch in a more noticable place to make sure you don't waste your stuns.
#showtooltip Kidney Shot
/cast Kidney Shot
/sw 25
/sw play

#showtooltip Cheap Shot
/cast Premeditation
/cast Cheap Shot
/sw 22
/sw play

Redirect Kidney with stopwatch:
#showtooltip
/castsequence [@focus, exists] reset=20 Redirect, Kidney Shot
/cast [mod:shift] Redirect; Redirect
/sw 25
/sw play

Couple of macros I am aware of for junk box farmng, thise are ideal for farming junkboxes if you want the epics, or if you are farming Rogue Draughts:

#showtooltip Pick Pocket
/console targetNearestDistance 5
/targetenemy [noharm][dead]
/cast Pick Pocket
/cleartarget

Just run around in stealth and spam it, pure win.
Fractured Front is a good spot for this as well as Elementium Depths and Normal Grim Batol. Recommend the glyphs of both Pick Pocket and Pick Lock enough, as well as a build including both quickening and Nightstalker.

Some others:

#showtooltip Pick Pocket#show Pick Pocket/cast [target=mouseover, exists] Sap; Sap/cast [target=mouseover, exists] Pick Pocket; Pick Pocket

#showtooltip Pick Pocket
/cast [target=mouseover, exists] Pick Pocket; Pick Pocket

(PPs your mouseover if you have one, your target if you don't)
