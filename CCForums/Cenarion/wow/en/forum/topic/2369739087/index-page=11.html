Aedistan, the careful aim section of shot rotations answers both your initial questions.

Because of the bonuses to Steady/Aimed Shot we DO NOT want to continuously re-apply Serpent Sting or use Chimera shot during this phase.

During dynamic haste effects blow 80% I use chim on cooldown.

You also want to be keeping the imp SS buff up at all times. But the scenario where you are focus capped with it falling off shouldn't happen in the first place if you had dumped that focus in the prior rotation.
