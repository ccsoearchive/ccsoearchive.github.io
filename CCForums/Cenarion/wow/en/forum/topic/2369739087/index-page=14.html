Ok, so a typical stat weight has haste and crit equal to less than 0.2 of a point of agility?


For me, I am sitting very close to the 1.5 second plateau, so adding more Haste is not beneficial at all. In fact, when I use FD to sim myself, as I add small amounts of Haste, the value of Haste drops significantly. As far as Hit goes, if you are already at the Hit cap, adding more would be a complete waste.

Because I don't know the rest of your gear, I can't tell with certainty that you will be able to obtain more Crit after equipping the Helm than you would have had before. Crit is weighted very close to Agility for me on FD, though.

So to answer your question, Haste might be worth 0.2 points of Agility when you're on a plateau, but Crit probably isn't. I would figure out if you really are able to obtain more Crit through reforging and then parsing that through FD before you make a decision.

The rule that I follow is to get as much Agility as I can from wherever I can get it. Because I can get Agility from gems, but not from reforging, I take as many Agility gems as possible and use reforging to handle everything else.
