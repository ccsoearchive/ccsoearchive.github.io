The BIS list only accounts for iLevel 372 I believe and Sinestra loot is 379. However this same question was raised in the previous thread and Mendia's answer was:

Itemization wise, you're comparing an extra socket on a piece that has Crit/Mastery(which you reforge mastery to Haste)+the T11 chest (Mastery/Haste) versus Omnotron Chest(Crit/Haste) and T11 Legs(Crit/Haste). Since the BiS list means you'll be taking the CS glyph+the corresponding Haste plateau to go with it, you will have a decent time trying to reach the Haste plateau, considering you are using 2 Hit/Crit rings(H Omnotron ring+H Council Ring to make up for losing Fluid Death and gaining H Prestor's).

If you look at the Stat breakdown in the Simcraft parse(http://www.simulationcraft.org/406/hunter_auto.html), you see that the end result of prioritizing the Hit cap, then the Haste plateau, but not at the expense of Crit, you end up with 10.75% Haste or 1376 Haste Rating, which is barely above the minimum Haste needed.

I actually need to update the CA section to avoid this confusion. I suggested casting SrS as your first shot to use some of your focus so that it isn't wasted when you do 2x Steadies to get ISS before you pop RF and go into Aimed/Steady shot spam.

I would recommend if you can to start pre-casting Aimed shot as the Tank is counting down, then do 2 steady shots to get ISS up and then go into your normal CA priority.

If your tank does not use a countdown or is unreliable, then you could just cast an instant like SrS or Chimera Shot then 2 Steadies and then start your CA priority.

Here is what we did on normal Magmaw, We had 2 tanks, one on magmaw, one just for the parasites. Me and the parasites tank would stay at range. Nobody attacked the parasites ever, just let them pile up on the tank. In normal mode they dont hit that hard at all and are easily tankable. I would stay on the boss full time only moiving to avoid the Pillars of flame. Everyone else would stand in Melee range.

This strat will allow you to focus exclusively on the boss with minimum movement. MM performs very well in this Strat becuase of not having to worry about the adds and there is very little movement. In fact I think I still have a top 20 parse on this fight as MM.

That seemed like the easiest strat. We have done it other ways where me (as SV) and one other ranged would blow up the adds, but it was so much easier with 2 tanks.

Hope that helps.
