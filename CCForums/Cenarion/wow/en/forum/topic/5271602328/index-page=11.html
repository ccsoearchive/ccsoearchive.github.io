If I'm still playing, I'll probably go to this special portion of Duskwood to sit quietly for a while before logging out for good.

What's so special about it? I sat there for hours with someone I cared for deeply. We would talk all day and all night sometimes, just chatting and cutting up and making big plans about life and such. This was back when I was Alliance, of course. But then I lost her and I remembered logging in from there one day. I posted a memorium screenshot to my Facebook of just myself sitting on that hill, in the dark of the night under the trees, surrounded by the spiders we used to poke at for fun together.

I knew all along that she wouldn't be in my life forever, but it still hurt like hell. So I'd probably sit there one last time, remembering all the nights I spent with her in the hospital, holding her hand, taking silly photos of her wearing my fedora and coat. Wondering what could have been. Remembering the bittersweetness of it all, the smiles and the laughter and the nights filled with agonizing pain. Hoping I always remember the faces, both the real one and the pixelated one.

I guess it just seems fitting to me.
