In wow? Well, back before flying, a friend and I used to spend hours climbing just for the challenge. One of our favorite spots, the "Wetlands farm" could be reached by slow-fall from Ironforge Airport. There you'll find small farmland plots around a pond at the base of a tall waterfall.

We used to sit there for hours just fishing and talking, listening to the sound of the water lapping the shore and the chirping of birds. My friend died of cancer just after Thanksgiving two years ago.
