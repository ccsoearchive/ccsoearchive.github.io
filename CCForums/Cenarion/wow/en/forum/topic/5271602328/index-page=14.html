I very much like that idea. I'd probably be somewhere in the middle of Westfall, though.

To me, Vanilla Westfall was the greatest area in the game. It felt heroic, on a really small scale; these were people that were in dire need of someone, anyone, to help them survive constant bandit attacks, and the Stormwind Army couldn't even be bothered to help them.

It was just such a great, meaningful build-up. You knew that you weren't a Warcraft-calibur Hero yet, but you felt like you got just a taste of what it would be like. Helping the common folk of Westfall would be your "origin story", not to mention Old Westfall was an absolutely GORGEOUS location (just look at the concept-art, not to mention the Harvest Golems).

It's a shame that Westfall has changed so much, as I *HATE* the Cataclysm makeover it was given. That said, that will probably be where Claymore, my first character, decides to end his adventure. Either there, or the Cathedral of Light.
