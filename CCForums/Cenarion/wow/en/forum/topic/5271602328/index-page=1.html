What will you do on your last day?
Will you go anywhere special to log off for the last time?


It's not like I'm dead! We've all been expecting Blizzard to have an end, but it is not an immediate one. It will have to take much time for their end to take place, and a beginning of a new game genre.

By the time Blizzard runs out of Ideas and closes WoW, I will still be moving on. The dramatic effect however will be a memorable one. The memories of fighting ragneros, the memories of gazing a new world through a dark portal, the memories of a risen Lich King, and memories of the destroyer of worlds being beat by heroes of Azeroth.

Will I be going somewhere? Possibly. Where? It will depend as to where else I want to explore. But do not feel depressed knowing that there is an end to great things; that's the way ideas and gaming has been.

Unless, though, Blizzard gives WoW a backup and turns the game re-vamp of everything. Then the cycle shall continue. But I'll still be playing, as Valendle; the Shaman who faught the destroyer of worlds.
