I'm still seeing PvP gear in the Armory. But, judging by your specs, I am going to assume that your secondary spec is your PvE spec, so I will give you some equations based on that spec. I also assume that you do not have the 4-Piece T11 set bonus. If you do, just let me know and I will edit this post.

First, I will assume that your raid provides the 10% Attack Speed bonus (Improved Icy Talons or Windfury Totem) and that you maintain the ISS buff for 15% haste. You are also specced into Hunting Party, so that is 3% additional haste. From Chorgahn's post (http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/2267490605?page=6#113), that gives you a haste multiplier of
M = 1.15*1.10*1.03 = 1.30295

Now, the first haste plateau occurs when our Steady Shot has a 1.5 second cast time. To determine the percent haste necessary, you must solve the equation
2.0/(1.30295*X) = 1.5
X = 2.0/(1.5*1.30295) = 1.0233
That means, you only need 2.33% Haste on your character sheet to reach the first plateau. This number differs from Whitefyst's number (http://elitistjerks.com/f74/t112408-cataclysm_marksmanship/#3_7_5_Haste_Calculations) because he does not include the Hunting Party talents. The next step is to convert Percent Haste into Haste Rating, via the following formula:
128.057*(2.33) = 1%*(2.33)
298.373 = 2.33%
The conversion factor is given by Kalilah on the first page. Of course, you should probably aim for 300 or so Haste to account for latency and reaction time.

Now, two notes. First, if you have the T11 4-Piece set bonus, you can replace the 2.0 in the second equation with 1.8 and then recalculate everything. Secondly, if you want to aim for the 1.25 second Steady Shot, you need to replace the 1.5 in the second equation with a 1.25.

Hope this helps you out and gets this thread stickied.

Here is the portion of Whitefysts post where he recommendes 9.64% haste:

6.5.1.1 Standard Phase Summary and Recommendations

For hunters without the T11 4-set bonus when under no dynamic haste effects, I suggest the following:
- Without the CS glyph:
-- Cast CS off CD with a CS-ASx2-SSx5 rotation (shot order is flexible).
-- Any MMM AI procs should generally replace the extra SS in the rotation with adding a little deadtime; however, to balance focus, an AS needs to be replaced instead of an SS about every 4th MMM AI proc.
-- For this cycle, 9.64% haste is ideal and recommended for both a tight and sustainable rotation with proper MMM AI replacement.
- Do not use the CS glyph (unless latency makes it favorable). Although workable rotations are possible, they are not optimal where using a different glyph is probably better.


However he also says:
For those of your without the T11 4-set bonus, the CS glyph, or other cases, sorry that I do not provide an overall haste recommendation, but there are too many possible combinations/choices to provide one for every situation.
