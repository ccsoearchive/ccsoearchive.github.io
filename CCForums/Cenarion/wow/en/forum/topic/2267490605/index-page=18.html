Actually last I checked the goblin racial did affect focus regen. Now that we get an ingame display of focus regen in 4.1 it should be easier to verify if this is indeed the case (at least for hunters, not pets), but the only attack speed buffs I'm aware of that don't affect hunter focus regen are improved steady shot and windfury.

I'm just confused why you have rapid killing in a raid environment? I know MM hunters don't have access to AoE damage right now, but barrage should be 2/2 at least for when they buff multishot in 4.1, no?


The big thing to notice here is that both of these talents are listed as optional, meaning pick the combo that best benefits your individual play style. Myself, I love Bombardment, but I need 5 or more targets to expect it to be useful at my levels of crit. That's the kicker when it comes to the talent, it's pretty bad for smaller packs of adds/trash.

The buff is great news for all specs, but will probably just help MM pull up to SV numbers, while providing a small buff to SV. Because the talent procs off of crits, increasing your crit rate is the best way to make this talent more useful.

The better question is, are you swimming in focus during AoE periods or are you focus starved? Unless I've got something wrong because it's too early, you should spec into Rapid Killing only when you're constantly low on focus during AoE phases and getting lots of killing blows. Again, that's a play style question.
