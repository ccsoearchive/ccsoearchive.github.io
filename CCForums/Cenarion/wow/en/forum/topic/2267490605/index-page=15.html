So the "focus dump" means after the careful aim (after 80 percent) forget about hard cast aimed and do arcane and chimera? rather than aimed and chimera?


While in a perfect standstill fight the Hard Cast Aimed shot priority will yield slightly more DPS (600 DPS) than Arcane focus dump, it is a lot less forgiving. If you end up not being able to cast 2 or 3 Aimed shots because of movement or some other reason, the Arcane Focus dump will pull ahead.

The Arcane Focus dump is much more forgiving and easier to adapt to casting on the move and usually ends up being more DPS than using Aimed Shot as a focus dump.

The general recommendation is to use Hard Cast Aimed shots during Careful Aim phases and any other Dynamic Haste effects such as Hero or Rapid Fire.
