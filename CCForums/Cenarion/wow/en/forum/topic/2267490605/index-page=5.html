Trueshot Aura: Grants a 10% Attack Power buff to all party/raid members. This is can be a substantial DPS increase for the entire raid, however this is an optional talent because there are 3 other classes that bring this buff. This buff can be covered by Druids Mark of the Wild and by Pally's Blessing of Kings. Very rarely will you find yourself in a 25/10 man raid without at least 1 pally and 1 Druid.
*Recommendation = Optional depending on your raid composition.


Mark of the Wild/Blessing of the Kings Increases Strength, Agility, Stamina, and Intellect by 5%, and all magical resistances not 10% AP the other 10% AP buffs are Unleash Fury (Enh Shaman) Blessing of Might (All Paladins) Abominations Might (Blood DK)

EDIT1: Look at haste AGAIN (sorry) are you sure that 301 (from gear?) with no CS glyph is all that is needed for a 5xSS rotation?

From Whitefyst:
For hunters without the T11 4-set bonus when under no dynamic haste effects, I suggest the following:
- Without the CS glyph:
-- Cast CS off CD with a CS-ASx2-SSx5 rotation (shot order is flexible).
-- Any MMM AI procs should generally replace the extra SS in the rotation with adding a little deadtime; however, to balance focus, an AS needs to be replaced instead of an SS about every 4th MMM AI proc.
-- For this cycle, 9.64% haste is ideal and recommended for both a tight and sustainable rotation with proper MMM AI replacement.
- Do not use the CS glyph (unless latency makes it favorable). Although workable rotations are possible, they are not optimal where using a different glyph is probably better.



Just some constructive criticism here, take it or leave it. I don't like/agree with Frostheim's haste explanations. I think he goes a little overboard with the numbers by showing scenarios that should not ever be used. For example, with the T11 set bonus a player should almost definitely have the CS glyph. Yet he shows the haste numbers without the glyph. Why confuse people? Same without the T11 bonus. You really don't even need to use the glyph; why show the haste values.

Personal opinion, show the rotations and haste numbers that people should aim for. ex:

No T11 (don't use CS glyph); CS - ASx2 - SSx5 = 1235 haste from gear
T11 (use CS glyph); CS - ASx2 - SSx5 = 1905 haste from gear

There are other options yes, but they don't seem to be ideal.
