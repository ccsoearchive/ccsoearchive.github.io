Yeah, I wouldn't use that flow chart as a rule, but more of a guideline. I would recommend the Raid Checklist Add-On. It takes up next to no space on your UI and can be moved to wherever you please. It automatically inspects each party member and determines what buffs/debuffs are present and how many people bring them. If you have an active pet, but it is not summoned, then the buff that pet can bring is shown in blue. I just check this list at the beginning of each instance and pull out the best pet for the job.

When considering only you and other physical dps classes, the Agi/Str buff is the best buff. However, DKs are very common, so this buff is almost always covered. Once you take into account magic dealers and healers, the 5% crit buff is probably the best buff. Personally, I find far fewer feral druids, sub rogues, ele shamans, and fury warriors than DKs, so I usually have my wolf out.
