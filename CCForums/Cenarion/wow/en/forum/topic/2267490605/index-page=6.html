My 10m does not have the 10% haste buff, how much haste would I need to reach the plateau without it?

Short answer: It depends

Long answer:
These sort or questions are going to run rampant in this thread, so I'll do my best to lay out the calculation required for others to figure this out themselves. You may or may not decide to put this in an "Advanced" portion of your guide:

Steady Shot Cast Times
Desired Steady Shot Cast time (I): 1.50 seconds
Desired Steady Shot Cast time (II): 1.25 seconds

Haste Multipliers
Pathing (3 Point Survival Talent): 1.03
Raid Buff (Windfury, Hunting Party, Improved Icy Talons): 1.10
Improved Steady Shot (ISS) (3 Point Marksman Talent): 1.15

Total Haste Multiplier = (Pathing Haste Multiplier)*(Raid Buff Haste Multiplier)*(ISS Haste Multiplier)


To determine your required haste rating, use the appropriate calculation below:

Without T11 4-Piece Bonus
Base Steady Shot Cast Time: 2.0 seconds

To Reach Haste Plateau I (1.50 seconds)
Required Haste Rating = [(2.0/[1.50*Total Haste Multiplier]) - 1] * 12805.70

To Reach Haste Plateau II (1.25 seconds)
Required Haste Rating = [(2.0/[1.25*Total Haste Multiplier]) - 1] * 12805.70

With T11 4-Piece Bonus
Base Steady Shot Cast Time: 1.80 seconds

To Reach Haste Plateau I (1.50 seconds)
Required Haste Rating = [(1.8/[1.50*Total Haste Multiplier]) - 1] * 12805.70

To Reach Haste Plateau II (1.25 seconds)
Required Haste Rating = [(1.8/[1.25*Total Haste Multiplier]) - 1] * 12805.70
