Asking for a class to be nerfed for something that doesn't need to be nerfed is BS.


The primary use of FF is to keep rogues from stealthing after they drop combat. Sounds like you owe us an explanation of why FF is fine as it is instead of just your repeated insistence that it's working as intended.

Look, in Arenas and Rated BGs FF should be dispelled. If it stays up long enough to affect a rogue then something ELSE went horribly wrong (i.e. you let your healer die).

But in non-rated BGs and pre-85 BGs FF is a serious matter. I've lost games because CoS was on cooldown and I had to run around hiding instead of killing. And the chance of getting a healer in a pug smart enough to dispel is stupendously small.

So nerfing the duration of FF won't affect arena or rated BGs (since it should get dispelled anyway) but will improve the lot of all rogues in non-rated or pre-85 BGs. Sounds like a no-brainer to me!

But considering Blizz threw every non-rated and pre-85 WSG player under the bus with the GY change just for the benefit of a few rated BG whiners I suspect it will never happen.
