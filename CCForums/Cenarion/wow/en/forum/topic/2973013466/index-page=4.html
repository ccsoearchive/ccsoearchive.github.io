I am not demanding a change and I am not going to quit if it doesn't get changed. But, my main gripe with FF is this:

Yes, it can be cloaked out of or dispelled (but lets face it, random BGs the odds of a dispel are far and few in between).

The problem is cloak has a 2 min CD and FF has a 6 second CD. I cloak (which I do not have at this level and am not looking forward to getting into the level where ferals get FFF), and jump on you again. You just reapply it.

Now, lets say I jump you at 30% health because I can, and I kill you, but just before you die, you hit me with FF.

And there is no one around to dispel it.

I cannot re-stealth for 30-35 seconds. And a rogue out of stealth is about as useless as !@#$ on a boar hog.

I would personally like to see this changed to 20 second duration just because it neuters rogues so badly. And it isn't like it is going to run out before the druid can reapply it during a fight if he needs to do so.

Don't change what it does, just for how long. I think it is a reasonable request.
