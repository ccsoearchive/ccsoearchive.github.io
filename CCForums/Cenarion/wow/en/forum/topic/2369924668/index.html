There once was a rogue from Darnassus
Who saw a hunter in Camo, defenseless.
So she snuck up behind
To give a piece of her mind
But lo! Her Cheap Shot was useless.

So she fled from the spider who saw her
And fretted: Woe! Precious opener!
It's melee, yet ranged!
A paradox! I'm deranged!
Why is he immune, this darned hunter?

As she was kited, and killed, she lamented:
Is this working as Blizz had intended?
Through our griefs and our ganks,
Rogues meant well with our pranks!
Is Camo because we offended?
