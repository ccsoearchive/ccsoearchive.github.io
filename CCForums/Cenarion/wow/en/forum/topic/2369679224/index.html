I'm not disagreeing at all with what you've said, just wanted to add some cautionary tales around this here camp fire.

The auto-gem option sometimes wigs out and does odd things*, especially with cog-wheels, so after you use it make sure things make sense.

The auto-reforge is good, but this is better,

http://elitistjerks.com/f78/t115053-complete_rogue_reforger_tool/

A few times Shadowcraft was a little off for reforging my gear. It just can't do the number of iterations (can be literally hundreds of millions) that the reforge tool on your own PC can, due to bandwidth restrictions. I use both in tandem to make give myself a, "second opinion," as it were.

*Note, if the odd thing you observe is to not match a socket bonus you thought you should, trust Shadowcraft, it's because the dynamic EP values have shifted and as such the socket isn't worth going for over straight agility. This catches a lot of people who look at the static EP weights from EJ, or places referencing EJ. Both sets of EPs come from the same source.
