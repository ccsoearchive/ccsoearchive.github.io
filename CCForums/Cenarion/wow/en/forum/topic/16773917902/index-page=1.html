Onward! Three divisions exist within the business, all equipped for different situations, and all prepared to adjust accordingly. We may seem somewhat non-specific at first glance, but know that this is intentional, as we wish to be versatile in regard to how we work with you.

“Diversity: the art of thinking independently together.”

Yielding Veil Services is a medium-heavy role-playing guild, and we are open to active players who are passionate not only about role-play, but about the inhabitants of our server as well. Our group operates as a business, and we encourage the role-playing community at large to make use of our plethora of services.

What is the Yielding Veil?

Yielding Veil Services is a distinguished, privately owned business founded by Lady Shira Margolin. The company's contracts are customarily arranged by the administration and then assigned to one of three distinctive divisions. All in all, we intend to provide a professional environment for individuals from many walks of life, and of many incommensurable talents. Those of us within the Yielding Veil ultimately wish for the triumph of the “greater good,” as they say — we simply have our own way of reaching it.

Why separate the business into three divisions?

Our divisions remain crucial to our mission, and that is to provide each unique associate the opportunity to find a desirable career path within our company. We wish to accommodate characters, rather than force characters to accommodate us — everybody has to make a living, after all. To that end, we will not at any point have restrictions in place concerning in-game race or class.

What does each branch of the business aim to do, precisely?

The Chariot is the more upfront division of the Yielding Veil. The ignorant few might refer to them as nothing other than the muscle of the company, but that would be an unfortunate underestimation. This division simply addresses the needs of the clients in a more direct manner, and one would be remiss to dismiss these professionals as all brawn and no brains. Combatants of any background, whether martial or magical, would likely feel at ease within this division, and associates of the Chariot may find themselves acting as the security detail for a client of import, or even as debt collectors for those in need of payment. If the task at hand requires one to stride where others cower, it will fall to the Chariot.

The Star is a division outfitted for the pacificators amongst us, all with a shared fervor for knowledge. They represent the mental acuity of Yielding Veil Services, shining a light of clarity upon the chaos. They may be researchers, scholars, or diplomats, and typically people who seek conflict resolution through more amiable channels would be in their element here. Associates of the Star may be tasked to lend their collective insight to fellow employees and clients alike, as well as carry out arbitration assignments between disputing parties.

The Sun is the only non-profit division of the enterprise and is devoted solely to social service. Those of a more altruistic nature may find themselves drawn to the Sun, and will represent the humane, hopeful, and philanthropic attributes of Yielding Veil Services. Associates of this branch will be charged with lending sundry forms of aid to refugees, bolstering communities, and providing proper care to those in need, amongst other efforts.

Within the company lies a third branch called the Tower, the methods of which are unexpressed and ever-changing.
