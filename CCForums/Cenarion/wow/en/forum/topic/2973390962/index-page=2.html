I don't want to get rid of focus. I want to streamline it. Right now, we're too restricted by it.


Focus, inherently, is fine.

Steady/Cobra being slowed drastic amounts and poor base regen isn't. Been an advocate of making them immune to slows and moving some of the regen between shots and base.

Increase the base regen by X amount
Reduce the amount gained from Steady/Cobra
Make Steady/Cobra immune to slows

Using Survival as an example (it's what I know the best.)

Cobra Shot is brought down to a 1.66 second cast time and I fire 3 between Explosives (6 seconds.) That means I generate 27 Focus every 6 seconds from Cobra Shot and 24 (26.1 in my level of Haste) baseline. Giving me 51 Focus:6

Now let's move some of those values around. Let's say 6 (about 6.5 in my gear) Focus per second and 4 Focus per shot. With this model, I'm now generating 51 Focus every 6 seconds, the exact same as I was before.

Looking at that, it shows that the Focus generation can be adjusted without harming PvE. 6 Focus per second baseline with 4 Focus on shots gives the EXACT SAME PvE generation, thus providing the same exact rotations, while at the same time not being as punishing to those playing in PvP since they can't chain Steady/Cobras due to LoS and min range.

Can I haz cookie?
