Yes, they are my wishlist and not real

Marksman
- Chimera Shot no longer uses focus
- Arcane Shot now also applies Mortal Strike Debuff
- Steady Shot is no longer effected by slow effects
- Steady Shot no longer has a minimum range
- Sick'em resigned: Your pet's auto-attack critical cause you to regen 5/10 focus
- Improved Steady Shot buff now resets for every consecutive Steady Shot after the first two
- Silencing Shot now has a 40 yard range [bug fix]
- Piercing Shot is now a debuff and stacks for 1/2/3 times for each talent point
- Trueshot Aura: Increases the attack power of party and raid members within 100 yards by 10% and whenever you are struck by a Stun or Immobilize effect you generate 50 Focus and 2% of your total health over 10 sec.
- Termination effects targets below 35% health (up from 25%)
- Resistance is Futile works off of Hunter's Mark and Marked for Death
- Master Marksman ability redesigned: Every time you cast Steady Shot, you gain a Master Marksman buff which reduces the Focus & Cast time of Aimed Shot by 20% per stack. Stacks up to 5 times.
- Posthaste now also reduces the duration of all movement slowing effects by 30%/60%.
- Rapid Fire: Cooldown reduced to 2 mins (down from 5 mins), Haste reduced to 20% (down from 40%)

Survival
- Explosive Shot no longer uses focus
- Traps no longer have a cooldown. When a new trap is dropped, the effects of the previous trap in the same category disappear.
- Entrapment now puts an Entrapment debuff on the target for 25 seconds. When Entrapment debuff is on a player, they are not effected by Entrapment ability. This debuff can be cleansed.
- Freezing trap Freezes first target and creates an ice slick around itself for 30 sec All enemies within 10 yards will be slowed by 50% while in the area of effect. Trap will exist for 1 min
- Explosive Trap will now do additional damage to the enemy which triggers it.
- Ice Trap Removed
- Immolation Trap Removed.
- Trap Launcher Removed.
- Added Freezing Arrow: Launch a frost trap to the target location that creates bla bla bla
- Added Burning Arrow: Launch an explosive trap to the target location that creates bla bla bla
- Added Snake Arrow: Launch a snake trap to the target location that creates bla bla bla
- Explosive Shot & Black Arrow are now un-linked
- Arcane Shot is removed from Lock and Load
- Scatter Shot is now a 4 second stun
- Deterrence now usable while stunned.
- Camouflage now usable in combat and suppresses damage taken by 30%. Can not be broken by DoT abilities.
- Wing clip is now removed
- Counterattack no longer has a parry requirement. Cooldown is now 20 seconds.
- Wyvern Sting no longer applies a dot
- Snakes trap no longer applies a dot
- Raptor Strike, damage doubled and now returns 10 focus when used. Cooldown reduced to 3 seconds (down from 6)
- Track Giant's now makes you 50% smaller

Beast Mastery
- Beast Mastery now also reduces the cost of Kill Command by 100%
- Intimidation is no longer on the global cooldown and has a 20 yard range
- Kill Command now has an 8 yard range
- Cobra Shot is no longer effected by slow effects
- Cobra Shot no longer has a minimum range
- Widow Venom removed from game
- Aspects can be changed while controlled (stunned/poly'd/trapped)
- Aspect of the Fox: removed hidden cooldown when getting hit to regain focus
- Aspect of the Pack/Hawk: Daze effect removed but any damage taken removes the aspect and puts it on a 10 second cooldown.
- Added Aspect of the Monkey: Increases dodge by 20%
- Spirit bond now also reduce cast time and focus cost of Revive Pet by 25%/50%
- Killing Streak Buff now lasts 15 seconds
- The Beast Within now also makes the hunter feel no pity or remorse
- Kindred Spirit now also increases pet damage by 10%/20% when pet is within 8 yards of the hunter
- Beast Mastery now also increases pet and hunter attack power by 10% while pet is active

Pet Talents:
- Bullheaded cooldown reduced to 1 minute (down from 3 mins)
- Lick your Wounds cooldown reduced to 1 minute (down from 3 mins)
- Lion Hearted stun and fear effect reduction increased to 30%/60% (up from 15%/30%)
- Roar of Recovery returns 100 focus (up from 30) over 10 seconds (up from 9) and has a 1 minute cooldown (down from 3 mins)
- Taunt cooldown reduced to 30 seconds (down from 1 minute)

Focus, inherently, is fine.

Steady/Cobra being slowed drastic amounts and poor base regen isn't. Been an advocate of making them immune to slows and moving some of the regen between shots and base.

Increase the base regen by X amount
Reduce the amount gained from Steady/Cobra
Make Steady/Cobra immune to slows

Using Survival as an example (it's what I know the best.)

Cobra Shot is brought down to a 1.66 second cast time and I fire 3 between Explosives (6 seconds.) That means I generate 27 Focus every 6 seconds from Cobra Shot and 24 (26.1 in my level of Haste) baseline. Giving me 51 Focus:6

Now let's move some of those values around. Let's say 6 (about 6.5 in my gear) Focus per second and 4 Focus per shot. With this model, I'm now generating 51 Focus every 6 seconds, the exact same as I was before.

Looking at that, it shows that the Focus generation can be adjusted without harming PvE. 6 Focus per second baseline with 4 Focus on shots gives the EXACT SAME PvE generation, thus providing the same exact rotations, while at the same time not being as punishing to those playing in PvP since they can't chain Steady/Cobras due to LoS and min range.
