So me and my guild have been doing a lot of lore searching on Kal'dorei and other aspects of Azeroth and because of that I have re-written/completed Tal's complete history and such!

Talanus Skyfeather
-Age: 4,582

-When/where: Talanus was born and raised in Ashenvale, Kalimdor 3,000 years into the Long Vigil.

-Life before Role(Sentinel): As he grew, Tal' would be in the forest everyday, all day. He always found peace and quite, and especially loved being with the animals. When he become of age, he picked up a bow and started training everyday out in the forest, but he trained alone as all the other Kal'dorei thought him strange for not seeking friendship and some one to grow with. But he preferred it that way. Just him, a bow, and the animals amongst the forests. He also one day rescued a cat, Rak'shiri, from a trap set up by goblins, but as he released her, the goblin himself appeared and took aim to Tal'. But the cat quickly pounced the goblin and ripped him to shreds. The two have never left each others sides since. As he grew older, The Sisters of Elune started taking note of Tal's skills with the bow and commanding Luna. He was an adept at the art of a Hunter. Sentinels' began tasking him with simple little quests such as Killing off animals( Which he hated the most) to maintain population, Killing off Grel, and other corrupted beings. He soon would travel all of Kalimdor do do such minor tasks for them as well.

-Significant events/people: Talanus was in the War of the Shifting Sands under a special Force Team that would infiltrate and destroy, Talanus being the being under the command of Fandral Staghelm, he was one of the groups of people who would scout areas for Silithid, and would also try to find best routes as possible. Here he was able to put his training to good use, becoming more and more skilled with his bow, and even creating a powerful bond with Luna.
Between the long period after the Shifting Sands, Tal spent most of his time scouting out lands the Trolls had taken. It was his job to map there locations, frequently visited places, and at times, take them out. Here he became an adept at using his bow long range, using trees to hide in, while Luna shredded any who came near the tress they had "Taken up".
He was also requested help from the Sentinels at the Battle for Mount Hyjal (Warcraft 3, not the newest one from cata) where he witnessed the destruction of both Arichimonde and Nordrassil. . After words he was sent to the new world tree, Teldrassil, when it was created to help fend off the corrupted animals, demons and people. He Also has a main home there now as well, though he ever spends time in it. He next was once again requested by the Sentinels for his skills in the eradication of the Blue dragon flight in Norhtrend.

When it comes to people, again Tal' hardly had friends for he chose to stick to himself. He felt he trained better when it was only him and Luna, only when he was placed within groups during wars did he make friends, but they always ended up dying. Because of that, he decided to Isolate himself even more, not wanting to cause more pain and death to those around him.Tal did find a girl once. Lanoura Moonshade. It was during the fight for Hyjal where he had been an outrunner for the Sentinels. She was a huntress for the Sentinels, and they took a liking to each other. Even their companions got along well. When they had some time off during the war they would escape out and up deep into the mountains where they spent time together. But has fate would have it, during the final fight for Hyjal, the two were separated during battle, only to find her after battle with an orcish arrow lodged into her chest. He brought her bodie back to the base camp and gave her a proper burial. This made him Isolate himself even further, only communicating with others when in need of supplies.

-What led to your path (Sentinel) : Over centuries and centuries of training as a Hunter along with his companion, Luna, Tal' wished to do more but petty quests for others. His true desire was to protect the pure, to protect nature and it's beasts, and to most of all thwart those who sought to destroy it.Since then it was his a goal of his to join the ranks of Sentinels.

-Significant event for it?:After the War of the Shifting Sands, Tal' was placed in a task force with two others he soon befriended. They would scout lands for any trolls and other Horde members. His specialty being Tracking and deadly accuracy with a bow, his skills were crucial for this team. One night while running atop the trees, they had been spotted by a large group of Trolls who were traveling. The three battled troll after troll, trying to my haste to escape, but it was becoming to much and they were running out of time. Tal' quickly whistled out for his companion whom he had left at base camp. He has such a powerful bond with her that when he whistles out, he can speak to her directly, mind to mind. But it was almost too late. As he battled, he heard the other two fall and cry for help, he quickly laid arrow after arrow into his foes, but heard no more of their cries. He then felt two arrows puncture his skin and dive deep into his body. One in his back, the other through his shoulder. It was too much, he fell too his knees, and the last he saw was Luna dashing in, tearing up the remaining Horde. He fainted soon after. When he did awake, He had found himself in a Cenarion Circle refugee with few Sentinel Officers. They asked him for the report, and he gave it to them. After his wounds where healed, he found himself at yet another Burial ceremony. Soon after words, Tal' was asked to become a full member of the Sentinels and not just a 'paper boy'. He joined their ranks as an outrunner, but he did not join for himself, he joined to protect nature, to save those in the future who would need help.
After once again proving his skill and bravery in the battle of Hyjal, he was awarded the rank of Sentinel, which meant nothing to him without Lanoura beside him.
-Developement/Teacher: Talanus was all self taught. He had to teach himself everything due to his isolatism him as he grew up. But if he did have a teacher per say, it was Lanoura, they always helped each other, gave tips to each other, to better in their art. But he always found his true teacher to be nature. It guided his arrow, his string as it flung, everything he did he believed was due to nature, that he would be nothing without the whispers of the wind, the teachings of the trees.

-Hardships: Once again, due to Talanus Isolation, he never had true friends, and when he did, they always died. Even the one he wished to become his mate had been killed before him. And after her, did never bring another thought of finding a new mate.
Also, Talanus found many hardships in dealing with other Races. He once met a Human and a Dwarf during his travels. They seemed friendly, and so they traveled together for a little while. But it was a set up. They asked Tal' for beautiful sights of the Forests, but when Talanus showed them such, they quickly dispersed the trees into fire. Destroying much of the Forest. Heart Stricken that he had helped others in the destruction of his sacred land, Tal' never trusts any other race he meets that is Kal'dorei. He believes the other younger races are to easily corrupted. Nor does he begin to friend them until they prove themselves pure and trustworthy. This once again led to him being alone with only Luna by his side. But she is all he ever needs know.

-Events: War of the Shifting Sands, Battle for Mount Hyjal(past), and helped cure Teldrassil until it was blessed. Helped with the invasion of Northrend

-Hopes: Talanus hopes to create a name for himself throughout his people. He no longer wishes or their dissaproval or isolationism.

-Drives?: What drives Talanus is the hope of cleansing Azeroth, and to fight for the one he had finally found, but loss, Lanoura. He also is fights for the ones who no longer can fight, for the fallen.

-Goals?: Tal' seeks to become the most skilled Hunter amongst Azeroth. He wishes to be fully tuned with all animals and nature. He also seeks to become a Warden so he may further help stop the corrupted, and restore peace and order.

-Fears: Talanus's only fear is that of losing, of finally dying. For that he rushes full head forward, to face the fear, to become what many have doubted him of. He only seeks to purify and restore peace to Azeroth before his body and soul is claimed to the Dream World

-rest of Life: Talanus wants to spend the rest of his life amongst nature, being a sage to his people, and a trainer to new hunters. He wants to become the guide to the world, the voice for the young.

And here is a quick story on how I obtained Luna my companion

So there I was. A profound young Night Elf with great skill in Bows. But that's all I was. By now many of the other hunters had tamed there first companion, always showing them off, ignorant to the fact that their companion is more then just a fashion statement. We are Elves of the Forest, Beauty roams inside through us from nature and granted to us by the gods. Animals are as such as well. Oh well, what can I do about it. Their ignorance is only a flame that will grow stronger untill it proceeds to consume them one day. For instance I was told of a story of a brave hunter amongst our people, but he died because of one fact, he did not earn the respect of his companion, he only used it for its beauty. Nature is more philosophical then what it shows us. Well anyways, where was I..Ah yes, so there I was, starring out into my village for what would probably be the last time I would see this part of Ashenvale. I had just received a letter from a priestess asking for me to check up on the Northern part of Wintergrasp. The reason for my dismay is they had sent a few other of our Kal'dorei to the same location, but have yet to return within a full cycle of the moon. I guess a couple of our own dead doesn't mean anything to anyone yet. Oh well, Let Malorne guide the path that is set for me. Tomorrow I take my leave to this 'Wintergrasp'.
The travel was not that bad. It was peacefull being alone with just nature and the wondrous amounts of animals that occupied it. I would stop ever so often to find a pack of cats or owls. Always looking food they are. This i why I always keep a bag full of treats. It is filled with meats, grains, and anything else the gods provide us with, so either way the animals always got what they liked. But anyways back to the trip now. Now with every adventure there is always danger, and only being one thousand four hundred and eighty three, I had encountered many different dangers from thieves, trolls, and at times the animals. I did well to never harm them, I had found my self of finding their weakness, exposing it, and then making peace with them. An example I can offer is at one time in Darkshore I had encountered a DuskSaber. It was angry, roaring in furiousity, But I did what came natural to me. I dodged it' attacks, first looking to find why it was attacking me, which is usually there weakness. It turned out that it's cubs where up the trees next to me, and the mother only seeked to protect them. I quickly used the ancient Elven ability to meld myself into the surroundings, some call it 'Shadowmeld'. Then I sat in the same spot as to not give away my position, Sabers have a great sense of smell, but we Elves smell like our surroundings we live in, but a slight move sends our hormones through out our bodies, which is what the Sabers smell for to track us. Yes it took time and Studies to figure this one out. But as I sat the mother began to grow calm, and went back to trying to get her cubs down. but she was still big and round from having them, her weight was too much to carry up so she continued slipping down the tree. I waited for the right moment, and when it came, I struck. Of course if you have been listening you would know I am not one to harm what nature makes, so what I did was hug onto her neck, using my hands to quickly find her pressure point. Within seconds after I found it and squeezed it the mother Saber had collapsed onto the floor into a deep dream. I then climbed the trees, bringing each cub down carefully as I could. I sat them next to their mother of course so they could drink the milk she provided from her body as she slept. I waited for the next couple of minutes for the mother to wake. This is the most crucial part, for the cubs become very vulnerable to predators when the mother is asleep, so it was my duty to watch them. The mother finally sprung life into her eyes as she awoke, the cubs overjoyed that they could now tug on her ears playfully. She glanced over to me as I continued sitting eating bread the priestesses had provided me or my trip. I locked eyes with her, feeling her emotions, sensing her thoughts. What she had to say was simple.
"Thank you." But a thanks was not necessary, It is my job as a Kal'dorei to protect Nature and those who thrive peacefully upon it. I got up, slowly walking towards her, giving her a soft pat on her head saying,
" Be safe, teach them the ways to protect not only themselves, but you as well great beast of the wild."
