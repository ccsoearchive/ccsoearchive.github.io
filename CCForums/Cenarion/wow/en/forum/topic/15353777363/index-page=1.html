Attention!

I am sure you have heard the news; a portal has been opened to our world. They call themselves the “Iron Horde” and they have begun an assault through the Dark Portal of the Blasted Lands. They pour through that portal every second we delay a course of action. That is why this order has been formed.

The time for peace must be put to a hold. We must put aside our differences and unite as one single force. A force that will journey to this alternate Draenor and save the innocents that were slaughtered by the orcish armies of the past. The Draenei, Kaldorei, and Gilneans have mutual motives, and much experience with the orcs after all they have done to us.

Join the Wardens of the Forest! Defend our homelands, our nations, and the innocent. Rally against those who would dare to question our honor.

Until we meet on the battlefield,
Steria Rainsong

Who we are:

The Wardens are a brand spankin’ new heavy military roleplaying guild whom revolve around Kaldorei, Gilnean, and Draenei society and the safety of Azeroth as a whole. We destroy corruption at it's core; swiftly and justly. Our main focus at the moment is providing intriguing story-lines and campaigns that encourage character development for every individual participating. That being said, anything PvP/PvE is a long term goal and we’ll see how things play out.

We have weekly events where we initiate new wardens, discuss future campaigns, and gives guildies a chance to let their characters meet each other.

We are accepting all levels, classes, beginner-experienced roleplayers alike, but only Draenei, Worgen, and Night Elves. The only thing we require is a positive attitude and a willingness to participate in guild events, though we understand if you can’t make every meeting. After all, RL > WoW.

How to become a warden

Whisper one of our officers saying you wish to join. After a brief OOC interview, you will be invited as an 'Apprentice.' In order to be promoted to an actual warden, you must be present during one of our weekly guild meetings. At the end of the meeting, Steria or one of the officers will properly initiate your character into the guild.

In order to be initiated, your character must swear these vows,

As the moon rises, let it be my witness to these words.
My watch over the forests, the night skies, and the meek has now begun.
I am no champion or king, but a tool sworn to protect all who need protection.
From the forests of Teldrassil, to the mountains of Dun Morogh.
I pledge my life and honor to the wardens.

Why are we race restricted?

Originally we were planning on being a Kaldorei only guild, but there are several of those already present on Wyrmrest Accord. We included the presence of the Draenei and Worgen to spice up the roleplay; we all share similar motives and have worked together briefly in the past.
