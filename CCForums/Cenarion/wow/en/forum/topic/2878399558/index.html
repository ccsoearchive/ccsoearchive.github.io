Bullet's Comprehensive List to Fix Hunters

Base Abilities

-Trap Launcher: 20 Focus. Your next trap will launch at the enemy target, triggering it's effect. 1.5 -second cooldown.
-Raptor Strike: In addition, roots the target for 4 seconds. This effect has a 1 minute cooldown.
-Disengage: Suppresses movement impairing effects for 3 seconds.
-Kill Command: Range increased by 3 yards
-Aspect of the Hawk: Now provides 15% bonus RAP instead of a static amount.

Beast Mastery

-Own With Nature: Increase the RAP gained in Aspect of the Hawk by 1/3/5%
-Killing Streak: In addition, increases Kill Command's damage by 10% at all times.
-Kindred Spirits: In addition, when you cast Multi-Shot, your pet's next 2 Basic Attacks strike all enemies within 8 yards.
-Pathfinding removed. Improved Revive Pet returned!

Marksmanship

-Improved Steady Shot now lasts 12 seconds. Does not effect the cast speed of Cobra Shot.
-Sic'em: Now also procs from Kill Command criticals

Survival

-Explosive Shot; Damage increases 10%. Alternative change: Explosive Shot deals 120% weapon damage as Fire damage and for each second for 2 seconds, instead of dealing RAP
-Counterattack removed. New talent. Fire in the Hole (NNF). When Black Arrow is dispelled, Lock and Load is triggered. This effect does not obey the Lock and Load cooldown but resets the cooldown.
-TnT: Chance to proc from Black Arrow now 10/20%, up from 6/12%
-Improved Serpent Sting: Now also affects Black Arrow.
-Sniper Training: Increase the damage dealt by Steady Shot and Cobra Shot by 2/4/6% at all times, instead of while stationary.

Pets

-Dash/Dive: Now increase movement speed by 100%, up from 80%. Costs 20 Focus, down from 30.
-Wolverine Bite: Damage increased 50%

Glyphs

-Glyph of Deterrence: Increase the cooldown of Deterrence by 30 seconds, but each second Deterrence is active, the Hunter is healed 5% of max health. This effect can only occur once every 120 seconds.

@Vvolf

Survival, it's already behind by a nice margin in 25m with a growing gap due to scaling

Explosive Shot with weapon scaling is a way to ensure the spec as a whole scales going into later tiers. 130% looks like more than it really is. If you noticed there's no RAP portion and if you take out the RAP modification on it and just make it a 130% fire proc like a more powerful ticking version of our 2set bonus, it hits slightly harder than it does now but has long term scaling with weapon gains.

Improved Serpent Sting gives Black Arrow more synergy with the tree instead of just being kinda tacked on and to provide a slight damage bonus that Surv could use to keep up with MM.

TnT is to smooth out the LnL randomness. The difference between top Survival parses and mediocre Survival parses can come down to how many LnLs you got. If you were already lucky and getting a proc on almost every Black Arrow, you'd be unaffected.

Fire in the Hole and the change to Sniper Training would be strictly for PvP and would provide no PvE benefit. Survival can already keep Sniper Training up 100% of the time, but cannot in PvP.

@Power

I'll address them one by one then.

The Trap Launcher change would send the trap at the person like a shot would and it would trigger the shots effect on impact and at the location. Like if I chose to shoot an Explosive Trap at someone for w/e, it would impact them and drop it on their location. If I chose Ice trap, it would make the ice slick at their location. Freezing Trap would basically be Freezing Arrow when toggled.

For Deterrence, the reason for the glyph is that you would get the heal once every 2 minutes and could not Readiness+Deterrence for ridiculous amounts of health.

Raptor Strike change would basically be to give a tool in melee range, and the root could be put on a 45second to a 1 minute cooldown, similar to Improved Hamstring.

Disengage movement suppression would be to allow all 3 specs to capitalize on Disengage, not just MM.

Hawk numbers can be tweaked down to 10% base then up to 15% with Own With Nature. The idea of the post is to submit the ideas, not the exact fine tuning.

Explosive Shot damage increase would not be OP since it would not have an RAP coefficient added. It would JUST be 1xx% weapon damage ticks. 130 was just used for example. 125-130% or so would be to make the ticks hit about as hard as an Arcane Shot but for 3 ticks. If you notice with the T12 2pc, 80% weapon damage while using my Arathar only hits for a bout 6.8k damage with Curse of Elements up and full raid buffs. Again, the exact number is tuneable.

Remember, the weapon scaling was also offered as an alternative solution (though would be a much needed one.) They could also just..buff the damage 10-15%.

Sniper Training would just be fine tuning for PvP while not impacting PvE at all. Raiding Surv Hunters can already keep it up 100% of the time.

Dive/Dash would be to aid BM in faster target swapping.
