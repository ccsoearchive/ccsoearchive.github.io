Just thought I'd chime in with my ideas. I'm just a casual noob player, and leveling this Hunter was the most fun I've had in WoW. I don't do any serious PVP or PVE, just purely casual, but even I can notice that the Hunter trees and abilities seem... incomplete and rushed. Anyway, my ideas:

General Changes:
- Passive skill at 60 called "Zen". For every 10 focus you have, gain 1% bonus to damage, hit and crit chance.
- Kill Command is no longer a baseline ability and is now a Beast Mastery special ability granted when choosing the specialization
- Intimidate is no longer a Beast Mastery specialization ability and is now baseline.
- Pets now gain a stat bonus when fed food that it favors
- Scatter Shot now knocks back opponents 10 yards in addition to existing effect.
- Trap Launcher is now a modal ability. Toggle the ability on and off to switch between launching traps and laying down traps at your feet
- Traps reworked to make to give them more of a 'hunter' flavor with some changes in effects. Each trap is now on its own cooldown
- Freezing Trap is now a Net Trap, no change in effect
- Ice Trap is now Caltrops Trap and also deals physical damage to affected targets and slows their movement speed
- Immolation Trap is now Bear Trap and deals bleed damage over time and slows down movement speed
- Explosive Trap is now Poison Trap and releases poison gas in the area that deals nature damage to affected targets
- Snake Trap is now Flashbang Trap and triggers an AOE blind affect on affected targets for 5 seconds (3 minute cooldown)
- Widow Venom is now stackable up to 3 times for up to 30% healing reduction
- Aspect of the Pack removed
- Apsect of the Cheetah reworked to provide a passive 30% movement speed increase and reduces the duration and effect of movement impairing effects by 20%. While in this mode, you may not use any offensive abilities

Survival Talents - Hunter utility and survivability tree.
- Explosive shot damage modified to include weapon damage
- Hunter vs. Wild now also provides 2/4/6% spell resistance in addition to existing effects
- Survival Tactics now also reduces cooldown of traps
- Resourcefulness redesigned. It is now an activated skill that creates a 'healing kit' that when consumed, heals the Hunter for 10/20/30% of his total health
- Counterattack removed and replaced with new talent 'High Impact Shot (2 points)'. This talent gives your explosive shot a chance to stun the enemy for 1 or 2 seconds
- Wyvern Sting no longer applies a damage over time effect when it ends. Instead it will apply an affect called 'Lethargy' that will reduce casting and movement speed by 20% for 5 seconds
- Serpent Spread is now a single point talent
- Black Arrow when dispelled, explodes in a fury of shadow damage, affecting all enemies in a 10 yard radius for the spell's remaining damage

Marksman - Focusing on increasing ranged damage with less emphasis on pets
- Go For the Throat and Sic'em now also provides the Hunter with 2 focus whenever you crit with any attack
- Master Marksman redesigned to now provide a 6/8/10% chance when casting steady shot to make your next Aimed shot instant and cost no focus
- Resistance is Futile removed and replaced by a talent called "Lone Wolf". When the Hunter has no pet, he gains 10/20% damage increase

Beast Mastery
- Kill Command now has a 10 yard range and will cause your pet to 'pounce' to the target
- Passive Specialization Bonus called 'Animal Handler' now gives you 4 more points to spend on your pet's talents.
- Pathfinding modified to give you and your pet a passive 5/10% chance to resist movement impairing effects
- Focus Fire modified and unlinked with Frenzy. Focus fire reduces the focus cost of your pets Basic Attack by 10 and your Arcane shot by 5 when you are attacking the same target
- Fervor replaced by 'Rampage'. Rampage will cause your pet's next Basic Attack to hit all targets within 8 yards, but increases the focus cost by 50%
- Killing Streak modified to include pet's Basic Attacks
- Beast Mastery talent removed and replaced by Stampede! (Not my original idea -- got it from another poster in Bullet's thread). Summons all your active pets to attack your enemies. Pets can only use their basic attack and will not gain benefit from any other beast mastery talent. (Think Army of the Dead, Treants or Spirit Wolves)
