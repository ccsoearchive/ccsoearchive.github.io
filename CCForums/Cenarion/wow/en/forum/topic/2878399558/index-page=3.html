Marksmanship
I found that MM is the tree that needs the least amount of changes, as everything within it looks pretty good to me. However, I do have two suggestions:

Improved Steady Shot: Effect now lasts 12 seconds up from 8.
Why? Assuming that the talent Termination is taken, once a Hunter passes the 25% mark on an enemy, their rotation goes haywire from the massive influx of focus. AN extra four seconds of breathing room is a good thing in this situation.
Resistance is Futile: Redesigned. When a target affected by Hunter’s Mark or Marked for Death attempts to move or flee, you have a 1/2% chance per second of movement to allow you to cast a Kill Shot as if the target were below 20% health, but this attack cannot critically hit.
Why? First, with Kill Command now a BM-only ability, this talent’s current iteration seems completely useless. Second, MM Hunters with this talent currently have to fight to make sure their Hunter’s Mark is the one on the target in order for this talent to work. I actually specced OUT of this talent and into Concussive Barrage instead, because that talent actually has some use. By allowing this talent to proc from any Hunter’s Mark or Marked for Death effect, there’s no more squabbling. Finally, this Kill Shot can’t crit because MM’s damage is already high enough. Let’s not make it even higher (hence the low proc chance).


Survival

Survival is definitely a tree where I can’t think of that many improvements to make. I have some suggestions, though..

Counterattack: Redesigned. When you Disengage, you deal 120% of your ranged weapon damage to all enemies within 5 yards of you.
Why? Counterattack in its current state is a big, fat joke. It’s a laughable relic from the days of Vanilla, when Survival was the melee tree. The current version needs to go, and in its place comes something I think will help Survival PvP by letting them deal damage as they get out of the red zone.
(the next three changes go hand-in-hand, you’ll see why)
Lock and Load: Now only affects Explosive Shot and not Arcane Shot.
Thrill of the Hunt: No longer affects Arcane Shot.
TNT: When the periodic effect of Explosive Trap, Immolation Trap, or Black Arrow expires, it will always proc Lock and Load.
Why? Currently, Kill Command is the Focus dump of choice when you reach 100 Focus during lock and Load. But with that ability now exclusive to BM, Arcane Shot must take it place. Furthermore, having Lock and Load allow for free Arcane Shots seems silly to me when Explosive Shot is learned at Level 10. Since Arcane Shot is now the focus cap dump, the focus return chance from it needs to be removed from Thrill of the Hunt. Finally, the TNT change is to make sure that at the very least, Lock and Load procs every 24 seconds. There is nothing more frustrating than watching Black Arrow go its full duration and not seeing LnL proc.
Toxicology: In addition to current effects, When your Serpent Sting deals periodic damage, you are healed for 1/2% of your maximum health. This effect does not work on more than one target.
Why? BM has a self-heal and bonus healing. MM has a self-heal. SV needs some self-healing as well, and with a name like SURVIVAL, it should have the arguably best self-healing of the three specs.

Pets:

Ferocity:
Heart of the Phoenix, Lick your Wounds: These talents are no longer connected to Bloodthirsty.
Why? These are two terrific talents, especially Heart of the Phoenix. Unfortunately, getting to it requires spending an extra talent point that could be put to better use elsewhere, even as BM. Having these talents connected to each other is fine with me, but not to Bloodthisty.

Cunning:
Wolverine Bite: This ability can be used after you make a critical strike, as well as your pet. Damage increased.
Why? This ability does crap for damage, but how much to increase it is beyond me. Making it available every cooldown? That sounds much better to me.
