4. New 31 point talent: I Need More Explosions! Well the thing with Survival is it fits into a playstyle of "love it or hate it" but when you break down the individual parts, it is a relatively fluid spec is one of the better designed specs when it comes to the rotational flow.

Another nice thing is with Survival is that, sans PvP issues, it only needs tune ups. Damage and scaling adjustments would bring it right back up to a competitive level.

As for BM, it's in the same boat as Surv. The prio system works. It feels really fluid and has a nice smooth flow. But the numbers just aren't there, coupled with lack of AoE and severely hindered by constant target movement or target swapping. It just needs some adjustments (what I outlined in the op would correct most of it.)

Most of Hunter issues can actually be fixed without providing drastically new talents or complete overhauls. The only new talent that actually could really be added is something along the "Fire in the Hole" talent that replaces Counterattack in order to give Survival some needed protection and burst.

Survival: Scaling and PvP issues. Making Explo Shot scale with weapon damage, smoothing out randomness by slightly increasing the proc chance on Lock and Load through TnT, and adding Black Arrow to Imp Serpent Sting would basically address the damage. Fire in the Hole and adjustment to Sniper Training are ways to bring it closer to PvP viability while having zero positive or negative influence on the PvE scene.

Beast Mastery: Scaling and mechanical issues. Adding 3 yards to Kill Command would stop the issue of the pet getting caught in the 6 yard deadzone on moving targets where it cannot Kill Command or melee attack. The idea for Kindred Spirits would be a way for BM to have reliable AoE so it can be effective on fights like Beth'tilac and Rhyolith while keeping to spec flavor. Killing Streak damage addition would be for a little extra oomph.

Marksmanship: Clunk. Adjustment to Improved Steady Shot would cut down on some of the clunkiness. Adding Kill Command to Sic'em would be a way to help BM not stay focus starved. BM relies on extended periods of Wild Hunt uptime to stay anywhere near "competitive."

Pets: The change to Dash/Dive would be just mainly be an aid to BM to get their pets from target A to target B faster. Wolverine Bite would try to smooth out large gap between Ferocity and Cunning for BM Hunters.

Base: Aspect of the Hawk scaling issues. Currently Hawk just stays a static value and as such, loses a lot of it's usefulness later on. By making it 15% with 5% (with 3 points) would make it almost identical in RAP gained now but keep Aspect of the Hawk's edge in the later tiers as well. 20% (with OwN maxed) is exactly how much Hawk provides Survival in full raid buffs by Firelands gear. It's also the last in the line of BM buffs to give it better scaling (which is desperately needed.)

Bullet's Comprehensive List to Fix Hunters

Base Abilities

-Trap Launcher: 20 Focus. Your next trap will launch at the enemy target, triggering it's effect. 1.5 -second cooldown.

Putting it in Wing Clip makes more sense.

-Disengage: Suppresses movement impairing effects for 3 seconds.
-Kill Command: Range increased by 3 yards
-Aspect of the Hawk: Now provides 15% bonus RAP instead of a static amount.

Good ideas.

Beast Mastery

-Own With Nature: Increase the RAP gained in Aspect of the Hawk by 1/3/5%
-Killing Streak: In addition, increases Kill Command's damage by 10% at all times.
-Kindred Spirits: In addition, when you cast Multi-Shot, your pet's next 2 Basic Attacks strike all enemies within 8 yards.
-Pathfinding removed. Improved Revive Pet returned!


Good, good, good and good.

Marksmanship

-Improved Steady Shot now lasts 12 seconds. Does not effect the cast speed of Cobra Shot.
-Sic'em: Now also procs from Kill Command criticals

Still good.

Survival

-Explosive Shot; Damage increases 10%. Alternative change: Explosive Shot deals 120% weapon damage as Fire damage and for each second for 2 seconds, instead of dealing RAP
-Counterattack removed. New talent. Fire in the Hole (NNF). When Black Arrow is dispelled, Lock and Load is triggered. This effect does not obey the Lock and Load cooldown but resets the cooldown.
-TnT: Chance to proc from Black Arrow now 10/20%, up from 6/12%
-Improved Serpent Sting: Now also affects Black Arrow.
-Sniper Training: Increase the damage dealt by Steady Shot and Cobra Shot by 2/4/6% at all times, instead of while stationary.

Gaming Sniper Training is so easy the change wouldn't be noticed much, but it'd be nice nonetheless.

Pets

-Dash/Dive: Now increase movement speed by 100%, up from 80%. Costs 20 Focus, down from 30.
-Wolverine Bite: Damage increased 50%

Win/Win

Glyphs

-Glyph of Deterrence: Increase the cooldown of Deterrence by 30 seconds, but each second Deterrence is active, the Hunter is healed 5% of max health. This effect can only occur once every 120 seconds.
