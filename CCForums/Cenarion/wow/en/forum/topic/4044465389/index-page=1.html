Well, I've put this off long enough.

It's been a good long run on Cenarion Circle, and now it's time to say good-bye.

I leave a happy player. Cenarion Circle has been a good, active home all my years, from my early days with the Erinyes Sisterhood on up through running the AAMS on Aeldgyth/Andelia.

(No, I still don't have a reason for all the names starting with A. It just happened that way.)

I wish I could stay. Life is too busy for that now. I don't see that changing any time soon, so alas this is goodbye for good.

It's been a wild ride. The Erinyes Sisterhood, Modas il Toralar, Da Doctas, Modas il Toralar again, Alterac University, Homeland, AAMS and AAMS Alliance Branch -- more opportunity than any scandal-laden troll could ever wish for.

Thank you all. I will always be glad I let friends bully me into trying a blocky-looking online fantasy game however-many years ago. It's been a wonderful way to waste my time and yours.

All the Best,

Arj
