DR will reset at 15 seconds past the end of the duration of a CC. Due to latency and the way the game checks for DR resets, you generally add about three seconds to the end of that just to be safe.

If you use a CC that's in the same category as one you've already used, that 15 seconds restarts at the end of the CC that is used.

You can use addons or the in-game stopwatch to track them to make things easier.

If you have any questions on DR's or what you can use to track them, feel free to make a thread (don't wanna derail this one too terribly much), and I, along with others, can probably help you figure out the best way to track them and help you achieve a better understanding of the matter. :)
