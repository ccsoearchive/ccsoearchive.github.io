Not gonna lie, I agree with Doctadubz. I have no problem with ShS on that tier with Burst of Speed.

In fact, in PvP I planned on taking Burst of Speed OVER ShS in BGs, the ability to both break snares/roots and become immune to them, AND to also after using a second time get Sprint status (in addition to Shadow Focus letting us have that +70% runspeed PERMANENTLY in stealth due to Burst having no CD and it costing no energy) sounds so much fun and more useful than ShS.

My problem, as a PvE Subt rogue, is having to choose between a pair of fun mobility CDs, and a required DPS CD. In PvP, almost every rogue will choose Prep, because Prep gives you an extra Sprint, Cloak, Evasion, Vanish, Bomb, AND Dismantle, giving you tons of survivability and added mobility thru Sprint. Even it's long CD doesn't deter it from completely outclassing the other 2 for PvP.

In PvE, Assn and Sub HAVE to pick Prep up. It is a DPS cooldown for both specs. Assn for Overkill, and Sub for Master of Subtlety/Find Weakness. Combat gets to go willy nilly and pick whatever they want. The other 2 specs are bottlenecked into having it.

Personally, I would rather Prep removed from the game, given to Sub only, Assn only, or given to all 3 specs, and balanced around that. Prefer honestly it only refreshing Kick, Dismantle, Sprint, and Vanish. Maybe bomb and/or cloak.
