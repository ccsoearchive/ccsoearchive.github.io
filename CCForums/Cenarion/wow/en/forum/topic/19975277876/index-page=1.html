Mondays only for now

Abandoned Tavern, Rustberg Village Tol Barad (( http://imgur.com/z3lrIPu ))

The Steadfast’s Rest is now open! A newly renovated tavern in the middle of Rustberg Village in Tol Barad is proud to serve you the finest drinks available! From Dwarven ale to famous Shattercast Wines, we have it all and it is all served by the ever efficient Valkenhar Barov, the Butler Extraordinair. While we understand that our tavern is not in the most friendly place in the world, do not fret. Valkenhar will either come escort you to the tavern, or he will bribe some adventurer’s with drinks to do so. Food will be served as soon as a decent cook is found!

Some amenities include
A house for rent
Beds for when you are far too drunk to get home
Sparring area (Keep your fights out of my bar!)
Event hall (Perfect for guild meetings!)
Lots of delicious booze!

(( My first attempt at a player run tavern, please be gentle! If you do come to the Steadfast I ask that you do not start fights in my tavern, if you feel you must fight take it outside! If you wish to participate in the tavern, whisper or mail me ingame! We will be starting on the 23rd of Oct if you wish to join us! ))

UPDATE:

We have some new staff joining us at the Steadfast's Rest!

Aeleeda is our new waitress and bartender in training, Aldravia is our new cook and will be providing all sorts of delicious food for you if you come in!
