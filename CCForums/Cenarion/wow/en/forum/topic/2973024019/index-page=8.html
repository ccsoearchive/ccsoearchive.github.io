So that's really the question, right? Is it worth it to fuss about PvP problems? There are (and have been) multiple threads on this forum and I assume many on other forums as well in which someone makes a complaint about PvP. Others agree or disagree about the complaint, and then one or more posters adds that WoW is essientially a PvE-focused game.

Then, PvP'ers argue vehemently that it isn't or shouldn't be primarily a PvE game while others argue the opposite. Finally, both sides get angry with one another because they're both essentially just speculating on the developers' investment in PvP in WoW.

This is obviously something no player can answer, but I guess my question is, "Is it worth it to complain about broad PvP concerns in WoW?" Phrased a different way (and I think this is the center of the implied paradox that the OP is illustrating by making a forum post to the devs about how he feels ignored by the devs), "How much do the devs care about WoW being a good PvP game, and how much work are they willing to put in to make that happen?"
