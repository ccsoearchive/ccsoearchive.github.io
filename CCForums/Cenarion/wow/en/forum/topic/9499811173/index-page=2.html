Fun.

This was fun again, because the elf wasn't boring. Obvious perhaps. Foolish for sure, but he fought doggedly and did not disappoint. For how long though? It was only a matter of minutes before his armor would crack under the extreme cold and heat transfers now that the disenchantment was wrapping up. Then what? He'd still stand there willing to fight, thinking he had what it took when even he was too young to understand the meaning of experience.

Oh, she laughed to herself from atop the iceberg. Visible again as she watched some of her familiars vanish from sight as they were impacted by their own weapons, while the two elementals absorbed the attack and growled hungrier and with the power frost fed them. He must of thought she was younger. Much younger.

Or perhaps he was suicidal. That's less fun though. And certainly boring. No, he was mistaken.

She laughed louder now. Calling the elf's attention to her position. She crossed her legs and folded her hands in her lap, speaking in his tongue.

"You fight well, if a tad bit obviously. It is all so familiar. Perhaps you could be more creative, like with the return volley. Besides how old are you child? Have you even met someone as experienced as me? I really am curious, because this ends only one way..."

An image stood behind the elf, identical to Izby down to the pores in her skin and she leaned forward to whisper in his ear.

"...with me walking away from you, back turned, and disappointed."

Meanwhile.

Another image appeared beside Nyuula and Sizy. A hand on their shoulders. "I will not eat him. That is not something we do as Nyuula said. The Small One is safe now in your hands, child?"
