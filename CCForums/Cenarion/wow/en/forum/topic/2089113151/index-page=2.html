Great start on the guide. I would add that gouge+smoke bomb can be used for a "free" restealth if LOS is unavailable.

Also for the sap macro, I have found that it is better to have it like this:

#showtooltip
/cleartarget [nomod]
/targetnearestenemyplayer [nomod]
/cast sap

This will do 2 things differently. 1) it will select enemy players only (no pets, totems, elementals etc). 2) If you really need to get a sap on one dude in a crowd, simply hold a mod key (shift, ctrl, alt) and it will not clear target and sap whoever you have targeted.

Not much on glyphs, here goes. Following from http://www.arenajunkies.com/topic/185898-guide-the-art-of-roguecraft/ with a couple of my additions.

Mutilate

Prime Glyphs:

Must-haves: Mutilate
Viable: Slice and Dice, Rupture, Vendetta

Notes - Rupture glyph is very handy for putting just a 1-2 cp rupture on someone, extending the duration and making it so you don't require a high cp rupture.

Major Glyphs:

Must-haves: Garrote
Viable: Sprint, Gouge, Cloak of Shadows, Blind, Fan of Knives and Kick

Notes - All of the Viable glyphs have usefulness that can only be defined by your playstyle. Unless you're a kick god, however, I personally wouldn't recommend the kick glyph. Also note, glyph of blind is recommended unless you're awesome and making sure there are no DoT's (your deadly poison or another persons dots) on the target before you blind. Idiot proof, so to speak XD.

Sub

Prime Glyphs:
Shadow Dance, Backstab, Hemorrhage

Notes - Not much lee-way here, some people prefer to swap out Shadow Dance for SnD glyph.

Major Glyphs:
Garrote, Preparation, Blind (arguable), Gouge

Notes - Prep glyph is pretty funky, prep kicks can be absolutely sensational. I added gouge glyph as I play with australian ping and that makes it a whole lot easier. Also note that it stops good players from turning their backs to you whilst casting that heal.

Note to editor - You're probs gunna want to put some underlines in there to tidy it up :P Hope I've been helpful!

There's a use for Gouge, if you have it talented with Improved Gouge, to negate ennemy roots.

Most roots last 6 seconds, and so does Improved Gouge. With a bit of quick response, you can gouge your opponent as he roots you and simply wait it out to resum your fight once both the root and Gouge have dissipated.
