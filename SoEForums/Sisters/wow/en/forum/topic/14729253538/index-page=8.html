Guys, I really don't like having to stress this, but please, please follow the template on the first post of this thread for your guild if you want it listed. I know you guys really like to post your pre-written stuff and your big descriptions about what's going on with your guild, but if it's too long or can't conform to the template, it isn't going to work here. I am looking for short, concise, and factual. If there's one thing I don't enjoy it's butchering your posts.

This isn't meant to be a full blown ad for your guild - it's supposed to act like a hook, and those that do get hooked by your guild listing will certainly research further than just this thread. That is what the "Recruitment" and "Website" sections are for. Please stick to your own guild advertisement threads and websites when it comes to the detailed stuff.
