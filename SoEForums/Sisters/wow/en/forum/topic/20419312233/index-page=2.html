Hey, Cianyr. We're actually working on the system till. So far, we can say, no one has died. In fact, we've had the opposite of that problem. We've been rolling too well and it's almost no fun.

But, to address this. I don't enforce the death rule, by simply being present - This dose not mean we do not use it or your character can't die, but in the system, once you hit a certain amount of points you enter in 'Critical' state. This means, your character is knocked out or can't fight. However, if you choose so. You can take the risk to keep fighting, with five points of health left. However, if you lose those 5 points, THEN you're dead.

But, I understand being quite attached to your character. Death is a rare case, but it's something we added to have that risk to adventure. Adventure! Risk! Rewards!
