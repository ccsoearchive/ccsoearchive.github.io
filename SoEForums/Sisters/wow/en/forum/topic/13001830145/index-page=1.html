This is a general shout out to any and all Alliance players on Cenarion Circle and Sisters of Elune that run Battlegrounds or have an interest in running Battlegrounds. The purpose of this Call to Arms is simple. I want to build a network between the max level Alli PVP players on this server so we can easily and readily form premade groups when we are on.

Ideally I would like us to get to a point where at least 3 (preferably 5+) of us commit ahead of schedule to a weekly PVP night. If we happen to get a big enough group and our characters are geared for it we can move onto doing rated Battlegrounds but for the time being I envision us sticking to running randoms.

Also don’t worry if you are intrigued by BGs but don’t have PVP gear or have had bad experiences with Alli PVP in the past. You’re still fully welcomed to run with us and I, along with others, will gladly give you tips on good places to get honor on the side, how to obtain PVP gear ect… Besides I assure you that doing BGs with several friends is a very different and far more enjoyable experience than running them alone.

If you are interested please post a reply to this thread or get in touch with me in game. We can then start adding one another to our friends or B ID list to further expand the network. I've also made an official Alliance PVP group on the Cenarion Circle dotOrg site called Alliance Army. You can find it at this link:

http://community.cenarion-circle.org/viewgroup/2-alliance-army

If you have a dotOrg account then please go there to sign up as well. There are numerous discussions already posted which cover PVP topics like specific battlegrounds and ways to obtain gear.

Also, for those who do not PVP much I still recommend solo queing for Isle of Conquest. It is a 40 man BG with a PVE objective and the Horde side usually has a lot of badly programmed bots (ai programmed players). While it can be a bit crazy if you've never done it so long as you follow the big rush of people going towards mid (the workshop) or towards the west (docks) you'll usually be fine.

How you specifically que for a Call of Arms BG is by clicking on the PVP icon. You'll see a screen with four large bars on the right hand side. The Call of Arms BG , Isle of Conquest, will be the second one down from the top. Simply highlight it then que by pressing the button at the bottom of the PVP screen.
