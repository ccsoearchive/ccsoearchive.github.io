Greetins' Dwarves of Wyrmrest! So due to popular request, I've created a forum post that for now on will contain the dates and information on every future community Clan Moot! The Clan Moots are Dwarven community RP events. Events where every Dwarven player, and the few non-dwarves we've officially named "dwarf-friends" are welcome. They are held on a monthly basis and hosted by the presiding guild who's leader holds the title of Grand Thane.

The Grand Thane title was initially just a nickname but it has since stuck, and is fitting for the current leader of the Dwarven Community! The Grand Thane is expected to preside over the community chat channel we've established for ourselves, as well as continue to spread news and look to recruit as many Dwarves possible into the Community! Working with the other guild leaders to establish public events, working for the benefit of not just the dwarves in the Clan Guilds, but all dwarven players period. The posistion is not permenant, and soon we will hold elections to put a fourth a new Grand Thane, elected amongst the Clans.

The Clan Moot itself is a mobile event, though it is preferred to be held in an outdoor area of what is current content. Originally, the first Clan Moot was held in Cataclysm, in an ancient tomb in the bad lands, neutral ground of sorts between Ironforge, Shadowforge, and Aerie Peak. The event itself is divided into three phases, the first is exhibition duels! These are actual PvP /duels, not for any major reward but merely for the chance to show off your skills in the community and personal glory for your Clan/Guild. The first phase also includes intermingling of the clans, which is just wandering around from your current circle of comfort and introducing yourself to the nearby Dwarven players outside of your guild, and hopefully creating IC connections or perhaps setting up a storyline.

The second phase is where the current Grand Thane will hear the concerns and ideas of the Dwarven Community, as well as sending out teams of Dwarves for IC missions, storylines and events on a monthly basis. During this phase there will also be IC sermons held for the major religions of the Dwarves, the Titans, the Light, and the ancestors and elements.

The third and final phase is a leader's meeting, where the current dwarven guild leaders or representatives of large groups will come to a location designated by the Grand Thane and discuss OOC matters if need be and engage in some exciting Dwarf Politics! Which always run with the risk of blood feuds coming to the surface, racism and stubborn dwarven pride, and perhaps a drunken brawl, good times!

These Clan Moots are a product designed for the Dwarven Community, and it's my hope that we'll all go stronger and more close knit as a player base because of it! Thank ye' for th' time you've taken to read this post, glory to th' Dwarves!
