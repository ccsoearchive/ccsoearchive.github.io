Hello denizens of Wyrmrest Accord! This post is to announce the planned server event of The Queens Hour. This is a two part event intended to promote Forsaken roleplay on the server. The main focus of this event will be to encourage players from both Horde and Alliance to roll a level 1 Forsaken for one evening and experience the community, culture and RP.

The tentative dates for the events are as follows:
The Harvest: 20th of December
The Raising: 13th of January

Please feel free to view these documents for a full write up of each event:
The Harvest: www.tinyurl.com/wraharvest
The Raising: www.tinyurl.com/wraraising

Main points of contact: Sillaine, Branntherick, Keleril
Other points of contact: Dayon, Tesi, Kelios

Below you will find the necessary info for each event, enjoy!
---------------------------------------------

The Harvest: Navigation Key

    Basic Info:
    Event Type: Open to server world scavenger event
    Locations: Start in Hinterlands - End in Arathi Highlands
    Event date: December 20th
    Event time: 7 PM - 8:30 PM server time(roughly)


    Event Premise:
    It’s the time before The Raising and the Forsaken are doing one last push to gather the bodies, limbs and leftover wealth of unfortunate adventurers buried in mass through graves across Azeroth. Word has reached Alliance ears that the Forsaken are on the move, digging up the dead. Now it falls to them to defend the fallen from the grasps of the devious Forsaken.


    The Nature of the Event:
    This is a fun and casual event meant to bridge the time between the announcement of the The Raising and the actual event. Much of the event will be OOC, the clues (discussed below) are given completely out of character to the raid group. Players will be asked to elect a leader IC and will also be encouraged to find the locations of the nodes via RP. Anyone who attends is also welcome to adopt the narrative of this event into their character's personal story.


    Event Breakdown:
    Scattered across The Hinterlands and Arathi Highlands are 5 nodes which must either be captured or defended depending on your team. The locations of these nodes will only be revealed through vague clues and riddles. It is up to each team to work together to try and crack the clue in order to discover the nodes location. After discovering a node the team will have a period of 2 mins in which they must reinforce the node. In the event that a node becomes contested each team will elect a Champion to fight on behalf of the team. This fight will be determined by a 1v1 duel done in grey armor (provided by the raid coordinators). This will be the only PVP of the event.

    During the time of the 1v1 duel the players on each team are strongly encouraged to equip the Dazzling Rod toy. Each hit your character takes from the toy may be interpreted in RP as a hit your character has taken in the skirmish of the node.

    The winner of the duel will decide which team claims the node. After a node has been successfully captured/defended the winning team will have a 15 second head start with the clue to the next node. Both teams will however always be competing for the same node to ensure a sense of fun and fairness.

    Note: Once a champion has fought they will not be able to fight again so teams must choose their champions strategically.


    How to Interpret This Event In RP:
    The narrative we would like to establish is that the graveyards are a place of battle between the Horde and Alliance. The war in Draenor is coming to a close but not all have returned from the front lines; therefore the battles in this event should be considered as skirmishes instead of massive military movements.

    In the pursuit of good natured entertainment we have chosen to not make this event a PVP event. However, if your character is involved in this event then you are free to take whatever hits against your character during the Dazzling Rod duels as injuries they received during the conflict. Feel free to be creative.


    DISCLAIMER: If there is a larger turnout for this event then initially expected the 1v1 duel at each node will change to 3 duels of 1v1 where the team with the majority of wins from the duels takes the node. If it is necessary under this system Champions will be able to fight multiple times.

  • Basic Info
  • Event Premise
  • Event Breakdown

    • ....

    Basic Info:
    Event Type: Open to server social event
    Location: Deathknell
    Event date: January 13th
    Event time: 6 PM - 9 PM server time


    Event Premise:
    With the campaign into Draenor coming to a close and another war behind them, the Forsaken are gathering in their homelands to perform The Raising-- an event centered around the mass resurrection of corpses retrieved from the War Front. Such an event is vital to the future of the Forsaken; as such civilians and war makers alike from across the Empire gather at the place of The Raising for a time of celebration and persuasion-- after all not all fresh from the grave are keen to join the Queens ranks.


    Event Breakdown:
    Players are invited to come and experience Forsaken RP and culture in a night of social fun. Reroll a brand new Forsaken, come on an alt, or hang out on your main and meet some of the resident Forsaken roleplayers and guilds!

    Things To Do:
  • Merchants Market: Artisans and armourers come together to sell their wares at this large gathering of Forsaken. Common goods found in these markets range from armour and weapons to spare body parts!
    • ...
  • Apothecary Station:[Newly risen Forsaken can make their way over to the Apothecary station to be patched up. Learn lessons on Shadow-mending, concoction brewing (mini game), stitching, and more!
    • ...
  • Meet the Cult:Come join the Cult for an open floor styled event. Ask questions about the Cult, hear the words of Cult leaders and guest speakers. Learn the basics and join the Cult!
    • ...
  • Military Training: Join the ranks of the Forsaken military! Learn basic combat tactics, drills, and marching orders. Have your strength and will tested! Understand what it means to pledge soul and blade to the Queen and Empire.
    • ...
  • “Necromancy & You”: As a newly risen come and sit in on a small tutorial on how to properly care for your new undead body. Learn the basics of Forsaken physiology and the methods used to prolong tissue.
    • ...
  • Unearthing the Arcane: Suffering from magical maladies? Need help mastering your talents? Come rediscover your latent magical potential!
    • ...
  • Forsaken Cooking Class: A feast for the senses! Learn how to prepare famous Forsaken dishes.
    • ...
  • Pet Battle Tournament: To help bring the evening to a close a pet battle tournament shall be held. Gold prizes shall be awarded to third, second and first place holders. Third place shall receive 1000g, second 2000g, and first 3000g.
    • ...
  • Rave in the Grave: Presented by the <The Might of Lordaeron> this party is sure to end the night with a bang!
    • ...
    The event shall primarily be a social activity with very loose organization. Attendee’s are ultimately responsible for how much RP they get out of this event. To put it simply, the more you put in, the more you get out.


    New to Forsaken RP?
    If you’re new to Forsaken roleplay this is a great time to jump in and check it out! Create a character and come mingle with new and old players alike.

    To get you started here some things to consider:

    How is your character going to react to being raised? Will they praise the Banshee Queen and accept their Forsaken identity? Will the process drive them mad? Or perhaps they’ll rally under a different banner and try to undo the Forsaken from within?

    Who was your character prior to being raised? Do they remember who they were? Do they adopt a new identity? Do they have family they left behind?

    These are just some fundamental questions to consider as you get started on your journey with your brand new character!


    On the matter of Guild Pride:
    As this is a community effort aimed at highlighting the Forsaken as a whole we are asking that guilds refrain from trying to actively recruit the newly risen. A character is free to identify as part of a certain guild, speak about that guild, wear that guild's tabard but we specifically ask that they do not speak of guild recruitment or use any macro’s/guild banners at this event.

    Once the event has concluded guilds may recruit to their hearts content.

    FAQ:

    Is this event restricted to Forsaken only?

    No. However this event is meant to celebrate Forsaken RP, community and culture. To get the most out of the experience we ask that you consider rolling a brand new Forsaken character or attending on a pre-existing Forsaken alt.


    How can I get involved in this?

    We are currently looking for individuals interested in hosting, or assisting, with the above listed events. To sign up as a volunteer please contact Sillaine, Branntherick, or Keleril.

    Other contacts: Dayon, Immogene.
