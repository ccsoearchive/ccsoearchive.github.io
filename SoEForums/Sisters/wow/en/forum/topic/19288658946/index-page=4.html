NEW SPOTLIGHT (& events updated)

RP Event Spotlight - Gund’harg

Before having a name change, this event was running as the Kash’harg. For those that are not in the know, the Kash’harg is an Orc event that brings the clans together in friendship and ritual, usually held in the shadow of the Oshu’gun Mountain, sacred to the Orcs.

The Stonehammer Clan has made the event more accessible by holding it in the Stonetalon Mountains, renaming it to the Gund’harg and sharing their traditions with other races.

When our group arrived, we were treated to a ritual feast, mostly meats from the hunt, laid out before us. The Stonehammer’s were very generous with sharing, offering a variety of dishes for all to enjoy. Drink was also provided and applied liberally as people broke away from the feasting tables to catch up on current events, or greet friends not often seen.

The Stonehammer’s were very receptive and open to those of all walks of life sharing in this tradition, which was nice to see from a race that so often is viewed as secular.

Following the feasting and the mingling was that test of strength! I am told that each month there is a new test, and this month’s was the breaking of boards. The contestants all lined up to test their bare hands (and in one case a head) against the bamboo, iron-bark and a rock! There were a few broken hands (and one broken head), and in the end, only a few came out victorious, proving that they had the strength to withstand anything.

All in all, it was a good time. Great for those that like to brings small groups to events, or those that like to meet new people and watch crowds. I look forward to attending more of these in the future and learning more about Orc culture.

Summary

What is it?

A celebration centered around Orc tradition.

Date & Time?

Once a month - usually toward the end.

Which Faction?

Horde

Which Server?

Wyrmrest Accord

Is it Open to Cross Server?

Yes

Location?

Stonetalon Mountains (24,12)

Who Hosts It?

The Stonehammer Clan

Who is the Contact?

Crogart

Level Requirement & Phased Status?

No phasing, and though the level of the area is 25-30, there are no mobs in the area where the event is held.
