RP Event Spotlight - Dance of the Earthmother
To see this event feature complete with pictures, visit http://thewarcraftlesstraveled.tumblr.com/

Hosted by the Wolfmane Tribe, the Dance of the Earthmother is a ritual celebration that the tribe celebrates at the turning of the seasons.

As usual with Wolfmane events, everything was very well organized. They had many helping hands to run the planned activities. They started out with an opening ceremony complete with a short speech and herb burning; then continued onto races, duels, and a dress contest.

All was ended with a feast of the Last Hunt, where all were invited to partake in the bounty of the Wolfmane’s season.

All in all, it was a wonderful event! There was much RP to be had, and the tribe was open and inviting to all races to share in their traditions.

The Wolfmane’s host some of the best Tauren tribal roleplay on the server, and so if you are looking to expose your characters to that, keep an eye out for their events!

If you would like to know more about the tribe and their events, visit www.wolfmane.net.

Summary

What is it?
A quarterly event that has tribal ritual as well as contests

Date & Time?
Once a season, depending on the hosting guilds schedule

RP Event Spotlight - Skypetal Teahouse

Tucked away into the beautiful, yet underused, Pandaria expansion is Mistfall Village and its lovely buildings, booths and scenery. This is the place that the Skypetal Teahouse, a guild on the Wyrmrest Accord server, has chosen to be their home.

I attended the grand opening of the Tea House last week and was treated to an event that was well organized, loaded with people and full of activity. I wasn’t surprised at all considering that the events lead Fei-lei, was no stranger to large events, helping to organize some of the Tirisfal Theater Troupe events.

Shortly after I arrived for an RP meeting that was to take place there, the trivia contest began… people were hooting and hollering answers so fast that I was losing track of the RP I was doing! A fine sign that there were many people enjoying the activity. Not a problem, though, as the town has wonderful buildings for RP and we just picked up and moved a bit from the crowd.

After prizes were handed out for the contest, the evening was finished up with dancing! Music streamed from a music sharing site hosted by Fei-lei helped to build ambiance and immersion as the tea house transformed into a hopping club.

All in all the night was lovely, and though it was a pain for my not-yet-Pandariaed Paladin to get there, it was worth the trouble. Skypetal Teahouse plans on having many events in the future (Jan 22nd is the next one), so make sure you check out this hidden RP jewel.

Summary

What is it?
A casual tavern style RP setting

Date & Time?
Varied (event calendar: http://skypetalteahouse.tumblr.com/Calendar)

Which Faction?
Horde

Which Server?
Wyrmrest Accord

Is it Open to Cross Server?
Yes

Location?
Mistfall Village (33,77)

Who Hosts It?
The Skypetal Teahouse

Who is the Contact?
Feilei

Level Requirement & Phased Status?
Phasing is required, it is recommended you do the Pandaria into quests beforehand to get there. If you are having trouble phasing into the Vale of Eternal Blossoms, see this Blue Post: http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/6794032135
