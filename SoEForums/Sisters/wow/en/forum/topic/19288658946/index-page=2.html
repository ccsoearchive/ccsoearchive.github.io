FULL MONTH

You can now find the the full month (and beyond) for all known Wyrmrest Accord, and Cross Realm RP events on our very own Google Calendar:

https://calendar.google.com/calendar/embed?src=lindi.kitty@gmail.com&ctz=America/Winnipeg.



Thinking of hosting an event? Here are some things to think about:

  • Take a look at the calendar, try to pick a date that there are no other events present, or think about partnering with an event that is running! That way you are not fighting another event for attention.
  • Make sure you have all the pertinent information in your event post: who, what, where, why and main contacts are a good start.
  • Tag your event in the title with [EVENT], [A-EVENT], or [H-EVENT]. These are attention getters.
  • Advertise! Post your event in the forum, in trade and LFG channels in game, ask for a link to be posted here, or in the weekly spotlight. You can also try the places like tumblr, Roleplayer Crossroads, and the cross realm forum. (All links are listed below)


Supporting events are a good way to make sure that our server continues to have public RP happen… show the love by attending the events, and if you can’t attend, try bumping an event post with some positive and kind comments.
