<Order of the Silver Hand> is an Alliance-side, paladin-centric heavy RP guild focused on guiding aspirant paladins through a detailed training process which culminates in their knighting at the Cathedral of Light in Stormwind City.

We'll be knighting two of our aspirants in the next two weeks and are thus accepting applications for those interested in giving their paladin an immersive RP training and mentoring experience. We're looking to bring in as many as 3 aspirants who will begin their training sometime in early January.

Please visit our website and forums at: silverhandwra.com

About the training process:
    • Aspirants are paired with a paladin knight for the duration of their training.
    • The training process includes IC training events, quests & missions.
    • The training process culminates in the paladin knighting event.
    • The knighting event reflects Arthas' knighting in the Christie Golden novel 'Arthas'
    • Paladins are gifted a Tabard of the Lightbringer upon their knighting

If you have any questions, please seek any of our members in-game, who will point you in the right direction. Also, please feel free to whisper either Truefaith or Dantil directly.

------------

<Over of the Silver Hand> represents the Northshire Chapter of the Silver Hand in-game. Our guild's primary focus is Silver Hand-centric paladin RP with an emphasis on training aspirant paladins into knighthood. We are a small, lawful-good heavy-RP guild primarily based out of Northshire Abbey and Stormwind City.
