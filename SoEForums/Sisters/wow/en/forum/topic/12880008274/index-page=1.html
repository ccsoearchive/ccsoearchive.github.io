(IC)
"Fellow druids, hear my plea; we have become splintered- far and few in between. With new adversaries we need to stand strong- together. Yes, the Horde and the Alliance have had their differences, and yes the tension is strong now than it ever has been, but let that not stop those of us that know true peace and what it means to fully enjoy life. In the past, we use to have monthly gatherings to talk about the current issues, talk politics, and overall enjoy ourselves- enjoy one another's company without the stench of burning oil or the destruction of our companions' homes for the sake of technological advancement.

I ask you all now; worgen, trolls, tauren, night elves, and even the wardens and guardians that have helped protect Moonglade and Hyjal for their sacred ground and what they mean to this world. I ask you now to help me- give me your support and meet with me in Moonglade (destination to be determined within) as we gather in Moonglade once more. Not as two differing factions with petty problems, but as an ancient force with so much wisdom to share.

It does not matter what order you come from, or what organization you work for- we are all druids and protectors of what no one else can protect. We are a family- a strong and potent breed of individuals with so much to give and contribute. Meet with me forty nights from now (June 28th, 2014) as we may host the first gathering since its initial hiatus many years ago, and begin the process of rebuilding our society and culture. We will discuss what we can do to get the word out, pick representatives of each race to garner information and send out the latest news and alerts to those in cities we cannot enter. After which we will mingle and rediscover just how magnificent we truly are."

(OOC)
Back when our server was new, there use to be a monthly event called the Gathering of the Druids were the tauren and night elves would gather in Moonglade and we had a person OOC to translate to the other party. This is where anyone that doesn't RP a druid can participate. IC we need translators. We have Skype and btag/RID that can help make this happen. During the meetings, we will have a Skype group (I recommend to stay in the same Skype group so we can socialize before and after each meeting instead of relying on tumblr and Facebook) to post all of the OOC (let's face it, we need some OOC communication to get at least the first meeting running and go smoothly with our ideas). I won't want an actual Skype call because that's too many people and too many people that will talk over one another. This way we can catalog what was discussed OOC and go back to it with ease.

Right now until I get a responsible Horde druid, I will be hosting the calendar invite on Dylinial (a blood elf rogue) and give moderator status to everyone I invite so they can invite their friends and get the word out. On Alliance I will be hosting the calendar invites on Nymphnote and host the same.

The way I hope this will work is to have a certain amount of representatives/moderators for each race depending on how large the support/turnout is. Please keep in mind that any violent interruptions will be HIGHLY unlikely as per Moonglade being a neutral zone with a lot of powerful druids and skilled wardens to protect it.. as well as other powerful entities. Any assassinations/attacks/brawls will NOT be tolerated and will be met with the appropriate force- you have been warned.

In the case of a planned event, however, that is different- still unlikely, but it would be planned. Harassment and tomfoolery will not be tolerated. There will be a great amount of respect needed to keep it peaceful. I understand there will be some bad blood between some RPers, but please be respectful to not only those who will be trying their hardest to run this thing, but also to the new RPers that really want this to come into fruition.

I want this to be big again, and to not stick to just one day of each month. It is our duty as RPers to help create a positive community to RP and enjoy ourselves in with as many connections and networks as possible and I believe this will help. This may not fix anything, but it will be a nice environment for everyone again.

The specifics and everyone's ideas will be discussed and I will post updates as soon as I can. You can contact me in-game and I will respond as soon as I am able if I forget to check the forum.

I look forward to hearing your feedback! If you are interested, send me mail in-game/message me, or you can post here with your name.

The second Gathering of the Druids will be at 5:00 PM server time and stationed in Moonglade!
