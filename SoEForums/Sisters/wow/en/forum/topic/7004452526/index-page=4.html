I'm in a similar boat to Slywin sort of. I recently came over from another server. The one I was part of (Since roughly launch) had sort of...Died of in terms of the Roleplay community. This wasn't anything new, it had started dieing out around the end of BC but...Ah, as much as I enjoy being cramped into varieing boss fights with several people I don't know only to die for hours on end, raiding wasn't something big on my list. However it had become sort of the only option. What I wanted was(and still is) to Roleplay.

In swapping over I also put myself into a character revision and am left with a rather large issue. I have no idea how a Sentinel would or should behave. Even when it was more prevalent back home, most of my Roleplaying experience came in the form of heavily scripted player stories (a few of my own as well). And while an Individual's story can be it's own thing (working as an Alliance mercenary/hero/diplomat/whatever the heroes call themselves) being a member of a Military group is something I'd not really gotten much practice at doing. I'm very interested in this, particularly the Sentinels. The problem I hit back there was...Well correct me if I'm wrong but the Sentinels are Women. The previous iteration of this character was a male. While that makes for a story of its own, a lot of what would be (and was) less than satisfactory for a Male Night Elf's personal progression weighed heavily on him. However a Female night elf wouldn't of had quite as many issues, they're the backbone of the Military the primary leaders ect.ect. even if I left the back story exactly as it was it would add up to a far more successful and less exhausted character to roleplay as. In fact in changing that aspect it sort of...changed the whole deal at least mentally. When I considered what sort of changes it would bring it resulted in a character for more pleasant to play. What I'd had before wound up being a deeply tragic (to the point of Absurdity) character, and while such a character can make for an interesting story they aren't often enjoyable to Role-play as or with (in my experience at least).

Tl:DR, I would like to Roleplay as a Sentinel. I have knowledge on playing a Night Elf but also some rather specific questions. Can I get help?
