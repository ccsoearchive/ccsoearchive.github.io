  • What is the Alliance Medical Association?
      • The Alliance Medical Association, or AMA for short, is a twice-monthly recurring event focusing on health and wellness on Azeroth. Generally, a member from the community offers to present a lecture on a chosen topic and how it can relate to healing and safety. Once the lecture is given the audience is encouraged to participate in a question and answer portion. Some events in the past have involved demonstrations, group projects, trivia, and more. Apprentice Grauenvoll (Skrimps in-game – me!) serves as the regular host who introduces presenters, and will present himself on occasion.


  • Where and when does this take place?
      • AMA meets every other Wednesday at 6pm server/pacific (7pm mountain, 8pm central, 9pm eastern) in the Ironforge Hall of Explorers just in front of the library. This event IS cross-realm compatible, which means your friends and alts from other rp servers can always participate.


  • Does my character have to be a healer or doctor? What if they don't know about tonight's topic?
      • Your level of IC or OOC knowledge should in no way affect your enjoyment of the night. Lectures begin with the basics and work up, and the question and answer portion is open to those who know and those who wish to know in equal measures. Come drop by and ask about a topic you wouldn't normally consider! Participation by the audience is encouraged but never required.


  • Can I present a topic?
      • Absolutely! Post here in the thread, contact me in-game on Skrimps, or message me the alliancemedical.tumblr.com tumblr page. I try to keep a very open mind for presentation topics, as nearly everything can relate to health, safety, and wellness considering the dangers of Azeroth and Draenor.
