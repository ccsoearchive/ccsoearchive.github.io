Do you love adventure?
So do we!
Do you love finding new things, old things, or just interesting things?
So do we!
Are you wanted for public indecency in six towns?
Well, we're still mostly alike.

Who are you?
I'm Kat!
...Oh. Oh, right! The Winds of Fortune is a travelling caravan that seeks out adventure and treasure, wherever it may take them. Helping people in need, exploring ruins, and meeting people along the way. We accept people of nearly any walk of life. So long as you prove yourself to be trustworthy, you'll always have a place at the campfire with the Winds of Fortune.

What does that mean?
We're something between a band of gypsies and a mercenary group. I was describing it to someone and they shouted something about dungeons and a dragon campaign, which didn't make a lot of sense to me but they seemed excited. We're a small group that goes and helps towns in exchange for goods or a safe place to set up camp for a while. We explore ruins, find interesting artifacts, then turn them in to the Explorer's League for rewards, or sell the things they're not interested at market events when we pass through major cities!

Why?
Because it's fun!

==================================================================

The Winds of Fortune is a guild that's been around for several years under various names now. Our goal is to try to run some D&D-style RP events with a roll system that the core user group has been using for about a year now, with a few extra additions.

The events rotate between some adventuring campaigns, market days to sell their goods, and some good old-fashioned stopping of Bad Guys.

Looking for more information? We have a website at http://windsoffortune.enjin.com/. The forums have information on our roll system, characters, events and more. If you're just trying to find us for some world RP, we now have a display on the main page that shows where our travels have landed us at the moment (though you may need to let us know if you're looking for us, some of us are still doing PvE things). If you have any specific questions that aren't answered there, feel free to whisper Katecia or Katkanesby in-game for clarifications.

Interested in joining? http://windsoffortune.enjin.com/recruitment has all the info on our application process and the application itself. We will not "just do an IC interview instead."

Given the nature of the guild, it's preferred that characters are at least level 55, or that a concerted effort to level up to that point is being made. Some travel routes take us through high-level areas. If events are constantly being derailed due to having to provide protection to a single player, then no one is going to have any fun.
