((From the end of the Mercenary "Minisodes", or prequels toward a new plot, found on our website))

Destän: For a moment, there's quiet. Then, a voice speaks up with authority. "Grab those two, and cut the bridge." From behind, Destan and Syll are grabbed and forced onto their knees. The Master slowly steps into the cavern from the path behind them and watches as the bridge is cut and slams into the plateau across the way. He raises his voice, speaking loud enough to fill the cavern. "Did I not warn you?"

Sylladara: Syl isn't one to bend knees to anyone, and she looks rather murderous, struggling to get away from whoever had a hold on her.

Destän: Syll is shoved more forcibly onto the ground, now having two guards holding her down

Destän: The Master stands at the edge of the rocks by him. "What happens next is of your own doing. You have no one to blame but yourselves."

Gaverick: Gav stares across the gap at the Master. He doesn't drop the blade...how badly he'd like to throw it, and see that cool voice raise into a scream as his body fell in two- but no. That would only get them all killed. He slowly lowers the sword.

Destän: Destan looks to Syll and speaks in Darnassian. "I am so sorry, Sylladara. But, if this is my end, I am honored to share it with you."

Sylladara: Syl shouts in Darnassian, some of it coming out as more of a pained croak. "This will not end. We're too strong. NOT NOW."

Destän: The Master moves to Syll, grabbing her jaw in a vice like grip. "I am talking, at present. Be silent."

Gillgameshi: Gilgamesh frowns under his helmet and steps forward slightly "What...do
you want?" he asks slowly

Gaverick: Gav bares his teeth, snarling at the Master as he reaches for Syll.

Sylladara: Syl silently looks to Gav, and then lets her eyes linger on Gil before she shuts them, seeming to give up the fight...for now.

Destän: The Master turns to regard the Mercs across the way. "I told you what I wanted already. You all chose not to listen. And now, you will suffer for your choice. But, I am a giving man. You cannot save these two. However, you will be allowed to leave. Leave here, and carry your failure with you."

Madigan: Madigan blinks slowly as she takes in the scene and the blackness swimming at the edge of her vision goes red. She drops the box onto the chair Duncan previously occupied and draws on the last of her reserves, bursting into a silent sprint up to the edge and -hurl- her swords towards the Master's face and neck. While not exactly balanced for throwing, Madigan has some experience in using the blades for such, and she launches them with all that experience.

Destän: The blades fly across the expanse, and do cut the Master. His shoulder and upper left arm are cut, and the blades clatter to the floor

Sylladara: Syl does mutter a few words, if she can get them out. Still in Darnassian. With those words come light tears. "It has been MORE than an honor..."

Madigan: Madigan isn't finished, she plucks out a throwing knife from her thigh sheaths in each hand and lets them both fly at the guards restraining Destan.

Destän: The Master, however, simply stands there. He removes his jacket calmly and hands it to one of his guards. He leans down, and picks up the blades, idly watching a throwing knife go wide. He speaks to Syll and Destan, quietly. "Say goodbye to them."

Destän: Destan looks across, and puts on a smile. He won't die looking pathetic. He looks to Madigan last, and speaks two sentences. "All of me. Forever."

Sylladara: Syl does shout, but it's not a goodbye. "Keep up hope. Even if we are gone." Then she looks at Gil, tearing up further, because she cannot bring forth any words.

Destän: The Master nods. "Those were good last words." Using Madi's blades, the Master stabs both Destan and Sylladara in the side, and then kicks them both to the water below; first Destan, and then Syll. The bodies fall amongst the water falls, and are lost among the foam. The Master looks up to the remaining Mercs and speaks. "Now, take my kindness and leave. Raise not a fist, and you will be unharmed."
