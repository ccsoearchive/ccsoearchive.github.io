Hesseth
90 Draenei Shaman
Overture
11805
((This takes place after the successful exorcism of Emily, a child that carried a demon inside of her. Ten weeks into our current Mercenary plotline))

[Esgaroth]: Several soldiers descend the stairs, guns aimed at the group. Two people descend after; Lord Rosemonte, kicking a bound Elise down the last few steps. The Priestess is beaten beyond measure, and seems not to know where she is
[Esgaroth]: Lord Rosemonte speaks. "It seems that Hector was much better at getting her to talk than I was. Pity, really; I was enjoying the conversation."
[Esgaroth]: Destan looks up, rage contained. "You are too late, Desmond. Gul'Macht is gone."

[Caeir]: Caeir doesn't move, but his eyes narrow but trusts the sense of not moving with a rifle on him.

[Esgaroth]: Lord Rosemonte smiles at that. "Good! Then, no one will object when I claim the Black Room. Men, bind them all as well, and bring Emily to me. If they resist; kill them."
[Esgaroth]: Lord Rosemonte speaks one last time, before turning to leave. "I would suggest complying."

[Gaverick]: Gav's hackles bristle, the worgen's lips drawing back to expose his fangs.

[Esgaroth]: Destan looks to the soldiers, and then to the mercs. Slowly, he puts his hands on his head and kneels.

[Madigan]: Madigan looks on in dismay, surely they weren't giving up that easy.

[Esgaroth]: The soldiers wait, watching the group closely

[Caeir]: Caeir looks between the group, tense as he looks between his friends and the soldiers.

[Hesseth]: At the numbers, Hess seems unsure what to do. But one thing is clear...She wouldn't let them have Emily. She turns her head up to the ceiling in contemplation and then looks to the soldiers. One quick prayer to the spirits of Air. "Guide me..." She
[Hesseth]: whispers, and she urges forth a line of chain lightning to bounce through the enemy.

[Gaverick]: Gav looks frustrated as all hell. He glowers at Destan, then looks to the guns aimed at the group. He growls, but remains frozen in his place.

[Esgaroth]: The lightning fires off, striking a wall rather than any of the soldiers. Before they can fire, however, laughing is heard.
[Esgaroth]: Lord Rosemonte steps back downstairs, looking to the Draenei. "I have to admit, I NEVER thought you'd actually try anything. But, I must ask; where are your elements now?" At that, he takes a gun from the nearest soldier speaks. "Fire, men. But, at her."

[Caeir]: Caeir grabs for his sword and charges forward at the guards, holding a hand out to blind the guards on the stairs.

[Gaverick]: Gav snarls. "Aw, like fel!" He lifts his head, letting out as loud a roar as he can manage. The bellow aimed to startle the men on the stairs, hoping to aid Caeir's attempts at disorienting them.

[Esgaroth]: Caeir's charge is a second too late. The bullets fly, and Hess's form is torn apart of the flying metal. She will fall to the ground, bleeding her lifeblood on holy ground.
[Esgaroth]: As Hess's lifeblood spills, the earth under them will shake. The fire on the candles burns brighter, and the air about the crypt roars. Tears fall from empty eye sockets, and the waters come to cover her, returning her body to the earth slowly. In her-
[Esgaroth]: place, a small plant rises, green and filled with life despite the surroundings.
[Esgaroth]: However, the guards still stand, guns aimed at the rest. Lord Rosemonte eyes that plant and chuckles. "There they are, it seems. A bit late, though."
[Esgaroth]: Destan speaks up. "Just take us, Desmond. No one else has to die!"
[Esgaroth]: Lord Rosemonte answers, cocking his gun again. "That is completely up to them."

[Madigan]: Madigan stares open-mouthed at where Hesseth's corpse was. Her blades droop and she snaps them up, only to sheathe them and glare hatefully at the men.

[Gaverick]: Gav flinches at the gunshots, turning as Hess topples to the ground. He stumbles a half step, looking completely gutted.

[Esgaroth]: Emily is numb at all this; she sits as more tears fall
[Esgaroth]: Lord Rosemonte speaks, with contempt in his tone. "Kneel now, or die as she did."

[Madigan]: Reluctantly, Madi drops to one knee, baleful glare never leaving the lord Rosemonte.

[Gaverick]: Gav wavers, looking sick...but slowly he sinks to a crouch, curling his hands over his face and letting his head hang.

[Esgaroth]: Lord Rosemonte smiles. "Smart ones, it seems. And you, Paladin?"

[Caeir]: Caeir comes to a halt, looking at the place Hesseth fell. The weight of so much exhaustion was catching up to him now. He looked from the sword in his hand, and then to Gave. He slowly comes to a crouch. "What are you going to do to Emily?"

[Esgaroth]: The Lord smiles. "Simple; I'm going to use her to claim the Black Room as my own. And, once that is done, I'll leave here there to rot. Men, bind them and take them, along with the Priestess, to the Glass Prison."

--Thus ends Act 2 and the life of Farseer Hesseth.--
