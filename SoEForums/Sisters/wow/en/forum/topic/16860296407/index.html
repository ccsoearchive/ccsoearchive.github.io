Lore thus far:

Gaius Artos was annointed the Bulwark of westfall, And as such sword to be the people of Westfall's shield, to protect them from the Defias, From the gnolls, From the kobolds, The murlocs, the harvesters, Any threat that would present itself, Sir Artos swore to defend westfall as best he could. However gradually he came to the realization that one man alone could do little, and even if there was a group in place to properly defend westfall, Westfall needed civilians, People to repopulate the one time breadbasket of the Kingdom of Stormwind.

As such he appealed to the Council of Nobles, and the nobles, eager to get taxes flowing from the province agreed to Gaius' request. Allowing him a charter to form an order which would aid in repopulation of Westfall, to restore it to its former self.

Thus the order of westfall was born.

Planned features:

1.) A unique blend of Military and town hall style politics.
2.) A civilian branch, as well as a military Branch.
3.) Westfall world events.
4.) Many Dungeon master styled events.
5.) A focus on individual character development.
6.) A navy.
7.) Fort Resurgence! (Very crude image and position http://imgur.com/5009frW)
OOC:

The Order of Westfall is a brand spanking new guild that is looking to start up RP and other such things soon, but in order to accomplish this goal, We would like -You- to join us and help influence the direction the guild goes.

Any questions feel free to ask below and I will do my best to answer.
You can find me in game, or send me a mail to contact me
