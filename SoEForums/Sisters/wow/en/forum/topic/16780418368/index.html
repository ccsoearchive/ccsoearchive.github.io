Greetings one and all. The Forsaken Watchers, a long running guild with many deep rooted traditions, is undergoing a large revitalization effort. We are looking for active, fun people to join us. The Watchers are going to be the center point of a recurring event called the UTAP, link below. We will also be running various Pro-Forsaken propaganda events from time to time in Kalimdor, as well as other fun things with other guilds on the server.

( http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/16544444682 )

We are primarily a diplomatic and scientific organization, at least on the outside. What we do in secret may be another story entirely.

So far I am the best point of contact as far as information is concerned, but you can send a tell to any Watcher online and they will be able to point you in the right direction. Membership is not some convoluted process, we only require an in character walkup RP session. The explanation can be as simple as your character saw a flyer, heard an advertisement, whatever. It doesn't matter. We usually are in Brill, though we do frequent other places within Tirisfal and Silverpine.

On another note, In Character, the Watchers are run by a council of equals, rather than one singular all powerful leader, although on OOC matters, I am still in charge. To that end, I will be evaluating members for their ability to be leaders within the guild and the community, for we are looking to add a few more Councilors to the ranks. There are also Advisor positions available, which comes with a bit less responsibility but the respect deserved of an officer rank.

I will be adding to this post sometime within the week with more detailed information, but I felt it was important to get the word out that, yes, we are back, and yes, we are looking for new recruits.

Happy questing!
