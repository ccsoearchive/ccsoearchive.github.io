Take a walk down the Path of Light outside the Temple of Karabor, take a deep breath and inhale the sweet scent of the Starflowers and Orchids growing along the pathway through the trees. Listen to the cheerful songs of the birds in the sky above, feel the warm light of the moon upon your cheek, and hear the crunch of the leaves beneath your hooves.

You are in the valley of Shadowmoon, home of the Exiled Ones....

We invite you to immerse yourself into an order that few others get the chance to be a part of, one where the once vast race of Eredar come together to experience an order like no other.

Where violence, greed, and power are not their priorities. Yet family, love, and hope come to focus. Where our people, the Draenei, wish to preserve and celebrate our people's rich culture. Our lands have been destroyed, our cities left in ruins and our heritage simply dwindling away.

We, the Draenei of the Sha'tor, wish to unite. support, and defend our fellow people and have charged our members with helping to enshrine the faith and hope amongst us all on Azeroth and Draenor. By providing a place to call home, to retreat and to pilgrimage. This is not an Order just for Vindicators or Anchorites, but for those Draenei who walk all paths.

Will you join us?

✮About the Sha'tor✮
--------------------------------------------------------------------
We are a dedicated group of strong-willed Draenei who stand at attention over our people, ever alert for the rise of new enemies, or the resurrection of old ones. Many of us have lived through the destruction caused by the Burning Legion, watched as our homes were turned to ruin and loved ones taken from us. We have fought to protect our homes against this horrible threat, though we never truly recovered. The Sha'tor has sworn themselves to preserve the Draenei way of life and we as a race, whether Vindicator, Anchorite, Artificer, or merchant, seek to rebuild our culture the way our ancestors had on Argus by bringing the feeling of hope, family and faith back to our hearts - without the need for violence. Though we are not defenseless as we prepare for the Army of Light.

The Sha'tor has been around since September 2014, prior to the release of World of Warcraft: Warlords of Draenor. Since the beginning, the Sha'tor is a guild that is focused on the unity and support of Draenei roleplayers on the Wyrmrest Accord server. Through the year(s), the guild has promoted "quality over quantity" as well as a lore-abiding and traditional roleplay setting that grasps the very depths of the Draenei's way of life.

As a community, the Sha'tor intends to cover not only the culture of the Draenei but also focus on YOU, the member! We will strive to help you build your character's story. We encourage an open tight-knit community, where every character knows one another, learning throughout the stories we play through together. In the process we hope to help everyone progress their characters and their stories, taking turns being main and supporting casts to the drama and adventures that will occur among the Order and beyond.

What are we looking for?
--------------------------------------------------------------------
✮Out of Character✮
  • Members who are knowledgeable on Warcraft lore - especially on the Draenei
  • Members who are mutually friendly, helpful, and good-hearted
  • Members interested in building & maintaining a welcoming guild community
  • Members willing to engage in social / public roleplay
  • Member who can be active & show dedication to their character and the guild
  • ...and Creativity and Imagination!
    • --------------------------------------------------------------------
    ✮In Character✮
  • Characters that are unique > It's okay to have flaws!
  • Draenei that are not "blue humans" - i.e. homosapien tendencies
  • Character willing to protect & defend the Sha'tor at all costs.
  • Characters that seek to help the order grow and engage in random social activities.
    • --------------------------------------------------------------------
    What does The Sha'tor have to offer?
    --------------------------------------------------------------------
    We offer you a leadership that has been together for years. They are dedicated to the Draenei and Warcraft lore, making sure everything is as lore-accurate as can be. As leaders, and as officers, they have conducted several combat / d20 / cultural / and public events - All being immersive and fun to engage in.

    A strong dedication to the wants and needs of our members. We are not the ones that make up the guild, our beloved members do.

    As our primary mission, we want to offer your character and you, the player, a place to call home. We intend to be as realistic of the Draenei way of life as possible - Whether that be with their religion, their personalities, or the military-esque side of things.

    We intend to make this a friendly and social roleplaying guild. Where our rules are not strict on whether or not you -have- to come to the events. We understand players aren't always in the mood to roleplay - We have been there. We intend to make this a place for you to log on to and feel welcomed and where you are able to create friendship, both In character and Out.
    --------------------------------------------------------------------
    Ranking Structure:
    --------------------------------------------------------------------
    The Sha'tor offers a dynamic and exciting array of ranks for our members to journey through. There is one primary ranking system, which is what you see currently within the game as well as listed below. However, there is a secondary ranking system, which is connected to the Paths (or sub-sections) of the Sha'tor. You can find more information about the ranking structure on our website!
    --------------------------------------------------------------------
    Primary Ranking Structure
    --------------------------------------------------------------------
    ✮Lorekeeper - Guild Leader

    ✮Exarch - Co Guild Leader

    ✮Elder Sage - Head Officer

    ✮Consul - Officers

    ✮A'dota-kei (Reliable One) - Honored Members

    ✮Crystalsworn - Advanced Members

    ✮Initiate - New Members

    Crystal Shards - Progressing Through the Ranks
    Crystal shards are collected through a variety of ways, through attendance of a shard event, random roleplay, activity on the website, or contributing positively to the guild community.These shards are used to progress through the ranks almost like a point system.

    --------------------------------------------------------------------
    Paths of the Sha'tor
    --------------------------------------------------------------------
    As Draenei roleplayers, we each understand that within their culture each individual Draenei must choose their own path in life, whether they choose to be a Vindicator, an Anchorite, or an Artificer. Within the Sha'tor, we wish to cater to those paths in which we offer two main Councils for members to choose from while they journey through the guild.

    ✮Path of the Naaru
    The Path of the Naaru, charged by Exarch Taliir to follow closely to the road the Light had carved for our people. Serving as not only the protectors of the Sha'tor, but as the defenders of the peace and the healers of the Draenei. This council is often home to Vindicators, Peacekeepers, Battle Mages, Healers, Rangari and Elementalists.

    ✮Path of the Prophet
    The Path of the Prophet, charged by Lorekeeper Taisnia to follow closely to the path of culture, faith, and wisdom. These are the intellectuals of the Sha'tor, those would seek the truth of the world. They are the keepers of Draenei history and culture as well as purveyors of the arts. This council is often home to scholars, innovators, inventors, linguists, artists, educators, and academics.

    Each path may occasionally host events focused on a theme relevant to that path. While such events may be focused more towards the members of a certain path, all Sha'tor members are welcome to attend on any character regardless of which one the character is in. We just ask that you please be respectful of the event and members that the event is focused on.
    --------------------------------------------------------------------
    How does one join The Sha'tor?
    --------------------------------------------------------------------
    It is required that all interested roleplayers fill out an application on our website. We only wish to stay organized when it comes to who is coming into the guild. We are about quality, not quantity. We also recommend meeting with us in the Exodar, usually located within the Inn, for some random roleplay or simply sending us an in-game mail.
    --------------------------------------------------------------------
    Draenei Community & World Rp
    --------------------------------------------------------------------
    The Sha'tor is one of the few Draenei Rp guilds here on Wyrmrest Accord. As such members need to be open and prepared to interact and roleplay with other Draenei guilds. The Sha'tor is not a closed community and we care about the development of the Draenei community at large, and expect a similar attitude from our members.

    New to roleplaying a Draenei? Or perhaps new to roleplay all together? We accept all types of Rpers - inexperienced and experienced. We are more than willing to help those in need. Whether it be to help character progression, not feeling comfortable with the way you roleplay etc. We are here to help!
