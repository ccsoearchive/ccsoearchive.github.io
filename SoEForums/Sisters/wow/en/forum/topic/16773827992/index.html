Brightmoon Legacy hosts the Brightmoon Faire & Market!

June 13th, starting at 6:00 PM - LOCATION TBA

Contact Lindiwe for questions, detail and formal invites. VENDOR spots are open!



Who: Brightmoon Legacy hosts the Brightmoon Faire & Market!

What: The Brightmoon Faire is a combined show and market event where people can buy and sell goods, and then take in a show or two!

When: June 13th @ 6 server time.

Where: RTBA

Details:

Brightmoon Legacy is hosting its monthly Faire & Market event on the 13th of June.

From 6:00 - 7.30 we host the Brightmoon Market, inviting all vendors that wish to run a booth to sell their wares! There are limited spots - only two of each vendor type can apply.

Vendors & services in attendance already are:

TBA

Our past vendors:
    Keyandros' Fine Dresses (Tailoring - dresses & robes only)
    Brightmoon's Food & Drink Wagon
    Lindiwe's Herbs (not that kind of herb) & Remedies (Herbalism mat & GHI items)
    Brightmoon's Oddities & Gifts (Rare Items)
    Brightmoon's Elixirs & Cure-alls (Rare Pots)
    Jiao Li's Lottery Tickets (Inscription)
    Senzals Rare Books (GHI items)
    Wolf's Leathers & Wears (Leatherworking)
    Kargrog's Weapons and Pointy Things (Blacksmith weapons)
    Klu's Shady Wares (Shady wares!)
    Soinn's Wondrous Apothecary (Alchemy)
    The Brotherhood of Arms (Defence contracting & surgical strikes)
    Kharg's Engineered Doodads (Engineering items)
    Dathgolo's Fine Magical Threads (Tailor & Enchanting)


Still looking for:

    Blacksmithing
    Tailors (non-dresses & robes, perhaps bags?)
    Enchanter
    Jewelcrafter
    Services (Does your guild have a service to offer?)
    Pet trainer
    Other (If you have an idea, feel free to approach with it)


For this market we are taking donations to run a stall/booth, in exchange we do all the advertising! If you want to be a vendor in this months market, send me word! (In-game mail, PST or visit our enjin site)

From 8:00 - onward we will be holding a show featuring various Brightmoon performers. This months featured acts are:

    TBA


All ended with a fireworks display.

If you have any questions, want to be a vendor or want a calendar invite to this event, feel free to contact Lindiwe, Keyandros or Sinnestria through in-game mail, PST, here on the forums, or our enjin site.

EDIT: Just to clear some up - items and crafting services that are tangible (EG: professions and in-game items) are traded for real gold to be set by the sellers. Other things such as GHI items and the shows are not paid for, but tips are greatly appreciated for those that are spending the time to not only put thing together, but to RP it out.

Soinn

Soinn
100 Blood Elf Paladin
Ash and Raven Co
7250
Soinn will deal in both imagined items and real items with appropriate collection. If you pay her with real gold, you will receive real items. If you pay her with imagined gold, you will only receive a description of your purchase. Keep reading this post for a list of her real gold services. Tips for her free services are welcome, but when I say tip, I mean tip; if the service says free, no gold is necessary. Her real gold services are available only as long as supplies last. Anything she can create that you bring all of the materials for is a free service. Please whisper Soinn if you want to order anything to guarantee its availability.

Buyer Be Warned

After a spirited argument with a pack horse (which the pack horse won), Soinn has bag upon bag of mostly broken vials. And no idea what is in the vials that survived! In an attempt to make up for her losses, she is selling them for one low price: 10g per vial.

For fairness’ sake, I am including a list of the potential outcomes of /roll for your out-of-character information. I am also imposing the limit of five per customer.

1-10: Oil of Immolation

11-20: Pygmy Oil

21-30: Deepstone Oil

31-40: Potion of Deepholm

41-50: Potion of Illusion

51-60: Darkwater Potion

61-70: Draenic Your Choice Potion

71-80: Transmorphic Tincture

81-90: Potion of Treasure Finding

91-97: Greater Draenic Your Choice Flask

98-100: It’s Aaalive! (Viscidus Globule)

It’s Aaalive!… Kinda (Companion Pets)

Soinn's attempts to create a potion capable of raising the dead were kinda successful... if you consider raising an army of mutant and/or undead odds and ends successful, anyway. Maybe leaving the potion where her young daughter, Jaszarinne, could find it was a bad idea. Becoming a mad scientist bent on Azerothian domination sounds like too much work, so she is selling off her unwanted soldiers.

Bailey
Adoption Cost: 1000g
"Breed:" Elekk Plushie
Bailey was an ordinary plushie until that fateful tea party, and it appears to remain a quiet, benevolent soul weathered by years as a child's plaything. Besides, if it does harbor any secret murderous inclinations, it doesn't have opposable thumbs! Or any thumbs.

Edward and Henry
Adoption Cost: 2000g
"Breed:" Crazy Carrot (Edward), Terrible Turnip (Henry)
When Soinn said “Eat your vegetables,” this wasn’t a desirable alternative. Edward and Henry are no fonder of Jaszarinne than she is of them. Of course, they don’t seem fond of anyone just yet.

Fido
Adoption Cost: 1000g
"Breed:" Zomstrok
Fido is Trevor’s less lucky twin. Trevor was restored with new-found magical prowess, but Fido merely returned with an insatiable hunger for flesh. He dreams of a home near the water, but any home away from his estranged brother is acceptable.

The Guilt Monster
Adoption Cost: 1000g
"Breed:" Fragment of Anger
Soinn doesn’t know what she did to deserve this, but the Guilt Monster will happily tell her. The Guilt Monster surfaced during her daughter’s escapades with her miracle potion. If you were thinking of adopting a cat for your family, think again; the Guilt Monster will provide all of the disdain for none of the feeding costs (except potentially your soul).

Trevor
Adoption Cost: 5000g
"Breed:" Magical Crawdad
Everyone loves Magical Trevor! Except Fido. Fido can't stand the guy.

Transmute: Savage Blood

For a mutable fee, Soinn will transmute for Savage Blood. Given 50 Alchemical Catalysts, she will charge 10g. Given 100 of three Draenor herbs, she will do the transmutation for free. Given 50 Alchemical Catalysts plus 7 Crescent Oil or 21 Crescent Saberfish Flesh, she will do the transmutation for free.

Transmute: Sorcerous Elements

For a mutable fee, Soinn will transmute the Sorcerous Elements. Given only 10 of your chosen Sorcerous Element, she will charge 25g. Given 10 of your chosen Sorcerous Element in addition to 8 of three Draenor herbs, she will do the transmutation for free. Given 10 of your chosen Sorcerous Element plus 4 Alchemical Catalysts, she will do the transmutation for free.

Vial of the Sands

For a mutable fee, Soinn's colleague, Tyvenn, will create Vials of the Sands. Given only 1 Pyrium-Laced Crystalline Vial and 8 Sands of Time, Soinn will charge 3000g for her reagents. Given 1 Pyrium-Laced Crystalline Vial and 8 Sands of Time plus 8 Deepstone Oil, 8 Flasks of the Winds, 8 Flasks of Titanic Strength and 12 Truegold, he will create the vial for free.
