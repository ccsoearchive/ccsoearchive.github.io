The Sha'tor welcomes Draenei and friends to journey to the peaceful village of Embaari, nestled in the center of Shadowmoon Valley, for an evening of social interactions, traditional contests and vendors selling a variety of goods!
--------------------------------------------------
Event Details:
--------------------------------------------------
After many, long months of Shadowmoon Valley undergoing conflict and disaster, Embaari's traditional market was left behind. Now, to celebrate life having returned to the village, the Sha'tor and people of Embaari have come together to organize an evening of:

✮Vendors
✮Dazzling Rod Game
✮Elekk Race
✮Fireworks
✮ And much more!

Date: Sunday - July 26, 2015
Time: 5:30pm - 8:30pm Server Time
Location: Embaari Village - Shadowmoon Valley, Draenor (Summons provided)
--------------------------------------------------
Who can attend?
--------------------------------------------------
Though it is considered Draenei-centric, it is NOT limited to only Draenei. All races of the Alliance are welcomed to join! Come by yourself, bring a friend, or come with your guild!
--------------------------------------------------
Out of Character Details:
--------------------------------------------------
This event will take place, officially, from 5:30 pm to 8:30 pm server time. People are, of course, more than welcome to show up at any other time of day, and stay longer. However, the events planned for the evening will begin at the times that will be determined during the announcements.

This open market is about allowing players to engage Draenei Roleplay in a Draenei setting, and is also a chance for outsiders who wish to interact with the Draenei, or simply wish to see what roleplay is like in Draenor, in this timeline.

Above all, remember that this event is open to all, and welcomes all. If anyone is interested in adding an event, please don't hesitate to contact Taisnia or Kysderal about it! There are contests and activities, but the event is focused on characters socializing and meeting new people.
--------------------------------------------------
Contact Information:
--------------------------------------------------
➨ Taisnia
➨ Kysderal

If anyone has any questions or wishes to reserve a stall for the market, - Please feel free to contact us in-game or simply post here.

If you want to be invited to the calendar event and made an admin so you can invite your guild(s), please post here with the name you want invited!
