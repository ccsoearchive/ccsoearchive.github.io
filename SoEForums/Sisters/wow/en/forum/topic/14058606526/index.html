Hello, Wyrmies.

You've seen my RP-PVP thread. Some of my ideal officers and I have decided that, through a lot of discussion, a Guild is the best way to go about this. There's not enough interest or means of easy coordination without it. There's no real option for Alliance RP-PvP. So, here we are.

THE LION'S BLADE

The Lion's Blade is a company-sized unit of men organized by special commision under the Field Marshal Anthony Valron. They are the aggressors of the Alliance. They are the first in and the last out. They do what has to be done, no questions ask. They are the best. Anthony is a former Brotherhood of the Horse Marshal, and has tapped several of his old brothers-in-arms, as well as friends and well-known soldiers to make up the starting ranks of the Blade. However, the company isn't really a company. There's not enough men. So, here is the recruitment thread for the Lion's Blade!

Requirements:
A roleplaying addon such as: MRP, TRP2, or XRP.
A decent ability to spell and punctuate.
A desire to PvP. We are an RP-PvP guild, our guild is based around PvP as much as it is RP.
A respectful and kind attitude. Don't be a jerk!
An IC interview.
The desire to be part of a brotherhood and follow IC and OOC rules.

WEBSITE: www.thelionsblade.enjin.com

Idry/Ariel is our webmaster. Thank you so much, Iddy, for putting this together.

IC:

With the Iron Horde threat, some things are best dealt with by those with experience and skill. The Lion's Blade is a para-military organization led by the esteemed Field Marshal Anthony, Lord Valron (Avaricex). They are the few, the proud and the very best the Alliance has to offer. They're among the first to fight and are noted for their bravery, their valor and their steadfast strength in the face of even the most daring odds.

"Immortality in Victory!" is our battle-cry. We're not for fame, or glory, or wealth. We're here because someone needs to be the gatekeepers. Someone needs to keep those who would destroy our societies out.

That's us. That's what we do. If it sounds like you might be the sort of individual who can help us, contact one of us today.
