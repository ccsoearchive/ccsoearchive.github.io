[H] Salvation (est. 2007) is a progression raiding guild on Cenarion Circle.

Objective

Salvation is actively recruiting players for Mythic content.Mythic Mode Progression is our goal! Keeping this in mind Salvation is ramping up recruitment efforts to facilitate certain classes missing or desperately in need for Mythic Hellfire Citadel.

We are also accepting players that engage in social aspects of the WoW community such as leveling, PvP, questing, dungeon finding, roleplaying and other challenging activites. We provide monthly guild events. For example, the Tournament of Tiny Champions. It’s a pet battle tournament where guildies compete with each other using 3-6 of their mightiest level 25 tiny champions. We are expanding this extremely fun side of Salvation so that everyone can join in on some action and prizes!

Raiding Schedule

Our schedule is as follows. We push content three times a week for 3 hours at a time starting at 6:15pm to 9:15pm PST Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Mondays. You must be able to raid all three nights.

Current Content Requirements

We are looking for reliable players that can make a vast majority of our raids. With this in mind those players must be willing to learn, adapt, take some criticism, and seek to better their effectiveness during raid.

• Ideally for us you should have or be in progress of receiving your ilvl735 legendary ring.
• Item level of 715 or above.
• Your gear must be enchanted and gemmed appropriately to your class.
• Open-minded on switching specializations on a per fight basis based on encounter and raid needs.
• Must have knowledge of current content boss mechanics. Reference Icyveins, Gabestah, Fatboss, Line of Sight Gaming or other videos/forums that may help increase your performance in raid.
• Have Mumble installed and working. A microphone to communicate is also required.

Warlock x2

Balance Druid x2

***Exceptional applicants can feel free to sign up. Outstanding applications will always be considered.
