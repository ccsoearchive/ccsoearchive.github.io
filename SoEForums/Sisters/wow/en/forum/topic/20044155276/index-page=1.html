Kalimdor: the Land of Eternal Starlight.

She has offered home and refuge to every Azerothian race for millennia on millennia. From her heart grows the very World Tree; her finger islands extend graciously toward the world, a beckon and a welcome; her rivers rush coolly to sustain us, and her forests are a lush blanket under which we hide.

The wildest of us have called her home for years uncounted--and yet we stand apart from one another, shy and suspicious, too proud and aloof to come solidly together as a whole Kalimdorian people.

No longer. Together, we will work toward a united Kalimdor within the Alliance; together, we work toward the safety and betterment of all of her Allied people.

------------------------

Flyers are tacked dutifully to boards and posts around Stormshield and all major Alliance settlements.

TO ALL ABLE:

By cooperation of the Hand of Argus and the Sisterhood of Elune, the unstructured militia KALIMDOR COLLECTIVE is recruiting for the purpose of aiding and supporting all Kalimdorian people, and all lands controlled or pursued by Kalimdorian peoples.

Any and all Kalimdorian persons and/or those who have vested interest in Kalimdor and/or our cause are welcome to inquire.

Those welcome include, in no particular order:

  • Any and all kaldorei
  • Any and all draenei
  • Any and all Gilneans who consider Darnassus a home
  • Any and all gnomes who make home in Stonetalon
  • Any and all dwarves, Wildhammer and otherwise, who’ve found home or interest in northern Kalimdor
  • Any and all humans displaced from Theramore or otherwise affiliated with Kalimdor
  • Any other with vested and proven interest in the races of Kalimdor and the betterment thereof


Please contact Moon Priestess Theleste Moonraven with further inquiries or to sign up.

----------------------

| OOC INFO: |

<Kalimdor Collective> is a fresh perspective on an Alliance Kalimdor-based guild with no race restrictions; rather, we work together for the aid and support of all Kalimdor-based and Kalimdor-interested people, regardless of racial affiliation.

IC, this is not a strictly-structured military guild built for training and fighting, but a loosely-structured group of capable citizens who have the ability and desire to help others of Kalimdor and beyond. We can most likely make your concept fit; if you have an idea, we will try to find a place for it.

OOC, we welcome everyone looking for a friendly place to call home. Our RP is Kalimdor-based, but we welcome RP-friendly people who just want to PvE or PvP and laugh with us. We've been known to raid, we've been known to arena, we've been known to just sit in Vent and laugh for awhile.

| THE BASICS: |

EST and CST-based
  • Events scheduled no later than 7:00 ST (and usually at 5:30-6:00 ST)
  • Most active in EDT and CDT evenings and weekends!


The usual guild & group activities
  • May put another raid group together--did normal Highmaul in our previous iteration (before our hiatus)
  • Noncompetitive and competitive PvP (grab your sword and fight the Horde!)
  • Guild Challenge Modes
  • Guild Mythic 5-Mans
  • Transmog runs
  • Horde sister/alt guild
  • Other things as requested/when they crop up


Adult players preferred
  • Older players/couples/those who work days jobs/etc welcome to join
  • Drama- and hate-speech-free environment


Light-to-Medium-RP
  • Scheduled RP events once per week
  • Freeform RP encouraged in the in-between, but not required
  • Events will be a mixture of social, cultural, and adventure/combat


Lastly, we function as a family: We are not looking for tons of new members just to buff numbers; we're looking for people to laugh with, PvX with, RP with, and generally enjoy our WoW time with. Quality over quantity!

----------------------

Apply today!

For further information or to apply, please visit kalimdorcollective.shivtr.com and/or PST an online member and ask for an officer!
