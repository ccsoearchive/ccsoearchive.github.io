The Wyrmcast, Servitors of Lothar edition, is now up! Go check it out at http://wyrmcast.tumblr.com or https://soundcloud.com/wyrmcast. Xortz was a wonderful host and as you will probably be able to tell from listening to the podcast, we had an absolute blast hanging out with him. Jo, M, and I recorded the show on Saturday and when technical difficulties required a re-record, our reaction was YAY WE GET TO HANG OUT SOME MORE. Unfortunately Jo was not able to be there for the second session, but Roiya and Brommidor stepped up admirably.

Jo brought up an insight that I neglected to mention during the re-recording and it may actually be one of the most important things about Servitors of Lothar. So I’m adding it here in an attempt to correct that.

As you can probably tell from the way we laugh and joke, the officers are friendly with each other. But the officers in SoL are not chosen because we’re friends. SoL officers are selected first and foremost on the basis of their good judgement. Ability to handle issues in a mature, thought-out manner, willingness to make RP happen for the whole guild, and strengths that complement each other (and, importantly, help make up for the Guild Leader’s weak points and blind spots) are other key attributes we look for in potential officers.

We firmly believe that this is one of the reasons the Servitors of Lothar have managed to exist and function as an active guild for so long.
