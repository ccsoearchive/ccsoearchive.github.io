*Stares at you awkwardly.*

While garrisons, guilds, roleplay channels and waiting for the next expansion to drop offers the amazingness that we all enjoy during our free time, there remains one thing that Warcraft has not yet been able to provide us with; that would be the ability to trap your best friends in a small box made of stone and then pour molten lava on them until they die screaming and cursing your name.

So that all of your sick and twisted needs are fulfilled - here is such an opportunity. The Silvertongue Minecraft server remains open for all you WoW nerds to enjoy!

More heavily focused on RP survival - there is also permission and space for people seeking creative building. For those wanting an adventure and challenge, however - there is the goal of setting up a few different survival camp groups for competition and PVP on survival, but there is also some kick back/relax time with guildies/friends to find as well.

A few guilds from WRA and MG have also set up RP extensions from WoW onto the server!


While survival is the bread and butter of the server, Creative is available for those who wish it - though there is the guideline/policy of not peeing on other people's stuff. *Clears throat.*

For any questions you have that aren't answered - be sure to leave a comment below. New players are thrust into the wilds, but if you want to join with one of the established Survival groups, be sure to contact me in game!

Super Easy Rules:
    1. Don't be a jerk.
    2. Open chat is mature - and is not heavily moderated. Discrimination, however, and harassment will not be tolerated.
    3. We have easy warp for people to easily transit between areas.
    4. Creative is granted to those who ask. If it's seen that they're abusing it to grief others - it will be removed.
    5. Derpy derp derp trolls and rude people who are disrespectful will be banned at the discretion of any OPs online. Harassment is not tolerated in the slightest. I will take pleasure in destroying your buildings block by block while you watch from the inside of a prison cell if you harass other people or grief or troll them. Report any harassment or griefing to OPs, please.
