Does the sermon allow worried Light slingers trying to form a better grasp on the shadow?


Father Lucas wishes to preach to all races and people of all faiths, hoping to convert one or two in the process. Scholars and people interested in the truth hidden in the dark are welcome and invited to participate by listening, commenting and asking questions. Father Lucas does not claim to have all the answers, nor does he claim that his interpretation of the Shadow is the only truth. Imagine a guy standing on a soapbox, preaching. Differing opinions and interaction is very much wanted.

As long as your character is aware of the situation he is placing himself in, he is more than welcome.

However, a warning:

We do not execute control, we merely guide the audience, if necessary.

If a Light slinger suddenly begins to proclaim that the Light is the only faith and that followers of the Shadow are heretics who'll burn in hell, he will have to expect retaliation. We will not step in and save said Light slinger from the reactions by the characters he angered, as long as said reactions do not endanger the event as a whole.

I am aware that this is a long reply to your simple question, Leileran. Discussions and revelations by former regulars prompted me to write a rather elaborate answer.

However, We remember that you have been a welcome guest of the sermons in the past and that we enjoyed your presence and questions. Of course you are welcome and invited.

I will send you a calendar invite as soon as I log on.

Edit: Wording.
