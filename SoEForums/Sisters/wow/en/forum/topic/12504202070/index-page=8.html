We've gotten a lot of whispers about the increased turn-around rate on calendar invites, so it might be best to post an explanation here where everyone can see it.
A few months back, the invite list hit maximum capacity. Since then, each event has brought in between 45 and 60 people, about half of whom are usually fist-time attendees. This is a good problem to have, but it has made it necessary to continually trim the list, culling the names of those we haven't seen for awhile in favor of those currently participating. In the case of those we know to alt hop, we're trying to send only one invite per person, usually to whichever character we saw last.
Just to be clear, if your name has dropped from the list, is does not mean you are no longer welcome. We're just making newcomers the priority because they're less likely to be aware of when each new event is held. Anyone who shows up and/or contacts one of us is automatically added again.

I hope to see everyone this Saturday. (And at this rate, I just might!)

P.S. - If your name contains a special character, you are my mortal enemy.
