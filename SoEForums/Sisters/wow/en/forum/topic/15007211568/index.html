The Coterie of Ashes has established themselves on Draenor, but still seeks skilled soldiers, researchers, craftsmen, scouts and more to aid them. All who are able-bodied and willing to particiapte in our campaigns across Draenor are invited to report to our headquarters in Frostfire Ridge for enrollment. For the Horde!

Who we are
Guild Master: Muireanne (Raisheal)
Officers: Bayuriel, Lytendrah, Finfarnel, Cagrekk
Senior Members: Yaks, Beryal, Sibra

http://coterieofashes.guildlaunch.com/

<Coterie of Ashes> is a roleplay guild. We have been on Wyrmrest Accord for nearly four years (goodness, where does the time go!), where we were formed as the guild <Parallax> by a core group of friends that is still in the guild to this day. Though we do not have an enormous presence on the server, that's fine by us - we prefer to play as a smaller group of close friends.

Along with our guildmates-as-friends policy, we also prefer to have a more relaxed environment about the guild. We do not have strict activity requirements, and our rules are best simply summed up as: "don't be a jerk." However, on the same token, we prefer members who are mature and upstanding, and able to police themselves. This policy has worked out very well for us!

Among our ranks, you'll find a lot of different kind of roleplayers. Some of us prefer exciting battles and long plots, while others are happiest kicking back at the inn after a day of questing. We have a variety of different races and classes, as well, all of which help to contribute to our overall storyline.

What we do
Lots, actually! We are first and foremost a roleplay guild. What's our general plot? We are a group of highly skilled soldiers, craftsmen, scholars, and more who are sworn to uphold the values of the Horde, and who fight against any corruption or threat to our lives. As for Warlords? We are chartering an expedition to brave these wild new lands, where will we stake our own foothold.

However, that's not our only drive, and we do our best to have new plots for everybody to participate in - we don't much like the idea of having one person own the stage around here. While we fully encourage everybody to participate in the guild's events and storyline, we do have a number of roleplayers who are not in character members of the Coterie, and we are just as happy to give them a home of friendly faces.

For our current expansion, our guild roleplay is exclusively in Draenor. We may return to Azeroth for some things, but for the most part, we've fought to get here, and we're here to stay and make a name for ourselves! We encourage RP out in the world, and are excited to be here in this amazing new world.

Roleplay is not our only focus, either. Many of our members are also avid PVEers, and we have a strong history in the guild of hosting current tier raids, as well as other content like dungeons and challenge modes. We also have a regular raid team that runs Sunday and Monday from 6PM to 9PM server doing normals and leading into heroics.

What else should I know?
Due to the sometimes mature nature of our chat we have an age restriction of 16 and up.

We are currently recruiting all races and classes!

If you are interested in joining us please whisper any of our online members and they will direct you to the right person!

Current plots
The Steamwheedle Preservation Society has come across some quite interesting artifacts, and the archaeologist Finfarnel Teshurr is interested in learning more. He's leading a team of Coterie members on this mission.

Muireanne sees double opportunities in Shadowmoon: A strategic place for an outpost, and an opportunity to learn more about the mysteries of the Light. She seeks to establish diplomatic ties with the draenei living in the valley for trade between the two of them, but trouble's brewing in Karabor.

Upcoming events

  • Monthly guild meeting is the last Saturday of every month at 6PM server.
    Next meeting: March 28th
  • Weekly raid on Sunday and Monday, 6-9PM server time. Permanent members and one-timers are invited.
  • Cursed Woods (March 17th, 7:30PM) Negotiations with the draenei of Shadowmoon to establish trade and safe travel to Shadowmoon have been disrupted by reports of Shadow Council infiltration in Karabor. The Council of Exarchs seeks the Coterie's help in order to prove their intentions.
  • Whispers from the Dark (March 18th, 7:30PM server) One of the Coterie's researches has found a strange artifact. What secrets does it hold?
  • Guild Barbecue (March 20th, 7PM) After the success of the first, the Coterie again gathers at Bladespire Citadel for a party. All are invited to attend!
