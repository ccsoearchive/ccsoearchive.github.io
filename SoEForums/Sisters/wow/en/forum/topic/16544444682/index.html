Hello everyone. As a few of you have been made aware, I have been talking up a program in some channels I call the UTAP, or Undeath Transition Assistance Program.

At its core, the UTAP is a community building tool for the forsaken community at large, and for other forsaken friendly guilds. This is not Forsaken centric only. All are welcome. In an In Character sense, the (TBA timeframe and occurrence) meetings are for newly risen Forsaken, to help them find their way in the strange society they find themselves in. Organizations that are accepting of Forsaken will participate with one or more representatives, which will all speak briefly on what they do and what they are looking for. Players will be given time to peruse the various organizations and speak with them.

Non-Forsaken and not-newly-risen may attend as well if they so desire, if they are looking for assistance in finding a good fit for themselves.

From an OOC standpoint, the UTAP is meant to bring us, as a community of roleplayers, together, with the common goal of helping some of our newer players (and veterans with new characters!) find a good home for their characters, with RP that fits their playstyle and intended character design.

I have a number of guilds backing me already, and the list grows almost daily. If you are in a guild that accepts Forsaken, and would like to participate, please don't hesitate to contact me. This is meant to be something done either once every couple weeks, or once a month, depending on community involvement.

I may edit this as time passes, but I wanted to get the initial advert out there for you guys to view, with some of my ideas on the program, and the direction I want to bring it in. Thank you for your time, happy questing! [quote][/quote]

All right everyone, tenative day set for the UTAP, when it happens, it will most likely be on Sundays. Still unsure as to frequency, if either once a week, every other week, once a month, honestly it all depends on how well things go. Of course with more regular frequency we can bring in more community RP more often, and that is the intended goal of this somewhat regular event.

Please get in contact with me either here or in game if you wish to participate in any fashion! You don't have to be a new forsaken looking for a home, you could be any member of the horde looking to offer your services to the Forsaken at large. Indeed, this is focused towards the Forsaken, but in the end, it is a community building tool.

This sunday I will be gathering as many leaders as possible for an OOC discussion about the UTAP, get a feel for where everyone stands, and introduce everyone to each other. I do have to mention, with the way things like this go, the event won't be very big at first, but with more frequency, and players seeing that we are indeed serious about making this a regular thing, it will gain more attendance and noteriety.
