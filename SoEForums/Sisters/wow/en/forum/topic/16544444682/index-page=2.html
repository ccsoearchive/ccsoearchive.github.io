I would like to thank everyone that came out to last nights UTAP meetup. We had lots of fun with each other, and found homes for a number of new (and old) characters. All in all, this was a great success. I am very happy with the way things went, and we will definitely be doing this again next week on Sunday.

Unless things change, expect to see us running this event every week from here on out!

As always, if you have a guild you would like to integrate into the program, you are more than welcome to attend, but if you would get with me prior, I can ensure you are kept in the loop as far as any changes that might occur.

Thanks again guys, I look forward to this becoming something special for the Forsaken Community!

I would like to ask the guilds which participate to give me a little post in this thread somewhere with information on their guild. The format will be easy enough, I will post an example below.

-----------------------------------------------

Guild: Forsaken Watchers
Information Thread: http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/16780418368
Point of Contact: Stanovic
Purpose: To ease the transition into Undeath for the newly risen, as well as foster better relations with the other races of the Horde.
Restrictions: None
