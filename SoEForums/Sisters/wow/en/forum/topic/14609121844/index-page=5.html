Hi-ho folks! Turwinkle here! Boy oh boy what a trip it has been. We have been to Blizzcon and back. So much to share with everyone. Hard to know where to even begin. I wanted to take the opportunity to give a big thank you to all the awesome folks that I had the great honor to meet in person! Our community is just the best! Hands down! It went too fast. This trip has really revitalized my passion for our community! To do more this coming year! With Legion now on the horizon and all the new stories and of course, Overwatch...I think we will all be going from here to there, and trying our best to balance it all. Turwinkle and I will be doing our very best to highlight for all you folks, the wonderful stories and creative outlets to be found within the Role Play community.

So much is planned for the coming year and I am really excited to start rolling out some of the ideas for all you folks to hopefully enjoy.

Other than to say, Turwinkle and I love you all and am looking forward to seeing you in this awesome game we all call home: World of Warcraft. Keep being the fantastic community that you all are! Continue to foster those friendships and meeting new and interesting Role Players out there! I know I will! :P
