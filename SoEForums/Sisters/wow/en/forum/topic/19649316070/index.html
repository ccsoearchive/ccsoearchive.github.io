Hand-made ads can be found all across the walls and trees of the city advertising for a bakery in old town. Bits of chocolate cover the corners of some of the discovered parchments.

Grand Opening of the Apple Rose Bakery!

Wednesday, November 11

5 am - 10 am, Server Time

Located in “Thane’s Boots” store in Old town between the times of 5am to 10am (Server time). We will be there to brighten your morning every Monday, Wednesday and Friday. For those looking to purchase delicious pastries, coffee, tea, our special ‘Apple Rose,’ or for those looking to have a nice chat in the morning before beginning a hard day's work!

For any and all inquiries please mail Damaris Haskell and or Gavia Thornwood.

Damaris,

The bakery will need takeaway menus. Make separate ones for the noble houses offering catering and special desserts for parties. Have both versions distributed.

-- Your lord

Damaris leaned back in her chair and rubbed her face. Her hands ached. The order from Demetrius Devereaux had been waiting for her when she awoke. She had done a rough draft before going out to hang the bakery's sign with Gavia and Verlai, revised it upon her return, and set to work.

Now, she had a master for each version, and ten of each to distribute today. The Blue Recluse, the Pig and Whistle, even the Lion's Pride Inn in Goldshire...those would be good places to start.

For someone else, not her. She had to get to Ironforge and the printers there. She'd drop off a few at The Commons when she was done.

THE APPLE ROSE BAKERY
GRAND OPENING WEDNESDAY, 11/11
HOURS OF OPERATION: M-W-F, 5 AM – 10 AM

MENU:
Muffins: (blueberry, corn, lemon and poppy seed)
Bagels: (plain, poppy seed, cinnamon)
Cupcakes: (chocolate, white, yellow – butter cream or chocolate frosting)
Apple Rose:(flaky pastry with apple slices rolled into it, dusted with powdered sugar and cinnamon)
Toppings: Butter, cream cheese, honey, jams (blueberry, blackberry, raspberry)
Drinks: Milk, spring water, coffee, tea (honeymint, chai)

Catering services and desserts for holiday parties available.
