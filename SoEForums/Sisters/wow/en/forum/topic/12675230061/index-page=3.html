Back to the top with you...

Want RP?

We have weekly RP events. Our tribe social event on Tuesday was very well attended in Sun Rock Retreat and we invited several new members. We regularly have events on Saturdays as well. I have run into many tribe members in Thunder Bluff and elsewhere and had some wonderful spontaneous walkup RP, too.

Want raiding?

We've taken down Highmaul on Normal and Heroic and we're almost done with Blackrock Foundry on Normal. We even tried Heroic Foundry last night on one of our two raiding nights. (Normally Wednesdays and Fridays).

Want PvP?

Come join in the defense of shu'halo lands against the cowardly dogs of the Alliance wherever they may threaten us! Thunder Bluff will always be defended no matter what...even by meek little seers like Rushala! And our tribe remembers what happened to Taurajo...attacks on any other shu'halo settlements will not be tolerated.

Bonus points for any more bulls that wish to join us. ;)
