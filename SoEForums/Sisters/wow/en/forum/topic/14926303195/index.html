Who.
<Vanitas>, a guild led by a co-GM force consisting of Malus and Kefka/Historia is now recruiting into the WOD expansion! We are a med RP guild - as we look into other aspects such as small-time PvE and have members knowledgeable in PvP - devoted to having realistic evil RP. We're based around a storyline and weekly RP events, as well as thriving around personal character strife and development.

We believe that most stories need conflict and we wish to be the force behind that conflict. Not only to drive the stories of Protagonist guilds, but to bolster our own storyline, characters, and experiences.

What.
What you can expect from <Vanitas>:
-Weekly RP events
-Friendly management
-Members who believe in fairness in RP
-Balanced conflict RP: Meaning we not only explore and delve into standard /emote RP, but we also cater to those who are more favorable to /rolling for their battles!
-Friendly members who respect Warcraft's lore.
We do -not- run around Stormwind proclaiming our diabolical intentions, but rather converse OOCly with protagonist guilds to drive IC storylines for *everyone*; guild, personal character, and realm.
We also enforce a "no discrimination" policy.
We are currently working on a website (cleaning up the old one) and I will update this thread as needed.

What we look for in members:
Good attitude
-Friendliness
-Fairness (No God-Modding)
-Roleplay
Side note: If you RP something out of the ordinary; such as a furbolg, dragon, grummle, mecha-gnome, etc.; we would love to get a feel for your character and storyline to see if it's compatible with the forces of evil!
Side-side note: Due to the purpose of roleplay and this guild, paladins are accepted on rare case-by-case deals, just to ensure things go smoothly, of course.

Officers:
Current officers to talk to about joining:
Guildmaster: Malus
Guildmaster: Kefka/Historia
Human Resources: Cyy
Officer Third In Command: Dinath
Officer: Baalhalla
Officer: Reife
Officer: Konghuman
Officer: Khaled

Go to our website http://vanitas.shivtr.com/ for more information about the guild as well as application services. We hope to see you in the new expansion and on Draenor!

There's is a long term agenda. ICly, it's to gather all of the outcasts, the 'criminals', and any other unmentionables into one cohesive unit, banding and binding together for a more unified force.

OOC, mainly our goal is to keep those good guilds on their toes. Keeping a nice steady, and realistic storyline for the people on our server to enjoy and love. Storylines filled with dark undertones and gritty moral questions.

For instance, there have been several people who did fight for good guilds, join my guild, then later tell me that they no longer really see us as villains mainly because we have reasons for what we do and those reasons make sense enough that the lines between black and white become blurred.

This' really what keeps RP exciting is to have antagonists who are playing for the long-term win; not your "Arthas" characters who are so black and white that their end is effectively inevitable from the start ("one-shot"). Recurring antagonists that have a justified reason for pursuing their agenda are some of the most exciting things to interact with in RP. Not just evil, but justified to the degree that 'good' guys are able to relate to them and understand why they do what they do.

I'm mostly curious about this being as Lunarim is on the lawful evil side of the alignment spectrum, granted that our goal is primarily to antagonize the Horde- not so much the Alliance (although that is certainly possible).
