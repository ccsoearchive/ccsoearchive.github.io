Welcome to the Sisters of Elune roleplaying server.

Below is a list of our active guilds*. Guild leaders/officers are encouraged to make an updated post for their guild using the following template. Please stick to the format:

-------------

Faction:
Guild Name:
Guild Type: (PvE / Raiding, PvP, Roleplaying, Social)
Contacts:
Web site:
Recruiting: (Yes or No)
Information:


--------------

How the new sticky works!

The GM or an officer posts a the template with their guild's information. I will go ahead and update the list to add your guild's name and a link to your post. Feel free to talk as much as you'd like about your guild, your post your rules (so long as its within the forum's Code of Conduct! ^^)

If there's any changes in name or description simply go ahead and change your post to reflect it! :3


Guildmasters!

Make sure you go ahead and post your information! So I can add you to the list!


Navigating the list

The guilds have been organized with the tags provided by the guild master or officer as well as faction. The categories are:
  • Social
  • PVE / Raiding
  • PVP
  • RP (Role-play)

    • To navigate to any of them simply click "control + F" and type whichever area you're interested in seeing:
  • Alliance
  • Social: ALLY-SOC
  • PVE / Raiding: ALLY-PVE
  • PvP: ALLY-PVP
  • RP: ALLY-RP
  • Horde
  • Social: H-SOC
  • PVE / Raiding: H-PVE
  • PvP: H-PVP
  • RP: H-RP

    • *List is still being populated!
