Though we are all pleased by the return of our beloved High Priest.....

......The truth must come out. This week, the Leviathan will set out on a quest to find the truth- Who is the informant that has not only infiltrated their ranks, but has been leaking information as well?

Only a few days past, Crown Chancellor Fulthen hosted a secret meeting where members of the Leviathan discussed potential persons that could be their prime suspects. Utilizing some common sense and cross checking a list of individuals who make up the body of the Coalition, the group agreed that a small list of members could easily be the informant. Headlining this list? None other than the High Priest herself.

But Silaine was not the only one- and the other names presented were no less troubling. A pair of powerful wizards, their location, and their reason for disappearance, unknown. A competent field agent savvy to the Coalition's greater secrets. An elusive Master who has been speaking through her Apprentice since the Leviathan's dawning days. The Crown Apothecary, who's own motivations and activities are foreign to all but none, and the aloof Grand Magus, who's vanity alone keeps her away from those who she deems lesser than herself, which is to say everyone. Each individual merits their own suspicions, but in the Forsaken society, where does one separate normal behavior from true deception?

Will career liars be able to catch their false friends in the web of deceit?

What will come remains to be seen......................

Despite mounting tensions within the Coalition the High Priest has called for an investigation of a suspicious letter. Addressed to The Leviathan was a small scrap of parchment with a single word written, "help". The embossed watermark on the back of the scrap parchment confirmed that it originated in Tarren Mill.
