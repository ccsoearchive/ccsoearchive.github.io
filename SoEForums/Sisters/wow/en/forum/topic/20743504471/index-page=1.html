Greetings friends, I am Dreyfus Xano.

I am proud to announce another ribbon event for Autism Awareness Month 2016. For those of you who do not know what this is, this is an event that I have hosted every April almost since coming to WRA for Autism Awareness. Essentially, I invite anyone from the Alliance and Horde to join me wearing Red, Blue and Yellow robes and dresses at the place in question. Previously, I had it at Menethil Lake just outside of Menethil but I am going to change it because I have had trouble with water walking messing up my shots.

Details:This will be an IC and OOC event to so I do not mind either.

When: Saturday, April 30th at 7 pm server.

Where: Iceflow Lake nearby Crushcog's Arsenel nearby Gnomeregan. Warlocks would be appreciated for summons. This area should be far away enough from any guards and any rogue mechs.

To make things interesting to, I want to host a Xmog competition as well with a Red Blue and Yellow theme. I'll have some gold available for any WRAers and I will offer a different type of prize for anyone not from WRA.

Hope to see you there.

Dreyfus Xano

Thank you to all of you for your support so far.

The last thing I want to do is force anyone to come to this if they feel uncomfortable, but I do enjoy offering some fun events to.

For me, Autism has been a weird thing. Sometimes it is almost a super power for me and sometimes it can be my worst enemy. Almost Spiderman-esque. I feel like because of my unique experiences I can be a positive force for something good.

For the record, I do not like hearing the term of autism or being autistic being used as an insult. It is thrown around way more than it should. This is not meant to demean anyone so I will not be paying any attention to any trolling posts. I have had enough trolls at my improv shows to know when to acknoweldge them or not.
