Posted on boards on Stormwind, Exodar, Shattrath, and various other places is a sign, written in Draenei, in glowing blue.

Brothers and Sisters under the Naaru it is time for Revola. Many of the younglings may not understand the significance of this term. Revola is a time to come back to the old ways and exault if former glories. All draenei are invited to come to our former capital, Shattrath, the City of Light in the common tongue. The Hand of Argus will personally be hosting this event along with any other draenei who wish to volunteer. We will travel throughout the Old Capital, and tell the younglings in the Orphanage of past times. I believe it is time we explain to the younglings of our Old Ways. We will revel in traditions we have had for millenia, and tell stories of Argus and Draenor. Will you come to Revola? Will you Return?

For the Naaru, For the Light!

Exarch Sholaad.

(OOC Information: This will be held Friday May 13th, 7:00 server time. Shattrath City. We will be meeting in A'dal's presence and then go to the lower city for the celebration.)

Kayten glanced at the paper and to her companion. His crimson eyes scanned the informative poster and his face remained impassive.
" Do you plan on going?"
He asked, reigning in his raptor and watching her gently pick up the announcement. Smiling, Kayten returned the Poster to the board and fastened it with several pegs, determined it should remain in sight. She mounted her ram slowly and glanced at him, her milky, unfocused eyes distant.
" Shattrath is no home of mine...but it is a pleasant memory. What cause have I to claim membership in my order if i do not show honor to the past? Yes. I will go."
She turned and made her way through the streets towards the Cathedral of the Light, trailed by her silent, thoughtful companion.
