The Apple Rose Bakery -- New Hours

"...and so, with the majority of our custom happening in the late morning, we decided to implement a hours of operation change.Granted, it is early in the business' existence, but it seemed for the best at this time. And there is the upcoming holidays. We had a request for rum cake, which currently is not on the menu. Later hours would give us more time to experiment with recipes and see what might be more suitable for the season. We've also had inquiries if we're only going to carry 'sweet stuff'.' Aneokame has offered to make sausage and egg croissants, and teach us how to make them. "

Demetrius Devereaux, enthroned behind his den's massive desk, looked at her. He drummed the fingers of his right hand, resting between the plate with his BLT and his glass of lemonade, in a slow, steady beat.

Damaris couldn't read his mood or his intention; his face had no expression at all. Pushing down a frisson of fear, she continued. She forced her hands to still at her sides; she kept wanting to fall into parade rest.

"I know you moved Gavia to House Arontala as your emissary, but she's very enthusiastic about the Apple Rose. I thought we could have a day or two of morning hours if you and Arontala's lord agreed. I don't think Gavia would object." And I miss her. She was not about to admit that, however.

"That's all I have to report, my lord."

Demetrius looked at her, his fingers maintaining their slow tattoo.

"Okay." He smiled. "Sounds good. You're doing excellent work, Damaris. Do keep it up."

Or else hung between them unspoken. "Thank you, my lord. I will put in an order for new flyers and takeaway menus today."

"Disturbringly organized as always. You may go. Oh -- wait." Demetrius reached for his glass, swirling the lemonade. "Is it true you have rose-shaped ice cubes at the bakery?"

"We do. A mage owed me a favor."

Demetrius smiled again. "Have some made of crows, Damaris."

"Yes, my lord."

***

THE APPLE ROSE BAKERY
HOURS OF OPERATION: M-W-F, 2 PM - 5 PM
MYSTERY MORNINGS! (Open in the morning two other days of the week, subject to change)

MENU:
Muffins: (blueberry, corn, lemon and poppy seed)
Bagels: (plain, poppy seed, cinnamon)
Cupcakes: (chocolate, white, yellow – butter cream or chocolate frosting)
Apple Rose:(flaky pastry with apple slices rolled into it, dusted with powdered sugar and cinnamon)
Sugar cookies(New!)
Sausage and egg croissants (New!)
Toppings: Butter, cream cheese, honey, jams (blueberry, blackberry, raspberry)
Drinks: Milk, spring water, coffee, tea (honeymint, chai)

Catering services and desserts for holiday parties available.
Seasonal treats for the holidays coming soon!
