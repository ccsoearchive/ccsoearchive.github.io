Today, Ruthar of Phoenix Guard completed his musical memorial to Zelara, an astounding composition henceforth known as "Flight of the Silverwing (Zelara's Theme)".

Driving, Soaring

Zipping through the dense foliage of wooded regions, the Silvering Sentinels pursue their foes with deadly accuracy and heated intensity. The years and years of training have prepared them for every skirmish, crafted each member into a being of supreme dedication and performance. Though no longer standing at the helm of the organization, Zelara Razorleaf will forever be remembered as one of the most prominent members of the Silverwing Sentinels.

If you are interested in listening to this amazing piece, please feel free to look on our website at silverwingsentinels.shivtr.com and scroll down a tad, or simply check it out on Ruthar's soundcloud at https://soundcloud.com/taylor-arthur-goodson/flight-of-the-silverwing-zelaras-theme.

Zelara will never be forgotten, and her spirit will live on in our guild, and this song. Thank you from the bottom of our hearts.
