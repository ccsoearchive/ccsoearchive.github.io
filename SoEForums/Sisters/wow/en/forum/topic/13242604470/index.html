Is your guild kinda...dead? Tired of not being taken seriously because you roll Gnome? Come talk to us.

    • G4 is the oldest, largest and most active all-Gnome guild on Wyrmrest, and quite possibly in all of Warcraft.

    • Founded in 2010. G4 features experienced, stable leadership with a solid track record of success, and a thriving community.

    • Regularly scheduled events including: Gnome RP Workshops, Retro-Raid Nights, Casual Progression Raiding and Guild Meetings.

    • G4 is proud of its Reputation in the Community, as well as its Family Friendly Social Environment, and Helpful Members.

    • Generous Repair Allowances for leveling characters, universal access to a well-stocked Guild Bank, and Vent channel.

    • Guild Website Access featuring Guild News, active General & RP Forums, Character Journals, and Screenshot- Artwork Galleries.

    • Our community is very creative, featuring Writers, Roleplayers, Video makers and Artists (including the author of the well-known Trigonometry web comic series).
