The Sunreavers
"Glory to the sin'dorei"

Description:
Followers of the great archmage Aethas, the Sunreavers represented the Sin'dorei in the ranks of the Kirin Tor, and helped bring the Horde to the sanctuary city of Dalaran. Now banished from their beloved city, following an unfortunate incident implicating a few members, the Sunreavers have made Silvermoon City their new, if not temporary, home.
The Sunreavers describe themselves primarily as explorers of magic, but certainly become rather millitaristic when push comes to shove.

Races:
Mostly Sin'dorei, though Taurens, Undead and Trolls have been seen within their ranks for years (Sunreaver Pavilion, Northrend/Isle of Thunder, Pandaria). A few Pandaren have also shown an interest in being emissaries for their people.

Alignment:
Quel'Thalas and ther Sin'dorei first, Azeroth second, Horde third.

Leader(s):
Magister Axiann Sungazer leads the local Silvermoon group, as their are Sunreavers stationned all over the globe. He reports weekly to archmage Aethas himself.

Saltyboot Cartel
"Ain't no salt in my boots; this is for real!"

Description:
A Cartel that came about from the desire for one thing: gold. Using brute strength, agile finesse, and calculated finances, Saltyboot Cartel looks to expand their influence by protecting their interests abroad, and winning over the public back home. Saltyboot Cartel seeks to penetrate all types of operations, whether it be fighting alongside members of the Horde to protect valued resources, loot large amounts of treasure from a dungeon deep within Azeroth, or sell wares made by members of the Cartel.

Races:
Any and all are accepted, and any all specialties, interests, and skills are accepted amongst applicants. Saltyboot sees potential in any new employee, and will find a place for anyone that applies.

Alignment:
The Cartel itself is above all, the core of their being. However, they do see the protection of their business partners as important, so they defend and help out other organizations if need be, and especially the Horde for fear of losing areas of business.

Leader(s):
'Ol Fizz Saltyboot is the Trade Prince of the Cartel. He's looking for aspiring applicants to serve as his Barons of Operations, so he can be less hands on, and more visible to the public.

Recruitment:
Please message Fizzget in game. See the following Saltyboot Cartel Forum Post for more information.
http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/20420204731

None as of yet, but will come in the future as more employees are hired. [u][/u]
