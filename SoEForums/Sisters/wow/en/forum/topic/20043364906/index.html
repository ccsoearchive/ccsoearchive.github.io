A Letter From the Desk of Apothecary Darkwater:

For years, the magnificent minds within the Apothecarium have been known only for their capabilities to bring death and desctruction to Azeroth by chemical means. Indeed even I, Aeterik Darkwater, once worked tirelessly to perfect the toxic mixtures in the not so far past. That said, today in an effort to better the quality of life on Azeroth as a whole and mend the relationship between the Forsaken and Horde, I announce the founding of the Darkblight Alchemical Company (D.A.C.).

The focus of the D.A.C., while simultaneously providing contracted potions and invigorating elixers, will be research on some of the worst afflictions that plague the citizens of Azeroth and treating them by chemicql methods. From the madness of Saronite poisoning to the varying degrees of Fel corruption; From the irradiated lepers to the rotbrained of the Undercity. We of the D.A.C. intend to use our years of alchemical study and practice to further the Horde by methods that do not focus on war and expansion.

The company will consist of two main branches. Manufacturing and R&D will be overseen by myself, and focus on the goals we will set internally. The company's Security will be run by the hand picked Captain of the Death Guard, Morwynell Dreadheart. Due to the sensitive nature of our coming research, all Apothecaries and Death Guards involved will be sworn to secrecy and the work done under D.A.C. budgets will remain with the company in the event of a termination.

Training, both in alchemical work and combat, will be offered to those who choose to join our medical initiative.

For the Horde!
Aeterik M. Darkwater, M.A.
Chief of Staff

The Inventory (Will be Updated as Research Completes):

  • Health and Mana Potions: Basic tinctures focused on rejuvenating the living in combat situations, tested thoroughly for safety purposes.
  • Fel Dampening Elixer: Originally designed in hopes of remedying Aeterik's fel corruption, the compound was found only to help prevent severe corruption. Made to order, take as instructed.
  • Acidic Compound DW181: A chemical designed for coating weapons and projectiles, DW181 is reactive to flesh but nearly harmless to the weapon. Causes severe chemical burns on surface, potential medical emergencies if injected/ingested.

    • The Semantics ((OOC Stuff)):

    Hello everyone, I am Aeterik and I'm really excited to announce that the D.A.C. is hiring! We're looking for a hand full of creative and active Forsaken players that are interested in joining the continuation of a story Morwynell and I have been part of for ages. A brief, brief synopsis;

    A plaguebringer who worked previously under Grand Apothecary Putress has aims at continuing the heretical masterminds work but sees the flaws in his predecessor's plans. Publicly he begins an initiative to mend ties between the Forsaken and the Horde while working quietly behind the scenes. Recruiting both scholars and soldiers, he founds a company to use as a front while they research tirelessly to bring about the true end to the living who cast them aside...

    Oh, isn't that exciting? I think so. Now, we will be conducting interviews for this company as it is a job. Our goal is 'Quality over Quantity', so we'd much rather have a small group that works together than a huge collective that barely ever interacts. Anyone interested should definitely find either Aeterik or Morwynell in game, and be sure to be ready- FOR SCIENCE!

    Dark Lady Watch Over You,
    Aeterik M. Darkwater

    P.S. Any guilds that may want to hang out, maybe start some feisty cuffs, are also more than welcome to give us a shout- It's no fun being bad if there's no good guys to pick on!

    P.S.S. The above letter can very much be used as IC knowledge if you wish to have a reason to find Aeterik or Morwynell. Some organizations may receive a 'sample pack' of potions as a heads up along with the letter. Let me know if you want to be one of those organizations!

    Like Aeterik, I've been dead for some time now. Wrath further increased this interest I firmly believe. Legion spoilers don't hurt that either.

    I think the main RP servers, and really all RP servers, need dedicated people to all races for RP. Being on the Forsaken side of things for years now I've noticed an ebb and flow kind of community. Population increases and decreases based on whats popular. Like in most things really.
    Our goal with the D.A.C is to have a small niche of players that all they do... is play Forsaken. A race based guild in every sense of the word. Players that know the Forsaken lore. Players that -play- the lore. Even players that can teach the lore to newcomers. This is the foundation for any good RP community.
    That's what we're forming this guild around is that approach to this idea of Forsaken RP. We're not looking to create this behemoth of a 250+ member RP guild. The individual looses out on opportunities if that is the case. Events become even more difficult to coordinate. Ever try to have a small RP event scheduled, only to have 15 people show up? The individuals story becomes lost in a sea of emotes and speech. Degrades the experience for everyone involved. We created this as much for ourselves as we did the community. So here is at -least- two deaders that are here to stay. Look forward to be a part of the WrA community, even excited to the same ends.

    Cheers!
