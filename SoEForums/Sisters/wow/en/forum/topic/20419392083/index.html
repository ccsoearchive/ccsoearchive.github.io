Slogan:

"A one-stop shop for your tailoring requirements and desires, partnered in kind with seamstresses and leather-workers. In addition to our fine quality materials, you can order your garments with an enchanted customization. The options are endless!"

___*____________________*____________________*____________________*___

Order Requests (OOC)

Not only is there role-play to be had on the days the shop is open for browsing, but you can visit the website:
( http://aethlvohl.enjin.com/forum/m/35606765/viewforum/6605670 )
and order particular ones and with specific enchantments if you prefer. We don't mind making just about anything you'd like, but keep in mind that the fraction of the cost comes with the price of role-play! More options for order to come.

___*____________________*____________________*____________________*___

Donating to the Community

"At Aethlvohl Tailoring, Co., we hold the community close to our hearts with sentimental value. From the refugees of Westfall and Hillsbrad, to the wounded soldiers who risk their lives and yet come back without a penny to their name, we aim to take care of our own within the Alliance.

Are you perhaps a new adventurer, and the prices of the supplies and equipment you need to get your start are simply too expensive? A visit to the shop will see you fitted in to an entire outfit of clothing and moderately sized bags to get you on your feet.

Are you interested in assisting us with donations or materials? We accept anything a person is willing to offer, and 100% of the proceeds go towards helping the community and civilians of the Alliance."


___*____________________*____________________*____________________*___

Allies & Affiliates

"At Aethlvohl Tailoring, Co., we’re very interested in connecting with other guilds, businesses, and factions in order to do our part in outfitting the war efforts, adventurers, and civilians. In addition, we like to unite with like-minded and charitable companies so that we may boost the benefits for everyone involved.

If you find yourself interest in ATCo considering this regard, please contact Gwynona!"

OOC: A letter from Gwynona's player.

Hello Wyrmrest Accord Roleplaying Community!

We're a new guild, and new faces on the server itself. However, the members of this guild are both new and old friends generally seeking other like-minded individuals to have some good ole fun. We do just about all aspects of the game other than official raiding and PvP (though you will most certainly catch us getting transmog gear, or PvPing for fun once in a blue moon if enough of us are online at the same time!) However, I must stress that this guild has a heavy roleplaying focus as the main concentration.

In regards to the people we're looking for, we don't have an age restriction as we've replaced the need to filter out any fear of 'maturity' or 'personality clashes' with our initiation phase of recruitment. How this works in a nutshell is, if this RP idea of a guild interests you in any aspect, and you want to hang out with a bunch of hilarious folks who can't get enough of the game and RP, then you PM myself, Ivick, or Morlanin in game. Tell us your interests, we'll explain the guild in further to you and what we expect / don't expect, and if that's your cup of tea -- we invite you. For two weeks, we will treat you like one of our own without any awkward, "Who the heck is this new guy?" phase, and if after 2 weeks the guild members vote yay, then you're in. It's easy as that. We feel confident that after two weeks of interaction with you, we can tell if you're going to enjoy yourself with us as much as we will - or not.

If you're new to roleplaying, that is absolutely okay! We're chill with teaching the craft, with assisting with lore (who doesn't still ask questions from time to time, anyway?) Don't feel intimidated, and if you've got concerns, come talk to me. I'm an open book and I'd love to help put your mind at ease.

We're most likely looking to gather a range of 20-30 individual player accounts in the guild for the time being. We don't want too many, but if we get to a point where that's a desire in our hearts, we'll discuss it like adults about the maintenance that goes with that. For now, though, expect a small group until we get there. Have patience, and have fun. :)

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact us either on the website or in game by whispering Gwynona, Ivick, or Morlanin. I look forward to seeing you guys in game and in-character!

Gwynona
ATCo Leader
