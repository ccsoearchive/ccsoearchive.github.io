The Herald walks at a slow pace around the Forsaken lands as she visits each location of interest to observe and admire the festivities of the great holiday. She stops and smiles under her hooded mask as she sees two guild mates help explain the lessons of the holiday to a newly risen, proud she is to be part of such a guiding group of brethren.

At the next Inn she notices a clean hole poked in one of her posts. " Curious." She speaks aloud as she ponders what could of caused it. She shrugs to herself and continues on her way, happy to be Forsaken.
