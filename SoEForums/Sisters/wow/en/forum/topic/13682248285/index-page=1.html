"The bell has been struck, a call to the hunters of the night. The shadowed blades, minds, and brawn have been called upon to stalk the darkness. We shall rend the veil that masks the cults, the demons, the wicked and the paranormal, striking them down with the shadows they have called their own for too long.

We shall serve this world in our duty to exterminate the vile creatures that skitter under the cover of darkness. We shall strike fear into the hearts of the corrupt.

Our bows, blades, and magic are the herald for their death knells, chiming across their blackened souls.

Let the wicked beware. Gravestone has arrived.

- Vex"

(( Heavy, chaotic neutral, Roleplay guild. Based upon the pursuit, expulsion and/or eradication of the paranormal, undead, demonic, cultist, and elemental threat.

Open to any and all classes/races. Best suited for any chaotic neutral character looking to join an organization that hunts down Lawful/Neutral/Chaotic orientated evil entities of any form.

Ill-suited for any character whom holds any Old God, demonic (aside from a reasonably roleplayed warlock), evil, insane (if contradictory to the focal philosophy of the guild) and cultist ties. Also poorly suited for any Lawful or Neutral Good oriented characters due to conflicts in interest.

Drop Vexonia-WyrmrestAccord an in-game mail or whisper for more information or if interested in applying. Quality Role Play is a non-negotiable standard that will be monitored and enforced during your membership within the guild.

Opening up our doors once again for the coming expansion! Feel free to contact any of our officers or drop a line on our guild's site: gravestone.shivtr.com! ))

]Sooo this is going to be a very long post, but I want to be as thorough as I can. Seeing as how Gravestone is new to WRA and as such; owe it to the veterans of the server and devoted RP'ers, to fully explain such an answer to your question. Also, I don't want to leave anything un-addressed.

I promise to return to the light-hearted Vexonia I am at heart as soon as I am through!

Regarding Inducting Members

As I'll cover below, we don't really interact with folks like these ICly. OOCly we'll be friendly, treat them as another player, but as we have obviously different goals and interests in roleplay, our paths shouldn't and won't be crossing. Hunting down or attacking, verbally or otherwise, any character without consent of the player is against our code of conduct. We'd much rather be approached about a hunt than make one of our own, on that note, as deciding "prey" out of the playerbase is FAR too close to unwarrented harrassment, and that is utterly unacceptable.

To be brutally honest, all of those examples are just simple out right rejected.

I like to -build- on lore, but within reason. No futas, kitty-princess-gods, etc etc. We do not accept such radical examples. We uphold a very high standard of quality, of which is screened during the IC and OOC interviews. Taking careful note of the character's execution, background, heritage, over all depth.

I have no patience for lore-breaking Role Play. I came from a guild that had that frequently and I did not enjoy it in the least. That and the fact I am quite the lore freak, so it'd be like a sin against my own conduct to let something in that would be such a perversion of lore.

I hate to sound so fire and brimstone, but Gravestone has no fit for that style of lore-breaking form. And I want that message to be very, very clear.

This goes along with God-Modding and Meta-gaming. Non-negotiable issue, for the very reason it is not only a terrible image for a guild, but taints the server's integrity as well. I can assure you Gravestone will not be a source for such abuse. On my word.

On our site we state:

Recognition of the game's universe.

Quality roleplay is a non-negotiable standard for becoming a member of Gravestone. While we do not shun any creative liberties taken upon the lore of World of Warcraft, please keep your roleplay ideas within reason and applicable to the set lore given in-game and through official lore sources. We do not accept any radical breaks from the World of Warcraft's universe. Time machines in the form of a blue police box, fallen angels, and cat-girls are a no-go.

If you feel your character might contradict this statement, our members are more than happy to discuss the issue. As we uphold a standard of keeping an open mind, we expect applicants and members to do the same, to reach compromise and understanding.

A love and need for quality roleplay is what gave birth Gravestone, and we carry that passion with us to this day. Gravestone's leaders and members will monitor and moderate any possible infractions that might upset this standard. If this issue does occur, Gravestone leaders will politely approach the member and speak with them privately.

(In regards to your first question.) Now, while approaching/approached by players like this, of course we are respectful and polite. We don't harass or slander them. But, if things were to progress beyond simple side talk or banter, we would explain our standing politely and explain that such character-forms aren't too congruent for Gravestone, or interacting with Gravestone.

Our conditions go on to address God-modding/Meta-Gaming:

Consideration of other roleplayers and players.

If you do paragraph roleplay in public arenas, please keep it reasonable and as concise as possible. We love and welcome any and all roleplay forms(as in mechanics of posts), but others are not so accepting. Keep in mind that a large series of paragraphs in a public area can drown out others' roleplay.

Moderate your roleplay behaviors.

God-modding and meta-gaming are formats of roleplay we do not accept nor condone. This behavior constructs a poor representation for our members and guild. Likewise, it is frowned upon within the community. We possess little patience for these actions.

(Again, a no tolerance policy is in place here.)

As far as what we hunt; we hunt Lore-friendly targets, with congruent stories to reasonable, lore friendly and player friendly aspects.

Overall we do not heckle, slander or harass your example players. We just choose not to take it beyond superficial chatter, if they reciprocate a respectful demeanor. Or we just avoid such encounters all together.
