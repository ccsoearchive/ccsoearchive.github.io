Attentive citizens of Stormwind, Darnassus and Ironforge might have noticed the sudden appearance of overtly colorful posters pinned to message boards, walls, trees and occasionally on the backs of unwitting passers-by.
Veiwable here: http://i.imgur.com/r43D2W3.png


What is Bonk?
Bonk is a Terenas Foundation hosted community roleplaying event to both promote the Terenas Foundation and celebrate a breakthrough in rear-mounted storage vessel technology! Participants will be given a foam sword from the provided foam sword rack, and faced off against other players in one-on-one and/or free for all combat. Anyone is welcome to come along, and no prior notice is necessary to participate, simply show up at 7:00PM server time on 25/09/15, grab a sword and choose your rival!

Where is Bonk Held?
A group will be formed prior to the event, which we will use to summon anyone and everyone to the tournament grounds, since our location is not accessible by foot. For those wishing to make their own way there, simply head to Darnassus and fly to the small lake a short distance behind the Temple of the Moon.

How We Do
The first half of the competition will involve one-on-one combat, in which victory will be claimed by bonking your opponent three times. Each victor will move onto the next round, where they will be pitted against other victors in tournament style, until a single victor remains. The combatant that claims victory in the first leg of the competition will win the title of 'Bonk Champion' to wear around Wyrmrest Accord with pride!

The final half of the competition will consist of all out warfare, in which anyone willing to participate will be thrown into a free for all match in which its every man for himself! The one left standing at the end of the death match will claim to title of 'Bonk Brawler' to strike admiration and fear into the hearts of both friend and foe across Wyrmrest Accord.

Prizes? Prizes!
In addition to the prestigious titles listed above, victors will claim four of the Terenas Foundation's newest advancement in backpack technology! Featuring a premium space management system, allowing comfortable transportation of up to 30 items per unit, sleek weight distributing straps to lighten even the heaviest of burdens, a whistle and flashlight for peace of mind when exploring even the darkest of dungeons, and the acclaimed liquid enabled lining (patent pending) that put Terenas Foundation bags on the map!

What Else?
  • Bonk is intended to be a fun event to bring a whole bunch of different personalities together from across Wyrmrest Accord. With so many people, there's likely to be some differences of opinion. To help dissuade any antisocial behavior, guards will be stationed throughout the tournament grounds and weapons will not be permitted.

  • In the interest of good sport, stealth abilities will be banned during bonk combat and judges reserve the right to temporarily hold combat if they believe fair play is in question.

  • Feel free to contact either Kaiyoshi, Oshiral or Katrignis in-game for further information about the event.

    Deep Inside the Shadowy Shadows of Stormwind City ... a man and his bags prepare.

    "ToNIGHT, my friend is the day you finally may see the Light." Astianel Katrignis gently ran a hand through the Hexweave Bag's sturdy straps. "Oh, how far we have come." A single tear ran down his cheek as he looked upon the attached whistle and light.

    The Hexweave Bag glimmered what could have been a loving smile. If it could speak, it would most surely say something of affection, hatred, or indifference.

    "Hush, now. You must rest." Katrignis closed the satchel ever so softly. "It is your big day. We do not need you over reaching yourself." He tilted his head to the side as another singular tear fell.

    Hexweave Bag laid there, motionless, without much -- if any -- of a response. If it had sentience, it would be heartbroken at the upcoming loss of its Father, but a longing and unbridled excitement for its new home.

    He would repeat this ritual with the other storage-capable-devices until he knew they were optimal and ready to be dispensed ... with a heavy, heavy heart.
