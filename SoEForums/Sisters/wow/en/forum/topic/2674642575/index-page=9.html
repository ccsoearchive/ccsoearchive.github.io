Overall this is an amazing idea of how to go about updating Blizzard models and adding a more customized and connected feel to the game. The only thing I dislike is some of the other model suggestions...here.

6. Sub-Races
In the lieu of (or combined with!) these overall customizations, consider allowing players access to the variant skins or functional models that exist.

The following list is only a handful of the model spectrum available in game, yet inaccessible. Note, although many would be consider lore bending, this does display what amount of functionality each model currently posses. (Ie: Can they wear armor? Do they have emotes? Genders? Etc).

All - Death Knight - Twilight Cult - Cult of the damned

http://i.imgur.com/Xx2Xm.jpg
Humans - Vrykul
Dwarf - Dark Iron - Wildhammer - Earthen
Gnome - Leper Gnome - MechaGnome - Sand Gnome
Night Elves - Druid of the Flame
Draenei - Eredar - Broken
Worgen - Northrend Variety

http://i.imgur.com/0yUBY.jpg
Orc - Mag'har Orc- Dragonmaw Clan - Fel Orc
Troll - Forest Troll - Frost Troll - Sand Troll - Dire Troll
Tauren - Tanuka
Forsaken - Forsaken Elves (aka Dark Rangers) - Valkyrie
Blood Elves - Felblood Elves - Wretched - High Elves - Darkfallen (ICC elves)
Goblin - Giblin


Some of these may be 'sub races' in a sense but are hardly connected even as far as in-game lore goes. Some of them don't even exist at all anymore in-game because we killed them. Others just have no place, some would die before they stepped into a city. Half of them don't belong in the starting zone. While fun for vanity items and play I think I'd have to beat my head against the desk if I saw twenty Darkfallen or Eredar running down the city.

However, some of these are great ideas! Trolls having more diverse models to acompany the different subspecies, the Forsaken having other undead counterparts for MANY races not just humans would be amazing. Draenei being able to play as Broken, while considered outcasts in society would still be accepted and able to play in other reindeer games.

Mechagnomes would be amazing as would the Dark Iron given the racial ties between clan leaders in the new lore as well as Dragonmaw orcs who recently decided to go with the horde. I mean if you added some new quests you likely -could- find fitting subspecies for almost every race, and a lot of them would be amazing expanding even beyond the current ideas. (Such as the one about forsaken. :( It always sucked being an undead but not being able to be one of the other races effected by it!)

This guide has all of the support I could possibly give. Really the model ideas and concepts presented both in this thread and on others are amazing. Hopefully more customization comes on, I really hope so! But I also hope that we don't forget about the lore behind the races we're trying to play. WoW would be nothing without it's story. I feel like too often players forget that. <3
