OOC INFO

Mulgore at Midnight is a medium RP guild that also raids! We welcome members who like to do both of those things, or just one of them, or neither. Do you want to hang out with fun people and play a video game? Cool. That’s what we want too.

ROLE PLAY

On the RP side, we are a loose alliance of thieves, treasure hunters, scumbags, decent individuals who need to pay off debts, adventure-seekers, bounty hunters...or well, basically anyone who wants or needs to fill their pockets with gold for whatever reason. There are no real leaders among the bunch, and our guild storylines differ on a regular basis depending on what new treasure or marks we might be seeking out.

We RP once a week, with Wednesdays being guild storyline night. These events are run as D&D style adventures, with participants roaming through the world and cursing their terrible luck on /roll 20, and a rotating DM laughing as they all fail to climb out of a pit of snakes, or bugs, or both.

We also try to attend many RP server events.

RAIDING

On the raiding side, we are currently making our way through Heroic HFC. We are generally an easy-going group that likes to have fun when we raid, but also likes to take it seriously when it comes to progressing into new content. We have a lot of ex-hardcore types in the group that are here to enjoy raiding instead of treating it like a job (some cool kids with Hand of Adal and Empire’s Twilight titles). We might spend a lot of time ripping on each other in vent and making other terrible jokes, but when we pull a boss, it’s time to put on your serious pants and get ‘er done.

We raid twice a week, with Thursday evenings (6pm server to about 8pm) being farm night and Saturday evenings (5pm server to about 9pm) being progression night. Right now, our raid group is small, so we can accept just about any role, but here are some more specific needs:

Tanks: Low need (we have dedicated tanks already, but if this is your bread and butter, let’s talk)
Ranged DPS: Medium need
Melee DPS: Medium need
Healers: Low need

We ask that all raiders have at least 680 ilvl gear, have at least done all of the fights on LFR, and can connect to our Ventrilo server to at least listen (no mic required if that’s not your thing). To attend progression night, you need to work to be the best you can be in your role. If you want to know more about how to do that, any of our core raiders will be more than happy to help.

We currently use open rolls with main spec > off spec as our loot system.

FIND OUT MORE ABOUT US

To learn more about M@M, please visit our website: www.mulgoreatmidnight.com

If you have any questions, please feel free to ask our officers in game. Just ask whoever is online in the guild and they can direct you to someone who would be able to chat with you. We’re pretty much always around in the evening.

There’s no application required to join, but we do ask that you chat with our officers for a bit to make sure we are the right guild for you.
