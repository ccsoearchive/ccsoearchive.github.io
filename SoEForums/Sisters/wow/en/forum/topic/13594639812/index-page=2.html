Caravan update:

The time at which we will be ready to receive merchants, protectors, and passengers at the base of the towers in Tirisfal is at 1915 (server). The crew will have the cargo that comes from Orgrimmar by zeppelin loaded into the wagons by then. We will make stops along the way to Hammerfall for trade and rest. With the help of some local Forsaken, we have the site picked out for where we will camp for that night. Our van list grows, space is getting tight. Any merchants who can contribute an extra wagon or two, shall be compensated for the use.
Each soul may negotiate in person a better offer but, we will give 15% of the profit made off each wagon you lend us. We will fully insure the wagons as well, for the duration of the trip only.

(( Not to confuse anyone but the event would take nearly four hours with walk, wait, and RP time. So on the 22nd it is counted as one day of a two day trip. In the hour and a half that the event takes place will see it end just as the stars start to shine. To simulate the caravan traveling from mid-morning to dusk which will be halfway to Hammerfall. About a week after will be the last half with the start where we left off, ICly making it the last day. (If you have GHI we can sign a binding contract for you official foke, in regards to pay.))

Both the Horde and Alliance have made strong connections with the natives of Draenor and constructed strong outposts throughout the land. The Bilgewater cartel is starting to take notice of the potential for profitable trade that can be made on Draenor. Before they can began to capitalize on such a venture however, they have need to send resources into that realm of the past.

Since the factions of Azeroth have all of their sizable portals and transports of note tied up in the war effort, work orders have been offered to trade guilds and companies of commerce! The caravan Zephyr is one such group that is contracted to work out the logistics and is sending their wagons to task. Fellow pursuers of wealth and seekers of adventures, if you are interested in tagging along for merriment and diversion, then come down to Bogpaddle in the Swamps of sorrows at the appointed time and date! Admission is open to all and free, The Zephyr crew will provide the food, drink and a good time while you bring the added flavor with your personality.

If you are for hire and would like to help protect the caravan on this journey, contact the van leader Mor'telof for your pay details.

(( This is a traveling RP event where we move as a caravan train down the road from Bogpaddle to our destination. There will be a few IC happenings along the way which will affect the caravan such as environment hazards and beast attacks. Everyone is encouraged to chat with one another as we travel to enhance the event. The food and drink will be served in Stonard where we will RP there for 15-20 min. We ask that all who come use a Kodo mount to help visualize the train of wagons. Protectors for hire will be different and should contact me ICly by mail. It's an open event for all LV20 and above. Though if we don't have any means to transport you there, then you would have to find your own way there and might have to catch us en route. Pst or mail for calendar invite.))
