I'll get the history portion out of the way and then get on to the good stuff.

I founded the guild about 6 years ago on Earthen Ring. We saw a lot of success as we offered something a little niche and certainly unique. From day one it was our goal to offer a great RP experience that was consistent, active, and full of great story that everyone could make their own. We hard capped the guild at 50 members and were often very close to that number. We made ourselves visible in the community and worked very hard to keep a great over-arching story and maintain a great series of stories within that. We were able to run a weekly event every week for 4 years without missing it a single time. We had a lot of great inter-guild RP and we strove to help anyone in need. Recently, there was a rather heated disagreement about the guilds current state. Some of us opted to come here and try our hand at fostering the original vision we had. We hope you enjoy it.

IC:
You see a letter on the wall and it reads:

This is Grand Exemplar Murdoque Hightower of Might of Lordaeron. We are currently trying to rebuild our order as we cannot help our lands alone. Any Forsaken or Sin'dorei looking to keep their home from those that would seek to drive us out, please contact me as soon as possible. All Knights hailing from Acherus are implored to seek us as well. We care little for your race as Lordaeron has become your home.

From Eversong to Arathi, we seek to be a Bulwark for our home. We will help where we can. We maintain our independence from any dedicated military and rather work along side them if the need arises... And it does. Gilneas remains in the throws of war against the traitor dogs that hid behind her walls rather than help us fight Arthas' Scourge. Arathi remains at threat by the living ones that seek to deny us our rightful home. The Scarlets crop up here and there and it is our job to seek them out and destroy them before they can make a foothold once again.

OOC:
We're super excited to be here. We've gotten a lot of love coming through the door and we believe we have something awesome to share with you. We are primarily an Undead and Blood Elf guild and we allow Death Knights of any race to join. Things are probably going to be a little slow going at first as we get built up, but in time, we hope to reach that same point we once were at.

What we offer;
The hope is to offer a premier RP experience that's a bit smaller than other guilds. For the time we hope to cap the guild at 50 unique accounts. Our RP will have an over-arching story that will involve every single one of our members from the GM to the brand new recruit. Every RP interaction, both in guild and with the community at large, is important to us and we make our RP about the participants rather than a central character or event.

Our structure:
The upper portion of the guild will be made up of the Exemplar's Court. In the RP it stands un juxtaposition to the Knights of the Silverhand during the time of the Scourge. Where stood Paladin and Priests of the Light will be Exemplars. Death Knights and Shadow Priests l, and Warlocks. The Court is lead by Grand Exemplar Murdoque Hightower, High Exemplar Hellwatcher (We are looking for a Shadow Priest or Warlock to fill the second High Exemplar spot). Followed by the Exemplars as a whole. Then we have the War Clad for Warriors, Rogues, Hunters, Monks, and Paladin. They are lead by an Executor. Executors hold an honorary spot on the Exemplars Court. Then we have the Magus Elite. This is for Mage, Priests and Warlocks. They are lead by a Grand Magus who also joins the Exemplar's Court. As a note, ranks are RP based. This is an RP guild first and for most. If you want to push for a certain rank, let us know. We'll work with you to figure out how to get you where you want to be.

Our culture;
It is my view that honesty, open mindedness, understanding, and keeping an open forum for conversation is important. It is my duty to make everyone feel welcome and invited. When running story, every member has a say and an opportunity to be involved from the veteran to the member we got 5 minutes ago. We want to keep the guild smaller. The reason being is to allow us to interact with everyone and so that no one feels lost, or that a line off communication isn't accessible. Leadership is here for the guild, not to have power and a fancy title. The community is important to us. We want to be a part of it. To share stories back and forth.

What we're looking for;
Right now we need five Death Knights of any race and six Shadow priests and Warlocks either undead or Blood Elf in order to get the Exemplar's Court up and running.

Post here or message myself or Hellwatcher via whisper or through mail if you're interested in joining.
