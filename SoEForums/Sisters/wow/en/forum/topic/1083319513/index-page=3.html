My recommendations are to change the Druid section.

Druids of the Claw revere Urso and Ursol, transforming into bears only.
Druids of the Talon revere Aviana and transform into stormcrows and other birds of prey.
Druids of the Fang turn into snakes.
Druids of the Pack turn into worgen, not wolves. They revere Goldrinn.
Druids of the Grove are a handful of druids trying to summon an ancient in Alterac.
Druids of the Antler are rumored to exist.

Cat, Moonkin, Aquatic, Tree of Life, all of these forms are tools of Druids of the Wild. They don't belong with any other sect of Druidism.
