According to the WoWRPG Core Rulebook age table, Night Elves are considered adults at 300. They are considered middle-aged at 500 years, old at 650 years, and venerable at 700 years. Obviously, there are some Night Elves who have been around since before the Sundering, and it seems that these elves have retained their immortality. However, I figure you could count on one hand the number of elves that old, and as such they shouldn't be player characters.


The much more recent wow encyclopedia by Blizzard which puts adulthood at 100 years and that they can live for a varying period, between hundreds and thousands of years.

As for their weights, females weigh around 180lbs and males weigh around 190, this according to the height/weight tables.


What I did was take the average pounds per foot of a human, both female and male, and then add a bit of weight to account for the extra muscle.

A female elf averaging 180 pounds would be exceptionally thin as the average human male at 6 feet tall weight 180 pounds and they're a foot shorter. A male at 190 pounds, when standing 7.5 feet tall would equal a human man at 6 feet tall weighting 150 pounds.

Given the muscle mass of both genders, this would be impossible.
