I thought about bringing the angry Dwarf out to reply to the thread but I decided the more Stoic Blood Elf Mage would be a better personality for this topic.

With all joking aside, lets look at the thread and the replies there in. The issues that are plaguing the community at large is very prevalent to say the least. From the perceived insults or bad mouthing to the ideas that there is just down right hatred. It is all getting old and unless you are a immature kid that still needs to learn how to carrying on an objective conversation it really does need to come to a end.

I am going on the assumption that we are all adults here and can work our way to a mutual understanding and direction for the betterment of the community for both factions. In all honesty I have wondered about that but I going to go with my instincts and continue with my assumption.

When a discussion about updating stickies turns in to four pages alternating between constructive ideas, rants (perceived or real) and insults (perceived or real), we have some issues to resolve. Ideas need to be presented and discussion need to be presented in such a way that the perceived insult is taken a way. On the other hand a post should not always be considered and insult or what have you as well. (I am using insult to describe anything that would cause bad feelings).

With all of that said updating the stickies and/or replacing with no ones is actually a good idea. If the information is outdated, it does not do anyone a bit of good. As someone that is new to the RP scene on SoE, if I had looked at the Stickies above and found the information was outdated then I probably would have gotten discouraged, and quite honestly almost did. If it had not been for Tamz posting the sistersofelune.net site and finding that information I probably would have gone looking for another server. Hopefully you see where I am coming from here.

Now comes the part that needs to be discussed. First who is going to maintain the stickies? In the case of people leaving the server who is going to maintain the character list sticky? The same can be said for the guilds. This is where I wish we had a way to use attachments on the boards, but at last that option does not exist. It comes down to supplying the information and maintaining said information.

As I have stated the time for talk is over, the time for serious discussions of ideas is upon us.

I am going to work on yet another idea that this thread has put in my head. Hopefully it will come to something.
