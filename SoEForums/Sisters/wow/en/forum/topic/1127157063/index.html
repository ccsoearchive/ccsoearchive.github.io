I can only speak for Alliance side - we have some weekly events including a player run tavern on Mondays, Fishing excursions on Wednesdays, and Storytelling on Thursdays. There are also random events thrown together that are usually announced in our OOC channels or posted in places like the SoE RP boards at http://www.sistersofelune.org/

Alliance roleplay can be a bit reclusive at times, but open roleplay can be had. I highly suggest making a test character to hang out in Stormwind; feel free to come around on an evening during one of our events to meet some other roleplayers if you'd like.

A Horde roleplayer would be better able to tell you what goes on that side, although making a test character for that faction wouldn't hurt as well.

In the end, roleplay is what you make of it and your mileage may vary :)

Best of luck!

This server has fallen since it's height back in BC days, when it first opened. Mostly, as has been pointed out, cliques have sprung up and people generally only RP with their established friends. So if you're going to RP on SoE, you're going to need a guild with a fair amount of RPers - get you some introductions into these groups.

Open RP is sparse, partly because of clique mentality, and just as much because of griefers (just yesterday my friend and I were harassed because we were RPing by the Smithy in the Dwarf District). You can ignore and report griefers, but the only thing you can do about group dynamics is change human nature; or change the way in which RP servers function.
