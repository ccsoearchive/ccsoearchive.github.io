Hi-ho folks! Turwinkle is back in a brand new, exciting episode of the Adventures of Turwinkle, the Gnome Mage! In this episode, we find our heroes heading back to the Plaguewood, deep withing the Eastern Plaguelands to help Argent Crusader Judkins. Judkins sends us to Terrordale to check in on a friend of his, Augustus the Touched. Turwinkle, George and Glenn head over and speak with the Forsaken, who is clearly insane. Augustus has Turwinkle and the gang kill off some Plagued Swine and retrieve his receipt book.

With that done, we find that we have indeed finished our quest within the EPL!! So we head back to Light House Chapel one last time and take a journey aboard the Uncrashable, the goblin rocket, which takes us to the Fuselight-by-the-sea, in the Badlands!

So, grab a soda, your favorite snack and cuddle up to all the Turwinkle goodness! :D
