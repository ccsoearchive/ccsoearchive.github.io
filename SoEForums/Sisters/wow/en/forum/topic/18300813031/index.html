On the door of your favorite tavern's outhouse, you see a garishly designed poster that looks like it was drawn up by a child that got into some Bloodthistle.

Mulgore at Midnight and Ziffo Savings and Loan present...

The Kalimdor Regional Pet Battle Tour!

Have you always dreamed of making a name for yourself by pitting small creatures against one another in a battle to the death? Have you also realized that there's way too many people out there that do this already and that your chance at glory is already long past? Well friend, it's not too late!

The regional masters of Kalimdor are making a one night only appearance at the Great Gate in Mulgore to show you how it's done.

Battle not one, not two, but up to six potential masters! They will be bringing their weakest and most pathetic pets to give you a fighting chance. Learn their secrets and you'll be able to walk away with the knowledge that you have defeated these amazing masters:

  • Avian Astersong will be bringing her famed knowledge of all things critter. Defeat her rabid varmints!
  • Wolfmon Raziax will show off his knowledge of tiny beasts. Prepare to fight the tiniest and most adorable of wolves!
  • The sultry warlock known only as Strumpetta will do battle with flying creatures. Don't get pooped on from above!
  • Ji Yaoguo will strike down his foes with elemental pets. They could be fiery hot or icy cold, who knows?
  • Thaddeus Rengrave will show off his skills with humanoids. Wait, is it even legal to keep them?


Defeat all five of these masters and you will get a chance to battle a sixth and final mystery master. We can't reveal what wondrous creatures they have, but if you defeat them, you will get a chance to win a fabulous prize!

Ziffo Savings and Loan will be sponsoring delicious concessions and good luck charm vendors to help you power through your battles.

So meet us at the Great Gate at 6pm on Wednesday, July 22nd. Save the date, bring even your lowliest pets, and don't miss out on this once in a lifetime opportunity!

You squint to see the tiny print on the bottom:

Nominal entrance fee required.

OOC Info:

***This is for low level pets only! Please only bring pets up to level 5 to battle our trainers!***

**The entrance fee is IC only. No actual gold is required.**

*We will have other entertainment available for those who aren't into pet battling.*

Where: The Great Gate in Mulgore
When: 6pm server time (PST) on Wednesday, July 22nd
Why: For fun and profit!

Prizes will be offered via a raffle if you defeat our mystery master. One person will win a nice chunk of gold and all other participants will have their choice of a rare pet, so please stick around until the end of the event and hang out!

If you have any questions, please whisper anyone in Mulgore at Midnight and they will either answer your question or point you to someone who can.
