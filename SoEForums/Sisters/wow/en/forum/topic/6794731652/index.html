when i got to do this quest i follow the npc

As you go into the now opened middle hallway of Guo-Lai Halls, Ren is there waiting for you in a room of tiles of varying colours but with many matching

Ren Firetongue says: Whoa! Careful over there! These tiles have violet tendencies...
Ren Firetongue says: Cloud tile is good. Other tiles bad. There was a tile at the bottom of the entrance stairs that tipped me off. My sharp eyes have saved my delicate features yet again!

As he mentions, there is a lone tile as you get through the first hallway and into the big room. I assume that is the safe tile for today/this quest but I am not sure.

Ren Firetongue says: The mogu, they seem not so good at this game, if their dead bodies are any indication.

There are some dead mogu on "bad" tiles. Dumb mogu. After noticing the mogu, he follows the correct path through a much larger room of good and bad tiles. You can follow him or go ahead safely.

Ren Firetongue says: I see, I see.
Ren Firetongue says: Mmmm, shall we?
Ren Firetongue says: I think I would quite enjoy skipping stones down that hallway. It would be quite the display!

He says these during the trip through the room, I can't record if it was at any specific moments, except for the final one which he says after going through a large, operable (right-clickable) door.

The room opens up to an even larger place with huge mogu statues and staircases. The mogu's swing their axes when you get close, making the ground tremble and dealing damage if you stand in it but they do not start moving about like normal monsters As you get further into this larger room, Ren remarks:

Ren Firetongue says: Now where's that big mogu baddy that came tramping through here a short bit ago?

At this moment, Zhao-Jin the Bloodletter appears once more and blocks you from taking the Helm that is floating a little off the floor, two large mogu statues next to it.

Ren Firetongue says: He's... so...
Ren Firetongue says: ...ugly!

then the NPC just vanishes

I found several threads in the EU and US beta forums mentioning this bug. You would think that these kind of things would get double checked, sigh. Going to abandon quest and pick it up again to see if the NPC stays this time. Edit: abandoning quest and picking it up again did nothing to solve this issue.
